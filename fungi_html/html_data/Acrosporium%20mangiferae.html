Nomenclature data for Acrosporium mangiferae

Erysiphe quercicola S. Takam. & U. Braun 2007 (Ascomycetes, Erysiphales)

= Oidium anacardii F. Noack 1898

    ≡ Acrosporium anacardii (F. Noack) J.A. Stev. 1975

    ≡ Pseudoidium anacardii (Noack) U. Braun & R.T.A. Cook 2012

= Oidium bauhiniae G.J.M. Gorter & Eicker 1995

    ≡ Pseudoidium bauhiniae (G.J.M. Gorter & Eicker) U. Braun 2012

= Oidium bixae Viégas 1944

= Oidium erysiphoides f. citri J.M. Yen 1967

    ≡ Oidium citri (J.M. Yen) U. Braun 1982

= Oidium mangiferae Berthet 1914

  ≡Acrosporium mangiferae (Berthet) Subram. 1971

    ≡ Oidium erysiphoides f. mangiferae (Berthet) J.M. Yen & Chin C. Wang 1973  Note: Nom. inval. fide Braun (1987).

= Oidium nephelii Hadiw. ex U. Braun 1986

     Pseudoidium nephelii (Hadiw. ex U. Braun) U. Braun & R.T.A. Cook 2012

     Oidium erysiphoides f. nephelii (Hadiw.) J.M. Yen 1966

Notes: Braun (2013. Taxon 62:1328) proposed conservation of E. quercicola over O. anacardii. Approved, 2017. From Pseudoidium nephelii - From Oidium nephelii - Invalid name, no Latin description. From Oidium erysiphoides f. nephelii - Invalid name, basionym invalid.

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Leaves, fruit, inflorescences.

Disease Note: Powdery mildew, dieback, discoloration, defoliation.

Host: Diverse woody hosts.
