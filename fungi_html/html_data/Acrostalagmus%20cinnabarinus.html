Notes: The connection of "Nectria" inventa is tenuous. This species belongs in the Plectosphaeriellaceae.

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: On organic debris (Domsch et al. Compendium of soil fungi. p. 501. 1980).

Disease Note: Saprobic; occasionally pathogenic, causing post-harvest rot of ginger rhizomes and red rust of needle mushrooms.

Host: Isolated from soil, wood and a wide variety of plants.
