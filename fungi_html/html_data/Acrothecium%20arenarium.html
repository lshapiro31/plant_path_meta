Nomenclature data for Acrothecium arenarium

Curvularia inaequalis (Shear) Boedijn 1933 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

≡ Helminthosporium inaequale Shear 1902

= Acrothecium arenarium Moreau & Mme. Moreau 1941

Distribution: North America; Europe; Australia; Asia.

Substrate: Seeds, roots, leaves.

Disease Note: Seed mold, leaf blight.

Host: Various hosts.

Supporting Literature:
