= Cercospora canavaliae-roseae J.M. Yen & Gilles 1975

    ≡ Stenella canavaliae-roseae (J.M. Yen & Gilles) J.M. Yen 1982

Notes: From Cercospora canavaliae-roseae - As 'canavaliae-rosae'. From Dendryphion canavaliae - As 'Dendryphium'.

Distribution: Widespread, especially subtropical and tropical regions.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Canavalia spp. (Fabaceae).
