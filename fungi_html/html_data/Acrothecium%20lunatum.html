Curvularia lunata (Wakker) Boedijn 1933 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

≡ Acrothecium lunatum Wakker 1898

≡Curvularia lunata var. lunata (Wakker) Boedijn 1933  Note: Not needed if var. aeria is treated as a distinct species.

= Helminthosporium caryopsidum Sacc. 1914

    ≡ Curvularia caryopsidum (Sacc.) Teng 1964

= Cochliobolus lunatus R.R. Nelson & F.A. Haasis 1964

    ≡ Pseudocochliobolus lunatus (R.R. Nelson & F.A. Haasis) Tsuda et al. 1978

= Helminthosporium sudanense Gonz. Frag. & Cif. 1926

    ≡ Curvularia sudanensis (Gonz. Frag. & Cif.) Castell. 1952

Notes: See included type variety.

Distribution: Cosmopolitan, most common in tropical regions.

Substrate: Wood, stems, leaves.

Disease Note: Blights, leaf spot, etc.; also saprobic.

Host: Multiple genera in multiple families, especially Poaceae.
