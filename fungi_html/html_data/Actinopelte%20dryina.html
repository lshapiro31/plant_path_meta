Nomenclature data for Actinopelte dryina

Tubakia dryina (Sacc.) B. Sutton 1973 (Ascomycetes, Diaporthales)

≡ Leptothyrium dryinum Sacc. 1878

≡Actinopelte dryina (Sacc.) Höhn. 1925

= Actinopelte americana Höhn. 1925

= Leptothyrium castaneicola Ellis & Everh. 1888

  Variant spelling Leptothyrium castanicola Ellis & Everh. 1888

  Variant spelling Leptothyrium castanicolum Ellis & Everh. 1888

= Dicarpella dryina Belisario & M.E. Barr 1991

= Actinothyrium gloeosporioides Tehon 1924

= Leptothyrella liquidambaris Tehon & G.L. Stout 1929

  Variant spelling Leptothyriella liquidambaris Tehon & G.L. Stout 1929

= Pirostoma nyssae Tehon 1924

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Various hosts, especially Fagaceae.
