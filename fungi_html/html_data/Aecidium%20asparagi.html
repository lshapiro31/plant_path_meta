Nomenclature data for Aecidium asparagi

Puccinia asparagi DC. 1805 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

≡Dicaeoma asparagi (DC.) Kuntze 1898

≡Persooniella asparagi (DC.) Syd. 1922

≡Puccinia discoidearum var. asparagi (DC.) Wallr. 1833

= Aecidium asparagi Lasch 1848

= Uredo asparagi Lasch 1848

Distribution: Worldwide in asparagus growing regions.

Substrate: Shoots, stems.

Disease Note: Autoecious rust.

Host: Asparagus spp. (Asparagaceae) and a few other hosts.

Supporting Literature:

Waterston, J.M. 1965. Puccinia asparagi. C.M.I. Descr. Pathog. Fungi Bact. 54: 1-2.
