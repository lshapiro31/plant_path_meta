Nomenclature data for Aecidium avocense

Puccinia avocensis Cummins & H.C. Greene 1954 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

= Aecidium avocense Cummins & H.C. Greene 1954

Notes: Aecial host possibly Callirhoe triangulata; telial host - Stipa spartea. From Aecidium avocense - The connection with P. avocensis is tentative.

Distribution: WI.

Disease Note: Rust.
