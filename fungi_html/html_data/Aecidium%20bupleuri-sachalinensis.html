Nomenclature data for Aecidium bupleuri-sachalinensis

Puccinia miyoshiana Dietel 1899 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

≡Dicaeoma miyoshianum (Dietel) Syd. 1922

= Aecidium bupleuri-sachalinensis I. Miyake 1906

= Puccinia kozukensis Dietel 1902

    ≡ Dicaeoma kozukense (Dietel) Syd. 1922

Distribution: Asia.

Disease Note: Rust.

Host: Aecial state on Bupleurum spp. (Apiaceae); uredinial and telial states on Poaceae.

Supporting Literature:
