Nomenclature data for Aecidium clematidis

Aecidium asperifolii Pers. : Pers. 1796 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

= Aecidium clematidis DC. 1805

Distribution: Cosmopolitan, see teleomorph.

Disease Note: Rust.

Host: Multiple species in multiple genera.
