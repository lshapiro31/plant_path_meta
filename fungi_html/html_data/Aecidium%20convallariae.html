Notes: Spelling should probably be corrected to maianthae; from host Maianthemum. Oldest epithet not in the correct genus.

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Leaves. On Liliaceae. Hiratsuka et al. (The rust flora of Japan. p. 587. 1992 as Puccinia sessilis).

Disease Note: Heteroecious rust.

Host: Spermogonia and aecia: Liliaceae. Uredinia and telia: Poaceae.
