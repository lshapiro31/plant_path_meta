Nomenclature data for Aecidium daleae

Puccinia andropogonis var. onobrychidis Arthur 1934 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

[= Aecidium amphigenum Ellis & Kellerm. 1886 - illegitimate later homonym, not included in search]

= Aecidium daleae Kellerm. & Swingle 1889

= Aecidium falcatae Arthur 1906

= Aecidium fluxum Arthur 1907

= Aecidium lupini Peck 1893

= Aecidium onobrychidis Burrill 1884

= Aecidium petalostemonis Kellerm. & Carleton 1907

Notes: ITS distinct from P. andropogonis varieties on other aecial hosts (Szabo 2002). From Aecidium petalostemonis - Author changed from Corlett (checked protologue).

Distribution: North America (USA).

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Heteroecious rust.

Host: Telial host: Grasses (Poaceae). Aecial host: Various genera in Fabaceae.

Supporting Literature:

Szabo, L.J. 2006. Deciphering species complexes: Puccinia andropogonis and Puccinia coronata, examples of differing modes of speciation. Mycoscience 47: 130-136.
