Nomenclature data for Aecidium epilobii

Puccinia pulverulenta Grev. 1824 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

≡Puccinia epilobii-tetragoni G. Winter 1884  Note: A superfluous name; Winter transferred what he considered the oldest epithet, but the anamorph cannot serve as the basionym.

  Variant spelling Puccinia epilobii-tetragonii G. Winter 1884  Note: Variant spelling.

    ≡ Dicaeoma epilobii-tetragoni (G. Winter) Arthur 1920

≡Puccinia vagans Arthur 1934  Note: A superfluous name; Arthur transferred what he considered the oldest epithet, but the anamorph cannot serve as the basionym.

= Aecidium epilobii DC. 1815

= Puccinia intermedia Dietel 1893

    ≡ Dicaeoma intermedium (Dietel) Kuntze 1898

= Uredo vagans a epilobii-tetragoni DC. 1805

Notes: Oldest epithet not in correct genus.

Distribution: Asia, North America, South America, Europe, Australia, New Zealand.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Autoecious rust.

Host: Epilobium spp. and other Onograceae.
