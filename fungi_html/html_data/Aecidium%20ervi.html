Nomenclature data for Aecidium ervi

Uromyces ervi Westend. 1854 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

≡Puccinia ervi Opiz 1852

= Aecidium ervi Wallr. 1833

Notes: Not Plowr. 1889. Uromyces appendiculatus Lev. 1879 pro parte, Aecidium orobi Pers., Uromyces fabae (Pers.) de Bary in plur. auct. (Guyot 1957).

Distribution: Europe, Asia.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Rust.

Host: Ervum spp., Vicia spp. (Fabaceae). Sydow 1910 cites cultural studies by Plowright et al. that U. ervi is not found on Vicia spp. but this is unconfirmed.
