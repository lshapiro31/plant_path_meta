Nomenclature data for Aecidium fragiforme

Aecidium fragiforme Ces. 1879 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

Distribution: Asia; Australia.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Rust fungus.

Host: Agathis spp. ( Araucariaceae).
