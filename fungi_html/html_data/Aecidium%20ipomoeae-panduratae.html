Albugo ipomoeae-panduratae (Schwein.) Swingle 1892 (Oomycetes, Peronosporales)

    Variant spelling Albugo ipomoeae-panduranae (Schwein.) Swingle 1892

≡ Aecidium ipomoeae-panduratae Schwein. 1822  Note: As 'ipomoeae-panduranae'.

≡Puccinia ipomoeae-panduratae (Schwein.) P. Syd. & Syd. 1904

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Disease Note: White blister rust.

Host: Especially members of Convolvulaceae.
