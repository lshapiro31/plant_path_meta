Nomenclature data for Aecidium kaernbachii

Endophyllum kaernbachii (Henn.) F. Stevens & Mendiola 1931 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

≡ Aecidium kaernbachii Henn. 1892

= Aecidium rechingeri Bubák 1905

Distribution: Africa, Asia.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Rust.

Host: Hewittia sublobata, Ipomoea spp., Lepistemon spp., Merremia spp., Pharbitis purpurea (Convolvulaceae).

Supporting Literature:
