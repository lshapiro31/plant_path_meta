Distribution: Europe; USSR; Chile; Japan; China. Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Leaves, petioles, stems.

Disease Note: Rust.

Host: Vicia, Lathyrus, Pisum spp. (Fabaceae). Records on Cucurbitaceae are questionable (J. Hernandez, pers. comm.).
