Nomenclature data for Aecidium mirabilis

Puccinia esclavensis Dietel & Holw. 1897 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

= Aecidium mirabilis Dietel & Holw. 1897

Notes: Includes the type variety.

Distribution: North America, South America.

Disease Note: Rust. From Aecidium mirabilis - Rust.

Host: Telial and uredinial stages on Panicum spp., Pennisetum spp. (Poaceae), Aecial stage on Mirabilis spp. (Nyctaginaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Cummins, G.B. 1971. The Rust Fungi of Cereals, Grasses and Bamboos. Springer-Verlag, New York, 570 pages.

Ramachar, P., and Cummins, G.B. 1965. The species of Puccinia on the Paniceae. Mycopathol. Mycol. Appl. 25: 7-60.
