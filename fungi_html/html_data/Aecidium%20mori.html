Nomenclature data for Aecidium mori

Aecidium mori (Barclay) Barclay 1891 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

≡ Caeoma mori Barclay 1890  Note: Host: Morus alba var. serrata.

≡Peridiopsora mori (Barclay) K.V. Prasad, B.R.D. Yadav & Sullia 1993

≡Uredo mori (Barclay) Sacc. 1891  Note: With author listed as Barc. but with reference to Caoma mori Barclay 1890.

Notes: This name was originally published as Aecidium mori Barclay n. sp. (i.e., as a new species), but was later determined by Barclay to be identical to the previously described Caeoma mori Barclay. Barclay broadened the species concept to include the fungus on Ficus palmata, which was subsequently referred to Uredo fici Cast.. by Syd. in 1907. (Erroneously cited as (Barclay) Syd. 1907.) The new combination Peridiopsora mori (Barclay) K.V. Prasad et al. 1993 has not been widely adopted by other authors.

Distribution: Asia.

Substrate: Leaves, petioles, branches, buds.

Disease Note: Rust. Listed by USDA APHIS as a regulated plant pest.

Host: Broussonetia spp. and Morus spp. (mulberry), (Moraceae).
