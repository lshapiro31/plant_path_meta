Nomenclature data for Aecidium pampeanum

Puccinia pampeana Speg. 1880 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

≡Dicaeoma pampeana (Speg.) Kuntze 1898

= Puccinia araucana Dietel & Neger 1897

= Aecidium capsici F. Kern & Whetzel 1930

    ≡ Puccinia capsicicola F. Kern & Thurst. 1940  Note: Valid as a teleomorph name since this is technically a teleomorph state. No Latin needed to introduce a new morph name in accordance with ICBN Art. 59 because there is only one morph type.

[= Puccinia capsici Av.-Saccá 1917 - illegitimate later homonym, not included in search]  Note: Not Mayor 1913. Nomen illeg.

= Puccinia capsici Mayor 1913

= Puccinia gonzalezii Mayor 1913

  Variant spelling Puccinia gonzalezi Mayor 1913

= Aecidium pampeanum Speg. 1880  Note: From Endophyllum pampeanum - The aecidioid sori (Endophyllum state) function as telia too (Hernandez and Hennen 2003). Since there is no nomenclatural provision for multiple teleomorph form genera in rusts with pleomorphic life cycles, the correct holomorph name is problematic. However, the genus Puccinia has priority over Endophyllum in regards to date, and even though Puccinia pampeana and Endophyllum pampeanum, based on Aecidium pampeanum, have equal priority at the species rank, pucciniologists (Hennen et al. 1984, Hennen and Hernandez 2003) have given the Puccinia species priority. This establishes it along with its type as the name with priority according to ICBN Art 11.5.

    ≡ Endophyllum pampeanum (Speg.) J.C. Lindq. 1963

= Puccinia paulensis Rangel 1918

= Puccinia solanina Speg. 1912

= Aecidium solaninum Speg. 1881

= Aecidium solaninum var. leve Speg. 1909

  Variant spelling Aecidium solaninum var. laevis Speg. 1909

Distribution: North America (Central America, Mexico), South America.

Substrate: Living stems and leaves.

Disease Note: Rust.

Host: Several taxa of Solanaceae.

Supporting Literature:

Hernandez, J.R., and Hennen, J.F. 2003. Rust fungi causing galls, witches' brooms, and other abnormal plant growths in northwestern Argentina. Mycologia 95: 728-755.
