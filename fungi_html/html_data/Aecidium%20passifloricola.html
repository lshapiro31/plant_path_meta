Nomenclature data for Aecidium passifloricola

Puccinia scleriae (Pazschke) Arthur 1917 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

≡ Rostrupia scleriae Pazschke 1892

≡Dicaeoma scleriae (Pazschke) Arthur 1920

= Aecidium passifloricola Henn. 1904

Notes: Oldest epithet not in the correct genus.

Distribution: Asia, North America (Caribbean), South America. From Aecidium passifloricola - Asia, North America (Caribbean), South America.

Substrate: Leaves, stems (culms). From Aecidium passifloricola - Leaves, stems (culms).

Disease Note: Rust.

Host: Aecial state on Passiflora spp. (Passifloraceae), uredinial and telial states on Scleria spp. (Cyperaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Arthur, J.C. 1907. Uredinales. N. Amer. Fl. 7: 83-848.
