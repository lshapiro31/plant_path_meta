Nomenclature data for Aecidium pini

Endocronartium pini (Willd. : Pers.) Y. Hirats. 1969 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

≡ Lycoperdon pini Willd. : Pers. 1788

≡Aecidium pini (Willd. : Pers.) Pers. ex J.F. Gmel. 1792

≡Caeoma pini (Willd. : Pers.) Nees 1817

≡Cronartium pini (Willd. : Pers.) Jorst. 1925

≡Peridermium pini (Willd. : Pers.) Lév. 1826

Notes: Genetically indistinguishable from Cronartium flaccidum (Alb. & Schwein.) G. Winter 1880 (see Hantula et al. 2002), but distinguished by difference in life cycle (Hiratsuka 1969).

Distribution: Europe, Asia (China).

Substrate: Branches, tree trunks.

Disease Note: Autoecious endocyclic rust. Aecioid telia.

Host: Pinus spp. (Pinaceae).
