Nomenclature data for Aecidium plantaginis

Puccinia cynodontis Lacr. ex Desm. 1859 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

= Dicaeoma cynodontis Kuntze 1898

= Aecidium plantaginis Ces. 1859

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Heteroecious rust.

Host: Aecial stage: Plantago spp., Veronica spp. (Plantaginaceae), Viola spp. (Violaceae), Adonis (Ranunculaceae) and others; uredial and telial stages: Cynodon spp., particularly Bermuda grass - C. dactylon (Poaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Hennen, J.F., and Cummins, G.B. 1956. Uredinales parasitizing grasses of the tribe Chlorideae. Mycologia 48: 126-162.

Mulder, J.L., and Holliday, P. 1971. Puccinia cynodontis. C.M.I. Descr. Pathog. Fungi Bact. 292: 1-2.
