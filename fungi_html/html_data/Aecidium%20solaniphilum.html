= Aecidium tubulosum Pat. & Gaillard 1888

= Dicaeoma tubulosum Arthur & Fromme 1920  Note: This is a teleomorph name, ICBN Art. 59.

= Aecidium uleanum Pazschke 1892

Notes: From Puccinia tubulosa - This is an anamorph name. No description. (ICBN Art. 59). Oldest epithet not in the correct genus.

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Heteroecious rust.

Host: Aecial stage on Solanaceae, uredinial and telial stages on Poaceae.
