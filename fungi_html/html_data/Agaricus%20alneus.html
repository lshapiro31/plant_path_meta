Notes: Older epithets unpriorable due to sanctioning. For additional synonyms, see Index Fungorum.

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Living and dead trunks.

Disease Note: Brown dieback, trunk rot, leaf drop; infecting host through wounds. More often saprobic.

Host: Numerous hosts.
