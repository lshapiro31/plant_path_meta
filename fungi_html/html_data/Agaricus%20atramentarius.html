Nomenclature data for Agaricus atramentarius

Coprinopsis atramentaria (Bull.) Redhead, Vilgalys & Moncalvo 2001 (Agaricomycetes, Agaricales)

≡ Agaricus atramentarius Bull. 1786

≡Coprinus atramentarius (Bull. : Fr.) Fr. 1838

Notes: White rot of hardwoods. Older literature indicated that this fungus caused brown rots.

Distribution: Temperate northern hemisphere.

Disease Note: Root rot.

Host: Inky cap mushroom.
