Nomenclature data for Agaricus citricolor

Mycena citricolor (Berk. & M.A. Curtis) Sacc. 1887 (Basidiomycetes, Agaricales)

≡ Agaricus citricolor Berk. & M.A. Curtis 1868

≡Omphalopsis citricolor (Berk. & M.A. Curtis) Murrill 1916

= Omphalia flavida Maubl. & Rangel 1914

    ≡ Mycena flavida (Maubl. & Rangel) Singer 1951

= Sphaerostilbe flavida Massee 1909

= Stilbum flavidum Cooke 1880

    ≡ Stilbella flavidum (Cooke) Henn. 1904

    ≡ Decapitatus flavidus (Cooke) Redhead & Seifert 2000

    ≡ Pistillaria flavida (Cooke) Speg. 1896

= Mycena tricolor Velen. 1920

Notes: Parasitic on leaves on many forest trees. Especially prevalent on coffee, causing the 'American leaf disease.'

Distribution: Subtropical and tropical America.

Disease Note: Thread blight, iron spot.

Host: On diverse hosts.

Supporting Literature:

Rao, D.V., and Tewari, J.P. 1988. Suppression of the symptoms of american leaf spot of coffee with calcium hydroxide. Pl. Dis. 72: 688-690.
