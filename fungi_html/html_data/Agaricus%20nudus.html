Notes: Several varieties exist, but their distinction requires further evidence (Bigelow & Smith (Brittonia 21:144-177, 1969). Bigelow & Smith transferred most species of Lepista to Clitocybe, but recent authors retain this species in Lepista (see Stott et al., Mycol. Res. 109:205-211, 2005).

Disease Note: Commercially harvested edible fungus.
