Nomenclature data for Albugo achyranthis

Wilsoniana achyranthis (Henn.) Thines 2005 (, )

≡ Cystopus bliti f. achyranthis Henn. 1901

≡Albugo achyranthis (Henn.) Miyabe ex S. Ito & Tokun. 1935

Distribution: Scattered reports, Africa, Asia.

Disease Note: White blister rust.

Host: Achyranthes spp. (Amaranthaceae).
