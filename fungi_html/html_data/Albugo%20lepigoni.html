Nomenclature data for Albugo lepigoni

Albugo caryophyllacearum (Wallr.) Cif. & Biga 1955 (Oomycetes, Peronosporales)

= Cystopus argentinus Speg. 1887

= Cystopus lepigoni de Bary 1863

  ≡Albugo lepigoni (de Bary) Kuntze 1891

Notes: Given the synonyms that Biga (1955) listed, A. caryophyllacearum is a nom. illeg. via superfluous (ICBN Art. 52) since the basionym did not have priority at the species rank (ICBN Art. 11). The taxonomy and nomenclature of this species needs additional work.

Distribution: Scattered reports.

Disease Note: White blister rust.

Host: Caryophyllaceae.
