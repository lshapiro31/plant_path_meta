Nomenclature data for Albugo mors-uvae

Podosphaera mors-uvae (Schwein.) U. Braun & S. Takam. 2000 (Ascomycetes, Erysiphales)

≡ Erysiphe mors-uvae Schwein. 1834

≡Albugo mors-uvae (Schwein.) Kuntze 1892

≡Desetangsia mors-uvae (Schwein.) Nieuwl. 1916

≡Sphaerotheca mors-uvae (Schwein.) Berk. & M.A. Curtis 1876

≡Sphaerotheca mors-uvae var. mors-uvae (Schwein.) Berk. & M.A. Curtis 1908

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Living fruits, leaves, petioles, and stems.

Disease Note: Powdery mildew.

Host: Ribes spp. (Grossulariaceae).
