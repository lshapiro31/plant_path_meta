Nomenclature data for Albugo occidentalis

Albugo occidentalis G.W. Wilson 1907 (Oomycetes, Peronosporales)

≡Cystopus occidentalis (G.W. Wilson) Sacc. & Trotter 1912

Distribution: Asia, Europe, North America.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: White blister rust.

Host: Spinacia oleracea, Chenopodium spp. (Amaranthaceae).
