Nomenclature data for Albugo trianthemae

Albugo trianthemae G.W. Wilson 1908 (Oomycetes, Peronosporales)

≡Cystopus trianthemae (G.W. Wilson) Sacc. & Trotter 1912

= Albugo mesembryanthemi S.D. Baker 1955

Distribution: Widespread based on scattered reports.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: White blister rust.

Host: Typically Aizoaceae.
