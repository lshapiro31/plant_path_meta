Nomenclature data for Aleurodiscus reflexus

Michenera artocreas Berk. & M.A. Curtis 1868 (Basidiomycetes, Russulales)

= Aleurodiscus orientalis Lloyd 1920

= Aleurodiscus reflexus Yasuda 1922

= Corticium subgiganteum Berk. 1873  Note: From Licrostroma subgiganteum - Oldest epithet not used in correct genus.

    ≡ Licrostroma subgiganteum (Berk.) P.A. Lemke 1964

    ≡ Aleurodiscus subgiganteus (Berk.) Höhn. 1912

    ≡ Peniophora subgigantea (Berk.) Massee 1899

Notes: Michenera 1868 and Licrostoma 1964 are synonyms, thus the oldest genus is used for this species.

Distribution: Widespread in temperate regions.

Substrate: Woody substrates.

Disease Note: Saprobic.

Host: Various hardwoods.

Supporting Literature:

Lemke, P.A. 1964. The genus Aleurodiscus (sensu lato) in North America. Canad. J. Bot. 42: 723-768.
