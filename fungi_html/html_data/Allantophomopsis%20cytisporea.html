Nomenclature data for Allantophomopsis cytisporea

Allantophomopsis cytisporea (Fr. : Fr.) Petr. 1925 (Ascomycetes, Helotiales)

≡ Sphaeria cytisporea Fr. : Fr. 1823

Distribution: Northeastern North America, Europe.

Substrate: Leaves, fruit.

Disease Note: Black rot.

Host: Ericaceae.
