Nomenclature data for Allantophomopsis lycopodina

Allantophomopsis lycopodina (Höhn.) Carris 1990 (Ascomycetes, Helotiales)

≡ Neottiospora lycopodina Höhn. 1909

≡Strasseria lycopodina (Höhn.) Höhn. 1924

Distribution: North America (Canada, USA), Europe, (Austria, Poland).

Substrate: Leaves, fruit, cone scales; often on stressed plants.

Disease Note: Leaf lesions, branch tip dieback, decline.

Host: Vaccinium spp. (Ericaceae), Pinus spp., Pseudotsuga menziesii, Larix occidentalis, Abies alba (Pinaceae), Lycopodium complanatum (Lycopodiaceae).
