Nomenclature data for Allescheria laricis

Rhabdocline laricis (Vuill.) J.K. Stone 2014 (Ascomycetes, Helotiales)

≡ Meria laricis Vuill. 1896

= Allescheria laricis R. Hartig. 1899

    ≡ Hartigiella laricis (R. Hartig) Dietel & P. Syd. 1900

Distribution: Temperate northern hemisphere, New Zealand.

Substrate: Needles.

Disease Note: Needle cast.

Host: Larix spp. (Pinaceae)

Supporting Literature:

Gadgil, P.D. 2005. Fungi on trees and shrubs in New Zealand. Fungi of New Zealand Volume 4. Fungal Diversity Press, Hong Kong, 437 pages.

Johnston, Peter R., Seifert Keith A., Stone, J.K., Rossman, Amy Y., and Marvanova, L. 2014. Recommendations on generic names competing for use in Leotiomycetes (Ascomycota). IMA Fungus 5: 91-120.
