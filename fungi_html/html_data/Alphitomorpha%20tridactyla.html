Nomenclature data for Alphitomorpha tridactyla

Podosphaera tridactyla (Wallr.) de Bary 1870 (Ascomycetes, Erysiphales)

≡ Alphitomorpha tridactyla Wallr. 1833

≡Podosphaera clandestina var. tridactyla (Wallr.) W.B. Cooke 1952

≡Podosphaera oxyacanthae var. tridactyla (Wallr.) E.S. Salmon 1900

≡Erysiphe tridactyla (Wallr.) Rabenh. 1844

= Alphitomorpha brayana Doith 1838

    ≡ Erysiphe brayana (Doith) Rabenh. 1844

= Podosphaera longiseta Sawada 1951

= Oidium passerinii Bertol. 1879

= Podosphaera pruni Golovin 1956

= Podosphaera pruni-ulmifoliae Golovin 1950

Notes: Braun (1987) lists some additional synonyms.

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: On living leaves.

Disease Note: Powdery mildew.

Host: Rosaceae.
