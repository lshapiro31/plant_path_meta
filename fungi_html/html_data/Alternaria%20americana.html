Nomenclature data for Alternaria americana

Alternaria solani Sorauer 1896 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

= Alternaria americana Sawada 1931

= Macrosporium solani Ellis & G. Martin 1882  Note: Error in hostList - please email webmaster. See bottom of page.

= Sporidesmium solani-varians Vanha 1904

Notes: Alternaria americana Sawada is a nom. nov. for the illegitimate A. solani (Ellis & G. Martin) L.R. Jones & Grout (1899).

Distribution: Temperate and tropical regions. Some isolates on tomato from Russia were determined to be A. tomatophila (Gannibal et al. 2013).

Substrate: Living leaves and fruit.

Disease Note: Early blight.

Host: Multiple genera on various hosts.
