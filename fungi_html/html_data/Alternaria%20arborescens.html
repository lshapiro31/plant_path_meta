Nomenclature data for Alternaria arborescens

Alternaria arborescens E.G. Simmons 1999 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Various genera on multiple plant families.
