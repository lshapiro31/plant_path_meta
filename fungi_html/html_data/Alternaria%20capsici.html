Alternaria crassa (Sacc.) Rands 1917 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

≡ Cercospora crassa Sacc. 1877

= Alternaria capsici E.G. Simmons 2000

= Macrosporium cookei Sacc. 1886

    ≡ Alternaria cookei (Sacc.) Bremer, Ismen, Karel, Özkan & M. Özkan 1948

  [≡ Macrosporium solani Cooke 1883 - illegitimate later homonym, not included in search]  Note: Not Ellis & G. Martin 1882. Nomen illeg.

= Cercospora crassa f. sonchi Woron. 1917

= Cercospora crassa var. solani-nigri C. Massal. 1900

= Cercospora daturae Peck 1884 [1882]

= Macrosporium daturae Fautrey 1894

    ≡ Alternaria daturae (Fautrey) Bubák & Ranoj. 1909

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Datura spp., Capsicum spp. (Solanaceae) and some reports from other taxa.
