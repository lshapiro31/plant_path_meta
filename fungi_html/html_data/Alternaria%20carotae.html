Alternaria poonensis Ragunath 1963

= Alternaria porri f. sp. dauci Neerg. 1945  Note: This name has no standing under the ICBN.

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Leaves, seeds.

Disease Note: Leaf blight of carrot.

Host: Multiple host families but primarily reported on Daucus (Apiaceae).
