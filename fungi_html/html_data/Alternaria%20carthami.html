Nomenclature data for Alternaria carthami

Alternaria carthami Chowdhury 1944 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

= Macrosporium anatolicum A. Savul. 1944

= Macrosporium carthami Savul. 1943  Note: M. carthami Savul. 1943 is an illegitimate later homonym.

Notes: Ellis & Holliday (C.M.I. Descr. 241. 1970).

Distribution: North America, Africa, Europe, Asia (India), Israel, South America (Brazil).

Substrate: Living leaves and seeds.

Disease Note: Leaf spot and blight.

Host: Primarily on Carthamnus (Asteraceae), reports on other hosts may be erroneous.
