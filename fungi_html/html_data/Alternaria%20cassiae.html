Nomenclature data for Alternaria cassiae

Alternaria cassiae Jurair & A. Khan 1960 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

= Alternaria hibiscinficiens E.G. Simmons & C.F. Hill 2003

= Alternaria sauropodis E.G. Simmons 2007

Distribution: Southeastern United States, Asia (India, Malaysia, Pakistan), Africa (South Africa), Oceania (Fiji).

Substrate: Living leaves and stems, seedlings.

Disease Note: Leaf spot and blight, seedling blight.

Host: Cassia, Senna, Vicia, Vigna (Fabaceae), Hibiscus (Malvaceae), Sauropus androgynus (Euphorbiaceae).
