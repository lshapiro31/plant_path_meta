Nomenclature data for Alternaria chartarum

Ulocladium chartarum (Preuss) E.G. Simmons 1967 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

≡ Alternaria chartarum Preuss 1851

= Alternaria stemphylioides Bliss 1944

    ≡ Alternaria chartarum f. stemphylioides (Bliss) P. Joly 1964

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: On a variety of organic substrates.

Disease Note: Fruit spoilage.

Host: Multiple genera in multiple families.
