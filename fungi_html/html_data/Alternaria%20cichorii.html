Nomenclature data for Alternaria cichorii

Alternaria cichorii Nattrass 1937 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

≡Alternaria porri f. sp. cichorii (Nattrass) T. Schmidt 1965

= Alternaria dauci f. endiviae Janežic  Note: Listed by Boerema as f. sp. not f.

Distribution: Europe, Asia (Korea, India), North America (USA (CA, FL)), South America (Brazil, Argentina), Papua New Guinea.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Necrotic leaf spot. Overwinters in crop debris; seed-borne.

Host: Cichorium spp. (Asteraceae).
