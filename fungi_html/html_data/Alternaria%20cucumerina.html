Nomenclature data for Alternaria cucumerina

Alternaria cucumerina (Ellis & Everh.) J.A. Elliott 1917 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

≡ Macrosporium cucumerinum Ellis & Everh. 1895

= Alternaria loofahae E.G. Simmons & Aragaki 2007

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Living leaves and fruit.

Disease Note: Leaf and fruit blight and spot.

Host: Primarily on Cucurbitaceae.
