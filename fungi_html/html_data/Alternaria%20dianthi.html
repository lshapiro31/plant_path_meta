Nomenclature data for Alternaria dianthi

Alternaria nobilis (Vize) E.G. Simmons 2002 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

≡ Macrosporium nobile Vize 1877

= Alternaria dianthi F. Stevens & J.G. Hall 1909

    ≡ Macrosporium dianthi (F. Stevens & J.G. Hall) Bewley 1923

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Leaves, stems, flowers, seed capsules, and seeds.

Disease Note: Carnation blight, leaf spot.

Host: Primarily Carophyllaceae, also on Asteraceae, Malvaceae, Oleaceae.
