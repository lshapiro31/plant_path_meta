Nomenclature data for Alternaria dianthicola

Alternaria dianthicola Neerg. 1945 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Leaf.

Disease Note: Leaf spot, flower blight.

Host: Dianthus spp. (Caryophyllaceae).
