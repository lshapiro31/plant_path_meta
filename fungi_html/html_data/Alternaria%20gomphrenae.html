Nomenclature data for Alternaria gomphrenae

Nimbya gomphrenae (Togashi) E.G. Simmons 1989 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

≡ Alternaria gomphrenae Togashi 1926

= Pseudocercospora gomphrenicola Chidd. 1957

Distribution: Widespread, especially warmer regions.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Gomphrena spp. (Amaranthaceae).

Supporting Literature:
