Nomenclature data for Alternaria hawaiiensis

Alternaria passiflorae J.H. Simmonds 1938 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

= Alternaria gaurae E.G. Simmons & C.F. Hill 2007

= Alternaria hawaiiensis E.G. Simmons 1993

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Fruits, leaves, stems.

Disease Note: Dieback, leaf and fruit spots.

Host: Passiflora spp. (Passifloraceae), Gaura (Onagraceae), Capsicum frutescens (Solanaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Ellis, M.B., and Holliday, P. 1970. Alternaria passiflorae. C.M.I. Descr. Pathog. Fungi Bact. 247: 1-2.

Simmons, E.G. 2007. Alternaria. An identification manual. CBS Biodivers. Ser. 6: 1-775.

Woudenberg, J.H.C., Truter, M., Groenewald, J.Z., and Crous, P.W. 2014. Large-spored Alternaria pathogens in section Porri disentangled. Stud. Mycol. 79: 1-47.
