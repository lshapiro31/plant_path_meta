Notes: Saccardo made this combination through publication of Alternaria brassicae (Berk.) Sacc. var. minor Sacc. = Alternaria brassicicola, resulting in much confusion. The acceptance of Alternaria brassicae (Berk.) Sacc. as validly published is unorthodox but follows the arguments provided by Wiltshire (1947). Bolle (1924) republished the combination, as Alternaria brassicae (Berk.) Bolle 1924, arguing that Saccardo's combination was invalid, but Wiltshire (1947) rejected this argument.

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Brassicaceae.
