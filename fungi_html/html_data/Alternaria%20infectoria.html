Nomenclature data for Alternaria infectoria

Alternaria infectoria E.G. Simmons 1986 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

≡ Pleospora infectoria Fuckel 1870 [1869]

≡Lewia infectoria (Fuckel) M.E. Barr & E.G. Simmons 1986

Distribution: Widespread in temperate regions.

Substrate: Kernels.

Disease Note: Black point disease.

Host: Poaceae, Tanacetum cinerariifolium (Asteraceae) and other hosts.
