Nomenclature data for Alternaria kikuchiana

Alternaria gaisen Nagano ex Hara 1928 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

= Alternaria kikuchiana S. Tanaka 1933

= Macrosporium nashi Miura 1928

Notes: Alternaria gaisen Nagano (1920) published without description (Art. 38.1); sometimes cited as in violation of Art. 39.1 (no Latin description), which does not apply because of the publication date.

Distribution: Asia, Europe, North America.

Substrate: Fruit, leaves.

Disease Note: Fruit rot, leaf spot.

Host: Pyrus (Rosaceae), Oryza sativa (Poaceae).
