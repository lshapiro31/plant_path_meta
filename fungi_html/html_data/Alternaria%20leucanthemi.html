Nomenclature data for Alternaria leucanthemi

Teretispora leucanthemi (Nelen) E.G. Simmons 2007 (, )

≡ Alternaria leucanthemi Nelen 1959

= Alternaria chrysanthemi E.G. Simmons & Crosier 1965

Notes: From Alternaria leucanthemi - Simmons (Mycotaxon 25:211. 1986); Ellis (C.M.I. Descr. 164. 1968 as Alternaria chrysanthemi).

Distribution: FL; Austria; India, Myanmar; The Netherlands.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Leucanthemum (Asteraceae).
