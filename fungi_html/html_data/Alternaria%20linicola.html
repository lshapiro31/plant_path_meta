Nomenclature data for Alternaria linicola

Alternaria scorzonerae (Aderh.) Loer. 1984 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

≡ Sporidesmium scorzonerae Aderh. 1903

= Alternaria linicola J.W. Groves & Skolko 1944

Distribution: Africa (Kenya), Eastern United States, Europe.

Substrate: Living leaves, seeds.

Disease Note: Leaf and seedling blight.

Host: Scorzonera and Tragopogon (Asteraceae), Linum usitatissimum (Linaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Groves, J.W., and Skolko, A.J. 1944. Notes on seed-borne fungi II. Alternaria. Canad. J. Res. 22: 217-234.
