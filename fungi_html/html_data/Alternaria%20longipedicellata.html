Nomenclature data for Alternaria longipedicellata

Alternaria macrospora Zimm. 1904 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

≡Macrosporium macrosporum (Zimm.) Nisik. & Oshima 1944

= Sporidesmium longipedicellatum Reichert 1921

  ≡Alternaria longipedicellata (Reichert) Snowden 1927

Notes: Not Alternaria macrospora (Sacc.) Sawada 1931 = Alternaria brassicae.

Distribution: Subtropical and tropical regions.

Substrate: Living leaves and seeds.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Gossypium (Malvaceae).
