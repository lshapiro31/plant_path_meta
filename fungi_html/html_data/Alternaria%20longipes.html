Nomenclature data for Alternaria longipes

Alternaria longipes (Ellis & Everh.) E. Mason 1928 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

≡ Macrosporium longipes Ellis & Everh. 1892

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Living leaves and stems.

Disease Note: Black and Brown leaf spot.

Host: Various genera on multiple families. The report on Daucus is probably erroneous.
