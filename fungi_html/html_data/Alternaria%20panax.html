Nomenclature data for Alternaria panax

Alternaria panax Whetzel 1912 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

    Variant spelling Alternaria panacis Whetzel 1915

= Macrosporium araliae Dearn. & House 1940  Note: Invalid via no Latin.

Notes: Per Art. 60.1, the original spelling of an epithet is to be retained unless correctable because of orthographical or typographical errors; for that reason, "panacis" must be regarded as a variant spelling.

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Leaves; also petioles, shoots, stems, fruit.

Disease Note: Leaf spot; also dieback.

Host: Araliaceae.
