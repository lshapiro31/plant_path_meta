Nomenclature data for Alternaria petroselini

Alternaria petroselini (Neerg.) E.G. Simmons 1976 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

≡ Stemphylium petroselini Neerg. 1942

      Stemphylium radicinum var. petroselini (Neerg.) Neerg. 1945

Distribution: Europe; USA (CA, FL): on report from Saudi Arabia.

Substrate: Living leaves, seeds.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Coriandrum, Foeniculum, Petroselinum (Apiaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Ellis, M.B. 1976. More dematiaceous Hyphomycetes. Commonwealth Mycological Institute, Kew, Surrey, England, 507 pages.

Simmons, E.G. 1995. Alternaria themes and variations (112-144). Mycotaxon 55: 55-163.

Simmons, E.G. 2007. Alternaria. An identification manual. CBS Biodivers. Ser. 6: 1-775.
