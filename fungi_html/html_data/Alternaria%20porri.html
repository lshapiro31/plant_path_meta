Nomenclature data for Alternaria porri

Alternaria porri (Ellis) Cif. 1930 [1929] (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

≡ Macrosporium porri Ellis 1879

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: On living leaves and bulbs.

Disease Note: Purple blotch, leaf blight and blub rot.

Host: Allium (Amaryllidaceae).
