Nomenclature data for Alternaria radicina

Alternaria radicina Meier, Drechsler & E.D. Eddy 1922 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

≡Thyrospora radicina (Meier, Drechsler & E.D. Eddy) Neerg. 1939 [1937-8]

≡Pseudostemphylium radicinum (Meier, Drechsler & E.D. Eddy) Subram. 1961

≡Stemphylium radicinum (Meier, Drechsler & E.D. Eddy) Neerg. 1939 [1938-1939]

= Macrosporium daucinum Yatel 1938

Distribution: Temperate northern hemisphere.

Substrate: Crown, roots, seedlings.

Disease Note: Semi-dry crown and root rot, damping-off.

Host: Various members of Apiaceae.
