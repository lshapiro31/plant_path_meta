Nomenclature data for Alternaria raphani

Alternaria japonica Yoshii 1941 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

= Alternaria matthiolae Neerg. 1945

= Alternaria raphani J.W. Groves & Skolko 1944

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Fruit, leaves, seeds.

Disease Note: Black pod blotch, leaf spot, seedling blight.

Host: Multiple genera of Brassicaceae. Also, Carya illinoensis (Juglandaceae), Kalanchoe sp. (Crassulaceae), Oryza sativa (Poaceae), Vigna unguiculata (Fabaceae).
