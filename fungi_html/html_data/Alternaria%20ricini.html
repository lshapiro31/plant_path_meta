Nomenclature data for Alternaria ricini

Alternaria ricini (Yoshii) Hansf. 1959 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

≡ Macrosporium ricini Yoshii 1929

Distribution: Southeastern United States; Africa; Asia, Europe.

Substrate: Living leaves and seeds.

Disease Note: Leaf blight and spot, boll rot.

Host: Ricinus (Euphorbiaceae).
