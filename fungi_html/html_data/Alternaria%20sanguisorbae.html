Notes: Alternaria alternata, the type species of Alternaria to be proposed for conservation over the older epithet provided by the also sanctioned Helminthosporium tenuissimum.

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Fruits, leaves, roots, etc.

Disease Note: Leaf spot, rots, fruit drop.

Host: Multiple genera in multiple families.
