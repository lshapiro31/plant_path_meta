Nomenclature data for Alternaria senecionis

Alternaria cinerariae Hori & Enjoji 1931 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

= Alternaria senecionis Neerg. 1946

Distribution: CA, FL; Asia; Europe; New Zealand; South Africa.

Substrate: Living leaves, seeds.

Disease Note: Leaf spot, leaf blotch, damping-off.

Host: Primarily Senecio spp., also Gynura bicolor, Ligularia sp., Pericallis sp. (Asteraceae).
