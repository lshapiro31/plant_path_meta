Nomenclature data for Alternaria sesami

Alternaria sesami (E. Kawam.) Mohanty & Behera 1958 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

≡ Macrosporium sesami E. Kawam. 1931

Distribution: Southeastern United States; South America; Africa; Asia.

Substrate: Living leaves and seeds.

Disease Note: Leaf blight and spot.

Host: Sesamum (Pedaliaceae).

Supporting Literature:
