Nomenclature data for Alternaria solani-nigri

Alternaria solani-nigri R. Dubey, S.K. Singh & Kamal 1999 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

= Alternaria ascaloniae E.G. Simmons & C.F. Hill 2007

= Alternaria beticola E.G. Simmons & C.F. Hill 2007

= Alternaria cyphomandrae E.G. Simmons 2000

= Alternaria glyceriae E.G. Simmons & C.F. Hill 2007

= Alternaria herbiculinae E.G. Simmons 2007

Distribution: Asia (India), New Zealand.From Alternaria ascaloniae - Oceania (New Zealand).

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot/lesions.

Host: Solanum, Cyphomandra (Solanaceae), Petroselinum (Apiaceae), Glyceria (Poaceae), Beta (Amaranthaceae), Allium ascalonicum (Amaryllidaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Simmons, E.G. 2007. Alternaria. An identification manual. CBS Biodivers. Ser. 6: 1-775.

Woudenberg, J.H.C., Truter, M., Groenewald, J.Z., and Crous, P.W. 2014. Large-spored Alternaria pathogens in section Porri disentangled. Stud. Mycol. 79: 1-47.
