Nomenclature data for Alternaria tagetica

Alternaria tagetica Shome & Mustafee 1966 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

Notes: Simmons (Mycotaxon 14:20. 1982) provided a recent discussion of this species.

Distribution: Asia (India), North America (USA), Europe (England).

Substrate: Leaves, seeds, petals.

Disease Note: Leaf spot, blight.

Host: Tagetes spp. (Asteraceae).

Supporting Literature:

Woudenberg, J.H.C., Truter, M., Groenewald, J.Z., and Crous, P.W. 2014. Large-spored Alternaria pathogens in section Porri disentangled. Stud. Mycol. 79: 1-47.
