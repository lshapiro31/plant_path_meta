Nomenclature data for Alternaria tomato

Alternaria tomato (Cooke) Jones 1896 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

≡ Macrosporium tomato Cooke 1883

Notes: Several isonyms exist for ths name (Simmons 2007). This name has often been used inappropriately (Simmons 1993).

Distribution: Widespread, but many reports are probably based on misidentifications.

Substrate: Fruits.

Disease Note: Nailhead spot of fruit.

Host: Predominantly Solanum lycopersicum (Solanaceae). Reports on Passiflora are based on misidentifications.
