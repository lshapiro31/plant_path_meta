Nomenclature data for Alternaria triticina

Alternaria triticina Prasada & Prabhu 1963 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

Distribution: Africa, Asia, North America (Mexico).

Substrate: Fruit, leaves, seeds, soil.

Disease Note: Leaf spot, blight.

Host: Triticum spp., e.g. Triticum aestivum (Poaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Anahosur, K.H. 1978. Alternaria triticina. C.M.I. Descr. Pathog. Fungi Bact. 583: 1-2.

Simmons, E.G. 2007. Alternaria. An identification manual. CBS Biodivers. Ser. 6: 1-775.
