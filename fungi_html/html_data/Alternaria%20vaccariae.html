Alternaria vaccariae (Savul. & Sandu) E.G. Simmons & S. Koike 2002 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

≡ Macrosporium vaccariae Savul. & Sandu 1932

Distribution: Romania, USA (CA).

Disease Note: Leaf spot.
