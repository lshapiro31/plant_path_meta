Nomenclature data for Alternaria zinniae

Alternaria zinniae M.B. Ellis 1972 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

Notes: Non Alternaria zinniae H. Pape (1942) - nom. inval. Art. 36.1.

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Living leaves and petals, seeds.

Disease Note: Leaf and petal spot, seed abnormality.

Host: Asteraceae.
