Nomenclature data for Amazonia psychotriae

Amazonia psychotriae (Henn.) Theiss. 1913 (Ascomycetes, Meliolales)

≡ Meliola asterinoides var. psychotriae Henn. 1904

= Amazonia psychotriae var. major Hansf. 1954

Distribution: Widespread in tropical regions.

Substrate: Epiphytic on leaves.

Disease Note: Black mildew; a minor pathogen.

Host: Diverse dicot hosts.

Supporting Literature:

Hongsanan, S., Tian, Q., Persoh, D., Zeng, X.-Y., Hyde, K.D., Chomnunti, P., Boonmee, S., Bahkali, A.H., and Wen, T.-C. 2015. Meliolales. Fung. Diversity 74: 91-141.
