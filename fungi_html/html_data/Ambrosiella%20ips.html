Ophiostoma montium (Rumbold) Arx 1952 (Ascomycetes, Ophiostomatales)

≡ Ceratostomella montium Rumbold 1941

= Tuberculariella ips J.G. Leach, L.W. Orr & C.M. Chr. 1934

  ≡Ambrosiella ips (J.G. Leach, L.W. Orr & C.M. Chr.) L.R. Batra 1967

    ≡ Hyalorhinocladiella ips (J.G. Leach, L.W. Orr & C.M. Chr.) T.C. Harr. 2010

Notes: Associated with Ips bark beetles. ------ Anamorph pesotum-like to hyalorhinocladiella-like.

Distribution: North America.

Substrate: Wood.

Disease Note: Blue stain.

Host: Pinus spp. (Pinaceae).
