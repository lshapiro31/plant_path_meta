Nomenclature data for Amerodiscosiella indica

Mycoleptodiscus indicus (Sahni) B. Sutton 1973 (Ascomycetes, Incertae sedis)

≡ Amerodiscosiella indica Sahni 1968

Notes: Rarely associated with humans and animals.

Distribution: Widespread, especially subtropical and tropical regions.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Multiple genera in multiple families.

Supporting Literature:

De Hoog, G.S., Guarro, J., Gené, J., and Figueras, M.J. 2000. Atlas of Clinical Fungi, 2nd Edition. Centraalbureau voor Schimmelcultures, Baarn, 1126 pages.

Sutton, B.C. 1973. Pucciniopsis, Mycoleptodiscus and Amerodiscosiella. Trans. Brit. Mycol. Soc. 60: 525-536.
