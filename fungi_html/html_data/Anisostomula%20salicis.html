Nomenclature data for Anisostomula salicis

Colletotrichum salicis (Fuckel) Damm, P.F. Cannon & Crous 2012 (Ascomycetes, Phyllachorales)

≡ Sphaeria salicis Fuckel 1870

≡Anisostomula salicis (Fuckel) Petr. 1925

≡Glomerella salicis (Fuckel) L. Holm 2000

≡Physalospora salicis (Fuckel) Sacc. 1882

≡Physosporella salicis (Fuckel) Höhn. 1918

≡Plectosphaera salicis (Fuckel) Arx & E. Müll. 1954

= Phyllachora amenti Rostr. 1891

    ≡ Haplothecium amenti (Rostr.) Theiss. & Syd. 1915

    ≡ Glomerella amenti (Rostr.) Arx & E. Müll. 1954

= Glomerella lycopersici F. Kruger 1913

= Physalospora miyabeana Fukushi 1921  Note: From Glomerella miyabeana - Treated by Guerber et al. (2003) as a distinct taxon.

    ≡ Glomerella miyabeana (Fukushi) Arx 1957

Distribution: North temperate regions including eastern North America.

Substrate: Stems, branches, fruits and leaves.

Disease Note: Black canker, willow dieback, anthracnose.

Host: Primarily Salix (Salicaceae) but other hosts as well.
