Nomenclature data for Antennaria heteracantha

Acrogenotheca elegans (L.R. Fraser) Cif. & Bat. 1963 (Ascomycetes, Dothideales)

≡Capnodium elegans L.R. Fraser 1935

= Antennaria heteracantha Speg. 1889

= Acrogenotheca pulcherrima Bat. & Cif. 1963

= Oedemium robustum Berk. 1855

Distribution: Tropical and subtropical regions.

Disease Note: Sooty mold.

Host: Multiple genera in multiple families.
