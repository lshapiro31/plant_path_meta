Nomenclature data for Anthracoidea arenariae

Anthracoidea arenariae (Syd.) Nannf. 1977 (Ustilaginomycetes, Ustilaginales)

≡ Cintractia arenariae Syd. 1924

= Cintractia leioderma (Lagerh.) Cif. 1931  Note: Basionym of Ustilago caricis unranked leioderma Lagerh. 1888.

    ≡ Ustilago caricis var. leioderma Lagerh. 1888  Note: Error for Ustilago caricis unranked leioderma Lagerh. 1888.

    ≡ Anthracoidea leioderma (Lagerh.) Kochman & T. Majewski 1973

    ≡ Cintractia caricis var. leioderma (Lagerh.) Bubák 1898

Distribution: Northern hemisphere.

Disease Note: Smut.

Host: Carex (Cyperaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Vanky, K. 2012. Smut Fungi of the World. APS Press, St. Paul, Minnesota, 1458 pages.
