Nomenclature data for Anthracoidea caryophylleae

Anthracoidea caryophylleae Kukkonen 1963 (Ustilaginomycetes, Ustilaginales)

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Ovaries.

Disease Note: Smut.

Host: Carex spp. from sections Mitratae, Acrocystis and Lamprochlaenae (Cyperaceae).

Supporting Literature:
