Nomenclature data for Anthracoidea limosa

Anthracoidea limosa (Syd.) Kukkonen 1963 (Ustilaginomycetes, Ustilaginales)

≡ Cintractia limosa Syd. 1924

≡Cintractia limosa var. limosa .

= Cintractia gigantissima Lehtola 1940

     Anthracoidea limosa var. gigantissima (Lehtola) Zambett. 1978  Note: Nom. inval.

    ≡ Cintractia limosa var. gigantissima (Lehtola) Savile 1952

= Cintractia subglobosa S. Ito 1935

Distribution: Northern hemisphere.

Substrate: Ovaries.

Disease Note: Smut.

Host: Carex spp. from section Limosae (Cyperaceae).
