Distribution: North America (Canada, USA), Caribbean (Jamaica, Cuba), Europe (Portugal, Russia), Africa (Zimbabwe), Asia, New Zealand.

Substrate: Wood.

Disease Note: White rot.

Host: Trees of multiple plant families, conifers and hardwoods.
