Notes: From Gnomonia veneta - Not Speg. 1879. Nomen illeg. From Discula platani - As '(Peck) Sacc.'.

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: On living and fallen leaves.

Disease Note: Anthracnose.

Host: Typically Platanus spp. (Platanaceae).
