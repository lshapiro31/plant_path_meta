Nomenclature data for Armillariella solidipes

Armillaria ostoyae (Romagn.) Herink 1973 (Basidiomycetes, Agaricales)

≡ Armillariella ostoyae Romagn. 1970

= Agaricus obscurus Schaeff. 1774

    ≡ Armillaria obscura (Schaeff.) Herink 1973

= Armillaria solidipes Peck 1900

  ≡Armillariella solidipes (Peck) T.J. Baroni 1981

Notes: From Armillaria solidipes - See Volk and Burdsall (1995) for additional synonyms. *A conservation proposal to conserve Armillariella ostoyae over Armillaria solidipes, Agaricus obscurus and Agaricus occultans accepted by the Nomenclature Committee for Fungi. The synonomy of Armillaria solidipes is uncertain.

Distribution: Asia, Europe, North America.

Substrate: Roots, butt.

Disease Note: Root rot.

Host: Numerous hosts in several genera.
