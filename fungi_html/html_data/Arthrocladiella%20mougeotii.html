Nomenclature data for Arthrocladiella mougeotii

Arthrocladiella mougeotii (Lév.) Vassilkov 1963 (Ascomycetes, Erysiphales)

≡ Microsphaera mougeotii Lév. 1851

≡Erysiphe mougeotii (Lév.) de Bary 1870

≡Podosphaera mougeotii (Lév.) Quél. 1875

= Arthrocladiella mougeotii var. polysporae .Y. Zhao 1981

Distribution: Eurasia, introduced elsewhere.

Substrate: Inflorescences, stalks, leaves.

Disease Note: Powdery mildew.

Host: Lycium spp. (Solanaceae).
