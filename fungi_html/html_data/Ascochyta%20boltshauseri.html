    ≡ Stagonospora boltshauseri (Sacc.) Grigoriu 1975

    ≡ Phoma boltshauseri (Sacc.) Boerema, R. Pieters & Hamers 1993

[≡Ascochyta hortensis (Sacc. & Malbr.) Jørst. 1945 - illegitimate later homonym, not included in search]  Note: Not Kabát & Bubák 1905

= Stagonopsis phaseoli Erikss. 1891

= Phoma subboltshauseri Boerema, Gruyter & Noordel. 1997  Note: Phoma subboltshauseri is a synanamorph of Stagonosporopsis hortensis.

Notes: Phoma subboltshauseri is a synanamorph of Stagonosporopsis hortensis.

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Stems, leaves, pods.

Disease Note: Stunting, red-brown blotches on stems, leaves, pods; leaf spot.

Host: Phaseolus vulgaris and Vigna unguiculata (Fabaceae).
