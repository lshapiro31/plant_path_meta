Nomenclature data for Ascochyta brachypodii

Ascochyta brachypodii var. brachypodii (Syd.) R. Sprague & Aar.G. Johnson 1950 (Ascomycetes, Incertae sedis)

≡ Diplodina brachypodii Syd. 1916

≡Ascochyta brachypodii (Syd.) R. Sprague & Aar.G. Johnson 1950

= Ascochyta graminicola var. brachypodii Trail 1887

Notes: Punithalingam (1979) reexamined the type specimen and restricted the species concept to exclude most of the American collections of Sprague & Johnson (1950), but this has not been universally accepted.

Distribution: Europe. Reports in North America have been referred by Punithalingam (1979) to other Ascochyta spp. Reports from Africa (Kenya) and Asia (China) presumably follow the species concept of Sprague & Johnson (1950).

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Punithalingam (1979) considered Ascochyta brachypodii var. brachypodii to be restricted to Brachypodium sylvaticum (Poaceae); in contrast, Sprague & Johnson (1950) considered Ascochyta brachypodii var. brachypodii to have a broad host range of Poaceae.

Supporting Literature:

Punithalingam, E. 1979. Graminicolous Ascochyta species. Mycol. Pap. 142: 1-214.

Sprague, R., and Johnson, A.G. 1950. Ascochyta leaf spots of cereals and grasses in the United States. Mycologia 42: 523-553.
