Nomenclature data for Ascochyta chrysanthemi

    ≡ Phoma ligulicola Boerema 1990

= Stagonosporopsis ligulicola var. ligulicola (K.F. Baker, Dimock & L.H. Davis) Aveskamp, Gruyter & Verkley 2010

    ≡ Didymella ligulicola (K.F. Baker, Dimock & L.H. Davis) Arx 1962

    ≡ Didymella ligulicola var. ligulicola (K.F. Baker, Dimock & L.H. Davis) Arx 1990

    ≡ Stagonosporopsis ligulicola (K.F. Baker, Dimock & L.H. Davis) Aveskamp, Gruyter & Verkley 2010

Distribution: Cosmopolitan, throughout host range, possibly indigenous to Japan.

Substrate: Roots, stems, leaves, flowers.

Disease Note: Ray blight, black leaf blotches, stem lesions.

Host: Chrysanthemum, Dendranthema-Grandiflorum hybrids (Asteraceae).
