Nomenclature data for Ascochyta doronici

Ascochyta doronici Allesch. 1897 (Ascomycetes, Incertae sedis)

= Ascochyta gerberae Maffei 1913

= Gloeosporium lappae Dearn. & House 1918

Notes: On Asteraceae. The epithet doronici does not appear to be the earliest available name of the synonyms listed by Melnik (A guide to the fungi of the genus Ascochyta Lib. p. 99. 1977).

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Asteraceae.

Updated on Mar 11, 2015
