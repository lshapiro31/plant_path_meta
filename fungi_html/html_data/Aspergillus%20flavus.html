Nomenclature data for Aspergillus flavus

Aspergillus flavus Link : Fr. 1809 (Ascomycetes, Eurotiales)

Notes: Numerous synonyms, many of them probable, are listed by Raper and Fennell (1965).

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Multitudinous substrates. Reported as a pathogen of humans.

Disease Note: Secondary pathogen of some plants. Rots of fruit and food where it produces highly carcinogenic chemicals.

Host: Multiple genera in multiple families.
