Nomenclature data for Atelocauda hyalospora

Endoraecium hyalosporum (Sawada) M. Scholler & Aime 2006 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

≡ Uromyces hyalosporus Sawada 1913

≡Atelocauda hyalospora (Sawada) Y. Ono 1984

≡Maravalia hyalospora (Sawada) Dietel 1924

≡Poliotelium hyalosporum (Sawada) Mains 1939  Note: Corrected spelling.

  Variant spelling Poliotelium hyalospora (Sawada) Mains 1939  Note: Original spelling.

≡Racospermyces hyalosporus (Sawada) J. Walker 2001

Distribution: Asia (Japan; China; Taiwan).

Substrate: Phyllodes, pods, shoots.

Disease Note: Full-cycled autoecious rust.

Host: Acacia confusa (Fabaceae).
