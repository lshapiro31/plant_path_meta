Nomenclature data for Aurantiporus fissilis

Aurantiporus fissilis (Berk. & M.A. Curtis) H. Jahn ex Ryvarden 1978 (Basidiomycetes, Polyporales)

≡ Polyporus fissilis Berk. & M.A. Curtis 1849

≡Tyromyces fissilis (Berk. & M.A. Curtis) Donk 1933

Distribution: Widespread in northern hemisphere; introduced in South America.

Substrate: Wood.

Disease Note: White heart-rot, canker.

Host: Diverse hosts.

Supporting Literature:

Rajchenberg, M., and Robledo, G. 2013. Pathogenic polypores in Argentina. Forest Pathol. 43: 171-184.
