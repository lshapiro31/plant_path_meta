Nomenclature data for Bactrodesmium betulicola

Bactrodesmium betulicola M.B. Ellis 1959 (Ascomycetes, Dothideales)

Distribution: Europe, North America; unconfirmed report from Asia (Hong Kong).

Substrate: Dead, fallen branches.

Disease Note: Probably saprobic; possibly also a bark endophyte.

Host: Betula spp. (Betulaceae); reports from unrelated host taxa.

Supporting Literature:

Cannon, P.F. 2009. Bactrodesmium betulicola. I.M.I. Descr. Fungi Bact. 1814: 1-3.
