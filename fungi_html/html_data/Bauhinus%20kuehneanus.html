Nomenclature data for Bauhinus kuehneanus

Microbotryum kuehneanum (R. Wolff) Vánky 1998 (Microbotryomycetes, Microbotryales)

≡ Ustilago kuehneana R. Wolff 1874

≡Bauhinus kuehneanus (R. Wolff) Denchev 1997

= Ustilago acetosellae Maire 1915

= Ustilago utriculosa var. rumicis Berk. 1874

    ≡ Ustilago rumicis (Berk.) G.P. Clinton 1904

Distribution: Europe, N Africa, North America.

Substrate: Flowers, leaves, stems.

Disease Note: Smut.

Host: Rumex spp. (Polygonaceae).
