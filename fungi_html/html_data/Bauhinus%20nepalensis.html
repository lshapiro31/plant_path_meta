Nomenclature data for Bauhinus nepalensis

Microbotryum nepalense (Liro) Vánky 1998 (Microbotryomycetes, Microbotryales)

≡ Ustilago nepalensis Liro 1924

≡Melanopsichium nepalense (Liro) Zundel 1953

≡Bauhinus nepalensis (Liro) Denchev 1997

= Sphacelotheca nankingensis Zundel 1944

Distribution: Africa, Asia, Europe.

Substrate: Stems.

Disease Note: Smut.

Host: Polygonaceae.
