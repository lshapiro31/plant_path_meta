Nomenclature data for Bauhinus vinosus

Bauhinus vinosus (Tul. & C. Tul.) R.T. Moore 1992 (Urediniomycetes, Microbotryales)

≡ Ustilago vinosa Tul. & C. Tul. 1847

≡Microbotryum vinosum (Tul. & C. Tul.) Denchev 1994  Note: (Tul. & C. Tul.) G. Deml & Prillinger 1991 is a nom. inval.

Distribution: Widespread.

Disease Note: Smut.

Host: Polygonaceae.
