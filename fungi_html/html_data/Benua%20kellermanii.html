Nomenclature data for Benua kellermanii

Benua kellermanii (Sacc.) Constant. 1998 (Oomycetes, Peronosporales)

≡ Plasmopara kellermanii Sacc. 1891  Note: Saccardo was the first to validate this name. Many other author citations exist in the literature for this name and its synonyms due to uncertainty about who should get credit.

      Variant spelling Plasmopara kellermani Sacc. 1891

≡Basidiophora kellermanii (Sacc.) G.W. Wilson 1907

  Variant spelling Basidiophora kellermanni (Sacc.) G.W. Wilson 1907

≡Basidiophora kellermanii var. kellermanii

Peronospora kellermanii Ellis & Halst. 1889  Note: Nom. inval.

  Variant spelling Peronospora kellermani Ellis & Halst. 1889  Note: Nom. inval.

= Basidiophora kellermanii var. paupercula Peck 1911 [1910]

  Variant spelling Basidiophora kellermanni var. paupercula Peck 1911 [1910]

Distribution: Europe, North America.

Disease Note: Downy mildew.

Host: Cyclachaena xanthifolia (Asteraceae).

Supporting Literature:

Constantinescu, O. 1998. A revision of Basidiophora (Chromista, Peronosporales). Nova Hedwigia 66: 251-265.
