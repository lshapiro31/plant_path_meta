Nomenclature data for Bipolaris hawaiiensis

Curvularia hawaiiensis (Bugnic.) Manamgoda, L. Cai & K.D. Hyde 2012 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

≡ Drechslera hawaiiensis Bugnic. ex M.B. Ellis 1971

≡Bipolaris hawaiiensis (M.B. Ellis) J.Y. Uchida & Aragaki 1979

= Cochliobolus hawaiiensis Alcorn 1978

    ≡ Pseudocochliobolus hawaiiensis (Alcorn) Tsuda & Ueyama 1981

Notes: Helminthosporium hawaiiensis Bugnic. (as hawaiiense) 1955 - nom. inval., no Latin (Art. 39.1). ---- Drechslera hawaiiensis Bugnic. ex Subram. & B.L. Jain (as hawaiiense). nom. inval., no Latin. ---- Bipolaris hawaiiensis (M.B. Ellis) Tsuda & Ueyama 1981 - nom. illeg (53.1).

Distribution: Widespread, subtropical and tropical regions.

Substrate: Various plant parts.

Disease Note: Leaf and seed related diseases.

Host: Multiple genera in multiple families.
