Nomenclature data for Bipolaris victoriae

Bipolaris victoriae (F. Meehan & Murphy) Shoemaker 1959 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

≡ Helminthosporium victoriae F. Meehan & Murphy 1946

≡Drechslera victoriae (F. Meehan & Murphy) Subram. & P.C. Jain 1966

= Cochliobolus victoriae R.R. Nelson 1960

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf blight.

Host: Primarily on Poaceae.
