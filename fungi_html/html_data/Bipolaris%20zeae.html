Nomenclature data for Bipolaris zeae

Bipolaris zeae Sivan. 1985 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

= Cochliobolus zeae H.S. Chang 1992

Distribution: Asia, Australia, South America.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Zea mays, Cenchrus spp. and other Poaceae; reported from Acer truncatum (Sapindaceae).
