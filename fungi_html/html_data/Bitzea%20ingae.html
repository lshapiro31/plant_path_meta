Nomenclature data for Bitzea ingae

Chaconia ingae (Syd.) Cummins 1956 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

≡ Maravalia ingae Syd. 1925

≡Bitzea ingae (Syd.) Mains 1939

= Uredo excipulata Syd. & P. Syd. 1904

= Uredo ingae Henn. 1899

    ≡ Ravenelia ingae (Henn.) Arthur 1907

    ≡ Haploravenelia ingae (Henn.) Syd. 1921

= Uromyces ingaeiphilus Speg. 1925

= Uromyces ingicola Henn. 1904

= Uredo mogy-mirim Viégas 1945

= Uromyces porcensis Mayor 1913

= Maravalia utriculata Syd. 1925  Note: Listed as a synonym by Ono & Hennen (1983), but with a question mark.

= Ravenelia whetzelii Arthur 1917

Notes: According to Ono and Hennen (1983), 'the characteristics and unusual morphology of aecio and urediniospores of this rust causes mycologists to misidentify the spores as teliospores.' From Uredo mogy-mirim - Listed by Ono & Hennen (1984) as mogi-mirin. From Haploravenelia ingae - Published as (Arthur) Syd.

Distribution: North America (Mexico), Central America, South America. From Uredo ingae - North America (Mexico), Central America, South America.

Substrate: Leaves. From Uredo ingae - Leaves.

Disease Note: Rust. From Uredo ingae - Rust.

Host: Inga spp. (Fabaceae). From Uredo ingae - Inga spp. (Fabaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Ono, Y., and Hennen, J.F. 1983. Taxonomy of the Chaconiaceous genera (Uredinales). Trans. Mycol. Soc. Japan 24: 369-402.
