Nomenclature data for Blennoria abietis

Chrysomyxa abietis (Wallr.) Unger 1840 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

≡ Blennoria abietis Wallr. 1834

Notes: Type of Chrysomyxa Unger 1840.

Distribution: Europe, Asia.

Substrate: Foliage.

Disease Note: Autoecious rust; needle cast. Listed by USDA APHIS as a Regulated Plant Pest.

Host: Spruces, Picea spp. (Pinaceae). Reports on Pinus abies L. = Picea abies (L.) H. Karst.
