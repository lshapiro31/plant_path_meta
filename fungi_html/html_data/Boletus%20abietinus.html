Nomenclature data for Boletus abietinus

Trichaptum abietinum (Dicks. : Fr.) Ryvarden 1972 (Basidiomycetes, Polyporales)

≡ Boletus abietinus Dicks. 1793

≡Coriolus abietinus (Dicks. : Fr.) Quél. 1886

≡Polyporus abietinus (Dicks. : Fr.) Fr. 1821

≡Polystictus abietinus (Dicks. : Fr.) Cooke 1886

Notes: On wood of conifers. Many records of this species are actually T. fuscoviolaceum or T. laricinum. See notes under those species. (Ryvarden. The Polyporaceae of North Europe. p. 441. 1978). For additional synonyms, see Index Fungorum.

Distribution: Circumboreal.

Substrate: Dead wood.

Disease Note: Saprobic.

Host: Mostly Pinaceae and other softwoods.
