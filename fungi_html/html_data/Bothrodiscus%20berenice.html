Nomenclature data for Bothrodiscus berenice

Ascocalyx berenice (Berk. & M.A. Curtis) Baschien 2014 (Ascomycetes, Helotiales)

≡ Fusisporium berenice Berk. & M.A. Curtis 1875

≡Bothrodiscus berenice (Berk. & M.A. Curtis) J.W. Groves 1968

= Ascocalyx abietis Naumov 1925 [1926]

    ≡ Godronia abietis (Naumov) Seaver 1945

    ≡ Fusarium berenice (Berk. & M.A. Curtis) Sacc. 1886

= Bothrodiscus pinicola Shear 1907

Notes: Ascocalyx proposed for conservation over Bothrodiscus and Pycnocalyx by Johnston et al. (2014).

Distribution: Asia (Japan), Northeastern North America.

Substrate: On branches.

Disease Note: Cankers; of minor importance.

Host: Abies spp, Larix spp. (Pinaceae).

Supporting Literature:
