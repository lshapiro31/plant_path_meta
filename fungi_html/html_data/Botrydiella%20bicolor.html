Nomenclature data for Botrydiella bicolor

Staphylotrichum coccosporum J.A. Mey. & Nicot 1957 (Ascomycetes, Incertae sedis)

= Botrydiella bicolor Badura 1963

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Isolated from soil and plant debris.

Disease Note: Saprophyte.

Host: Assorted plant taxa.
