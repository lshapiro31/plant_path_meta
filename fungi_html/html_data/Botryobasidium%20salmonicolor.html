= Corticium zimmermannii Sacc. & P. Syd. 1902

Notes: Cunningham's (1963) synonyms are accepted in this nomenclaotor.

Distribution: Cosmopolitan in subtropical to tropical regions.

Disease Note: Pink disease, limb blight.

Host: Multiple genera in multile families.
