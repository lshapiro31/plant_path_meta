Nomenclature data for Botryosphaeria australis

Neofusicoccum australe (Slippers, Crous & M.J. Wingf.) Crous, Slippers & A.J.L. Phillips 2006 (Incertae sedis, Incertae sedis)

≡ Fusicoccum australe Slippers, Crous & M.J. Wingf. 2004

= Botryosphaeria australis Slippers, Crous & M.J. Wingf. 2004

Notes: From Botryosphaeria australis - Not (Cooke) Petr., a taxon that was never formally described.

Distribution: Europe (Italy, Portugal, Spain), Australia, Africa (South Africa), North America (Mexico, USA: CA), South America (Chile, Uruguay), New Zealand.

Substrate: Bark.

Disease Note: Stem cankers, dieback.

Host: Various hosts.
