Nomenclature data for Botryosphaeria foliorum

Phyllosticta foliorum (Sacc.) Wikee & Crous 2013 (Ascomycetes, Dothideales)

≡ Physalospora gregaria var. foliorum Sacc. 1882

≡Botryosphaeria foliorum (Sacc.) Arx & E. Müll. 1954

≡Melanops foliorum (Sacc.) Petr. 1931

≡Pyreniella foliorum (Sacc.) Theiss. 1916

Notes: From Pyreniella foliorum - Checked.

Distribution: Europe, North America (USA, Mexico).

Substrate: Dead plant parts.

Disease Note: Possibly causing canker; saprobic.

Host: Taxus baccata, Cryptomeria japonica (Taxodiaceae), reports from Agavaceae (Yucca sp.) and Ericaceae (Rhododendron ponticum).

Supporting Literature:

Wikee, S., Lombard, L., Nakashima, C., Motohashi, K., Chukeatirote, E., Cheewangkoon, R., McKenzie, E.H.C., Hyde, K.D., and Crous, P.W. 2013. A phylogenetic re-evaluation of Phyllosticta (Botryosphaeriales). Stud. Mycol. 76: 1-29.
