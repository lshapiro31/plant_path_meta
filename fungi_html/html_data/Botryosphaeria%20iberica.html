Nomenclature data for Botryosphaeria iberica

Dothiorella iberica A.J.L. Phillips, J. Luque & A. Alves 2005 (Ascomycetes, Dothideales)

= Botryosphaeria iberica A.J.L. Phillips, J. Luque & A. Alves 2005

Distribution: Africa (Algeria), Europe (Italy, Slovenia, Spain), North America (USA: CA), New Zealand.

Substrate: Dead twigs.

Disease Note: Dieback.

Host: Various hosts; noted pathogen of Vitis (Vitaceae).
