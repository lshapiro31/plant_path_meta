Nomenclature data for Botryosphaeria melanops

Melanops tulasnei Fuckel 1870 (Ascomycetes, Dothideales)

≡ Dothidea melanops Tul. & C. Tul. 1856

≡Botryosphaeria melanops (Tul. & C. Tul.) G. Winter 1887

= Dothiorella advena Sacc. 1882

    ≡ Fusicoccum advenum (Sacc.) Died. 1915 [1912]

= Dothiorella meliae Traverso 1906

= Fusicoccum testudo Höhn. 1903

Notes: Nom. nov. The anamorph is Fusicoccum-like.

Distribution: Northern hemisphere.

Substrate: On wood.

Disease Note: Saprobic.

Host: Fagaceae, especially Quercus spp.

Supporting Literature:

Phillips, A.J.L., and Alves, A. 2009. Taxonomy, phylogeny, and epitypification of Melanops tulasnei, the type species of Melanops. Fung. Diversity 38: 155-166.
