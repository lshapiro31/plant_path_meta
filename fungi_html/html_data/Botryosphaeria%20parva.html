Nomenclature data for Botryosphaeria parva

Neofusicoccum parvum (Pennycook & Samuels) Crous, Slippers & A.J.L. Phillips 2006 (Incertae sedis, Incertae sedis)

≡ Fusicoccum parvum Pennycook & Samuels 1985

= Botryosphaeria parva Pennycook & Samuels 1985

Notes: Commonly misidentified as Dothiorella dominicana Petr. & Cif. 1930, which is a possible taxonomic synonym (Slippers et al. 2005).

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Branches, trunk.

Disease Note: Canker, dieback, leaf spot, shot hole disease.

Host: Multiple genera in multiple families.
