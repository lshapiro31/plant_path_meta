Nomenclature data for Botryosphaeria proteae

Saccharata proteae (Wakef.) Denman & Crous 2004 (Dothideomycetes, Botryosphaeriales)

≡ Phyllachora proteae Wakef. 1922

≡Botryosphaeria proteae (Wakef.) Denman & Crous 1999

= Fusicoccum proteae Denman & Crous 1999

Distribution: Africa (South Africa), Australia, Europe (Portugal, Spain), North America (USA: Ca, HI), Oceania.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Saccharata leaf tip dieback.

Host: Leucospermum spp., Protea spp. (Proteaceae).
