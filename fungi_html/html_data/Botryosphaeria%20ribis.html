Nomenclature data for Botryosphaeria ribis

Neofusicoccum ribis (Slippers, Crous & M.J. Wingf.) Crous, Slippers & A.J.L. Phillips 2006 (Incertae sedis, Incertae sedis)

≡ Fusicoccum ribis Slippers, Crous & M.J. Wingf. 2004

= Botryosphaeria ribis Grossenb. & Duggar 1911

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Stems, branches, twigs, fruits.

Disease Note: Dieback, canker, fruit rot.

Host: Woody plants in multiple families.
