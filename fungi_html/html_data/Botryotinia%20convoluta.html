Nomenclature data for Botryotinia convoluta

Botrytis convoluta Whetzel & Drayton 1932 (Ascomycetes, Helotiales)

= Sclerotinia convoluta Drayton 1937  Note: A serious pathogen of Iris; rhizome rot (Boerema & Hamers. Neth. J. Pl. Pathol. 95(3):4. 1989; Maas. Phytopathology 60:185. 1970).

  ≡Botryotinia convoluta (Drayton) Whetzel 1945

Distribution: Northern North America; Europe, Asia (China, Iran).

Disease Note: Gray-mold rot.

Host: Iris (Iridaceae).
