Nomenclature data for Botryotinia fabae

Botrytis fabae Sardiña 1933 (Ascomycetes, Helotiales)

= Botryotinia fabae J.Y. Lu & T.H. Wu 1991

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Fruit debris, leaf, seed, shoot, soil.

Disease Note: Chocolate leaf spot, grey mould, shoot blight.

Host: Multiple genera of Fabaceae.
