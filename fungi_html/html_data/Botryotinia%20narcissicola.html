Nomenclature data for Botryotinia narcissicola

Botrytis narcissicola Kleb. 1906 (Ascomycetes, Helotiales)

= Sclerotinia narcissicola P.H. Greg. 1941

  ≡Botryotinia narcissicola (P.H. Greg.) N.F. Buchw. 1949

Notes: Smoulder, grey mold of Narcissus spp. (Williams & Spooner. I.M.I. Descr. 1083. 1991 as Sclerotinia narcissicola).

Distribution: Widespread in North America and Europe; USSR; Iraq; Australia; New Zealand.

Substrate: Overwintered leaves and bulbs.

Disease Note: Neck rot.

Host: Primarily on Narcissus (Amaryllidaceae).
