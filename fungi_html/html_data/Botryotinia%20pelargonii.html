Nomenclature data for Botryotinia pelargonii

Botrytis pelargonii Røed 1949 (Ascomycetes, Helotiales)

≡Sclerotinia pelargonii (Røed) Røed 1958

= Botryotinia pelargonii Røed 1949

Distribution: Asia, Europe.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Blight.

Host: Pelargonium inquinans, Pelargonium sp. (Geraniaceae).
