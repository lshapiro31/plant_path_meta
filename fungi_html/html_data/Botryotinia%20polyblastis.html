Nomenclature data for Botryotinia polyblastis

Botrytis polyblastis Dowson 1926 (Ascomycetes, Helotiales)

= Sclerotinia polyblastis C.T. Greg. 1938

  ≡Botryotinia polyblastis (C.T. Greg.) N.F. Buchw. 1949

Distribution: Widespread in Europe and North America.

Substrate: Flowers and leaves.

Disease Note: Flower and leaf blight.

Host: Primarily on Narcissus (Amaryllidaceae).

Updated prior to 1989
