Nomenclature data for Botryotinia porri

Botrytis porri N.F. Buchw. 1949 (Ascomycetes, Helotiales)

= Sclerotinia porri J.F.H. Beyma 1927

  ≡Botryotinia porri (J.F.H. Beyma) Whetzel 1947

Notes: At least five species of Botrytis are reported on species of Allium as discussed by Zhang et al. (2010).

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: On living leaves and bulbs.

Disease Note: Neck rot.

Host: Allium spp. (Amaryllidaceae).
