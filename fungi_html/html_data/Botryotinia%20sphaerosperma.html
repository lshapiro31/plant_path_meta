Nomenclature data for Botryotinia sphaerosperma

Botrytis sphaerosperma N.F. Buchw. 1949 (Ascomycetes, Helotiales)

= Sclerotinia sphaerosperma P.H. Greg. 1941

  ≡Botryotinia sphaerosperma (P. Greg.) N.F. Buchw. 1949

Notes: From Botryotinia sphaerosperma - Hennebert (Friesia 9:52. 1969; Persoonia 7:190. 1973).

Distribution: Asia, Europe, New Zealand, North America.

Substrate: On living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Allium and other Liliaceae.

Supporting Literature:

Scheck, H.J., Fogle, D.G., and Tidwell, T.E. 2003. First report of botryotinia blight of asiatic hybrid lily caused by Botryotinia sphaerosperma in North America. Pl. Dis. 87: 1396.
