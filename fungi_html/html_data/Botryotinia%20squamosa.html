Nomenclature data for Botryotinia squamosa

Botrytis squamosa J.C. Walker 1925 (Ascomycetes, Helotiales)

= Botryotinia squamosa Vienn.-Bourg. 1953

    ≡ Sclerotinia squamosa (Vienn.-Bourg.) Dennis 1956

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Living leaves and bulbs.

Disease Note: Leaf rot and neck rot of onions.

Host: Allium (Liliaceae).

Supporting Literature:
