Nomenclature data for Botrytis allii

Botrytis aclada Fresen. 1850 (Ascomycetes, Helotiales)

= Botrytis allii Munn 1917

Notes: (Ellis & Waller. C.M.I. Descr. 433. 1974 as B. allii).

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: On bulbs and basal stems.

Disease Note: Gray-mold neck rot.

Host: Allium spp. (Amaryllidaceae).

Supporting Literature:
