Nomenclature data for Botrytis bifurcata

Amphobotrys ricini (N.F. Buchw.) Hennebert 1973 (Ascomycetes, Helotiales)

≡ Botrytis ricini N.F. Buchw. 1949

= Botrytis bifurcata J.H. Mill., Giddens & A.A. Foster 1957

= Sclerotinia ricini Godfrey 1919

    ≡ Botryotinia ricini (Godfrey) Whetzel 1945

Distribution: Asia (China, South Korea)North America (USA), South America (Brazil),

Substrate: Leaves, stems, racemes.

Disease Note: Leaf blight, canker.

Host: Ricinus communis, Euphorbia spp., Acalypha spp. (Euphorbiaceae), reports from Fabaceae and Aracaceae.
