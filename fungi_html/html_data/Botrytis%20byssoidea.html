Nomenclature data for Botrytis byssoidea

Botrytis byssoidea J.C. Walker 1925 (Ascomycetes, Helotiales)

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: On living bulbs and stems.

Disease Note: Neck rot, damping off.

Host: Allium spp. (Liliaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Nielsen, K., and Yohalem, D.S. 2001. Origin of a polyploid Botrytis pathogen through interspecific hybridization between Botrytis aclada and B. byssoidea. Mycologia 93: 1064-1071.
