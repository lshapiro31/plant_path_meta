Nomenclature data for Botrytis destructor

Peronospora destructor (Berk.) Casp. ex Berk. 1860 (Oomycetes, Peronosporales)

≡ Botrytis destructor Berk. 1841

= Peronospora alliorum Fuckel 1863

= Peronospora schleidenii Unger 1847

  Variant spelling Peronospora schleideni Unger 1847

    ≡ Peronospora schleideniana de Bary 1863  Note: Nom. illeg. via superfluous. Attributed to Unger.

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Downy mildew.

Host: Allium spp. (Amaryllidaceae).
