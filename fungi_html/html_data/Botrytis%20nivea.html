Nomenclature data for Botrytis nivea

Plasmopara nivea (Unger) J. Schröt. 1886 (Oomycetes, Peronosporales)

≡ Peronospora nivea Unger 1847

Notes: Plasmopara typified by P. nivea, which was neotypified by Constantinescu et al. (2005). This combination is not based upon "Botrytis nivea Unger" (a wrongly attributed name) but upon Peronospora nivea Unger, which is based on a mixed collection.

Distribution: Asia, Europe.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Downy mildew.

Host: Apiaceae.
