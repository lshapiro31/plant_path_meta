Nomenclature data for Brachysporiella arengae

Acarocybellina arengae (Matsush.) Subram. 1992 (Ascomycetes, Incertae sedis)

≡ Sporidesmium arengae Matsush. 1975

≡Brachysporiella arengae (Matsush.) Hol.-Jech. 1983

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Most plant parts.

Disease Note: Black mildew.

Host: Several genera in few families, primarily Arecaceae.

Supporting Literature:

Dubey, R., and Moonnambeth, N.A. 2013. Acarocybellina arengae- A new generic and species record from India. Indian Phytopathol. 66: 326-327.
