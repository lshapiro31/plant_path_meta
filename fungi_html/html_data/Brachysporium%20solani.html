Nomenclature data for Brachysporium solani

Helminthosporium solani Durieu & Mont. 1849 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

≡Brachysporium solani (Durieu & Mont.) Sacc. 1886

= Dematium atrovirens Harz 1871

    ≡ Spondylocladium atrovirens (Harz) C. Harz ex Sacc. 1886

  Variant spelling Spondylocladium atro-virens (Harz) C. Harz ex Sacc.

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Tubers.

Disease Note: Silver scurf; a major pathogen of stored potatoes.

Host: Solanum tuberosum (Solanaceae).
