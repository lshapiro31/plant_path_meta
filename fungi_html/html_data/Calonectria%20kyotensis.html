Nomenclature data for Calonectria kyotensis

Calonectria kyotensis Terash. 1968 (Ascomycetes, Hypocreales)

= Calonectria floridana Sobers 1969

= Cylindrocladium floridanum Sobers & C.P. Seym. 1967

Notes: The oldest epithet Cylindrocladium floridanum cannot be placed in Calonectria because the name was already used.

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Leaves, stems, roots.

Disease Note: Canker, leaf spot, root rot, wilt, etc.

Host: Multiple genera in multiple families.

Supporting Literature:

Crous, P.W. 2002. Taxonomy and pathology of Cylindrocladium (Calonectria) and allied genera. American Phytopathological Society, St. Paul, Minnesota : 278.
