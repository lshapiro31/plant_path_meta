Nomenclature data for Calonectria pauciramosa

Calonectria pauciramosa C.L. Schoch & Crous 1999 (Ascomycetes, Hypocreales)

= Cylindrocladium pauciramosum C.L. Schoch & Crous 1999

Distribution: Widespread, especially in subtropical to tropical regions.

Substrate: All plant parts.

Disease Note: Crown, cutting, and root rots, leaf spot, stem canker.

Host: Mulitple genera in multiple families.
