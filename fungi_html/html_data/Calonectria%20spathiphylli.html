Nomenclature data for Calonectria spathiphylli

Calonectria spathiphylli El-Gholl, J.Y. Uchida, Alfenas, T.S. Schub., Alfieri & A.R. Chase 1992 (Ascomycetes, Hypocreales)

= Cylindrocladium spathiphylli Schoulties, El-Gholl & Alfieri 1982

     Cylindrocladium musae Semer, D.J. Mitch., M.E. Mitch., F.N. Martin & Alfenas 1987  Note: Nom. inval.

Notes: The oldest epithet Cylindrocladium spathiphylli cannot be placed in Calonectria because the name is already used.

Distribution: Widespread in subtropical to tropical regions. Spread further by human activity.

Disease Note: Foliar and stem disease, root and crown rots.

Host: Multiple genera in multiple families.

Supporting Literature:

Crous, P.W. 2002. Taxonomy and pathology of Cylindrocladium (Calonectria) and allied genera. American Phytopathological Society, St. Paul, Minnesota : 278.
