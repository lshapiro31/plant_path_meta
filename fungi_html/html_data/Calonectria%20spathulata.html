Nomenclature data for Calonectria spathulata

Calonectria spathulata El-Gholl, Kimbr., E.L. Barnard, Alfieri & Schoult. 1986 (Ascomycetes, Hypocreales)

= Cylindrocladium spathulatum El-Gholl, Kimbr., E.L. Barnard, Alfieri & Schoult. 1986

Distribution: South America.

Disease Note: Cutting rot, leaf spot.

Host: Few genera in diverse families including Eucalyptus (Myrtaceae) and Ilex (Aquifoliaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Crous, P.W. 2002. Taxonomy and pathology of Cylindrocladium (Calonectria) and allied genera. American Phytopathological Society, St. Paul, Minnesota : 278.
