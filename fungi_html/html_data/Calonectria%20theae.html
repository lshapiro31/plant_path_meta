Nomenclature data for Calonectria theae

Calonectria indusiata (Seaver) Crous 2002 (Ascomycetes, Hypocreales)

≡ Nectria indusiata Seaver 1928

= Calonectria theae Loos 1950

= Cercosporella theae Petch 1917  Note: From Cylindrocladium theae - There is a homonymous combination by Alfieri & Sobers in 1972.

    ≡ Cylindrocladium theae (Petch) Subram. 1972

    ≡ Candelospora theae (Petch) Wakef. ex Gadd 1949

Notes: The oldest name Cercosporella theae cannot be placed in Calonectria because the name was already used.

Distribution: Widespread especially in subtropical to tropical regions.

Disease Note: Leaf spot, root rot, stem and petiole lesions, etc.

Host: Multiple genera in multiple families.

Supporting Literature:

Crous, P.W. 2002. Taxonomy and pathology of Cylindrocladium (Calonectria) and allied genera. American Phytopathological Society, St. Paul, Minnesota : 278.
