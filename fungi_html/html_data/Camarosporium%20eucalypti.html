Nomenclature data for Camarosporium eucalypti

Neofusicoccum vitifusiforme (Van Niekerk & Crous) Crous, Slippers & A.J.L. Phillips 2006 (Incertae sedis, Incertae sedis)

≡ Fusicoccum vitifusiforme Van Niekerk & Crous 2004

= Neofusicoccum corticosae Crous & Summerell 2006

= Camarosporium eucalypti G. Winter 1886

    ≡ Dichomera eucalypti (G. Winter) B. Sutton 1975

Notes: Neofusicoccum eucalypti (G. Winter) Maleme, Pavlic & Slippers (2008) was introduced in a graduate thesis and not validly published (Art. 30.8).

Distribution: Scattered reports.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Eucalyptus corticosa spp. (Myrtaceae). Vitis vinifera (Vitaceae), Olea europea (Oleaceae), Prunus spp. (Rosaceae), Vaccinium corymbosum (Ericaceae).
