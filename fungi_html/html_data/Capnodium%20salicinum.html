Nomenclature data for Capnodium salicinum

Capnodium salicinum Mont. 1849 (Ascomycetes, Capnodiales)

≡Polychaeton salicinum (Mont.) Kuntze 1891

= Fumagospora capnodioides G. Arnaud 1911

Notes: The Fumagospora pycnidia are often intimately associated with the ascomata. Fraser (Proc. Linn. Soc. New South Wales 58:384. 1933) provided additional synonyms. From Fumagospora capnodioides - Hughes (Mycologia 68:693. 1976).

Distribution: Temperate northern hemisphere.

Substrate: Living leaves and twigs.

Disease Note: Sooty mould.

Host: Diverse plant hosts.

Supporting Literature:

Hughes, S.J. 1976. Sooty molds. Mycologia 68: 693-820.

Updated prior to 1989
