Nomenclature data for Capnodium walteri

Capnodium walteri Sacc. 1893 (Ascomycetes, Capnodiales)

= Phaeoxyphiella morototoni Bat. & Cif. 1963

= Phaeoxyphiella walteri Bat., Nascim. & Cif. 1963

Notes: 'Sooty Mold.' Hughes (Mycologia 68:796-799. 1976) gives a detailed account of the status of C. walteri Sacc. (Hughes. Advancing frontiers of mycology and plant pathology. pp. 29-31. 1981)

Distribution: Australia; New Zealand; South America; Caribbean; South Africa; south eastern U.S.A. From

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Sooty mold.

Host: Several genera in several families.

Supporting Literature:

Batista, A.C., and Ciferri, R. 1963. The sooty-molds of the family Asbolisiaceae. Quaderno 31: 1-229.
