Nomenclature data for Catabotrys deciduum

Catabotrys deciduum (Berk. & Broome) Seaver & Waterston 1946 (Ascomycetes, Boliniales)

≡ Hypoxylon deciduum Berk. & Broome 1875

= Bagnisiella palmarum Pat. 1887

    ≡ Catabotrys palmarum (Pat.) Theiss. & Syd. 1915

Distribution: Pantropical.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Endophytic/saprobic; not known to be pathogenic.

Host: Arecaceae.

Supporting Literature:

Hyde, K.D., and Cannon, P.F. 1999. Fungi causing tar spots on palms. Mycol. Pap. 175: 1-114.
