Nomenclature data for Cephalosporium diospyri

Nalanthamala diospyri (Crand.) Schroers & M.J. Wingf. 2005 (Ascomycetes, Incertae sedis)

≡ Cephalosporium diospyri Crand. 1945

≡Acremonium diospyri (Crand.) W. Gams 1971

Distribution: Southern United States and South Africa.

Disease Note: Persimmon Wilt

Host: Diospyros virginiana and Psidium guajava
