Nomenclature data for Cephalosporium gramineum

Hymenula cerealis Ellis & Everh. 1894 (Ascomycetes, Incertae sedis)

≡Phialophora cerealis (Ellis & Everh.) Nirenberg & Dalchow 1990

= Cephalosporium gramineum Y. Nisik. & Ikata 1934

Notes: The generic position of this species is in need of revision.

Distribution: North America; southern Africa; temperate Europe; Japan.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: 'Cephalosporium stripe'; chloritic stripes, stunting.

Host: Cereals and other grasses (Poaceae).

Supporting Literature:
