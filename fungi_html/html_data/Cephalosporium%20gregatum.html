Nomenclature data for Cephalosporium gregatum

Cadophora gregata (Allington & D.W. Chamb.) T.C. Harr. & McNew 2003 (Ascomycetes, Incertae sedis)

≡ Cephalosporium gregatum Allington & D.W. Chamb. 1948

≡Phialophora gregata (Allington & D.W. Chamb.) W. Gams 1971

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Leaves, stems.

Disease Note: Brown stem rot of soybeans and other legumes.

Host: Fabaceae, but especially Glycine max.

Supporting Literature:
