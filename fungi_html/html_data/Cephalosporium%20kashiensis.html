Nomenclature data for Cephalosporium kashiensis

Corallomycetella repens (Berk. & Broome) Rossman & Samuels 1999 (Ascomycetes, Incertae sedis)

≡ Sphaerostilbe repens Berk. & Broome 1873

= Stilbum hibisci Pat. 1891

    ≡ Rhizostilbella hibisci (Pat.) Seifert 1985

    =  Stilbum incarnatum var. dioscoreae Sacc. 1918

= Cephalosporium kashiense R.Y. Roy & G.N. Singh 1968

    Variant spelling Cephalosporium kashiensis R.Y. Roy & G.N. Singh 1968

    ≡ Acremonium kashiense (R.Y. Roy & G.N. Singh) W. Gams 1971

= Rhizostilbella rubra Wolk 1914

Notes: From Cephalosporium kashiense - Listed as a synonym by Seifert in 1985 but not in 1990.

Distribution: Asia.

Substrate: Soil, roots, bark.

Disease Note: Mildly parasitic or saprophytic.

Host: Trees of various plant families.
