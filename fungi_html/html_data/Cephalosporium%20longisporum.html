Nomenclature data for Cephalosporium longisporum

Lecanicillium longisporum (Petch) Zare & W. Gams 2001 (Ascomycetes, Incertae sedis)

≡ Cephalosporium longisporum Petch 1925

= Acrostalagmus aphidum Oudem. 1902  Note: Synonymy based on a drawing; no type available.

= Cephalosporium dipterigenum Petch 1931

Distribution: Asia (Sri Lanka), Europe (UK), South America (Peru). Also reported from Caribbean Islands (Virgin Islands, Puerto Rico).

Disease Note: A strain of L. longisporum has been used for biocontrol of aphids under the name Vertalec, but Vertalec may also include a strain of L. muscarium.

Supporting Literature:

Zare, R., and Gams, W. 2001. A revision of Verticillium section Prostrata. IV. The genera Lecanicillium and Simplicillium gen. nov. Nova Hedwigia 73: 1-50.
