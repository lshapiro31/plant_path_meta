Nomenclature data for Cephalosporium maydis

≡ Cephalosporium maydis Samra, Sabet & Hing. 1963

≡Harpophora maydis (Samra, Sabet & Hing.) W. Gams 2000

Distribution: Asia (India), Europe (Hungary, Portugal, Spain),

Substrate: Infects seedlings through roots or mesocotyl.

Disease Note: Late wilt.

Host: Zea (Poaceae)
