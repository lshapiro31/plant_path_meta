Nomenclature data for Cephalosporium proliferatum

Fusarium proliferatum (Matsush.) Nirenberg ex Gerlach & Nirenberg 1982 (Ascomycetes, Hypocreales)

≡ Cephalosporium proliferatum Matsush. 1971

= Gibberella fujikuroi var. intermedia Kuhlman 1982

Notes: Combination was invalid when originally proposed in 1976 due to omission of page # for basionym citation (Art. 41.4).

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Leaves, roots, fruit.

Disease Note: Foot rot, fruit rot, leaf spot.

Host: Various plant families.
