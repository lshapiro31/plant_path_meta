Nomenclature data for Cephalosporium sacchari

Fusarium sacchari (E.J. Butler & Hafiz Khan) W. Gams 1971 (Ascomycetes, Hypocreales)

≡ Cephalosporium sacchari E.J. Butler 1913

= Fusarium neoceras Wollenw. & Reinking 1925

[≡Gibberella sacchari Summerell & J.F. Leslie 2005 - illegitimate later homonym, not included in search]

Distribution: Subtropical and tropical regions; New Zealand.

Substrate: Inflorescences.

Disease Note: Pokkah boeng disease of sugar cane, head blight.

Host: Various plant families, but especially Saccharum spp. (Poaceae).
