Nomenclature data for Cephalosporium sclerotigenum

Acremonium sclerotigenum (Moreau & R. Moreau ex Valenta) W. Gams 1971 (Ascomycetes, Hypocreales)

≡ Cephalosporium sclerotigenum Moreau & R. Moreau ex Valenta 1948

Notes: Isolated from soil, fungi, sunflower seeds and various kinds of debris.

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Fruit.

Disease Note: Brown fruit spot.

Host: Diverse hosts.

Supporting Literature:

Li, B.H., Wang, C.C., Dong, X.L., Zhang, Z.F., and Wang, C.X. 2014. Acremonium brown spot, a new disease caused by Acremonium sclerotigenum on bagged apple fruit in China. Pl. Dis. 98(7): 1012.
