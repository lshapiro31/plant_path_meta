Nomenclature data for Cephalosporium zonatum

Acremonium zonatum (Sawada) W. Gams 1971 (Ascomycetes, Hypocreales)

≡ Cephalosporium zonatum Sawada 1919

= Cephalosporium eichhorniae Padwick 1946

= Cephalosporium fici Tims & Olive 1948

Distribution: Cosmopolitan in subtropical and tropical regions.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot, important on fig and coffee.

Host: Multiple genera in multiple families.

Supporting Literature:

Hawksworth, D.L. 1976. Acremonium zonatum. C.M.I. Descr. Pathog. Fungi Bact. 502: 1-2.
