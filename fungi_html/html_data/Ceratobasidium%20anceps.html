Nomenclature data for Ceratobasidium anceps

Rhizoctonia anceps (Bres., Syd. & P. Syd.) Oberw., R. Bauer, Garnica, R. Kirschner 2013 (Basidiomycetes, Polyporales)

≡ Tulasnella anceps Bres. & Syd. 1910

≡Ceratobasidium anceps (Bres. & Syd.) H. Jacks. 1949

≡Corticium anceps (Bres. & Syd.) M. Gregor 1932

= Sclerotium deciduum Davis 1919

Distribution: Central and northern North America, Europe.

Substrate: Leaves, stems.

Disease Note: Web-blight.

Host: Numerous hosts.

Supporting Literature:

Oberwinkler, F., Riess, K., Bauer, R., Kirschner, R., and Garnica, S. 2013. Taxonomic re-evaluation of the Ceratobasidium-Rhizoctonia complex and Rhizoctonia butinii, a new species attacking spruce. Mycol. Progr. 12: 763-776.
