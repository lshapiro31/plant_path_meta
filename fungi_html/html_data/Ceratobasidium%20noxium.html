Nomenclature data for Ceratobasidium noxium

Rhizoctonia noxia (Donk) Oberw., R. Bauer, Garnica & R. Kirschner 2013 (Basidiomycetes, Polyporales)

≡ Koleroga noxia Donk 1958

≡Ceratobasidium noxium (Donk) P. Roberts 1999

Notes: Pellicularia koleroga is an illegitimate name based on a mixture of several taxa; by extension, so are the names Corticium koleroga and Botryobasidium koleroga.

Distribution: Widespread in tropics.

Substrate: Living leaves, stems.

Disease Note: Web blight.

Host: Multiple genera in multiple families.

Supporting Literature:
