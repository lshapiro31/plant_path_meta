Nomenclature data for Ceratocystis albofundus

Ceratocystis albifundus M.J. Wingf., De Beer & M.J. Morris 1996 (Ascomycetes, Microascales)

    Variant spelling Ceratocystis albofundus M.J. Wingf., De Beer & M.J. Morris 1996

Notes: Anamorph thielaviopsis-like.

Distribution: Africa.

Substrate: Stems.

Disease Note: Cankers, dieback, gummosis; wilt and death on more susceptible hosts.

Host: Acacia spp. (Fabaceae), Protea spp. (Proteaceae).
