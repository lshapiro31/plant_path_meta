Nomenclature data for Ceratocystis coerulescens

Endoconidiophora coerulescens Münch 1907 (Ascomycetes, Microascales)

≡Ceratocystis coerulescens (Münch) B.K. Bakshi 1950

≡Ophiostoma coerulescens (Münch) Nannf. 1934

= Chalara ungeri Sacc. 1886

    ≡ Thielaviopsis ungeri (Sacc.) A.E. Paulin, T.C. Harr. & McNew 2002

Notes: From Ophiostoma coerulescens - Author changed from (Munch) Syd. & P. Syd. (authors of O. coeruleum 1919). Anamorph thielaviopsis-like. Synonomy of Chalara ungeri uncertain per de Beer et al (2014).

Distribution: Asia (Japan), Europe, North America.

Substrate: On wood; often causes blue stain.

Disease Note: Sapstain.

Host: Pinaceae.
