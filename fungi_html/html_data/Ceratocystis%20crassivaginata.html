Nomenclature data for Ceratocystis crassivaginata

Grosmannia crassivaginata (H.D. Griffin) Zipfel, Z.W. de Beer & M.J. Wingf. 2006 (Ascomycetes, Ophiostomatales)

≡ Ceratocystis crassivaginata H.D. Griffin 1968

≡Ceratocystiopsis crassivaginata (H.D. Griffin) H.P. Upadhyay 1981

≡Leptographium crassivaginatum (H.D. Griffin) M.J. Wingf. 1985  Note: Valid only as a new combination, not available for use of the anamorph exclusively (ICBN Art. 59).

≡Ophiostoma crassivaginatum (H.D. Griffin) T.C. Harr. 1987

Notes: Anamorph leptographium-like.

Distribution: Northern North America.

Disease Note: Canker.

Host: Pinaceae, Salicaceae.
