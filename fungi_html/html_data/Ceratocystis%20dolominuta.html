Nomenclature data for Ceratocystis dolominuta

Ceratocystiopsis minuta (Siemaszko) H.P. Upadhyay & W.B. Kendr. 1975 (Ascomycetes, Ophiostomatales)

≡ Ophiostoma minutum Siemaszko 1939

≡Ceratocystis minuta (Siemaszko) J. Hunt 1956

≡Ceratostomella minuta (Siemaszko) R.W. Davidson 1942

= Ceratocystis dolominuta H.D. Griffin 1968

Notes: Anamorph hyalorhinocladiella-like.

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Wood.

Disease Note: Sap stain; not pathogenic.

Host: Pinaceae.
