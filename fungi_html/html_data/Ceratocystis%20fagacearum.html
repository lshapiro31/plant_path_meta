Nomenclature data for Ceratocystis fagacearum

Bretziella fagacearum (Bretz) Z.W. de Beer, Marinc., T.A. Duong & M.J. Wingf. 2017 (, )

= Chalara quercina B.W. Henry 1944

    ≡ Thielaviopsis quercina (B.W. Henry) A.E. Paulin, T.C. Harr. & McNew 2002

Notes: Formal proposal submitted to conserve the basionym provided by Bretz over that of Chalara quercina B.W. Henry (1944).

Distribution: Eastern North America.

Substrate: Bark, roots.

Disease Note: Oak wilt.

Host: Quercus spp. (Fagaceae).
