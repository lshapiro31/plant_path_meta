Nomenclature data for Ceratocystis fraxinopennsylvanica

Phaeoacremonium fraxinopennsylvanicum (T.E. Hinds) D. Gramaje, L. Mostert & Crous 2015 (Ascomycetes, Incertae sedis)

≡ Ceratocystis fraxinopennsylvanica T.E. Hinds 1975

≡Calosphaeria fraxinopennsylvanica (T.E. Hinds) H.P. Upadhyay 1981

≡Togninia fraxinopennsylvanica (T.E. Hinds) Hausner, Eyjólfsdóttir & J. Reid 1992

= Phaeoacremonium mortoniae Crous & W. Gams 2001

Notes: From Calosphaeria fraxinopennsylvanica - Nomen. inval..

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Bark and stems, roots.

Disease Note: Isolated from brown stain, under stripped bark, and associated with larval galleries; root reduction.

Host: Vitis spp. (Vitaceae), Fraxinus spp. (Oleaceae), Malus (Rosaceae).
