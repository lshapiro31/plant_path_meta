Nomenclature data for Ceratocystis ips

Ophiostoma ips (Rumbold) Nannf. 1934 (Ascomycetes, Ophiostomatales)

≡ Ceratostomella ips Rumbold 1931

≡Ceratocystis ips (Rumbold) C. Moreau 1952

≡Grosmannia ips (Rumbold) Goid. 1936

Notes: Scoplaria rumboldii was published without a Latin diagnosis at a time when it was required. ------------------------------- Anamorph pesotum to hyalorhinocladiella-like.

Distribution: Widespread; origin in northern hemisphere.

Substrate: Wood.

Disease Note: Causes sapwood or blue stain.

Host: Pinus sp., also Larix and Picea (Pinaceae).
