Nomenclature data for Ceratocystis laricicola

Endoconidiophora laricicola (Redfern & Minter) Z.W. de Beer, T.A. Duong & M.J. Wingf. 2014 (Ascomycetes, Microascales)

≡ Ceratocystis laricicola Redfern & Minter 1987

Notes: Associated with Ips bark beetles.

Distribution: Asia, Europe.

Substrate: Wood.

Disease Note: Sapstain.

Host: Larix spp. (Pinaceae).
