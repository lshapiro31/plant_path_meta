Nomenclature data for Ceratocystis minor

Ophiostoma minus (Hedgc.) Syd. & P. Syd. 1919 (Ascomycetes, Ophiostomatales)

≡ Ceratostomella minor Hedgc. 1907

≡Ceratocystis minor (Hedgc.) J. Hunt 1956

= Ceratostomella exigua Hedgc. 1907

    ≡ Ophiostoma exiguum (Hedgc.) Syd. & P. Syd. 1919  Note: Presumed to be the obligate synonym.

= Ceratostomella pini Münch 1907

    ≡ Ceratocystis pini (Münch) C. Moreau 1952

    ≡ Ophiostoma pini (Münch) Syd. & P. Syd. 1919

    ≡ Grosmannia pini (Münch) Goid. 1936

Notes: Anamorph hyalorhinocladiella-like.

Distribution: Temperate northern hemisphere.

Substrate: Wood.

Disease Note: Sapstain.

Host: Multiple taxa in Pinaceae.
