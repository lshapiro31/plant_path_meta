Nomenclature data for Ceratocystis polychroma

Ceratocystis polychroma M. van Wyk, M.J. Wingf. & E.C.Y. Liew 2004 (Ascomycetes, Microascales)

Notes: Anamorph thielaviopsis-like.

Distribution: Asia (Indonesia).

Host: Syzygium aromaticum (Myrtaceae).
