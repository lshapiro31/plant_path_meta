Nomenclature data for Ceratocystis populicola

Ophiostoma populicola (Olchow. & J. Reid) Zipfel, Z.W. de Beer & M.J. Wingf. 2013 (Ascomycetes, Ophiostomatales)

≡ Ceratocystis populicola Olchow. & J. Reid 1974

Notes: Anamorph sporothrix-like.

Distribution: From Ceratocystis populicola - North America (Canada).

Host: From Ceratocystis populicola - Populus tremuloides (Salicaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Seifert K.A., De Beer, Z.W., and Wingfield, M.J. 2013. A nomenclator for ophiostomatoid genera and species in the Ophiostomatales and Microascales. CBS Biodivers. Ser., 245-322 pages. in Seifert, K.A., Beer, Z.W. De, and M.J. Wingfield. Ophiostomatoid fungi: Expanding frontiers. CBS Biodivers. Ser.. 337 pg.

Wingfield, M.J., Seifert, K.A., and Webber, J.F., Eds. 1993. Ceratocystis and Ophiostoma. Taxonomy, Ecology, and Pathogenicity. APS Press, St. Paul, Minnesota, 293 pages.

Updated on Aug 08, 2014

Ceratocystis harringtonii Z.W. de Beer & M.J. Wingf. 2013 (Ascomycetes, Microascales)

≡ Ceratocystis populicola J.A. Johnson & T.C. Harr. 2006

Notes: Replacement name for Ceratocystis populicola J.A. Johnson & T.C. Harr., an illegitimate later homonym of Ceratocystis populicola Olchow. & J. Reid. Anamorph theilaviopsis-like.

Distribution: North America.

Host: Populus spp. (Salicaceae).

Supporting Literature:

De Beer, Z.W., Duong, T.A., Barnes, I., Wingfield, B.D., and Wingfield, M.J. 2014. Redefining Ceratocystis and allied genera. Stud. Mycol. 79: 187-219.
