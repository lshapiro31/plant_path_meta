Nomenclature data for Ceratocystis populina

Ophiostoma populinum (T.E. Hinds & R.W. Davidson) De Hoog & R.J. Scheffer 1984 (Ascomycetes, Ophiostomatales)

≡ Ceratocystis populina T.E. Hinds & R.W. Davidson 1968 [1967]

Notes: Anamorph sporothrix-like.

Distribution: North America.

Disease Note: Canker.

Host: Populus tremuloides (Salicaceae).
