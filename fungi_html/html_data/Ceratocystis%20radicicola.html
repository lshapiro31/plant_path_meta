Nomenclature data for Ceratocystis radicicola

Thielaviopsis radicicola (Bliss) Z.W. de Beer & W.C. Allen 2016 (Ascomycetes, Microascales)

≡ Ceratostomella radicicola Bliss 1941

≡Ceratocystis radicicola (Bliss) C. Moreau 1952

≡Ophiostoma radicicola (Bliss) Arx 1952

= Chalaropsis punctulata Hennebert 1967

    ≡ Thielaviopsis punctulata (Hennebert) A.E. Paulin, T.C. Harr. & McNew 2002

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Roots.

Disease Note: Root rot, wilt.

Host: Diverse hosts, but particularly Arecaceae.

Supporting Literature:
