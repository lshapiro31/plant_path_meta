Nomenclature data for Ceratocystis virescens

DAvidsoniella virescens (R.W. Davidson) Z.W. de Beer, T.A. Duong & M.J. Wingf. 2014 (, )

≡ Endoconidiophora virescens R.W. Davidson 1944

≡Ceratocystis virescens (R.W. Davidson) C. Moreau 1952

≡Ophiostoma virescens (R.W. Davidson) Arx 1952

Notes: The anamorph is Chalara-like. According to Witthuhn et al. (2000), isolates from Fagaceae should be recognized as a species distinct from isolates on Acer and Liriodendron.

Distribution: North America (USA).

Substrate: Wood.

Disease Note: Sapstain.

Host: Acer spp. (Sapindaceae), Liroidendron spp. (Magnoliaceae), Fagus spp., Quercus spp. (Fagaceae).
