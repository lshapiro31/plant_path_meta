Nomenclature data for Ceratostoma phoenicis

Serenomyces phoenicis (Rolland) E. Müll & S. Ahmad 1962 (Ascomycetes, Phyllachorales)

≡ Ceratostoma phoenicis Rolland 1891

≡Sphaerodothis phoenicis (Rolland) P. Joly 1961

Notes: In India and Pakistan, frequently associated with Leiosphaerella phoenicis E. Müll. & S. Ahmad.

Distribution: Asia (India, Israel, Pakistan, Thailand), Europe (France).

Substrate: Dead petioles.

Disease Note: Lesions/tar spot; no evidence of necrosis.

Host: Phoenix dactylifera, Phoenix sp. (Arecaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Hyde, K.D., and Cannon, P.F. 1999. Fungi causing tar spots on palms. Mycol. Pap. 175: 1-114.
