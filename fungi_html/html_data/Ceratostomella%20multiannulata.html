Nomenclature data for Ceratostomella multiannulata

Ophiostoma multiannulatum (Hedgc. & R.W. Davidson) Arx 1937 (Ascomycetes, Ophiostomatales)

≡ Ceratostomella multiannulata Hedgc. & R.W. Davidson 1935

≡Ceratocystis multiannulata (Hedgc. & R.W. Davidson) J. Hunt 1956

Notes: Anamorph sporothrix-like.

Distribution: North America (Southeastern USA).

Disease Note: Sapstain.

Host: Pinus spp. (Pinaceae).
