Nomenclature data for Ceratostomella pluriannulata

Ophiostoma pluriannulatum (Hedgc.) Syd. & P. Syd. 1919 (Ascomycetes, Ophiostomatales)

≡ Ceratostomella pluriannulata Hedgc. 1907 [1906]

≡Ceratocystis pluriannulata (Hedgc.) C. Moreau 1952

Notes: Anamorph sporothrix-like.

Distribution: Widespread.

Disease Note: Sapstain.

Host: Multiple genera in multiple families.
