Nomenclature data for Cercodeuterospora trichophila

Mycovellosiella cajani (Henn.) Rangel ex Trotter 1931 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Cercospora cajani Henn. 1902

≡Passalora cajani (Henn.) U. Braun & Crous 2003

≡Vellosiella cajani (Henn.) Rangel 1915

= Cercospora indica U.B. Singh 1934

     Passalora cajani var. indica (U.B. Singh) U. Braun & Crous 2003  Note: Crous and Braun (2003) listed the varieties of M. cajani as synonyms of P. cajani, and the word Mycovellosiella was left out of the nomenclators. The combination in Passalora was not made and is not valid.

    ≡ Mycovellosiella cajani var. indica (U.B. Singh) Deighton 1974

= Cercodeuterospora trichophila Curzi 1932

    ≡ Mycovellosiella cajani var. trichophila (Curzi) Deighton 1974

     Passalora cajani var. tricophila (Curzi) U. Braun & Crous 2003  Note: Crous and Braun (2003) listed the varieties of M. cajani as synonyms of P. cajani, and the word Mycovellosiella was left out of the nomenclators. The combination in Passalora was not made and is not valid.

Notes: There is no evidence that Rangel provided this combination, and it was not present when he replaced Vellosiella with Mycovellosiella. However, Trotter attributes the combination to Rangel. It is preferable to cite this as Rangel ex Trotter or Trotter. From Dendryphion cajani - As 'Dendryphium'. Invalid name. Listed as a synonym of M. cajani var. trichophila in the past. From Vellosiella cajani - The genus Vellosiella Rangel is illeg. since it is a later homonym of a name in Scrophulariaceae. From Passalora cajani - Crous and Braun (2003) and Braun (personal communication) consider this to be one variable species. Varieties of P. cajani are not validly published.

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Cajanus spp. (Fabaceae).
