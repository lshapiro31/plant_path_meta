Pseudocercospora egenula (Syd.) U. Braun & Crous 2003 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Cercoseptoria egenula Syd. 1935

≡Cercospora egenula (Syd..) Chupp & Doidge 1948

≡Paracercospora egenula (Syd.) Deighton 1979

= Cercospora solani-melongenae Chupp 1948

Distribution: Africa, Asia, Hawaii.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Solanum spp. (Solanaceae).
