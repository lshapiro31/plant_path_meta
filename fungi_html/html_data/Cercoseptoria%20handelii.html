[= Cercospora rhododendri Marchal & Verpl. 1924 - illegitimate later homonym, not included in search]

Notes: From Cercospora rhododendri - Not Ferraris 1910. Nomen illeg. From Pseudocercospora handelii - Reports from Actinidia should be excluded (Crous and Braun 2003).

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Rhododendron spp. (Ericaceae).
