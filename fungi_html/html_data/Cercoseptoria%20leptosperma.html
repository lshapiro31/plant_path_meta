Nomenclature data for Cercoseptoria leptosperma

Pseudocercosporella leptosperma (Peck) U. Braun 1995 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Cercospora leptosperma Peck 1878 [1876]

≡Cercoseptoria leptosperma (Peck) Petr. 1925

≡Cercosporella leptosperma (Peck) Davis 1919

≡Septoriopsis leptosperma (Peck) Davis 1922  Note: 'leptospermum' is an orthographical variant.

≡Cylindrosporium leptospermum (Peck) Davis 1914

Distribution: Asia (Russia), North America.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Acanthopanax sessiliflorus, Aralia spp. (Araliaceae).
