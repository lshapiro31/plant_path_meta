Nomenclature data for Cercoseptoria ligustrina

Thedgonia ligustrina (Boerema) B. Sutton 1973 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Cercospora ligustrina Boerema 1962

≡Cercoseptoria ligustrina (Boerema) Arx 1981

Notes: The link to Mycosphaerella ligustri is incorrect (Crous and Braun 2003, Crous et al. 2009).

Distribution: Widespread in northern hemisphere.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Ligustrum spp. (Oleaceae).
