Nomenclature data for Cercoseptoria ocellata

Pseudocercospora ocellata (Deighton) Deighton 1987 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡Cercoseptoria ocellata Deighton 1983  Note: Nom nov.

≡Cercospora theae Breda de Haan 1900

Distribution: Widespread.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Camellia spp. (Theaceae).
