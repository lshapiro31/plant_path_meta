Nomenclature data for Cercoseptoria prunicola

Pseudocercospora prunicola (Ellis & Everh.) U. Braun 1997 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Cercospora prunicola Ellis & Everh. 1887

≡Cercoseptoria prunicola (Ellis & Everh.) J.M. Yen 1981

= Cercospora pruni-persicae J.M. Yen 1978

    ≡ Cercoseptoria pruni-persicae (J.M. Yen) J.M. Yen 1981  Note: As 'pruni-perciae'.

= Pseudocercospora pruni-yedoensis Goh & W.H. Hsieh 1991

     Cercospora pruni-yedoensis Sawada 1943  Note: Invalid name.

Distribution: Predominantly Asia and North America.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Prunus spp. (Rosaceae).
