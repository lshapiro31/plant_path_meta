Nomenclature data for Cercoseptoria sesami

Pseudocercospora sesami (Hansf.) Deighton 1987 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Cylindrosporium sesami Hansf. 1938

≡Cercoseptoria sesami (Hansf.) Deighton 1976

= Cercospora sesamicola Mohanty 1958

= Mycosphaerella sesamicola Sivan. 1985

Distribution: Widespread, especially subtropical to tropical regions.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Sesamum spp. (Pedaliaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Braun, U., and Freire, F.C.O. 2002. Some cercosporoid hyphomycetes from Brazil - II. Cryptog. Mycol. 23: 295-328.

Crous, P.W., and Braun, U. 2003. Mycosphaerella and its anamorphs: 1. Names published in Cercospora and Passalora. Centraalbureau voor Schimmelcultures, Utrecht, 571 pages.
