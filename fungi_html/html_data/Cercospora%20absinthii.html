Nomenclature data for Cercospora absinthii

Ragnhildiana ferruginea (Fuckel) U. Braun, C. Nakash., Videira & Crous 2017 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Cercospora ferruginea Fuckel 1863

≡Mycovellosiella ferruginea (Fuckel) Deighton 1979

≡Passalora ferruginea (Fuckel) U. Braun & Crous 2003

= Helminthosporium absinthii Peck 1878

  ≡Cercospora absinthii (Peck) Sacc 1886

= Ramularia absinthii Laubert 1920

= Cercospora olivacea G.H. Otth 1869

Notes: From Cercosporidium artemisiae - Invalid name.

Distribution: Widespread, especially northern hemisphere.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Artemisia spp. (Asteraceae). Several records from some hosts are doubtful and/or should be excluded.
