Nomenclature data for Cercospora acalyphae

Cercospora acalyphae Peck 1881 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

= Cercospora acalypharum Tharp 1917

    ≡ Cercosporina acalypharum (Tharp) Sacc. 1931

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Acalypha spp. (Euphorbiaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Chupp, C. 1954. Monograph of the fungus genus Cercospora. Published by the Author, Ithaca, New York, 667 pages.
