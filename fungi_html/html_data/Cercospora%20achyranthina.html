Nomenclature data for Cercospora achyranthina

Cercospora achyranthina Thirum. & Chupp 1948 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

Notes: Crous and Braun (2003) consider this to be Cercospora apii s. lat.

Distribution: Africa, Asia, South America (Venezuela).

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Achyranthes spp., Digera spp. (Amaranthaceae).

Supporting Literature:
