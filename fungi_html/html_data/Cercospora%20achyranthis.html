Nomenclature data for Cercospora achyranthis

Cercospora achyranthis Syd. & P. Syd. 1909 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

Distribution: Asia, Caribbean.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Achyranthes spp. (Amaranthaceae).

Supporting Literature:
