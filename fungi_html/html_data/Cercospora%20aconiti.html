Nomenclature data for Cercospora aconiti

Ramularia crassiuscula (Unger) U. Braun 1988 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Cylindrosporium crassiusculum Unger 1833  Note: As 'Cylindrospora crassiuscula'.

[= Ramularia delphinii Unamuno 1931 - illegitimate later homonym, not included in search]  Note: Not Jaap 1913. Nomen illeg.

= Ramularia delphinii Jaap 1913 [1912]

= Ramularia monticola Speg. 1881

  [=  Ramularia delphinii Dearn. & House 1915 - illegitimate later homonym, not included in search]  Note: Not Jaap 1913. Nomen illeg.

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Aconitum spp., Delphinium spp. (Ranunculaceae).
