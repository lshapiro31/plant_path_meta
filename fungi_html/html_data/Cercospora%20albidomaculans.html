Nomenclature data for Cercospora albidomaculans

Cercospora ricinella Sacc. & Berl. 1885 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡Cercosporina ricinella (Sacc. & Berl.) Speg. 1910

= Cercospora albido-maculans G. Winter 1885

    Variant spelling Cercospora albidomaculans G. Winter 1885

= Cercospora ricini Speg. 1899

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Ricinus communis (Euphorbiaceae).
