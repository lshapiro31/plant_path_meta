Nomenclature data for Cercospora aleuritis

Pseudocercospora aleuritis (I. Miyake) Deighton 1976 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡Mycosphaerella aleuritis S.H. Ou 1940

  Variant spelling Mycosphaerella aleuritidis S.H. Ou 1940

Distribution: Throughout range of host.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Aleurites spp. (Euphorbiaceae).
