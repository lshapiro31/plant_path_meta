Nomenclature data for Cercospora amadelpha

Pseudocercospora myrticola (Speg.) Deighton 1976 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Cercospora myrticola Speg. 1883

= Cercospora amadelpha Syd. 1932

= Fusariella cladosporioides P. Karst. 1891

= Cercospora myrti Erikss. 1885

= Cercospora saccardoana Scalia 1901  Note: Variant uncertain.

  Variant spelling Cercospora saccardiana Scalia 1901  Note: Original spelling.

Notes: Many collections identified as this species are misidentified (Crous and Braun 2003).

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Several taxa in Myrtaceae.

Supporting Literature:

Chupp, C. 1954. Monograph of the fungus genus Cercospora. Published by the Author, Ithaca, New York, 667 pages.

Crous, P.W., and Braun, U. 2003. Mycosphaerella and its anamorphs: 1. Names published in Cercospora and Passalora. Centraalbureau voor Schimmelcultures, Utrecht, 571 pages.
