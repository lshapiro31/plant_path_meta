Nomenclature data for Cercospora amaranthi

Cercospora brachiata Ellis & Everh. 1888 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

= Cercospora acnidae Ellis & Everh. 1891

= Cercospora amaranthi Lobik 1928

  Variant spelling Cercospora amaranti Lobik 1928

Notes: Crous and Braun (2003) consider this to be Cercospora apii s. lat.

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Amaranthus spp. (Amaranthaceae).
