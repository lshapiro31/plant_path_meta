Nomenclature data for Cercospora angolensis

Pseudocercospora angolensis (T. Carvalho & O. Mendes) Crous & U. Braun 2003 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Cercospora angolensis T. Carvalho & O. Mendes 1953

≡Phaeoramularia angolensis (T. Carvalho & O. Mendes) P.M. Kirk 1986

≡Pseudophaeoramularia angolensis (T. Carvalho & O. Mendes) U. Braun 1999

Distribution: Africa, Asia (Yemen).

Substrate: Fruits, leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf and fruit spot.

Host: Citrus spp. (Rutaceae).
