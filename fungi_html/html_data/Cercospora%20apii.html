Nomenclature data for Cercospora apii

Cercospora apii Miura 1957 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

    Variant spelling Cercospora apiodis Miura 1957

Notes: Invalid via no Latin. Also, it would be a formal homonym of C. apii Fresen.

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Apium graveolens (Apiaceae), Apios fortunei (Fabaceae), Plumeria rubra (Apocynaceae), Spondias mombin (Anacardiaceae), numerous genera of other plant families.

Updated on Oct 30, 2013

Cercospora apii Fresen. 1863 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡Cercosporina apii (Fresen.) Miura 1928

= Cercospora levistici Kvashnina 1928

= Cercospora penicillata var. apii Fuckel 1863

Notes: Numerous infrageneric taxa exist for this species. Crous and Braun (2003) believe that many taxa that are virtually indistinguishable, but historically considered as distinct taxa based on host range, are Cercospora apii s. lat.

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Fruits, leaves, stems.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Multiple genera in multiple families.
