Nomenclature data for Cercospora arborescentis

Cercospora hydrangeae Ellis & Everh. 1892 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

= Cercospora arborescentis Tehon & E.Y. Daniels 1925

= Cercospora hydrangeana Tharp 1917

    ≡ Cercosporina hydrangeana (Tharp) Sacc. 1931

= Cercosporina hydrangeicola Speg. 1910

    ≡ Cercospora hydrangeicola (Speg.) Vassiljevsky 1937

Notes: Apud G.F. Atk. 1892. Crous and Braun (2003) consider this to be Cercospora apii s. lat.

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Malvaceae, almost all reports from Hydrangea spp.

Supporting Literature:

Chupp, C. 1954. Monograph of the fungus genus Cercospora. Published by the Author, Ithaca, New York, 667 pages.

Crous, P.W., and Braun, U. 2003. Mycosphaerella and its anamorphs: 1. Names published in Cercospora and Passalora. Centraalbureau voor Schimmelcultures, Utrecht, 571 pages.
