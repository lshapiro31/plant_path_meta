Nomenclature data for Cercospora arctii-ambrosiae

Cercospora arctii-ambrosiae Halst. 1893 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

= Cercospora arctii F. Stevens 1925

= Cercospora lappae T. Watan. & N. Takah. 1934

Notes: As 'arcti-ambrosiae'.

Distribution: Asia, North America (USA), South America (Venezuela).

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Ambrosia spp., Arctium spp. (Asteraceae).

Supporting Literature:

Chupp, C. 1954. Monograph of the fungus genus Cercospora. Published by the Author, Ithaca, New York, 667 pages.

Crous, P.W., and Braun, U. 2003. Mycosphaerella and its anamorphs: 1. Names published in Cercospora and Passalora. Centraalbureau voor Schimmelcultures, Utrecht, 571 pages.
