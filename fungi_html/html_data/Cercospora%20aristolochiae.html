Nomenclature data for Cercospora aristolochiae

Cercospora olivascens Sacc. 1878 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡Cercosporina olivascens (Sacc.) Sacc. 1931

= Cercospora aristolochiae Roum. 1880  Note: Nomen nud., Invalid name.

= Cercospora aristolochiae-contortae H.D. Shin & U. Braun 1996

= Cercospora olivascens var. minor Serebrian. 1927

= Cercospora propinqua C. Massal. 1912

    ≡ Cercosporina propinqua (C. Massal.) Sacc. 1931

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Living and fading leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Aristolochia spp. (Aristolochiaceae).
