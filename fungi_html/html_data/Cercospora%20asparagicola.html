Nomenclature data for Cercospora asparagicola

Cercospora asparagi Sacc. 1877 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

= Cercosporina asparagicola Speg. 1910

  ≡Cercospora asparagicola (Speg.) Vassiljevsky 1937

= Cercospora caulicola G. Winter 1885

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Cladodes, branches.

Disease Note: Cercospora blight of Asparagus.

Host: Asparagus spp. (Asparagaceae).
