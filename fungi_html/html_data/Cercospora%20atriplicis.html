Nomenclature data for Cercospora atriplicis

Cercospora chenopodii Fresen. 1863 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡Cercosporina chenopodii (Fresen.) Miura 1928

= Cercospora atriplicis Lobik 1928

= Cercospora chenopodii var. micromacula Dearn. 1929

= Ramularia dubia Riess 1854  Note: From Cercospora dubia - Not Speg. 1880, nomen illeg.

    ≡ Cercospora dubia (Riess) G. Winter 1883

    ≡ Passalora dubia (Riess) U. Braun 1995

    ≡ Cercosporidium dubium (Riess) X.J. Liu & Y.L. Guo 1982

     Passalora dubia (Riess) Poonam Srivast. 1994

= Cercospora dubia var. atriplicis Bondartsev 1906

= Cercospora dubia var. urbica Roum. 1893

Notes: From Passalora dubia - Nomen invalid.

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Atriplex spp., Chenopodium spp. (Chenopodiaceae).
