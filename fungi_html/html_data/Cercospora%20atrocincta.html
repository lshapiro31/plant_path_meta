Nomenclature data for Cercospora atrocincta

Cercospora zinniae Ellis & G. Martin 1885 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

= Cercospora atricincta Heald & F.A. Wolf 1911

  Variant spelling Cercospora atro-cincta Heald & F.A. Wolf 1911

    Variant spelling Cercospora atrocincta Heald & F.A. Wolf 1911

= Cercosporina zinniae Takah. & Yosh. 1953

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Cosmos sp., Zinnia spp. (Asteraceae).
