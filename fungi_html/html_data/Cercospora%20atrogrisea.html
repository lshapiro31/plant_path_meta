= Cercospora nasturtii subsp. barbareae Sacc. 1882

    ≡ Cercospora barbareae (Sacc.) Chupp 1944

     Cercospora nasturtii var. barbareae Sacc. 1882  Note: Invalid name. Published as subsp.

Notes: From Cercospora lepidii - Invalid name.

Distribution: Widespread, especially northern hemisphere.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Several taxa of Brassicaceae, especially Armoracia spp., Gaura sp. (Onagraceae), Acacia mangium (Fabaceae).
