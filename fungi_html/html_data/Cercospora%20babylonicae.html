Nomenclature data for Cercospora babylonicae

Pseudocercospora salicina (Ellis & Everh.) Deighton 1976 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Cercospora salicina Ellis & Everh. 1887

= Cercospora minutipes J.M. Yen 1966

= Cercospora populina Ellis & Everh. 1887

= Cercospora reducta Syd. & P. Syd. 1903

= Cercospora salicis-babylonicae J.M. Yen 1966

[= Cercospora sessilis Ellis & Everh. 1902 - illegitimate later homonym, not included in search]  Note: Not Sorokin 1892. Nomen illeg.

= Mycosphaerella togashiana S. Ito & Tak. Kobay. 1953

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Populus spp., Salix spp. (Salicaceae).
