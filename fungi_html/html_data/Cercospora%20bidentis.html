Nomenclature data for Cercospora bidentis

Cercospora bidentis Tharp 1917 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

[= Cercospora bidentis É.J. Marchal & Steyaert 1929 - illegitimate later homonym, not included in search]  Note: Not Tharp 1917.

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Several taxa of Asteraceae.
