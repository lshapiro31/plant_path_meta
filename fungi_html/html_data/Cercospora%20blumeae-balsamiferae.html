Nomenclature data for Cercospora blumeae-balsamiferae

Pseudocercospora blumeae-balsamiferae Goh & W.H. Hsieh 1987 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

Notes: This combination (Sawada) Y.L. Guo & X.J. Liu 1989 was invalid due to invalid basionym.

Distribution: Asia, South America (Argentina).

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Blumea spp. (Asteraceae).
