Nomenclature data for Cercospora boehmeriae

Pseudocercospora boehmeriigena U. Braun 1997 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Cercospora boehmeriae Peck 1881

[≡Pseudocercospora boehmeriae (Peck) Y.L. Guo & X.J. Liu 1989 - illegitimate later homonym, not included in search]  Note: Not Goh & W.H. Hsieh 1989. Nomen illeg.

Notes: Pseudocercospora boehmeriigena is a nom. nov..

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Boehmeria spp. (Urticaceae).
