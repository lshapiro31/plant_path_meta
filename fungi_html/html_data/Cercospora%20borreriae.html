Nomenclature data for Cercospora borreriae

Pseudocercospora borreriae (Ellis & Everh.) Deighton 1976 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Cercospora borreriae Ellis & Everh. 1894

Distribution: Widespread, especially warmer regions.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Borreria spp., Mitracarpus hirtus (Rubiaceae).
