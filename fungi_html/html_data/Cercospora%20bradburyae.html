Nomenclature data for Cercospora bradburyae

Pseudocercospora bradburyae (E. Young) Deighton 1976 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Cercospora bradburyae E. Young 1916

≡Cercosporina bradburyae (E. Young) Sacc. 1931

= Cercospora cylindrospora F. Stevens & Solheim 1931

Distribution: Widespread, especially warmer regions.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Centrosema spp. (Fabaceae).
