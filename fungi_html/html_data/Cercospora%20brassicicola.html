Nomenclature data for Cercospora brassicicola

Cercospora brassicicola Henn. 1905 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

= Cercospora brassicae-campestris Rangel 1917

    ≡ Cercosporina brassicae-campestris (Rangel) Sacc. 1931

  Variant spelling Cercospora brassicae-junciae Sawada 1959

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Several taxa of Brassicaceae, especially Brassica spp.

Supporting Literature:
