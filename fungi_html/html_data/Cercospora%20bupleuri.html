Nomenclature data for Cercospora bupleuri

Passalora bupleuri (Pass.) U. Braun 1997 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Cercospora bupleuri Pass. 1879

= Cercospora bupleurina Lobik 1928

= Cercospora chaerophylli Höhn. 1903

= Cercosporella chaerophylli Aderh. 1902

    ≡ Passalora chaerophylli (Aderh.) U. Braun 1993

= Cercospora coriandri Rjach. 1931

= Cercospora trachyspermi Golovin 1950

Distribution: Widespread, especially Eurasia.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Several taxa of Apiaceae.

Supporting Literature:

Chupp, C. 1954. Monograph of the fungus genus Cercospora. Published by the Author, Ithaca, New York, 667 pages.

Crous, P.W., and Braun, U. 2003. Mycosphaerella and its anamorphs: 1. Names published in Cercospora and Passalora. Centraalbureau voor Schimmelcultures, Utrecht, 571 pages.
