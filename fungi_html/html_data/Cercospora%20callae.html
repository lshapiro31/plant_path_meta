Nomenclature data for Cercospora callae

Cercospora callae Peck & G.P. Clinton 1876 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

= Cercospora pachyspora Ellis & Everh. 1891

Distribution: Europe (Belarus), North America.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Several taxa of Araceae. Crous and Braun (2003) state that records from Alisma are actually from Peltandra virginica.

Supporting Literature:

Braun, U., Crous, P.W., and Nakashima, C. 2014. Cercosporoid fungi (Mycosphaerellaceae) 2. Species on monocots (Acoraceae to Xyridaceae, excluding Poaceae). IMA Fungus 5: 203-390.

Chupp, C. 1954. Monograph of the fungus genus Cercospora. Published by the Author, Ithaca, New York, 667 pages.

Crous, P.W., and Braun, U. 2003. Mycosphaerella and its anamorphs: 1. Names published in Cercospora and Passalora. Centraalbureau voor Schimmelcultures, Utrecht, 571 pages.
