Nomenclature data for Cercospora callicarpae

Pseudocercospora callicarpae (Cooke) Y.L. Guo & W.X. Zhao 1989 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Cercospora callicarpae Cooke 1878

= Cercospora callicarpicola N. Naito 1940  Note: Possible synonym (Crous and Braun 2003).

Distribution: Asia, North America (USA).

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Callicarpa spp. (Lamiaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Chupp, C. 1954. Monograph of the fungus genus Cercospora. Published by the Author, Ithaca, New York, 667 pages.
