Nomenclature data for Cercospora canescens

Cercospora canescens Ellis & G. Martin 1882 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡Cercosporiopsis canescens (Ellis & G. Martin) Miura 1928

= Cercospora latens f. europaea Gonz. Frag. 1921

    ≡ Cercospora europea (Gonz. Frag.) Constant. 1967

  Variant spelling Cercospora latens f. europea Gonz. Frag. 1921

= Cercospora psoraleae-bituminosae Savul. & Sandu 1940

    ≡ Pseudocercospora psoraleae-bituminosae (Savul. & Sandu) Sarbajna 1989  Note: Misapplied, actually refers to Pseudocercospora psoraleae (Crous and Braun 2003).

  Variant spelling Cercospora psoraleae-bituminosa Savul. & Sandu 1940

= Cercospora vignicaulis Tehon 1937

Notes: From Cercospora psoraleae-bituminosae - As 'psoraleae-bituminosa'.

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf and stem spot.

Host: Cyamopsis tetragonoloba, Psoralea spp. (Fabaceae).
