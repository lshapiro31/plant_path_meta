Nomenclature data for Cercospora cannabina

Pseudocercospora cannabina (Wakef.) Deighton 1976 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Cercospora cannabina Wakef. 1917

Distribution: Widespread based on scattered reports, especially Asia.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Cannabis sativa (Cannabaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Chupp, C. 1954. Monograph of the fungus genus Cercospora. Published by the Author, Ithaca, New York, 667 pages.
