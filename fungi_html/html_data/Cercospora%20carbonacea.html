Nomenclature data for Cercospora carbonacea

Pseudocercospora carbonacea (Miles) N. Pons & B. Sutton 1988 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Cercospora carbonacea Miles 1917

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Dioscorea spp. (Dioscoreaceae).
