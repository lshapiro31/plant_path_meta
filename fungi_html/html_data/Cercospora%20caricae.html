Nomenclature data for Cercospora caricae

Asperisporium caricae (Speg.) Maubl. 1913 (Ascomycetes, Incertae sedis)

≡ Cercospora caricae Speg. 1886

≡Fusicladium caricae (Speg.) Sacc. 1902

= Pucciniopsis caricae Earle 1902

[≡Pucciniopsis caricae (Speg.) Höhn. 1923 - illegitimate later homonym, not included in search]  Note: Not Earle 1902.

= Scolicotrichum caricae Ellis & Everh. 1892

  Variant spelling Scolecotrichum caricae Ellis & Everh. 1892

= Epiclinium cumminsii Massee 1898

Distribution: Widespread, especially subtropical and tropical regions.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Primarily Carica papaya and Carica chilensis (Caricaceae).
