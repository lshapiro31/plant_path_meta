Nomenclature data for Cercospora caricina

Cercospora caricis Oudem. 1892 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

= Cercospora caricina Ellis & Dearn. 1897

= Cercosporina caricis (Dearn. & House) Sacc. 1931

  [≡ Cercospora caricis Dearn. & House 1916 - illegitimate later homonym, not included in search]  Note: Not Oudem. 1892. Nomen illeg.

= Cercospora microstigma Sacc. 1912

Notes: Further study is required to resolve species on different hosts (Crous and Braun 2003).

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Cyperaceae.

Supporting Literature:
