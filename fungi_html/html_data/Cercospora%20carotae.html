Nomenclature data for Cercospora carotae

Cercospora carotae (Pass.) Kazn. & Siemaszko 1929 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Cercospora apii var. carotae Pass. 1889

Notes: (Pass.) Solheim 1929 is an isonym fide Crous and Braun (2003).

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Living leaves, stems.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Daucus spp. (Apiaceae).
