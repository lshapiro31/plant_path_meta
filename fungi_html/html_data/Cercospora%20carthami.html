Nomenclature data for Cercospora carthami

Cercospora carthami Sundar. & T.S. Ramakr. 1928 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

[= Cercospora carthami Mend. 1941 - illegitimate later homonym, not included in search]  Note: Not Sundar. & T.S. Ramakr. 1928. Nomen illeg.

= Cercospora carthamicola Vassiljevsky 1937

    ≡ Cercosporina carthami Syd. & P. Syd. 1913

Notes: Crous and Braun (2003) consider this to be Cercospora apii s. lat.

Distribution: Widespread.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Carthamus spp. (Asteraceae).

Supporting Literature:

Chupp, C. 1954. Monograph of the fungus genus Cercospora. Published by the Author, Ithaca, New York, 667 pages.

Crous, P.W., and Braun, U. 2003. Mycosphaerella and its anamorphs: 1. Names published in Cercospora and Passalora. Centraalbureau voor Schimmelcultures, Utrecht, 571 pages.
