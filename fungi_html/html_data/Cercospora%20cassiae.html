Nomenclature data for Cercospora cassiae

Passalora aenea (Cif.) U. Braun & Crous 2003 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Berteromyces aeneus Cif. 1954

= Cercospora cassiae Henn. 1893

    ≡ Cercosporidium cassiae (Henn.) Deighton 1967

    ≡ Phaeoisariopsis cassiae (Henn.) Arx 1983

  [≡ Passalora cassiae (Henn.) U. Braun 1996 - illegitimate later homonym, not included in search]  Note: Not Syd. 1939. Nomen illeg. An isonym of (Henn.) Poonam Srivast.1994 is invalid.

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Cassia spp., Senna spp. (Fabaceae).
