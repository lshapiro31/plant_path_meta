Nomenclature data for Cercospora cassiaecola

Passalora occidentalis (Cooke) U. Braun 2000 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Cercospora occidentalis Cooke 1878

≡Ramularia cassiicola Heald & F.A. Wolf 1912  Note: As 'cassiaecola'. A nomen nov.

≡Cercosporina occidentalis (Cooke) Sacc. 1931

≡Phaeoramularia occidentalis (Cooke) Deighton 1976

    Variant spelling Cercospora cassiaecola Roum. 1886

= Cercospora iponemensis Henn. 1909

    ≡ Cercosporina iponemensis (Henn.) Sacc. 1931

[= Cercospora occidentalis Ellis & Kellerm. 1912 - illegitimate later homonym, not included in search]  Note: Not Cooke 1878. Nomen illeg.

= Cercospora paulensis Henn. 1909

= Cercospora personata var. cassiae-occidentalis Berk. & M.A. Curtis 1875

= Cladosporium personatum var. cassiae Thüm. 1881

= Cercospora somalensis Curzi 1932

= Cercospora sphaeroidea Speg. 1880

    ≡ Phaeoisariopsis sphaeroidea (Speg.) L.G. Br. & Morgan-Jones 1976

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Several taxa of Fabaceae, especially Cassia spp. and Senna spp.

Supporting Literature:
