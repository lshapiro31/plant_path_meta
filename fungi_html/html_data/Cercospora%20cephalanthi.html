Nomenclature data for Cercospora cephalanthi

Passalora cephalanthi (Ellis & Kellerm.) U. Braun & Crous 2003 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Cercospora cephalanthi Ellis & Kellerm. 1884

≡Phaeoramularia cephalanthi (Ellis & Kellerm.) U. Braun 1998

≡Ramularia cephalanthi (Ellis & Kellerm.) Heald 1912

= Cercospora perniciosa Heald & F.A. Wolf 1911

Distribution: Asia (China, Taiwan), North America (USA).

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Cephalanthus occidentalis (Rubiaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Chupp, C. 1954. Monograph of the fungus genus Cercospora. Published by the Author, Ithaca, New York, 667 pages.

Crous, P.W., and Braun, U. 2003. Mycosphaerella and its anamorphs: 1. Names published in Cercospora and Passalora. Centraalbureau voor Schimmelcultures, Utrecht, 571 pages.
