Nomenclature data for Cercospora chionea

Passalora chionea (Ellis & Kellerm.) U. Braun 2003 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Cercospora chionea Ellis & Everh. 1884

≡Cercosporella chionea (Ellis & Everh.) Sacc. 1892

≡Pseudocercospora chionea (Ellis & Kellerm.) X.J. Liu & Y.L. Guo 1989  Note: Misapplied.

= Cercospora cercidis W.W. Ray 1941

Notes: Asian material on Cercis chinensis represents Pseudocercospora cercidis-chinensis (Crous and Braun 2003).

Distribution: North America (USA).

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Cercis canadensis (Fabaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Chupp, C. 1954. Monograph of the fungus genus Cercospora. Published by the Author, Ithaca, New York, 667 pages.

Crous, P.W., and Braun, U. 2003. Mycosphaerella and its anamorphs: 1. Names published in Cercospora and Passalora. Centraalbureau voor Schimmelcultures, Utrecht, 571 pages.
