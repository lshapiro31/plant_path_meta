Nomenclature data for Cercospora citri-grisea

Zasmidium citri-griseum (F.E. Fisher) U. Braun 2014 (Ascomycetes, Incertae sedis)

≡ Cercospora citri-grisea F.E. Fisher 1961

≡Stenella citri-grisea (F.E. Fisher) Sivan. 1984

= Mycosphaerella citri Whiteside 1972

    ≡ Zasmidium citri (Whiteside) Crous 2009

Notes: Per Braun et al. (2014), the connection to Mycosphaerella citri is unproven.

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Living leaves and fruit.

Disease Note: Greasy spot of Citrus.

Host: Several taxa of Rutaceae, especially Citrus spp.; also reported on Eucalyptus sp. (Myrtaceae), Acacia mangium (Fabaceae), Musa sp. (Musaceae).
