Nomenclature data for Cercospora citrullina

Cercospora citrullina Cooke 1883 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

= Cercospora chardoniana Chupp 1934

= Cercospora cucurbitae Ellis & Everh. 1888

= Cercospora luffae Hara 1928?

[= Cercospora momordicae Mend. 1941 - illegitimate later homonym, not included in search]  Note: Not McRae 1929. Nomen illeg.

= Cercospora momordicae McRae 1929

= Cercospora sechii J.A. Stev. 1919

= Cercospora trichosanthis McRae 1929

Notes: Crous and Braun (2003) consider this to be Cercospora apii s. lat.

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Numerous taxa of Cucurbitaceae.
