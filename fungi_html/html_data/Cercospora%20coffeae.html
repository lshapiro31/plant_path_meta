Nomenclature data for Cercospora coffeae

Cercospora coffeicola Berk. & Cooke 1881 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

= Cercospora coffeae Zimm. 1904

= Sphaerella coffeicola Cooke 1880

    ≡ Mycosphaerella coffeicola (Cooke) J.A. Stev. & Wellman 1944

= Cercospora herrerana Farneti 1904

Notes: From Mycosphaerella coffeicola - Isonym.

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Brown eye spot of coffee.

Host: Coffea spp. (Rubiaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Braun, U., and Urtiaga, R. 2012. New species and new records of cercosporoid hyphomycetes from Cuba and Venezuela (Part 1). Mycosphere 3: 301-329.
