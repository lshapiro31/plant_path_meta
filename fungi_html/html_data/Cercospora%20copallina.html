Nomenclature data for Cercospora copallina

Pseudocercospora rhoina (Cooke & Ellis) Deighton 1976 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Cercospora rhoina Cooke & Ellis 1878

= Cercospora copallina Cooke 1883

= Cercospora rhoina var. nigromaculans Peck 1889

Notes: Per Braun et al. (2016), P. rhoina represents a complex of races and possibly cryptic species.

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Rhus spp., Searsia natalensis, Toxicodendron spp. (Anacardiaceae); records from North America on Cotinus coggygria are based on confusion with P. cotini (Braun et al. 2016).

Supporting Literature:
