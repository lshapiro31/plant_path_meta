Nomenclature data for Cercospora cydoniae

Pseudocercospora cydoniae (Ellis & Everh.) Y.L. Guo & X.J. Liu 1992 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Cercospora cydoniae Ellis & Everh. 1902

≡Cercosporina cydoniae (Ellis & Everh.) Sacc. 1931

Notes: (Ellis & Everh.) U. Braun & H.D. Shin 1993 is a homonym.

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Chaenomeles spp., Cydonia spp. (Rosaceae).
