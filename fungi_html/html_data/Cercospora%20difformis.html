Nomenclature data for Cercospora difformis

Cercospora violae Sacc. 1876 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

= Cercospora difformis Tehon 1948

= Cercospora violae var. minor Rota-Rossi 1914

= Cercospora violae-kiusianae Sawada 1943

     Cercospora kiusana Sawada  Note: Cited incorrectly by Chupp (1953).

= Cercospora violae-tricoloris Briosi & Cavara 1892

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Viola spp. (Violaceae).
