Nomenclature data for Cercospora diospyri

Zasmidium diospyri (Thüm.) U. Braun 2010 (Ascomycetes, Incertae sedis)

≡ Cercospora diospyri Thüm. 1874

≡Sirosporium diospyri (Thüm.) Deighton 1976

≡Stenella diospyri (Thüm.) U. Braun 1995

[≡Cercospora diospyri Cooke 1883 - illegitimate later homonym, not included in search]

= Helminthosporium diospyri Thüm. 1879

Notes: From Cercospora diospyri - As (Thüm.) Cooke. Is a nomen illeg. From Stenella diospyri - Crous and Braun (2003) discuss the confusing nomenclatre of this fungus.

Distribution: North America, South America (Brazil).

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Diospyros spp.(Ebenaceae).

Supporting Literature:
