Nomenclature data for Cercospora duddiae

Cercospora duddiae Welles 1923 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leafspot or withertip on onion and garlic.

Host: Allium spp. (Amaryllidaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Braun, U., Crous, P.W., and Nakashima, C. 2014. Cercosporoid fungi (Mycosphaerellaceae) 2. Species on monocots (Acoraceae to Xyridaceae, excluding Poaceae). IMA Fungus 5: 203-390.
