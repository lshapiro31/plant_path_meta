Nomenclature data for Cercospora elaeidis

Uwemyces elaeidis (Steyaert) Hern.-Restr., G.A. Sarria & Crous 2016 (, )

≡ Cercospora elaeidis Steyaert 1948

      Variant spelling Cercospora elaedis Steyaert 1948

≡Pseudospiropes elaeidis (Steyaert) Deighton 1985

Distribution: Africa, South America (Colombia).

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Elaeis guineensis, E. oleifera (Arecaceae).
