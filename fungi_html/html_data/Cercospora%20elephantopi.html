Nomenclature data for Cercospora elephantopi

Pseudocercospora elephantopodis (Ellis & Everh.) R.F. Castañeda & U. Braun 1989 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Cercospora elephantopodis Ellis & Everh. 1887

      Variant spelling Cercospora elephantopi Ellis & Everh. 1887

[= Pseudocercospora elephantopodis W.H. Hsieh & Goh 1990 - illegitimate later homonym, not included in search]  Note: Not (Ellis & Everh.) R.F. Castañeda & U. Braun 1989. Nomen illeg.

  Variant spelling Pseudocercospora elephantopi W.H. Hsieh & Goh 1990

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Elephantopus spp., Pseudelephantopus spp. (Asteraceae).

Supporting Literature:

Chupp, C. 1954. Monograph of the fungus genus Cercospora. Published by the Author, Ithaca, New York, 667 pages.

Crous, P.W., and Braun, U. 2003. Mycosphaerella and its anamorphs: 1. Names published in Cercospora and Passalora. Centraalbureau voor Schimmelcultures, Utrecht, 571 pages.
