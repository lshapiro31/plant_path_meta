Nomenclature data for Cercospora fagopyri

Cercospora fagopyri N. Nakata & S. Takim. 1928 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

[= Cercospora fagopyri Abramov 1937 - illegitimate later homonym, not included in search]  Note: Not N. Nakata & S. Takim. 1928. Nomen illeg.

  Cercospora fagopyri Abramov 1932  Note: Invalid name. Nom. nud.

[= Cercospora fagopyri Chupp & A.S. Mull. 1942 - illegitimate later homonym, not included in search]  Note: Not N. Nakata & S. Takim. 1928. Nomen illeg.

Notes: Crous and Braun (2003) consider this to be Cercospora apii s. lat.

Distribution: Widespread, especially northern hemisphere.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Fagopyrum spp. (Polygonaceae).

Crous, P.W., and Braun, U. 2003. Mycosphaerella and its anamorphs: 1. Names published in Cercospora and Passalora. Centraalbureau voor Schimmelcultures, Utrecht, 571 pages.
