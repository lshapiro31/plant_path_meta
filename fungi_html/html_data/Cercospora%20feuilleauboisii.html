Nomenclature data for Cercospora feuilleauboisii

Cercospora solani Thüm. 1880 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

= Cercospora feuilleauboisii Sacc. 1886

  [≡ Cercospora solani Feuilleaub. 1884 - illegitimate later homonym, not included in search]  Note: Not Thüm. 1880. Nomen illeg.

     Cercospora solani subsp. feuilleauboisii Sacc. 1886  Note: Invalid name. Saccardo did not publish an infraspecific name.

= Cercospora nigrescens G. Winter 1885

Notes: According to Chupp (1953), many identifications of this fungus from the USA were incorrect.

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Solanum spp. (Solanaceae).
