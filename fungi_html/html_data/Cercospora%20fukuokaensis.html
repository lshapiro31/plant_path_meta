Nomenclature data for Cercospora fukuokaensis

Pseudocercospora fukuokaensis (Chupp) X.J. Liu & Y.L. Guo 1992 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Cercospora fukuokaensis Chupp 1952

Notes: DNA sequence data for different isolates from Styrax japonica collected in Japan are identical, and distinct from the strain collected in Korea, suggesting the Korean material represents a different taxon.

Distribution: Asia.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Styrax spp. (Styracaceae).
