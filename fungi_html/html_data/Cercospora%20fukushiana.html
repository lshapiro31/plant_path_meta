Nomenclature data for Cercospora fukushiana

Cercospora fukushiana (Matsuura) W. Yamam. 1934 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Cercosporina fukushiana Matsuura 1927

= Cercospora balsaminae Mend. 1941

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Impatiens spp. (Balsaminaceae).
