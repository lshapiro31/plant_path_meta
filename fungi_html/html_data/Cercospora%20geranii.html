Nomenclature data for Cercospora geranii

Pseudocercospora geraniicola U. Braun 1993 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡Cylindrosporium geranii Ellis & Everh. 1888

= Cercospora geranii Kellerm. & Swingle 1889

Notes: New name was needed due to occupation of epithet in Pseudocercospora.

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Geranium spp. (Geraniaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Chupp, C. 1954. Monograph of the fungus genus Cercospora. Published by the Author, Ithaca, New York, 667 pages.

Crous, P.W., and Braun, U. 2003. Mycosphaerella and its anamorphs: 1. Names published in Cercospora and Passalora. Centraalbureau voor Schimmelcultures, Utrecht, 571 pages.
