Nomenclature data for Cercospora gossypina

Cercospora gossypina Cooke 1883 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

= Sphaerella gossypina G.F. Atk. 1891  Note: From Mycosphaerella gossypina - Isonyms of (G.F. Atk.) Siemaszko 1915, (G.F. Atk.) Jacz. 1917, (G.F. Atk.) Woron. 1923, and (G.F. Atk.) Cif. & Mont. exist.

    ≡ Mycosphaerella gossypina (G.F. Atk.) Earle 1900

Distribution: Worldwide in cotton-growing regions.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot of cotton.

Host: Gossypium spp. (Malvaceae).
