Nomenclature data for Cercospora hibisci-manihotis

Pseudocercospora abelmoschi (Ellis & Everh.) Deighton 1976 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Cercospora abelmoschi Ellis & Everh. 1893

= Phaeoisariopsis hibicina S.K. Majumdar, S. Singh & Anuradha 2004

= Cercospora hibisci Tracy & Earle 1895

= Cercospora hibisci-manihotis Henn. 1904

Distribution: Africa, Asia, Australia, Central America, North America (USA), South America.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Hibiscus spp. (Malvaceae) (Crous & Braun 2003). Also reported on Abelmoschus esculentus (Malvaceae) and Dioscorea alata (Dioscoreaceae).

Supporting Literature:
