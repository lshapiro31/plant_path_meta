Nomenclature data for Cercospora jussiaeae

Pseudocercospora jussiaeae (G.F. Atk.) Deighton 1976 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Cercospora jussiaeae G.F. Atk. 1892

= Cercospora ludwigiae G.F. Atk. 1892

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Ludwigia spp. (Onagraceae).
