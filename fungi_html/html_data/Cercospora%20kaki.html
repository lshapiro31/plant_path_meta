Nomenclature data for Cercospora kaki

Pseudocercospora kaki Goh & W.H. Hsieh 1990 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

= Cercospora kaki Ellis & Everh. 1887

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Diospyros spp. (Ebenaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Crous, P.W., and Braun, U. 2003. Mycosphaerella and its anamorphs: 1. Names published in Cercospora and Passalora. Centraalbureau voor Schimmelcultures, Utrecht, 571 pages.
