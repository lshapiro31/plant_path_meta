Cercospora althaeina Sacc. 1878 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

= Cercospora althaeicola J.M. Yen & S.K. Sun 1983

= Cercospora althaeina var. althaeae-officinalis Savul. & Sandu 1933

= Cercospora althaeina var. praecincta Davis 1915

    ≡ Cercospora praecincta (Davis) Chupp 1954

= Cercospora kellermanii Bubák 1903

  Variant spelling Cercospora kellermani Bubák 1903

= Cercospora ramularia Siemaszko 1923

    ≡ Cercosporina ramularia (Siemaszko) Sacc. 1931

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Numerous taxa of Malvaceae.
