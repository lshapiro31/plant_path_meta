Nomenclature data for Cercospora kikuchii

Cercospora kikuchii (T. Matsumoto & Tomoy.) M.W. Gardner 1927 [1926] (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Cercosporina kikuchii T. Matsumoto & Tomoy. 1925

= Mycosphaerella phaseoli Chona & Munjal 1956

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Leaves, stems, and pods.

Disease Note: Leaf spot, seed stain, etc.

Host: Fabaceae.
