Nomenclature data for Cercospora koepkei

Passalora koepkei (W. Krüger) U. Braun & Crous 2003 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Cercospora koepkei W. Krüger 1890  Note: From Cercospora koepkii - Fide IndexFungorum.

      Variant spelling Cercospora koepkii W. Krüger 1890

≡Mycovellosiella koepkei (W. Krüger) Deighton 1979

= Cercospora koepkei var. sorghi K. Goto, K. Hirano & Fukatsu 1962

= Pseudocercospora miscanthi Katsuki 1956

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Miscanthus spp., Saccharum spp. (Poaceae).
