Nomenclature data for Cercospora lagerstroemiae-subcostatae

Pseudocercospora lythracearum (Heald & F.A. Wolf) X.J. Liu & Y.L. Guo 1992 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Cercospora lythracearum Heald & F.A. Wolf 1911

= Cercospora lagerstroemiae-subcostatae Sawada 1931

    ≡ Pseudocercospora lagerstroemiae-subcostatae (Sawada) Goh & W.H. Hsieh 1990

Distribution: Africa, Asia, North America, South America.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Lagerstroemia spp., Punica granatum (Lythraceae).
