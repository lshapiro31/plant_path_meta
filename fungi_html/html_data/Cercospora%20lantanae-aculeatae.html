Nomenclature data for Cercospora lantanae-aculeatae

Pseudocercospora formosana (W. Yamam.) Deighton 1976 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Cercospora formosana W. Yamam. 1934

= Mycovellosiella lantanae var. verbenacearum Bhalla, S.K. Singh & A.K. Srivast. 1999

= Cercospora lantanae-aculeatae J.M. Yen 1966

    ≡ Pseudocercospora lantanae-aculeatae (J.M. Yen) J.M. Yen 1980

= Cercospora lantanae-camarae J.M. Yen & Gilles 1973

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Lantana spp. (Verbenaceae).
