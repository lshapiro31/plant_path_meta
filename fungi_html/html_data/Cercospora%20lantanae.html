Nomenclature data for Cercospora lantanae

Passalora lantanae (Chupp) U. Braun & Crous 2003 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Cercospora lantanae Chupp 1931

≡Mycovellosiella lantanae (Chupp) Deighton 1974

≡Mycovellosiella lantanae var. lantanae (Chupp) Deighton 1974

≡Passalora lantanae var. lantanae (Chupp) U. Braun & Crous 2003

= Cladosporium trichophilum Petr. & Cif. 1932

    ≡ Chaetotrichum lantanae Petr. 1951

Notes: Includes type variety.

Distribution: Widespread, but especially subtropical to tropical North and South America.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Lantana spp. (Verbenaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Chupp, C. 1954. Monograph of the fungus genus Cercospora. Published by the Author, Ithaca, New York, 667 pages.

Crous, P.W., and Braun, U. 2003. Mycosphaerella and its anamorphs: 1. Names published in Cercospora and Passalora. Centraalbureau voor Schimmelcultures, Utrecht, 571 pages.
