Nomenclature data for Cercospora latens

Pseudocercospora latens (Ellis & Everh.) Y.L. Guo & X.J. Liu 1989 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Cercospora latens Ellis & Everh. 1888

Notes: Isonym of (Ellis & Everh.) U. Braun 1997.

Distribution: Widespread in northern hemisphere.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Several taxa in Fabaceae.
