Nomenclature data for Cercospora latimaculans

Pseudocercospora bauhiniae (Syd. & P. Syd.) Deighton 1976 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Cercospora bauhiniae Syd. & P. Syd. 1914

= Cercospora latimaculans Wakef. 1918

= Mycosphaerella piliostigmatis Tak. Kobay. & F.D. Guzman 1988

Distribution: Widespread, especially warmer regions.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Fabaceae, especially Bauhinia spp..

Supporting Literature:

Chupp, C. 1954. Monograph of the fungus genus Cercospora. Published by the Author, Ithaca, New York, 667 pages.
