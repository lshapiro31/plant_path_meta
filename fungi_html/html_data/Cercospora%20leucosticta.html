Nomenclature data for Cercospora leucosticta

Cercospora meliae Ellis & Everh. 1887 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

= Cercospora leucosticta Ellis & Everh. 1888

Notes: Crous and Braun (2003) consider this to be Cercospora apii s. lat.

Distribution: North America (USA: Gulf States) and reports from numerous other areas, especially Asia. Crous and Braun (2003) state that many of those reports represent Pseudocercospora subsessilis.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Melia azedarach (Meliaceae).
