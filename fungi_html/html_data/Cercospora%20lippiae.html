Nomenclature data for Cercospora lippiae

Cercospora lippiae Ellis & Everh. 1887 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡Pseudocercospora lippiae (Ellis & Everh.) A.K. Das & Chattopadh. 1990

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Lippia spp., Phyla spp. (Verbenaceae).
