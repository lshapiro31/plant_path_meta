Nomenclature data for Cercospora longipes

Cercospora longipes E.J. Butler 1906 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

Notes: Not the same as Cercospora koepkii. (Deighton 1979).

Distribution: Worldwide in sugar cane-growing regions.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Brown spot of sugarcane.

Host: Saccharum spp. (Poaceae).

Supporting Literature:
