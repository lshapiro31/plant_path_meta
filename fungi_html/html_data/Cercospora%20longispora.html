Nomenclature data for Cercospora longispora

Cercospora longispora Peck 1884 [1882] (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡Cercoseptoria longispora (Peck) Petr. 1925

≡Septoriopsis longispora (Peck) Davis 1922

= Cylindrosporium longisporum Ellis & Dearn. 1893

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Lupinus spp., Vigna spp. (Fabaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Chupp, C. 1954. Monograph of the fungus genus Cercospora. Published by the Author, Ithaca, New York, 667 pages.

Crous, P.W., and Braun, U. 2003. Mycosphaerella and its anamorphs: 1. Names published in Cercospora and Passalora. Centraalbureau voor Schimmelcultures, Utrecht, 571 pages.

Cercospora lactucae-sativae Sawada 1943 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

[≡Cercospora lactucae J.A. Stev. 1917 - illegitimate later homonym, not included in search]  Note: Not Henn. 1902. Nomen illeg.

[= Cercospora longissima Cugini ex Trav. 1903 - illegitimate later homonym, not included in search]  Note: Not Cooke & Ellis 1889. Nomen illeg.

  ≡Cercospora longispora Cugini ex Trav. 1903  Note: Not Peck 1884. Nomen illeg.

Notes: Not published in 1928. Used as a replacement name? for Cercospora lactucae J.A. Stev. which was cited by Sawada as a synonym. Otherwise invalid (Japanese diagnosis only).

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Asteraceae, especially Lactuca spp.

Supporting Literature:

Groenewald, J.Z., Nakashima, C., Nishikawa, J., Shin, H.-D., Park, J.-H., Jama, A.N., Groenewald, M., Braun, U., and Crous, P.W. 2013. Species concepts in Cercospora: spotting the weeds among the roses. Stud. Mycol. 75: 115-170.
