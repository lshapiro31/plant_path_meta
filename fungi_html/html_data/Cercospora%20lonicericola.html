Nomenclature data for Cercospora lonicericola

Pseudocercospora lonicericola (W. Yamam.) Deighton 1976 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Cercospora lonicericola W. Yamam. 1934

Distribution: Asia, New Zealand.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Lonicera spp. (Caprifoliaceae).

Supporting Literature:
