Nomenclature data for Cercospora mikaniae-cordatae

Cercospora mikaniicola F. Stevens 1917 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

    Variant spelling Cercospora mikaniacola F. Stevens 1917

    Variant spelling Cercospora mikaniaecola F. Stevens 1917

= Cercospora mikaniae-cordatae J.M. Yen 1965

Notes: As 'mikaniacola'.

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Mikania spp. (Asteraceae).
