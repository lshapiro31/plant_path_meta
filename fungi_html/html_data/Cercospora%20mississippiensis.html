Nomenclature data for Cercospora mississippiensis

Exosporium petersii (Berk. & M.A. Curtis) U. Braun 2014 (Ascomycetes, Incertae sedis)

≡ Helminthosporium petersii Berk. & M.A. Curtis 1875

≡Cercospora petersii (Berk. & M.A. Curtis) Atk. 1892

= Cercospora mississippiensis Tracy & Earle 1895

    ≡ Pseudocercospora mississippiensis (Tracy & Earle) R.F. Castañeda & U. Braun 1989

Notes: From Cercospora confusa - Unpublished and invalid name.

Distribution: North America.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Smilax spp. (Smilacaceae).
