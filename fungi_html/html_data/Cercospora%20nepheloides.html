Nomenclature data for Cercospora nepheloides

Pseudocercospora nepheloides (Ellis & Holw. ex Heald & F.A. Wolf) U. Braun, D.F. Farr & Minnis 2009 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Cercospora nepheloides Ellis & Holw. ex Heald & F.A. Wolf 1912  Note: Name but not validating description attributed to Ellis & Holw. Effective publication in Jan.

= Cercospora eustomatis Peck 1912  Note: Effective publication in Mar.

  Variant spelling Cercospora eustomae Peck 1912

    ≡ Pseudocercospora eustomatis (Peck) U. Braun 1999

    ≡ Cercosporina eustomatis (Peck) Sacc. 1931  Note: As 'eustomae'.

  Variant spelling Cercosporina eustomae (Peck) Sacc. 1931

Distribution: North America, South America.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Eustoma spp. (Gentianaceae).
