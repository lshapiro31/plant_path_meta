Nomenclature data for Cercospora nesaeae

Pseudocercospora nesaeae (Ellis & Everh.) U. Braun 1999 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Cercospora nesaeae Ellis & Everh. 1893  Note: As 'neseae'.

      Variant spelling Cercospora neseae Ellis & Everh. 1893

= Cercospora decodontis Tehon & E.Y. Daniels 1925

Distribution: North America (USA).

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Decodon verticillatus (Lythraceae).
