Nomenclature data for Cercospora olacicola

Pseudocercospora olacicola (Muthappa) Kamal, M.K. Khan & R.K. Verma 1990 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Cercospora olacicola Muthappa 1968 [1967]

Distribution: Asia (India, Thailand).

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Olax spp., Ximenia sp. (Olacaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Crous, P.W., and Braun, U. 2003. Mycosphaerella and its anamorphs: 1. Names published in Cercospora and Passalora. Centraalbureau voor Schimmelcultures, Utrecht, 571 pages.
