Nomenclature data for Cercospora opuli

Pseudocercospora opuli (Höhn.) U. Braun & Crous 2003 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Cercospora penicillata f. opuli Fuckel 1863

≡Cercospora opuli (Fuckel) Höhn. 1907

≡Pseudophaeoramularia opuli (Höhn.) U. Braun 1997

= Cercospora penicillata var. opuli Fuckel 1863

= Pseudocercospora viburni U. Braun 1992

= Passalora viburni-sargentii Y.L. Guo 2012

Distribution: Widespread in northern hemisphere.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Viburnum spp. (Adoxaceae).

Supporting Literature:
