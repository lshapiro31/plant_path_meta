Nomenclature data for Cercospora pachyderma

Distocercospora pachyderma (Syd. & P. Syd.) N. Pons & B. Sutton 1988 (Ascomycetes, Incertae sedis)

≡Cercospora pachyderma Syd. & P. Syd. 1914

≡Cercosporina pachyderma (Syd. & P. Syd.) Sacc. 1931

= Cercospora dioscoreae-bulbiferae J.M. Yen & Gilles 1973 [1971]

Distribution: Widespread

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Dioscorea spp. (Dioscoreaceae).
