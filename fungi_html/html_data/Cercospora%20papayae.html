Nomenclature data for Cercospora papayae

Cercospora papayae Hansf. 1943 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

[= Cercospora papayae Viégas & Chupp 1945 - illegitimate later homonym, not included in search]  Note: Not Hansf. 1943.

    ≡ Cercospora mamaonis Viégas & Chupp 1954

    ≡ Pseudocercospora mamaonis (Viégas & Chupp) Tak. Kobay. & Tokash. 1995  Note: Misapplied, probably an undescribed Pseudocercospora fide Crous and Braun (2003).

Notes: Crous and Braun (2003) consider this to be Cercospora apii s. lat.

Distribution: Widespread in tropical regions.

Substrate: Fruits, leaves.

Disease Note: Black spot of papaya, leaf spot.

Host: Carica spp. (Caricaceae).
