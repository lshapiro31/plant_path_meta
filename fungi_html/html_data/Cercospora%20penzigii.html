Nomenclature data for Cercospora penzigii

Cercospora penzigii Sacc. 1886 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

= Cercospora aurantia Heald & F.A. Wolf 1911

= Cercospora daidai Hara 1954

[= Cercospora fumosa Penz. 1882 - illegitimate later homonym, not included in search]  Note: Not Sacc. 1880. Nomen illeg.

Notes: According to Crous and Braun (2003), this is Cercospora apii s. lat.

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Several genera of Rutaceae.

Supporting Literature:

Chupp, C. 1954. Monograph of the fungus genus Cercospora. Published by the Author, Ithaca, New York, 667 pages.

Crous, P.W., and Braun, U. 2003. Mycosphaerella and its anamorphs: 1. Names published in Cercospora and Passalora. Centraalbureau voor Schimmelcultures, Utrecht, 571 pages.
