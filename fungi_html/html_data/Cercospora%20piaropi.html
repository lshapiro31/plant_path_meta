Nomenclature data for Cercospora piaropi

Cercospora piaropi Tharp 1917 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡Cercosporina piaropi (Tharp) Sacc. 1931

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Eichhornia crassipes (Pontederiaceae).
