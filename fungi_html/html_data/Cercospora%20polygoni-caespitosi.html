Nomenclature data for Cercospora polygoni-caespitosi

Cercospora polygonacea Ellis & Everh. 1885 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

    Variant spelling Cercospora polygonaceae Ellis & Everh. 1885

= Cercospora avicularis var. sagittati Atk. 1892

Notes: Chupp (1953) incorrectly lists only Ellis as the authority.

Distribution: Widespread in northern hemisphere, especially Asia, North America.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Persicaria spp., Polygonum spp., Fallopia convolvulus (Polygonaceae).
