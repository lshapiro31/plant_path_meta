Nomenclature data for Cercospora sagittariae

Cercospora sagittariae Ellis & Kellerm. 1886 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

= Cercosporella macrospora Bres. 1896

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Sagittaria spp. (Alismataceae).
