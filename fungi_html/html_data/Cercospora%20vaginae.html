Nomenclature data for Cercospora vaginae

Passalora vaginae (W. Krüger) U. Braun & Crous 2003 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Cercospora vaginae W. Krüger 1896

≡Mycovellosiella vaginae (W. Krüger) Deighton 1979

Distribution: Widespread, especially tropical and subtropical regions.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Saccharum spp., Sorghum vulgare (Poaceae).
