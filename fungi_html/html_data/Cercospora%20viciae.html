Nomenclature data for Cercospora viciae

Cercospora zonata G. Winter 1883 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

= Cercospora fabae Fautrey 1891

    ≡ Cercosporina fabae (Fautrey) T. Takah. & Hashio Suzuki 1929

= Cercospora viciae Ellis & Holw. 1885

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Vicia spp. and a few other questionable records from other taxa in Fabaceae.

Supporting Literature:
