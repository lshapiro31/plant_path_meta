Nomenclature data for Cercospora vignae-vexillatae

Passalora vanderystii (Henn.) U. Braun & Crous 2003 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Cercospora vanderystii Henn. 1907

      Variant spelling Cercospora vanderysti Henn. 1907

= Cercospora vignae-vexillatae R.E.D. Baker & W.T. Dale 1951

Distribution: Widespread in warmer regions.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Phaseolus vulgaris, Vignus spp. (Fabaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Chupp, C. 1954. Monograph of the fungus genus Cercospora. Published by the Author, Ithaca, New York, 667 pages.

Crous, P.W., and Braun, U. 2003. Mycosphaerella and its anamorphs: 1. Names published in Cercospora and Passalora. Centraalbureau voor Schimmelcultures, Utrecht, 571 pages.
