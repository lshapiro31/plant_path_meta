Nomenclature data for Cercospora vincetoxici

Passalora bellynckii (Westend.) U. Braun 1995 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Cladosporium bellynckii Westend. 1854

≡Cercosporidium bellynckii (Westend.) X.J. Liu & Y.L. Guo 1982

≡Mycovellosiella bellynckii (Westend.) Constant. 1982

= Cercospora vincetoxici Sacc. 1901

Distribution: Widespread.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Several taxa of Asclepiadaceae.

Supporting Literature:

Passalora vincetoxici-hirsuti U. Braun & Crous 2003 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Cercospora vincetoxici Ellis & Everh. 1902  Note: Not Sacc. 1901. Nomen illeg.

Distribution: North America (USA: AL, TX).

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Vincetoxicum hirsutum (Asclepiadaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Crous, P.W., and Braun, U. 2003. Mycosphaerella and its anamorphs: 1. Names published in Cercospora and Passalora. Centraalbureau voor Schimmelcultures, Utrecht, 571 pages.
