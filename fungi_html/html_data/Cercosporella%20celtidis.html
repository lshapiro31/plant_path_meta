Nomenclature data for Cercosporella celtidis

Pseudocercosporella celtidis (Ellis & Kellerm.) U. Braun 1992 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Ramularia celtidis Ellis & Kellerm. 1885

≡Cercosporella celtidis (Ellis & Kellerm.) Davis 1924

Distribution: Central and eastern United States.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Celtis occidentalis, C. laevigata (Cannabaceae).

Supporting Literature:
