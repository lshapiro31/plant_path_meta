Nomenclature data for Cercosporidium campi-silii

Cercospora campi-silii Speg. 1880 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡Cercosporidium campi-silii (Speg.) X.J. Liu & Y.L. Guo 1982

≡Passalora campi-silii (Speg.) U. Braun 1995

= Cercospora impatientis Bäumler 1888

Notes: From Passalora campi-silii - This combination of (Speg.) Poonam. Srivast. 1994 is invalid.

Distribution: Widespread in northern hemisphere.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Balsaminaceae, especially Impatiens spp.
