Nomenclature data for Cercosporidium sequoiae

Passalora sequoiae (Ellis & Everh.) Y.L. Guo & W.H. Hsieh 2003 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Cercospora sequoiae Ellis & Everh. 1887

      Variant spelling Cercospora sequojae Ellis & Everh. 1887

≡Asperisporium sequoiae (Ellis & Everh.) B. Sutton & Hodges 1990

≡Cercosporidium sequoiae (Ellis & Everh.) W.A. Baker & Partr. 2000

= Cercospora cryptomeriae Shirai 1916

= Cercospora thujina Plakidas 1945

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Needles.

Disease Note: Blight.

Host: Several taxa of Cupressaceae.
