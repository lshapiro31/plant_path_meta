Nomenclature data for Cercosporina zebrina

Cercospora zebrina Pass. 1877 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡Cercospora helvola var. zebrina (Pass.) Ferraris 1910

≡Cercosporina zebrina (Pass.) Matsuura 1930

= Cercospora helvola Sacc. 1882

= Cercospora stolziana Magnus 1905

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Several taxa of Fabaceae.
