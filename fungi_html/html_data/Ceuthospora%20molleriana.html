Nomenclature data for Ceuthospora molleriana

Phacidium mollerianum (Thüm.) Crous & U. Braun 2014 (Ascomycetes, Helotiales)

≡ Sphaeropsis molleriana Thüm. 1879

≡Ceuthospora molleriana (Thüm.) Petr. 1925

≡Macrophoma molleriana (Thüm.) Berl. & Voglino 1886

≡Phoma molleriana (Thüm.) Sacc. 1884

Distribution: Europe (Italy, Netherlands), South America (Argentina), USA (CA, FL).

Substrate: Living leaves and twigs.

Disease Note: Leaf spot, twig dieback.

Host: Eucalyptus (Myrtaceae), Polygonatum odoratum (Asparagaceae).

Supporting Literature:
