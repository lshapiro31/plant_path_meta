Nomenclature data for Chaetomium coccodes

Colletotrichum coccodes (Wallr.) S. Hughes 1958 (Ascomycetes, Phyllachorales)

≡ Chaetomium coccodes Wallr. 1833

= Vermicularia atramentaria Berk. & Broome 1850

    ≡ Colletotrichum atramentarium (Berk. & Broome) Taubenh. 1916

= Colletotrichum biologicum Chaudhuri 1924

= Gloeosporium phomoides Sacc. 1884

    ≡ Colletotrichum phomoides (Sacc.) Chesters & A. Bell 1894

Notes: Numerous other synonyms exist. Liu et al. (2013) segregated C. coccodes into two species both occurring on genera in the Solanaceae and other hosts.

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Various plant parts including fruits.

Disease Note: Anthracnose of fruits, black dot of tomato and potato, brown rot of stems of Solanaceae. Leaf lesions and deformity.

Host: Numerous hosts in numerous families.
