Nomenclature data for Chaetomium cochlioides

Chaetomium globosum Kunze : Fr. 1817 (Ascomycetes, Sordariales)

= Chaetomium cochliodes Palliser 1910

    Variant spelling Chaetomium cochlioides Palliser

= Chaetomium olivaceum Cooke & Ellis 1878

Notes: See von Arx et al. (1986) for a long list of additional taxonomic synonyms.

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Leaves, seeds.

Disease Note: Leaf spot, leaf drop.

Host: Multiple genera in multiple families.
