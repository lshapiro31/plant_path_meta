Nomenclature data for Chaetothyrina musarum

Chaetothyrina musarum (Speg.) Theiss. 1913 (Ascomycetes, Incertae sedis)

≡ Chaetothyrium musarum Speg. 1898

= Plochmopeltidella smilacina Mend. 1925

Notes: Sooty blotch of banana. Muller & Arx (Beitr. Kryptogamenfl. Schweiz 11:548. 1962).

Distribution: HI; tropical America; Africa; south Asia.

Substrate: Fruit surface.

Disease Note: Sooty blotch.

Host: Musa (Musaceae), Smilax spp. (Smilacaceae).
