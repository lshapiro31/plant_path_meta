Nomenclature data for Choanephora persicaria

Gilbertella persicaria (E.D. Eddy) Hesselt. 1960 (Zygomycetes, Mucorales)

≡ Choanephora persicaria E.D. Eddy 1925

Distribution: Asia (China, India), North and South America.

Substrate: Fruit.

Disease Note: Gilbertella rot - fruit rot; spots coalescing to cover fruit.

Host: Prunus spp. (Rosaceae), Hyalocereus costaricensis, (Cactaceae) Syzygium cumini (Myrtaceae), Carica papaya (Caricaceae), Solanum melongena (Solanaceae).
