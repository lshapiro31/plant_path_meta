Nomenclature data for Chrysomyxa chiogenis

Chrysomyxa chiogenis Dietel 1894 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

≡Melampsoropsis chiogenis (Dietel) Arthur 1906

Distribution: North America.

Disease Note: Rust.

Host: Spermogonia and aecia on Picea (Pinaceae), uredinia and telia on Gaultheria (Ericaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Crane, P. E. 2001. Morphology, taxonomy, and nomenclature of the Chrysomyxa ledi complex and related rust fungi on spruce and Ericaceae in North America and Europe. Canad. J. Bot. 79: 957-982.
