Nomenclature data for Chrysomyxa empetri

Chrysomyxa empetri J. Schröt. ex Cummins 1956 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

= Uredo empetri Pers. ex DC. 1815

    ≡ Erysibe empetri (Pers. ex DC.) Wallr. 1833

    ≡ Caeoma empetri (Pers. ex DC.) Link 1825  Note: Isonym of (Pers. ex DC.) G. Winter 1884.

    ≡ Thekopsora empetri (Pers. ex DC.) P. Karst. 1879

  Variant spelling Thecopsora empetri (Pers. ex DC.) P. Karst. 1879

    ≡ Melampsoropsis empetri (Pers. ex DC.) Arthur 1906

  Variant spelling Thecopsora empetri (Pers. ex DC.) P. Karst. 1879

  ≡Chrysomyxa empetri (Pers. ex DC.) J. Schröt. 1887  Note: Isonym of (Pers. ex DC.) Rostr. 1888.

  Variant spelling Thecopsora empetri (Pers. ex DC.) P. Karst. 1879

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: On living leaves and woody plant parts.

Disease Note: Rust.

Host: Spermogonia and aecia on Picea (Pinaceae), uredinia and telia on Empetrum (Empetraceae).
