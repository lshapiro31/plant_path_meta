Nomenclature data for Chrysomyxa ledi

Chrysomyxa ledi de Bary 1879 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

≡Melampsoropsis ledi (de Bary) Arthur 1906  Note: As '(Alb. & Schwein.) Arthur'.

≡Chrysomyxa ledi var. ledi de Bary 1950

= Uredo ledi Alb. & Schwein. 1805

    ≡ Erysibe ledi (Alb. & Schwein.) Wallr. 1833

    ≡ Coleosporium ledi (Alb. & Schwein.) J. Schröt. 1879

    ≡ Caeoma ledi (Alb. & Schwein.) Schltdl. 1824  Note: Isonym of (Alb. & Schwein.) Link 1825.

    ≡ Uredo ovoideo-aurantiaca Bonord. 1860  Note: Nom. illeg. via superfluous.

    ≡ Pucciniastrum ledi (Alb. & Schwein.) P. Karst. 1879

Notes: Several synonyms relating to Aecidium abietinum and Melampsoropsis abietina are best ignored since they represent more than one fungus as the original material was mixed (Crane 2001).

Distribution: Eurasia, not in North America.

Substrate: Cones, needles.

Disease Note: Rust.

Host: Spermogonia and aecia on Picea (Pinaceae), uredinia and telia on Ledum (Ericaceae).
