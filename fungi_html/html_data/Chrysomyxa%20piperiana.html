Nomenclature data for Chrysomyxa piperiana

Chrysomyxa piperiana Sacc. & Trotter ex Cummins 1956 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

= Peridermium parksianum Faull 1934

= Melampsoropsis piperiana Arthur 1907

    ≡ Caeoma piperianum (Arthur) Sacc. & Trotter 1912

  ≡Chrysomyxa piperiana (Arthur) Sacc. & Trotter 1912  Note: Isonym of (Arthur) Hotson 1925.

Notes: From Melampsoropsis piperiana - Based on uredinia. From Peridermium parksianum - Aecial state. Oldest epithet is not in the correct genus.

Distribution: Pacific Northwest, also reported from China.

Substrate: Living needles.

Disease Note: Rust.

Host: Spermogonia and aecia on Picea (Pinaceae), uredinia and telia on Rhododendron (Ericaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Cummins, G.B. 1956. Nomenclatural changes for some North American Uredinales. Mycologia 48: 601-608.
