Nomenclature data for Chrysomyxa succinea

Chrysomyxa succinea (Sacc.) Tranzschel 1939 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

≡ Gloeosporium succineum Sacc. 1880

≡Stilbechrysomyxa succinea (Sacc.) M.M. Chen 1984  Note: As (Tranzschel) M.M. Chen.

= Chrysomyxa alpina Hirats. f. 1929

= Chrysomyxa expansa Dietel 1900

= Peridermium piceae-hondoensis Dietel 1905

Notes: From Peridermium piceae-hondoensis - Aecial state.

Distribution: Asia.

Disease Note: Rust.

Host: Spermogonia and aecia on Picea (Pinaceae); uredinia and telia on Rhododendron (Ericaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Crane, P. E. 2005. Rust fungi on rhododendrons in Asia: Diaphanopellis forrestii gen. et sp. nov., new species of Caeoma, and expanded descriptions of Chrysomyxa dietelii and C. succinea. Mycologia 97: 534-548.
