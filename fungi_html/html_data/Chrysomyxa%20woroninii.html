Nomenclature data for Chrysomyxa woroninii

Chrysomyxa woroninii Tranzschel 1903 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

    Variant spelling Chrysomyxa woronini Tranzschel 1903

Notes: Aecial stage Peridermium coruscans? (Savile 1950).

Distribution: Northern hemisphere.

Disease Note: Rust.

Host: Spermogonia and aecia on Picea (Pinaceae), uredinia and telia on Ledum (Ericaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Savile, D.B.O. 1950. North American species of Chrysomyxa. Canad. J. Res., Sect. C, Bot. Sci. 28: 318-330.
