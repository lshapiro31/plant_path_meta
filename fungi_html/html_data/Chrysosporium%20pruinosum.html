Nomenclature data for Chrysosporium pruinosum

Phanerochaete chrysosporium Burds. 1974 (Basidiomycetes, Polyporales)

= Phanerochaete macrocystidiata Hallenb. 1978

= Sporotrichum pruinosum Gilman & E.V. Abbott 1927

  ≡Chrysosporium pruinosum (Gilman & E.V. Abbott) J.W. Carmich. 1962

= Sporotrichum pulverulentum Novobr. 1972

Notes: The oldest epithet is not in the correct genus.

Distribution: Temperate northern hemisphere. From Sporotrichum pruinosum - Widespread.

Disease Note: Gray mold.

Supporting Literature:

Burdsall Jr., H.H., and Eslyn, W.E. 1974. A new Phanerochaete with a Chrysosporium imperfect state. Mycotaxon 1: 123-133.
