Nomenclature data for Chytridium brassicae

Olpidiaster brassicae (Woronin) Doweld 2014 (Chytridiomycetes, Spizellomycetales)

≡ Chytridium brassicae Woronin 1878

≡Olpidium brassicae (Woronin) P.A. Dang. 1886

≡Pleotrachelus brassicae (Woronin) Sahtiy. 1962

= Olpidium borzii De Wild. 1893

    ≡ Olpidium radicicola De Wild. 1896  Note: As 'radicicolum'. Nom. illeg. via superfluous.

= Asterocystis radicis De Wild. 1893

    ≡ Olpidiaster radicis (De Wild.) Pascher 1917

Notes: From Pleotrachelus brassicae - Nom. inval. via no basionym reference. From Olpidium brassicae - This species represents a complex (see O. virulentus). Recent studies suggest that O. brassicae in a strict sense is limited to crucifers (Sasaya and Koganezawa 2006, Hartwright et al. 2010).

Distribution: From Olpidium brassicae - Widespread.

Disease Note: From Olpidium brassicae - Important as a vector for viruses.

Host: From Olpidium brassicae - Taxa of Brassicaceae, many reports from other hosts are doubtful based on taxonomic revision.
