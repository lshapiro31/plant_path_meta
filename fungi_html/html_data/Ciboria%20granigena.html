Nomenclature data for Ciboria granigena

Gloeotinia granigena (Quél.) T. Schumacher 1979 (Ascomycetes, Helotiales)

≡ Phialea granigena Quél. 1883

≡Ciboria granigena (Quél.) N.F. Buchw. 1949

≡Sclerotinia granigena (Quél.) Sacc. 1889

≡Stromatinia granigena (Quél.) Boud. 1907

Distribution: Temperate North America; Europe.

Substrate: Seeds.

Disease Note: Blind seed disease.

Host: Bromus erectus and other Poaceae; specific host range uncertain due to confusion with Gloeotinia temulenta.

Supporting Literature:

Alderman, S.C. 1998. Gloeotinia temulenta and G. granigena, two distinct species. Mycologia 90: 422-426.
