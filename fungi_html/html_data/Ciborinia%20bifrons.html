Nomenclature data for Ciborinia bifrons

Ciborinia whetzelii (Seaver) Seaver 1951 (Ascomycetes, Helotiales)

≡ Sclerotinia whetzelii Seaver 1940

= Ciborinia bifrons Whetzel 1951

Distribution: North America (Canada, USA).

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Ink spot disease; black spots, leaf browning and defoliation.

Host: Populus spp. (Salicaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Callan, B.E. 1998. Diseases of Populus in British Columbia: a diagnostic manual. Natural Resources Canada, Canadian Forest Service, 157 pages.
