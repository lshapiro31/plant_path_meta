≡Cladodendron frondosus (Dicks.) Lázaro Ibiza 1916

≡Polypilus frondosus (Dicks. : Fr.) P. Karst. 1882

≡Polyporus frondosus (Dicks. : Fr.) Fr. 1838  Note: Sanctioned by Fries, Syst. Mycol. I:355, 1821.

Notes: Index Fungorum lists additional taxonomic synonyms.

Distribution: Europe, Asia, North America.

Substrate: Roots of living trees, sometimes at base of dead trees and stumps.

Disease Note: Butt rot of heartwood; not pathogenic. Commercially harvested as an edible fungus.

Host: Hardwoods, especially Quercus and Castanea spp., rarely conifers.
