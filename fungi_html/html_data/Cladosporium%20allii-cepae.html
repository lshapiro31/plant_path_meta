Nomenclature data for Cladosporium allii-cepae

Cladosporium allii-cepae (Ranoj.) M.B. Ellis 1976 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Heterosporium allii-cepae Ranoj. 1910

Distribution: British Isles.

Substrate: On Allium cepa; leaf blotch (Anonymous. C.M.I. Descr. 842. 1986).

Disease Note: Leaf blotch.

Host: On Allium cepa (Liliaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Bensch, K., Braun, U., Groenewald, J.Z., and Crous, P.W. 2012. The genus Cladosporium. Stud. Mycol. 72: 1-401.
