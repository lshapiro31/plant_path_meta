Nomenclature data for Cladosporium cladosporioides

Cladosporium cladosporioides (Fresen.) G.A. De Vries 1952 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Penicillium cladosporioides Fresen. 1850

≡Hormodendrum cladosporioides (Fresen.) Sacc. 1880

= Monilia humicola Oudem. 1902

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Leaves, seeds, inflorescences. Often dead plant material.

Disease Note: Leaf spot and blight, flower blight, scab, sooty mold; also saprobic and hyperparasitic.

Host: Multiple genera in multiple families. Cited as cause of disease in Rubia cordifolia (Rubiaceae) and strawberries.
