Nomenclature data for Cladosporium colocasiicola

Cladosporium colocasiae Sawada 1916 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

= Cladosporium colocasiicola Sawada 1959  Note: Nom. inval.

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Living but old leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Especially Colocasia (Araceae). Other host records doubtful.
