Nomenclature data for Cladosporium cucumerinum

Cladosporium cucumerinum Ellis & Arthur 1889 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

= Cladosporium cucumerinum var. europaeum Bubák  Note: In herb. only. Nom. inval.

= Cladosporium cucumeris A.B. Frank 1893

= Scolicotrichum melophthorum Prill. & Delacr. 1891

  Variant spelling Scolecotrichum melophthorum Prill. & Delacr. 1891

    ≡ Macrosporium melophthorum (Prill. & Delacr.) Rostr. 1893

= Cladosporium scabies Cooke 1903

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Living fruits, leaves, petioles, and stems.

Disease Note: Causing cucumber gummosis or scab and crown blight.

Host: Especially Cucurbitaceae.
