Notes: From Cladosporium osterici - In herb. only. Invalid name. From Fusicladium peucedani - Not Ellis & Holw. 1895. From Fusicladium depressum - Year changed from 1886. From Passalora depressa - Passalora depressa (Berk. & Broome) Poonam Srivast. 1994 comb inval.

Distribution: Temperate northern hemisphere.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Apiaceae.
