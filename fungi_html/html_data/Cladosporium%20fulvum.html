Nomenclature data for Cladosporium fulvum

Fulvia fulva (Cooke) Cif. 1954 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Cladosporium fulvum Cooke 1883

≡Mycovellosiella fulva (Cooke) Arx 1983

≡Passalora fulva (Cooke) U. Braun & Crous 2003

Notes: From Cladosporium fulvum - Isonym.

Distribution: Cosmopolitan wherever tomatos are grown.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf mold of tomato.

Host: Solanum lycopersicum (Solanaceae).
