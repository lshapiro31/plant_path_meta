Nomenclature data for Cladosporium guanicense

Passalora guanicensis (F. Stevens) U. Braun & R.F. Castañeda 1994 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Cladosporium guanicense F. Stevens 1917

      Variant spelling Cladosporium guanicensis F. Stevens 1917

≡Cercosporidium guanicense (F. Stevens) Deighton 1967

≡Polythrincium guanicense (F. Stevens) Cif. 1938

= Cercospora whetzelii Chupp 1931

  Variant spelling Piricularia whetzelii (Chupp) Bat. & R. Garnier 1960  Note: Should be corrected to Pyricularia.

    ≡ Pyricularia whetzelii (Chupp) Bat. & R. Garnier 1960

Distribution: Widespread, especially neotropical region.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Argemone mexicana (Papaveraceae).
