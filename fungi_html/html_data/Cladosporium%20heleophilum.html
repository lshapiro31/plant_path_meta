Nomenclature data for Cladosporium heleophilum

Cladosporium heleophilum J.C. David 1912 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

Distribution: Europe (Germany, Latvia, UK), North America (USA, WA).

Substrate: Dead leaves.

Disease Note: Saprobic.

Host: Typha latifolia (Typhaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Bensch, K., Braun, U., Groenewald, J.Z., and Crous, P.W. 2012. The genus Cladosporium. Stud. Mycol. 72: 1-401.
