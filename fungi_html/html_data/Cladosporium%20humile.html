Nomenclature data for Cladosporium humile

Venturia acerina Plakidas ex M.E. Barr 1942 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

= Cladosporium humile Davis 1919

    ≡ Fusicladium humile (Davis) K. Schub & U. Braun 2002

    ≡ Fusicladosporium humile (Davis) Partridge & Morgan-Jones 2003

Notes: Oldest epithet not in correct genus, pending conservation of Venturia.

Distribution: Northeastern North America.

Substrate: Living leaves (anamorph), overwintered dead leaves (teleomorph).

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Acer (Sapindaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Schubert, K., and Braun, U. 2002. Venturia acerina. I.M.I. Descr. Fungi Bact. 1520: 1-2.

Sivanesan, A. 1977. The Genus Venturia. J. Cramer, -- pages.
