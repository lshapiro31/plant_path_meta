Nomenclature data for Cladosporium iridis

Cladosporium iridis (Fautrey & Roum.) G.A. de Vries 1952 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Scolicotrichum iridis Fautrey & Roum. 1891

      Variant spelling Scolecotrichum iridis Fautrey & Roum. 1891

≡Heterosporium iridis (Fautrey & Roum.) J.E. Jacques 1941

= Scolicotrichum cladosporioideum Maire 1906

  Variant spelling Scolecotrichum cladosporioideum Maire 1906

= Heterosporium gracile Sacc. 1886  Note: As (Wallr.?) Sacc. Treatment follows Dugan et al. (2004).

= Heterosporium iridis-pumilae Savul. & Sandu 1935

= Didymellina macrospora Kleb. 1924

    ≡ Mycosphaerella macrospora (Kleb.) Jørst. 1945

    ≡ Davidiella macrospora (Kleb.) Crous & U. Braun 2003

= Heterosporium montenegrinum Bubák 1903

= Heterosporium pruneti Nicolas & Aggéry 1928

Notes: Oldest epithet occupied in Cladosporium by C. gracile Corda (1837).

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot and blotch.

Host: Iris spp., Belamcanda chinensis, Juno spp., Gladiolus spp., Pardanthopsis spp. (Iridaceae), Hemerocallis (Xanthorrhoeaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Bensch, K., Braun, U., Groenewald, J.Z., and Crous, P.W. 2012. The genus Cladosporium. Stud. Mycol. 72: 1-401.
