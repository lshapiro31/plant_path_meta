Nomenclature data for Cladosporium levieri

Fusicladium levieri Magnus 1900 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

≡Cladosporium levieri (Magnus) Hara 1937

≡Phaeoramularia levieri (Magnus) U. Braun 1997

≡Ragnhildiana levieri (Magnus) Vassiljevsky 1937

= Fusicladium diospyri Chona, Munjal & J.N. Kapoor 1957 [1956]

= Fusicladium kaki Hori & Yoshino 1905

Distribution: Asia, Caucasus, Europe, North America.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Diospyros spp. (Ebenaceae).

Supporting Literature:
