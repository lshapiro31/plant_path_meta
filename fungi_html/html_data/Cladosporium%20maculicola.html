Nomenclature data for Cladosporium maculicola

Venturia borealis A. Funk 1989 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

= Pollaccia borealis A. Funk 1989

= Torula maculicola Romell & Sacc. 1893

    ≡ Phaeoramularia maculicola (Romell & Sacc.) B. Sutton 1970

  ≡Cladosporium maculicola (Romell & Sacc.) M. Morelet 1972

= Fusicladium romellianum Ondrej 1973

Notes: From Torula maculicola - Author changed from Roum. & Sacc. Problem with names.

Distribution: Asia, Europe, North America. Some records may be Pollacia radiosa.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Populus spp. (Salicaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Ellis, M.B. 1976. More dematiaceous Hyphomycetes. Commonwealth Mycological Institute, Kew, Surrey, England, 507 pages.

Morelet, M. 1972. Sur trois parasites foliaires des peupliers. Bull. Soc. Sci. Nat. Archeol. Toulon Var 201: 3-4.

Schubert, K., Ritschel, A., and Braun, U. 2003. A monograph of Fusicladium s. lat. (Hyphomycetes). Schlechtendalia 9: 1-132.
