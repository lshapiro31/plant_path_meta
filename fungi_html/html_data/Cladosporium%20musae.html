Nomenclature data for Cladosporium musae

Metulocladosporiella musae (E.W. Mason) Crous, Schroers, J.Z. Groenew., U. Braun & K. Schub. 2006 (Eurotiomycetes, Chaetothyriales)

≡ Cladosporium musae E.W. Mason 1945

≡Periconiella sapientumicola Siboe 1994

Distribution: Widespread in subtropical and tropical regions.

Disease Note: Cladosporium speckle.

Host: Livistona chinensis (Arecaceae), Musa spp. (Musaceae).
