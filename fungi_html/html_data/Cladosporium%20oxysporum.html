Nomenclature data for Cladosporium oxysporum

Cladosporium oxysporum Berk. & M.A. Curtis 1869 [1868] (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

Distribution: Widespread in subtropical and tropical regions.

Substrate: Plant tissues.

Disease Note: Leaf spot, blight, defoliation.

Host: Diverse plant families.
