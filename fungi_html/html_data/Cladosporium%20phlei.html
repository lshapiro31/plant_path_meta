Nomenclature data for Cladosporium phlei

Cladosporium phlei (C.T. Greg.) G.A. De Vries 1952 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Heterosporium phlei C.T. Greg. 1919

Notes: Eye spot of timothy.

Distribution: Western United States; Asia, Europe; New Zealand.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Eye and leaf spot.

Host: Agrophyron, Phleum and related grasses (Poaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Bensch, K., Braun, U., Groenewald, J.Z., and Crous, P.W. 2012. The genus Cladosporium. Stud. Mycol. 72: 1-401.
