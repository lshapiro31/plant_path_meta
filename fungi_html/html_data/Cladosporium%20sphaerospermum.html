Nomenclature data for Cladosporium sphaerospermum

Cladosporium sphaerospermum Penz. 1882 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

= Cladosporium hibisci Reichert 1921

= Torula lichenopsis Hohnel 1927

= Cladosporium papyricola Berk. & Broome 1883

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Multitudinous substrates.

Disease Note: Secondary invader of plant tissues.

Host: Multiple genera in multiple families.
