Nomenclature data for Cladosporium tenuissimum

Cladosporium tenuissimum Cooke 1878 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

= Cladosporium herbarum var. citricola H.S. Fawc. 1911

Distribution: Cosmopolitan, especially common in tropical regions.

Substrate: Leaves, flowers, fruit.

Disease Note: Leaf spot, flower blight; also saprobic and hyperparasitic to rust fungi.

Host: Multiple genera in multiple families.
