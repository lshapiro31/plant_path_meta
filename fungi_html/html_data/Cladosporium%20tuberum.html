Nomenclature data for Cladosporium tuberum

Cladosporium delicatulum Cooke 1876 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

= Cladosporium fasciculatum f. scirpi-lacustris Roum. 1881

= Cladosporium fasciculatum var. densum Ravenel 1882  Note: Nom. nud. Nom. inval.

= Cladosporium tuberum Cooke 1883

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Dead leaves.

Disease Note: Saprobic.

Host: Air, building material, dust and diverse plant material.
