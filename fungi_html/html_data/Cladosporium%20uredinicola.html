Nomenclature data for Cladosporium uredinicola

Cladosporium uredinicola Speg. 1912 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

Distribution: Widespread.

Disease Note: Hyperparasitic on telia and uredinia of rusts, especially Cronartium and Puccinia. Also other fungi.
