Nomenclature data for Cladosporium variabile

Cladosporium variabile (Cooke) G.A. De Vries 1952 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Heterosporium variabile Cooke 1877

= Cladosporium subnodosum Cooke 1889

= Davidiella variabile Crous, K. Schub. & U. Braun 2007

Notes: Davidiella variable should be D. variabilis.

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Leaves. Seed-borne.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Spinachia oleracea (Amaranthaceae).
