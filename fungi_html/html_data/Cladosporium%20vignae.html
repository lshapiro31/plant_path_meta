Nomenclature data for Cladosporium vignae

Cladosporium vignae M.W. Gardner 1925 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Pods, peduncles, stems, petioles and leaves.

Disease Note: Scab, leaf and pod blight.

Host: Lespedeza spp. and Vigna spp. (Fabaceae).
