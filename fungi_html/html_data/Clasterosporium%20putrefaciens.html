Notes: Stemphylium has been recommended as the generic name to use for this genus rather than Pleospora. The use of the oldest specific epithet provided by Sphaeria herbarum is blocked by Stemphylium herbarum E.G. Simmons (1986); in lieu of conservation, the next oldest epithet has been used.

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Leaves, roots, seeds.

Disease Note: Leaf spot. Ring spot of clovers and lucerne, Red Nose of dwarf beans, Black Stalk Rot of onions.

Host: Wide host range including Allium spp. (Amaryllidaceae), Asparagus officinalis (Asparagaceae), Pyrus communis (Rosaceae), Hordeum vulgare (Poaceae), Helianthus annuus (Asteraceae).
