Nomenclature data for Clathrospora elymae

Clathrospora elynae Rabenh. 1857 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

    Variant spelling Clathrospora elymae Rabenh. 1857

≡Pleospora elynae (Rabenh.) Ces. & De Not. 1863

Notes: Alternaria-like anamorph.

Distribution: Europe, North America.

Substrate: Wood, stems.

Disease Note: Saprobic.

Host: Carex spp. (Cyperaceae), Juncaceae, Brassicaceae.

Supporting Literature:
