Nomenclature data for Coccomyces kerriae

Blumeriella kerriae (V.B. Stewart) Korf 1971 (Ascomycetes, Helotiales)

≡ Coccomyces kerriae V.B. Stewart 1917

≡Higginsia kerriae (V.B. Stewart) Nannf. 1932

Notes: Leaf and twig blight. From Cylindrosporium kerriae - This name was never validated by Stewart (Phytopathology 7:399. 1917).

Distribution: North America (USA), Europe (UK).

Substrate: Leaves, twigs, stems.

Disease Note: Lesions, defoliation, canker, dieback.

Host: Kerria japonica (Rosaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Robinson, R.J., Konyves, K., and Scrace, J. 2017. First record of the fungus Blumeriella kerriae in the UK. New Disease Reports 35: 34.
