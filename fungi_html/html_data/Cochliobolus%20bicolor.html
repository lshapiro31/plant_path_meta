Nomenclature data for Cochliobolus bicolor

Bipolaris bicolor (Mitra) Shoemaker 1959 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

≡ Helminthosporium bicolor Mitra 1931

≡Drechslera bicolor (Mitra) Subram. & B.L. Jain 1966

= Drechslera bhawanii O. Prakash & A.P. Misra 1980

= Cochliobolus bicolor A.R. Paul & Parbery 1966

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Leaves, seeds, roots.

Disease Note: Foot rot, leaf spot.

Host: Mainly Poaceae; also reported from Quercus sp. (Fagaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Manamgoda, D.S., Rossman, A.Y., Castlebury, L.A., Crous, P.W., Madrid, H., Chukeatirote, E., and Hyde, K.D. 2014. The genus Bipolaris. Stud. Mycol. 79: 221-288.
