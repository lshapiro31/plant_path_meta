Nomenclature data for Cochliobolus carbonum

Bipolaris zeicola (G.L. Stout) Shoemaker 1959 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

≡ Helminthosporium zeicola G.L. Stout 1930

≡Drechslera zeicola (G.L. Stout) Subram. & P.C. Jain 1966

= Cochliobolus carbonum R.R. Nelson 1959

= Helminthosporium carbonum Ullstrup 1944

    ≡ Drechslera carbonum (Ullstrup) Sivan. 1984

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Living leaves, ears of corn, stalk, plant debris.

Disease Note: Leaf spot. Ear and kernel rot. Northern corn leaf spot.

Host: Corn, Zea mays, sometimes on Sorghum (Poaceae) and various other plant families.
