Nomenclature data for Cochliobolus cynodontis

Bipolaris cynodontis (Marignoni) Shoemaker 1959 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

≡ Helminthosporium cynodontis Marignoni 1909

≡Drechslera cynodontis (Marignoni) Subram. & P.C. Jain 1966

= Cochliobolus cynodontis R.R. Nelson 1964

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf blight and spot; also a secondary invader and saprobe. Not a cause of serious disease per Manamgoda et al. (2014).

Host: Various hosts.

Supporting Literature:

Manamgoda, D.S., Rossman, A.Y., Castlebury, L.A., Crous, P.W., Madrid, H., Chukeatirote, E., and Hyde, K.D. 2014. The genus Bipolaris. Stud. Mycol. 79: 221-288.
