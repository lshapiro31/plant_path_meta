Nomenclature data for Cochliobolus eragrostidis

Curvularia eragrostidis (Henn.) J.A. Mey. 1959 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

≡ Brachysporium eragrostidis Henn. 1908

= Pseudocochliobolus eragrostidis Tsuda & Ueyama 1985

  ≡Cochliobolus eragrostidis (Tsuda & Ueyama) Sivan. 1987

= Spondylocladium maculans C.K. Bancr. 1913

    ≡ Curvularia maculans (C.K. Bancr.) Boedijn 1933

Distribution: Subtropical and tropical regions.

Substrate: Living leaves, fruit.

Disease Note: Foliar blight, leaf spot, withertip, fruit rot.

Host: Diverse plant families.
