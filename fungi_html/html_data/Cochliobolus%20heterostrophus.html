Nomenclature data for Cochliobolus heterostrophus

Bipolaris maydis (Y. Nisik. & Miyake) Shoemaker 1959 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

≡ Helminthosporium maydis Y. Nisik. & Miyake 1926

≡Drechslera maydis (Y. Nisik. & Miyake) Subram. & P.C. Jain 1966

= Ophiobolus heterostrophus Drechsler 1925  Note: From Cochliobolus heterostrophus - Southern leaf blight of maize (Ellis & Holliday. C.M.I. Descr. 301. 1971).

  ≡Cochliobolus heterostrophus (Drechsler) Drechsler 1934

Notes: Helminthosporium maydis Y. Nisik. & C. Miyake conserved against H. maydis Brond. and Ophiobolus heterostrophus%.

Distribution: Warm temperate, subtropical, and tropical regions.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Southern corn leaf blight.

Host: Multiple hosts but primarily Poaceae.
