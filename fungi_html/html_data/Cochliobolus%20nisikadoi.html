Nomenclature data for Cochliobolus nisikadoi

Curvularia coicis E. Castell. 1956 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

= Helminthosporium coicis Y. Nisik. 1928

    ≡ Drechslera coicis (Y. Nisik.) Subram. & Jain 1966

    ≡ Bipolaris coicis (Y. Nisik.) Shoemaker 1959

= Pseudocochliobolus nisikadoi Tsuda, Ueyama & Nishih. 1977

  ≡Cochliobolus nisikadoi (Tsuda, Ueyama & Nishih.) Alcorn 1983

Distribution: Asia, one report from Brazil.

Substrate: Living leaves and seeds.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Primarily on Coix but also reported on other Poaceae.

Supporting Literature:

Ahmadpour, A., Pordel, A., Heidarian, Z., and Javan-Nikkhah, M. 2013. Bipolaris coicis causing adlay leaf blight in Iran. Australasian Plant Disease Notes 8: 137-139.
