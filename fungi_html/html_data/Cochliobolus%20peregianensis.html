Nomenclature data for Cochliobolus peregianensis

Bipolaris peregianensis Alcorn 1982 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

= Cochliobolus peregianensis Alcorn 1982

Distribution: Asia (China, Taiwan), Australia.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Cynodon dactylon, Rottboellia (Poaceae).
