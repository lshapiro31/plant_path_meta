Nomenclature data for Cochliobolus setariae

Bipolaris setariae (Sawada) Shoemaker 1959 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

≡ Helminthosporium setariae Sawada 1912

≡Drechslera setariae (Shoemaker) Subram. & B.L. Jain 1966

= Ophiobolus setariae S. Ito & Kurib. 1930

  ≡Cochliobolus setariae (S. Ito & Kurib.) Drechsler ex Dastur 1942

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Spots and blights, defoliation.

Host: Poaceae and reports from other various hosts.
