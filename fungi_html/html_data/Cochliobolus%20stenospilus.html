Nomenclature data for Cochliobolus stenospilus

Bipolaris stenospila (Drechsler) Shoemaker 1959 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

≡ Helminthosporium stenospilum Drechsler 1928

≡Drechslera stenospila (Drechsler) Subram. & P.C. Jain 1966

Notes: According to Sivanesan (Mycol. Pap. 158:99. 1987), Cochliobolus stenospilus was published without a latin diagnosis and is therefore not valid.

Distribution: Worldwide in sugarcane-growing regions.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Brown stripe.

Host: Saccharum officinarum (Poaceae).

Supporting Literature:
