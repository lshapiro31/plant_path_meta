Nomenclature data for Coleophoma cylindrospora

Coleophoma cylindrospora (Desm.) Höhn. 1919 (Ascomycetes, Incertae sedis)

≡ Sphaeropsis cylindrospora Desm. 1849

≡Macrophoma cylindrospora (Desm.) Berl. & Voglino 1886

≡Phoma cylindrospora (Desm.) Sacc. 1879

= Macrophoma collabens Berl. & Voglino 1886  Note: As comb. nov., but it is a nom. nov. since the basionym is a nomen illeg.

  [≡ Phoma collabens Cooke 1885 - illegitimate later homonym, not included in search]  Note: Not Dur. & Mont. 1849. Nomen illeg.

    ≡ Coleophoma collabens (Berl. & Voglino) Petr. & Syd. 1927  Note: As '(Cooke) Petr. & Syd'.

= Bactropycnis concentrica Höhn. 1920

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Living and dead leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Numerous hosts in numerous families.
