Nomenclature data for Coleosporium asterum

Coleosporium asterum (Dietel) Syd. & P. Syd. 1914 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

≡ Stichopsora asterum Dietel 1899

= Peridermium montanum Arthur & F. Kern 1906

Notes: Hiratsuka et al. (1992) believed that the North American forms on Aster and Solidago are distinct species from C. asterum described from Japan.

Distribution: Asia, North America.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Rust, dwarfing, supressed flowering.

Host: Aecia on Pinus spp. (Pinaceae), Telia on Asteraceae.
