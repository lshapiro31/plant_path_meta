Nomenclature data for Coleosporium fuchsiae

Mikronegeria fuchsiae P.E. Crane & R.S. Peterson 2007 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

= Coleosporium fuchsiae Cooke 1886  Note:

    ≡ Uredo fuchsiae (Cooke) Henn. 1903

= Caeoma peltatum C.G. Shaw bis & C.G. Shaw 1976

Notes: From Caeoma peltatum - Aecial state name.

Distribution: Oceania (New Zealand).

Disease Note: Heteroecious rust.

Host: Teliospores on Fuchsia spp (Onagraceae).

Supporting Literature:

Crane, P.E., and Peterson, R.S. 2007. Mikronegeria fuchsiae sp. nov., a rust fungus on Fuchsia and Phyllocladus in New Zealand. New Zealand J. Bot. 45: 707-713.
