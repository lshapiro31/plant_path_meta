Nomenclature data for Coleosporium madiae

Coleosporium madiae Cooke 1907 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

Distribution: Western North America.

Substrate: On living leaves and stems of Asteraceae; on woody plant parts of Pinus.

Disease Note: Heteroecious rust fungus.
