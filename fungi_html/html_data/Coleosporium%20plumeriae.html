Nomenclature data for Coleosporium plumeriae

Coleosporium plumeriae Pat. 1902 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

    Variant spelling Coleosporium plumierae Pat. 1902

= Uredo domingensis Berk. 1852

     Coleosporium domingensis (Berk.) Arthur, nom. illeg. 1918

Notes: From Coleosporium plumierae - Autoecious. On Plumeria sp. McRitchie (Pl. Pathol. Cir. 319:1-2. 1989).

Distribution: Asia (China), North America (USA: FL), West Indies, Central America.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Rust.

Host: Plumeria spp. (Apocynaceae).
