Nomenclature data for Coleosporium solidaginis

Coleosporium solidaginis (Schwein.) Thüm. 1878 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

≡ Uredo solidaginis Schwein. 1822

≡Stichopsora solidaginis (Schwein.) Dietel 2017

= Peridermium acicola Underw. & Earle 1896

Notes: The name Coleosporium asterum has been frequently misapplied to this species.

Distribution: North America, Europe, Asia.

Substrate: Leaves, petioles.

Disease Note: Rust.

Host: Telial and uredenial host: Solidago spp. (Asteraceae), aecical host: Pinus spp. (Pinaceae).
