Nomenclature data for Colletotrichum crassipes

Colletotrichum crassipes (Speg.) Arx 1957 (Ascomycetes, Phyllachorales)

≡Gloeosporium crassipes Speg. 1878

= Gloeosporium passiflorae Speg. 1899

Notes: Numerous synonyms are listed by von Arx (1957, 1970).

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Disease Note: Anthracnose, leaf spot, fruit rots, etc.

Host: Multiple genera in multiple families.

Supporting Literature:

von Arx, J.A. 1957. Die arten der Gattung Colletotrichum Cda. Phytopathol. Z. 29: 413-468.

von Arx, J.A. 1970. A revision of the fungi classified as Gloeosporium. Biblioth. Mycol. 24: 1-203.
