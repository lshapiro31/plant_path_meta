Nomenclature data for Colletotrichum linicola

Colletotrichum lini (Westerd.) Tochinai 1926 (Ascomycetes, Phyllachorales)

≡ Gloeosporium lini Westerd. 1916

[≡Colletotrichum lini T.F. Manns & Bolley 1932 - illegitimate later homonym, not included in search]  Note: A homonym exists for this name.

= Colletotrichum linicola Pethybr. & Laff. 1918

Notes: From Colletotrichum linicola - As 'linicolum'.

Distribution: Widespread based on scattered reports.

Substrate: Leaves, petioles, seedlings.

Disease Note: Anthracnose.

Host: Linum spp. (Linaceae), Trifolium spp., Medicago sativa (Fabaceae), Raphanus raphanistrum (Brassicaceae).
