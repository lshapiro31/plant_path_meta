Nomenclature data for Colletotrichum malvarum

Colletotrichum malvarum (A. Braun & Casp.) Southw. 1891 (Ascomycetes, Phyllachorales)

≡ Steirochaete malvarum A. Braun & Casp. 1854

= Colletotrichum althaeae Southw. 1890

= Colletotrichum magnusianum Bres. 1892

= Gloeosporium malvae P. Syd. 1899

Distribution: Widespread.

Disease Note: Leaf spot of Malvaceae.

Host: Malvaceae.
