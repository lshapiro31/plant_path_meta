    ≡ Gloeosporium orbiculare (Berk.) Berk. 1853

    ≡ Myxosporium orbiculare (Berk. & Mont.) Berk. 1860

     Sirogloea orbicularis (Berk.) Arx 1957

= Macrophoma sheldonii Rodigin 1928

Notes: Sirogloea orbicularis (Berk.) Arx (1957) and Colletotrichum orbiculare (Berk.) Arx (1957) are in violation of ICN Art. 36.2, which makes two separate names based on the same type and published at the same time invalidly published. In Damm et.al. (2013), this is listed as Art. 34.2, the rule's place in the now outdated Vienna code. Glomerella cingulata var. orbiculare S.F. Jenkins & Winstead (1962) invalidly published (Art. 39.1 - no Latin description).

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Leaves, stems, fruits.

Disease Note: Anthracnose and fruit rot.

Host: Cucurbitaceae.
