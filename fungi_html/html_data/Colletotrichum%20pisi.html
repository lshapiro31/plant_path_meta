Nomenclature data for Colletotrichum pisi

Colletotrichum pisi Pat. 1891 (Ascomycetes, Phyllachorales)

Notes: Mostly likely a synonym of another species of Colletotrichum.

Distribution: North America.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot and anthracnose.

Host: Genera of the Fabaceae.
