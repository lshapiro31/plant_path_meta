Nomenclature data for Colletotrichum xanthorrhoeae

Colletotrichum xanthorrhoeae R.G. Shivas, Bathgate & Podger 1998 (Ascomycetes, Phyllachorales)

Distribution: Australia.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Xanthorrhoea spp. (Xanthorrhoeaceae).
