Nomenclature data for Coniella castaneicola

Coniella castaneicola (Ellis & Everh.) B. Sutton 1980 (Ascomycetes, Diaporthales)

≡ Gloeosporium castaneicola Ellis & Everh. 1895

      Variant spelling Gloeosporium castanicola Ellis & Everh.

≡Pilidiella castaneicola (Ellis & Everh.) Arx 1957

= Phyllosticta castaneicola Ellis & Everh. 1895

    ≡ Asteromella castaneicola (Ellis & Everh.) Petr. 1957

  Variant spelling Asteromella castanicola (Ellis & Everh.) Petr.

  Variant spelling Phyllosticta castanicola Ellis & Everh.

  Variant spelling Asteromella castanicola (Ellis & Everh.) Petr.

Notes: From Pilidiella castaneicola - The anamorph-teleomorph connection is based on the synonymy of Sutton (The Coelomycetes. p. 420. 1980) and the study by Maas et al. (Mycologia 71:92. 1979).

Distribution: From Pilidiella castaneicola - Warm-temperate to tropical regions.

Substrate: From Pilidiella castaneicola - On dead and decaying leaves.

Disease Note: From Pilidiella castaneicola - Leaf spot.

Host: From Pilidiella castaneicola - Various plant genera.
