Nomenclature data for Coniosporium phaeospermum

Arthrinium phaeospermum (Corda) M.B. Ellis 1965 (Ascomycetes, Incertae sedis)

≡ Gymnosporangium phaeospermum Corda 1837

≡Coniosporium phaeospermum (Corda) Sacc. 1881

= Melanconium arundinis Pers. 1818

= Botryoconis sanguinea Tubaki 1952

= Stilbospora sphaerosperma Pers. 1796

    ≡ Papularia sphaerosperma (Pers.) Höhn. 1916

    ≡ Melanconium sphaerospermum (Pers.) Link 1825

Notes: For additoinal synonyms, see Index Fungorum. Non A. sphaerospermum 1874 and A. arundinis 1979.

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Leaves, stems.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Diverse plant families. Pathogenic on Olea europaea var. sativa (Oleaceae), Phyllostachys viridis (Poaceae).
