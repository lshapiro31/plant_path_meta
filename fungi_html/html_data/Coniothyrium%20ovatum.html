Nomenclature data for Coniothyrium ovatum

Teratosphaeria ovata (H.J. Swart) Crous & Summerell 2009 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

≡ Coniothyrium ovatum H.J. Swart 1986

= Coniothyrium parvum H.J. Swart 1986

Notes: Anamorphic type.

Distribution: Scattered reports.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Eucalyptus spp. (Myrtaceae).
