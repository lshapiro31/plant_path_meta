Ophiostoma floccosum Math.-Käärik 1951 (Ascomycetes, Ophiostomatales)

≡Ceratocystis floccosa (Math.-Käärik) J. Hunt 1956

= Graphium aureum Hedgc. 1906

    ≡ Pesotum aureum (Hedgc.) McNew & T.C. Harr. 2001

  ≡Coremium aureum (Hedgc.) Sacc. 1913

Notes: Anamorph pesotum-like.

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Bark.

Disease Note: Blue stain.

Host: Pinaceae.
