Nomenclature data for Coryneum cardinale

Seiridium cardinale (W. Wagener) B. Sutton & I. Gibson 1972 (Ascomycetes, Xylariales)

≡ Coryneum cardinale W. Wagener 1939

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Bark/trunks.

Disease Note: Canker.

Host: Multiple genera of Cupressaceae. Also, reports on Protea sp. (Proteaceae).
