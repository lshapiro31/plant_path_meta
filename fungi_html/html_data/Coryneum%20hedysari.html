Nomenclature data for Coryneum hedysari

Parodiella hedysari (Schwein.) S. Hughes 1958 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

≡ Didymosporium hedysari Schwein. 1822

≡Coryneum hedysari (Schwein.) Schwein. 1832

= Dothidea perisporioides Berk. & M.A. Curtis 1876

    ≡ Parodiella perisporioides (Berk. & M.A. Curtis) Speg. 1898

Notes: Parasitic on Fabaceae (Sutton. Mycol. Pap. 138. p. 117. 1975).

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Causes little damage to host, even though parasitic.

Host: Papilionoid Fabaceae.

Supporting Literature:
