Nomenclature data for Coryneum umbonatum

Pseudovalsa longipes (Tul.) Sacc. 1879 (Ascomycetes, Diaporthales)

≡ Melanconis longipes Tul. 1856

= Coryneum kunzei Corda 1840

= Coryneum pustulatum Peck 1879

= Melanconis sigmoidea Cooke & Ellis 1878

    ≡ Pseudovalsa sigmoidea (Cooke & Ellis) Sacc. 1883

= Coryneum umbonatum Nees 1825

Notes: From Coryneum umbonatum - According to Sutton (Mycol. Pap. 138:50. 1975) this species is restricted to Quercus. Oldest epithet not in the correct genus, assuming Pseudovalsa is used.

Distribution: Temperate North America; Europe.

Substrate: On limbs.

Disease Note: Canker.

Host: On diverse hardwoods.
