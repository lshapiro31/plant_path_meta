Nomenclature data for Cristulariella depraedans

Cristulariella depraedans (Cooke) Höhn. 1916 (Ascomycetes, Helotiales)

≡ Polyactis depraedans Cooke 1885

≡Botrytis depraedans (Cooke) Sacc. 1886

≡Myrioconium depraedans (Cooke) Arx 1970

= Nervostroma depraedans Narumi & Y. Harada 2006

= Illosporium diedickeanum Sacc. 1908

Distribution: Northern hemisphere.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot and leaf wilt.

Host: Acer spp. (Sapindaceae) and reports from some other hosts.

Supporting Literature:

Cho, S.E., Park, J.H., Choi, Y.J., Lee, S.H., Lee, C.K., and Shin, H.D. 2017. Occurence of leaf spot casued by Cristulariella depraedans on Acer ginnala. Forest Pathol. 47: e12302.
