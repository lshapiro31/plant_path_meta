Nomenclature data for Cristulariella moricola

Grovesinia moricola (I. Hino) Redhead 2014 (Ascomycetes, Helotiales)

≡ Botrytis moricola I. Hino 1929

≡Cristulariella moricola (I. Hino) Redhead 1980 [1979]

≡Hinomyces moricola (I. Hino) Narumi & Y. Harada 2006

= Sclerotium cinnamomi Sawada 1933

= Sclerotinia moricola I. Hino 1929

    ≡ Botryotinia moricola (I. Hino) W. Yamam. 1959  Note: Name based on an anamorphic type.

= Cristulariella pyramidalis Waterman & R.P. Marshall 1947

= Grovesinia pyramidalis M.N. Cline, J.L. Crane & S.D. Cline 1983

Notes: Grovesinia proposed for conservation over Hinomyces by Johnston et al. (2014).

Distribution: Asia, North America.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Numerous hosts in several families.
