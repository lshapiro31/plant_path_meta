Nomenclature data for Cronartium comptoniae

Cronartium comptoniae Arthur 1906 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

= Peridermium comptoniae Orton & J.F. Adams 1914

Notes: Sweet fern blister rust. Heteroecious; aecial host - Pinus spp.; telial host - Comptonia and Myrica. Mordue & Gibson (C.M.I. Descr. 579. 1978). From Peridermium comptoniae - Anamorph of sweet fern blister rust. Heteroecious; aecial host - Pinus spp.; telial host - Comptonia and Myrica. Perennial cankers on stems and branches of certain pines (Ziller. The tree rusts of western Canada. p. 88. 1974).

Distribution: Temperate North America.

Disease Note: Sweet fern blister rust.

Supporting Literature:

Mordue, J.E.M., and Gibson, I.A.S. 1978. Cronartium comptoniae. C.M.I. Descr. Pathog. Fungi Bact. 579: 1-2.
