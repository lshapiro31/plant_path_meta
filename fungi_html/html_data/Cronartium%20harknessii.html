Nomenclature data for Cronartium harknessii

Endocronartium harknessii (J.P. Moore) Y. Hirats. 1969 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

≡ Peridermium harknessii J.P. Moore 1884

      Variant spelling Peridermium harknessi J.P. Moore

= Peridermium cerebroides E. Meinecke 1927

= Cronartium harknessii E. Meinecke, nom. nud. 1920

Notes: Autoecious. Western gall rust, Woodgate rust. According to Epstein & Buurlage (Mycologia 80:235. 1988) this fungus is an asexual rust.

Distribution: Temperate North America.

Disease Note: Western gall rust.

Supporting Literature:
