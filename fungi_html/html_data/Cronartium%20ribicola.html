Nomenclature data for Cronartium ribicola

Cronartium ribicola J.c. Fisch. 1872 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

= Peridermium kurilense Dietel 1905

= Peridermium strobi Kleb. 1888

Distribution: Northern hemisphere.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: White pine blister rust, currant rust.

Host: Pinus spp. (Pinaceae) for aecial state; Pedicularis spp., Castilleja spp. (Orobanchaceae), Grossularia spp, Ribes spp. (Grossulariaceae) for uredinial and telial states.
