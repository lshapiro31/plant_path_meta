Nomenclature data for Crumenula laricina

Gremmeniella laricina (Ettl.) Petrini, L.E. Petrini, Lafl. & Ouell. 1989 (Ascomycetes, Helotiales)

≡ Crumenula laricina Ettl. 1945

≡Encoeliopsis laricina (Ettl.) J.W. Groves 1969

≡Scleroderris laricina (Ettl.) Gremmen 1954

= Brunchorstia laricina Ettl. 1945

Notes: From Encoeliopsis laricina - Shoot-blight disease of larch (Funk. Canad. J. Bot. 47:1509. 1969).

Distribution: Asia, Western North America, European Alps.

Substrate: Living twigs.

Disease Note: Twig dieback.

Host: Larix spp. (Pinaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Petrini, O., Toti, L., Petrini, L.E., and Heiniger, U. 1990. Gremmeniella abietina and G. laricina in Europe: characterization and identification of isolates and laboratory strains by soluble protein electrophoresis. Canad. J. Bot. 68: 2629-2635.
