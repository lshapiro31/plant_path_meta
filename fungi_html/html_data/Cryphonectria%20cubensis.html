Nomenclature data for Cryphonectria cubensis

Chrysoporthe cubensis (Bruner) Gryzenh. & M.J. Wingf. 2004 (, )

≡ Diaporthe cubensis Bruner 1917

≡Cryphonectria cubensis (Bruner) Hodges 1980

= Cryptosporella eugeniae Nutman & F.M. Roberts 1952

    ≡ Endothia eugeniae (Nutman & F.M. Roberts) J. Reid & C. Booth 1969

Notes: Van der Merwe et al. (2010) demonstrate this species is a complex. Historical records must be considered with caution.

Distribution: Widespread in subtropical and tropical regions.

Substrate: Bark and cankered wood.

Disease Note: Canker, dieback.

Host: Reported from several hosts in few families, especially Eucalyptus spp. and other genera of Myrtaceae.
