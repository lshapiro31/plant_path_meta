Nomenclature data for Cryphonectria eucalypti

Holocryphia eucalypti (M. Venter & M.J. Wingf.) Gryzenh. & M.J. Wingf. 2006 (, )

≡ Cryphonectria eucalypti M. Venter & M.J. Wingf. 2002

Distribution: Africa (South Africa, Uganda), Australia, New Zealand.

Substrate: Bark.

Disease Note: Canker.

Host: Myrtaceae, Tibouchina urvilleana (Melastomataceae).

Supporting Literature:

Gryzenhout, M. , Vermeulen, M., Dick, M., and Wingfield, M.J. 2010. The Eucalyptus canker pathogen Holocryphia eucalypti on Eucalyptus in New Zealand. Australasian Plant Disease Notes 5: 5-8.

Gryzenhout, M. , Wingfield, B.D., and Wingfield, M.J. 2009. Taxonomy, Phylogeny, and Ecology of Bark-Inhabiting and Tree-Pathogenic Fungi in the Cryphonectriaceae. APS Press, St. Paul, Minnesota, 119 pages.
