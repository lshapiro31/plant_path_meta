Nomenclature data for Cryphonectria havanensis

Microthia havanensis (Bruner) Gryzenh. & M.J. Wingf. 2006 (, )

≡ Endothia havanensis Bruner 1916

≡Cryphonectria havanensis (Bruner) M.E. Barr 1978

Distribution: Widespread based on scattered reports, especially North America.

Host: Especially Eucalyptus spp. (Myrtaceae) and several reports from taxa in other families.
