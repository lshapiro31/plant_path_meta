Nomenclature data for Cryptocline phacidiella

Pyrenopeziza laurocerasi (Desm.) Nannf. 1932 (Ascomycetes, Helotiales)

≡ Phacidium laurocerasi Desm. 1846

≡Trochila laurocerasi (Desm.) Fr. 1846

= Gloeosporium phacidiellum Grove 1912  Note: From Cryptocline phacidiella - Leaf spot. Arx (Biblioth. Mycol. 24:30. 1970).

  ≡Cryptocline phacidiella (Grove) Arx 1957

Notes: Greenhalgh & Morgan-Jones (Trans. Brit. Mycol. Soc. 47:315. 1964) presented details of the morphology of this species under the name Trochila laurocerasi.

Distribution: CA; Europe.

Substrate: On fallen leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: On Prunus (Rosaceae).
