Nomenclature data for Cryptodiaporthe aculeans

Amphiporthe aculeans (Schwein.) M.E. Barr 1978 (Ascomycetes, Diaporthales)

≡ Sphaeria aculeans Schwein. 1832

≡Calospora aculeans (Schwein.) Sacc. 1883

≡Cryptodiaporthe aculeans (Schwein.) Wehm. 1933

≡Cryptospora aculeans (Schwein.) Ellis & Everh. 1892

≡Diaporthe aculeans (Schwein.) Höhn. 1918

≡Valsa aculeans (Schwein.) Berk. 1876  Note: Erroneously cited by Cooke (1877) as Valsa aculeatus (Spielman 1985).

Distribution: North America (USA), Asia (Japan).

Substrate: Bark of branches.

Disease Note: Canker, dieback.

Host: Rhus spp. (Anacardiaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Spielman, L.J. 1985. A monograph of Valsa on hardwoods in North America. Canad. J. Bot. 63: 1355-1378.
