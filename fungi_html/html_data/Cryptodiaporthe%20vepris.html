Nomenclature data for Cryptodiaporthe vepris

Cryptodiaporthe vepris (Delacr.) Petr. 1934 (Ascomycetes, Diaporthales)

≡ Sphaeria vepris Delacr. 1862

≡Anisogramma vepris (Delacr.) Merezhko 1986

≡Apioporthe vepris (Delacr.) Wehm. 1933

≡Apioporthella vepris (Delacr.) M.E. Barr 1991

≡Apiothecium vepris (Delacr.) Lar.N. Vassiljeva 1987

≡Diaporthe vepris (Delacr.) Fuckel 1870

[≡Gnomonia vepris (Delacr.) Keissl. 1912 - illegitimate later homonym, not included in search]  Note: Not Mouton 1901. Nom. illeg.

= Phoma vepris Sacc. 1884

    ≡ Phomopsis vepris (Sacc.) Höhn. 1906

Notes: A member of the Diaporthales, but not assignable to any described family. Taxonomy needs work.

Distribution: Widespread in temperate regions.

Substrate: Stems.

Disease Note: Branch canker.

Host: Primarily on Rubus (Rosaceae).
