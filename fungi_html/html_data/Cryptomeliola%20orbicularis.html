≡Meliolina orbicularis (Berk. & M.A. Curtis) F. Stevens 1927

Distribution: North America (USA).

Substrate: Epiphytic on living leaves.

Disease Note: Black mildew.

Host: Ilex spp. (Aquifoliaceae)
