Nomenclature data for Cryptomyces maximus

Cryptomyces maximus (Fr. : Fr.) Rehm 1888 (Ascomycetes, Rhytismatales)

≡ Rhytisma maximum Fr. : Fr. 1823

Distribution: Southwestern United States, Europe.

Substrate: On living branches and twigs.

Disease Note: Decreased leaf formation.

Host: Salix spp. (Salicaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Rama, T. 2016. Cryptomyces maximus rediscovered in Finland after 99 years. Karstenia 56: 55-60.
