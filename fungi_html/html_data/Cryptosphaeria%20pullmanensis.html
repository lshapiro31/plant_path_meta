Nomenclature data for Cryptosphaeria pullmanensis

Cryptosphaeria pullmanensis Glawe 1984 (Ascomycetes, Xylariales)

Distribution: North America (USA: CA, NV, WA), Asia (China, Iran).

Substrate: Branches, trunk, bark, twigs.

Disease Note: Dieback, wood discoloration, canker.

Host: Populus spp., Salix spp. (Salicaceae) and reports from Vitis.
