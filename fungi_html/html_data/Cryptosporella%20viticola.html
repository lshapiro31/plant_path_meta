Nomenclature data for Cryptosporella viticola

Cryptosporella viticola Shear 1911 (Ascomycetes, Diaporthales)

Notes: The telemorph-anamorph connection is considered to be tenuous at best by Punithalingam (1979).

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Disease Note: Dead arm.

Host: Vitis spp. (Vitaceae).
