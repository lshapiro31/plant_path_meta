Nomenclature data for Cryptosporiopsis perennans

Neofabraea perennans Kienholz 1939 (Ascomycetes, Helotiales)

≡Pezicula perennans (Kienholz) Dugan, R.G. Roberts & G.G. Grove 1993

= Gloeosporium perennans Zeller & Childs 1925

  ≡Cryptosporiopsis perennans (Zeller & Childs) Wollenw. 1939

Distribution: Europe, North America.

Substrate: Branches, twigs.

Disease Note: Perennial canker, bitter rot of fruit.

Host: Malus spp. (Rosaceae).
