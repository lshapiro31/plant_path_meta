Nomenclature data for Cryptosporiopsis pruinosa

Pezicula pruinosa Farl. 1922 (Ascomycetes, Helotiales)

≡Pezicula pruinosa var. pruinosa

[≡Dermatea pruinosa (Farl.) Petr. 1922 - illegitimate later homonym, not included in search]  Note: Not Ellis & Everh. 1888. Nom. illeg.

= Sphaeronaema pruinosum Peck 1872

  ≡Cryptosporiopsis pruinosa (Peck) Wollenw. 1938

    ≡ Lagynodella pruinosa (Peck) Petr. 1922

Distribution: Northern North America.

Substrate: On dead twigs and limbs.

Disease Note: Saprobic.

Host: Primarily on Amelanchier (Rosaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Verkley, G.J.M. 1999. A monograph of the genus Pezicula and its anamorphs. Stud. Mycol. 44: 1-180.
