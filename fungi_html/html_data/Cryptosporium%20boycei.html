≡Brunchorstia boycei (Dearn.) G.G. Hahn 1947

≡Foveostroma boycei (Dearn.) A. Funk 1979

≡Micropera boycei (Dearn.) J.W. Groves 1968

= Dermea pseudotsugae A. Funk 1967

Notes: From Foveostroma boycei - Groves (Mycologia 60:718.1968) provided a history of the name. From Dermea pseudotsugae - Bark and phloem necrosis, cankers, or die-back on young conifers.

Distribution: Pacific Northwest.

Substrate: Woody plant parts.

Disease Note: Dieback, bark and branch canker.

Host: Pinaceae.
