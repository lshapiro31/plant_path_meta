Nomenclature data for Cryptosporium epiphyllum

Discella ochroleuca (Berk. & M.A. Curtis) Arx 1957 (Ascomycetes, Incertae sedis)

≡ Septoria ochroleuca Berk. & M.A. Curtis 1873

≡Marssonia ochroleuca (Berk. & M.A. Curtis) Schwarze 1917  Note: Cited by Lentz as J.E. Humphrey in litt. (p. 262).

≡Marssonina ochroleuca (Berk. & M.A. Curtis) Lentz 1950  Note: The genus name Marssonina is a replacement name for Marssonia, also sometimes spelled Marsonia (Lentz 1950).

≡Gloeosporium ochroleucum (Berk. & M.A. Curtis) Ellis & Everh. 1885  Note: Original spelling.

  Variant spelling Gloeosporium ochroleuca (Berk. & M.A. Curtis) Ellis & Everh. 1885

= Cryptosporium epiphyllum Cooke & Ellis 1878  Note: Listed by Ellis & Everhart (1885) as a synonym of Gloeosporium ochroleuca.

Notes: Seymour (1929) also listed Septogloeum ochroleucum (B. & C.) Dearness and Phyllosticta ochroleuca (B. & C.) Pk. as synonyms, but no record of valid publication of these names could be found.

Distribution: North America (Canada, USA).

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Castanea spp. (Fagaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Lentz, P.L. 1950. The genus Marssonina on Quercus and Castanea. Mycologia 42: 259-264.

von Arx, J.A. 1957. Revision der zu Gloeosporium Gestellten Pilze. N. V. Noord-Hollandsche Uitgevers Maatschappij, -- pages.
