Discosia artocreas (Tode) Fr. 1849 (Ascomycetes, Incertae sedis)

≡ Sphaeria artocreas Tode 1791

= Phacidium platani Schwein. 1834

= Discosia syzygii Mhaskar & V.G. Rao 1976

= Discosia virginiana Thüm. 1882

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Leaves and occasionally other plant parts.

Disease Note: Weak leaf spot pathogen and saprobe.

Host: Numerous hosts.

Supporting Literature:
