Nomenclature data for Cryptostictis cupressi

Seiridium cupressi (Guba) Boesew. 1983 (Ascomycetes, Xylariales)

≡ Cryptostictis cupressi Guba 1961

= Rhynchosphaeria cupressi Nattrass, C. Booth & B. Sutton 1963  Note: From Lepteutypa cupressi - From Seiridium unicorne - Krokene et al. (2004) treat Seiridium unicorne and Seiridium cupressi as two very closely related, but separate species with the latter being much more pathogenic. S. cupressi was originally given as the ananmorph name.

    ≡ Lepteutypa cupressi (Nattrass, C. Booth & B. Sutton) H.J. Swart 1973

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: On living leaves and wood.

Disease Note: Cypress canker

Host: Primarily on Cupressaceae.
