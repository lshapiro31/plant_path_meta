Nomenclature data for Cryptostictis discosioides

Diploceras discosioides (Ellis & Everh.) Nag Raj 1993 (Ascomycetes, Xylariales)

≡Cryptostictis discosioides (Ellis & Everh.) B. Sutton 1963  Note: See Sutton 1963; apparently overlooked in the synonymies of Nag Raj 1993 and Sutton 1980.

≡Monochaetia discosioides (Ellis & Everh.) Sacc. 1906

≡Pestalotia discosioides Ellis & Everh. 1888

  Variant spelling Pestalozzia discosioides Ellis & Everh. 1888

≡Seimatosporium discosioides (Ellis & Everh.) Shoemaker 1964

= Coryneum microstictum var. folii Dearn. & Overh. 1928  Note: Corrected spelling (from folium, genitive folii).

  Variant spelling Coryneum microstictum var. foliae Dearn. & Overh. 1928  Note: Original spelling.

= Amphichaeta rosicola H.C. Greene 1959 [1958]

Notes: Nag Raj (1993) applied a narrow generic concept to Seimatosporium, reviving four other genera including Diploceras for species previously assigned to Seimatosporium, and then transferring Seimatosporium discosioides to Diploceras. This has not been universally accepted; some taxonomists apply a broad generic concept for Seimatosporium and retain this species as Seimatosporium discosioides (e.g., www.indexfungorum.org).

Distribution: North America (Canada, USA) (Nag Raj 1993). Also reported from Asia (India (Mathur 1979) and Korea (Cho & Shin 2004)).

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Rosa spp. (Rosaceae). Also reported on Geum triflorum and Pyrus communis (Rosaceae).
