Nomenclature data for Cryptostictis falcata

Seimatosporium falcatum (B. Sutton) Shoemaker 1964 (Ascomycetes, Xylariales)

≡ Cryptostictis falcata B. Sutton 1963

Distribution: Australia, New Zealand.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Eucalyptus spp. (Myrtaceae).
