Nomenclature data for Cryptostroma corticale

Cryptostroma corticale (Ellis & Everh.) P. Greg. & S. Waller 1951 (Ascomycetes, Incertae sedis)

≡ Coniosporium corticale Ellis & Everh. 1889

Notes: Based on molecular data per Koukol et al, this species belongs to the genus Biscogniauxia, though no new combination has yet been made.

Distribution: Temperate North America (introduced); Europe.

Substrate: Inner bark, wood.

Disease Note: Sooty bark disease; wilting, branch dieback. Attacks trees weakened by adverse weather conditions.

Host: Acer spp. (Sapindaceae).
