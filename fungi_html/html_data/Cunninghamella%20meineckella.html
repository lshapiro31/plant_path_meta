Nomenclature data for Cunninghamella meineckella

Heterobasidion annosum (Fr. : Fr.) Bref. 1888 (Basidiomycetes, Russulales)

≡ Polyporus annosus Fr. : Fr. 1821

≡Fomitopsis annosa (Fr. : Fr.) P. Karst. 1881

≡Fomes annosus (Fr. : Fr.) Cooke 1885

= Oedocephalum lineatum B.K. Bakshi 1950

= Cunninghamella meineckella A.J. Olson 1941  Note: From Spiniger meineckellus - Since this anamorph was described from American material, it almost certainly represents a different species. The taxonomic synonym listed here was described from Scotland.

    ≡ Spiniger meineckellus (A.J. Olson) Stalpers 1974

    ≡ Oedocephalum meineckellus (A.J. Olson) Donk 1971

Notes: A partial list of synonyms is presented here. In a braod sense, this represents a species complex. In a strict sense, this is a species restricted to Asia and Europe (Otrosina and Garbelotto 2010) i.e. it does not occur in North America.

Distribution: Widespread in Asia, Europe. Reports from North America are most likely H. irregulare or H. occidentale.

Substrate: Roots, wood.

Disease Note: Root and butt rot.

Host: Various hosts, particularly Abies spp. (Pinaceae).
