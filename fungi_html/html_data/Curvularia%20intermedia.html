Nomenclature data for Curvularia intermedia

Curvularia intermedia Boedijn 1933 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

= Cochliobolus intermedius R.R. Nelson 1961 [1960]

Distribution: Widespread, especially subtropical and tropical regions.

Substrate: On living leaves and flowers.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Multiple genera in few families, especially Poaceae.

Supporting Literature:

Pak, D., You, M.P., Lanoiselet, V., and Barbetti, M.J. 2017. Reservoir of cultivated rice pathogens in wild rice in Australia. Eur. J. Pl. Pathol. 147(2): 295-311.
