Nomenclature data for Cyclaneusma minus

Cyclaneusma minus (Butin) DiCosmo, Peredo & Minter 1983 (Ascomycetes, Rhytismatales)

≡ Naemacyclus minor Butin 1973

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Needles.

Disease Note: Needle cast; often an endophyte.

Host: Pinus spp. (Pinaceae).
