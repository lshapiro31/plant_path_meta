Nomenclature data for Cycloconium oleagineum

Venturia oleaginea (Castagne) Rossman & Crous 2015 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

≡ Cycloconium oleagineum Castagne 1845

      Variant spelling Cycloconium elaeoginum Castagne 1845

≡Spilocaea oleaginea (Castagne) S. Hughes 1953

  Variant spelling Spilocaea oleagina (Castagne) S. Hughes 1953

≡Fusicladium oleagineum (Castagne) Ritschel & U. Braun 2003

Distribution: Asia, Europe (Mediterranean), Africa, North America, South America, New Zealand.

Substrate: Living leaves, fruit stalks and fruits.

Disease Note: Peacock spot/olive scab.

Host: Olea europaea (Oleaceae).
