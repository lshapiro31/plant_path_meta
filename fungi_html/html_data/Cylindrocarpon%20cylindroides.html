Nomenclature data for Cylindrocarpon cylindroides

Cylindrocarpon cylindroides Wollenw. 1913 (Ascomycetes, Hypocreales)

Notes: According to Castlebury et al. (in press), the anamorph Cylindrocarpon cylindroides is closely related but distinct from Neonectria neomacrospora.

Distribution: Europe. Reports from North America (Canada) may be Neonectria neomacrospora.

Substrate: Wood, bark.

Disease Note: Canker.

Host: Abies spp. (Pinaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Booth, C. 1979. Nectria macrospora. C.M.I. Descr. Pathog. Fungi Bact. 623: 1-2.
