Nomenclature data for Cylindrocarpon obtusiusculum

Neonectria candida (Ehrenb.) Rossman, L. Lombard & Crous 2015 (Ascomycetes, Hypocreales)

≡ Fusarium candidum Ehrenb. 1818

≡Ramularia candida (Ehrenb.) Wollenw. 1913

≡Cylindrocarpon ehrenbergii Wollenw. 1926

= Fusarium eichleri Bres. 1903

  Variant spelling Fusarium eichlerii Bres. 1903

[= Cylindrocarpon magnusianum Wollenw. 1928 - illegitimate later homonym, not included in search]

= Fusarium obtusiusculum Sacc. 1881

  ≡Cylindrocarpon obtusiusculum (Sacc.) U. Braun 1993

    ≡ Fusarium oxysporum var. obtusiusculum (Sacc.) Cif. 1924

= Neonectria ramulariae Wollenw. 1917

    ≡ Nectria ramulariae (Wollenw.) E. Müll. 1962

Notes: From Cylindrocarpon ehrenbergii - Nom. nov.; the epithet candidum was preoccupied in Cylindrocarpon.

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Wood, soil, roots, tubers, fruit, seeds.

Disease Note: Canker, rots.

Host: Multiple plant families.
