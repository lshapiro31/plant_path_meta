Thelonectria jungneri (Henn.) P. Chaverri & C. Salgado 2011 (, )

≡ Nectria jungneri Henn. 1895

= Nectria azureo-ostiolata Yoshim. Doi 1977

= Nectria cinereopapillata Henn. & E. Nyman 1900

  Variant spelling Nectria cinereo-papillata Henn. & E. Nyman 1900

= Nectria eustoma Penz. & Sacc. 1898 [1897]

= Nectria leucocoma Starbäck 1899

= Nectria striatospora Zimm. 1901

= Cylindrocarpon victoriae Wollenw. 1928

Distribution: Pantropical.

Substrate: On bark of recently killed or dying trees.

Disease Note: Stem rot and dieback.

Host: Various hosts.
