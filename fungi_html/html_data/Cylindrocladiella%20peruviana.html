Nomenclature data for Cylindrocladiella peruviana

Cylindrocladiella peruviana (Bat., J.L. Bezerra & Herrera) Boesew. 1982 (Ascomycetes, Hypocreales)

≡ Cylindrocladium peruvianum Bat., J.L. Bezerra & M.M.P. Herrera 1965

Distribution: Widespread, especially subtropical to tropical regions.

Substrate: Trunk, roots.

Disease Note: Cutting and root rots.

Host: Multiple genera in multiple families.

Supporting Literature:

Crous, P.W. 2002. Taxonomy and pathology of Cylindrocladium (Calonectria) and allied genera. American Phytopathological Society, St. Paul, Minnesota : 278.

Koike, S.T., Bettiga, L.J., Nguyen, T.T., and Gubler, W.D. 2016. First report of Cylindrocladiella lageniformis and C. peruviana as grapevine pathogens in California. Pl. Dis. 100(8): 1783-1784.
