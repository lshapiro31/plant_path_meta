Nomenclature data for Cylindrocladium brassicae

Calonectria brassicae (Pamwar & Bohra) Lombard, M.J. Wingf. & Crous 2009 (Ascomycetes, Hypocreales)

≡ Cylindrocladium brassicae Panwar & Bohra 1975 [1974]

      Cylindrocladium gracile (Bugnic.) Boesew. 1982

= Cylindrocladium clavatum Hodges & L.C. May 1972

Notes: The teleomorph is unknown yet this fungus was not classified in a form genus by Lombard et al. (2009).

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Roots, seedlings.

Disease Note: Root rot. Affected trees have pitch-soaked roots with copious resinous exudates (Peerally 1974).

Host: Trees of various plant families.
