Nomenclature data for Cylindrocladium buxicola

Calonectria pseudonaviculata (Crous, J.Z. Groenew. & C.F. Hill) L. Lombard, M.J. Wingf. & Crous 2010 (Ascomycetes, Hypocreales)

≡ Cylindrocladium pseudonaviculatum Crous, J.Z. Groenew. & C.F. Hill 2002

= Cylindrocladium buxicola Henricot 2002

Distribution: Asia (Georgia, Iran), Europe, New Zealand, North America (Canada, USA).

Substrate: Leaves and shoots.

Disease Note: Blight.

Host: Buxus spp., Pachysandra spp., Sarcococca (Buxaceae).
