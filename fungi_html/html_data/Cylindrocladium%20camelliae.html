Nomenclature data for Cylindrocladium camelliae

Cylindrocladiella camelliae (Venkataram. & C.S.V. Ram) Boesew. 1982 (Ascomycetes, Hypocreales)

≡ Cylindrocladium camelliae Venkataram. & C.S.V. Ram 1961

Distribution: Widespread, especially subtropical and tropical regions.

Disease Note: Cutting and root rots, leaf spot.

Host: Mulitple genera in multiple families.

Supporting Literature:

Crous, P.W. 2002. Taxonomy and pathology of Cylindrocladium (Calonectria) and allied genera. American Phytopathological Society, St. Paul, Minnesota : 278.
