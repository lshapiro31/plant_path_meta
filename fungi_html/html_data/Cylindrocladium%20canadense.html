Nomenclature data for Cylindrocladium canadense

Calonectria canadiana L. Lombard, M.J. Wingf. & Crous 2010 (Ascomycetes, Hypocreales)

≡Cylindrocladium canadense J.C. Kang, Crous & C.L. Schoch 2001

[≡Calonectria canadensis (J.C. Kang, Crous & C.L. Schoch) L. Lombard, M.J. Wingf. & Crous 2010 - illegitimate later homonym, not included in search]  Note: Not (Ellis & Everh.) Berl. & Voglino 1886. Nom illeg.

Notes: This is a nom. nov since the epithet 'canadensis' was occupied in Calonectria.

Distribution: North America, South America (Brazil), Asia (Japan).

Substrate: All plant parts.

Disease Note: Root rot, leaf blight, defoliation, etc.

Host: Assorted genera in several families.
