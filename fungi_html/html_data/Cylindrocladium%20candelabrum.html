Nomenclature data for Cylindrocladium candelabrum

Calonectria candelabrum (Viégas) Rossman, L. Lombard & Crous 2015 (Ascomycetes, Hypocreales)

≡ Cylindrocladium candelabrum Viégas 1946

= Calonectria scoparia Ribeiro & Matsuoka ex Peerally 1991

Distribution: Africa and South America (Brazil, Venezuela).

Substrate: Living or dead leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot, cutting and root rot.

Host: Annona sp. (Annonaceae), Eucalyptus sp., Luma sp. (Myrtaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Crous, P.W. 2002. Taxonomy and pathology of Cylindrocladium (Calonectria) and allied genera. American Phytopathological Society, St. Paul, Minnesota : 278.
