Nomenclature data for Cylindrocladium colhounii

Calonectria colhounii var. colhounii Peerally 1973 (Ascomycetes, Hypocreales)

≡Calonectria colhounii Peerally 1973  Note: See type variety.

= Cylindrocladium colhounii var. colhounii Peerally 1973

  ≡Cylindrocladium colhounii Peerally 1973  Note: See type variety.

Distribution: Africa (Mauritius, South Africa), Asia (Thailand, Japan, India), Australia, Central America (Costa Rica, Colombia), North America (USA).

Substrate: Decaying, leathery leaves, twigs, cone scales.

Disease Note: A common but secondary foliage disease; leaf spot (Peerally 1974).

Host: Trees of various plant families.
