Nomenclature data for Cylindrocladium heptaseptatum

Calonectria hurae (Linder & Whetzel) L. Lombard, M.J. Wingf. & Crous 2010 (Ascomycetes, Hypocreales)

≡ Cercosporella hurae Linder & Whetzel 1937  Note: Anamorphic type.

≡Cylindrocladiopsis hurae (Linder & Whetzel) U. Braun 1994

≡Cylindrocladium hurae (Linder & Whetzel) Crous 2002

= Cylindrocladium heptaseptatum Sobers, Alfieri & Knauss 1975

= Cylindrocladiopsis lagerstroemiae J.M. Yen 1979  Note: As 'lagerstraemiae'.

Distribution: Widespread in subtropical to tropical Asia and North and South America.

Substrate: Living leaves, stems.

Disease Note: Leaf spot, stem lesions.

Host: Genera in multiple families.

Supporting Literature:
