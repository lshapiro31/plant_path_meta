Nomenclature data for Cylindrocladium parasiticum

Calonectria ilicicola Boedijn & Reitsma 1950 (Ascomycetes, Hypocreales)

= Cylindrocladium parasiticum Crous, M.J. Wingf. & Alfenas 1993

= Calonectria theae var. crotalariae Loos 1950

    ≡ Calonectria crotalariae (Loos) D.K. Bell & Sobers 1966

Distribution: Widespread especially Asia, North and South America.

Substrate: All plant parts.

Disease Note: Blight, crown rot, damping off, leaf spot, peanut peg, pod, and root necrosis, etc.

Host: Multiple genera in multiple families.
