Nomenclature data for Cylindrocladium parvum

Cylindrocladiella parva (P.J. Anderson) Boesew. 1982 (Ascomycetes, Hypocreales)

≡ Cylindrocladium parvum P.J. Anderson 1919

Notes: Many incorrect literature reports exist (Crous 2002).

Distribution: Widespread, especially subtropical to tropical regions.

Substrate: Leaves, shoots, roots.

Disease Note: Damping off, seedling blight, root rot.

Host: Multiple genera in multiple families.

Supporting Literature:

Brielmaier-Liebetanz, U., Wagner, S., and Werres, S. 2013. First Report of Dieback on Euonymus fortunei Caused by Cylindrocladiella parva in Germany. Pl. Dis. 97: 1120.
