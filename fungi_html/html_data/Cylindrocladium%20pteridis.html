Nomenclature data for Cylindrocladium pteridis

Calonectria pteridis Crous, M.J. Wingf. & Alfenas 1993 (Ascomycetes, Hypocreales)

= Cylindrocladium pteridis F.A. Wolf 1926

Distribution: Widespread, especially subtropical to tropical regions.

Substrate: Leaves and stems.

Disease Note: Leaf spot, stalk and root rot.

Host: Multiple genera in multiple families.

Supporting Literature:
