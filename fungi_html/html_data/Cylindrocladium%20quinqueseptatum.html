Nomenclature data for Cylindrocladium quinqueseptatum

Calonectria reteaudii (Bugnic.) C. Booth 1966 (Ascomycetes, Hypocreales)

≡ Neonectria reteaudii Bugnic. 1939

= Cylindrocladium quinqueseptatum Boedijn & Reitsma 1950

= Cylindrocarpon reteaudii Bugnic. 1939

    ≡ Cylindrocladium reteaudii (Bugnic.) Boesew. 1982

Distribution: Africa (Madagascar, Mauritius), Asia, Europe, Oceania.

Disease Note: Blights, damping-off, dieback, leaf spot, etc.

Host: Multiple genera in multiple families.

Supporting Literature:
