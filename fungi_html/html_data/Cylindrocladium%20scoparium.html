Nomenclature data for Cylindrocladium scoparium

Calonectria cylindrospora (Ellis & Everh.) Rossman, L.Lombard & Crous 2015 (Ascomycetes, Hypocreales)

≡ Diplocladium cylindrosporium Ellis & Everh. 1900

= Cylindrocladium ellipticum Alfieri, C.P. Seym. & Sobers 1970

= Calonectria morganii Crous, Alfenas & M.J. Wingf. 1993

= Cylindrocladium pithecolobii Petch 1917

= Cylindrocladium scoparium Morg. 1892

Notes: Use of oldest epithet in Calonectria blocked by Calonectria scoparia Ribeiro & Matsuoka ex Peerally (1991).

Distribution: Widespread, but distribution obscured by taxonomic confusion.

Substrate: All plant parts.

Disease Note: Blight, cankers, damping off, leaf spot, rots, etc.

Host: Multiple genera in multiple families.
