Nomenclature data for Cylindrosporella betulae-papyriferae

Ophiognomonia intermedia (Rehm) Sogonov 2008 (Ascomycetes, Diaporthales)

≡ Gnomonia intermedia Rehm 1908

= Gloeosporium betulae Westend. 1854

    ≡ Discula betulae (Westend.) Pennycook 2007

= Gloeosporium betulae-papyriferae Dearn. & Overh. 1924

  ≡Cylindrosporella betulae-papyriferae (Dearn. & Overh.) Arx 1956

Notes: This name is proposed for conservation over Gloeosporium betulae.

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Anthracnose.

Host: Betula spp. (Betulaceae).
