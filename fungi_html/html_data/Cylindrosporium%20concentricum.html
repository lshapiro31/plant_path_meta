Nomenclature data for Cylindrosporium concentricum

Pyrenopeziza brassicae B. Sutton & Rawl. 1979 [1978] (Ascomycetes, Helotiales)

= Cylindrosporium concentricum Grev. 1822

    ≡ Gloeosporium concentricum (Grev.) Berk. & Broome 1850

    ≡ Cylindrodochium concentricum (Grev.) Bonord.

Notes: From Cylindrosporium concentricum - Type of the genus (currently unispecific). The name P. brassicae will be proposed for conservation.

Distribution: Europe (the Netherlands, UK), Australasia. From Cylindrosporium concentricum - Europe (UK, the Netherlands), Australasia.

Substrate: Leaves, seed-borne. From Cylindrosporium concentricum - Leaves. Seed-borne.

Disease Note: Light leaf spot. From Cylindrosporium concentricum - Light leaf spot. Minor crop losses previous to 1970, but this fungus caused severe epidemics during the 1970s.

Host: Brassica spp. (Brassicaceae). From Cylindrosporium concentricum - Brassica spp. (Brassicaceae).
