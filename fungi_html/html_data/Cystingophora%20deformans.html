Notes: According to Cummins (Rust fungi on legumes and composites in North America. p. 340. 1978), this fungus does not occur in North America. The aecial stage of Spumula heteromorpha J.W. Baxter may have been confused with this species. From Aecidium hieronymi - Aecial anamorph
