Nomenclature data for Cytospora abietis

Cytospora abietis Sacc. 1884 (Ascomycetes, Diaporthales)

= Valsa abietis Fr. : Fr. 1849

Distribution: Circumboreal on conifers.

Substrate: Woody plant parts.

Disease Note: Canker, dieback.

Host: Cupressaceae, Pinaceae.
