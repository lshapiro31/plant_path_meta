Nomenclature data for Cytospora eucalypticola

Cytospora eucalypticola Van der Westh. 1965 (Ascomycetes, Diaporthales)

= Valsa fabianae G.C. Adams, M.J. Wingf. & Jol. Roux 2005

Notes: From Valsa fabianae - Previously considered to be Valsa ceratosperma.

Distribution: Australia, Africa (Uganda, South Africa).

Substrate: Bark of living and fallen branches.

Disease Note: Canker.

Host: Eucalyptus spp. (Myrtaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Adams, G.C., Wingfield, M.J., Common, R., and Roux, J. 2005. Phylogenetic relationships and morphology of Cytospora species and related teleomorphs (Ascomycota, Diaporthales, Valsaceae) from Eucalyptus. Stud. Mycol. 52: 1-144.
