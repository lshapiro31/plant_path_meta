Nomenclature data for Cytospora kunzei

Cytospora kunzei Sacc. 1884 (Ascomycetes, Diaporthales)

≡Leucocytospora kunzei (Sacc.) Z. Urb. 1958

= Sphaeria kunzei Fr. 1823

    ≡ Leucostoma kunzei (Fr.) Munk 1955 [1954]

    ≡ Valsa kunzei (Fr.) Fr. 1846

Distribution: Northern temperate regions.

Substrate: Woody plant parts.

Disease Note: Basal stem rot, canker.

Host: Primarily on Cupressaceae and Pinaceae.
