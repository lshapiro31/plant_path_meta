Nomenclature data for Cytospora personata

Cytospora personata (Fr. : Fr.) Sacc. 1884 (Ascomycetes, Diaporthales)

≡ Sphaeria personata Fr. : Fr. 1818

≡Leucocytospora personata (Fr. : Fr.) Höhn. 1917

= Valsa auerswaldii Nitschke 1870

    ≡ Leucostoma auerswaldii (Nitschke) Höhn. 1928

= Valsa subclypeata Cooke & Peck 1875

Notes: Sexual state connection based on Gaiova (1985). Gvritishvili (1982) listed C. personata as a synonym of Cytospora leucostoma. Synonymy based on Barr et al. (1986); closely related but possibly distinct based on ITS. Barr et al. (1986) considered V. subclypeata a synonym of this species.

Distribution: Asia, Europe, North America.

Substrate: Bark.

Disease Note: Bark necrosis. This is the most aggressive pathogen of the four Cytospora spp. found on apples in Germany (Kastirr & Ficke 1984).

Host: Betula spp. (Betulaceae), Malus (Rosaceae), Platanus (Platanaceae).
