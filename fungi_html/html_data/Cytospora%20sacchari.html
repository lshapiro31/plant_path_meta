Cytospora sacchari E.J. Butler 1906 (Ascomycetes, Diaporthales)

Notes: Sheath rot of sugarcane (Sivanesan. C.M.I. Descr. 777. 1983).

Distribution: Worldwide in sugarcane-growing regions.
