Nomenclature data for Cytospora schulzeri

Cytospora schulzeri Sacc. & P. Syd. 1899 (Ascomycetes, Diaporthales)

= Valsa malicola Z. Urb. 1956

Distribution: Asia, Europe, North America.

Substrate: Dead or dying twigs.

Disease Note: Associated with dieback.

Host: Malus spp. and other Rosaceae.

Supporting Literature:

Adams, G.C., Roux, J., and Wingfield, M.J. 2006. Cytospora species (Ascomycota, Diaporthales, Valsaceae): introduced and native pathogens of trees in South Africa. Australasian Plant Disease Notes 35: 521-548.

Spielman, L.J. 1985. A monograph of Valsa on hardwoods in North America. Canad. J. Bot. 63: 1355-1378.
