Nomenclature data for Dactuliophora glycines

Coniothyrium glycines (R.B. Stewart) Verkley & Gruyter 2012 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

≡ Pyrenochaeta glycines R.B. Stewart 1957

≡Dactuliochaeta glycines (R.B. Stewart) G.L. Hartm. & J.B. Sinclair 1988

= Dactuliophora glycines C.L. Leakey 1946

Notes: Phoma glycinicola Gruyter & Boerema nom. illegit (Art 36.1).

Distribution: Africa (Ethiopia, Zambia, Zimbabwe) . Asian reports questionable.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot. Leaf abscission in susceptible varieties of soybean.

Host: Glycine spp. (Fabaceae).
