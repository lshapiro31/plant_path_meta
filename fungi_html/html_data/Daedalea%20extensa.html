Nomenclature data for Daedalea extensa

Wolfiporia cocos (F.A. Wolf) Ryvarden & Gilb. 1984 (Basidiomycetes, Polyporales)

≡ Poria cocos F.A. Wolf 1922

= Sclerotium cocos Schwein. 1822

    ≡ Macrohyporia cocos (Schwein.) I. Johansen & Ryvarden 1979

    ≡ Pachyma cocos (Schwein.) Fr. 1822

= Daedalea extensa Peck 1891  Note: From Wolfiporia extensa - Oldest epithet provided by Sclerotium cocos Schwein. (1822) but new combination in Wolfiporia blocked by Wolfiporia cocos (F.A. Wolf) Ryvvarden & Gilb. (1984).

    ≡ Wolfiporia extensa (Peck) Ginns 1984

    ≡ Macrohyporia extensa (Peck) Ginns & J. Lowe 1983

Notes: The basionym Poria cocos was conserved over Daedalea extensa as proposed by Redhead & Ginns (Taxon 55: 1027. 2006).

Distribution: Asia; North America.

Substrate: Roots and wood.

Disease Note: Wood-rotter, root rot, produces large, edible sclerotia called tuckahoe.

Host: Various hosts.

Supporting Literature:

Davidson, R.W., and Campbell, W.A. 1954. Poria cocos, a widely distributed wood-rotting fungus. Mycologia 46: 234-237.
