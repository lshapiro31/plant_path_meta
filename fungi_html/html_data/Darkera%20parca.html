Darkera parca H.S. Whitney, J. Reid & Piroz. 1975 (Ascomycetes, Rhytismatales)

≡ Sphaeropsis parca Berk. & Broome 1850

≡Sirococcus parcus (Berk. & Broome) M. Morlet 1973

  Variant spelling Sirococcus parca (Berk. & Broome) M. Morlet 1973

Distribution: Western North America; Europe.

Substrate: Living needles.

Disease Note: Needle blight, needle cast.

Host: Pinaceae.
