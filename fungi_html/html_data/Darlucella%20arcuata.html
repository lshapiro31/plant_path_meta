Notes: Parasitic on rusts; uredospores are enclosed by the stromata and are totally demolished. Eriksson (Bot. Not. 119:33. 1966) provided a detailed account of the taxonomy and nomenclature of this fungus, with notes on its ecology and distribution. From Dothidea genistalis - Also sanctioned, but Sphaeria filum has priority. From Ascochyta dianthi - Not (Alb. & Schwein.) Berk. 1860 nor Lasch. From Diplodia punctata - Author changed from (Lev.) Zambett. 1955, see Sutton 1980. Lev. 1840 not in collection. From Diplodothiorella ladurneri - Originally published as sadurneri. From Ascochyta solidaginis - Not Ascochyta solidaginis H.C. Green 1960 = Ascochyta greenii. From Sphaerellopsis filum - Sutton (1980) and Nag Raj (1993) include an extensive list of synonyms; we include all synonyms listed by either author.

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Rust pustules.

Disease Note: Hyperparasite, growing on parasitic rusts.

Host: Uredinales on plant hosts from multiple families.
