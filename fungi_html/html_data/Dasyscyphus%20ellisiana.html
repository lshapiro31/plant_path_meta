Nomenclature data for Dasyscyphus ellisiana

Dasyscyphus ellisiana (Rehm) Sacc. (Ascomycetes, Helotiales)

    Variant spelling Dasyscypha ellisiana (Rehm) Sacc.

≡ Peziza ellisiana Rehm 1876

≡Lachnella ellisiana (Rehm) Seaver 1951

Distribution: Central and eastern United States.

Substrate: On bark of various conifers.

Disease Note: Twig canker.
