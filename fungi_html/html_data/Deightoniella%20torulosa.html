Nomenclature data for Deightoniella torulosa

Deightoniella torulosa (Syd.) M.B. Ellis 1957 (Ascomycetes, Incertae sedis)

≡ Brachysporium torulosum Syd. 1909

≡Helminthosporium torulosum (Syd.) S.F. Ashby 1932

= Cercospora musarum S.F. Ashby 1913

Distribution: Widespread in banana growing regions.

Substrate: Fruits, leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot, speckle (Swamp Spot), black tip, rot of pseudostem.

Host: Musa spp. (Musaceae). Rare reports from a few other plant taxa.
