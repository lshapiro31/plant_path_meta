Nomenclature data for Dendrophagus colossus

Ganoderma colossus (Fr.) C. Baker 1918 (Basidiomycetes, Polyporales)

≡ Polyporus colossus Fr. 1851

≡Dendrophagus colossus (Fr.) Murrill 1905

≡Tomophagus colossus (Fr.) Murrill 1905

Distribution: Subtropical and tropical regions.

Substrate: Base and upper trunk of trees.

Disease Note: White rot, root rot, yellowing, dieback.

Host: Numerous tree hosts.
