Nomenclature data for Dendryphion penicillatum

Alternaria penicillata (Corda) Woudenb. & Crous 2013 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

≡ Brachycladium penicillatum Corda 1838

≡Dendryphion penicillatum (Corda) Fr. 1849

= Cucurbitaria papaveracea De Not. 1863

    ≡ Pleospora papaveracea (De Not.) Sacc. 1883

    ≡ Crivellia papaveracea (De Not.) Inderb. & Shoemaker 2006

[= Sphaeria papaveris Tul. & C. Tul. 1861 - illegitimate later homonym, not included in search]

Distribution: Widespread.

Disease Note: Leaf blight.

Host: Papaver spp. (Papaveraceae).
