Nomenclature data for Desmella aneimiae

Desmella aneimiae Syd. 1919 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

Distribution: Tropical New World, Australia.

Substrate: Lower leaf surface.

Disease Note: Rust.

Host: Nephrolepis spp. (Nephrolepidaceae) and other pteridophytes.

Supporting Literature:

McTaggart, A.R., Geering, A.D.W., and Shivas, R.G. 2014. Uredinopsis pteridis and Desmella aneimiae, the first rust fungi (Pucciniales) reported on ferns (Pteridophyta) in Australia. Australasian Plant Disease Notes : 1-4.
