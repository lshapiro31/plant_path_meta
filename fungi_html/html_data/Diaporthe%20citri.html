Nomenclature data for Diaporthe citri

Diaporthe citri (H.S. Fawc.) F.A. Wolf 1926 (Ascomycetes, Diaporthales)

≡ Phomopsis citri H.S. Fawc. non (Sacc.) Traverso & Spessa, hom. illeg. 1912

= Phomopsis caribaea W.T. Horne 1922

= Diaporthe citrincola Rehm 1914

Notes: Phomopsis citri H.S. Fawc. nom. conserv. prop non Phomopsis citri (Sacc.) Traverso & Spessa (Udayanga, 2014).

Distribution: Worldwide in citrus-growing regions.

Substrate: Stems, fruit, leaves.

Disease Note: Melanose, stem end rot.

Host: Citrus spp., Fortunella japonica, Poncirus trifoliata (Rutaceae).
