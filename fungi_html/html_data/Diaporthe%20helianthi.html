Nomenclature data for Diaporthe helianthi

Diaporthe helianthi Munt.-Cvetk., Mihaljc. & M. Petrov 1981 (Ascomycetes, Diaporthales)

= Phomopsis helianthi Munt.-Cvetk., Mihaljc. & M. Petrov 1981

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Living leaves and stems.

Disease Note: Stem canker, grey spot disease.

Host: Helianthus, Arctium lappa, Xanthium italicum, X. strumarium (Asteraceae).
