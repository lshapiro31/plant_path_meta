Nomenclature data for Diaporthe phaseolorum

Diaporthe phaseolorum (Cooke & Ellis) Sacc. 1882 (Ascomycetes, Diaporthales)

≡ Sphaeria phaseolorum Cooke & Ellis 1878

Distribution: Caribbean (Cuba, Jamaica), North America (USA), Asia (China), Europe.

Substrate: Stems, leaves, pods.

Disease Note: Stem canker, blight, pod rot.

Host: Phaseolus spp. (Fabaceae) and other diverse hosts.
