Nomenclature data for Diaporthe rudis

Diaporthe rudis (Fr. : Fr.) Nitschke 1870 (Ascomycetes, Diaporthales)

≡ Sphaeria rudis Fr. : Fr. 1828

≡Aglaospora rudis (Fr. : Fr.) Tul. & C. Tul. 1863

≡Phomopsis rudis (Fr. : Fr.) Höhn. 1906

≡Rabenhorstia rudis (Fr. : Fr.) Fr. 1849

= Valsa faginea Curr. 1858

    ≡ Diaporthe faginea (Curr.) Sacc. 1882

= Diaporthe macrostoma Nitschke 1870

= Diaporthe medusaea Nitschke 1870

= Diaporthe silvestris Sacc. & Berl. 1885

= Diaporthe viticola Nitschke 1870

Distribution: Africa (South Africa), Australia, Europe, New Zealand, North America (Canada, USA).

Substrate: Bark of branches and twigs, also reported on leaves.

Disease Note: Cane spot, stem end rot.

Host: A wide host range of dicots.
