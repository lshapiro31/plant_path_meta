Nomenclature data for Dicaeoma nakanishikii

Puccinia nakanishikii Dietel 1904 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

≡Dicaeoma nakanishikii (Dietel) Syd. 1922

Distribution: HI; Africa; Australia; Asia, North America (USA: CA, FL).

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Rust/leaf blight.

Host: Cymbopogon citratus and other Cymbopogon spp., Botriochloa spp., Sorghum spp. (Poaceae).
