Nomenclature data for Dicaeoma ormosiae

Dicheirinia ormosiae (Arthur) Cummins 1935 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

≡ Puccinia ormosiae Arthur 1917

≡Dicaeoma ormosiae (Arthur) Arthur 1920

Distribution: North and South America.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Rust.

Host: Ormosia spp. (Fabaceae).
