Nomenclature data for Dichaena quercina

Ascodichaena rugosa Butin 1977 (Ascomycetes, Rhytismatales)

≡ Lichen rugosus L. 1753

      Dichaena rugosa (L. : Fr.) Fr. 1849

= Opegrapha quercina Pers. 1794  Note: From Polymorphum quercinum - Beech bark fungus (Hawksworth. Taxon 32:212. 1983).

    ≡ Polymorphum quercinum (Pers.) Chev. 1822

  ≡Dichaena quercina (Pers.) Sacc. 1883

Notes: A bark parasite. Hawksworth (Taxon 32:212. 1983).

Distribution: Northern North America; Europe.

Substrate: On bark of trunck.

Disease Note: A weak parasite.

Host: On Fagaceae.

Supporting Literature:

Minter, D.W., and Cannon, P.F. 2002. Ascodichaena rugosa. I.M.I. Descr. Fungi Bact. 1471: 1-4.
