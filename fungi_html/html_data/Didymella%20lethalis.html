Nomenclature data for Didymella lethalis

Didymella lethalis (R. Stone) Sivan. 1984 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

≡ Mycosphaerella lethalis R. Stone 1912

≡Didymellina lethalis (R. Stone) Höhn. 1918

≡Sphaerella lethalis (R. Stone) Trotter 1928

= Ascochyta lethalis Ellis & Barthol. 1903  Note: From Peyronellaea lethalis - Ascochyta viciae Lib. 1837 would be an earlier name for this species if the two are synonymous as suggested by Melnik (1977). The epithet 'meliloti' has priority in Peyronellaea.

    ≡ Peyronellaea lethalis (Ellis & Barthol.) Aveskamp, Gruyter & Verkley 2010

= Gloeosporium meliloti Trel. 1885  Note: Name should be based on G. meliloti.

  [≡ Ascochyta meliloti (Trel.) Davis 1919 - illegitimate later homonym, not included in search]  Note: Not Trusova 1915. Nom. illeg.

Distribution: Most reports from North America.

Substrate: Stems.

Disease Note: Black stem.

Host: Melilotus spp. (Fabaceae).
