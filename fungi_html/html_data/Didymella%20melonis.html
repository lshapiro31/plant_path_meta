Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Seed-borne, leaves, stems, fruits.

Disease Note: Leaf spot, stem canker, vine wilt, black fruit rot. (Gummy stem blight).

Host: Cucurbitaceae, occasionally also on Solanaceae, Caricaceae, Primulaceae.
