Nomenclature data for Didymella strobiligena

Delphinella strobiligena (Desm.) Sacc. ex Clem. & Shear 1931 (Ascomycetes, Dothideales)

≡ Sphaeria strobiligena Desm. 1846

≡Didymella strobiligena (Desm.) Sacc. 1882

≡Hariotia strobiligena (Desm.) P. Karst. 1889

≡Pleoglonis strobiligena (Desm.) Clem. 1909

≡Glonium strobiligenum (Desm.) Mouton 1889

Distribution: Europe (France, Greece).

Substrate: Twigs, wood, needles, cone scales.

Disease Note: Parasitic; no disease symptoms specified.

Host: Pinus halepensis, Pinus sp. (Pinaceae).
