Nomenclature data for Didymellina dianthi

Cladosporium echinulatum (Berk.) G.A. De Vries 1952 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Helminthosporium echinulatum Berk. 1870

≡Heterosporium echinulatum (Berk.) Cooke 1877

= Didymellina dianthi C.C. Burt 1936  Note:

    ≡ Mycosphaerella dianthi (C.C. Burt) Jørst. 1945

= Heterosporium dianthi Sacc. & Roum. 1881

= Heterosporium echinulatum var. dianthi Losa 1952

= Helminthosporium exasperatum Berk. & Broome 1873

    ≡ Heterosporium exasperatum (Berk. & Broome) Cooke 1888

Distribution: Worldwide.

Substrate: On leaves and flowers.

Disease Note: Leaf spot of carnation (David. C.M.I. Descr. 957. 1988).

Host: Dianthus and other genera of the Caryophyllaceae.
