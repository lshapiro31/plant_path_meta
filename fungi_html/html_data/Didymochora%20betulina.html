Nomenclature data for Didymochora betulina

≡Rehmiodothis betulina (Fr.) Arx 1954

= Didymochora betulina Höhn. 1918

= Dothidella betulina var. yakutatiana Sacc. & Scalia 1904

Distribution: North America; northern Europe.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Tar spot.

Host: Betula spp. (Betulaceae).
