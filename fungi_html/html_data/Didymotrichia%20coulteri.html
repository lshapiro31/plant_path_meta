Nomenclature data for Didymotrichia coulteri

Herpotrichia coulteri (Peck) S.K. Bose 1961 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

≡ Sphaeria coulteri Peck 1872

≡Didymotrichia coulteri (Peck) Berl. 1892

≡Enchnosphaeria coulteri (Peck) Sacc. 1883

≡Lasiosphaeria coulteri (Peck) Ellis & Everh. 1892

≡Neopeckia coulteri (Peck) Sacc. 1883

= Lasiosphaeria acicola Cooke 1880

    ≡ Amphisphaeria acicola (Cooke) Sacc. 1882

Notes: Often classified in Neopeckia, but generic status requires taxonomic study.

Distribution: Northern hemisphere.

Disease Note: Snow mold.

Host: Various conifers.
