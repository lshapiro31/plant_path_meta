Nomenclature data for Digitosporium piniphilum

Crumenulopsis sororia (P. Karst.) J.W. Groves 1969 (Ascomycetes, Helotiales)

≡ Crumenula sororia P. Karst. 1871

≡Godronia sororia (P. Karst.) P. Karst. 1885

= Digitosporium piniphilum Gremmen 1953

Distribution: Europe. Records from North America not genuine (Groves 1969).

Substrate: On bark and cones.

Disease Note: Pine dieback.

Host: Pinus spp. (Pinaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Groves, J.W. 1969. Crumenulopsis, a new name to replace Crumenula Rehm. Canad. J. Bot. 47: 47-51.

Sutton, B.C. 1980. The Coelomycetes. Fungi Imperfecti with Pycnidia, Acervuli and Stromata. Commonwealth Mycological Institute, Kew, Surrey, England, 696 pages.
