Nomenclature data for Diorchidium tricholaenae

Puccinia levis var. tricholaenae (Syd. & P. Syd.) Ramachar & Cummins 1965 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

≡ Diorchidium tricholaenae Syd. & P. Syd. 1912

≡Puccinia tricholaenae (Syd. & P. Syd.) T.S. Ramakr. & K. Ramakr. 1948

= Uromyces tricholenae Gonz. Frag. & Cif. 1925

Distribution: Circumglobal in warmer regions.

Disease Note: Rust.

Host: Rhynchelytrum repens (Poaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Cummins, G.B. 1971. The Rust Fungi of Cereals, Grasses and Bamboos. Springer-Verlag, New York, 570 pages.
