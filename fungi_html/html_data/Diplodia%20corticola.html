Nomenclature data for Diplodia corticola

Diplodia corticola A.J.L. Phillips, A. Alves & J. Luque 2004 (Ascomycetes, Dothideales)

= Botryosphaeria corticola A.J.L. Phillips, A. Alves & J. Luque 2004

Distribution: Southern Europe, Africa (Tunisia), North America (USA: CA, FL, ME, TX, WV).

Substrate: Branches.

Disease Note: Cankers, dieback.

Host: Vitis vinifera (Vitaceae), Quercus spp. (Fagaceae).
