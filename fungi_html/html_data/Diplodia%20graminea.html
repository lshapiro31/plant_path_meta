Nomenclature data for Diplodia graminea

Ascochyta graminea var. graminea (Sacc.) R. Sprague & Aar.G. Johnson 1950 (Ascomycetes, Incertae sedis)

≡ Diplodia graminea Sacc. 1881

≡Ascochyta graminea (Sacc.) R. Sprague & Aar.G. Johnson 1950

≡Diplodina graminea (Sacc.) Sacc. 1884

Notes: Punithalingam (1979) referred most of Sprague's American specimens to Ascochyta graminea var. minuscula.

Distribution: Africa (Ethiopia, Kenya). Also reported from Europe (Bulgaria). Reports from North America (USA) may be Ascochyta graminea var. minuscula.

Substrate: Culms, leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot. Not considered by Punithalingam (1979) to be a serious pathogen.

Host: Cynodon dactylon (Poaceae). Reports on other hosts (particularly Bouteloua spp.) may be Ascochyta graminea var. minuscula.

Supporting Literature:

Punithalingam, E. 1979. Graminicolous Ascochyta species. Mycol. Pap. 142: 1-214.
