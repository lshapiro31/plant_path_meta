Nomenclature data for Diplodia porosum

Sphaeropsis porosa (Van Niekerk & Crous) A.J.L. Phillips & A. Alves 2013 (Ascomycetes, Dothideales)

≡ Diplodia porosum Van Niekerk & Crous 2004

= Phaeobotryosphaeria porosa (Van Niekerk & Crous) Crous & A.J.L. Phillips 2008

Distribution: Africa (South Africa).

Substrate: Pruning debris.

Disease Note: Potentially important pathogen based on innoculation tests.

Host: Vitis vinifera (Vitaceae).
