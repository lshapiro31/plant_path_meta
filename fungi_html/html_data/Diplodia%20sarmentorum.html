Nomenclature data for Diplodia sarmentorum

Dothiorella sarmentorum (Fr. : Fr.) A.J.L. Phillips, A. Alves & J. Luque 2005 (Ascomycetes, Dothideales)

≡ Sphaeria sarmentorum Fr. : Fr. 1818

≡Diplodia sarmentorum (Fr. : Fr.) Fr. 1849

= Diplodia pruni Fuckel 1870 [1869]

= Botryosphaeria sarmentorum A.J.L. Phillips, A. Alves & J. Luque 2005

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Branches, twigs.

Disease Note: Saprobic or parasitic.

Host: Several hosts in several families.
