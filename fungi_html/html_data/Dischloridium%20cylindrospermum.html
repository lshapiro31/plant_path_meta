Nomenclature data for Dischloridium cylindrospermum

Monilochaetes infuscans Ellis & Halst. 1890 (Ascomycetes, Incertae sedis)

= Dischloridium cylindrospermum S.K. Srivast. 1987 [1986]

Notes: Sometimes attributed as Ellis & Halst. ex Harter 1916.

Distribution: Widespread.

Disease Note: Sweet potato scurf.

Host: Ipomoea (Convolvulaceae).

Supporting Literature:
