= Scolecotrichum fraxini Pass. 1884

  Variant spelling Scolecotrichum fraxinii Pass.

  Variant spelling Scolicotrichum fraxini Pass.

Notes: From Fusicladium granulosum - In herb.

Distribution: Asia, Caucasus, Europe, North America (FL).

Substrate: Living leaves, petioles.

Disease Note: Withering of leaves, premature leaf fall.

Host: Ash, Fraxinus spp. (Oleaceae).
