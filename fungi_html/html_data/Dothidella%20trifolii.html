Nomenclature data for Dothidella trifolii

Polythrincium trifolii Kunze : Fr. 1817 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡Mycosphaerella killianii Petr. 1941

  Variant spelling Mycosphaerella killiani Petr. 1941

  Variant spelling Mycosphaerella killianti Petr. 1941

= Dothidella trifolii Bayliss-Elliott & Stansfield 1924

= Plowrightia trifolii Kill. 1923

    ≡ Cymadothea trifolii (Kill.) F.A. Wolf 1935

Notes: From Plowrightia trifolii - Often erroneously cited as (Pers.) Kill. based on anamorph Sphaeria trifolii Pers.:Fr. 1801 From Mycosphaerella killianii - Nom. nov.

Distribution: Temperate regions.

Substrate: On leaves.

Disease Note: Sooty blotch of clover.
