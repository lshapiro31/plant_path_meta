Nomenclature data for Dothidina palmicola

Coccostromopsis palmicola (Speg.) K.D. Hyde & P.F. Cannon 1999 (Ascomycetes, Phyllachorales)

≡ Auerswaldia palmicola Speg. 1885

≡Bagnisiopsis palmicola (Speg.) Petr. 1928

≡Coccostroma palmicola (Speg.) Arx & E. Müll. 1954

≡Dothidina palmicola (Speg.) Theiss. & Syd. 1915

≡Sphaerodothis palmicola (Speg.) Shear 1909

Distribution: Scattered reports in New World tropics.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Poorly defined leaf lesions.

Host: Arecaceae.

Supporting Literature:

Hyde, K.D., and Cannon, P.F. 1999. Fungi causing tar spots on palms. Mycol. Pap. 175: 1-114.
