Nomenclature data for Dothiorella ribis

Diaporthe strumella (Fr. : Fr.) Fuckel 1870 [1869] (Ascomycetes, Diaporthales)

≡ Sphaeria strumella Fr. : Fr. 1823

= Podosporium ribis Fuckel 1869  Note: From Dothiorella ribis - Phomopsis synanamorph.

  ≡Dothiorella ribis (Fuckel) Sacc. 1884

Distribution: Temperate North America; Europe.

Substrate: On woody limbs, especially of Ribes spp.

Disease Note: Canker, fruit rot.

Host: Primarily on Ribes.
