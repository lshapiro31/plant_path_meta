Nomenclature data for Drechslera australiensis

Curvularia australiensis (Bugnic. ex M.B. Ellis) Manamgoda, L. Cai & K.D. Hyde 2012 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

≡ Drechslera australiensis Bugnic. ex M.B. Ellis 1971

≡Bipolaris australiensis (Bugnic. ex M.B. Ellis) Tsuda & Ueyama 1981

Notes: Helminthosporium australiense Bugnic. 1956 nom. inval. Art 39.1 (no description). Drechslera australiensis Bugnic. ex Subram. & B.L. Jain 1966 (as 'australiense') nom. inval. Articles 39.1 and 40.1.; validated by Ellis (1971).

Distribution: HI, Asia, Africa, Australia.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot; seed-borne.

Host: Primarily on Poaceae.
