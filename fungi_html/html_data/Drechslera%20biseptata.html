Nomenclature data for Drechslera biseptata

Pyrenophora biseptata (Sacc. & Roum.) Crous 2013 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

≡ Helminthosporium biseptatum Sacc. & Roum. 1882

≡Drechslera biseptata (Sacc. & Roum.) M.J. Richardson & E.M. Fraser 1968

≡Marielliottia biseptata (Sacc. & Roum.) Shoemaker 1999

≡Brachysporium biseptatum (Sacc. & Roum.) Sacc. 1886

= Helminthosporium biforme E.W. Mason & S. Hughes 1948

    ≡ Drechslera biformis (E.W. Mason & S. Hughes) Subram. & B.L. Jain 1966

Notes: From Marielliottia biseptata - From Helminthosporium biseptatum - as 'Helmisporium'

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Leaves, seeds.

Disease Note: Leaf spot; also saprobic.

Host: Typically Poaceae, but also reports from a few other genera outside of this family.
