Nomenclature data for Drechslera brizae

Bipolaris brizae (Y. Nisik.) Shoemaker 1959 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

≡ Helminthosporium brizae Y. Nisik. 1939

≡Drechslera brizae (Y. Nisik.) Subram. & P.C. Jain 1966

Notes: Placement in Bipolaris is questionable.

Distribution: Africa (South Africa), Asia (Japan), Australia, Europe (Portugal), New Zealand, North America (USA: CA).

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf lesions.

Host: Briza spp., Gastridium ventricosum (Poaceae).
