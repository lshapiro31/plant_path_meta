Nomenclature data for Drechslera bromi

Pyrenophora bromi (Died.) Drechsler 1923 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

≡ Pleospora bromi Died. 1903

= Helminthosporium bromi Died. 1903  Note: From Drechslera bromi - Leaf spot of Bromus and other grasses.

  ≡Drechslera bromi (Died.) Shoemaker 1959

Notes: Leaf spot of Bromus and other grasses.

Distribution: Temperate North America; Australia; Europe.

Substrate: On living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot, leaf blotch.

Host: On Bromus (Poaceae).
