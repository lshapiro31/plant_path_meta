Nomenclature data for Drechslera cactivora

Bipolaris cactivora (Petr.) Alcorn 1983 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

≡ Helminthosporium cactivorum Petr. 1931

≡Drechslera cactivora (Petr.) M.B. Ellis 1971

= Helminthosporium cactacearum Bongini 1932

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Flowers, fruit, stems.

Disease Note: Stem rot of cacti.

Host: Cactaceae and reports from other succulents.

Supporting Literature:

Manamgoda, D.S., Rossman, A.Y., Castlebury, L.A., Crous, P.W., Madrid, H., Chukeatirote, E., and Hyde, K.D. 2014. The genus Bipolaris. Stud. Mycol. 79: 221-288.
