Nomenclature data for Drechslera catenaria

Pyrenophora catenaria (Drechsler) Rossman & K.D. Hyde 2015 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

≡ Helminthosporium catenarium Drechsler 1923

≡Bipolaris catenaria (Drechsler) Somal 1975

≡Drechslera catenaria (Drechsler) Ito 1930

Distribution: Asia (India), Northern North America.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot, leaf bllight, crown rot.

Host: Poaceae.
