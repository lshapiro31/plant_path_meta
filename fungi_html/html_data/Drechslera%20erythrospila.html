Nomenclature data for Drechslera erythrospila

Pyrenophora erythrospila A.R. Paul 1972 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

= Helminthosporium erythrospilum Drechsler 1935

  ≡Drechslera erythrospila (Drechsler) Shoemaker 1959

Distribution: Temperate regions including Australasia.

Substrate: On living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Agrostis (Poaceae), Protea sp. (Proteaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Crous, P.W., Denman, S., Taylor, J.E., Swart, L., Bezuidenhout, C.M., Hoffman, L., Palm, M.E., and Groenewald, J.Z. 2013. Cultivation and disease of Proteaceae: Leucadendron, Leucospermum, and Protea: Second edition. CBS Biodivers. Ser. N/A: 360.
