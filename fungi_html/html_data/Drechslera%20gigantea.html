Nomenclature data for Drechslera gigantea

Drechslera gigantea S. Ito 1930 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

= Helminthosporium giganteum Heald & F.A. Wolf 1911  Note: The name H. giganteum is a later homonym of Renault & Roche 1898 amd this cannot be used.

Notes: Error in hostList - please email webmaster. See bottom of page.

Distribution: North America (USA, Mexico), Central and South America Asia (Japan).

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot, zonate eyespot.

Host: Poaceae, Salix (Salicaceae), Musa (Musaceae); reports from other hosts.
