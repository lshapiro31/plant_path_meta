Nomenclature data for Drechslera heveae

Bipolaris heveae (Petch) Arx 1987 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

≡ Helminthosporium heveae Petch 1906

≡Drechslera heveae (Petch) M.B. Ellis 1971

= Cochliobolus heveicola Tsukib. & W.H. Chung 2005

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Bird's eye spot of rubber.

Host: Restricted to Hevea brasiliensis (Euphorbiaceae).
