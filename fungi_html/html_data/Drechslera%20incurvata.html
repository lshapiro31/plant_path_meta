Nomenclature data for Drechslera incurvata

Bipolaris incurvata (C. Bernard) Alcorn 1983 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

≡ Helminthosporium incurvatum C. Bernard 1906

≡Drechslera incurvata (C. Bernard) M.B. Ellis 1971

Distribution: FL; Jamaica; Asia; Australia; Oceania (Seychelles).

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Cocos nucifera (Arecaceae).

Supporting Literature:
