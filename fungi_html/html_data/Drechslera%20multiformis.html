Notes: These authors follow Manamgoda et al. 2014.

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Living leaves, seedlings and roots.

Disease Note: Leat spot, seedling blight, and root rot.

Host: Primarily on Poaceae but also numerous and diverse other hosts.
