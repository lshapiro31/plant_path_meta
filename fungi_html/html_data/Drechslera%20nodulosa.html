Nomenclature data for Drechslera nodulosa

Curvularia nodulosa (Sacc.) Manamgoda, Rossman & K.D. Hyde 2014 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

≡ Helminthosporium nodulosum Berk. & M.A. Curtis ex Sacc. 1886

≡Bipolaris nodulosa (Sacc.) Shoemaker 1959

≡Drechslera nodulosa (Sacc.) Subram. & B.L. Jain 1966

= Helminthosporium leucostylum Drechsler 1923

    ≡ Bipolaris leucostyla (Drechsler) Shoemaker 1959

    ≡ Drechslera leucostyla (Drechsler) Subram. & B.L. Jain 1966

= Cochliobolus nodulosus Luttr. 1957

Notes: Helminthosporium nodulosum is a replacement name for the illegitimate Helminthosporium nodosum Berk. & M.A. Curtis 1875 (non Wallr. 1833).

Distribution: Subtropical and tropical regions.

Substrate: Leaves, seeds, inflorescences.

Disease Note: Leaf spot, seedling blight, foot rot, sooty inflorescences.

Host: Elusine spp., Hordeum vulgare, Eragrostis spp. (Poaceae).
