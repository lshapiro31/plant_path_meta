Nomenclature data for Drechslera oryzae

Bipolaris oryzae (Breda de Haan) Shoemaker 1959 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

≡ Helminthosporium oryzae Breda de Haan 1900

≡Drechslera oryzae (Breda de Haan) Subram. & P.C. Jain 1966

≡Luttrellia oryzae (Breda de Hann) Gornostai 1978

= Ophiobolus miyabeanus Ito & Kurib. 1927

    ≡ Cochliobolus miyabeanus (Ito & Kurib.) Drechsler ex Dastur 1942

= Helminthosporium zizaniae Y. Nisik. 1929

    ≡ Bipolaris zizaniae (Y. Nisik.) Shoemaker 1959

    ≡ Drechslera zizaniae (Y. Nisik.) Subram. & B.L. Jain 1966

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Leaves, glumes. Less commonly on coleoptile, leaf sheaths, panicle branches, roots of young seedlings, and stems (Ou 1985).

Disease Note: Brown spot, leaf spot and seedling blight.

Host: Principal hosts: cultivated Oryza sativa (rice) and Zizania aquatica (wild rice). Less common on non-cultivated Oryza spp. and other Poaceae under natural conditions. Able to infect a wide range of Poaceae when inoculated (Ou 1985).
