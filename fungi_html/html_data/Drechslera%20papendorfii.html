Nomenclature data for Drechslera papendorfii

Curvularia papendorfii Aa 1967 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

≡Bipolaris papendorfii (Aa) Alcorn 1983

≡Drechslera papendorfii (Aa) M.B. Ellis 1971

Notes: Curvularia siddiquii S.I. Ahmed & M. Qureshi 1960 (as 'siddiqui') nom. inval. Art. 39.1 (no Latin diagnosis).

Distribution: Africa, Asia.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Various genera of Poaceae.
