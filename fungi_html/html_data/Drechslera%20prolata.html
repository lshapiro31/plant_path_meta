Nomenclature data for Drechslera prolata

Exserohilum prolatum K.J. Leonard & Suggs 1974 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

≡Drechslera prolata (K.J. Leonard & Suggs) Sivan. 1984

= Setosphaeria prolata K.J. Leonard & Suggs 1974

Notes: Leaf spot of grasses (Sivanesan. C.M.I. Descr. 888. 1986). From Setosphaeria prolata - Leaf spot of grasses (Sivanesan. C.M.I. Descr. 888. 1986).

Distribution: Southeastern United States; Central America; eastern Africa.

Substrate: On living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Poaceae.
