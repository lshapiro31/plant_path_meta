Nomenclature data for Drechslera ravenelii

Curvularia ravenelii (M.A. Curtis) Manamgoda, L. Cai, & K.D Hyde 2012 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

≡ Helminthosporium ravenelii M.A. Curtis 1868

≡Bipolaris ravenelii (M.A. Curtis) Shoemaker 1959

≡Drechslera ravenelii (M.A. Curtis) Subram. & P.C. Jain 1966

= Cochliobolus ravenelii Alcorn 1981

Notes: False smut or head mold of grasses, especially Sporobolus spp. (Sivanesan. C.M.I. Descr. 884. 1987).

Distribution: Southern United States; warm temperate to tropical regions.

Substrate: Inflorescences.

Disease Note: False smut.

Host: Sporobolus spp. (Poaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Sivanesan, A. 1986. Cochliobolus ravenilii. C.M.I. Descr. Pathog. Fungi Bact. 884: 1-2.
