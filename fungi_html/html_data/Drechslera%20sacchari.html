Nomenclature data for Drechslera sacchari

Bipolaris sacchari (E.J. Butler) Shoemaker 1959 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

≡ Helminthosporium sacchari E.J. Butler 1913

≡Drechslera sacchari (E.J. Butler) Subram. & B.L. Jain 1966

= Helminthosporium ocellum Faris 1928

= Cercospora sacchari Breda de Haan 1892

    ≡ Bipolaris sacchari (Breda de Haan) Subram. 1971  Note: Not (E.J. Butler) Shoemaker 1959. Nomen illeg.

Distribution: Widespread, especially subtropical and tropical regions.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot, crown rot of bananna.

Host: Poaceae, Musa x paradisiaca (Musaceae), other monocots.
