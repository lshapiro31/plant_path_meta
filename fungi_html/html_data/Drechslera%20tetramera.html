= Cochliobolus spicifer R.R. Nelson 1964

    ≡ Pseudocochliobolus spicifer (R.R. Nelson) Tsuda, Ueyama & Nishih. 1978 [1977]

= Helminthosporium tetramerum McKinney 1925

  Variant spelling Helminthosporium tetramera McKinney 1925

    ≡ Bipolaris tetramera (McKinney) Shoemaker 1959

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Blight.

Host: Numerous hosts.
