Nomenclature data for Drechslera tritici-repentis

Pyrenophora tritici-repentis (Died.) Drechsler 1923 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

≡ Pleospora tritici-repentis Died. 1903

≡Pleospora trichostoma f. sp. tritici-repentis Died. 1902

= Helminthosporium tritici-repentis Died. 1903  Note: From Drechslera tritici-repentis - Yellow leaf spot of cereals and grasses (Ellis & Waller. C.M.I. Descr. 494. 1976).

  ≡Drechslera tritici-repentis (Died.) Shoemaker 1959

    ≡ Helminthosporium gramineum f. sp. tritici-repentis Died. 1902

= Helminthosporium tritici-vulgaris Y. Nisik. 1928

    ≡ Drechslera tritici-vulgaris (Y. Nisik.) S. Ito 1930

= Pyrenophora tritici-vulgaris J.G. Dicks. 1956  Note: Nom. nud.

Notes: From Helminthosporium tritici-vulgaris - as 'Helmisporium'

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: On living leaves.

Disease Note: Yellow leaf spot of cereals and grasses, leaf blight.

Host: Poaceae.
