= Helminthosporium fragosoi Bubák 1915

    ≡ Drechslera japonica (Ito & Kurib.) Shoemaker 1959

= Pyrenophora secalis M.D. Whitehead & Dicks. 1952

= Drechslera tuberosa (Atk.) Shoemaker 1959

     Helminthosporium tuberosum Atk. 1897

Notes: Oldest epithet not in correct genus.

Distribution: Temperate northern hemisphere.

Substrate: Leaves and stems.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Poaceae.
