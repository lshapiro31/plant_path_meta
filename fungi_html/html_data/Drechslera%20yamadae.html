Nomenclature data for Drechslera yamadae

Bipolaris yamadae (Y. Nisik.) Shoemaker 1959 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

≡ Helminthosporium yamadae Y. Nisik. 1929  Note: as 'yamadai'

≡Drechslera yamadae (Y. Nisik.) Subram. & B.L. Jain 1966

= Helminthosporium euphorbiae Hansf. 1943

    ≡ Bipolaris euphorbiae (Hansf.) J.J. Muchovej & A.O. Carvalho 1989

    ≡ Drechslera euphorbiae (Hansf.) M.B. Ellis 1971

Notes: as 'yamadai'

Distribution: Asia (China, Japan) Caribbean (Cuba), North America (USA: IA, ID, ND, WI).

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Panicum spp., also reported from Oryza sp., Saccharum sp., Setaria plicata (Poaceae).
