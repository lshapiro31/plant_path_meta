Nomenclature data for Drepanopeziza populi-albae

Drepanopeziza populi-albae (Kleb.) Nannf. 1932 (Ascomycetes, Helotiales)

≡ Pseudopeziza populi-albae Kleb. 1918

= Gloeosporium castagnei Desm. & Mont. 1849

    ≡ Marssonina castagnei (Desm. & Mont.) Magnus 1906

Distribution: Temperate northern hemisphere.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Anthracnose, leaf spot.

Host: Populus (Salicaceae).
