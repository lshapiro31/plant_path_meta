Nomenclature data for Drepanopeziza punctiformis

Drepanopeziza tremulae Rimpau 1962 (Ascomycetes, Helotiales)

= Gloeosporium brunneum Ellis & Everh. 1889  Note: From Marssonina brunnea - Pirozynski (Fungi Canadenses 13. 1974). Spiers (Eur. J. Forest Pathol. 18:140. 1988) provided nomenclatural and taxonomic discussions.

    ≡ Marssonina brunnea (Ellis & Everh.) Magnus 1906

= Drepanopeziza punctiformis Gremmen 1965

= Marssonina tremulae Kleb. 1918

Distribution: Temperate regions in both the northern and southern hemispheres.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Populus (Salicaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Gremmen, J. 1965. Three poplar-inhabiting Drepanopeziza species and their life-history. Nova Hedwigia 9: 170-176.

Pirozynski, K.A. 1974. Marssonina brunnea. Fungi Canadenses 13: 1-2.

Spiers, A.G. 1988. Comparative studies of type and herbarium specimens of Marssonina species pathogenic to poplars. Eur. J. Forest Pathol. 18: 140-156.

Spiers, A.G., and Hopcroft, D.H. 1998. Morphology of Drepanopeziza species pathogenic to polars. Mycol. Res. 102: 1025-1037.
