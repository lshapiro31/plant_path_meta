Nomenclature data for Durandiella fraxini

Durandiella fraxini (Schwein. : Fr.) Seaver 1951 (Ascomycetes, Helotiales)

≡ Peziza fraxini Schwein. : Fr. 1922

≡Godronia fraxini (Schwein. : Fr.) Höhn. 1945

= Sphaeronaema fraxini Peck 1878

    ≡ Sphaerographium fraxini (Peck) Sacc. 1883

= Sphaeronaema spina Berk. & Ravenel 1874

    ≡ Corniculariella spina (Berk. & Ravenel) DiCosmo 1978

    ≡ Cornularia spina (Berk. & Ravenel) Sacc. & Syd. 1902

Notes: as 'fraxinii'

Distribution: Eastern North America, scattered reports from Europe.

Substrate: Dead branches.

Disease Note: Saprobic.

Host: Fraxinus (Oleaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Sutton, B.C. 1980. The Coelomycetes. Fungi Imperfecti with Pycnidia, Acervuli and Stromata. Commonwealth Mycological Institute, Kew, Surrey, England, 696 pages.
