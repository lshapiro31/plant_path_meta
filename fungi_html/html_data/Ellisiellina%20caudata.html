Nomenclature data for Ellisiellina caudata

Colletotrichum caudatum (Sacc.) Peck 1909 (Ascomycetes, Phyllachorales)

≡ Ellisiella caudata Sacc. 1880

≡Ellisiellina caudata (Peck) Sousa da Câmara 1949

Distribution: North America (USA).

Substrate: On leaves and stems.

Disease Note: Anthracnose, leaf spot.

Host: Sorghastrum nutans (Poaceae).
