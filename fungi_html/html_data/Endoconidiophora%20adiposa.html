Nomenclature data for Endoconidiophora adiposa

Ceratocystis adiposa (E.J. Butler) C. Moreau 1952 (Ascomycetes, Microascales)

≡ Sphaeronaema adiposum E.J. Butler 1906

≡Ceratostomella adiposa (E.J. Butler) Sartoris 1927

≡Endoconidiophora adiposa (E.J. Butler) R.W. Davidson 1935

≡Ophiostoma adiposum (E.J. Butler) Nannf. 1934

= Ceratostomella major J.F.H. Beyma 1935

    ≡ Ophiostoma majus (J.F.H. Beyma) Goid. 1935

    ≡ Ceratocystis major (J.F.H. Beyma) C. Moreau 1952

Notes: Per de Berr et al. (2014), this is a species of uncertain placement that may eventually be provided with a new genus.

Distribution: Asia, North America (USA), especially warmer regions.

Substrate: Roots.

Disease Note: Black rot.

Host: Several genera in several families.
