Nomenclature data for Endomyces geotrichum

Geotrichum candidum Link 1809 (Saccharomycetes, Saccharomycetales)

= Endomyces geotrichum E.E. Butler & L.J. Petersen 1972  Note: From Galactomyces geotrichum - See De Hoog (1986) for numerous synonyms.

    ≡ Galactomyces geotrichum (E.E. Butler & L.J. Petersen) Redhead & Malloch 1977  Note: For additional synonyms, please see Index Fungorum.

    ≡ Dipodascus geotrichum (E.E. Butler & L.J. Petersen) Arx 1977

= Oospora lactis (Fresen.) Sacc. 1886

= Oospora lactis var. parasitica Pritchard & Porte 1923

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: On a variety of organic substrates.

Disease Note: Usually fruit rots.

Host: Multiple genera in multiple families.
