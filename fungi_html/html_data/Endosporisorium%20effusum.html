Nomenclature data for Endosporisorium effusum

Macalpinomyces effusus (Syd. & P. Syd.) Vánky 1997 (Ustilaginomycetes, Ustilaginales)

≡ Ustilago effusa Syd. & P. Syd. 1906

≡Endosporisorium effusum (Syd. & P. Syd.) Vánky 1995

Distribution: Asia (India) and a single report from Spain.

Disease Note: Smut.

Host: Reports from several taxa of Poaceae. Some are questionable (Vanky 2005).
