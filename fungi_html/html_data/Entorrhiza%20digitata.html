Nomenclature data for Entorrhiza digitata

Entorrhiza casparyana (Magnus) Lagerh. 1888 (Ustilaginomycetes, Entorrhizales)

≡ Schinzia casparyana Magnus 1888

≡Entorrhiza casparyana var. casparyana (Magnus) Lagerh.

≡Melanotaenium casparyanum (Magnus) Thirum. & M.D. Whitehead 1968

= Entorrhiza digitata Lagerh. 1888

    ≡ Melanotaenium digitatum (Lagerh.) Thirum. & M.D. Whitehead 1968

    ≡ Schinzia digitata (Lagerh.) Magnus 1891

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Roots.

Disease Note: Root smut; galls.

Host: Juncus (Juncaceae).
