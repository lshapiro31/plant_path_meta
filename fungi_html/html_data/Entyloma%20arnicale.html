Nomenclature data for Entyloma arnicale

Entyloma arnicale Ellis & Everh. 1895 (Ustilaginomycetes, Entylomatales)

= Entyloma arnicae Syd. & P. Syd. 1918  Note: Nom. nud. Nom. inval.

= Ramularia arnicalis Ellis & Everh. 1891

    ≡ Entylomella arnicalis (Ellis & Everh.) Cif. 1931

= Entyloma circaeae Dearn. 1939

Distribution: Europe, North America.

Disease Note: Smut.

Host: Arnica (Asteraceae).

Supporting Literature:

Vanky, K. 2012. Smut Fungi of the World. APS Press, St. Paul, Minnesota, 1458 pages.
