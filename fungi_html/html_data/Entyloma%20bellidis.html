Nomenclature data for Entyloma bellidis

Entyloma bellidis Krieg. 1896 (Ustilaginomycetes, Entylomatales)

≡Entyloma calendulae f. bellidis (Krieg.) Ainsw. & Sampson 1950

= Entyloma thrinciae Maire 1907

Distribution: N Africa, Europe.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Smut.

Host: Astranthium spp., Bellis (Asteraceae).
