Nomenclature data for Entyloma calendulae

Entyloma calendulae (Oudem.) de Bary 1874 (Ustilaginomycetes, Entylomatales)

≡ Protomyces calendulae Oudem. 1873

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Smut.

Host: Calendula spp. (Asteraceae).
