Nomenclature data for Entyloma endogenum

Melanotaenium endogenum (Unger) de Bary 1874 (Ustilaginomycetes, Urocystales)

≡ Protomyces endogenus Unger 1833

≡Entyloma endogenum (Unger) Wünsche 1877

≡Physoderma endogenum (Unger) Cornu 1883

≡Protomyces galii T. Nees & Henry 1837  Note: Nom. illeg. via superfluous. Correct author citation uncertain.

Notes: Protomyces galii T. Nees & Henry (1837) is a superfluous nom. nov.; non Protomyces galii Rabenh. (1860).

Distribution: Europe, N Africa, Asia.

Substrate: Leaves, stems.

Disease Note: Smut.

Host: Asperula spp., Galium spp. (Rubiaceae).
