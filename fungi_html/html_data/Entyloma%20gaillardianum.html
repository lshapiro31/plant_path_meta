Nomenclature data for Entyloma gaillardianum

Entyloma gaillardianum Vánky 1982 (Ustilaginomycetes, Entylomatales)

[= Entyloma gaillardiae P.A. Lemke 1952 - illegitimate later homonym, not included in search]  Note: Not (Speg.) Speg. 1925. Nom. illeg.

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Smut.

Host: Gaillardia (Asteraceae).

Supporting Literature:

Garibaldi, A., Gilardi, G., Matic, S., and Gullino, M.L. 2018. First report of leaf smut caused by Entyloma gaillardianum on Gaillardia aristata in Italy. Pl. Dis. 102(3): 678.

Vanky, K. 2012. Smut Fungi of the World. APS Press, St. Paul, Minnesota, 1458 pages.
