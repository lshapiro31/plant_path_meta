Nomenclature data for Entyloma hieracii

Entyloma hieracii Syd. & P. Syd. ex Cif. 1924 (Ustilaginomycetes, Entylomatales)

= Entyloma crepidis Kawecka-Starmachowa 1939

= Entyloma lavrovianum Schwarzman 1960

Distribution: Northern hemisphere.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Smut.

Host: Hieracium (Asteraceae).

Supporting Literature:

Vanky, K. 2006. Taxonomic studies on Ustilaginomycetes - 26. Mycotaxon 95: 1-65.

Vanky, K. 2012. Smut Fungi of the World. APS Press, St. Paul, Minnesota, 1458 pages.
