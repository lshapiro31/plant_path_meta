Jamesdicksonia irregularis (Johanson) R. Bauer, Begerow, A. Nagler & Oberw. 2001 (Ustilaginomycetes, Georgefischeriales)

≡ Entyloma irregulare Johanson 1884

Distribution: Europe, North America, New Zealand.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Blister smut.

Host: Poa (Poaceae).
