Nomenclature data for Entyloma lapponicum

Entyloma lapponicum Liro 1939 (Ustilaginomycetes, Entylomatales)

= Entyloma montis-rainieri G.W. Fisch. & W.B. Cooke 1952

Distribution: Northern hemisphere.

Disease Note: Smut.

Host: Polemonium (Polemoniaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Vanky, K. 2012. Smut Fungi of the World. APS Press, St. Paul, Minnesota, 1458 pages.
