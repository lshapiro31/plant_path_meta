Nomenclature data for Entyloma microsporum

Entyloma microsporum (Unger) J. Schröt. 1874 (Ustilaginomycetes, Entylomatales)

≡ Protomyces microsporus Unger 1834

≡Entyloma microsporum var. microsporum .

≡Entyloma ungerianum de Bary 1874  Note: Superfluous.

= Caeoma bullosum Sacc. 1915

= Entyloma microsporum var. pygmaeum Allesch. 1897

    ≡ Entyloma pygmaeum (Allesch.) Cif. 1924

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Smut.

Host: Ranunculus (Ranunculaceae).
