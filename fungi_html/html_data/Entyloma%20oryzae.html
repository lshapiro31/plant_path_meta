Nomenclature data for Entyloma oryzae

Eballistra oryzae (Syd. & P. Syd.) R. Bauer, Begerow, A. Nagler & Oberw. 2001 (Ustilaginomycetes, Georgefischeriales)

≡ Entyloma oryzae Syd. & P. Syd. 1914

Distribution: Cosmopolitan in warmer regions.

Substrate: Leaves

Disease Note: Smut.

Host: Oryza (Poaceae).
