Nomenclature data for Entyloma physalidis

Entyloma australe Speg. 1880 (Ustilaginomycetes, Entylomatales)

= Entylomella australe Cif. 1959

= Entyloma besseyi Farl. 1883

= Protomyces physalidis Kalchbr. & Cooke 1880  Note: Published after E. australe Speg.

  ≡Entyloma physalidis (Kalchbr. & Cooke) G. Winter 1883

Notes: From Entylomella australe - Nom. nud. fide Vanky (2011).

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: White smut.

Host: Solanaceae, especially Physalis spp.
