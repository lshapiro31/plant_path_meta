Nomenclature data for Entyloma ranunculi

Entyloma ficariae A.A. Fisch. Waldh. 1877 (Ustilaginomycetes, Entylomatales)

= Protomyces ficariae Cornu & Roze 1874  Note: Nom. nud.

= Fusidium ranunculi Bonord. 1851

    ≡ Cylindrosporium ranunculi (Bonord.) Sacc. 1879

  ≡Entyloma ranunculi (Bonord.) J. Schröt. 1877

    ≡ Entylomella ranunculi (Bonord.) Höhn. 1916

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Smut.

Host: Ranunculus (Ranunculaceae).
