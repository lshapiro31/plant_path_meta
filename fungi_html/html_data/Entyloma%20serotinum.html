Entyloma serotinum J. Schröt. 1876 (Ustilaginomycetes, Entylomatales)

= Entyloma boraginis Cif. 1924

  Variant spelling Entyloma borraginis Cif. 1924

= Entyloma luteomaculans H.H. Hume 1902

  Variant spelling Entyloma luteo-maculans H.H. Hume 1902

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Smut.

Host: Boraginaceae.
