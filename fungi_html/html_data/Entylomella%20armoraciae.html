Notes: Cylindrospora armoraciae is a variant spelling of Cylindrosporium armoracium; commonly listed as "Cylindrosporium armoraciae", where gender of adjectival epithet and genus do not agree. This situation is correctable under Art. 23.5.

Distribution: Africa, Asia, Europe, North America.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Brassicaceae.
