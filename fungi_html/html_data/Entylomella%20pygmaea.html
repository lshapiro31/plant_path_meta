Nomenclature data for Entylomella pygmaea

Entyloma veronicae (Halst.) Lagerh. 1891 (Ustilaginomycetes, Entylomatales)

≡ Entyloma linariae f. veronicae Halst. 1890

= Entyloma linariae f. veronicae G. Winter 1884  Note: Nom. nud.

= Entyloma peregrinum Speg. 1909

= Ramularia pygmaea Lindr. 1902

  ≡Entylomella pygmaea (Lindr.) U. Braun 1994

= Entylomella veronicae Cif. 1959

= Entyloma veronicicola Lindr. 1904

= Septoria veronicicola P. Karst. 1887

    ≡ Entylomella veronicicola (P. Karst.) U. Braun 2000

    ≡ Pseudocercospora veronicicola (P. Karst.) W.P. Wu, B. Sutton & Gange 1996

Notes: Entyloma and Entylomella are taxonomically congruent and the latter name appears to be a nom. nud. (see Höhnel in : Österreichische Botanische Zeitschrift 66: 105 (1916)).

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Smut.

Host: Veronica spp. (Plantaginaceae).
