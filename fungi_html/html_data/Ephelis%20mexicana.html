Nomenclature data for Ephelis mexicana

Balansia claviceps Speg. 1885 (Ascomycetes, Hypocreales)

= Ephelis mexicana Fr. 1849

Notes: Oldest epithet not in the correct genus.

Distribution: Widespread in tropical regions.

Substrate: On living inflorescences.

Disease Note: False smut, flower blight.

Host: Poaceae.
