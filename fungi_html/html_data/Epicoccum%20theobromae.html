= Epicoccum theobromae Petch 1925

Notes: For full synonymy, see Mueller & von Arx (1962).

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Plurivorous. Living and dead plant parts, air, animals, soil.

Disease Note: Secondary invader or saprophyte.

Host: Multiple plant families.
