Nomenclature data for Eremothecium coryli

Eremothecium coryli (Peglion) Kurtzman 1995 (Saccharomycetes, Saccharomycetales)

≡ Nematospora coryli Peglion 1901

= Nematospora lycopersici A. Schneid. 1916

= Nematospora nagpuri Dastur & Jiwan Singh 1930

= Nematospora phaseoli Wingard 1922

Notes: From Nematospora coryli - Mukerji (C.M.I. Descr. 184. 1968). From Nematospora lycopersici - Isolated from various crop plants. From Nematospora phaseoli - Possibly a synonym of N. coryli Peglion (Batra. U.S.D.A. Tech. Bull. 1469:24. 1973).

Distribution: Warm-temperate to subtropical regions.

Substrate: Fruits, nuts, seeds.

Disease Note: Dry rot, lesions, pod spot.

Host: Various plants.

Supporting Literature:

Batra, L.R. 1973. Nematosporaceae (Hemiascomycetidae): Taxonomy, pathogenicity, distribution, and vector relationships. U.S.D.A. Agric. Res. Serv. Techn. Bull. 1469: 1-71.
