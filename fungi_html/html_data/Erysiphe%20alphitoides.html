Nomenclature data for Erysiphe alphitoides

Erysiphe alphitoides var. alphitoides (Griffon & Maubl.) U. Braun & S. Takam. 2000 (Ascomycetes, Erysiphales)

≡ Microsphaera alphitoides var. alphitoides Griffon & Maubl. 1982

      Microsphaera alphitoides Griffon & Maubl. 1912

≡Erysiphe alphitoides (Griffon & Maubl.) U. Braun & S. Takam. 2000

= Oidium alphitoides Griffon & Maubl. 1910

= Microsphaera dentatae Liou 1931

    ≡ Microsphaera alni var. dentatae (Liou) F.L. Tai 1935

= Oidium dubium Jacz. 1910

= Microsphaera querci Sawada 1951

= Oidium quercinum var. gemmiparum Ferraris 1909

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: On living leaves and inflorescences.

Disease Note: Powdery mildew; leaf distortion and death.

Host: Multiple genera in multiple families, especially Fagaceae.
