Nomenclature data for Erysiphe astragali

Erysiphe astragali DC. (Ascomycetes, Erysiphales)

≡Alphitomorpha astragali (DC.) Wallr. 1819

≡Microsphaera astragali (DC.) Trevis. 1853

≡Trichocladia astragali (DC.) Neger 1901

Distribution: Asia, Europe, North America (USA).

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Powdery mildew.

Host: Astragalus spp., Oxytropis spp. (Fabaceae).

Supporting Literature:
