Nomenclature data for Erysiphe azaleae

Erysiphe azaleae (U. Braun) U. Braun & S. Takam. 2000 (Ascomycetes, Erysiphales)

≡ Microsphaera azaleae U. Braun 1982

= Oidium ericinum Erikss. 1885

= Microsphaera penicillata f. rhododendri Jacz. 1926

     Microsphaera rhododendri (Jacz.) Bunkina 1974

Notes: Microsphaera azaleae conserved against Oidium ericinum in Braun, 2013.

Distribution: Europe (Italy, Latvia, Poland), North America (USA: CO, DE, PA).

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Powdery mildew.

Host: Rhododendron (Ericaceae).

Supporting Literature:
