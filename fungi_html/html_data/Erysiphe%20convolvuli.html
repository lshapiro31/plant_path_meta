Nomenclature data for Erysiphe convolvuli

Erysiphe convolvuli DC. 1805 (Ascomycetes, Erysiphales)

Distribution: Africa (north), Asia, Europe, North America (USA: ID), South America (introduced).

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Powdery mildew.

Host: Calystegia spp., Convolvulus spp. (Convolvulaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Braun, U., and Cook, R.T.A. 2012. Taxonomy Manual of the Erysiphales (Powdery Mildews). CBS Biodivers. Ser. 11: 703.
