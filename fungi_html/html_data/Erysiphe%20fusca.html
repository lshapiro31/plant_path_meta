Nomenclature data for Erysiphe fusca

Podosphaera fusca (Fr. : Fr.) U. Braun & S. Takam. 2000 (Ascomycetes, Erysiphales)

≡ Erysiphe fusca Fr. : Fr. 1829

≡Sphaerotheca fusca (Fr. : Fr.) S. Blumer 1933

= Erysiphe doronici Duby 1830

= Sphaerotheca melampyri L. Junell 1966

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Living leaves, stems.

Disease Note: Powdery mildew.

Host: Multiple genera in multiple families.
