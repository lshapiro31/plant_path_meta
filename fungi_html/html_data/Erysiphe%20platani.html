Nomenclature data for Erysiphe platani

Erysiphe platani (Howe) U. Braun & S. Takam. 2000 (Ascomycetes, Erysiphales)

≡ Microsphaera platani Howe 1874

Distribution: Africa (South Africa), Asia (China, Japan, Korea), Europe, North America (USA), Australia, South America (Argentina).

Substrate: Leaves, petioles, inflorescences.

Disease Note: Powdery mildew.

Host: Platanus spp. (Platanaceae), Ailanthus altissima (Simaroubaceae) .
