Nomenclature data for Erysiphe ravenelii

Erysiphe ravenelii (Berk.) U. Braun & S. Takam. 2000 (Ascomycetes, Erysiphales)

≡ Microsphaera ravenelii Berk. 1876

Distribution: North America (USA).

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Powdery mildew.

Host: Gleditschia triacanthos (Fabaceae).
