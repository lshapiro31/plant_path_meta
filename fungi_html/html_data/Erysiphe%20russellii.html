Nomenclature data for Erysiphe russellii

Erysiphe russellii (Clinton) U. Braun & S. Takam. 2000 (Ascomycetes, Erysiphales)

≡ Microsphaera russellii Clinton 1872

≡Trichocladia russellii (Clinton) Jacz. 1926

= Oidium oxalidis McAlpine 1894

    ≡ Acrosporium oxalidis (McAlpine) Subram. 1971

    ≡ Pseudoidium oxalidis (McAlpine) U. Braun & R.T.A. Cook 2012

Distribution: Widespread; native to North America.

Substrate: Living leaves, inflorescences.

Disease Note: Powdery mildew.

Host: Oxalis spp. (Oxalidaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Braun, U., and Takamatsu, S. 2000. Phylogeny of Erysiphe, Microsphaera, Uncinula (Erysipheae) and Cystotheca, Podosphaera, Sphaerotheca (Cystotheceae) inferred from rDNA ITS sequences - some taxonomic consequences. Schlechtendalia 4: 1-33.
