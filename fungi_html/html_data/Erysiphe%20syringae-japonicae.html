Nomenclature data for Erysiphe syringae-japonicae

Erysiphe syringae-japonicae (U. Braun) U. Braun & S. Takam. 2000 (Ascomycetes, Erysiphales)

≡ Microsphaera syringae-japonicae U. Braun 1982

Distribution: Asia, Europe (Germany, Poland, Turkey).

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Powdery mildew.

Host: Ligustrum vulgare, Syringa spp. (Oleaceae).

Supporting Literature:
