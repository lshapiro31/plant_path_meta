Nomenclature data for Erysiphe trifolii

Erysiphe trifoliorum (Wallr.) U. Braun 2010 (Ascomycetes, Erysiphales)

≡ Alphitomorpha trifoliorum Wallr. 1819

= Erysiphe trifolii Grev. 1824

    ≡ Microsphaera trifolii (Grev.) U. Braun 1981

     Microsphaera trifolii var. Trifolii (Grev.) U. Braun 1981

    ≡ Erysiphe trifolii var. trifolii Grev. 1824

Notes: Braun et al. (2010) determined this was the correct name for a fungus previously considered to be E. trifolii. Several taxa listed as synonyms by Braun (1987) are being reassessed.

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Leaves, petioles, stem, floral receptacles.

Disease Note: Powdery mildew.

Host: Numerous hosts in several families, but taxonomy in flux.
