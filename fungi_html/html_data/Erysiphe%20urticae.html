Nomenclature data for Erysiphe urticae

Erysiphe urticae (Wallr.) S. Blumer 1933 (Ascomycetes, Erysiphales)

≡ Alphitomorpha urticae Wallr. 1819

≡Erysiphe pisi var. urticae (Wallr.) Ialongo 1992

Distribution: Asia, Europe.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Powdery mildew.

Host: Pilea spp., Urtica spp. (Urticaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Braun, U., and Cook, R.T.A. 2012. Taxonomy Manual of the Erysiphales (Powdery Mildews). CBS Biodivers. Ser. 11: 703.
