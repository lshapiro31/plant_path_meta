Erysiphe aggregata (Peck) Farl. (Ascomycetes, Erysiphales)

≡ Erysiphella aggregata Peck 1876

≡Erysiphe vernalis var. aggregata (Peck) L. Junell 1967

Distribution: Temperate North America.

Substrate: On twigs and female catkins.

Disease Note: Powdery mildew.

Host: Alnus spp. (Betulaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Braun, U., and Cook, R.T.A. 2012. Taxonomy Manual of the Erysiphales (Powdery Mildews). CBS Biodivers. Ser. 11: 703.
