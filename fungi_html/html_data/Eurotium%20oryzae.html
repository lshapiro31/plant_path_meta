Nomenclature data for Eurotium oryzae

Aspergillus oryzae (Ahlb.) Cohn 1884 (Ascomycetes, Eurotiales)

≡ Eurotium oryzae Ahlb. 1878

≡Aspergillus flavus var. oryzae Kurtzman, M.J. Smiley, Robnett & Wicklow 1986  Note: As a var. of subsp. flavus in the species flavus.

Notes: Numerous probable synonyms are listed by Raper and Fennel (1965). This fungus is used extensively in fermenting soybeans and and other legumes (Klich 2002).

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Multitudinous substrates.

Disease Note: Rots of various foods.

Host: Isolated from varied species in numerous families as a saprophyte.

Supporting Literature:

Klich, M.A. 2002. Identification of common Aspergillus species. Centraalbureau voor Schimmelcultures, Utrecht : 116.

Raper, K.B., and Fennell, D.I. 1965. The Genus Aspergillus. Williams and Wilkins Company, Baltimore, 686 pages.
