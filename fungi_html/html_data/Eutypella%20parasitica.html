Nomenclature data for Eutypella parasitica

Eutypella parasitica R.W. Davidson & Lorentz 1938 (Ascomycetes, Xylariales)

Notes: Canker disease (Johnson & Kuntz. Canad. J. Bot. 56:1518. 1978).

Distribution: North America (east of the Rocky Mountains), most common in the Great Lakes region, Asia (China), Europe.

Substrate: Bark, stem.

Disease Note: Canker.

Host: Acer spp. (Sapindaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Cech, T.L., Schwanda, K., Klosterhuber, M., Straber, L., and Kirisits, T. 2016. Eutypella canker of maple: first report from Germany and situtation in Austria. Forest Pathol. 46(4): 336-340.
