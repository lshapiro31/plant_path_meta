Nomenclature data for Exobasidium camelliae

Exobasidium camelliae Shirai 1896 (Ustilaginomycetes, Exobasidiales)

Notes: Parasitic on flower buds and shoots.

Distribution: United States; Australasia; Europe; Great Britain.

Substrate: On living leaves and stems.

Disease Note: Leaf and stem gall.

Host: On Camelliae (Theaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Baxter Jr., L.W., Fagan, S.G., and Segars, S.B. 1988. New suscepts of Exobasidium camelliae, the camellia leaf gall fungus. Pl. Dis. 72: 79.

Ezuka, A. 1990. Notes on some species of Exobasidium in Japan (I). Trans. Mycol. Soc. Japan 31: 375-388.
