Nomenclature data for Exosporium stilbaceum

Helminthosporiella stilbacea (Moreau) Hern.-Restr., G.A. Sarria & Crous 2016 (, )

≡ Cercospora palmicola f. stilbacea C. Moreau 1947

≡Helminthosporium stilbaceum (Moreau) S. Hughes 1952

≡Exosporium stilbaceum var. macrosporum Subramon. & V.G. Rao 1971

≡Exosporium stilbaceum var. stilbaceum (Moreau). M.B. Ellis 1961

Notes: From Cercospora palmicola f. stilbacea - Not a Cercospora fide Chupp (1953).

Distribution: Africa, South America (Colombia).

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Elaeis guineensis, E. oleifera (Arecaceae).
