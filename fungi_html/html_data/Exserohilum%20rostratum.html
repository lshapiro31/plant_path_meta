Nomenclature data for Exserohilum rostratum

Exserohilum rostratum (Drechsler) K.J. Leonard & Suggs 1974 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

≡ Helminthosporium rostratum Drechsler 1923

≡Bipolaris rostrata (Drechsler) Shoemaker 1959

≡Drechslera rostrata (Drechsler) M.J. Richardson & E.M. Fraser 1968

= Helminthosporium halodes Drechsler 1923

    ≡ Bipolaris halodes (Drechsler) Shoemaker 1959

    ≡ Exserohilum halodes (Drechsler) K.J. Leonard & Suggs 1974

    ≡ Drechslera halodes (Drechsler) Subram. & B.L. Jain 1966

= Setosphaeria rostrata K.J. Leonard 1976  Note: From Luttrellia rostrata - Nom. inval.

    ≡ Luttrellia rostrata (Drechsler) Gornostai 1978

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot and foot rot of wheat and other grasses, blight, damping-off, rots including leaf spot of banana.

Host: Multiple hosts, especially Poaceae and other monocots.
