Nomenclature data for Farysia cariciphila

Farysia thuemenii (A.A. Fisch. Waldh.) Nannf. 1959 (Ustilaginomycetes, Ustilaginales)

≡ Ustilago thuemenii A.A. Fisch. Waldh. 1878

= Cintractia caricicola Henn. 1895

= Ustilago caricicola Tracy & Earle 1899

= Ustilago cariciphila Speg. 1925

    ≡ Cintractia cariciphila (Speg.) Cif. 1931

  ≡Farysia cariciphila (Speg.) Hirschh. 1986

= Cintractia caricis f. minor Sousa da Câmara & Oliveira 1945

= Uredo olivacea DC. 1815

  [≡ Farysia olivacea (DC.) Syd. & P. Syd. 1919 - illegitimate later homonym, not included in search]  Note: Not (Jaap) Höhn. 1917. Nom. illeg.

    ≡ Ustilago olivacea (DC.) Tul. & C. Tul. 1847

    ≡ Caeoma olivaceum (DC.) Schltdl. 1824

    ≡ Erysibe olivacea (DC.) Wallr. 1833

    ≡ Elateromyces olivaceus (DC.) Bubák 1912

    ≡ Farysia caricis (DC.) Liro 1938  Note: Based on Uredo segetum unranked caricis DC. 1805, which was given the name Uredo olivacea at the species rank.

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Flowers.

Disease Note: Smut.

Host: Carex spp. (Cyperaceae).
