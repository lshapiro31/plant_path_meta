Nomenclature data for Fistulina hepatica

Fistulina hepatica (Schaeff. : Fr.) Sibth. 1792 (Basidiomycetes, Agaricales)

≡ Boletus hepaticus Schaeff. : Fr. 1774

= Ceriomyces hepaticus Sacc. 1888  Note: Ceriomyces hepaticus Sacc. (1888) is a later, illegitimate homonym (Art. 53.1) of Ceriomyces hepaticus Arcang. (1878).

    ≡ Confistulina hepatica (Sacc.) Stalpers 1983

    ≡ Ptychogaster hepaticus (Sacc.) Lloyd 1909

Distribution: Northern hemisphere; reports from South America.

Substrate: Wood, roots, butt.

Disease Note: Brown heart rot, butt rot, red wood discoloration.

Host: Castanea sativa, Quercus spp. (Fagaceae).
