Nomenclature data for Fomes australis

Ganoderma australe (Fr. : Fr.) Pat. 1889 (Basidiomycetes, Polyporales)

≡ Polyporus australis Fr. : Fr. 1828

≡Fomes australis (Fr. : Fr.) Cooke 1885

Notes: According to Steyaert (Trans. Brit. Mycol. Soc. 65:451. 1975), G. australe is a synonym of G. tornatum (Pers.) Bres. (1827). Per Rajchenberg (2006), this species needs further taxonomic elaboration. For numerous additional synonyms, see Index Fungorum.

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Wood; heartwood, roots.

Disease Note: White heart-rot.

Host: Numerous hosts.
