Nomenclature data for Fomes igniarius

Phellinus igniarius (L. : Fr.) Quél. 1886 (Basidiomycetes, Hymenochaetales)

≡ Boletus igniarius L. 1753

≡Fomes igniarius (L.) Fr. 1849

Notes: For numerous additional synonyms, see Index Fungorum.

Distribution: Temperate northern hemisphere.

Substrate: Wood.

Disease Note: White heart rot.

Host: Salix spp. (Salicaceae); records from other taxa represent other species in the same species complex per Sell (2008).
