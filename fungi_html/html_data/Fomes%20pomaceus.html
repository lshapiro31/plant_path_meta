Nomenclature data for Fomes pomaceus

Phellinus pomaceus (Pers.) Maire 1933 (Basidiomycetes, Hymenochaetales)

≡ Polyporus pomaceus Pers. 1922

≡Fomes pomaceus (Pers.) Lloyd 1910

Distribution: Northern hemisphere; introduced in South America (Argentina).

Substrate: Trunk, limbs.

Disease Note: White heart-rot.

Host: Prunus spp. (Rosaceae) and other hardwoods.

Supporting Literature:

Rajchenberg, M., and Robledo, G. 2013. Pathogenic polypores in Argentina. Forest Pathol. 43: 171-184.
