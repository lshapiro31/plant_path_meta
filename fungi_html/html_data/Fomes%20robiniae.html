Nomenclature data for Fomes robiniae

Fulvifomes robiniae (Murrill) Murrill 1914 (Basidiomycetes, Hymenochaetales)

≡ Pyropolyporus robiniae Murrill 1903

≡Fomes robiniae (Murrill) Sacc. & D. Sacc. 1905

≡Phellinus robiniae (Murrill) A. Ames 1913

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: On living trees or dead wood.

Disease Note: Opportunistic pathogen causing heart rot; also a saprobe.

Host: Robinia spp., occasionally of other Fabaceae.

Supporting Literature:

Riffle, J.W., Myatt, A.K., and Davis, R.L. 1985. Incidence of Phellinus robineae in black locust plantings in Oklahoma. Pl. Dis. 69: 116-118.
