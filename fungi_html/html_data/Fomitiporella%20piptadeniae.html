Nomenclature data for Fomitiporella piptadeniae

Phellinotus piptadeniae (Teixeira) Drechsler-Santos & Robledo 2016 (, )

≡ Phellinus piptadeniae Teixeira 1950

≡Fomitiporella piptadeniae (Teixeira) Teixeira 1992

Distribution: South America (Brazil, Peru).

Substrate: Living trees.

Disease Note: Pathogenic.

Host: Piptadenia spp., Mimosa spp., Senegalia sp. (Fabaceae), Eugenia (Myrtaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Phellinus piptadeniae (Hymenochaetales: Hymenochaetaceae): taxonomy and host range of a species with disjunct distribution in South American seasonally dry forests. Pl. Syst. Evol. 301: 1887-1896.
