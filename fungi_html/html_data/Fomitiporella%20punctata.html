Nomenclature data for Fomitiporella punctata

Fomitiporia punctata (P. Karst.) Murrill 1947 (Basidiomycetes, Hymenochaetales)

≡ Poria punctata P. Karst. 1882

≡Fomitiporella punctata (P. Karst.) Teixeira 1992

≡Fuscoporia punctata (P. Karst.) G. Cunn 1948

≡Phellinus punctatus (P. Karst.) Pilát 1942

Notes: For additional synonyms, see Index Fungorum.

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Branches and trunks of living trees and on slash of hardwoods and conifers.

Disease Note: White rot.

Host: Numerous hosts.
