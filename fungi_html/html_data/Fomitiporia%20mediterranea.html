Nomenclature data for Fomitiporia mediterranea

Fomitiporia mediterranea M. Fisch. 2002 (Basidiomycetes, Hymenochaetales)

Distribution: Asia (Iran, Turkey), Europe (Germany, Italy).

Substrate: Trunk, branches.

Disease Note: Esca disease; wood decay, discoloration.

Host: Vitis vinifera (Vitaceae); reports from Corylus avellana (Betulaceae), Actinidia deliciosa (Actinidiaceae); possibly Olea europea (Oleaceae) and other hosts.
