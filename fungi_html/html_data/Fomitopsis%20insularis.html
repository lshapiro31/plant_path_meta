Nomenclature data for Fomitopsis insularis

Heterobasidion insulare (Murrill) Ryvarden 1972 (Basidiomycetes, Russulales)

≡ Trametes insularis Murrill 1908

≡Fomitopsis insularis (Murrill) Imazeki 1943

= Tyromyces tibeticus J.D. Zhao & X.Q. Zhang 1983  Note: Cited as synonym on Mycobank.

Distribution: Asia.

Substrate: Wood.

Disease Note: Trunk rot, root rot.

Host: Members of Pinaceae.

Supporting Literature:

Zhao, J.D., and Zhang, X.Q. 1992. The polypores of China. Biblioth. Mycol. 145: 1-524.
