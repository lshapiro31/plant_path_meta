Nomenclature data for Fusarium acuminatum

Fusarium acuminatum Ellis & Everh. 1895 (Ascomycetes, Hypocreales)

≡Microcera acuminata (Ellis & Everh.) Höhn. 1919

≡Fusarium gibbosum var. acuminatum (Ellis & Everh.) Bilai 1987

≡Fusarium scirpi subsp. acuminatum (Ellis & Everh.) Raillo 1950

≡Fusarium scirpi var. acuminatum (Ellis & Everh.) Wollenw. 1930

= Gibberella acuminata Wollenw. 1943

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Leaves, roots.

Disease Note: Leaf tip dieback, light brown lesions, root rot.

Host: Varied host families.
