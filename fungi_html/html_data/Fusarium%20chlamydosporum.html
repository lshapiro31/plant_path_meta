Nomenclature data for Fusarium chlamydosporum

Fusarium chlamydosporum Wollenw. & Reinking 1925 (Ascomycetes, Hypocreales)

≡Fusarium sporotrichioides var. chlamydosporum (Wollenw. & Reinking) Joffe 1974

= Dactylium fusarioides Gonz. Frag. & Cif. 1928

    ≡ Fusarium fusarioides (Gonz. Frag. & Cif. ) C. Booth 1971

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Roots, root collar.

Disease Note: Collar rot.

Host: Diverse plant families.
