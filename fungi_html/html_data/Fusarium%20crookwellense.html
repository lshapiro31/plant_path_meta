Nomenclature data for Fusarium crookwellense

Fusarium cerealis (Cooke) Sacc. 1886 (Ascomycetes, Hypocreales)

≡ Fusisporium cerealis Cooke 1878

≡Fusarium culmorum var. cereale (Cooke) Wollenw. 1930

≡Fusarium sambucinum var. cereale (Cooke) Raillo 1950

= Fusarium crookwellense L.W. Burgess, P.E. Nelson & Toussoun 1982

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Soil-borne; commonly isolated from cereals and other seeds.

Disease Note: Seedling blight, brown foot rot, and ear blight in wheat and other cereals.

Host: Diverse plant families.
