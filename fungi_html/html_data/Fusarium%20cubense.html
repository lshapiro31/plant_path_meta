Nomenclature data for Fusarium cubense

Fusarium oxysporum f. sp. cubense (E.F. Sm.) W.C. Snyder & H.N. Hansen 1940 (Ascomycetes, Hypocreales)

≡ Fusarium cubense E.F. Sm. 1910

≡Fusarium oxysporum var. cubense (E.F. Sm.) Wollenw. 1935

= Fusarium cubense var. inodoratum E.W. Brandes 1919

Notes: Formae speciales have no standing with the ICBN. Four races are currently recognized.

Distribution: Widespread in tropical and subtropical regions.

Substrate: Roots.

Disease Note: Panama disease of banana, wilt.

Host: Typically Musa spp. (Musaceae); symptomless in non-host plants.
