Nomenclature data for Fusarium flocciferum

Fusarium flocciferum Corda 1828 (Ascomycetes, Hypocreales)

= Gibberella heterochroma Wollenw. 1916

Notes:

Distribution: Temperate North America; Europe.

Substrate: Wide range of plant parts (particularly tubers), soil.

Disease Note: Storage rot.

Host: Various hosts.
