Notes: Neonectria coccinea sensu stricto is restricted to Fagus spp. in Europe. Records from Fagus in North America are referred to Nectria coccinea var. faginata, transferred to species rank in the genus Neonectria (Castlebury et al. 2006).

Distribution: Reported from Europe, North America, New Zealand, Africa (Malawi). According to Castlebury et al. (2006), Neonectria coccinea sensu stricto is restricted to Fagus spp. in Europe. Records from Fagus in North America are referred to Nectria

Substrate: Hardwood trees.

Disease Note: Cankers.

Host: Restricted to Fagus (Fagaceae). Many misidentified reports.
