Nomenclature data for Fusarium fujikuroi

Fusarium fujikuroi Nirenberg 1976 (Ascomycetes, Hypocreales)

= Lisea fujikuroi Sawada 1919

    ≡ Gibberella fujikuroi (Sawada) S. Ito 1931

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: On various plant parts.

Disease Note: Top rot, cause of foolish seedling/bakanae disease/fusariosis.

Host: Various plant hosts.
