Nomenclature data for Fusarium niveum

Fusarium oxysporum f. sp. niveum (E.F. Sm.) W.C. Snyder & H.N. Hansen 1940 (Ascomycetes, Hypocreales)

≡ Fusarium niveum E.F. Sm. 1899

≡Fusarium bulbigenum var. niveum (E.F. Sm.) Wollenw. 1931

Notes: Forma speciales not recognized by the International Code of Nomenclature.

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Collar, roots, stem.

Disease Note: Wilt, discoloration, necrosis, death.

Host: Citrullus lanatus (Cucurbitaceae).
