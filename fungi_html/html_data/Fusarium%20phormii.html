Nomenclature data for Fusarium phormii

Colletotrichum phormii (Henn.) D.F. Farr & Rossman 2006 (Ascomycetes, Phyllachorales)

≡ Fusarium phormii Henn. 1898

≡Gloeosporium phormii (Henn.) Sacc. 1915  Note: Isonym of (Henn.) Wollenw. 1916.

= Physalospora phormii J. Schröt. 1894

    ≡ Glomerella phormii (J. Schröt.) D.F. Farr & Rossman 2006

    ≡ Hypostegium phormii (J. Schröt.) Theiss. 1916

= Cryptosporium rhodocyclum Mont. 1909

    ≡ Colletotrichum rhodocyclum (Mont.) Petr. 1927

    ≡ Gloeosporidium rhodocyclum (Mont.) Höhn. 1920

Distribution: Africa (South Africa), Europe, Australia, New Zealand; North America (USA: OR).

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Phormium spp. (Asphodelaceae).
