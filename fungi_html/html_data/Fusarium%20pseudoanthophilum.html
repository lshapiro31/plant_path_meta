Nomenclature data for Fusarium pseudoanthophilum

Fusarium brevicatenulatum Nirenberg, O'Donnell, Kroschel & Andrianaivo 1998 (Ascomycetes, Hypocreales)

= Fusarium pseudoanthophilum Nirenberg, O'Donnell & Mubat. 1998

Notes: Amata et al. (2010) treated these two species as synonyms and established F. brevicatenulatum as the species having nomenclatural priority (ICBN Art. 11.5) even though they incorrectly state that it already had priority.

Distribution: Africa, Europe (Italy).

Substrate: Inflorescences/infructescences.

Disease Note: Ear rot, mycotoxin formation.

Host: Poaceae.
