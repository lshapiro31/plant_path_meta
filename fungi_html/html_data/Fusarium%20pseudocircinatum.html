Nomenclature data for Fusarium pseudocircinatum

Fusarium pseudocircinatum O'Donnell & Nirenberg 1998 (Ascomycetes, Hypocreales)

Distribution: Papua New Guinea, Philippines, Central America (Panama), Caribbean (Dominican Republlic), North America (Mexico), Africa (Ghana), Asia (Indonesia), Australia.

Substrate: Shoots, inflorescences.

Disease Note: Mango malformation disease (MMD).

Host: Mangifera indica (Anacardiaceae), Solanum sp. (Solanaceae), Musa acuminata (Musaceae), Vanilla planifolia (Orchidaceae).
