Nomenclature data for Fusarium sporotrichioides

Fusarium sporotrichioides Sherb. 1915 (Ascomycetes, Hypocreales)

≡Fusarium sporotrichiella var. sporotrichioides (Sherb.) Bilai 1953  Note: Nom. inval.?

Distribution: Temperate and tropical regions.

Substrate: Roots, leaves, seeds, fruit.

Disease Note: Root rot, leaf spot, dieback, etc.

Host: Numerous hosts.
