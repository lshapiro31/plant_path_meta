Nomenclature data for Fusarium sterilihyphosum

Fusarium sterilihyphosum Britz, Marasas & M.J. Wingf. 2002 (Ascomycetes, Hypocreales)

Distribution: Africa (Egypt, South Africa), Asia (India), South America (Brazil).

Substrate: Flowers, stems.

Disease Note: Mango malformation disease.

Host: Mangifera indica (Anacardiaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Britz, H., Steenkamp, E.T., Coutinho, T.A., Wingfield, B.D., Marasas, W.F.O., and Wingfield, M.J. 2002. Two new species of Fusarium section Liseola associated with mango malformation. Mycologia 94: 722-730.
