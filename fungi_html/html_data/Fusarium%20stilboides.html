Nomenclature data for Fusarium stilboides

Fusarium stilboides Wollenw. 1924 (Ascomycetes, Hypocreales)

= Fusarium lateritium var. longum Wollenw. 1931

= Gibberella stilboides W.L. Gordon 1971

Distribution: General in tropical areas; also known from temperate regions.

Disease Note: Bark disease, collar rot, damping off.

Updated on Dec 18, 2013
