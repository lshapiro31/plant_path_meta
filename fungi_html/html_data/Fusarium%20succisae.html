Nomenclature data for Fusarium succisae

Fusarium succisae J. Schröt. 1892 (Ascomycetes, Hypocreales)

Distribution: Asia (Korea), Europe (Germany).

Substrate: Flowers.

Disease Note: Flower rot.

Host: Coreopsis verticillata (Asteraceae), Succisa pratensis (Dipsacaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Choi, H.-W., Hong, S.K., Lee, Y.K., and Kim, W.G. 2014. First report of Fusarium succisae causing flower rot on thread-leaf Coreopsis. Pl. Dis. 98(7): 1002.
