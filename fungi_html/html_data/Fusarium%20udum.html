Nomenclature data for Fusarium udum

Fusarium udum E.J. Butler 1913 (Ascomycetes, Hypocreales)

≡Fusarium oxysporum f. udum (E.J. Butler) W.C. Snyder & H.N. Hansen 1940

= Gibberella indica B. Rai & R.S. Upadhyay 1982

= Fusarium udum var. cajani Padwick 1940

Notes: See also Rai and Upadhyay (Mycologia 74:343. 1982 as Gibberella indica).

Distribution: Asia (India, Vietnam), Europe (Germany, Italy), Africa (Tanzania, Uganda).

Substrate: Crown.

Disease Note: Dieback, wilt.

Host: Primarily on Cajanus and Crotalaria (Fabaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Booth, C. 1978. Fusarium udum. C.M.I. Descr. Pathog. Fungi Bact. 575: 1-2.

Wang, C.L., and Dai, Y.L. 2018. First report of sunn hemp Fusarium wilt caused by Fusarium udum f. sp. crotalariae in Taiwan. Pl. Dis. 102(5): 1031.
