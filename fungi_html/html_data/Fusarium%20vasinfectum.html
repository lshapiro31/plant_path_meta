Nomenclature data for Fusarium vasinfectum

Fusarium oxysporum f. vasinfectum (G.F. Atk.) W.C. Snyder & H.N. Hansen 1940 (Ascomycetes, Hypocreales)

≡ Fusarium vasinfectum Atk. 1892

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Plant material, soil.

Disease Note: Vascular wilt of cotton. Root decay, seedling blight, wilt, etc.

Host: Multiple species in multiple families, especially Gossypium spp. (Malvaceae).

Supporting Literature:
