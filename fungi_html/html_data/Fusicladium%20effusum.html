Nomenclature data for Fusicladium effusum

Venturia effusa (G. Winter) Rossman & W.C. Allen 2016 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

≡ Fusicladium effusum G. Winter 1885

≡Fusicladosporium effusum (G. Winter) Partridge & Morgan-Jones 2003

= Fusicladium caryigenum Ellis & Langl. 1888

    ≡ Cladosporium caryigenum (Ellis & Langl.) Gottwald 1982

[≡Cladosporium effusum (G. Winter) Demaree 1928 - illegitimate later homonym, not included in search]

Notes: From Cladosporium effusum - Not Berk. & M.A. Curtis 1875. Nomen illeg.

Distribution: Africa, North America, Central America, South America, New Zealand.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Carya spp. and possibly Juglans spp. (Juglandaceae).
