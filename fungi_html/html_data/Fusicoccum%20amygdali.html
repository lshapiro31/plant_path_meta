Nomenclature data for Fusicoccum amygdali

Diaporthe amygdali (Delacr.) Udayanga, Crous & K.D. Hyde 2012 (Ascomycetes, Diaporthales)

≡ Fusicoccum amygdali Delacr. 1905

≡Phomopsis amygdali (Delacr.) J.J. Tuset & M.T. Portilla 1989

Distribution: Africa (South Africa), Europe (Portugal), North America (USA; GA).

Substrate: Twigs, leaves.

Disease Note: Canker, blight.

Host: Typically Prunus spp. (Rosaceae), but also Vitis vinifera (Vitaceae), Pieris japonica (Ericaceae).
