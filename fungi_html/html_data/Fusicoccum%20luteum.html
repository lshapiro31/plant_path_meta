Nomenclature data for Fusicoccum luteum

Neofusicoccum luteum (Pennycook & Samuels) Crous, Slippers & A.J.L. Phillips 2006 (Incertae sedis, Incertae sedis)

≡ Fusicoccum luteum Pennycook & Samuels 1985

= Botryosphaeria lutea A.J.L. Phillips 2002

Distribution: Australia, Europe (Italy, Portugal, Spain), New Zealand, North America (USA: CA), South America (Uruguay).

Substrate: Fruit, leaf, stem.

Disease Note: Canker, stem-end rot, leaf necrosis, Dothiorella canker. This pest is quarantine/actionable.

Host: Numerous hosts including Persea americana (Lauraceae): many reports from Vitis (Vitaceae).
