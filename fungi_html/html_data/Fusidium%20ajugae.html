Nomenclature data for Fusidium ajugae

Ramularia ajugae (Niessl) Sacc. 1881 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Fusidium ajugae Niessl 1860

≡Cylindrosporium ajugae (Niessl) J. Schröt. 1897

= Ramularia tozziae Lindau 1906

Distribution: Africa, Asia, Europe.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Ajuga spp. (Lamiaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Braun, U. 1998. A Monograph of Cercosporella, Ramularia and Allied Genera (Phytopathogenic Hyphomycetes). IHW-Verlag 2: 337.
