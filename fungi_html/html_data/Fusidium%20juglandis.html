Notes: Nomenclatural status and publication date of Exobasidium juglandis uncertain.

Distribution: Temperate northern hemisphere.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot, witches' broom, catkin blight.

Host: Primarily on Juglandaceae.
