Nomenclature data for Fusidium pteridis

Cryptomycina pteridis (Rebent. : Fr.) Höhn. 1918 [1917] (Ascomycetes, Incertae sedis)

≡ Sphaeria pteridis Rebent. 1804

≡Cryptomyces pteridis (Rebent. : Fr.) Rehm 1896

≡Phyllachora pteridis (Rebent. : Fr.) Fuckel 1870 [1869]

= Fusidium pteridis Kalchbr. 1861

    ≡ Cryptomycella pteridis (Kalchbr.) Höhn. 1925

Notes: Leaf-roll disease of ferns (DiCosmo et al. Mycotaxon 11:32. 1984).

Distribution: Temperate regions.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf roll of ferns, tar spot.
