≡ Fusidium torulosum Berk. & M.A. Curtis 1875

≡Fusoma torulosum (Berk. & M.A. Curtis) Sacc. 1886

= Fusarium elegantum Pratt 1918

= Fusarium polymorphum Matr. 1892

= Fusarium sambucinum var. coeruleum Wollenw. 1917

= Fusarium sclerodermatis Oudem. 1889

= Fusarium subpallidum Sherb. 1915

Distribution: Australia, Europe, North America (Canada).

Host: Diverse hosts; commonly grasses.
