Nomenclature data for Ganoderma orbiforme

Ganoderma orbiforme (Fr.) Ryvarden 2000 (Basidiomycetes, Polyporales)

≡ Polyporus orbiformis Fr. 1838

≡Ganoderma lucidum var. orbiformis (Fr.) Rick 1960

≡Scindalma orbiforme (Fr.) Kuntze 1898

≡Fomes orbiformis (Fr.) Cooke 1885

= Ganoderma boninense Pat. 1889

Notes: Colonisation of rubber wood and oil palm blocks by monokaryons and dikaryons of Ganoderma boninense - implications to infection in the field. Journal Planter 2003 Vol. 79 No. 922 pp. 31-38. Ganoderma - the scourge of oil palm in the coastal area. Gurmit Singh. Proceedings of Ganoderma workshop, Bangi, Selangor, Malaysia, 11 September 1990. 1991 pp. 7-35.

Distribution: Asia.

Substrate: Butt, roots.

Disease Note: Dry rot.

Host: Arecaceae; important pathogen of oil palm.
