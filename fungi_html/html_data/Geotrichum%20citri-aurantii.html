Nomenclature data for Geotrichum citri-aurantii

Geotrichum citri-aurantii (Ferraris) E.E. Butler 1988 (Saccharomycetes, Saccharomycetales)

≡ Oidium citri-aurantii Ferraris 1899

≡Geotrichum candidum var. citri-aurantii (Ferraris) R. Ciferri & F. Cif. 1955

≡Oospora citri-aurantii (Ferraris) Sacc. & P. Syd. 1902

= Galactomyces citri-aurantii E.E. Butler 1988

Distribution: Worldwide.

Disease Note: Sour rot.

Host: Especially Citrus spp. (Rutaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Butler, E., Fogle, D., and Miranda, M. 1988. Galactomyces citri-aurantii a newly found teleomorph of Geotrichumcitri-aurantii the cause of sour rot of citrus fruit. Mycotaxon 33: 197-212.
