Nomenclature data for Gerwasia chinensis

Gerwasia rubi Racib. 1909 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

= Uredo chinensis Dietel 1898

= Uromyces chinensis Dietel 1912

  ≡Gerwasia chinensis (Dietel) Hirats. 1939

    ≡ Mainsia chinensis (Dietel) H.S. Jacks. 1931

    ≡ Spirechina chinensis (Dietel) Dietel 1928

= Uredo constellata Berk. & M.A. Curtis 1858

= Gerwasia fasciculata Arthur & Cummins 1937

Notes: Oldest epithet not in correct genus.

Distribution: Asia, North America (Mexico). From Uredo constellata - Asia (Japan).

Substrate: Leaves. From Uredo constellata - Leaves.

Disease Note: Rust.

Host: Rubus spp. (Rosaceae).
