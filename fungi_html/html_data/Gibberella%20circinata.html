Nomenclature data for Gibberella circinata

Fusarium circinatum Nirenberg & O'Donnell 1998 (Ascomycetes, Hypocreales)

≡Fusarium subglutinans f. sp. pini J.C. Correll et al. 1991

= Gibberella circinata Nirenberg & O'Donnell 1998

Notes: Previously referred to as Fusarium moniliforme var. subglutinans Wollenw. & Reinking.

Distribution: Africa (South Africa), North America (USA, Mexico), South America, Asia.

Substrate: Trunk, branches, roots, shoots, cones, seedlings.

Disease Note: Pitch canker, damping off, collar rot, root rot.

Host: Pinus spp., Pseudotsuga menziesii (Pinaceae), ; asymptomatic colonizer of Poaceae.
