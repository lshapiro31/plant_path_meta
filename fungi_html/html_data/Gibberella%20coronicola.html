Nomenclature data for Gibberella coronicola

Fusarium pseudograminearum O'Donnell & T. Aoki 1999 (Ascomycetes, Hypocreales)

= Gibberella coronicola T. Aoki & O'Donnell 1999

Distribution: Asia, North America, South Africa.

Substrate: Inflorescences.

Disease Note: Blight.

Host: Cereal crops.
