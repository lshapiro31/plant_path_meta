Nomenclature data for Gibberella thapsina

Fusarium thapsinum Klittich, J.F. Leslie, P.E. Nelson & Marasas 1997 (Ascomycetes, Hypocreales)

= Gibberella thapsina Klittich, J.F. Leslie, P.E. Nelson & Marasas 1997

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Stems, seeds, tubers, roots, crown.

Disease Note: Seedling death, stalk rot; commonly an endophyte.

Host: Sorghum sp. (Poaceae) and other grasses, Glycine max, Arachis hypogaea (Fabaceae), Musa sp. (Musaceae).
