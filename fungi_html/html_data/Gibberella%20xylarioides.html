Nomenclature data for Gibberella xylarioides

Fusarium xylarioides Steyaert 1948 (Ascomycetes, Hypocreales)

≡Fusarium lateritium f. sp. xylarioides W.L. Gordon 1965

≡Fusarium oxysporum f. xylarioides (Steyaert) Delassus 1954

= Gibberella xylarioides R. Heim & Saccas 1950

Distribution: Africa, South America (Brazil).

Substrate: Vascular tissue.

Disease Note: Vascular wilt, tracheomycosis of coffee.

Host: Coffea spp. (Rubiaceae), Glycine max (Fabaceae), Gossypium hirsutum (Malvaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Booth, C., and Waterston, J.M. 1964. Gibberella xylarioides. C.M.I. Descr. Pathog. Fungi Bact. 24: 1-2.
