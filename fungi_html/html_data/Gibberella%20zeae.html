Nomenclature data for Gibberella zeae

Fusarium graminearum Schwabe 1838 (Ascomycetes, Hypocreales)

= Sphaeria zeae Schwein. : Fr. 1822  Note: From Gibberella zeae - The name Gibberella saubinetii (= Gibberella cyanogena) has frequently been misapplied to the teleomorph of this species. Non Fusarium zeae (Westend.) Sacc.

  ≡Gibberella zeae (Schwein. : Fr.) Petch 1936

    =  Gibberella roseum f. cerealis (Cooke) W.C. Snyder & H.N. Hansen

Notes: Use of oldest epithet blocked by Fusarium zeae (Westend.) Sacc. 1886.

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Roots, shoots, culms, heads, kernels, leaves, flowers.

Disease Note: Seedling blight, pre- and post-emergence blight, root and foot rot, brown rot, culm decay, head or kernel blight (scab or ear scab) of cereals. Leaf and flower rot of ornamental flowers.

Host: Principal hosts: Poaceae, including Zea mays (corn), Triticum aestivum (wheat), and Oryza sativa (rice). Additional hosts: various plant families.
