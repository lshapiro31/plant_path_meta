Nomenclature data for Gliocladiopsis sagariensis

Gliocladiopsis tenuis (Bugnic.) Crous & M.J. Wingf. 1993 (Ascomycetes, Hypocreales)

≡ Cylindrocarpon tenue Bugnic. 1939

≡Cylindrocladium tenue (Bugnic.) T. Watan. 1994

= Gliocladiopsis sagariensis S.B. Saksena 1954

= Cylindrocladiella tenuis Chuan Fei Zhang & P.K. Chi 1996  Note: Possible synonym.

= Glionectria tenuis Crous & C.L. Schoch 2000

Distribution: Widespread especially in subtropical to tropical regions.

Substrate: On leaves, fruits and rhizosphere.

Disease Note: Root rot.

Host: Multiple genera in multiple families.

Supporting Literature:

Crous, P.W. 2002. Taxonomy and pathology of Cylindrocladium (Calonectria) and allied genera. American Phytopathological Society, St. Paul, Minnesota : 278.
