Notes: Sogonov et al. (2007) discussed numerous anamorph names for this fungus.

Distribution: Asia (Korea), Europe, North America.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Anthracnose, blight.

Host: Primarily Fagus, Quercus, and Tilia, but several other hardwoods are hosts.
