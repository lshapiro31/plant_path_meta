Nomenclature data for Gloeosporium limetticola

Colletotrichum limetticola (R.E. Clausen) Damm, P.F. Cannon & Crous 2012 (Ascomycetes, Phyllachorales)

≡ Gloeosporium limetticola R.E. Clausen 1912

Distribution: Widespread in Citrus-growing regions.

Substrate: Twigs and leaves.

Disease Note: Anthracnose, wither tip.

Host: Citrus (Rutaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Damm, U., Cannon, P.F., Woudenberg, J.H.C., and Crous, P.W. 2012. The Colletotrichum acutatum species complex. Stud. Mycol. 73: 37-113.
