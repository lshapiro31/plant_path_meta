Nomenclature data for Gloeosporium nymphaearum

Colletotrichum nymphaeae (Pass.) Aa 1978 (Ascomycetes, Phyllachorales)

≡ Ascochyta nymphaeae Pass. 1877

≡Phyllosticta nymphaeae (Pass.) Pass. 1888

= Colletotrichum mahoniae Fabric. 1950

= Ramularia nymphaeae Bres. 1894

    ≡ Ovularia nymphaeae (Bres.) Bres. 1896

= Gloeosporium nymphaearum Allesch. 1895

    ≡ Ramularia nymphaearum (Allesch.) Ramsb. 1914

    ≡ Ovularia nymphaearum (Allesch.) Bres. & Allesch. 1903

    ≡ Ovulariella nymphaearum (Allesch.) Bubák & Kabát 1912

Distribution: Temperate regions including South Africa.

Substrate: Primarily leaves but also isolated from petiole, fruit and crown of strawberry.

Disease Note: Leaf spot and anthracnose, bitter rot.

Host: Primarily Nymphaeaceae; Malus, Fragaria spp. (Rosaceae), Protea spp. (Proteaceae).
