Nomenclature data for Gloeosporium robergei

Monostichella robergei (Desm.) Höhn. 1916 (Ascomycetes, Helotiales)

≡ Gloeosporium robergei Desm. 1853

Notes: Type of the genus Monostichella. Sexual state is said to be Sphaerognomonia (Diaporthales).

Distribution: Temperate North America; Europe.

Substrate: On living leaves.

Disease Note: Anthracnose.

Host: Primarily on Carpinus and Ostrya (Betulaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Sutton, B.C. 1980. The Coelomycetes. Fungi Imperfecti with Pycnidia, Acervuli and Stromata. Commonwealth Mycological Institute, Kew, Surrey, England, 696 pages.
