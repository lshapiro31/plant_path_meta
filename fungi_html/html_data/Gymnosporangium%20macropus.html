Nomenclature data for Gymnosporangium macropus

Gymnosporangium juniperi-virginianae Schwein. 1822 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

≡Podisoma juniperi-virginianae (Schwein.) Fr. 1832

= Gymnosporangium macropus Link 1825

= Roestelia pyrate Thaxt. 1887

= Aecidium pyratum Schwein. 1832

= Gymnosporangium virginianum Spreng. 1827

Notes: A few minor forgotten synonyms listed by Arthur (1921) are omitted here.

Distribution: North America (Canada, USA).

Substrate: Fruits, galls, leaves, stems.

Disease Note: Heteroecious rust. Cedar-apple rust.

Host: Crataegus spp., Malus spp., Pyrus spp. (Rosaceae) for aecial state, Juniperus spp. (Cupressaceae) for telial state.
