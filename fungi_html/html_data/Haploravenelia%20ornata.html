Nomenclature data for Haploravenelia ornata

Ravenelia ornata Syd. & P. Syd. 1906 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

≡Haploravenelia ornata (Syd. & P. Syd.) Dietel 1928

= Uredo abri Henn. 1908

Notes: Uromyces kaernbachii Henn. (1892)a probable but not certain synonym; a nomenclatural change would be necessary if the connection were proven.

Distribution: Africa, Asia, Papua New Guinea(?).

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Autoecious rust.

Host: Abrus spp. (Fabaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Cannon, P.F. 2008. Ravenelia ornata. I.M.I. Descr. Fungi Bact. 1757: 1-3.
