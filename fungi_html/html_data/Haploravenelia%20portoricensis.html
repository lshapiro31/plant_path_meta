Nomenclature data for Haploravenelia portoricensis

Ravenelia portoricensis Arthur 1904 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

≡Haploravenelia portoricensis (Arthur) Syd. 1921  Note: Based on Arthur's description of uredinial anamorph in a teleomorph genus.

= Ravenelia arthuri Long. 1906

  Variant spelling Ravenelia arthurii Long 1906

Notes: Description of uredinial anamorph in teleomorph genus.

Distribution: Central America (Cuba, Puerto Rico, Santo Domingo, Jamaica), South America (Venezuela).

Disease Note: Rust.

Host: Cassia emarginata (Fabaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Baxter, J.W. 1965. Studies of North American species of Ravenelia. Mycologia 57: 77-84.
