Nomenclature data for Harknessia arctostaphyli

Harknessia arctostaphyli Cooke & Harkn. 1885 (Ascomycetes, Diaporthales)

Distribution: North America (Mexico, USA).

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf blight, defoliation.

Host: Arctostaphylos spp. (Ericaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Moreno-Rico, O., Groenewald, J.Z., and Crous, P.W. 2014. Foliicolous fungi from Arctostaphylos pungens in Mexico. IMA Fungus 5(1): 7-15.
