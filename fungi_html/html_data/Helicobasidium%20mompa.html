Nomenclature data for Helicobasidium mompa

Helicobasidium mompa Tanaka 1891 (Ustilaginomycetes, Incertae sedis)

≡Septobasidium mompa (Tanaka) Racib. 1909

Distribution: Africa, Asia.

Substrate: Roots.

Disease Note: Root rot. Violet root rot.

Host: Multiple genera in multiple families.

Supporting Literature:

Henk, D.A., and Vigalys, R. 2007. Molecular Phylogeny Suggest a single origin of Insect Symbiosis in the Pucciniomycetes with support for some relationships within the genus Septobasidium. Amer. J. Bot. 94: 1515-1526.
