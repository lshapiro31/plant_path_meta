Nomenclature data for Helicosingula leucadendri

Helicosingula leucadendri P.S. van Wyk, Marasas, Baard & Knox-Dav. 1985 (Ascomycetes, Incertae sedis)

Distribution: Africa (South Africa).

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Helicosingula leaf spot.

Host: Leucadendron spp. (Proteaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Crous, P.W., Denman, S., Taylor, J.E., Swart, L., and Palm, M.E. 2004. Cultivation and diseases of Proteaceae: Leucadendron, Leucospermum and Protea. Centraalbureau voor Schimmelcultures, Utrecht, 227 pages.
