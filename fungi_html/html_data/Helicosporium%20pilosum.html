≡Acanthostigma scopulum (Cooke & Peck) Peck 1887

= Helicosporium pilosum Ellis & Everh. 1897

Notes: Sphaeria scopula Cooke & Peck 188o is a later homonym of Sowerby 1803.

Distribution: Temperate regions.

Substrate: Decorticated coniferous wood.

Disease Note: Saprobic.

Host: Various woody hosts.
