Nomenclature data for Helminthosporium cheiranthi

Alternaria cheiranthi (Lib.) P.C. Bolle 1924 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

Helminthosporium cheiranthi Lib. 1827  Note: as 'Helmisporium'

Macrosporium cheiranthi (Lib.) Fr. 1832

Notes: Common on wallflower, reported from throughout Europe (Ellis. Dematiaceous Hyphomycetes. p. 472. 1971). The report from United States is questionable.

Distribution: Asia, Europe, South Africa, USA (OK).

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Primarily Brassicaceae but also reported on Apiaceae, Chenopodiaceae, and Fabaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Simmons, E.G. 1995. Alternaria themes and variations (112-144). Mycotaxon 55: 55-163.

Simmons, E.G. 2007. Alternaria. An identification manual. CBS Biodivers. Ser. 6: 1-775.
