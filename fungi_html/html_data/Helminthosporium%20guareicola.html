Nomenclature data for Helminthosporium guareicola

Spiropes guareicola (F. Stevens) Cif. 1955 (Ascomycetes, Incertae sedis)

≡ Helminthosporium guareicola F. Stevens 1918

≡Pleurophragmidium guareicola (F. Stevens) S. Hughes 1958

= Cladosporium elegans var. singaporense Sacc. 1921

Notes: From Helminthosporium guareicola - as 'Helmisporium'

Distribution: Puerto Rico; Ghana; Sierra Leone; Uganda; Papua New Guinea; Solomon Islands; India; Malaya; Sabah; Sarawak; Philippines; Cambodia; Japan.

Substrate: Other fungi on leaves.

Disease Note: Hyperparasite.

Host: Meliola butleri on Citrus grandis.
