Nomenclature data for Helminthosporium hadrotrichoides

Bipolaris hadrotrichoides (Ellis & Everh.) Luttr. 1970 [1969] (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

≡ Helminthosporium hadrotrichoides Ellis & Everh. 1888

Distribution: Central and eastern United States.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Eragrostis spp. (Poaceae).
