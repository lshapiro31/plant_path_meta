Nomenclature data for Helminthosporium nodosum

Strossmayeria atriseda (Saut.) Iturr. 1990 (Ascomycetes, Helotiales)

≡ Peziza atriseda Saut. 1845

≡Tapesia atriseda (Saut.) Poetsch & Schied. 1872

= Helminthosporium nodosum Wallr. 1875

    ≡ Pseudospiropes nodosus (Wallr.) M.B. Ellis 1971

    ≡ Pleurophragmium nodosum (Wallr.) S. Hughes 1958

    ≡ Brachysporium nodosum (Wallr.) Sacc. 1886

Notes: From Helminthosporium nodosum - as 'Helmisporium'

Distribution: Temperate regions.

Substrate: On decorticated wood.

Disease Note: Saprobic.

Host: Various hardwoods.

Supporting Literature:
