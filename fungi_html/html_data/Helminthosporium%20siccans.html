Nomenclature data for Helminthosporium siccans

Pyrenophora lolii Dovaston 1948 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

= Helminthosporium siccans Drechsler 1923

    ≡ Drechslera siccans (Drechsler) Shoemaker 1959

Notes: Oldest epithet not in correct genus.

Distribution: Australia, Europe, North America.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot; seed-borne.

Host: Festuca spp., Lolium spp., Dactylis glomerata, Poa pratensis and other Poaceae.
