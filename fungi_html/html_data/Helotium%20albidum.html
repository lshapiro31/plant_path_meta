Nomenclature data for Helotium albidum

Hymenoscyphus albidus (Gillet) W. Phillips 1887 (Ascomycetes, Helotiales)

≡ Peziza albida Roberge ex Desm. 1851  Note: Not With. 1796 or Lamark 1797. Nomen illeg.

≡Phialea albida Gillet 1881  Note: As 'Desm.'. Is a nom. nov.

≡Helotium robergei Dennis 1956  Note: Nom. nov. Nom. illeg. Superfluous.

≡Helotium scutula var. albidum P. Karst. 1871  Note: As '(Desm.) Karst.'. Is a nom. nov. at this rank.

[≡Helotium albidum (Gillet) Pat. 1885 - illegitimate later homonym, not included in search]  Note: As 'Rob.'. Not P. Crouan & H. Crouan 1867. Nomen illeg.

Notes: As 'Rob'.

Distribution: Europe. Also, possibly Asia (Japan).

Disease Note: Reported to be connected to ash dieback. Recent studies suggest this is a nonpathogenic cryptic species related to the pathogenic H. pseudoalbidus.

Host: Fraxinus (Oleaceae).
