Nomenclature data for Helvella inflata

Rhizina undulata Fr. : Fr. 1815 (Ascomycetes, Pezizales)

= Helvella inflata Schaeff. 1774  Note: From Rhizina inflata - A homonym exists for this name.

    ≡ Rhizina inflata (Schaeff.) P. Karst. 1886

= Rhizina laevigata Fr. 1815

  Variant spelling Rhizina levigata Fr. 1815

= Rhizina laevigata var. praetexta (Ehrenb.) Pers. 1822

  Variant spelling Rhizina levigata var. praetexta (Ehrenb.) Pers. 1822

Notes: Older epithet unpriorable because of sanctioning.

Distribution: Temperate northern hemisphere; Africa (South Africa - introduced).

Substrate: On roots and debris of conifers. Often prevalent after fires.

Disease Note: Root rot.

Host: Primarily Pinaceae; also hardwoods.
