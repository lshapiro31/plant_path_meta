Nomenclature data for Hemibeltrania cinnamomi

Hemibeltrania cinnamomi (Deighton) Piroz. 1963 (Ascomycetes, Incertae sedis)

≡ Hansfordia cinnamomi Deighton 1960

Distribution: Africa, Asia.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot/blotch.

Host: Cinnamomum spp. (Lauraceae).

Supporting Literature:
