Nomenclature data for Hemileia scholzii

Hemileia solaninum (Henn.) Judith & Rossman 2014 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

≡ Uredo solaninum Henn. 1901

= Hemileia scholzii Syd. & P. Syd. 1910

= Uredo scholzii Henn. 1904

Distribution: Africa.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Slight discoloration.

Host: Clerodendron spp. (Lamiaceae).

Supporting Literature:
