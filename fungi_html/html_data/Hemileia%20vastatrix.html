Nomenclature data for Hemileia vastatrix

Hemileia vastatrix Berk. & Broome 1869 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

Distribution: Asia, Africa, South America, Central America (including Mexico), Papua New Guinea.

Substrate: Leaves. Rarely on berries and young shoots. Seed-borne.

Disease Note: Monoecious rust. The most serious disease of coffee, of worldwide significance (Laundon 1964).

Host: Principal host: Coffea spp. Also on Gardenia spp. (Rubiaceae).
