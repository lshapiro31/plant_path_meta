Nomenclature data for Hendersonia acicola

Hendersonia acicola Münch & Tubeuf 1910 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

Notes: Hendersonia considered a synonym of Stagonospora by Sutton (1980).

Distribution: Europe.

Substrate: Needles.

Disease Note: Associated with trees already under attack by and antagonistic toward Lophodermella sulcigena; not itself a pathogen.

Host: Pinus spp. (Pinaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Jalkanen, R. 1985. The occurrence and importance of Lophodermella sulcigena and Hendersonia acicola on Scots pine in Finland. Karstenia 25: 53-61.

Sutton, B.C. 1980. The Coelomycetes. Fungi Imperfecti with Pycnidia, Acervuli and Stromata. Commonwealth Mycological Institute, Kew, Surrey, England, 696 pages.
