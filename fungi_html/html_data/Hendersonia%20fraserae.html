Nomenclature data for Hendersonia fraserae

Sonderhenia eucalypticola (A.R. Davis) H.J. Swart & J. Walker 1988 (Ascomycetes, Incertae sedis)

≡ Hendersonia eucalypticola A.R. Davis 1912

= Hendersonia fraserae Hansf. 1954  Note: Published as 'fraseri'.

= Mycosphaerella walkeri R.F. Park & Keane 1984

Distribution: Australia, New Zealand, North America (USA: CA). Intercepted from Central America (Colombia and Ecuador).

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Eucalyptus spp. (Myrtaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Park, R.F., and Keane, P.J. 1984. Further Mycosphaerella species causing leaf diseases of Eucalyptus. Trans. Brit. Mycol. Soc. 83: 93-105.

Swart, H.J., and Walker, J. 1988. Australian leaf-inhabiting fungi. XXVIII. Hendersonia on Eucalyptus. Trans. Brit. Mycol. Soc. 90: 633-641.
