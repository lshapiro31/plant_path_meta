= Pyrenochaeta hirta Sacc. 1884

Notes: Anamorphs Pyrenochaeta hirta Sacc. 1884; Hendersonia hirta Sacc. 1883 (synanamorphs). From Hendersonia hirta - Invalid. No description of anamorph. As Sphaeria (Hendersonia) hirta Fr. From Pyrenochaeta hirta - Type specimen does not exist; generic affinity uncertain (Schneider 1984). Pyrenochaeta hirta is a synanamorph of Hendersonia hirta Sacc.

Distribution: CO; Europe. From Hendersonia hirta - North America (USA:CO), Europe.

Septoriella hirta (Sacc.) M. Hern.-Restr. & Crous 2015 (Ascomycetes, Dothideales)

≡ Wojnowicia hirta Kuntze 1893

= Hendersonia crastophila Sacc. 1878

= Hendersonia graminis McAlpine 1904

    ≡ Wojnowicia graminis (McAlpine) Sacc. & D. Sacc. 1906

[≡Hendersonia hirta J. Schröt. 1890 - illegitimate later homonym, not included in search]

= Wojnowicia tenella Pat. 1897

Notes: From Hendersonia hirta - Not Sacc. 1883 nor (Fr.) Curr 1859 From Wojnowicia hirta - Not (Fr.) Zerov 1971. Saccardo (1892, Sylloge Fungorum) validly published the genus Wojnowicia by providing a diagnosis and indicating the type, but he definitely did not associate the epithet 'hirta' with the new genus name as per ICBN Art. 33.1 and make a new combination. Kuntze in Revisio Generum Plantarum (1893) definitely associated the epithet 'hirta' with Wojnowicia. Since Hendersonia hirta J. Schröt. 1890 is a nom. illeg., there is no parenthetical author and the name is a nom. nov. attributed to Kuntze.

Distribution: From Wojnowicia hirta - Cosmopolitan.

Disease Note: From Wojnowicia hirta - Foot rot and root rot of grasses.

Host: From Wojnowicia hirta - Poaceae.

Supporting Literature:

Punithalingam, E. 1983. Wojnowicia hirta. C.M.I. Descr. Pathog. Fungi Bact. 773: 1-3.

Sutton, B.C. 1975. Wojnowicia and Angiopomopsis. Ceska Mykol. 29: 97-104.

Updated prior to 1989
