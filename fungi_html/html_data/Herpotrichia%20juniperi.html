Nomenclature data for Herpotrichia juniperi

Herpotrichia juniperi (Duby) Petr. 1925 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

≡ Sphaeria juniperi Duby 1854

≡Laestadia juniperi (Duby) Sacc. 1891

= Herpotrichia nigra R. Hartig 1888

Notes: Barr (2009) lists several other synonyms. This appears to be a species complex (Schneider et al. 2009).

Distribution: Northern hemisphere.

Substrate: Twigs, needles.

Disease Note: Snow mold.

Host: Various conifers.

Supporting Literature:
