Nomenclature data for Herpotrichia parasitica

Nematostoma parasiticum (R. Hartig) M.E. Barr 1997 (Ascomycetes, Incertae sedis)

≡ Trichosphaeria parasitica R. Hartig 1884

≡Herpotrichia parasitica (R. Hartig) Rostr. 1890

≡Acanthostigma parasiticum (R. Hartig) Sacc. 1891

= Pyrenochaeta parasitica Freyer & Aa 1975

Notes:

Distribution: Europe, North America (Canada), Asia (Japan).

Substrate: Living needles.

Disease Note: Needle browning.

Host: Abies and Tsuga spp. (Pinaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Barr, M.E. 1997. Notes on some 'dimeriaceous' fungi. Mycotaxon 64: 149-171.
