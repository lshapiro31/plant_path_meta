Nomenclature data for Herpotrichiella longispora

Acanthostigma longisporum (Remler) Reblova & M.E. Barr 2000 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

≡ Herpotrichiella longispora Remler 1980 [1979]

≡Acanthostigmina longispora (Remler) J.L. Crane, Shearer & M.E. Barr 1998

= Tubeufia alpina L. Holm & Nograsek 1990

    ≡ Acanthostigmina alpina (L. Holm & Nograsek) J.L. Crane, Shearer & M.E. Barr 1998

Distribution: Canada (BC); Europe.

Substrate: On dead branches (Remler. Biblioth. 68:94. 1979).

Disease Note: Saprobic.

Host: Primarily on Ericaceae; also Rosaceae.

Supporting Literature:

Crane, J.L., Shearer, C.A., and Barr, M.E. 1998. A revision of Boerlagiomyces with notes and a key to the saprobic genera of Tubeufiaceae. Canad. J. Bot. 76: 602-612.

Reblova, M., and Barr, M.E. 2000. The genus Acanthostigma (Tubeufiaceae, Pleosporales). Sydowia 52: 258-285.
