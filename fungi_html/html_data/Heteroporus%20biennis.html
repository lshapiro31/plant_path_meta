Nomenclature data for Heteroporus biennis

Abortiporus biennis (Bull. : Fr.) Singer 1944 (Basidiomycetes, Polyporales)

≡ Boletus biennis Bull. : Fr. 1789

≡Heteroporus biennis (Bull. : Fr.) Lázaro Ibiza 1916

≡Polyporus biennis (Bull. : Fr.) Fr. 1838 [1836]

= Polyporus distortus (Schwein. : Fr.) Fr. 1920

Notes: Gilbertson & Ryvarden (North American Polypores 1:85. 1986) stated that the fungus reported as Polyporus oleae on olive trees in CA is A. biennis. Buchanan and Hood (New Zealand J. Bot. 30:95. 1992).

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Roots, butt, stumps.

Disease Note: White heart-rot.

Host: Numerous hosts.

Supporting Literature:
