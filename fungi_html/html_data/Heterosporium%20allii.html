Nomenclature data for Heterosporium allii

Cladosporium allii (Ellis & G. Martin) P.M. Kirk & J.G. Crompton 1984 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Heterosporium allii Ellis & G. Martin 1885

Distribution: Temperate regions.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf blotch.

Host: Allium and other genera (Liliaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Bensch, K., Braun, U., Groenewald, J.Z., and Crous, P.W. 2012. The genus Cladosporium. Stud. Mycol. 72: 1-401.
