Nomenclature data for Heterosporium magnusianum

Cladosporium ossifragi (Rostr.) U. Braun & K. Schub. 2007 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Napicladium ossifragi Rostr. 1901

≡Heterosporium ossifragi (Rostr.) Lind 1913

= Heterosporium magnusianum Jaap 1902

    ≡ Cladosporium magnusianum (Jaap) M.B. Ellis 1976

Distribution: Europe (Austria, Denmark, Germany, Great Britain, ireland, Norway).

Substrate: Living or fading leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot (indistinct), occasionally discolorations.

Host: Narthecium ossifragum (Nartheciaceae) .
