Nomenclature data for Heterosporium ornithogali

Cladosporium ornithogali (Klotzsch ex Cooke) G.A. de Vries 1952 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Heterosporium ornithogali Klotzsch ex Cooke 1877

= Didymellina ornithogali J.E. Jacques 1941

    ≡ Mycosphaerella ornithogali (J. Jacques) G.A. De Vries

    ≡ Davidiella ornithogali (J.E. Jacques) Crous & U. Braun 2003

= Heterosporium ornithogali var. minus Bauemler 1897

Notes: From Didymellina ornithogali - From Mycosphaerella ornithogali - Known primarily in the anamorphic state.

Distribution: Africa (Kenya, South Africa), Europe (numerous countries), North America (USA). From Didymellina ornithogali - From Mycosphaerella ornithogali - Northern United States; Europe.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spots; elongated ellipsoid patches.

Host: Ornithogalum spp. (Asparagaceae) and Gagea spp. (Liliaceae). Unconfirmed reports on Muscari spp. (Asparagaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Bensch, K., Braun, U., Groenewald, J.Z., and Crous, P.W. 2012. The genus Cladosporium. Stud. Mycol. 72: 1-401.
