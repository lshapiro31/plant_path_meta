= Heterosporium tuberculans Ellis & Everh. 1893

= Heterosporium typharum Cooke & Massee 1888

= Cladosporium vincae Fairm. 1911  Note: Identical with C. macrocarpum fide Braun et al. (2008).

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Dead leaves, stems and other organic material.

Disease Note: Inflorescence blight, leaf spot.

Host: Diverse plant families.
