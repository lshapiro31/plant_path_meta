Nomenclature data for Heterosporium tropaeoli

Acroconidiella tropaeoli (Bondartsev) J.C. Lindq. & Alippi 1964 (Ascomycetes, Incertae sedis)

≡ Heterosporium tropaeoli Bondartsev 1947

Distribution: North and South America; Africa; Australia; East Indies.

Substrate: Living leaves, seeds.

Disease Note: Leaf spot, discoloration.

Host: Tropaeolum majus (Tropaeolaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Ellis, M.B. 1968. Acroconidiella tropaeoli. C.M.I. Descr. Pathog. Fungi Bact. 161: 1-2.

Vieira, B.S., and Barreto, R.W. 2002. First record of leaf-spot caused by Acroconidiella tropaeoli on Tropaeolum majus in Brazil. New Disease Reports 5: 12.
