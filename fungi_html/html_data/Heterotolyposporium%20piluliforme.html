Nomenclature data for Heterotolyposporium piluliforme

Heterotolyposporium piluliforme (Berk.) Vánky 1997 (Ustilaginomycetes, Ustilaginales)

≡ Uredo piluliformis Berk. 1843

≡Sorosporium piluliforme (Berk.) McAlpine 1910

≡Tolyposporium piluliforme (Berk.) M. Piepenbr. & Begerow 2000

≡Cintractia piluliformis (Berk.) Henn. 1898

≡Ustilago piluliformis (Berk.) Tul. & C. Tul. 1847

= Ustilago marmorata Berk. 1872

= Ustilago muelleriana Thüm. 1878

    ≡ Cintractia muelleriana (Thüm.) Cif. 1931

Distribution: Africa, Australasia.

Substrate: Various plant parts.

Disease Note: Smut.

Host: Juncus spp. (Juncaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Vanky, K. 2012. Smut Fungi of the World. APS Press, St. Paul, Minnesota, 1458 pages.
