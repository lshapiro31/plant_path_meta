Nomenclature data for Homostegia diplocarpa

Phyllachora sylvatica Sacc. & Speg. 1878 (Ascomycetes, Phyllachorales)

    Variant spelling Phyllachora silvatica Sacc. & Speg.

= Homostegia diplocarpa Ellis & Everh. 1897

    ≡ Phyllachora diplocarpa (Ellis & Everh.) Ellis & Everh. 1897

Distribution: Temperate regions; Dominican Republic; Peru.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Tar spot.

Host: Distichlis spicata, Festuca spp., Spartina spp., Sporobolus spp. (Poaceae).

Supporting Literature:
