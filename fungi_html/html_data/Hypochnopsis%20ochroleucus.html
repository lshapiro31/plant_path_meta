Nomenclature data for Hypochnopsis ochroleucus

Ceratobasidium ochroleucum (F. Noack) Ginns & M.N. Lefebvre 1993 (Basidiomycetes, Ceratobasidiales)

≡ Hypochnopsis ochroleucus F. Noack 1898

      Hypochnus ochroleucus F. Noack 1902

      Corticium ochroleucum (F. Noack) Burt 1915

= Corticium stevensii Burt 1918

    ≡ Corticium stevensii (Burt) Venkatar. 1973

Notes: From Corticium stevensii - According to Talbot (Persoonia 3:401. 1965), this species belongs in or near Ceratobasidium.

Distribution: Widespread in warm temperate and tropical regions.

Substrate: Living leaves and branches.

Disease Note: Thread blight, burning-the-wire disease.

Host: Woody plants in diverse families.

Supporting Literature:

Ginns, J., and Lefebvre, M.N.L. 1993. Lignicolous Corticioid Fungi (Basidiomycota) of North America. Systematics, Distribution, and Ecology. APS Press, St. Paul, Minnesota. 247p. Mycol. Mem. 19: 1-243.
