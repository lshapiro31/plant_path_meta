Nomenclature data for Hypodermella nervata

Lirula nervata (Darker) Darker 1967 (Ascomycetes, Rhytismatales)

≡ Hypodermella nervata Darker 1932

Distribution: North America (Canada; Ontario, USA; northeast.

Substrate: Needles.

Disease Note: Discoloration; 'needle cast'.

Host: Abies balsamea (Pinaceae).
