Nomenclature data for Hypodermina hartigii

Lirula macrospora (R. Hartig) Darker 1967 (Ascomycetes, Rhytismatales)

≡ Hypoderma macrospora R. Hartig 1874

≡Lophodermium macrosporum (R. Hartig) Rehm 1887

= Lophodermium filiforme Darker 1932

    ≡ Lirula filiformis (Darker) Y.R. Lin & S.J. Wang 2004

= Hypodermina hartigii Hilitzer 1929

Notes: Cannon & Minter (C.M.I. Descr. 794. 1984) state that this fungus was introduced into Vermont. Other reports may be erroneous.

Distribution: Asia, Europe, North America (USA: VT).

Substrate: Needles.

Disease Note: Needle disease; browning.

Host: Picea spp. (Pinaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Cannon, P.F., and Minter, D.W. 1984. Lirula macrospora. C.M.I. Descr. Pathog. Fungi Bact. 794: 1-3.
