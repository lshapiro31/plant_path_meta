Nomenclature data for Hypoxylon aquilum

Rosellinia aquila (Fr. : Fr.) De Not. 1844 (Ascomycetes, Xylariales)

≡ Sphaeria aquila Fr. 1817

≡Byssitheca aquila (Fr.: Fr.) Bonord 1864

≡Byssosphaeria aquila (Fr.: Fr.) Cooke 1887

≡Hypoxylon aquilum (Fr. : Fr.) Bref. 1891

= Sphaeria byssiseda Tode 1791

    ≡ Rosellinia byssiseda (Tode) J. Schröt. 1908

Notes: Anamorph: geniculosporium-like.

Distribution: Temperate northern hemisphere.

Substrate: Corticated and decorticated branches, roots.

Disease Note: Root rot.

Host: Various hardwoods and conifers.

Supporting Literature:

Petrini 2013. Rosellinia - a world monograph. Biblioth. Mycol. 205: 410.

Spaulding, P. 1956. Diseases of North American Forest Trees Planted Abroad. An Annotated List. U.S.D.A. Agric. Handb. 100: 361.
