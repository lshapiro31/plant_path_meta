≡Tyromyces spumeus (Sowerby) Imazeki 1943

= Polyporus foetidus Velen. 1927

    ≡ Leptoporus foetidus (Velen.) Pilát 1938

    ≡ Spongipellis foetidus (Velen.) Kotl. & Pouzar 1965

= Spongipellis occidentalis Murrill 1907

Notes: According to an LSU-based phylogram in Tomsovsky (2011), S. spumeus, the type species of Spongipelis, does not group with other species assigned to that genus, which should probably be assigned (and often are) to Sarconontia.

Distribution: Temperate northern hemisphere.

Substrate: On hardwoods.

Disease Note: White rot?

Host: Numerous hardwoods.
