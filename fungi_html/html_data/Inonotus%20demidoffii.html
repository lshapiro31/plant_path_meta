Nomenclature data for Inonotus demidoffii

Pyrofomes demidoffii (Lév.) Kotl. & Pouzar 1964 (Basidiomycetes, Polyporales)

≡ Polyporus demidoffii Lév. 1842

≡Fomes demidoffii (Lév.) Cooke 1885

≡Fulvifomes demidoffii (Lév.) Murrill 1947

≡Inonotus demidoffii (Lév.) Pilát 1937

≡Phellinus demidoffii (Lév.) Bondartsev & Singer 1941

≡Scindalma demidoffii (Lév.) Kuntze 1898

≡Trametes demidoffii (Lév.) P. Karst. 1882

≡Xanthochrous demidoffii (Lév.) Pat. 1900

= Pyropolyporus earlei Murrill 1903

    ≡ Fomes earlei (Murrill) Sacc. & D. Sacc. 1905

= Polyporus juniperinus H. Schrenk 1912

    ≡ Fomes juniperus (H. Shrenk) Sacc. & P. Syd. 1902

    ≡ Pyropolyporus juniperinus (H. Shrenck) Murrill 1903

Distribution: Africa, Europe; per Assefa et al. (2015), North American specimens represent a different taxon.

Substrate: Trunk.

Disease Note: Wood rot. Can be a serious parasite.

Host: Juniperus spp. and other taxa in Cupressaceae.
