Nomenclature data for Inonotus glomeratus

Inonotus glomeratus (Peck) Murrill 1920 (Basidiomycetes, Hymenochaetales)

≡ Polyporus glomeratus Peck 1872

Distribution: Eastern North America (common), western North America (rare); Australasia; Europe.

Substrate: Living trees.

Disease Note: Heart rot.

Host: Hardwoods.
