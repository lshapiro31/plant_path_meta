Nomenclature data for Inonotus jamaicensis

Inocutis jamaicensis (Murrill) A.M. Gottlieb, J.E. Wright & Moncalvo 2002 (Basidiomycetes, Hymenochaetales)

≡ Inonotus jamaicensis Murrill 1904

≡Polyporus jamaicensis (Murrill) Sacc. & Trotter 1912

Notes: For additional synonyms, see Gottlieb et al. 2002.

Distribution: North America (USA).

Substrate: Wood.

Disease Note: White heart-rot.

Host: Numerous woody hosts.

Supporting Literature:
