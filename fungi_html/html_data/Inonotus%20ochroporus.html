Nomenclature data for Inonotus ochroporus

Inonotus ochroporus (Van der Byl) Pegler 1964 (Basidiomycetes, Hymenochaetales)

≡ Polyporus ochroporus Van der Byl 1922

= Daedalea fuscospora Lloyd 1924

Distribution: Africa (South Africa, Tanzania), South America (Argentina); pan tropical.

Substrate: Wood.

Disease Note: White heart-rot; pathogenicity uncertain.

Host: Diverse hosts.

Supporting Literature:

Gottlieb, A.M., Wright, J.E., and Moncalvo, J.-M. 2002. Inonotus s.l. in Argentina - morphology, cultural characters and moecular analyses. Mycol. Progr. 1(3): 299-313.

Rajchenberg, M., and Robledo, G. 2013. Pathogenic polypores in Argentina. Forest Pathol. 43: 171-184.
