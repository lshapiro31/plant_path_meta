Nomenclature data for Inonotus patouillardii

Inonotus patouillardii (Rick) Imazeki 1943 (Basidiomycetes, Hymenochaetales)

≡ Polystictus patouillardii Rick 1907

      Variant spelling Polystictus patouillardi Rick 1907

≡Polyporus patouillardii (Rick) Rick 1935

Distribution: Pantropical.

Substrate: Wood.

Disease Note: White-heart and sap rot.

Host: Quercus spp. (Fagaceae) and other hardwood hosts.

Supporting Literature:

Gottlieb, A.M., Wright, J.E., and Moncalvo, J.-M. 2002. Inonotus s.l. in Argentina - morphology, cultural characters and moecular analyses. Mycol. Progr. 1(3): 299-313.
