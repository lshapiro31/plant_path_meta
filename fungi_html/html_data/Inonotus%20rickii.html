Nomenclature data for Inonotus rickii

Inonotus rickii (Pat.) D. Reid 1957 (Basidiomycetes, Hymenochaetales)

≡ Xanthochrous rickii Pat. 1908

≡Phaeoporus rickii (Pat.) Spirin, Zmitr. & Malysheva 2006

≡Polyporus rickii (Pat.) Sacc. & Trotter 1912

= Ptychogaster cubensis Pat. 1896

    ≡ Ceriomyces cubensis (Pat.) Sacc. & Syd. 1899

Notes: Oldest epithet not in correct genus.

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Wood.

Disease Note: Wood decay and cankers.

Host: Various hardwoods.
