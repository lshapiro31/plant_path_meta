Nomenclature data for Inonotus sulphurascens

Coniferiporia sulphurascens (Pilát) L.W. Zhou & Y.C. Dai 2016 (, )

≡ Phellinus sulphurascens Pilát 1936

≡Inonotus sulphurascens (Pilát) M.J. Larsen, Lombard & J.W. Clark 1994

≡Phellinidium sulphurascens (Pilát) Y.C. Dai 1995

= Phellinidium cryptocystidiatum Spirin & Zmitr. 2006

Notes: http://www.fs.fed.us/r6/nr/wildlife/decaid/IandDSpecies/Laminated20root20rot.html (add a percentage sign before 20 and after root to restore URL).

Distribution: Asia, Europe, North America (western).

Substrate: Roots, butt.

Disease Note: Laminated root rot; a serious and costly pathogen.

Host: Pseudotsuga menziesii and other Pinaceae.
