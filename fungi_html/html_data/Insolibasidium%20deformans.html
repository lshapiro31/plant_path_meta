Nomenclature data for Insolibasidium deformans

Insolibasidium deformans (C.J. Gould) Oberw. & Bandoni 1984 (Ustilaginomycetes, Incertae sedis)

≡ Herpobasidium deformans C.J. Gould 1945

= Glomopsis lonicerae Donk 1966

Notes: Earlier name Glomerularia lonicerae Dearn. & House published with no description.

Distribution: Europe (Germany, UK), North America ((Canada, USA), New Zealand.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Necrosis/leaf blight.

Host: Lonicera spp. (Caprifoliaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Beales, P.A., Scrace, J. , Cook, R.T.A., Barnes, A.V., and Lane, C.R. 2004. First report on honeysuckle leaf blight (Insolibasidium deformans) on honeysuckle (Lonicera spp.) in the UK. New Disease Reports 9: 3.
