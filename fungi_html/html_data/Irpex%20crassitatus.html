Notes: According to an LSU-based phylogram in Tomsovsky (2011), S. spumeus, the type species of Spongipelis, does not group with other species assigned to that genus, which should probably be assigned (and often are, as in this case) to Sarconontia. For additional synonyms, see Index Fungorum.

Distribution: Temperate northern hemisphere.

Substrate: Wood, bark.

Disease Note: Canker, white rot (but not decay of heartwood).

Host: Quercus spp. (Fagaceae), Platanus spp. (Platanaceae).
