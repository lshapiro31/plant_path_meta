Nomenclature data for Irpex lacteus

Irpex lacteus (Fr. : Fr.) Fr. 1828 (Basidiomycetes, Polyporales)

≡ Sistotrema lacteum Fr. 1818

≡Hydnum laceteum (Fr.) Fr. 1823

= Boletus tulipiferae Schwein. 1822  Note: From Polyporus tulipiferae - as 'tulipiferus'

    ≡ Irpex tulipiferae (Schwein.) Schwein. 1832

    ≡ Polyporus tulipiferae (Schwein.) Overh. 1915

  Variant spelling Polyporus tulipiferus (Schwein.) Overh.

Notes: For numerous additional synonyms, please see Index Fungorum.

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: On wood.

Disease Note: Decline, cankers, decay, and mortality of trees growing in sub-optimal conditions; normally saprobic.

Host: Numerous hosts.

Supporting Literature:

Bergdahl, D.R., and French, D.W. 1985. Association of wood decay fungi with decline and mortality of apple trees in Minnesota. Pl. Dis. 69: 887-890.
