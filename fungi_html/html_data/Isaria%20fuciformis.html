Nomenclature data for Isaria fuciformis

Laetisaria fuciformis (McAlpine) Burds. 1979 (Basidiomycetes, Polyporales)

≡ Hypochnus fuciformis McAlpine 1906

= Isaria fuciformis Berk. 1872

Notes: Earliest epithet cannot be placed in correct genus.

Distribution: Northeastern United States; Australia; Europe.

Substrate: Leaves

Disease Note: Red thread disease; common disease of turf grasses.

Host: Poaceae.

Supporting Literature:

Burdsall Jr., H.H. 1979. Laetisaria (Aphyllophorales, Corticiaceae), a new genus for the teleomorph of Isaria fuciformis. Trans. Brit. Mycol. Soc. 72: 419-422.

Kaplan, J.D., and Jackson, N. 1983. Red thread and pink patch diseases of turfgrass. Pl. Dis. 67: 159-162.
