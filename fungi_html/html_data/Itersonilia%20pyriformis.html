Nomenclature data for Itersonilia pyriformis

Itersonilia perplexans Derx 1948 (Basidiomycetes, Incertae sedis)

= Itersonilia pastinacae Channon 1963

= Itersonilia pyriformis Nyland 1949

Distribution: Asia (Japan), Australia, Europe, (Austria, Greece, Netherlands, Portugal), New Zealand, North America (Canada, USA), South America (Uruguay).

Substrate: Seeds, leaves, flowers, plant debris.

Disease Note: Seedling blight, root canker, leaf spot/necrosis, dieback.

Host: Anethum graveolens, Heracleum and other Apiaceae; Chrysanthemum spp., Emilia fosbergii, Helianthus spp. and other Asteraceae. Reported as saprobic on other plants.
