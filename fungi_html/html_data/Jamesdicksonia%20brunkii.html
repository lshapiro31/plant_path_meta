Nomenclature data for Jamesdicksonia brunkii

Jamesdicksonia brunkii (Ellis & B.T. Galloway) J. Walker & R.G. Shivas 1998 (Ustilaginomycetes, Georgefischeriales)

≡ Ustilago brunkii Ellis & B.T. Galloway 1890

≡Tilletia brunkii (Ellis & B.T. Galloway) Durán 1973 [1972]  Note: Invalid name via no basionym reference.

≡Tolyposporella brunkii (Ellis & B.T. Galloway) G.P. Clinton 1902

= Ustilago apiculata Ellis & B.T. Galloway 1890

Distribution: North America, Australia, South America.

Substrate: Leaaves.

Disease Note: Smut.

Host: Andropogon spp., Hyparrhenia rufa, Dichanthium sericeum (Poaceae).
