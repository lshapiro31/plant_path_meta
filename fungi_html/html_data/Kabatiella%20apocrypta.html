Nomenclature data for Kabatiella apocrypta

Kabatiella apocrypta (Ellis & Everh.) Arx 1957 (Ascomycetes, Dothideales)

≡ Gloeosporium apocryptum Ellis & Everh. 1888

≡Aureobasidium apocryptum (Ellis & Everh.) Herm.-Nijh. 1977

≡Microstroma apocryptum (Ellis & Everh.) W.B. Cooke 1962

Notes: Butin, H. Effect of endophytic fungi from oak (Quercus robur L.) on mortality of leaf inhabiting gall insects European Journal of Forest Pathology (1992).

Distribution: Temperate northern hemisphere.

Substrate: On leaves.

Disease Note: Anthracnose; possibly induced to pathogenicity by activities of feeding insects.

Host: Acer spp. (Sapindaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Vujanovic, V., and Britton, J. 2002. A comparative study of endophytic mycobiota in leaves of Acer saccharum in eastern North America. Mycol. Progr. 1: 147-154.
