Nomenclature data for Kabatiella caulivora

Kabatiella caulivora (Kirchn.) Karak. 1923 (Ascomycetes, Dothideales)

≡ Gloeosporium caulivorum Kirchn. 1902

≡Exobasidiopsis caulivora (Kirchn.) Karak. 1922

≡Aureobasidium caulivorum (Kirchn.) W.B. Cooke 1962

Notes: Possibly belongs in Aureobasidium. See Hoog (1999); Stud. Mycol. 43: 31.

Distribution: Worldwide in red clover-growing regions.

Substrate: Leaves, seeds.

Disease Note: Clover scorch.

Host: Trifolium spp. (Fabaceae).
