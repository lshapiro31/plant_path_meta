Nomenclature data for Kabatiella lini

Kabatiella lini (Laff.) Karak. 1957 (Ascomycetes, Dothideales)

≡ Polyspora lini Laff. 1921 [1920-22]

≡Aureobasidium lini (Laff.) Herm.-Nijh. 1977

= Guignardia fulvida F.R. Sand. 1965

Distribution: Worldwide in flax-growing regions.

Substrate: Stems, fruit, sepals, leaves.

Disease Note: Browning, leaf spot, stem break.

Host: Linum usitatissimum (Linaceae).
