Nomenclature data for Kabatiella microsticta

Kabatiella microsticta Bubák 1907 (Ascomycetes, Dothideales)

≡Aureobasidium microstictum (Bubák) W.B. Cooke 1962

= Gloeosporium convallariae Allesch. 1895

Notes: From Aureobasidium microstictum - Gloeosporium convallariae is a poorly known species, possibly the same as Aureobasidium microstictum (Bubák) W.B. Cooke (Arx. Biblioth. Mycol. 24:113. 1970).

Distribution: Asia, Europe, North America.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Primarily occurring on monocotyleconous plants.
