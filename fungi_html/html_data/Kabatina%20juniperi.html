Nomenclature data for Kabatina juniperi

Kabatina juniperi R. Schneid. & Arx 1966 (Ascomycetes, Incertae sedis)

≡Kabatina thujae var. juniperi (R. Schneid. & Arx) M. Morelet 1966

Distribution: Central and eastern United States; Europe.

Substrate: Foliage.

Disease Note: Dieback.

Host: Juniperus spp., Thuja spp. and other Cupressaceae.

Supporting Literature:
