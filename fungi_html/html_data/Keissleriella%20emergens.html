Nomenclature data for Keissleriella emergens

Diplodia tumefaciens (Shear) Zalasky 1964 (Ascomycetes, Dothideales)

≡ Macrophoma tumefaciens Shear 1915

= Sphaeria emergens P. Karst. 1873

  ≡Keissleriella emergens (P. Karst.) S.K. Bose 1961

Notes: See also Zalasky in CJB, Volume 42, Number 8 (1964).

Distribution: North America; northern Europe.

Substrate: Twigs, branches, bark, roots.

Disease Note: Galls and rough bark caused by asexual state; warted and swollen roots/galls caused by sexual state.

Host: Populus spp. (Salicaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Callan, B.E. 1998. Diseases of Populus in British Columbia: a diagnostic manual. Natural Resources Canada, Canadian Forest Service, 157 pages.

Zalasky, H. 1974. Keissleriella emergens, a perfect state of Diplodia tumefaciens inroots of poplar. Canad. J. Bot. 52: 11-13.
