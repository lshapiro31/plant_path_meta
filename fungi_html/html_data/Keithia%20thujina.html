Nomenclature data for Keithia thujina

Didymascella thujina (E.J. Durand) Maire 1927 (Ascomycetes, Helotiales)

≡ Keithia thujina E.J. Durand 1913

Notes: Leaf blight of Thuja species. Dennis (British Ascomycetes. p. 237. 1978). C.M.I. Map 149. 1980.

Distribution: North America; Europe.

Substrate: On living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf blight.

Host: Primarily on Thuja (Cupressaceae).
