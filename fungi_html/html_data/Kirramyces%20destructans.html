Nomenclature data for Kirramyces destructans

Teratosphaeria destructans (M.J. Wingf. & Crous) M.J. Wingf. & Crous 2009 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

≡ Kirramyces destructans M.J. Wingf. & Crous 1996

≡Phaeophleospora destructans (M.J. Wingf. & Crous) Crous, F.A. Ferreira & B. Sutton 1997

≡Readeriella destructans (M.J. Wingf. & Crous) Crous & U. Braun 2007

Distribution: Asia (China, Indonesia, Vietnam), Australia.

Substrate: leaves, buds, shoots.

Disease Note: Blight, leaf distortion, defoliation.

Host: Eucalyptus spp. (Myrtaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Burgess, T.I., Andjic, V., Wingfield, M.J., and Hardy, G.E.St.J. 2007. The eucalypt leaf blight pathogen Kirramyces destructans discovered in Australia. Australasian Plant Disease Notes 2: 141-144.
