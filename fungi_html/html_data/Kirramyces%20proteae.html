Nomenclature data for Kirramyces proteae

Phaeophleospora congesta (Syd. & P. Syd.) Crous & M.E. Palm 1999 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

    Variant spelling Phaeophleospora congestum (Syd. & P. Syd.) Crous & M.E. Palm 1999

≡ Didymosporium congestum Syd. & P. Syd. 1912

= Kirramyces proteae B. Sutton 1993

    ≡ Phaeophleospora proteae (B. Sutton) Crous, F.A. Ferreira & B. Sutton 1997

= Coniothyrium proteae-abyssinicae Bacc. 1917

Notes: Phaeophleospora congesta with incorrect Latin termination in original new combination (as 'congestum'). Correct authorship of Didymosporium congestum is Syd. & P. Syd. (original publication authored by both) instead of simply Syd..

Distribution: Africa (South Africa, Ethiopia).

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Protea caffra, P. gaguedi (Proteaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Crous, P.W., Denman, S., Taylor, J.E., Swart, L., and Palm, M.E. 2004. Cultivation and diseases of Proteaceae: Leucadendron, Leucospermum and Protea. Centraalbureau voor Schimmelcultures, Utrecht, 227 pages.

Crous, P.W., Denman, S., Taylor, J.E., Swart, L., Bezuidenhout, C.M., Hoffman, L., Palm, M.E., and Groenewald, J.Z. 2013. Cultivation and disease of Proteaceae: Leucadendron, Leucospermum, and Protea: Second edition. CBS Biodivers. Ser. N/A: 360.
