Nomenclature data for Kneiffia farinosa

Aegerita candida Pers. : Fr. 1794 (Basidiomycetes, Polyporales)

= Sclerotium aegerita Hoffm. 1795

= Kneiffia farinosa Bres. 1903  Note: From Bulbillomyces farinosus - Oldest epithet not in the correct genus.

    ≡ Bulbillomyces farinosus (Bres.) Jülich 1974

    =  Peniophora aegerita Höhn. & Litsch. 1907

    ≡ Peniophora farinosa (Bres.) Höhn. & Litsch. 1908

    =  Peniophora aegerita Höhn. & Litsch. 1907

    ≡ Metulodontia farinosa (Bres.) Parmasto 1968

    =  Peniophora aegerita Höhn. & Litsch. 1907

Notes: Aegerita 1801 and Bulbillomyces 1974 compete for use because their type species are synonyms. Priority is given to the older names.

Disease Note: White rot.

Host: Woody plants.

Supporting Literature:

Legon, N.W. 2006. Profiles of fungi. Bulbillomyces farinosus. Mycologist 20: 81-82.
