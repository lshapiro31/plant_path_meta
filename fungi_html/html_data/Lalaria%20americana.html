Nomenclature data for Lalaria americana

Taphrina americana Mix 1947 (Taphrinomycetes, Taphrinales)

= Lalaria americana R.T. Moore 1990

Distribution: Temperate North America.

Substrate: Branches.

Disease Note: Witch's brooms.

Host: Betula spp. (Betulaceae).

Supporting Literature:
