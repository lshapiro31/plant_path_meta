Nomenclature data for Lalaria confusa

Taphrina confusa (G.F. Atk.) Giesenh. 1895 (Taphrinomycetes, Taphrinales)

≡ Exoascus confusus G.F. Atk. 1894

= Exoascus cecidomophilus G.F. Atk. 1894

    ≡ Taphrina cecidomophilus (G.F. Atk.) Giesenh. 1895

= Lalaria confusa R.T. Moore 1990

= Exoascus unilateralis Peck 1898

    ≡ Taphrina unilateralis (Peck) Mix 1936

Distribution: Temperate North America.

Substrate: Flowers, fruit, leaves.

Disease Note: Deformed flowers and fruits, leaf curl.

Host: Prunus virginiana (Rosaceae).

Supporting Literature:
