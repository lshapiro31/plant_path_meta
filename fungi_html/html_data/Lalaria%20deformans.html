Taphrina deformans (Berk.) Tul. 1866 (Taphrinomycetes, Taphrinales)

≡ Ascomyces deformans Berk. 1857

≡Exoascus deformans (Berk.) Fuckel 1870

= Exoascus amygdali Jacz. 1926

    ≡ Taphrina amygdali (Jacz.) Mix 1936

= Lalaria deformans R.T. Moore 1990

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf curl.

Host: Prunus spp. (Rosaceae); a common pathogen of peaches.
