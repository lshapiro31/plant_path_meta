Nomenclature data for Lecanosticta pini

Lecanosticta acicola (Thüm.) Syd. 1924 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Cryptosporium acicola Thüm. 1878

≡Dothistroma acicola (Thüm.) Schischkina & Tsanava 1967

≡Septoria acicola (Thüm.) Sacc. 1884

= Oligostroma acicola Dearn. 1926

    ≡ Mycosphaerella dearnessii M.E. Barr 1972

    ≡ Scirrhia acicola (Dearn.) Sigg. 1939

    ≡ Systremma acicola (Dearn.) F.A. Wolf & Barbour 1941

    ≡ Dothidea acicola (Dearn.) M. Morelet 1968

    ≡ Eruptio acicola (Dearn.) M.E. Barr 1996

= Lecanosticta pini Syd. 1921

Notes: Mycosphaerella dearnessii is a nom nov. created to prevent conflict with Mycosphaerella acicola (Cooke & Harkn.) Lindau 1897. The type species of Lecanosticta is L. pini.

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Needles.

Disease Note: Brown spot needle blight.

Host: Pinus spp. (Pinaceae).
