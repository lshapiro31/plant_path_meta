Nomenclature data for Lentinus lecomtei

Panus neostrigosus Drechsler-Santos & Wartchow 2012 (Basidiomycetes, Polyporales)

≡ Lentinus strigosus (Schwein. : Fr.) Fr. 1838 [1836]

= Lentinus lecomtei Fr. 1838 [1836]

= Panus rudis Fr. 1838 [1836]

Notes: Drechsler-Santos et al. "Studies on lentinoid fungi (Lentinus and Panus) from the semi-arid region of Brazil" The Journal of the Torrey Botanical Society (2012). Panus neostrigosus is a nom nov. created to prevent conflict with Panus strigosus Berk. & M.A. Curtis.

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Wood; dead parts of living trees.

Disease Note: Not pathogenic.

Host: Numerous hosts.

Supporting Literature:

Petersen, D.H. 1960. Hymenomycetous fungi except Polyporus associated with wood decay of living peach trees in South Carolina. Pl. Dis. Reporter 44: 891-893.
