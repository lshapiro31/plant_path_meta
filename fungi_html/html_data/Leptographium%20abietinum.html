Nomenclature data for Leptographium abietinum

Leptographium abietinum (Peck) M.J. Wingf. 1985 (Ascomycetes, Ophiostomatales)

≡ Sporocybe abietina Peck 1879

≡Periconia abietina (Peck) Sacc. 1886

≡Verticicladiella abietina (Peck) S. Hughes 1953

= Leptographium engelmannii R.W. Davidson 1955

Distribution: Europe, North America.

Disease Note: Thought of as weak pathogen.

Host: Pinaceae.
