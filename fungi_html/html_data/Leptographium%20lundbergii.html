Nomenclature data for Leptographium lundbergii

Leptographium lundbergii Lagerb. & Melin 1927 (Ascomycetes, Ophiostomatales)

≡Scopularia lundbergii (Lagerb. & Melin) Goid. 1933

= Scopularia venusta Preuss 1851

Notes: Type materal of Scopularia venusta is in very poor condition; if synonymous with L. lundbergii, the epithet 'venusta' would be used in Leptographium.

Distribution: Widespread.

Disease Note: Pathogen.

Host: Pinaceae.
