Nomenclature data for Leptographium yunnanense

Grosmannia yunnanensis Yamaoka, Masuya & M.J. Wingf. 2008 (Ascomycetes, Ophiostomatales)

= Leptographium yunnanense X.D. Zhou, K. Jacobs, M.J. Wingf. & M. Morelet 2000

Notes: Anamorph leptographium-like.

Distribution: Asia.

Disease Note: Pathogenic.

Host: Pinus spp. (Pinaceae).
