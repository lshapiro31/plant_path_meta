Nomenclature data for Leptosphaeria weimeri

Leptosphaeria weimeri Shoemaker, C.E. Babc. & J.A.G. Irwin 1991 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

= Gloeosporium medicaginis Ellis & Kellerm. 1887

= Septoria medicaginis Roberge & Desm. 1847

= Depazea meliloti Lasch 1842  Note: From Stagonospora meliloti - Stagonospora meliloti is a synanamorph of Phoma meliloti.

    ≡ Stagonospora meliloti (Lasch) Petr. 1919

     Gloeosporium trifolii Peck

= Phoma meliloti Allesch. 1892

Notes: Previously confused with Leptosphaeria pratensis. Oldest epithet not in correct genus, although correct genus not known.

Distribution: North America; Europe; Australia.

Substrate: Living leaves, stems, roots.

Disease Note: Leaf spot, stem canker, and root rot.

Host: Forage legumes. F
