Nomenclature data for Leptostroma rostrupii

Lophodermium seditiosum Minter, Staley & Millar 1978 (Ascomycetes, Rhytismatales)

= Leptostroma austriacum Oudem. 1904

= Depazea linearis Rostr. 1883

= Leptostroma rostrupii Minter 1980

Notes: From Leptostroma rostrupii - Nom. nov. Oldest epithet cannot be placed in correct genus.

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Needles.

Disease Note: Needle cast; an aggressive pathogen for which related species often take the blame (Minter, 1981).

Host: Pinus spp. (Pinaceae).
