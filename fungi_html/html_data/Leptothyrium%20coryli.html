Nomenclature data for Leptothyrium coryli

Cylindrosporella coryli (Fuckel) Arx 1981 (Ascomycetes, Diaporthales)

≡ Leptothyrium coryli Fuckel 1870

≡Asteroma coryli (Fuckel) B. Sutton 1980

= Phyllosticta coryli Westend. 1851  Note: Not Sacc. & Speg. 1878.

Distribution: Scattered reports in northern hemisphere.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Corylus spp. (Betulaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Boerema, G.H., De Gruyter, J., Noordeloos, M.E., and Hamers, M.E.C. 2004. Phoma identification manual: differentiation of specific and infra-specific taxa in culture. CABI Publishing, 470 pages.

Kaneko, S., and Kobayashi, T. 2002. Cylindrosporella on betulaceous trees in Japan. Mycoscience 43: 181-183.
