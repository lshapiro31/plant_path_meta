Nomenclature data for Leptothyrium pomi

Schizothyrium pomi (Mont.) Arx 1959 (Ascomycetes, Microthyriales)

≡ Labrella pomi Mont. 1834

≡Leptothyrium pomi (Mont.) Sacc. 1880

= Asterina gaultheriae M.A. Curtis 1885

= Microthyriella paludosa C. Booth 1958 [1957]

Notes: As (Mont. ex Fr.) Arx comb. nov., but the basionym should be attributed only to Mont. Arx (1959) lists numerous additional synonyms not given here.

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Cuticle of fruit.

Disease Note: Flyspeck.

Host: Reported from a large number of hosts.
