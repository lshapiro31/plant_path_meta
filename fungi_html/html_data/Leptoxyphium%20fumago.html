Nomenclature data for Leptoxyphium fumago

Leptoxyphium fumago (Woron.) R.C. Srivast. 1982 (Ascomycetes, Capnodiales)

≡ Caldariomyces fumago Woron. 1927

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Sooty mould.

Host: Numerous hosts.

Supporting Literature:
