Nomenclature data for Leucostoma translucens

Leucostoma translucens (De Not.) Höhn. 1928 (Ascomycetes, Diaporthales)

Distribution: Asia, North America.

Substrate: Shoots, twigs, branches.

Disease Note: Possibly a symptomless endobiont or parasite; later saprobic.

Host: Celtis australis (Cannabaceae), Populus spp., Salix spp. (Salicaceae), Cornus sanguinea (Cornaceae).
