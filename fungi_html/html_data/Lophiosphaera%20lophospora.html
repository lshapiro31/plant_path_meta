Nomenclature data for Lophiosphaera lophospora

Lophiostoma fuckelii Sacc. 1878 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

= Lophiosphaera lophospora (Sacc. & Speg.) Corlett 1981

= Lophiostoma pulveraceum Sacc. 1878

Notes: Holm and Holm (1988) provide a more complete list of synonyms based on their circumscription.

Distribution: Africa (South Africa), Asia, North America, Europe.

Substrate: Leaves, stems.

Disease Note: Lophiostoma leaf spot.

Host: Multiple species in multiple families.

Supporting Literature:
