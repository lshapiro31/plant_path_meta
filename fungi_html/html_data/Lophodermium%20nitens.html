Nomenclature data for Lophodermium nitens

Lophodermium nitens Darker 1932 (Ascomycetes, Rhytismatales)

≡Lophodermina nitens (Darker) Tehon 1935

Distribution: Widespread, especially northern hemisphere.

Substrate: Needles.

Disease Note: Probably saprophytic.

Host: Pinus spp. (Pinaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Deckert, R.J. , Hsiang, T., and Peterson, R.L. 2002. Genetic relationships of endophytic Lophodermium nitens isolates from needles of Pinus strobus. Mycol. Res. 106: 305-313.
