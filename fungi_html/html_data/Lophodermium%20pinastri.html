Nomenclature data for Lophodermium pinastri

Lophodermium pinastri (Schrad.) Chevall. 1826 (Ascomycetes, Rhytismatales)

≡ Hysterium pinastri Schrad. 1799

≡Hypoderma pinastri (Schrad.) DC. 1805

≡Lophodermellina pinastri (Schrad.) Höhn. 1917

= Lophodermium pinicola Tehon 1935  Note: As 'pinicolum'. Nom. illeg.

Distribution: Worldwide.

Substrate: Needles.

Disease Note: Endophytic; causes no disease symptoms. Disease reports most likely refer to L. seditiosum (Minter, 1981),

Host: Pinus spp. (Pinaceae).
