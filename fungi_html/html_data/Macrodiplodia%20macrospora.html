Nomenclature data for Macrodiplodia macrospora

Stenocarpella macrospora (Earle) B. Sutton 1977 (Ascomycetes, Incertae sedis)

≡ Diplodia macrospora Earle 1897

≡Macrodiplodia macrospora (Earle) Höhn. 1918

≡Macrodiplodia zeae var. macrospora (Earle) Petr. & Syd. 1923

= Stenocarpella zeae Syd. & P. Syd. 1917

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Leaves, ears, stalks.

Disease Note: Dry rot, leaf lesions.

Host: Zea mays (Poaceae).
