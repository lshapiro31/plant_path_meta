Nomenclature data for Macrodiplodiopsis desmazieri

Macrodiplodiopsis desmazieri (Mont.) Petr. 1922 (Ascomycetes, Incertae sedis)

≡ Hendersonia desmazieri Mont. 1849

      Variant spelling Macrodiplodiopsis desmazieresii (Mont.) Petr. 1922

= Hendersonia platani Peck 1872

= Massaria platani Ces. 1861

    ≡ Splanchnonema platani (Ces.) M.E. Barr 1982

= Stegonsporium platani Preuss 1853

Notes: From Splanchnonema platani - Kehr, Rolf & Hermann-Josef Krauthausen "First report of damage to Plane trees (Platanus Ã— hispanica) in Germany caused by the fungus Splanchnonema platani" Nachrichtenblatt des Deutschen Pflanzenschutzdienstes (2004).

Distribution: North America; Europe.

Substrate: Branches.

Disease Note: Lesions/branch drop; seldom fatal.

Host: Platanus occidentalis, P. orientalis, P. x acerifolia (Platanaceae).
