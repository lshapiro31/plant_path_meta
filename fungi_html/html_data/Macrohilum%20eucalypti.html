Nomenclature data for Macrohilum eucalypti

Macrohilum eucalypti H.J. Swart 1988 (Ascomycetes, Incertae sedis)

Distribution: Australia, New Zealand.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Eucalyptus spp. (Myrtaceae).
