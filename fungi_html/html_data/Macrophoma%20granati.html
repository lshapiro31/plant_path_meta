Nomenclature data for Macrophoma granati

Coniella granati (Sacc.) Petr. & Syd. 1927 [1926] (Ascomycetes, Diaporthales)

≡ Phoma granati Sacc. 1876

≡Macrophoma granati (Sacc.) Berl. & Voglino 1886

≡Pilidiella granati (Sacc.) Aa 1972

= Anthasthoopa simba Subram. & K. Ramakr. 1956

    ≡ Coniella simba (Ellis & Everh.) B. Sutton 1969

= Phoma versoniana Sacc. 1881

    ≡ Zythia versoniana (Sacc.) Sacc. 1884

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Branches, twigs, fruit.

Disease Note: Wilting, dieback, fruit rot, crown rot.

Host: Punica granatum (Lythraceae).
