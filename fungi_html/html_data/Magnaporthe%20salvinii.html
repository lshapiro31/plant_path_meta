    ≡ Heptameria salvinii (Catt.) Cooke 1889

= Helminthosporium sigmoideum Cavara 1889  Note: Nakataea sigmoidea is a conidial synanamorph of Sclerotium oryzae.

    ≡ Vakrabeeja sigmoidea var. sigmoidea

Distribution: Widespread. Worldwide in rice-growing regions.

Substrate: Isol. from various plant parts.

Disease Note: Stem rot disease of rice.

Host: Orzya, Zizania and other Poaceae.
