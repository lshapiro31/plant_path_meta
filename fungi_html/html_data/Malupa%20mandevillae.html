Nomenclature data for Malupa mandevillae

Crossopsora stevensii Syd. 1925 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

= Uredo mandevillae Mayor 1913

  ≡Malupa mandevillae (Mayor) Buriticá 1996

Notes: Earliest epithet cannot be put in correct genus.

Distribution: Central America, South America (Colombia, French Guiana, Trinidad).

Disease Note: Rust.

Host: Mandevilla spp., Matelea spp. (Apocynaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Berndt, R. 2012. First catalogue of the rust fungi of French Guiana, northern South America. Mycol. Progr. 12: 193-211.
