Curvularia aeria (Bat., J.A. Lima & C.T. Vasconc.) Tsuda 1994 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

≡ Malustela aeria Bat., J.A. Lima & C.T. Vasconc. 1960

≡Curvularia lunata var. aeria (Bat., I.H. Lima & Vasconc.) M.B. Ellis 1966

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf blight.

Host: Multiple genera in multiple families.
