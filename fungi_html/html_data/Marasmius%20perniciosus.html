Nomenclature data for Marasmius perniciosus

Moniliophthora perniciosa (Stahel) Aime & Phillips-Mora 2006 [2005] (Basidiomycetes, Agaricales)

≡ Marasmius perniciosus Stahel 1915

≡Crinipellis perniciosa (Stahel) Singer 1943

≡Crinipellis perniciosa var. perniciosa (Stahel) Singer 1943

≡Marasmius perniciosus var. perniciosus Stahel 1915

Distribution: South America, Caribbean (Trinidad, Grenada). Also reported in Indonesia (unconfirmed).

Substrate: Buds, flowers, fruits, stems.

Disease Note: The APS common names are Crinipellis pod rot and witches broom. This fungal disease may destroy up to 75 percent of the crop.

Host: Theobroma spp. including Theobroma cacao (cacao), Herrania spp. (Malvaceae).
