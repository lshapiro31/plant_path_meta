Nomenclature data for Massariopsis futilis

Didymosphaeria futilis (Berk. & Broome) Rehm 1879 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

≡ Sphaeria futilis Berk. & Broome 1852

≡Massariopsis futilis (Berk. & Broome) Kirschst. 1935

≡Microthelia futilis (Berk. & Broome) Kuntze 1898

= Didymosphaeria albescens Niessl 1875

= Didymosphaeria brunneola Niessl 1875

    ≡ Massariopsis brunneola (Niessl) Kirschst. 1935

Notes: See Aptroot (1995) for an extensive list of synonyms.

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Leaves, stems.

Disease Note: Blight, canker, leaf spot.

Host: Multiple species in multiple families.
