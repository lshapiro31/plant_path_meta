Nomenclature data for Mastigosporium album

Mastigosporium album Riess 1863 (Ascomycetes, Incertae sedis)

≡Bactridium album (Riess) Bonord. 1862

= Excipulina graminum P. Karst. 1887

    ≡ Mastigosporium graminum (P. Karst.) Nag Raj 1979

= Pestalozzina soraueriana Sacc. 1894

Notes: Includes the type variety.

Distribution: Asia, Europe, North America.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Poaceae.
