Nomenclature data for Mastigosporium calvum

Mastigosporium rubricosum (Dearn. & Barthol.) Nannf. 1939 (Ascomycetes, Incertae sedis)

≡ Fusoma rubricosum Dearn. & Barthol. 1917

= Mastigosporium album var. calvum Ellis & J.J. Davis 1903

  ≡Mastigosporium calvum (Ellis & J.J. Davis) R. Sprague 1938

= Amastigosporium graminicola Bond.-Mont. 1921  Note: as 'graminicolum'

= Mastigosporium rubricosum var. agrostidis Bollard 1950

Notes: Mastigosporium calvum (Ellis & J.J. Davis) R. Sprague (1940) - nom. illeg. (Art. 53.1).

Distribution: Asia, Europe, North America, New Zealand.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Agrostis spp. (Poaceae).
