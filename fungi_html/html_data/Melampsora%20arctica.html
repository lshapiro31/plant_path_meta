Nomenclature data for Melampsora arctica

Melampsora arctica Rostr. 1888 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

Distribution: Asia (China), North America (Canada, USA)

Substrate: Leaves, stems.

Disease Note: Rust.

Host: Uredenial and telial stage on Salix spp. (Salicaceae); aecical stage on Saxifraga spp. (Saxifragaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Zhao, P., Kakishima, M., Wang, Q., and Cai, L. 2017. Resolving the Melampsora epitea complex. Mycologia 109(3): 391-407.
