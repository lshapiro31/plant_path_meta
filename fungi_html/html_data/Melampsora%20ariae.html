Nomenclature data for Melampsora ariae

Ochropsora ariae (Fuckel) Ramsb. 1914 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

≡ Melampsora ariae Fuckel 1870

= Aecidium anemones Pers. ex J.F. Gmel. : Pers. 1792

= Aecidium leucospermum DC. 1805

= Melampsora pallida Rostr. 1877

    ≡ Ochropsora pallida (Rostr.) Lind 1913

= Caeoma sorbi Oudem. 1873 [1872]

= Melampsora sorbi G. Winter 1884  Note: From Ochropsora sorbi - Ono (2006) discusses the author citation of this name. He neglected to point out that this name is a nomen illeg. since the epithet pallida should have been adopted.

    ≡ Ochropsora sorbi (G. Winter) Dietel 1895

Notes: (Fuckel) Sydow & P. Sydow 1915 is an isonym. Hiratsuka et al. (1992) and Sydow & Sydow (1915) list some additional archaic synonyms of this name. These are not included here. From Uredo ariae - Invalid via nom. nud. fide Wilson & Henderson 1966. From Aecidium anemones - Aecial state name. Author citation uncertain. From Melampsora sorbi - Superfluous and nomen illeg. since M. pallida cited as a synonym. Oldest epithet not in correct genus.

Distribution: Asia, Europe.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Rust. From Aecidium anemones - Rust.

Host: Anemone spp. (Ranunculaceae) for aecial state, multiple genera of Rosaceae for uredinial and telial states.
