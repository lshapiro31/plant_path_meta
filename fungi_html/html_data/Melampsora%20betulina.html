Nomenclature data for Melampsora betulina

Melampsoridium betulinum Kleb. 1899 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

Melampsora betulina G. Winter

Notes: Birch rust. Heteroecious; aecial host - Larix spp.; telial host - Betula and Alnus spp. Kaneko & Hiratsuka (Trans. Mycol. Soc. Japan 22:463. 1981) reidentified a specimen on Ostrya purported to be M. carpini as M. betulinum.

Distribution: Temperate northern hemisphere; New Zealand.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Rust fungus.

Host: Alnus, Betula (Betulaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Hantula, J., Kurkela, T., Hendry, S. , and Yamaguchi, T. 2009. Morphological measurements and ITS sequences show that the new alder rust in Europe is conspecific with Melampsoridium hiratsukanum in eastern Asia. Mycologia 101: 622-631.
