Nomenclature data for Melampsora coleosporioides

Melampsora coleosporioides Dietel 1902 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

Distribution: Asia (China, Japan), Australia, New Zealand.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Salix spp. (Salicaceae); spermogonia and aecia formed on Corydalis incisa (Papaveraceae) but alternate host not necessary.

Supporting Literature:

Okane, I., Koide, Y., Nakamura, H., and Yamaoka, Y. 2014. Life cycle of Melampsora coleosporioides, a leaf rust of Salix babylonica in Japan. Mycoscience 55: 431-438.
