Nomenclature data for Melampsora epitea

Melampsora epitea Thüm. 1879 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

= Melampsora alpina Juel 1894

= Melampsora hartigii Thüm. 1879

= Melampsora repentis Plowr. 1891

Distribution: Europe, Australia, Asia.

Substrate: Living leaves and needles.

Disease Note: Rust.

Host: Larix spp. (Pinaceae) and numerous other hosts for aecial state, Salix spp. (Salicaceae) for uredinial and telial states.
