Nomenclature data for Melampsora farlowii

Melampsora farlowii (Arthur) Davis 1915 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

≡ Necium farlowii Arthur 1907

Notes: Aecia and uredinia lacking; telia on Pinaceae.

Distribution: Northeastern North America.

Substrate: On living needles.

Disease Note: Needle rust.

Host: On Tsuga (Pinaceae).
