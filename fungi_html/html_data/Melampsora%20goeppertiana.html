Nomenclature data for Melampsora goeppertiana

Pucciniastrum goeppertianum (Kühn) Kleb. 1904 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

≡ Calyptospora goeppertiana J.G. Kühn 1869

≡Melampsora goeppertiana (J.G. Kühn) G. Winter 1881

= Aecidium columnare Alb. & Schwein. 1805

= Peridermium columnare Kunze & J.C. Schmidt 1815

= Calyptospora columnaris J.G. Kühn 1886

= Melampsora columnaris Wettst. 1885

= Uredo columnaris Spreng. 1827

= Caeoma columneum Link 1825

= Peridermium holwayi Syd. 1903

= Peridermium ornamentale Arthur 1901

Notes: For comparison with P. epilobii and a discussion of the Peridermium states, see Hiratsuka et al. (Canad. J. Bot. 45:1913. 1967). Oldest epithet not in correct genus.

Distribution: Asia, Europe, North America.

Substrate: Needles, stems, leaves.

Disease Note: Heterecious rust; needle rust, witch's brooms of blueberries.

Host: Abies spp. (Pinaceae), Vaccinium spp. (Ericaceae).
