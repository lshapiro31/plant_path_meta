Nomenclature data for Melampsora guttata

Pucciniastrum guttatum (J. Schröt.) Hyl., Jørst. & Nannf. 1953 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

≡ Melampsora guttata J. Schröt. 1870

≡Thekopsora guttata (J. Schröt.) P. Syd. & Syd. 1915

= Pucciniastrum galii E. Fisch. 1904

Notes: Heteroecious; aecial host - unknown; telial host - Rubiaceae. Hylander et al. (Opera Bot. 1:81. 1953).

Distribution: Temperate northern hemisphere.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Rust.

Host: Galium spp., Cruciata glabra (Rubiaceae).

Supporting Literature:
