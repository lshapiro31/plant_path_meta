Nomenclature data for Melampsora larici-populina

Melampsora laricis-populina Kleb. 1902 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

    Variant spelling Melampsora larici-populina Kleb. 1902

Notes: Gäumann (Beitr. Kryptogamenfl. Schweiz 12:232. 1959). Siwecki (Rep. Tottori Mycol. Inst. 28:95. 1990) did a detailed ultrastructural study.

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: On living needles and leaves.

Disease Note: Larch-poplar rust.
