Nomenclature data for Melampsora laricis-epitea

Melampsora laricis-epitea Kleb. 1899 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

    Variant spelling Melampsora larici-epitea Kleb. 1899

Distribution: Europe.

Substrate: Leaves/needles.

Disease Note: Rust.

Host: Larix spp. (Pinaceae), Salix spp. (Salicaceae).
