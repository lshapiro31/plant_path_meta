Notes: From Uredo fuchsiae - A homonym exists for this name.

Distribution: North America; Central America; South America; Europe; USSR; Asia; Australia; New Zealand.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Rust.

Host: Aecial host - Abies spp.; telial host - Epilobium spp. and other Onagraceae.
