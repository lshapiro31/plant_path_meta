Nomenclature data for Melampsora ribesii-purpureae

Melampsora ribesii-purpureae Kleb. 1901 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

Distribution: Temperate northern hemisphere.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Heterecious rust.

Host: Aecical stage on Ribes spp. (Grossulariaceae): Salix spp. (Salicaceae) on uredinal and telial stages.
