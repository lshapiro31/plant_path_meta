Nomenclature data for Melampsora ribesii-viminalis

Melampsora ribesii-viminalis Kleb. 1900 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

Distribution: Asia (Japan), Europe.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Rust.

Host: Ribes spp. (Grossulariaceae), Salix spp. (Salicaceae).
