Nomenclature data for Melampsora yezoensis

Melampsora yezoensis Miyabe & T. Matsumoto 1915 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

Distribution: Asia (China, Japan, Korea).

Substrate: Living leaves, petioles.

Disease Note: Rust.

Host: Salix spp. (Salicaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Yun, Y.H., Ahn, G.R., Yoon, S.K., Kim, H.H., Son, S.Y., and Kim, S.H. 2016. New rust disease of Korean willow (Salix koreensis) caused by Melampsora yezoensis, unrecorded pathogen in Korea. Mycobiology 44(4): 335-337.
