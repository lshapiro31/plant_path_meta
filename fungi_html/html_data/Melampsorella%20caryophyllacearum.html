Nomenclature data for Melampsorella caryophyllacearum

Melampsorella caryophyllacearum J. Schröt. 1874 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

= Melampsorella cerastii G. Winter 1882

= Melampsorella elatina Arthur

Notes: As 'caryophylleacearum'. Fir broom rust. Heteroecious; aecial host - Abies spp.; telial host - Carophyllaceae.

Distribution: Temperate northern hemisphere; Falkland Island (introduced).

Substrate: On living leaves and needles.

Disease Note: Fir broom rust.

Host: On Caryophyllaceae and Pinaceae.

Supporting Literature:

White, B.L., and Merrill, W. 1969. Pathological anatomy of Abies balsamea infected with Melampsorella caryophyllacearum. Phytopathology 59: 1238-1242.
