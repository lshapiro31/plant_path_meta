Nomenclature data for Meliola clerodendricola

Meliola clerodendricola Henn. 1898 (Ascomycetes, Meliolales)

Distribution: Asia.

Substrate: Epiphytic on living leaves.

Disease Note: Black mold.

Host: Clerodendrum spp. (Lamiaceae).

Supporting Literature:
