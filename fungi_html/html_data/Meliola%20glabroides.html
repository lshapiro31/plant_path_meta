Nomenclature data for Meliola glabroides

Asteridiella glabroides (F. Stevens) Hansf. 1961 (Ascomycetes, Meliolales)

≡ Meliola glabroides F. Stevens 1916

≡Asbolisia glabroides (F. Stevens) Speg. 1918

≡Fumiglobus glabroides (F. Stevens) D.R. Reynolds & G.S. Gilbert 2006

Notes: Asbolisia glabroides is apparently unpublished and not the in publication cited by Reynolds et al. (2006).

Distribution: Caribbean, Central America.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Sooty mold.

Host: Diverse hosts.

Supporting Literature:

Bose, T., Reynolds, D.R., and Berbee, M.L. 2014. Common, unsightly and until now undescribed: Fumiglobus pieridicola sp. nov., a sooty mold infesting Pieris japonica from western North America. Mycologia 106(4): 746-756.
