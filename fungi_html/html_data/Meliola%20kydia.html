Nomenclature data for Meliola kydia

Meliola garciniae H.S. Yates 1918 (Ascomycetes, Meliolales)

≡Meliola garciniae var. garciniae H.S. Yates 1949

= Meliola kydia Sacc. 1921

Notes: Type variety is included in this nomenclator.

Distribution: Asia, North America (Dominican Republic).

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Sooty mold.

Host: Garcinia spp., Mammea sp. (Clusiaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Hansford, C.G. 1961. The Meliolineae. A monograph. Beih. Sydowia 2: 1-806.
