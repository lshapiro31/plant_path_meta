Nomenclature data for Meliola mangiferae

Meliola mangiferae Earle 1905 (Ascomycetes, Meliolales)

Distribution: Widespread in warmer regions.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Sooty mold.

Host: Anacardium occidentale, Mangifera spp. (Anacardiaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Rodríguez, M., and Minter, D.W. 1998. Meliola mangiferae. I.M.I. Descr. Fungi Bact. 1355: 1-2.
