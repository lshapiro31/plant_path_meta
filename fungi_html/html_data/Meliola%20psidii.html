Nomenclature data for Meliola psidii

Meliola psidii var. psidii Fr. : Fr. 1963 (Ascomycetes, Meliolales)

≡ Meliola psidii Fr. : Fr. 1830

= Meliola horrida Ellis & Everh. 1893

= Sphaeria trichostroma Kunze 1827

    ≡ Meliola trichostroma var. trichostroma (Kunze) Toro 1961

    ≡ Meliola trichostroma (Kunze) Toro 1952

Notes: Fries adopted Meliola psidii in his Index even though he listed Sphaeria trichostroma as a synonym in the same work. Thus, the former name is sanctioned and has priority.

Distribution: North America (Caribbean, Mexico, Nicaragua), South America.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Disease minor.

Host: Psidium spp. (Myrtaceae).
