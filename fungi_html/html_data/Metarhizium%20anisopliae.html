Nomenclature data for Metarhizium anisopliae

Metarhizium anisopliae var. Anisopliae (Metschn.) Sorokin 1976 (Ascomycetes, Hypocreales)

≡ Entomophthora anisopliae Metschn. 1879

≡Isaria anisopliae (Metschn.) Pettit 1895

≡Metarhizium anisopliae (Metschn.) Sorokin 1883  Note: See type variety.

Notes: This is the 'short-spored' of the two M. anisopliae varieties. Occurs on a wide range of insects. Tulloch (Trans. Brit. Mycol. Soc. 66(3):407-411. 1976).

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: On insects.

Disease Note: Useful in biocontrol of insects.
