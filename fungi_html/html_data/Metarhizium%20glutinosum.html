Nomenclature data for Metarhizium glutinosum

Myrothecium verrucaria (Alb. & Schwein.) Ditmar : Fr. 1813 [1817] (Ascomycetes, Hypocreales)

≡ Peziza verrucaria Alb. & Schwein. 1805

= Gliocladium fimbriatum Gilman & E.V. Abbott 1927

= Metarhizium glutinosum S.A. Pope 1944

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Seeds, leaves, roots; soil.

Disease Note: Necrosis/decay, leaf spot.

Host: Wide host range; a faculative parasite of many cultivated plants.
