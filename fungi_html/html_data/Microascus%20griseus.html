Nomenclature data for Microascus griseus

Microascus cinereus Curzi 1931 (Ascomycetes, Microascales)

= Scopulariopsis cinerea Emile-Weil & L. Gaudin 1919

= Peristomium desmosporum var. verticillium Lechmere 1913

= Microascus griseus P.N. Mathur & Thirum. 1963

= Microascus lunasporus P.M. Jones 1936

= Microascus pedrosoi C.A. Fuentes & Wolf{?} 1956

Notes: From Scopulariopsis cinerea - From Microascus cinereus - Often isolated from soil, decaying matter, flowers and seeds. This nomenclator is based on the assumption that the generic name Scopulariopsis will be conserved over Microascus.

Distribution: Widely distributed.

Disease Note: Occasional human pathogen. Theobroma branch dieback.

Host: Helianthus, Sapindus, Theobroma, Zea (multiple families).

Supporting Literature:
