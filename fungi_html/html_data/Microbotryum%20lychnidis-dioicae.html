Nomenclature data for Microbotryum lychnidis-dioicae

Microbotryum lychnidis-dioicae (Liro) G. Deml & Oberw. 1982 (Microbotryomycetes, Microbotryales)

≡ Ustilago lychnidis-dioicae Liro 1924  Note: Uredo antherarum unranked lychnidis dioicae is invalid via nom. nud (Vanky 1994). Thus, this is the valid publication of a new species by Liro.

Notes: As 'lynchis-dioicae'.

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Anthers.

Disease Note: Smut.

Host: Reported on Caryophyllaceae, especially Silene spp.

Supporting Literature:

Denchev, C.M., Giraud, T., and Hood, M.E. 2009. Three new species of anthericolous smut fungi on Caryophyllaceae. Mycol. Balcan. 6: 79-84.
