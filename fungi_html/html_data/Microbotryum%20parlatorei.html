Nomenclature data for Microbotryum parlatorei

Bauhinus parlatorei (A.A. Fisch. Waldh.) Denchev 1997 (Urediniomycetes, Microbotryales)

≡ Ustilago parlatorei A.A. Fisch. Waldh. 1876

≡Microbotryum parlatorei (A.A. Fisch. Waldh.) Vánky 1998

= Ustilago lagerheimii Bref. 1895

Distribution: Widespread.

Disease Note: Smut.

Host: Rumex spp. (Polygonaceae).
