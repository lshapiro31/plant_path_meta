Nomenclature data for Microcera curta

Microcera larvarum (Fuckel) Gräfenhan, Seifert & Schroers 2011 (Ascomycetes, Hypocreales)

≡ Fusarium larvarum Fuckel 1870

≡Fusarium larvarum var. larvarum Fuckel

≡Fusarium nivale var. larvarum (Fuckel) Bilai 1955

= Microcera aurantiicola Petch 1921

= Nectria aurantiicola Berk. & Broome 1873

    ≡ Sphaerostilbe aurantiicola (Berk. & Broome) Petch 1920

    ≡ Corallomyces aurantiicola (Berk. & Broome) Höhn. 1910

    ≡ Cosmospora aurantiicola (Berk. & Broome) Rossman & Samuels 1999

    ≡ Cucurbitaria aurantiicola (Berk. & Broome) Kuntze 1898

= Microcera curta Sacc. 1909

= Microcera perlatoriae Trab. 1907

= Microcera tonduzii Pat. 1912

  Variant spelling Microcera tonduzi Pat. 1912

Notes: The anamorph/teleomorph connection needs reexamination (Grafenhan et al. 2011).

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Insects, fungal tissues.

Disease Note: Hyperparasite and parasite of insects.

Supporting Literature:

Booth, C. 1981. Nectria aurantiicola. C.M.I. Descr. Pathog. Fungi Bact. 714: 1-2.
