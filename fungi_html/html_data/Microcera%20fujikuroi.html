Nomenclature data for Microcera fujikuroi

Microcera diploa (Berk. & M.A. Curtis) Gräfenhan & Seifert 2011 (Ascomycetes, Hypocreales)

≡ Nectria diploa Berk. & M.A. Curtis 1869  Note: Sometimes erroneously cited as Berk. & Cooke.

≡Calonectria diploa (Berk. & M.A. Curtis) Wollenw. 1926

≡Cosmospora diploa (Berk. & M.A. Curtis) Rossman & Samuels 1999

≡Creonectria diploa (Berk. & M.A. Curtis) Seaver 1909

≡Cucurbitaria diploa (Berk. & M.A. Curtis) Kuntze 1898

= Fusarium coccidicola Henn. 1904

= Nectria coccophila Nomura 1901  Note: Not the illegitimate later homonym Nectria coccophila (Tul. & C. Tul.) Wollenw. & Reink. 1935 = Cosmospora flammea.

= Microcera fujikuroi Miyabe & Sawada 1913

= Aschersonia henningsii Koord. 1907

    ≡ Pseudomicrocera henningsii (Koord.) Petch 1921

    ≡ Microcera henningsii (Koord.) Petch 1914

= Microcera merrillii Syd. & P. Syd. 1914

Distribution: Tropical regions (Rossman et al. 1999).

Substrate: Scale insects.

Disease Note: Parasitic on scale insects.

Host: Scale insects.

Supporting Literature:
