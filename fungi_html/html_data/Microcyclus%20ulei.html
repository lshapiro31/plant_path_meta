Nomenclature data for Microcyclus ulei

Pseudocercospora ulei (Henn.) Hora Junior & Mizubuti 2014 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Dothidella ulei Henn. 1904

≡Melanopsammopsis ulei (Henn.) Stahel 1917

≡Microcyclus ulei (Henn.) Arx 1962

= Fusicladium heveae K. Schub. & U. Braun 2003

     Fusicladium macrosporum Kuyper 1911

= Aposphaeria ulei Henn. 1904

Notes: From Passalora heveae - In herb. only. Nomen invalid. From Fusicladium heveae - nom. nov. (non Fusicladium macrosporium Bonord. 1864).

Distribution: New World tropics.

Substrate: Living leaves, petioles, stems.

Disease Note: South American leaf blight of rubber.

Host: Hevea spp. (Euphorbiaceae).
