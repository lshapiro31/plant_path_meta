Nomenclature data for Microdochium bolleyi

Microdochium bolleyi (R. Sprague) De Hoog & Herm.-Nijh. 1977 (Ascomycetes, Xylariales)

≡ Gloeosporium bolleyi R. Sprague 1948

≡Aureobasidium bolleyi (R. Sprague) Arx 1957

Notes: Root necrosis and decay of grasses. Recorded on at least 120 species of grasses. DeHoog & Hermanides-Nijhof (Stud. Mycol. 15:194. 1977).

Distribution: Central and western North America.

Substrate: Roots.

Disease Note: Root necrosis and rot; seedling root rot.

Host: Primarily on Poaceae.

Supporting Literature:

Ernst, M, Neubert, K., Mendgen, K.W., and Wirsel, S.G.R. 2011. Niche differentiation of two sympatric species of Microdochium colonizing the roots of common reed. BMC Microbiol. 11: 242.
