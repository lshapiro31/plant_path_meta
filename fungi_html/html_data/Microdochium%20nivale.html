= Lanosa nivalis Fr. 1849

  ≡Microdochium nivale (Fr. : Fr.) Samuels & I.C. Hallett 1983

     Fusarium nivale (Fr.) Sorauer 1901

Notes: Causes diseases of cereal and turf (Booth. C.M.I. Descr. 309. 1971). Two varieties are currently recognized (Boerema et al. Neth. J. Pl. Pathol. 98(1):13. 1992). From Lanosa nivalis - From Fusarium nivale - A homonym exists for this name.

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: On leaves and seeds.

Disease Note: Snow mold, leaf spot.

Host: Primarily on Poaceae.
