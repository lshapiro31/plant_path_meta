Nomenclature data for Microdochium phragmitis

Microdochium phragmitis Syd. 1924 (Ascomycetes, Xylariales)

Distribution: Australia, Europe.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Phragmites australis (Poaceae).
