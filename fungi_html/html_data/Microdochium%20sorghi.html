Nomenclature data for Microdochium sorghi

Microdochium sorghi (D.C. Bain & Edgerton ex Deighton) U. Braun 1995 (Ascomycetes, Xylariales)

≡ Gloeocercospora sorghi D.C. Bain & Edgerton ex Deighton 1971  Note: Not validly published in 1943.

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: On living leaves.

Disease Note: Zonate leaf spot or copper spot of grasses

Host: Poaceae.
