Nomenclature data for Micronectriella cucumeris

Plectosphaerella cucumerina (Lindf.) W. Gams 1972 (Ascomycetes, Phyllachorales)

≡ Venturia cucumerina Lindf. 1919

≡Monographella cucumerina (Lindf.) Arx 1984

= Cephalosporium ciferrii Verona 1939

= Plectosphaerella cucumeris Kleb. 1929

  ≡Micronectriella cucumeris (Kleb.) C. Booth 1971

= Cephalosporiopsis imperfecta Moreau 1941

= Cephalosporium tabacinum J.F.H. Beyma 1933

    ≡ Microdochium tabacinum (J.F.H. Beyma) Arx 1984

    ≡ Plectosporium tabacinum (J.F.H. Beyma) M.E. Palm, W. Gams & Nirenberg 1995

    ≡ Fusarium tabacinum (J.F.H. Beyma) W. Gams 1968

Notes: The name Nectria septomyxa Wollenw. 1926 has been misapplied to this species (Palm 1995). He names Septomyxa affinis (Sherb.) Wollenw. 1930, Septomyxa persicina (Fres.) Sacc. 1884, and Cephalosporium curtipes Sacc. 1881 have been misapplied to this species.

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Various plant parts.

Disease Note: Causes disease of various plants; also reported as a parasite of crayfish.

Host: Various plant families.
