Nomenclature data for Microsphaera baeumleri

Erysiphe baeumleri (Magnus) U. Braun & S. Takam. 2000 (Ascomycetes, Erysiphales)

≡ Microsphaera baeumleri Magnus 1899

      Variant spelling Microsphaera baumleri Magnus 1899

≡Trichocladia baeumleri (Magnus) Neger 1905

= Microsphaera marchica Magnus 1899

Notes: The synonym Erysiphe viciae Fuss 1878 is an invalid name, nom. nud.

Distribution: Asia, Europe, North America (Braun 1995), South America (Argentina, Chile).

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Powdery mildew.

Host: Vicia spp. (Fabaceae). Report on Spartium junceum requires confirmation.

Supporting Literature:

Braun, U. 1987. A monograph of the Erysiphales (powdery mildews). Beih. Nova Hedwigia 89: 1-700.

Braun, U., and Cook, R.T.A. 2012. Taxonomy Manual of the Erysiphales (Powdery Mildews). CBS Biodivers. Ser. 11: 703.
