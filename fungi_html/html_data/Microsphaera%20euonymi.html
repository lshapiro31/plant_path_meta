Erysiphe euonymi DC. 1815 (Ascomycetes, Erysiphales)

    Variant spelling Erysiphe evonymi DC. 1815

≡Microsphaera euonymi (DC.) Sacc. 1882

Distribution: Eurasia, North America (USA).

Substrate: Living leaves,

Disease Note: Powdery mildew.

Host: Euonymus spp. (Celastraceae).
