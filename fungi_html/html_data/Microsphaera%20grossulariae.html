Nomenclature data for Microsphaera grossulariae

Erysiphe grossulariae (Wallr.) de Bary 1870 (Ascomycetes, Erysiphales)

≡ Alphitomorpha penicillata var. grossulariae Wallr. 1819

≡Microsphaera grossulariae (Wallr.) Lév. 1851

Notes: Erysiphe penicillata (Wallr.) Fr. 1829 is a partial synonym (pro parte).

Distribution: Asia, Australia, Europe, North America (USA).

Disease Note: Powdery mildew.

Host: Ribes spp. (gooseberry, Grossulariaceae), Sambucus spp. (Adoxaceae) and plants from other families.
