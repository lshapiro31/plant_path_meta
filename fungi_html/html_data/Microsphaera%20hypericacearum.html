Nomenclature data for Microsphaera hypericacearum

Erysiphe hyperici (Wallr.) S. Blumer 1933 (Ascomycetes, Erysiphales)

≡ Alphitomorpha hyperici Wallr. 1819

≡Microsphaera hypericacearum U. Braun 1981

Notes: Microsphaera hyperici was already occupied. Braun created a nom. nov. for this reason.

Distribution: Asia, Europe, North America.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Powdery mildew.

Host: Hypericum spp. (Hypericaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Braun, U., and Takamatsu, S. 2000. Phylogeny of Erysiphe, Microsphaera, Uncinula (Erysipheae) and Cystotheca, Podosphaera, Sphaerotheca (Cystotheceae) inferred from rDNA ITS sequences - some taxonomic consequences. Schlechtendalia 4: 1-33.
