Nomenclature data for Microsphaera hypophylla

Erysiphe hypophylla (Nevod.) U. Braun & Cunningt. 2003 (Ascomycetes, Erysiphales)

≡ Microsphaera hypophylla Nevod. 1952

Distribution: Widespread, especially Asia, Europe. Introduced in New Zealand.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Powdery mildew.

Host: Quercus spp. (Fagaceae). Records on Paeonia represent Erysiphe alphitoides (Takamatsu et al. 2007).

Supporting Literature:
