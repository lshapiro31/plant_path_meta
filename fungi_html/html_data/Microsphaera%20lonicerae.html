Nomenclature data for Microsphaera lonicerae

Erysiphe lonicerae var. lonicerae DC. : Fr. 1815 (Ascomycetes, Erysiphales)

≡Alphitomorpha divaricata var. lonicerae (DC.) Schltdl. 1819

≡Microsphaera lonicerae (DC. : Fr.) G. Winter 1884

Distribution: Asia, Europe.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Powdery mildew.

Host: Lonicera spp. (Caprifoliaceae).
