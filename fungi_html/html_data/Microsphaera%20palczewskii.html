Nomenclature data for Microsphaera palczewskii

Erysiphe palczewskii (Jacz.) U. Braun & S. Takam. 2000 (Ascomycetes, Erysiphales)

≡ Microsphaera palczewskii Jacz. 1927

= Microsphaera subtrichotoma U. Braun 1985

    ≡ Erysiphe subtrichotoma (U. Braun) U. Braun & S. Takam. 2000

Distribution: Asia, Europe, North America.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Powdery mildew.

Host: Caragana, Robinia pseudoacacia, Colutea arborescens (Fabaceae).
