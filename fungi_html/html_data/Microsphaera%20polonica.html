Nomenclature data for Microsphaera polonica

Erysiphe friesii var. friesii (Lév.) U. Braun & S. Takam. 2000 (Ascomycetes, Erysiphales)

≡ Microsphaera friesii Lév. 1851

≡Erysiphe friesii (Lév.) U. Braun & S. Takam. 2000

= Microsphaera polonica Siemaszko 1933

Distribution: Asia, Europe.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Powdery mildew.

Host: Rhamnus spp. (Rhamnaceae), also reported from other plant families.
