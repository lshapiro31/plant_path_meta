Nomenclature data for Microsphaera symphoricarpi

Erysiphe symphoricarpi (Howe) U. Braun & S. Takam. 2000 (Ascomycetes, Erysiphales)

≡ Microsphaera symphoricarpi Howe 1874  Note: Braun (Nova Hedwigia 39:239. 1984).

≡Trichocladia symphoricarpi (Howe) Jacz. 1927

Distribution: Europe, North America.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Powdery mildew.

Host: Symphoricarpos spp. (Caprifoliaceae).
