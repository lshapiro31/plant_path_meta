Nomenclature data for Microsphaeropsis olivacea

Microsphaeropsis olivacea (Bonord.) Höhn. 1917 (Ascomycetes, Dothideales)

≡ Coniothyrium olivaceum Bonord. 1869

Notes: As 'olivaceus'. Additional hosts are listed in Sutton (The Coelomycetes. p. 425. 1980).

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Branches, twigs.

Disease Note: Saprobic; also a pathogen causing brown spine rot of Alhagi maurorum.

Host: Numerous hosts.
