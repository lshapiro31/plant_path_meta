Nomenclature data for Monilia azaleae

Monilinia azaleae Honey 1936 (Ascomycetes, Helotiales)

= Monilia azaleae Batra 1991

Notes: Fruit mummification and leaf blight (Batra. Mycol. Mem. 16:195. 1991).

Distribution: Eastern United States.

Substrate: On living leaves and twigs.

Disease Note: Twig blight.

Host: Rhododendron (Ericaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Batra, L.R. 1991. World species of Monilinia (Fungi): Their ecology, biosystematics and control. Mycol. Mem. 16: 1-246.
