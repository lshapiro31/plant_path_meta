Nomenclature data for Monilia crataegi

Monilinia johnsonii (Ellis & Everh.) Honey 1936 (Ascomycetes, Helotiales)

≡ Ciboria johnsonii Ellis & Everh. 1894

= Monilia crataegi Died. 1904

Distribution: Northeastern North America; Europe.

Substrate: On living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf blight.

Host: On Crataegus (Rosaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Batra, L.R. 1991. World species of Monilinia (Fungi): Their ecology, biosystematics and control. Mycol. Mem. 16: 1-246.
