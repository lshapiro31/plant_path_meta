Nomenclature data for Monilia digitata

Penicillium digitatum (Pers. : Fr.) Sacc. 1883 (Ascomycetes, Eurotiales)

≡ Aspergillus digitatus Pers. : Fr. 1794

≡Monilia digitata (Pers. : Fr.) Pers. 1801

≡Mucor digitata (Pers. : Fr.) Mérat 1821

Notes: As pointed out by Onions (1966), many synonyms probably exist.

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Fruit.

Disease Note: Fruit rots, especially Citrus.

Host: Multiple genera in multiple families.
