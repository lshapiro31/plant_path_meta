Nomenclature data for Monilia herbarum

Torula herbarum (Pers. : Fr.) Link 1809 (Ascomycetes, Incertae sedis)

≡ Monilia herbarum Pers. : Fr. 1801

= Torula monilis Pers. 1794

Notes: Torula monilis Pers. (1794) provides the oldest epithet but is unpriorable because Monilia herbarum is sanctioned.

Distribution: Cosmopolitan, most common in temperate regions.

Substrate: All plant parts and numerous additional substrates.

Disease Note: Leaf and stem rots, foot rot, etc.

Host: Numerous hosts.
