Nomenclature data for Monilia sitophila

Neurospora sitophila Shear & B.O. Dodge 1927 (Ascomycetes, Sordariales)

= Oidium aurantiacum Lév. 1843

    ≡ Oospora aurantiaca (Lév.) Herter 1922

  [≡ Monilia aurantiaca (Lév.) Herter 1933 - illegitimate later homonym, not included in search]  Note: Not Monilia aurantiaca Peck & Sacc. 1888.

= Penicillium sitophilum Mont. 1843  Note: From Chrysonilia sitophila - This species is the type of the genus Chrysonilia. The epithet aurantiacum from Oidium aurantiacum Lév. 1843 may have equal priority, but it is not clear whether this name was validly published. According to Herter (1922) and Shear & Dodge (1927), Oidium aurantiacum was described and attributed to Léveillé in a publication by Payen (1843), but it is not in our collection so we can not confirm this. Saccardo (Syll Fung. VI:35 1886) cited Oidium aurantiacum as Lév. ex Mont., but we could find no evidence that Montagne validated this name. Herter provided no additional description when making the new combinations Oospora aurantiaca (1922) and Monilia aurantiaca (1933); if the name Oidium aurantiacum is invalid then these combinations are also invalid.

    ≡ Chrysonilia sitophila (Mont.) Arx 1981

Notes: From Oidium aurantiacum - Possibly an invalid name, nom. nud. Synonymy based on Herter (1922) and Shear & Dodge (1927).
