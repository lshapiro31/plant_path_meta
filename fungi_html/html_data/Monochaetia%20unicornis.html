Nomenclature data for Monochaetia unicornis

Seiridium unicorne (Cooke & Ellis) B. Sutton 1975 (Ascomycetes, Xylariales)

    Variant spelling Pestalozzia unicornis Cooke & Ellis 1878

≡ Pestalotia unicornis Cooke & Ellis 1878

≡Monochaetia unicornis (Cooke & Ellis) Sacc. 1906

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Woody plant parts.

Disease Note: Canker.

Host: Cupressaceae.
