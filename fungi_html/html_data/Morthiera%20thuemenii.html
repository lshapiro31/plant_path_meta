Nomenclature data for Morthiera thuemenii

Entomosporium thuemenii (Cooke) Sacc. 1884 (Ascomycetes, Helotiales)

≡ Morthiera thuemenii Cooke 1877

Notes: Leaf blight of English hawthorn. Stowell & Backus (1966) consider this species a synonym of Entomosporium maculatum, now Diplocarpon maculatum. This species belongs in Diplocarpon.

Distribution: Northern North America.

Substrate: On living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf blight of hawthorne.

Host: On Crataegus (Rosaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Greene, H.C. 1956. Notes on Wisconsin parasitic fungi. XXII. Trans. Wisconsin Acad. Sci. 45: 177-191.

Stowell, E.A., and Backus, M.P. 1966. Morphology and cytology of Diplocarpon maculatum on Crataegus. I.The Entomosporium stage. Mycologia 58: 949-960.

Updated prior to 1989
