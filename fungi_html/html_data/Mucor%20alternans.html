Nomenclature data for Mucor alternans

Mucor circinelloides Tiegh. 1875 (Zygomycetes, Mucorales)

≡Calyptromyces circinelloides (Tiegh.) Sumst. 1910

≡Circinomucor circinelloides (Tiegh.) Arx 1982

≡Mucor circinelloides f. circinelloides

= Mucor alternans Tiegh.

= Mucor ambiguus Vuill. 1887 [1886]

= Mucor javanicus Wehmer 1900

= Mucor prainii Chodat & Nechitsche 1904

  Variant spelling Mucor praini Chodat & Nechitsche 1904

Notes: Different taxonomic treatments may include other synonyms.

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: In soil and on a variety of organic substrates.

Disease Note: Postharvest rot.

Host: Numerous plant hosts.
