Nomenclature data for Mucor stolonifer

Rhizopus stolonifer (Ehrenb. : Fr.) Vuill. 1902 (Zygomycetes, Mucorales)

≡ Mucor stolonifer Ehrenb. 1818

= Rhizopus artocarpi Racib. 1959

= Rhizopus necans Massee 1897

= Rhizopus nigricans Ehrenb. 1821

= Rhizopus nigricans var. luxurians J. Schröt. 1886

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Fruit, bulbs, etc.

Disease Note: Rots, especially fruits and vegetables.

Host: Multiple genera in multiple families.
