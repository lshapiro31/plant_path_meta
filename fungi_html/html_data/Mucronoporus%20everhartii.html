Nomenclature data for Mucronoporus everhartii

Phellinus everhartii (Ellis & Galloway) A. Ames 1972 (Basidiomycetes, Hymenochaetales)

≡ Mucronoporus everhartii Ellis & Galloway 1889

≡Fomes everhartii (Ellis & Galloway) H. Schrenk & Spauld. 1909

≡Pyropolyporus everhartii (Ellis & Galloway) Murrill 1903

≡Scindalma everhartii (Ellis & Galloway) Kuntze 1898

Notes: http://www.reviberoammicol.com/2000-17/064068.pdf

Distribution: North America, South America.

Substrate: Wood.

Disease Note: White heart rot, canker.

Host: Various hardwoods, especially Quercus spp. (Fagaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Rosenthal, G.A. 1963. A new host of Fomes everhartii. Pl. Dis. Reporter 47: 152-153.
