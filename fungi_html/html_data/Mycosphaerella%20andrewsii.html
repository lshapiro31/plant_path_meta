Nomenclature data for Mycosphaerella andrewsii

Mycosphaerella andrewsii (Sacc.) Davis 1929 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Sphaerella andrewsii Sacc. 1917

= Asteromella andrewsii Petr. 1929

= Sphaeria lichenoides var. gentianicola DC. 1815

    ≡ Phyllosticta gentianicola (DC.) Ellis & Everh. 1896

    ≡ Leptothyrium gentianicola (DC.) Baumler 1885

Notes: These names need work!

Distribution: Central United States; Italy (type). From Asteromella andrewsii - Eastern North America; Europe.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: On Gentiana (Gentianaceae).
