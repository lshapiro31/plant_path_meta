Nomenclature data for Mycosphaerella arachidicola

Didymella arachidicola (Khokhr.) Tomilin 1979 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

≡ Mycosphaerella arachidicola Khokhr. 1934

≡Didymosphaeria arachidicola (Khokhr.) Alcorn, Punith. & G. McCarthy 1976

  Variant spelling Didymosphaeria arachdicola (Khokhr.) Alcorn, Punith. & G. McCarthy 1976

≡Peyronellaea arachidicola (Khokhr.) Aveskamp, Gruyter & Verkley 2010

≡Mycosphaerella jenkinsii Tomilin 1968

= Mycosphaerella arachidicola W.A. Jenkins 1938

= Phoma arachidicola Marasas, Pauer & Boerema 1974

= Mycosphaerella arachidis Deighton 1967

= Mycosphaerella argentinensis Frezzi 1969

Notes: Not (Khokhr.) Taber et al. 1984. From Mycosphaerella arachidicola - Not W.A. Jenkins 1938. From Peyronellaea arachidicola - Peyronellaea has an anamorphic type. From Mycosphaerella arachidicola - Not Khokhr. 1934. Nomen illeg. From Mycosphaerella jenkinsii - Superfluous. Nomen illeg.

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Net blotch, web blotch, leaf blotch. Often, complete disintegration of leaves.

Host: Peanut, Arachis hypogaea (Fabaceae).
