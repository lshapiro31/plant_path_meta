Nomenclature data for Mycosphaerella caricae

Stagonosporopsis caricae (Sydow & P. Sydow) Aveskamp, Gruyter & Verkley 2010 (Ascomycetes, Incertae sedis)

≡ Mycosphaerella caricae Syd. & P. Syd. 1913  Note: Not (Maubl.) Hansf. 1941.

= Sphaerella caricae Maubl. 1913

  ≡Mycosphaerella caricae (Maubl.) Hansf. 1941  Note: Not Syd. & P. Syd. 1913.

Distribution: Africa, South America, North America (FL, HI), Asia.

Substrate: Leaves, petioles, fruits, stems.

Disease Note: Fruit rot, leaf spot and stem rot.

Host: Papaya, Carica papaya (Caricaceae). Also Brassica sp. and Curcurbitaceae.
