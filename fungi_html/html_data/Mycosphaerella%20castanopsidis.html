Nomenclature data for Mycosphaerella castanopsidis

Mycosphaerella janus (Berk. & M.A. Curtis) Petr. 1958 [1957] (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Sphaeria janus Berk. & M.A. Curtis 1876

≡Dothidella janus (Berk. & M.A. Curtis) Höhn. 1915

≡Leptosphaeria janus (Berk. & M.A. Curtis) Sacc. 1883

= Dothidella castanopsidis Dearn. 1924  Note: as 'castanopsii'

  Variant spelling Dothidella castanopsii Dearn.

  ≡Mycosphaerella castanopsidis (Dearn.) Petr. 1941

Notes: Although the genus Mycosphaerella is now considered a synonym of Ramularia, many species have not been placed elsewhere.

Distribution: Southern and western North America; Nepal.

Substrate: On leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Fagaceae.
