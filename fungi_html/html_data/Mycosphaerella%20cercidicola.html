Nomenclature data for Mycosphaerella cercidicola

Passalora cercidicola (Ellis) U. Braun 1995 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Cercospora cercidicola Ellis 1882

= Sphaerella cercidicola Ellis & Kellerm. 1884

  ≡Mycosphaerella cercidicola (Ellis & Kellerm.) F.A. Wolf 1940

    =  Cercospora cercidicola var. coremioides Tehon 1924

Distribution: Australasia, Japan, USA.

Substrate: On living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: On Cercis spp. (Fabaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Chupp, C. 1954. Monograph of the fungus genus Cercospora. Published by the Author, Ithaca, New York, 667 pages.

Crous, P.W., and Braun, U. 2003. Mycosphaerella and its anamorphs: 1. Names published in Cercospora and Passalora. Centraalbureau voor Schimmelcultures, Utrecht, 571 pages.
