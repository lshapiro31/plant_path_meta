Nomenclature data for Mycosphaerella confusa

Pseudocercospora rubi (Sacc.) Deighton 1976 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Cercospora rubi Sacc. 1876

= Mycosphaerella confusa F.A. Wolf 1936

  [≡ Mycosphaerella dubia F.A. Wolf 1935 - illegitimate later homonym, not included in search]

= Cercospora garbiniana C. Massal. 1902

= Cercospora septorioides Ellis & Everh. 1896

Notes: From Mycosphaerella dubia - Not L.E. Miles 1918. Nomen illeg.

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: On living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Rubus spp. (Rosaceae) and rare questionable reports from few other taxa in same family.

Supporting Literature:

Chupp, C. 1954. Monograph of the fungus genus Cercospora. Published by the Author, Ithaca, New York, 667 pages.

Crous, P.W., and Braun, U. 2003. Mycosphaerella and its anamorphs: 1. Names published in Cercospora and Passalora. Centraalbureau voor Schimmelcultures, Utrecht, 571 pages.
