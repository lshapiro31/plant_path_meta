Nomenclature data for Mycosphaerella crotalariae

Cercospora crotalariae Sacc. 1913 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

= Sphaerella crotolariae Petch 1906

  ≡Mycosphaerella crotalariae (Petch) Hansf. 1942

Notes: Crous and Braun (2003) consider this to be Cercospora apii s. lat.

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Crotalaria spp. (Fabaceae).

Supporting Literature:
