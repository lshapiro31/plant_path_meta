Nomenclature data for Mycosphaerella fijiensis

Pseudocercospora fijiensis (M. Morelet) Deighton 1976 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Cercospora fijiensis M. Morelet 1969

≡Paracercospora fijiensis (M. Morelet) Deighton 1979

= Mycosphaerella fijiensis M. Morelet 1969

= Cercospora fijiensis var. difformis J.L. Mulder & R.H. Stover 1976

    ≡ Paracercospora fijiensis var. difformis (J.L. Mulder & R.H. Stover) Deighton 1979

= Mycosphaerella fijiensis var. difformis J.L. Mulder & R.H. Stover 1976

Distribution: Widespread in banana growing regions.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Black Sigatoka (black leaf streak) of banana.

Host: Musa spp. (Musaceae).
