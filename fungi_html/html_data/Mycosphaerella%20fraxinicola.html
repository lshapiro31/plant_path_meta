Nomenclature data for Mycosphaerella fraxinicola

Mycosphaerella fraxinicola (Schwein.) House 1921 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Sphaeria fraxinicola Schwein. 1832

≡Sphaerella fraxinicola (Schwein.) Cooke 1883

= Phyllosticta viridis Ellis & Kellerm. 1889

Notes: Leaf spot (Wolf. Mycologia 31:258. 1939).

Distribution: Central and eastern North America.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Fraxinus spp. (Oleaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Horst, R.K. 2001. Westcott's Plant Disease Handbook, Sixth Edition. Kluwer Academic Publishers, 1008 pages.
