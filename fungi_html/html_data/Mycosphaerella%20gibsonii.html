Nomenclature data for Mycosphaerella gibsonii

Pseudocercospora pini-densiflorae (Hori & N. Nambu) Deighton 1987 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Cercospora pini-densiflorae Hori & N. Nambu 1917

≡Cercoseptoria pini-densiflorae (Hori & N. Nambu) Deighton 1976

= Mycosphaerella gibsonii H. Evans 1984

Notes: From Mycosphaerella gibsonii - Ascostromata variable; more extensive, investing the entire cortex in East African specimens, but more reduced, predominantly uniloculate and discrete in Asian specimens (Evans. Mycol. Pap. 153:61. 1984).

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Needles.

Disease Note: Leaf blight.

Host: Pinus spp. (Pinaceae).
