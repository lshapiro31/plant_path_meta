Nomenclature data for Mycosphaerella graminicola

Zymoseptoria tritici (Desm.) Quaedvlieg & Crous 2011 (, )

≡ Septoria tritici Desm. 1842

= Sphaeria graminicola Fuckel 1865

  ≡Mycosphaerella graminicola (Fuckel) J. Schröt. 1894

    ≡ Sphaerella graminicola (Fuckel) Fuckel 1870

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Living leaves, leaf sheaths.

Disease Note: Leaf spot or speckled leaf blotch of wheat.

Host: Triticum and possibly a few other grasses.
