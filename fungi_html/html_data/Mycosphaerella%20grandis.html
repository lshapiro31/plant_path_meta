Nomenclature data for Mycosphaerella grandis

Austroafricana parva (R.F. Park & Keane) Quaedvl. & Crous 2014 (, )

≡ Mycosphaerella parva R.F. Park & Keane 1982

≡Teratosphaeria parva (R.F. Park & Keane) Crous & U. Braun 2007

= Mycosphaerella grandis Carnegie & Keane 1994

Distribution: Africa, Australia, South America (Chile).

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Eucalyptus spp. (Myrtaceae), Protea spp. (Proteaceae).
