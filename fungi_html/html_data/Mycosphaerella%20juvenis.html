Teratosphaeria nubilosa (Cooke) Crous & U. Braun 2007 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

≡ Sphaerella nubilosa Cooke 1891

≡Mycosphaerella nubilosa (Cooke) Hansf. 1956

= Mycosphaerella juvenis Crous & M.J. Wingf. 1996

= Uwebraunia juvenis Crous & M.J. Wingf. 1996

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Eucalyptus spp. (Myrtaceae), Acacia auriculiformis (Fabaceae).
