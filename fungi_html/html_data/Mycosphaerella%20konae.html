Nomenclature data for Mycosphaerella konae

Pallidocercospora konae (Crous, Joanne E. Taylor & M.E. Palm) Crous 2012 (, )

≡ Mycosphaerella konae Crous, Joanne E. Taylor & M.E. Palm 1999

Distribution: Asia (Thailand), North America (USA: HI), South America (Colombia).

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Proteaceae. Also reported from Eucalyptus camaldulensis (Myrtaceae) and Acacia spp. (Fabaceae).
