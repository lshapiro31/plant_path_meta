Nomenclature data for Mycosphaerella lupini

Thedgonia lupini (Davis) U. Braun 1994 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Ramularia lupini Davis 1907

= Mycosphaerella lupini W.J. Kaiser & Crous 1998

Distribution: North America (USA).

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot. Heavy infection leads to premature defoliation (Kaiser & Crous 1998).

Host: Lupinus spp. (Fabaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Braun, U. 1994. Studies on Ramularia and allied genera (VII). Nova Hedwigia 58: 191-222.

Braun, U. 1995. A Monograph of Cercosporella, Ramularia and Allied Genera (Phytopathogenic Hyphomycetes), Vol. 1. IHW-Verlag, 333 pages.
