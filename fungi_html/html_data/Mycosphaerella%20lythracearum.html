Nomenclature data for Mycosphaerella lythracearum

Pseudocercospora punicae (Henn.) Deighton 1976 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Cercospora punicae Henn. 1906

= Mycosphaerella lythracearum F.A. Wolf 1927

Distribution: Widespread, especially subtropical and tropical regions.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf blotch, fruit spot.

Host: Punica granatum (Lythraceae).
