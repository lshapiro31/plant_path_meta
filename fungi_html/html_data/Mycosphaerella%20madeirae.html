Nomenclature data for Mycosphaerella madeirae

Mycosphaerelloides madeirae (Crous & Denman) Videira & Crous 2016 (, )

≡ Mycosphaerella madeirae Crous & Denman 2004

Distribution: Europe (Madeira--Portugal).

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot: also endophytic.

Host: Eucalyptus globulus (Myrtaceae), Quercus robur (Fagaceae).
