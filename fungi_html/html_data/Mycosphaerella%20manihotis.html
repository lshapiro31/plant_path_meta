[≡Mycosphaerella manihotis Ghesq. & P. Henrard 1924 - illegitimate later homonym, not included in search]

= Septogloeum manihotis Zimm. 1902

Notes: From Passalora henningsii - (Allesch) Poonam Srivast. 1994 is invalid comb. From Mycosphaerella manihotis - Not Syd. & P. Syd. 1901. Nomen illeg.

Distribution: Widespread, especially subtropical and tropical regions.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Manihot spp. (Euphorbiaceae).
