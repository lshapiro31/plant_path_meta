Nomenclature data for Mycosphaerella musicola

Pseudocercospora musae (Zimm.) Deighton 1976 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Cercospora musae Zimm. 1902

= Mycosphaerella musicola J.L. Mulder 1976

Notes: Cercospora musae Massee nom. illeg. (Art. 53.1). Mycosphaerella musicola R. Leach nom. inval. (Art. 39.1).

Distribution: Worldwide in banana-growing regions.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Yellow Sigatoka disease of banana.

Host: Musa spp. (Musaceae).
