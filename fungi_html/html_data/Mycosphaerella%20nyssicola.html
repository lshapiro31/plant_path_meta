Nomenclature data for Mycosphaerella nyssicola

Ramularia nyssicola (Cooke) Videira & Crous 2015 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Sphaerella nyssicola Cooke 1878

      Variant spelling Sphaerella nyssoecola Cooke 1878

≡Mycosphaerella nyssicola (Cooke) F.A. Wolf 1940

  Variant spelling Mycosphaerella nyssaecola (Cooke) F.A. Wolf 1940

= Phyllosticta nyssae Cooke 1883

    ≡ Asteromella nyssae (Cooke) Aa 2002

Distribution: Eastern United States.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot, discoloration, defoliation.

Host: Nyssa spp. (Cornaceae).
