Nomenclature data for Mycosphaerella pini

Dothistroma septosporum (Dorog.) M. Morelet 1968 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Cytosporina septospora Dorog. 1911

≡Septoria septospora (Dorog.) Arx 1983

≡Septoriella septospora (Dorog.) Sacc. 1931

= Actinothyrium marginatum Sacc. 1920

= Mycosphaerella pini Rostr. 1957

    ≡ Eruptio pini (Rostr.) M.E. Barr 1996

= Scirrhia pini A. Funk & A.K. Parker 1966

  ≡Mycosphaerella pini (A. Funk & A.K. Parker) Arx 1983  Note: Not. Rostr. 1957. Nom. illeg.

= Dothistroma pini var. keniense M.H. Ivory 1967  Note: As 'keniensis'.

    ≡ Dothistroma septosporum var. keniense (M.H. Ivory) B. Sutton 1980

= Dothistroma pini var. lineare Thyr & C.G. Shaw 1964

    ≡ Dothistroma septosporum var. lineare (Thyr & C.G. Shaw) B. Sutton 1980

Notes: As 'septospora' - gender of Dothistroma is neuter.

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: On living needles.

Disease Note: Red band needle blight.

Host: Pinus spp.; infections on other Pinaceae are from trees in close proximity to diseased pines.
