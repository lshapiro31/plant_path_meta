Nomenclature data for Mycosphaerella populicola

Sphaerulina populicola (Peck) Quaedvlieg, Verkley & Crous 2013 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Septoria populicola Peck 1887

= Mycosphaerella populicola G.E. Thomps. 1941

Distribution: Asia (China), temperate North America. Reported from Italy.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Populus spp. (Salicaceae).

Supporting Literature:
