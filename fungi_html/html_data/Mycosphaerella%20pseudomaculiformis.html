Nomenclature data for Mycosphaerella pseudomaculiformis

Ramularia pseudomaculiformis (Desm.) Rossman & W.C. Allen 2016 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Sphaeria pseudomaculiformis Desm. 1846

≡Sphaerella pseudomaculiformis (Desm.) Auersw. 1869

= Scolicotrichum bulbigerum Fuckel 1867

    ≡ Isariopsis bulbigera (Fuckel) Savile 1957

    ≡ Ovularia bulbigera (Fuckel) Sacc. 1886

  Variant spelling Scolecotrichum bulbigerum Fuckel

    ≡ Phacellium bulbigerum (Fuckel) U. Braun 1990

= Ramulaspera poterii Ranoj. 1918

= Mycosphaerella pseudomaculiformis (Desm.) J. Schröt. 1894

Distribution: Central Asia, Europe, North America.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Sanguisorba spp. (Rosaceae).

Supporting Literature:
