Nomenclature data for Mycosphaerella puerariicola

Pseudocercospora puerariicola (W. Yamam.) Deighton 1976 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Cercospora puerariicola W. Yamam. 1934

      Variant spelling Cercospora pueraricola W. Yamam. 1934

= Mycosphaerella puerariicola J.L. Weimer & Luttr. 1948

Notes: Originally spelled 'pueraricola'.

Distribution: Asia, North America (USA), Oceania, South America (Brazil).

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Dioclea guianensis, Pueraria spp. (Fabaceae).
