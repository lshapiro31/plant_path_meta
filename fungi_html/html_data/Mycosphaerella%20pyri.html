Nomenclature data for Mycosphaerella pyri

Septoria pyri Castagne

= Sphaerella pyri Auersw. 1869  Note: From Mycosphaerella pyri - On Pyrus species; white spot or leaf fleck of pear (Sivanesan. C.M.I. Descr. 989. 1990). Boerema (Neth. J. Pl. Pathol. 76:166. 1970) provided a nomenclatural discussion.
