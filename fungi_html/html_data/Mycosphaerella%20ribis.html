Nomenclature data for Mycosphaerella ribis

Mycosphaerella grossulariae (Fr.) Lindau 1897 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Sphaeria grossulariae Fr. 1823

≡Sphaerella grossulariae (Fr.) Auersw. 1869

= Mycosphaerella aurea R. Stone 1916

= Sphaerella ribis Sacc. 1882

  ≡Mycosphaerella ribis (Sacc.) Lindau 1903

[= Sphaeria ribis Fuckel 1870 - illegitimate later homonym, not included in search]

Notes: Per Videira et al., this species belongs in Sphaerulina but the collection of additional materials is needed before a formal recombination can be proposed. Boerema (Neth. J. Pl. Pathol. 78:30. 1972) and Jorstad (Skr. Norske Videnski.-Akad. Oslo, Mat.-Naturvidensk. Kl. 24:64. 1965) provided nomenclatural discussions.

Distribution: Temperate northern hemisphere.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Ribes spp. (Grossulariaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Kobayashi, T. 2007. Index of fungi inhabiting woody plants in Japan. Host, Distribution and Literature. Zenkoku-Noson-Kyoiku Kyokai Publishing Co., Ltd., 1227 pages.

Videira, S.I.R., Groenewald, J.Z., Nakashima, C., Braun, U., Barreto, R.W., de Wit, P.J.G.M., and Crous, P.W. 2017. Mycosphaerellaceae - chaos or clarity?. Stud. Mycol. 87: 257-421.
