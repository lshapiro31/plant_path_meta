    ≡ Pallidocercospora thailandica (Crous, Himaman & M.J. Wingf.) Phookamsak, Wulandari & K.D. Hyde 2016

Distribution: Asia (Thailand), West Indies (Windward Islands).

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Eucalyptus spp. (Myrtaceae), Musa sp. (Musaceae), Rhapis sp. (Arecaceae).
