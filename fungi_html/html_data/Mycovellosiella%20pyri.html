Nomenclature data for Mycovellosiella pyri

Cercosporella pyri (Farl.) Karak. 1937 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

    Variant spelling Cercosporella piri (Farl.) Karak. 1937

≡ Cercospora pyri Farl. 1884

      Variant spelling Cercospora piri Farl. 1884

≡Mycovellosiella pyri (Farl.) U. Braun 1993

Distribution: North America.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Aronia spp., Pyrus spp.; report from Malus sylvestris questionable (Rosaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Braun, U. 1995. A Monograph of Cercosporella, Ramularia and Allied Genera (Phytopathogenic Hyphomycetes), Vol. 1. IHW-Verlag, 333 pages.
