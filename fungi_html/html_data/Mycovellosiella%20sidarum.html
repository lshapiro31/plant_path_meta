Nomenclature data for Mycovellosiella sidarum

Passalora sidarum (Petr. & Cif.) U. Braun & Crous 2003 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Ramularia sidarum Petr. & Cif. 1932

≡Mycovellosiella sidarum (Petr. & Cif.) Deighton 1974

= Ramularia sidae L.S. Olive 1948

    ≡ Mycovellosiella sidae (L.S. Olive) Deighton 1974

Distribution: Australia, North America.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Sida spp. (Malvaceae).
