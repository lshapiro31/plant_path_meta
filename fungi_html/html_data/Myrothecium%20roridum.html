Nomenclature data for Myrothecium roridum

Paramyrothecium roridum (Tode : Fr.) L. Lombard & Crous 2016 (, )

≡ Myrothecium roridum Tode : Fr. 1790

= Myrothecium advena Sacc. 1908

= Myrotheciella catenuligera Speg. 1909

= Myrothecium fragosianum Sacc. 1917

= Exotrichum leucomelas Syd. & P. Syd. 1914

    ≡ Myrothecium leucomelas (Syd. & P. Syd.) Höhn. 1925

= Gliocladium nigrum Moreau & Moreau 1941

= Myrothecium roridum var. eichhorniae Ponnappa 1970

= Hymenopsis tenuis Petch 1927

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Leaves, fruit, roots.

Disease Note: Leaf spot, cankers; boll, fruit, petiole, and root rots, etc.

Host: Multiple genera in multiple families.
