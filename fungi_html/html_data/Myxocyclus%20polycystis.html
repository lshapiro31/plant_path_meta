Splanchnonema argus (Berk. & Broome) Kuntze 1898 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

= Stegonsporium fautreyi Sacc. & Syd. 1899

= Coryneum irregulare Berk. & M.A. Curtis 1881

    ≡ Stegonsporium irregulare (Berk. & M.A. Curtis) Sacc. 1884

= Stegonsporium muricatum Bonord. 1851

= Massaria niessleana Rehm 1882

    ≡ Splanchnonema niesslianum (Rehm) Kuntze 1898

  Variant spelling Splanchnonema niessleanum (Rehm) Kuntze

= Hendersonia polycystis Berk. & Broome 1850

  ≡Myxocyclus polycystis (Berk. & Broome) Sacc. 1908

  Variant spelling Myxocyclus polycistis (Berk. & Broome) Sacc.

    ≡ Stegonsporium polycystis (Berk. & Broome) Arx 1981

  Variant spelling Myxocyclus polycistis (Berk. & Broome) Sacc.

Notes: Stegonsporium irregulare Fautrey nom. illeg. (Art. 53.1). Citation for Hendersonia polycistis alternatively given as Berk. & Broome, Ann. Mag. nat. Hist., Ser. 2 5: no. 415 (1850) - and - Berk. & Broome, An. Mag. nat Hist. Ser. 2, 5: 374 (1850). Neither could be verified as of 3/27/2015. "Hendersonia polycistis" Sacc. (1884) appears to be merely a citation of Berkeley and Broome's name by Saccardo; oldest epithet not in correct genus.

Distribution: Australia, Eastern North America, Europe.

Substrate: Branches.

Disease Note: Saprobic; possibly an endophyte.

Host: Betula spp. (Betulaceae).
