Nomenclature data for Myxosporium musae

Colletotrichum musae (Berk. & M.A. Curtis) Arx 1957 (Ascomycetes, Phyllachorales)

≡ Myxosporium musae Berk. & M.A. Curtis 1874

= Gloeosporium musarum Cooke & Massee 1887

= Glomerella musarum Petch 1917

Notes: Based on a study of the type specimen, Fusarium semitectum is a synonym (Booth and Sutton1984). This synonymy has not been accepted (See nomenclature entry for F. semitectum).

Distribution: Widespread in subtropical and tropical regions.

Substrate: Fruits, flowers, leaves, stems.

Disease Note: Anthracnose, fruit lesions, rots.

Host: Primarily Musa spp. (Musaceae). Scattered reports from other genera in multiple families.
