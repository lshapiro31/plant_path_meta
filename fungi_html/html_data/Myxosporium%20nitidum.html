Nomenclature data for Myxosporium nitidum

Aurantioporthe corni (Wehm.) G. Beier & Blanchette 2015 (, )

≡ Apioporthe corni Wehm. 1923

≡Cryptodiaporthe corni (Wehm.) Petr. 1941

= Sphaeronaema aurantiacum Peck 1879

    ≡ Zythia aurantiaca (Peck) Sacc. 1884

    ≡ Phoma aurantiacum (Peck) Höhn. 1915

= Myxosporium nitidum Berk. & M.A. Curtis 1874

    ≡ Phoma nitidum (Berk. & M.A. Curtis) Höhn. 1915

Notes: Myxosporium nitidum Berk. & M.A. Curtis and Sphaeronema aurantiacum Peck proposed for rejection by Beier et al. (2015). Hapalocystis corni (Wehm.) M.E. Barr 1979 incorrectly included as a nomenclatural synonym of Apioporthe corni Wehm. by Beier et al. (2015); actually based on Melanconis corni Wehm. 1940.

Distribution: North America (Canada, USA).

Substrate: Bark, phloem.

Disease Note: Golden canker.

Host: Cornus alternifolia (Cornaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Beier, G.L., Hokanson, S.C., Bates, S.T., and Blanchette, R.A. 2015. Aurantioporthe corni gen. et comb. nov., an endophyte and pathogen of Cornus alternifolia. Mycologia 107(1): 66-79.
