Nomenclature data for Nectria austroamericana

Thyronectria austroamericana (Speg.) Seeler 1940 (Ascomycetes, Incertae sedis)

≡ Pleonectria austroamericana Speg. 1880

      Variant spelling Pleonectria austro-americana Speg.

≡Nectria austroamericana (Speg.) Rossman 1989

= Gyrostroma austroamericanum Seeler 1940

= Pleonectria denigrata G. Winter 1883

    ≡ Thyronectria denigrata (G. Winter) Seaver 1909

= Kaskaskia gleditsiae Born & J.L. Crane 1972

= Pleonectria guaranitica Speg. 1885

= Pleonectria nigropapillata Starbäck 1904

Distribution: North America, South America (Brazil, Paraguay).

Substrate: Bark.

Disease Note: Bark canker of honey locust (Bedker & Wingfield. Trans. Brit. Mycol. Soc. 81:179. 1983).

Host: Gleditsia triacanthos, Gleditsia japonica (honey-locust), also reported from other Fabaceae.
