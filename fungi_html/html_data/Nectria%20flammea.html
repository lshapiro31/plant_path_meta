  Variant spelling Corallomyces brachysporos Penz. & Sacc. 1901  Note: Erroneous spelling.

= Sphaerostilbe coccophila Tul. & C. Tul. 1865

  [≡ Nectria coccophila (Tul.) Wollenw. & Reinking 1930 - illegitimate later homonym, not included in search]  Note: Nectria coccophila (Tul.) Wollenw. & Reinking is a later illegitimate homonym of Nectria coccophila Nomura 1901 = Cosmospora diploa (Rossman et al. 1999).

= Sphaerostilbe flammea Tul. & C. Tul. 1865

  ≡Nectria flammea (Tul. & C. Tul.) Dingley 1951

    ≡ Cosmospora flammea (Tul. & C. Tul.) Rossman & Samuels 1999

= Nectria laeticolor Berk. & M.A. Curtis 1868

    ≡ Corallomyces laeticolor (Berk. & M.A. Curtis) Höhn. & Weese 1910  Note: Illegitimate genus (Art. 53).

= Nectria passeriniana Cooke 1884

= Microcera pluriseptata Cooke & Massee 1888

= Nectria subcoccinea Sacc. & Ellis 1881

Notes: The anamorph/teleomorph connection needs reexamination (Grafenhan et al. 2011). From Sphaerostilbe flammea - Year changed from 1861 (Rossman et al. 1999). From Sphaerostilbe coccophila - The epithet coccophila had equal priority with flammea; Rossman & Samuels established the priority by choosing to transfer flammea to Cosmospora (Art. 11.5). From Nectria subcoccinea - Rossman et al. (1999) list the year as 1881. From Corallomyces brachysporus - Illegitimate genus (Art. 53).

Distribution: Subtropical and tropical regions.

Substrate: Insects on leaves or bark.

Disease Note: Parasitic on scale insects.

Host: Scale insects on various plant hosts.
