Nomenclature data for Nectria fuscopurpurea

Nectria nigrescens Cooke 1878 (Ascomycetes, Hypocreales)

≡Cucurbitaria nigrescens (Cooke) Kuntze 1898

= Nectria cinnabarina f. dendroidea Fuckel 1874

    ≡ Nectria cinnabarina var. dendroidea (Fuckel) Wollenw. 1926

= Nectria cinnabarina var. minor Wollenw. 1926

= Nectria fuscopurpurea Wakef. 1918

  Variant spelling Nectria fusco-purpurea Wakef. 1918

= Nectria meliae Earle 1898

= Tubercularia ulmea J.C. Carter 1947

Distribution: Europe, North America.

Substrate: Dead wood.

Disease Note: Canker diseases including canker and dieback of Siberian elm (Carter. Phytopathology 37:246. 1947).

Host: Various hosts.

Supporting Literature:
