Nomenclature data for Nectria haematococca

Fusarium haematococcum Nalim, Samuels & Geiser 2011 (Ascomycetes, Hypocreales)

= Nectria haematococca Berk. & Broome 1873

    ≡ Hypomyces haematococcus (Berk. & Broome) Wollenw. 1926

    ≡ Haematonectria haematococca (Berk. & Broome) Samuels & Rossman 1999

    ≡ Neocosmospora haematococca (Berk. & Broome) Samuels, Nalim & Geiser 2011

Notes: Not synonymous with F. solani per Samuels (2011).

Distribution: Cosmopolitan (sensu lato). The type specimen from Sri Lanka is distinct from more widely distributed specimens; the true distribution (sensu stricto) is unclear.

Substrate: Bark, decorticated wood, herbaceous tissue, fruits, fungal sporocarps, roots, soil.

Disease Note: Fruit rot, stem rot, root and collar rot.

Host: Numerous hosts.
