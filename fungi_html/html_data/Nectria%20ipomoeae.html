Nomenclature data for Nectria ipomoeae

Neocosmospora ipomoeae (Halst.) L. Lombard & Crous 2015 (Ascomycetes, Hypocreales)

≡ Nectria ipomoeae Halst. 1891

≡Creonectria ipomoeae (Halst.) Seaver 1910

≡Cucurbitaria ipomoeae (Halst.) Kuntze 1898

≡Haematonectria ipomoeae (Halst.) Samuels & Nirenberg 1999

≡Hypomyces ipomoeae (Halst.) Wollenw. 1913

= Fusarium javanicum Koord. 1907

    ≡ Fusarium javanicum var. javanicum Koord. 1907  Note: Autonym no longer needed.

    ≡ Fusarium javanicum subsp. javanicum Koord. 1907  Note: Autonym no longer needed.

Distribution: Cosmopolitan, but most common in warm regions and greenhouses.

Substrate: Stems, roots, fruits (melons, squash), tubers.

Disease Note: Rots.

Host: Multiple plant families.
