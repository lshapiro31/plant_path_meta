Nomenclature data for Nectria lugdunensis

Neonectria lugdunensis (Sacc. & Therry) L. Lombard & Crous 2014 (Ascomycetes, Hypocreales)

≡ Heliscus lugdunensis Sacc. & Therry 1880

= Heliscus aquaticus Ingold 1942

= Nectria lugdunensis J. Webster 1959

= Neonectria shennongjiana J. Luo & W.Y. Zhuang 2010

Distribution: Temperate and cold regions.

Substrate: On organic debris in aquatic habitats (Nilsson. Symb. Bot. Upsal. 18:97. 1964).

Disease Note: Not pathogenic.

Host: Diverse plant hosts.

Supporting Literature:

Lombard, L., van der Merwe, N.A., Groenewald, J.Z., and Crous, P.W. 2014. Lineages in Nectriaceae: re-evaluating the generic status of Ilyonectria and allied genera. Phytopathol. Medit. 53: 515-532.

Luo, J., and Zhuang, W.-Y. 2010. Three new species of Neonectria (Nectriaceae, Hypocreales) with notes on their phylogenetic positions. Mycologia 102: 142-152.
