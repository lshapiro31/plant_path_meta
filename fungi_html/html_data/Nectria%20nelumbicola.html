= Cylindrocarpon ianthothele var. majus Wollenw. 1928

= Cylindrocarpon ianthothele var. rugulosum C. Booth 1966

= Nectria mammoidea var. rugulosa Weese 1916

= Nectria nelumbicola Henn. 1898

Distribution: Europe, New Zealand and possibly other temperate regions.

Substrate: Decaying plant matter.

Disease Note: Saprobic.

Host: Numerous hosts.
