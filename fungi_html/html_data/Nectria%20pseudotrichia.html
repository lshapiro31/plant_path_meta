Notes: From Stilbum nanum - Stilbum nanum Massee is an illegitimate later homonym of Stilbum nanum (Ehrenb.) Sprengel 1827. From Dendrostilbella bonarii - as 'bonari'. Non Nectria lateritia (P. Karst.) Rossman 1983.

Distribution: Widespread in tropical regions.

Substrate: On woody substrates especially newly killed wood.

Disease Note: Occasionally causing cankers.

Host: Diverse woody hosts.
