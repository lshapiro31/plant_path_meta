Nomenclature data for Nectria punicea

Neonectria punicea (J.C. Schmidt : Fr.) Castl. & Rossman 2006 (Ascomycetes, Hypocreales)

≡ Sphaeria punicea J.C. Schmidt : Fr. 1817

≡Cucurbitaria punicea (J.C. Schmidt. : Fr.) Kuntze 1898

≡Nectria punicea (J.C. Schmidt : Fr.) Rabenh. 1864

= Fusarium album Sacc. 1880  Note: From Cylindrocarpon album - A weak pathogen of various trees and other plants.

    ≡ Cylindrocarpon album (Sacc.) Wollenw. 1916

= Nectria punicea var. ilicis C. Booth 1959

Notes: Saccardo (II:480) cited the name as Nectria punicea (Kunze & J.C. Schmidt:Fr.) Fr. 1849, but Fries only mentioned the name Sphaeria punicea under Nectria, and did not definitively transfer the name to Nectria. The first valid publication of the name was by Rabenhorst in 1864.

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Wood, bark.

Disease Note: Canker.

Host: Reported from several taxa.
