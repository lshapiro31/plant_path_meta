Nomenclature data for Nectria pyrochroa

Calonectria pyrochroa (Desm.) Sacc. 1878 (Ascomycetes, Hypocreales)

≡ Nectria pyrochroa Desm. 1856

= Nectria abnormis Henn. 1897

= Calonectria daldiniana De Not. 1867

= Ophionectria puiggarii Speg. 1889

Notes: Calonectria pyrochroa is the type of the genus Calonectria. Distinct from Calonectria ilicicola (Crous et al. 1993). Lechat et al. (2010) showed the anamorphic Cylindrocladium ilicicola was not linked to this teleomorph name and they recognized the anamorph as the distinct species Calonectria lauri.

Distribution: Cosmopolitan to widespread.

Substrate: Rotting leaves, decaying twigs, woody fruits.

Disease Note: Pathogenic and saprophytic on a variety of hosts and substrates.

Host: Multiple genera in multiple families.
