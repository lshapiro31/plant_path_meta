Nomenclature data for Nectria tasmanica

≡ Sphaeria discophora Mont. 1835

≡Cucurbitaria discophora (Mont.) Kuntze 1898

≡Nectria discophora (Mont.) Mont. 1850

≡Neonectria discophora (Mont.) Mantiri & Samuels 2001

≡Neonectria discophora var. discophora (Mont.) Mantiri & Samuels 2001

= Creonectria discostiolata Chardón 1939  Note: Nom. inval. via no Latin.

= Cylindrocarpon ianthothele var. minus Reinking 1936

= Nectria mammoidea var. minor Reinking 1936

= Nectria tasmanica Berk. 1860

    ≡ Cucurbitaria tasmanica (Berk.) Kuntze 1898

= Nectria umbilicata Henn. 1902

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Bark of twigs and branches of recently dead trees, less frequently on palm trunks.

Disease Note: Pathogen and saprobe.

Host: Multiple plant families.
