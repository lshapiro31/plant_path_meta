Nomenclature data for Nectriella rousseliana

Pseudonectria buxi (DC.) Seifert, Gräfenhan & Schroers 2011 (Ascomycetes, Hypocreales)

≡ Tubercularia buxi DC. 1815

≡Chaetodochium buxi (DC.) Höhn. 1932

≡Chaetostroma buxi (DC.) Corda 1838

≡Volutella buxi (DC.) Berk. 1860

= Nectria rousseliana Mont. 1851

    ≡ Pseudonectria rousseliana (Mont.) Seaver 1931

    ≡ Stigmatea rousseliana (Mont.) Fuckel 1870

  ≡Nectriella rousseliana (Mont.) Sacc. 1877

    ≡ Notarisiella rousseliana (Mont.) Sacc. ex Clem. & Shear 1931

    ≡ Lasionectria rousselliana (Mont.) Cooke 1884

Distribution: Asia (China), Europe, North America.

Substrate: Leaves and twigs.

Disease Note: Canker, dieback.

Host: Buxus (Buxaceae).
