  ≡Nemania maxima (Weber) House 1925

= Hypoxylon ustulatum Bull. 1791

Notes: For additional synonyms, see Index Fungorum.

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Heartwood, roots.

Disease Note: Rots of butt, heartwood, roots; less pathogenic in temperate regions.

Host: Diverse hosts.
