Nomenclature data for Nemania serpens

Nemania serpens (Pers. : Fr.) Gray 1821 (Ascomycetes, Xylariales)

≡ Sphaeria serpens Pers. : Fr. 1823

≡Hypoxylon serpens (Pers. : Fr.) J. Kickx fil. 1835

= Hypoxylon lilacinofuscum Bres. 1892

  Variant spelling Hypoxylon lilacino-fuscum Bres.

= Geniculosporium serpens Chesters & Greenh. 1964

= Hypoxylon subluteum Ellis & Everh. 1892

Notes: From Hypoxylon serpens - H. serpens has been resolved into several species, e.g. H. aeneum, H. atropurpureum, and H. bipapillatum.

Distribution: Temperate and subtropical regions.

Substrate: On hardwoods (Hawksworth. C.M.I. Descr. 358. 1972; Petrini & Rogers. Mycotaxon 26:401-436. 1986).

Disease Note: Sapwood rot.

Host: Various woody plants.

Supporting Literature:

Ju, Y.-M., and Rogers J.D. 2002. The genus Nemania (Xylariaceae). Nova Hedwigia 74: 74-120.
