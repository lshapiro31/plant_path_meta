Nomenclature data for Nematosporangium butleri

Pythium aphanidermatum (Edson) Fitzp. 1923 (Oomycetes, Pythiales)

≡ Rheosporangium aphanidermatum Edson 1915  Note: as 'aphanidermatus'

≡Nematosporangium aphanidermatum (Edson) Fitzp. 1923

= Nematosporangium aphanidermatum var. hawaiiensis Sideris 1931

= Pythium butleri Subraman. 1919

  ≡Nematosporangium butleri (Subraman.) Sideris 1930

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Leaves, stems, roots.

Disease Note: Blights, cottony leak, damping-off, grease spot, rots, wilt, etc.

Host: Multiple genera in multiple families.
