Nomenclature data for Nematosporangium monospermum

Pythium monospermum Pringsh. 1858 (Oomycetes, Pythiales)

≡Nematosporangium monospermum (Pringsh.) J. Schröt. 1897  Note: Not (Pringsh.) Jacz. 1931, isonym.

= Pythium akanense Tokun. 1932

= Pythium complens A. Fisch. 1891

= Pythium fecundum Wahrlich 1887  Note: Possible synonym, fungus poorly known.

= Pythium reptans de Bary 1860

Notes: Pythium gracile Schenk sensu de Bary is also a synonym (van der Plaats-Niterink 1981).

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Insects in water, soil, plant material.

Disease Note: Downy mildew. Root necrosis, not known as a strong pathogen.

Host: Multiple genera in multiple families.

Supporting Literature:
