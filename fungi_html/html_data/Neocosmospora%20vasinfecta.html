Nomenclature data for Neocosmospora vasinfecta

Fusarium neocosmosporiellum O'Donnell & Geiser 2013 (Ascomycetes, Hypocreales)

≡ Neocosmospora vasinfecta E.F. Sm. 1899

= Pseudonectria ornata Bat. & H. Maia 1955

= Neocosmospora vasinfecta var. major P.Rama Rao 1963

= Neocosmospora vasinfecta var. nivea E.F. Sm. 1899

= Neocosmospora vasinfecta var. tracheiphila E.F. Sm. 1899

Notes: Nom. nov - non Fusarium vasinfectum G.F. Atk. (1892). From Neocosmospora ornamentata - Invalid name, no type designated (Art. 37). From Nectriella tracheiphila - Nom. inval. Art. 32, 34 (Rossman et al. 1999).

Distribution: Warm temperate to tropical regions.

Substrate: Soil, nematodes, roots, fruits, leaves, stems, seedlings.

Disease Note: Fruit and root rot, seedling damping-off.

Host: Nematodes, various plant families.
