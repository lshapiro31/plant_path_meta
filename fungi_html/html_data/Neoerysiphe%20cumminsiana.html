Nomenclature data for Neoerysiphe cumminsiana

Neoerysiphe cumminsiana (U. Braun) U. Braun 1999 (Ascomycetes, Erysiphales)

≡ Erysiphe cumminsiana U. Braun 1983

≡Golovinomyces cumminsianus (U. Braun) Heluta 1988

Distribution: Central America, North America, South America.

Substrate: Leaves, stems.

Disease Note: Powdery mildew.

Host: Multiple genera of Asteraceae.

Supporting Literature:

Baiswar, P., Chandra, S., and Ngachan, S.V. 2008. First record of Neoerysiphe cumminsiana on horseweed in India. Australasian Plant Disease Notes 3: 156-157.

Braun, U. 1999. Some critical notes on the classification and the generic concept of the Erysiphaceae. Schlechtendalia 3: 48-54.

Braun, U., and Cook, R.T.A. 2012. Taxonomy Manual of the Erysiphales (Powdery Mildews). CBS Biodivers. Ser. 11: 703.
