Nomenclature data for Neoerysiphe galii

Neoerysiphe galii (S. Blumer) U. Braun 1999 (Ascomycetes, Erysiphales)

≡ Erysiphe galii S. Blumer 1933

≡Golovinomyces galii (S. Blumer) Heluta 1988

≡Erysiphe galii var. galii S. Blumer 1983

Distribution: Asia, Europe.

Substrate: Living leaves, fruits, stems.

Disease Note: Powdery mildew.

Host: Multiple genera of Rubiaceae.

Supporting Literature:

Braun, U. 1999. Some critical notes on the classification and the generic concept of the Erysiphaceae. Schlechtendalia 3: 48-54.

Braun, U., and Cook, R.T.A. 2012. Taxonomy Manual of the Erysiphales (Powdery Mildews). CBS Biodivers. Ser. 11: 703.
