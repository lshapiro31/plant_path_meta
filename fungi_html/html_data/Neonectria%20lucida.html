Nomenclature data for Neonectria lucida

Thelonectria lucida (Höhn.) P. Chaverri & C. Salgado 2011 (, )

≡ Nectria lucida Höhn. 1909

≡Neonectria lucida (Höhn.) Samuels & Brayford 2004

= Cylindrocarpon lucidum Booth 1966

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Bark of recently killed or dying trees, rarely on vines.

Disease Note: Trunk and root rot.

Host: Multiple plant families.
