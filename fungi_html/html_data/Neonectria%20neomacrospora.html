Nomenclature data for Neonectria neomacrospora

Neonectria neomacrospora (C. Booth & Samuels) Mantiri & Samuels 2001 (Ascomycetes, Hypocreales)

≡ Nectria neomacrospora C. Booth & Samuels 1981

≡Nectria cucurbitula var. macrospora Wollenw. 1931

≡Nectria fuckeliana var. macrospora (Wollenw.) C. Booth 1966

[≡Nectria macrospora (Wollenw.) Ouell. 1975 - illegitimate later homonym, not included in search]  Note: Not Starb. 1899 nor Henn. & E. Nyman 1899 nor Becc. nor (Chardon) G. Stev. 1975.

Distribution: North America (Canada, USA), Europe.

Substrate: Wood, bark.

Disease Note: Canker.

Host: Abies balsamea, Tsuga heterophylla (Pinaceae).
