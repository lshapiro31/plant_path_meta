Nomenclature data for Neonectria rugulosa

Rugonectria rugulosa (Pat. & Gaillard) P. Chaverri & C. Salgado 2011 (, )

≡ Nectria rugulosa Pat. & Gaillard 1889 [1888]

≡Cucurbitaria rugulosa (Pat. & Gaillard) Kuntze 1898

≡Neonectria rugulosa (Pat. & Gaillard) Mantiri & Samuels 2001

= Nectria cainitonis Henn. 1908  Note: See Samuels & Brayford 1994 for synonymy.

= Nectria congensis Syd. 1909

= Cylindrocarpon rugulosum Brayford & Samuels 1994

= Nectria sakanensis Henn. 1908

Distribution: Widespread in warmer regions.

Substrate: Bark.

Disease Note: Occasional cankers.

Host: Multiple plant families.
