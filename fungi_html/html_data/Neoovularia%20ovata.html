Neoovularia ovata (Fuckel) U. Braun 1992 (Ascomycetes, Incertae sedis)

≡ Ramularia ovata Fuckel 1866

≡Ovularia ovata (Fuckel) Sacc. 1881

= Ovularia iranica Petr. 1953

Distribution: Asia, Europe.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Salvia spp. (Lamiaceae).
