Nomenclature data for Neoramularia asteris

Neoovularia asteris (Solheim) U. Braun 1998 (Ascomycetes, Incertae sedis)

≡ Ovularia asteris Solheim 1940

≡Neoramularia asteris (Solheim) U. Braun 1991

≡Ramularia solheimii U. Braun 1988

Notes: Ramularia solheimii is a nom. nov. created to prevent conflict with Ramularia asteris (W. Phillips & Plowr.) Bubák 1908.

Distribution: Western United States.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Eucephalus engelmannii, E. breweri, Xylorhiza glabriuscula (Asteraceae).

Supporting Literature:

Braun, U. 1998. A Monograph of Cercosporella, Ramularia and Allied Genera (Phytopathogenic Hyphomycetes). IHW-Verlag 2: 337.
