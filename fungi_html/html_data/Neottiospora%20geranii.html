Nomenclature data for Neottiospora geranii

Pestalozziella subsessilis Sacc. & Ellis 1882 (Ascomycetes, Incertae sedis)

= Dilophospora geranii J. Schröt. 1887

  ≡Neottiospora geranii (J. Schröt.) H.C. Greene 1959

= Pestalozziella geranii-pusilli C. Massal. 1889

Distribution: Asia, Eastern North America, Europe, New Zealand.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot, leaf loss.

Host: Geranium spp. (Geraniaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Andrianova, T.V., and Minter, D.W. 2012. Pestalozziella subsessilis. I.M.I. Descr. Fungi Bact. 1938: 1-4.

Nag Raj, T.R., and Kendrick, B. 1972. Genera Coelomycetarum. III. Pestalozziella. Canad. J. Bot. 50: 607-617.
