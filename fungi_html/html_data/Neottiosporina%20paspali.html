Nomenclature data for Neottiosporina paspali

Neottiosporina paspali (Atk.) B. Sutton & Alcorn 1974 (Ascomycetes, Incertae sedis)

≡ Stagonospora paspali G.F. Atk. 1897

Notes: Sutton & Alcorn. (Austral. J. Bot. 22:519. 1974) discussed the problems of misidentified specimens.

Distribution: Asia (Hong Kong, Japan), North America (USA), South America (Argentina).

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Paspalum spp.; Phragmites australis and other Poaceae, Schoenoplectus sp. (Cyperaeae).
