Nomenclature data for Neotyphodium coenophialum

Neotyphodium coenophialum (Morgan-Jones & W. Gams) Glenn, C.W. Bacon & Hanlin 1996 (Ascomycetes, Hypocreales)

≡ Acremonium coenophialum Morgan-Jones & W. Gams 1982

Distribution: North America, Europe, New Zealand.

Substrate: Endophyte.

Disease Note: Produces toxins.

Host: Festuca arundinacea (Poaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Glenn, A.E., Bacon, C.W., Price, R., and Hanlin, R.T. 1996. Molecular phylogeny of Acremonium and its taxonomic implications. Mycologia 88: 369-383.
