Nomenclature data for Neovossia barclayana

Tilletia barclayana (Bref.) Sacc. & Syd. 1899 (Ustilaginomycetes, Tilletiales)

≡ Neovossia barclayana Bref. 1895

= Tilletia ajrekarii Mundk. 1939

= Neovossia macrospora Petr. 1947

= Tilletia pennisetina Syd. 1929

Notes: Sensu lato. Historically confused with Tilletia horrida (Castlebury et al. 2005). Reports of this species on Oryza are probably erroneous.

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Flowering heads.

Disease Note: Smut.

Host: Poaceae, especially Pennisetum; reports on Oryza are probably erroneous.
