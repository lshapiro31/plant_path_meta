Nomenclature data for Neovossia horrida

Tilletia horrida Takah. 1896 (Ustilaginomycetes, Tilletiales)

≡Neovossia horrida (Takah.) Padwick & A. Khan 1944

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Ovaries of inflorescence.

Disease Note: Smut on rice.

Host: Oryza spp. (Poaceae).
