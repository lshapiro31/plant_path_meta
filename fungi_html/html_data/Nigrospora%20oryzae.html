Nomenclature data for Nigrospora oryzae

Nigrospora oryzae (Berk. & Broome) Petch 1924 (Ascomycetes, Trichosphaeriales)

≡ Monotospora oryzae Berk. & Broome 1873

= Basisporium gallarum Molliard 1902

= Khuskia oryzae H.J. Huds. 1963

    ≡ Apiospora oryzae (H.J. Huds.) Arx 1974

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Plant parts, air, soil.

Disease Note: Saprophyte and weak parasite; leaf spot of Rosemary, cob and stalk rot of maize.

Host: Multiple plant families.
