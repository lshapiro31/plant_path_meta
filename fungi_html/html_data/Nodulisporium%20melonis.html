Nomenclature data for Nodulisporium melonis

Plectosphaerella melonis (Ts. Watan. & Mas. Sato) A.J.L. Phillips, A. Carlucci & M.L. Raimondo 2012 (Ascomycetes, Phyllachorales)

≡ Nodulisporium melonis Ts. Watan. & Mas. Sato 1995

= Acremonium cucurbitacearum Alfaro-Garcia, W. Gams & J. Garcia-Jim. 1996

Distribution: Warm temperate regions of Europe and the USA.

Substrate: Roots.

Disease Note: Melon rot.

Host: Primarily on Citrullus and Cucumis (Cucurbitaceae).

Supporting Literature:
