Nomenclature data for Nyssopsora thwaitesii

Nyssopsora thwaitesii (Berk. & Broome) Syd. 1921 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

≡ Triphragmium thwaitesii Berk. & Broome 1873

= Nyssopsora schefflerae Ramachar, Bagyan., Subbal. & Hosag. 1987

Distribution: South Asia.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Rust.

Host: Araliaceae.

Supporting Literature:
