Nomenclature data for Odontia spathulata

Hyphodontia spathulata (Schrad. : Fr.) Parmasto 1968 (Basidiomycetes, Hymenochaetales)

≡ Hydnum spathulatum Schrad. : Fr. 1794

≡Grandinia spathulata (Schrad. : Fr.) Jülich 1982

≡Odontia spathulata (Schrad. : Fr.) Litsch. 1922

= Hydnum pithyophilum Berk. & M.A. Curtis 1849

Distribution: Northern hemisphere.

Substrate: On bark, dead branches, or decayed wood; occasionally causing a localized rot of living trees.

Disease Note: Brown rot.

Host: Hardwoods.
