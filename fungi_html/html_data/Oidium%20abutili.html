Nomenclature data for Oidium abutili

Pseudoidium abutili (Hosag.) U. Braun & R.T.A. Cook 2012 (Ascomycetes, Erysiphales)

≡ Oidium abutili Hosag. 1991

Distribution: Asia (India).

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Powdery mildew.

Host: Abutilon spp. (Malvaceae).
