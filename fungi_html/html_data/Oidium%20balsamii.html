Nomenclature data for Oidium balsamii

Golovinomyces verbasci (Jacz.) Heluta 1988 (Ascomycetes, Erysiphales)

≡ Erysiphe cichoracearum f. verbasci Jacz. 1927

≡Erysiphe verbasci (Jacz.) S. Blumer 1933

= Oidium balsamii Mont. 1854

Notes: Erysiphe verbasci conserved against the older Oidium balsamii.

Distribution: North America (introduced); New Zealand (introduced); temperate northern hemisphere.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Powdery mildew.

Host: Verbascum spp. (Scrophulariaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Braun, U. 2013. (2210-2232) Proposals to conserve the teleomorph-typified name Blumeria against the anamorph-typified name Oidium and twenty-two teleomorph-typified powdery mildew species names against competing anamorph-typified names (Ascomycota: Erysiphaceae). Taxon 62: 1328-1331.
