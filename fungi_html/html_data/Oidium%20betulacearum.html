Nomenclature data for Oidium betulacearum

Erysiphe miyabei (E.S. Salmon) U. Braun & S. Takam. 2000 (Ascomycetes, Erysiphales)

≡ Uncinula salicis var. miyabei E.S. Salmon 1900

≡Uncinula miyabei (E.S. Salmon) Sacc. & P. Syd. 1902

= Oidium betulacearum Hosag. & Monahan 1990

Distribution: Asia.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Powdery mildew.

Host: Alnus spp. (Betulaceae); reports from other families questionable.
