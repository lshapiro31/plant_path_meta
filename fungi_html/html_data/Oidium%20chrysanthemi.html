Nomenclature data for Oidium chrysanthemi

Golovinomyces chrysanthemi (Rabenh.) M. Bradshaw, U. Braun, J. Meeboon & S. Takam. 2017 (Ascomycetes, Erysiphales)

≡ Oidium chrysanthemi Rabenh. 1853

≡Acrosporium chrysanthemi (Rabenh.) Subram. 1971

≡Acrosporium chrysanthemi (Rabenh.) Põldmaa 1967

≡Euoidium chrysanthemi (Rabenh.) U. Braun & R.T.A. Cook 2012

Notes: Acrosporium chrysanthemi (Rabenh.) Subram. (1971) is a superfluous combination.

Distribution: Nearly circumglobal.

Substrate: Living leaves, stalks, inflorescences.

Disease Note: Powdery mildew.

Host: Chrysanthemum x morifolium (Asteraceae).

Supporting Literature:

Bradshaw, M., Braun, U., Gotz, M., Meeboon, J., and Takamatsu, S. 2017. Powdery mildew of Chrysanthemum x morifolium: phylogeny and taxonomy in the context of Golovinomyces species on Asteraceae hosts. Mycologia 109(3): 508-519.
