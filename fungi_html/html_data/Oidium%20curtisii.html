Nomenclature data for Oidium curtisii

Haplotrichum curtisii (Berk.) Hol.-Jech. 1976 (Basidiomycetes, Thelephorales)

≡ Aspergillus curtisii Berk. 1855  Note: Date of publication for A. curtisii is wrong. It should be 1855 per Partridge et al. (2001).

≡Acladium curtisii (Berk.) M.B. Ellis 1976

≡Oidium curtisii (Berk.) Linder 1942

= Corticium vagum Berk. & M.A. Curtis 1873

    ≡ Botryobasidium vagum (Berk. & M.A. Curtis) D.P. Rogers 1935

    ≡ Pellicularia vaga (Berk. & M.A. Curtis) D.P. Rogers ex Linder 1942

Notes: Oldest epithet not in the correct genus i.e. Haplotrichum.

Distribution: Widespread in temperate regions.

Substrate: Rotten wood.

Disease Note: Saprobic.

Host: Various plant families.

Supporting Literature:

Partridge, E.C., Baker, W.A., and Morgan-Jones, G. 2001. Notes on Hyphomycetes. LXXXII. A further contribution toward a monograph of the genus Haplotrichum. Mycotaxon 78: 127-160.
