Nomenclature data for Oidium cylindricum

Erysiphe betae (Vanha) Weltzien 1963 (Ascomycetes, Erysiphales)

≡ Microsphaera betae Vanha 1903

≡Erysiphe communis f. betae (Vanha) Jacz. 1927

= Oidium cylindricum Sawada 1959  Note: Braun & Cooke (2012) suggest that this is an invalid name.

Notes: in Clade 1 (Oidium subgenus Pseudoidium mitosporic state) based on ITS ref. Saenz 1999 From Oidium cylindricum - nom. nud. (no latin diagnosis)

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Powdery mildew.

Host: Beta spp. (Amaranthaceae).
