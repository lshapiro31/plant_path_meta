Nomenclature data for Oidium dianthi

Erysiphe buhrii U. Braun 1978 (Ascomycetes, Erysiphales)

≡Erysiphe pisi var. buhrii (U. Braun) Ialongo 1992

= Oidium dianthi Jacz. 1927

Notes: Erysiphe buhrii conserved against Oidium dianthi (2017).

Distribution: Widespread, especially Asia, Europe.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Powdery mildew.

Host: Caryophyllaceae.
