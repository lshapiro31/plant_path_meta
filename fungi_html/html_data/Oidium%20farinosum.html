= Sphaerotheca castagnei f. mali Sorauer 1889

= Oidium farinosum Cooke 1887

Notes: Sphaerotheca leucotricha conserved against Oidium farinosum.

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Powdery mildew.
