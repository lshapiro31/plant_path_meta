Nomenclature data for Oidium heveae

Oidium heveae B.A. Steinm. 1925 (Ascomycetes, Erysiphales)

≡Acrosporium heveae (B.A. Steinm.) Subram. 1971

Notes: A species insufficiently known morphologically and of uncertain generic affinity per Braun (2012). ------ According to Braun & Cooke (2012), this name has been confused with a "Pseudoidium often found on Hevea spp.

Distribution: Africa, Asia.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Powdery mildew.

Host: Hevea spp. (Euphorbiaceae).

Supporting Literature:
