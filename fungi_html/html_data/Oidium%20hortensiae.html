Nomenclature data for Oidium hortensiae

Pseudoidium hortensiae (Jørst.) U. Braun & R.T.A. Cook 2012 (Ascomycetes, Erysiphales)

≡ Oidium hortensiae Jørst. 1925

Distribution: Nearly circumglobal.

Substrate: Living leaves, stems, flowers.

Disease Note: Powdery mildew.

Host: Hydrangea spp. (Hydrangeaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Braun, U. 1987. A monograph of the Erysiphales (powdery mildews). Beih. Nova Hedwigia 89: 1-700.

Braun, U., and Cook, R.T.A. 2012. Taxonomy Manual of the Erysiphales (Powdery Mildews). CBS Biodivers. Ser. 11: 703.
