Nomenclature data for Oidium latisporum

Golovinomyces ambrosiae (Schwein.) U. Braun & R.T.A. Cook 2009 (Ascomycetes, Erysiphales)

≡ Erysiphe ambrosiae Schwein. 1832

= Erysiphe cichoracearum var. latispora U. Braun 1983

    ≡ Golovinomyces cichoracearum var. latisporus (U. Braun) U. Braun 1999

= Oidium latisporum U. Braun 1982

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Powdery mildew.

Host: Helianthus annuus, Rudbeckia spp. and several other Asteraceae.
