Podosphaera pannosa (Wallr. : Fr.) de Bary 1870 (Ascomycetes, Erysiphales)

≡ Alphitomorpha pannosa Wallr. : Fr. 1819

≡Erysiphe pannosa (Wallr. : Fr.) Schltdl. 1824  Note: Not (Wallr. : Fr.) Link 1824.

≡Sphaerotheca pannosa (Wallr.: Fr.) Lév. 1851

≡Sphaerotheca pannosa var. pannosa (Wallr. : Fr.) Lév. 1914

= Oidium leucoconium Desm. 1829

    ≡ Acrosporium leucoconium (Desm.) Sumst. 1913

= Sphaerotheca pannosa var. persicae Woron. 1914

    ≡ Sphaerotheca persicae (Woron.) Erikss. 1928

= Sphaerotheca pannosa var. rosae Woron. 1914

= Eurotium rosarum Grev. 1823

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Living flowers, fruits, leaves, and stems.

Disease Note: Powdery mildew.

Host: Multiple genera in multiple families especially on Rosa spp. and Prunus spp. (Rosaceae).
