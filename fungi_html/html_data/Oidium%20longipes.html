Nomenclature data for Oidium longipes

Euoidium longipes (Noordel. & Loer.) U. Braun & R.T.A. Cook 2012 (Ascomycetes, Erysiphales)

≡ Oidium longipes Noordel. & Loer. 1989

Distribution: Europe, North America (USA: NJ), New Zealand.

Substrate: Living leaves, petioles.

Disease Note: Powdery mildew; curling, yellowing, defoliation.

Host: Solanaceae.
