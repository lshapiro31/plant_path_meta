Nomenclature data for Oidium matthiolae

Erysiphe cruciferarum Opiz ex L. Junell 1967 (Ascomycetes, Erysiphales)

≡Erysiphe pisi var. cruciferarum (Opiz ex L. Junell) Ialongo 1992

= Oidium brassicae N. Ahmad 2006

  Variant spelling Erysiphe cruciferarum var. cleomae Y.S. Paul & V. Thakur 2006  Note: Nom. inval. via ICBN Art. 37.6.

= Oidium matthiolae Rayss 1940

= Oidium papaveracearum Bappamm., Hosag. & Udaiyan 1995

= Erysiphe radulescui Docea 1968

Notes: Braun (1987) provided a list of additional synonyms with ranks below species. The names Erysiphe polygoni DC. and E. communis (Wallr. : Fr.) Schltdl. have been misapplied to this species.

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Leaves, stems, pods.

Disease Note: Powdery mildew.

Host: Numerous hosts in numerous families.
