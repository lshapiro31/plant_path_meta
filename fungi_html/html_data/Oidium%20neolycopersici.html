Nomenclature data for Oidium neolycopersici

Pseudoidium neolycopersici (L. Kiss) L. Kiss 2012 (Ascomycetes, Erysiphales)

≡ Oidium neolycopersici L. Kiss 2001

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Leaves, stems.

Disease Note: Powdery mildew, chlorosis.

Host: Reported mostly from few taxa of Solanaceae, especially tomatoes.
