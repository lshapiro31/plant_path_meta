Nomenclature data for Oidium papayae

Erysiphe diffusa (Cooke & Peck) U. Braun & S. Takam. 2000 (Ascomycetes, Erysiphales)

≡ Microsphaera diffusa Cooke & Peck 1873

≡Trichocladia diffusa (Cooke & Peck) Jacz. 1927

= Oidium caricae F. Noack 1898

    ≡ Acrosporium caricae (F. Noack) Subram. 1971

= Oidium papayae M. Seq. 1992

Notes: Previously referred to as Erysiphe polygoni (Paxon & Rogers. Mycologia 66:894. 1974).

Distribution: Widespread in subtropical and tropical regions.

Substrate: Leaves, stems.

Disease Note: Powdery mildew.

Host: Fabaceae, Carica papaya (Caricaceae).
