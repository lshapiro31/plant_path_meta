Nomenclature data for Oncobasidium theobromae

Rhizoctonia theobromae (P.H.B. Talbot & Keane) Oberw., R. Bauer, Garnica, R. Kirschner 2013 (Basidiomycetes, Polyporales)

≡ Oncobasidium theobromae P.H.B. Talbot & Keane 1971

≡Ceratobasidium theobromae (P.H.B. Tsalbot & Keanee) Samuels & Keane 2011

≡Thanatephorus theobromae (P.H.B. Talbot & Keane) P. Roberts 1999

Distribution: Asia, Australasia (Papua New Guinea).

Substrate: Vascular tissue (xylem) of branches and stem.

Disease Note: Vascular wilt, vascular streak dieback.

Host: Primary host: Cocoa, Theobroma cacao (Malvaceae); also reported on Avocado, Persea americana (Lauraceae). Indigenous host unknown.
