Nomenclature data for Onnia circinata

Onnia circinata (Fr.) P. Karst. 1889 (Basidiomycetes, Hymenochaetales)

≡ Trametes circinatus Fr. 1849

≡Inonotus circinatus (Fr.) Gilb. 1964

≡Polyporus circinatus (Fr.) Fr. 1857

Notes:

Distribution: Temperate northern hemisphere.

Substrate: On living trunks or roots of conifers; also on dead wood.

Disease Note: Butt rot.

Host: Pinaceae.

Supporting Literature:
