Nomenclature data for Onnia tomentosa

Onnia tomentosa (Fr. : Fr.) P. Karst. 1889 (Basidiomycetes, Hymenochaetales)

≡ Polyporus tomentosus Fr. : Fr. 1821

≡Coltricia tomentosa (Fr. : Fr.) Murrill 1904

≡Inonotus tomentosus (Fr. : Fr.) S. Teng 1964

Notes: "Survival of Inonotustomentosus in stumps and subsequent infection of young stands in north central British Columbia" Katherine J. Lewis, Everett M. Hansen Canadian Journal of Forest Research, 1991, 21(7): 1049-1057.

Distribution: Temperate northern hemisphere.

Substrate: On dead wood of conifers or on living trunks or roots.

Disease Note: Tomentosus root disease.

Host: Pinaceae.
