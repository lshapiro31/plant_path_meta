Nomenclature data for Ophiobolus graminis

Gaeumannomyces graminis (Sacc.) Arx & D. Olivier 1952 (Ascomycetes, Incertae sedis)

≡ Rhaphidophora graminis Sacc. 1875

≡Ophiobolus graminis (Sacc.) Sacc. 1881

≡Gaeumannomyces graminis var. graminis (Sacc.) Arx & D.L. Olivier 1952

= Sphaeria cariceti Berk. & Broome 1861

    ≡ Ophiobolus cariceti (Berk. & Broome) Sacc. 1883

    ≡ Linocarpon cariceti (Berk. & Broome) Petr. 1952

    ≡ Gaeumannomyces cariceti (Berk. & Broome) Lar.N. Vassiljeva 1998

Notes: With the elevation of G. graminis var. avenae and G. graminis var. tritici to species status by Hernandez-Restrepo et. al. (2016), no infraspecific varieties are still recognized.

Distribution: Cosmopolitan. From Sphaeria cariceti - From Ophiobolus cariceti - Southern United States. From Gaeumannomyces graminis var. graminis - Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: From Gaeumannomyces graminis var. graminis - Roots, culms, leaf sheaths.

Disease Note: From Gaeumannomyces graminis var. graminis - Crown and sheath rot. Take all of rice. Also Arkansas foot root.

Host: From Gaeumannomyces graminis var. graminis - Oryza sativa (rice) and other Poaceae.
