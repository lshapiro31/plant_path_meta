Nomenclature data for Ophiognomonia padicola

Ophiognomonia padicola (Lib.) M. Monod 1983 (Ascomycetes, Diaporthales)

≡ Sphaeria padicola Lib. 1832

≡Gnomonia padicola (Lib.) Kleb. 1908

= Ophiognomonia padi Jaap 1905

Distribution: Europe.

Substrate: Overwintered leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf blotch.

Host: Prunus padus (Rosaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Sogonov, M.V., Castlebury, L.A., Rossman, A.Y., Mejia, L.C. , and White, J.F. 2008. Leaf-inhabiting genera of the Gnomoniaceae, Diaporthales. Stud. Mycol. 62: 1-79.
