Nomenclature data for Ophiosphaerella agrostidis

Ophiosphaerella agrostidis Dern., M.P.S. Câmara, N.R. O'Neill, Berkum & M.E. Palm 2000 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

    Variant spelling Ophiosphaerella agrostis Dern., M.P.S. Câmara, N.R. O'Neill, Berkum & M.E. Palm 2000

Distribution: North America.

Disease Note: Necrosis of leaves, stolons, roots. Turfgrass spots.

Host: Agrostis spp., Cynodon ×dactylon-transvaalensis (Poaceae).

Supporting Literature:
