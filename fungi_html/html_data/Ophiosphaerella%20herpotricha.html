Nomenclature data for Ophiosphaerella herpotricha

Ophiosphaerella herpotricha (Fr.) J.C. Walker 1980 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

≡ Sphaeria herpotricha Fr. 1823

≡Ophiochaeta herpotricha (Fr.) Sacc. 1895

≡Phaeosphaeria herpotricha (Fr.) L. Holm 1957

≡Rhaphidophora herpotricha (Fr.) Cooke 1889

≡Ophiobolus herpotrichus (Fr.) Sacc. 1881

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Leaf sheaths, roots, rhizomes.

Disease Note: Spring dead spot disease.

Host: Poaceae, especially Cynodon dactylon (Bermudagrass).

Supporting Literature:

Perry, D. H., Tomaso-Peterson, M., and Baird, R. 2008. First Report of Ophiosphaerella herpotricha Causing Spring Dead Spot of Bermudagrass in Mississippi. Pl. Dis. 92: 482.
