Nomenclature data for Ophiosphaerella korrae

Ophiosphaerella korrae (J.C. Walker & A.M. Sm. bis) Shoemaker & C.E. Babc. 1989 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

≡ Leptosphaeria korrae J. Walker & A.M. Sm. bis 1972

Distribution: Australia, North America.

Substrate: Roots, rhizomes.

Disease Note: Spring dead spot.

Host: Poaceae, especially Cynodon dactylon (Bermudagrass) and Poa praetensis.
