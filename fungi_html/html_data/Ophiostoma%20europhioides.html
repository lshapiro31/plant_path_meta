Nomenclature data for Ophiostoma europhioides

Grosmannia europhioides (E.F. Wright & Cain) Zipfel, Z.W. de Beer & M.J. Wingf. 2006 (Ascomycetes, Ophiostomatales)

≡ Ceratocystis europhioides E.F. Wright & Cain 1961

≡Ophiostoma europhioides (E.F. Wright & Cain) H. Solheim 1986

Notes: Anamorph leptographium-like.

Distribution: Asia (Japan), North America.

Disease Note: Sapstain.

Host: Pinaceae.
