Nomenclature data for Ophiostoma huntii

Grosmannia huntii (Rob.-Jeffr.) Zipfel, Z.W. de Beer & M.J. Wingf. 2006 (Ascomycetes, Ophiostomatales)

≡ Ceratocystis huntii Rob.-Jeffr. 1964

≡Leptographium huntii (Rob.-Jeffr.) M.J. Wingf. 1985  Note: Valid only as a new combination, not available for use of the anamorph exclusively (ICBN Art. 59).

≡Ophiostoma huntii (Rob.-Jeffr.) De Hoog & R.J. Scheffer 1984

Notes: Anamorph leptographium-llike.

Distribution: Northwestern North America, New Zealand, South America (Argentina).

Substrate: Wood.

Disease Note: Sapstain.

Host: Pinus spp. (Pinaceae).
