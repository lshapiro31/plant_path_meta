Nomenclature data for Ophiostoma novo-ulmi

Ophiostoma novo-ulmi Brasier 1991 (Ascomycetes, Ophiostomatales)

≡Ophiostoma novo-ulmi subsp. novo-ulmi Brasier 2001  Note: The included type subspecies corresponds to the EAN race.

Notes: This fungus has sporothrix and pesotum-like anamorphs.

Distribution: Europe, North America, New Zealand.

Substrate: Xylem.

Disease Note: Dutch elm disease.

Host: Ulmus spp. (Ulmaceae).
