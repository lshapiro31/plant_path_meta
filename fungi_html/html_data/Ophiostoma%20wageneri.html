Nomenclature data for Ophiostoma wageneri

Grosmannia wageneri (Goheen & F.W. Cobb) Zipfel, Z.W. de Beer & M.J. Wingf. 2006 (Ascomycetes, Ophiostomatales)

≡ Ceratocystis wageneri Goheen & F.W. Cobb 1978

≡Ophiostoma wageneri (Goheen & F.W. Cobb) T.C. Harr. 1987

= Verticicladiella wageneri var. ponderosa T.C. Harr. & F.W. Cobb 1986

    ≡ Leptographium wageneri var. ponderosum (T.C. Harr. & F.W. Cobb) T.C. Harr. & F.W. Cobb 1987

Notes: Anamorph leptographium-like.

Distribution: North America (USA: Western states).

Substrate: Roots.

Disease Note: Black stain root.

Host: Multiple genera of Pinaceae.

Supporting Literature:
