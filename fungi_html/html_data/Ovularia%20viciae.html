Nomenclature data for Ovularia viciae

Ramularia sphaeroidea Sacc. 1878 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡Ovularia sphaeroidea (Sacc.) Sacc. 1880

= Ovularia babajaniae Osipjan 1971

= Ovularia chesneyae N.P. Golovina 1960

= Peronospora exigua W.G. Smith 1884

    ≡ Ovularia exigua (W.G. Smith) Sacc. 1895

= Ovularia lotophaga Ellis & Everh. 1895

= Ovularia schwarziana Magnus 1901

    ≡ Ramularia schwarziana (Magnus) Gunnerb. 1967

= Pseudovularia trifolii Speg. 1910

    ≡ Ramularia argentinensis Deighton 1972

= Ramularia viciae A.B. Frank 1880

  ≡Ovularia viciae (A.B. Frank) Sacc. 1892

Notes: Ramularia argentinensis is a nom. nov. created to prevent conflict with R. trifolii Jaap (1910).

Distribution: Australia, Central Asia, Europe, North America, South America, New Zealand.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Chesneya ternata, Glycyrrhiza glabra, Lotus spp., Trifolium spp., Vicia spp. (Fabaceae).
