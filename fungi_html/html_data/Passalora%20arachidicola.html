Nomenclature data for Passalora arachidicola

Passalora arachidicola (Hori) U. Braun 1999 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Cercospora arachidicola Hori 1917

= Cercospora arachidis var. macrospora Maffei 1922

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Disease Note: Leaf spot of peanuts.

Host: Arachis spp. (Fabaceae).
