Nomenclature data for Passalora astragali

Passalora astragali (Ellis & Holw.) U. Braun 1993 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Ramularia astragali Ellis & Holw. 1885

≡Didymaria astragali (Ellis & Holw.) Sacc. 1886  Note: Isonym of (Ellis & Holw.) Davis 1915.

[= Ramularia astragali N.P. Golovina 1964 - illegitimate later homonym, not included in search]  Note: Not (Ellis & Holw.) Sacc. 1886. Nomen illeg.

Distribution: Asia (Uzbekistan), North America (USA).

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Astragalus spp. (Fabaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Braun, U. 1998. A Monograph of Cercosporella, Ramularia and Allied Genera (Phytopathogenic Hyphomycetes). IHW-Verlag 2: 337.

Crous, P.W., and Braun, U. 2003. Mycosphaerella and its anamorphs: 1. Names published in Cercospora and Passalora. Centraalbureau voor Schimmelcultures, Utrecht, 571 pages.
