Nomenclature data for Passalora bataticola

Passalora bataticola (Cif. & Bruner) U. Braun & Crous 2003 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Cercospora bataticola Cif. & Bruner 1931

≡Phaeoisariopsis bataticola (Cif. & Bruner) M.B. Ellis 1976

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Ipomoea batatas (Convolvulaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Chupp, C. 1954. Monograph of the fungus genus Cercospora. Published by the Author, Ithaca, New York, 667 pages.
