Nomenclature data for Passalora capsicicola

Phaeoramularia capsicicola (Vassiljevsky) Deighton 1976 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Cercospora capsicicola Vassiljevsky 1937

≡Passalora capsicicola (Vassiljevsky) U. Braun & F.O. Freire 2002

= Cladosporium capsici Kovatsch. 1938

  [≡ Cercospora capsici É.J. Marchal & Steyaert 1929 - illegitimate later homonym, not included in search]  Note: Not Heald & F.A. Wolf 1911. Nomen illeg.

= Cercospora unamunoi Castell. 1948

     Phaeoramularia unamunoi (Castell.) Munt.-Cvetk. 1960  Note: Invalid name. No basionym reference.

  [≡ Cercospora capsici Unamuno 1932 - illegitimate later homonym, not included in search]  Note: Not Heald & F.A. Wolf 1911. Nomen illeg.

Distribution: Widespread, especially subtropical and tropical regions.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Capsicum spp. (Solanaceae).
