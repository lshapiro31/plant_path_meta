Nomenclature data for Passalora clavata

Passalora clavata (W.R. Gerard) U. Braun 1997 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Helminthosporium clavatum W.R. Gerard 1874

≡Cercospora clavata (W.R. Gerard) Cooke 1875

≡Virgasporium clavatum (W.R. Gerard) Cooke 1875

= Cercospora incarnata Ellis & Everh. 1897

Notes: Includes type variety.

Distribution: Widespread, especially North America.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Asclepias spp. (Asclepiadaceae).

Supporting Literature:
