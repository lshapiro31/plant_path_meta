Nomenclature data for Passalora galii

Passalora galii (Ellis & Holw.) Arx 1983 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

= Cercospora concinna Syd. 1932

= Fusicladium ruthenicum Petr. 1921

    ≡ Scolicotrichum ruthenicum (Petr.) Karak. & Vassiljevsky 1937

    ≡ Passalora ruthenica (Petr.) Petr. 1941

Notes: Passalora galii (Ellis & Holw.) Poonam Srivast 1994 comb. inval.

Distribution: Asia (Russia), Europe, North America.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Galium spp. (Rubiaceae).
