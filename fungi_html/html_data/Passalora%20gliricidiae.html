Nomenclature data for Passalora gliricidiae

Passalora gliricidiae (Syd. & P. Syd.) U. Braun & Crous 2002 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Cercospora gliricidiae Syd. & P. Syd. 1913

≡Mycovellosiella gliricidiae (Syd. & P. Syd.) U. Braun 1999

≡Sirosporium gliricidiae (Syd. & P. Syd.) Deighton 1976

= Cercospora atropurpurascens Chupp 1934

  Variant spelling Cercospora atro-purpurascens Chupp 1934

Notes: (Syd. & P. Syd.) Poonam Srivast. 1994 is invalid.

Distribution: Widespread, especially warmer regions.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Gliricidia spp. (Fabaceae).
