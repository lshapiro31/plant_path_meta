Nomenclature data for Passalora gnaphaliaceae

Ragnhildiana gnaphaliacea (Cooke) Videira, H.D. Shin, C. Nakash. & Crous 2017 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

    Variant spelling Passalora gnaphaliaceae (Cooke) U. Braun & Crous 2003

≡ Cercospora gnaphaliacea Cooke 1880

≡Passalora gnaphaliacea (Cooke) U. Braun & Crous 2003

≡Phaeoisariopsis gnaphaliacea (Cooke) Morgan-Jones 1975 [1974]

= Cercospora gnaphalii Harkn. 1884

Notes: From Passalora gnaphaliacea - This epithet appears with the orphographical variant 'gnaphaliaceae' for unknown reasons.

Distribution: Widespread, especially North and South America.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Gnaphalium spp. (Asteraceae).
