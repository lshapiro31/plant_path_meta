Nomenclature data for Passalora janseana

Cercospora janseana (Racib.) Constant. 1982 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Napicladium janseanum Racib. 1900

≡Passalora janseana (Racib.) U. Braun 2000

= Cercospora oryzae I. Miyake 1910

= Cercospora oryzae var. rufipogonis R.A. Singh & Pavgi 1968

= Sphaerulina oryzina Hara 1918

Distribution: Widespread in the rice-growing regions of the world including the United States (AL, AR, FL, LA, TX).

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Narrow brown leaf spot.

Host: Primiarily on Oryza sativa but also reported from other grasses.
