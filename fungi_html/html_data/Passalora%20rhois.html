Nomenclature data for Passalora rhois

Passalora guoana U. Braun 2016 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

[≡Mycovellosiella rhois Y.L. Guo 2000 - illegitimate later homonym, not included in search]

  Variant spelling Mycovellosiella rhoidis Y.L. Guo 2000

[≡Passalora rhois (Y.L. Guo) Y.L. Guo 2011 - illegitimate later homonym, not included in search]

  Variant spelling Passalora rhoidis (Y.L. Guo) Y.L. Guo 2011

Notes: Passalora guoana is a nom. nov. created to replace the illegitimate P. rhois Y.L. Guo 2011 (non P. rhois (E. Castell.) U. Braun & Crous 2003), itself based on the illegitimate Mycovellosiella rhois Y.L. Guo 2000 (non M. rhois Goh & W.H. Hsieh 2003). Generic affinity uncertain.

Distribution: Asia (China).

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Rhus sp. (Anacardiaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Braun, U., Crous, P.W., and Nakashima, C. 2016. Cercosporoid fungi (Mycosphaerellaceae) 5. Species on dicots (Anacardiaceae to Annonaceae). IMA Fungus 7(1): 161-216.
