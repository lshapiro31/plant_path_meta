Nomenclature data for Passalora robiniae

Passalora robiniae (Shear) S. Hughes 1953 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Fusicladium robiniae Shear 1902

≡Camptomeris robiniae (Shear) Cif. 1951

≡Phaeoisariopsis robiniae (Shear) Deighton 1976

Notes: Per Videira et al. (2017), this species clusters in the Pseudocercospora clade but more material is needed for study before a formal recombination can be proposed.

Distribution: Eastern United States. Also reported from Korea.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Robinia pseudoacacia (Fabaceae).
