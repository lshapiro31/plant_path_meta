Nomenclature data for Penicillium cyclopium

Penicillium aurantiogriseum Dierckx 1901 (Ascomycetes, Eurotiales)

    Variant spelling Penicillium aurantio-griseum Dierckx

= Penicillium cyclopium Westling 1911

= Penicillium martensii Biourge 1923

Notes: For additional synonyms, see Index Fungorum.

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Numerous organic substrates.

Disease Note: Blue mold.

Host: Numerous plant and animal hosts.
