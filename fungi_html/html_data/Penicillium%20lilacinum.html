Nomenclature data for Penicillium lilacinum

Purpureocillium lilacinus (Thom) Luangsa-ard, Houbraken, Hywel-Jones & Samson 2011 (, )

≡ Penicillium lilacinum Thom 1910

≡Paecilomyces lilacinus (Thom) Samson 1974

Notes: From Paecilomyces lilacinus - Isolated from insects and a variety of plant materials (Bissett. Fungi Canadenses 156. 1979).

Distribution: Widespread.

Disease Note: Used in biological control of insects.

Host: On insects.
