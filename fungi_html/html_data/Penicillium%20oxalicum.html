Nomenclature data for Penicillium oxalicum

Penicillium oxalicum Currie & Thom 1915 (Ascomycetes, Eurotiales)

Notes: http://www.aspergilluspenicillium.org/index.php/penicillium-names-b/penicillium-names-o

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Various plant substrates.

Disease Note: Pre and post harvest rot; a common pathogen of greenhouse cucumbers.

Host: Numerous hosts.

Supporting Literature:
