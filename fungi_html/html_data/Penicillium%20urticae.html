Nomenclature data for Penicillium urticae

Penicillium griseofulvum Dierckx 1901 (Ascomycetes, Eurotiales)

    Variant spelling Penicillium griseo-fulvum Dierckx 1901

= Penicillium patulum Bainier 1906

= Penicillium urticae Bainier 1907

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: On a variety of plant materials.

Disease Note: Blue mold.

Host: Numerous hosts.
