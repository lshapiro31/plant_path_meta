Nomenclature data for Peniophora albomarginata

Dendrophora albobadia (Schwein. : Fr.) Chamuris 1987 (Basidiomycetes, Russulales)

≡ Thelephora albobadia Schwein. : Fr. 1822  Note: Published as albo-badia.

≡Peniophora albobadia (Schwein. : Fr.) Boidin 1961

≡Stereum albobadium (Schwein. : Fr.) Fr. 1838

  Variant spelling Stereum albo-badium (Schwein. : Fr.) Fr.

= Thelephora albomarginata Schwein. 1847

  ≡Peniophora albomarginata (Schwein.) Massee 1889

  Variant spelling Thelephora albo-marginata Schwein. 1847

= Stereum heterosporum Burt 1920  Note: Specimens reported under this name may be Peniophora malenconii.

    ≡ Lopharia heterospora (Burt) D.A. Reid 1969  Note: Specimens reported under this name may be Peniophora malenconii.

    ≡ Peniophora heterospora (Burt) Boidin & Lanq. 1974

Distribution: North America (USA), Caribbean Islands, Central America, South America (Chamuris 1988).

Substrate: Dead, usually corticate wood of branches and twigs.

Disease Note: White rot.

Host: Hardwoods, multiple families.

Supporting Literature:

Chamuris, G.P. 1987. Notes on stereoid fungi I. The genus Dendrophora, stat. nov., and Peniophora malenconii subsp. americana, subsp. nov. ('Stereum heterosporum'). Mycotaxon 28: 543-552.
