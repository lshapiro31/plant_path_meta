Nomenclature data for Perenniporia robiniophila

Perenniporia robiniophila (Murrill) Ryvarden 1983 (Basidiomycetes, Polyporales)

≡ Trametes robiniophila Murrill 1907

≡Polyporus robiniophilus (Murrill) Lloyd 1912

Notes: Dai, Yucheng & Gao, Q. "A New Heart Rot on Robinia pseudoacacia in Northern China" Journal of Northeast Forestry University (2005). ------ Ryvarden "Two Perenniporia species from Pakistan" Mycotaxon Vol XVII, pp. 517-520 (1983).

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: On hardwoods.

Disease Note: Heart rot.

Host: Robinia pseudoacacia (Fabaceae) and other hardwoods.
