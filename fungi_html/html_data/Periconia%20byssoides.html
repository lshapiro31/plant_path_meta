Nomenclature data for Periconia byssoides

Periconia byssoides Pers. 1801 (Ascomycetes, Incertae sedis)

≡Cephalotrichum byssoides (Pers.) Kuntze 1898

≡Sporocybe byssoides (Pers.) Fr. 1832

≡Stilbum byssoides (Pers.) Spreng. 1827

= Periconia botrytiformis Fresen. 1850

= Periconia pycnospora Fresen. 1850

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Leaves, stems.

Disease Note: Associated with leaf and stem spots, blight, twig dieback, fruit mold.

Host: Multiple genera in multiple families.
