Nomenclature data for Periconia igniaria

Didymosphaeria igniaria C. Booth 1968 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

= Periconia igniaria E.W. Mason & M.B. Ellis 1953

Notes: Booth (Trans. Brit. Mycol. Soc. 51:803. 1968).

Distribution: Europe; India (type); Jamaica; USA (FL).

Substrate: On living leaves and shoots.

Disease Note: Shoot blight.
