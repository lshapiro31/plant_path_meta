Nomenclature data for Peridermium ephedrae

Peridermium ephedrae Cooke 1877 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

Notes: Kern & Keener (Science 131:298. 1960).

Distribution: Southwestern United States; Mexico.

Disease Note: Rust.

Host: Ephedra spp. (Ephedraceae).

Supporting Literature:

Hiratsuka Y., and Maruyama, P.J. 1968. Nuclear Condition of the germ tubes of Peridermium ephedrae. Mycologia 60: 437-438.
