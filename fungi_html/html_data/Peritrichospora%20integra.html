Nomenclature data for Peritrichospora integra

Corollospora maritima Werderm. 1922 (Ascomycetes, Halosphaeriales)

= Arenariomyces cinctus Hohnk 1954

= Peritrichospora integra Linder 1944

Distribution: Widespread in marine habitats.

Substrate: Organic and non-organic beach debris.

Disease Note: Saprobic.

Host: Numerous hosts.

Supporting Literature:

Enriquez, D.I., and Minter, D.W. 2009. Corollospora maritima. I.M.I. Descr. Fungi Bact. 1805: 1-7.
