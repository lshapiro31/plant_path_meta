Nomenclature data for Peronoplasmopara cubensis

Pseudoperonospora cubensis (Berk. & M.A. Curtis) Rostovzev 1903 (Oomycetes, Peronosporales)

≡ Peronospora cubensis Berk. & M.A. Curtis 1868

≡Peronoplasmopara cubensis (Berk. & M.A. Curtis) G.P. Clinton 1905 [1904]  Note: as 'cubense'

≡Plasmopara cubensis (Berk. & M.A. Curtis) C.J. Humphrey 1891

≡Plasmopara cubensis var. cubensis .

= Peronospora cubensis var. atra Zimm. 1902

    ≡ Plasmopara cubensis var. atra (Zimm.) Sacc. & D. Sacc. 1905

= Peronoplasmopara luffae Sawada 1931  Note: Possible synonym of Pseudoperonospora cubensis (Constantinescu 1991).

    ≡ Peronospora luffae (Sawada) Skalický 1966

= Pseudoperonospora tweriensis Rostovzev 1903

    ≡ Plasmopara cubensis var. tweriensis (Rostovzev) Sacc. & D. Sacc. 1905

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Downy mildew.

Host: Cucurbitaceae, Impatiens irvingii (Balsaminaceae). Reports from various plant families.
