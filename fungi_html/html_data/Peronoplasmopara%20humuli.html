Nomenclature data for Peronoplasmopara humuli

Pseudoperonospora humuli (Miyabe & Takah.) G.W. Wilson 1914 (Oomycetes, Peronosporales)

≡ Peronoplasmopara humuli Miyabe & Takah. 1905

≡Peronospora humuli (Miyabe & Takah.) Skalický 1966

≡Plasmopara humuli (Miyabe & Takah.) Sacc. 1912

= Pseudoperonospora celtidis var. humuli Davis 1910

Distribution: Temperate regions.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Downy mildew.

Host: Cannabaceae.
