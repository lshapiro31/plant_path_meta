Nomenclature data for Peronosclerospora sacchari

Peronosclerospora sacchari (T. Miyake) Shirai & Hara 1927 (Oomycetes, Sclerosporales)

≡ Sclerospora sacchari T. Miyake 1912 [1911]

Notes: Type of Peronosclerospora. This name is sometimes erroneously cited as P. sacchari (T. Miyake) C.G. Shaw based on a later superfluous publication. According to Bonde et al. (1984) and Yao et al. (1991), Peronosclerospora philippinensis is a possible synonym.

Distribution: Asia, Australia. Reports from Central America are unsubstantiated (Peterson, pers. comm.).

Substrate: Leaves; seed-borne.

Disease Note: Downy mildew.

Host: Principal host: sugar cane, Saccharum officinarum; also corn, Zea mays and other Poaceae.
