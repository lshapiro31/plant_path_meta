Nomenclature data for Peronosclerospora sorghi

Peronosclerospora sorghi (W. Weston & Uppal) C.G. Shaw 1978 (Oomycetes, Sclerosporales)

≡ Sclerospora sorghi W. Weston & Uppal 1932

≡Sclerospora graminicola var. andropogonis-sorghi Kulk. 1913

Notes: This fungus was originally described as Sclerospora graminicola (Sacc.) Schroet. var. andropogonis-sorghi by Kulkarni (1913). When raising it to species rank, Weston & Uppal gave it a new name, Sclerospora sorghi, thereby discarding the varietal epithet. Safeeulla & Thirumalachar (1955) erroneously argued that the epithet andropogonis-sorghi had priority in Sclerospora, but epithets have no priority outside their rank (Art. 11.2).

Distribution: North, Central and South America; Africa; Asia; Australia. Once restricted to Africa and Asia.

Substrate: Systemic infection or local infection of leaves.

Disease Note: Downy mildew. Soil-borne, sometimes seed-borne.

Host: Sorghum spp., also Zea mays and other grasses.
