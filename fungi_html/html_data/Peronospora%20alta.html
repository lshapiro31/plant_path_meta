Nomenclature data for Peronospora alta

Peronospora alta Fuckel 1863 (Oomycetes, Peronosporales)

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Downy mildew.

Host: Plantago spp. (Plantaginaceae).
