Nomenclature data for Peronospora arborescens

Peronospora arborescens (Berk.) Casp. 1855 (Oomycetes, Peronosporales)

≡ Botrytis arborescens Berk. 1846

= Peronospora grisea var. minor Casp. 1857

Distribution: Temperate and warm-temperate regions.

Substrate: Living leaves and stems; systemic infection.

Disease Note: Downy mildew of poppy; also distortion, chlorosis, stunting, etc.

Host: Papaveraceae.
