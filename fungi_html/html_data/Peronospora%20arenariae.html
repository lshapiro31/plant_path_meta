Nomenclature data for Peronospora arenariae

Peronospora arenariae (Berk.) Tul. 1854 (Oomycetes, Peronosporales)

≡ Botrytis arenariae Berk. 1846

Distribution: Scattered reports in northern hemisphere.

Disease Note: Downy mildew.

Host: Caryophyllaceae.

Supporting Literature:

Constantinescu, O. 1991. An annotated list of Peronospora names. Thunbergia 15: 1-110.
