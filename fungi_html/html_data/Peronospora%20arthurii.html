Nomenclature data for Peronospora arthurii

Peronospora arthurii Farl. 1883 (Oomycetes, Peronosporales)

    Variant spelling Peronospora arthuri Farl. 1883

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Downy mildew; discoloration.

Host: Onagraceae.
