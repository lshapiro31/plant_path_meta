Nomenclature data for Peronospora astragalina

Peronospora astragalina Syd. 1923 (Oomycetes, Peronosporales)

Distribution: Eurasia.

Disease Note: Downy mildew.

Host: Astragalus spp. (Fabaceae).

Supporting Literature:
