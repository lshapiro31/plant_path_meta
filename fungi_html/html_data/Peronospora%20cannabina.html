Nomenclature data for Peronospora cannabina

Pseudoperonospora cannabina (G.H. Otth) Curzi 1926 (Oomycetes, Peronosporales)

≡ Peronospora cannabina G.H. Otth 1869

≡Peronoplasmopara cannabina (G.H. Otth) Peglion 1917

Notes: Isonym of (G.H. Otth) Hoerner 1940.

Distribution: Widespread.

Disease Note: Downy mildew.

Host: Cannabis spp.

Supporting Literature:

Waterhouse, G.M., and Brothers, M.P. 1981. The taxonomy of Pseudoperonospora. Mycol. Pap. 148: 1-28.
