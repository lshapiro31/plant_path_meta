Nomenclature data for Peronospora cristata

Peronospora cristata Tranzschel 1902 (Oomycetes, Peronosporales)

Distribution: Europe, Australia (Tasmania).

Substrate: Living leaves and stems.

Disease Note: Downy mildew.

Host: Papaveraceae.
