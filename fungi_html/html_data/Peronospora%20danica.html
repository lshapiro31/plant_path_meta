Nomenclature data for Peronospora danica

Peronospora radii de Bary 1864 (Oomycetes, Peronosporales)

≡Peronospora radii f. radii de Bary 1864

≡Peronospora radii var. radii de Bary 1864  Note: Type variety not needed.

= Peronospora danica Gäum. 1923

= Peronospora radii var. epiphylla G. Poirault 1915

Distribution: Europe, Middle East (Israel), North America (Mexico) (Constantinescu 1989). Also reported in Asia (Russia, China), and U.S.A. (CA).

Substrate: Florets, rarely leaves and stems.

Disease Note: Downy mildew.

Host: Multiple genera of Asteraceae, including Chrysanthemum spp.

Supporting Literature:

Constantinescu, O. 1989. Peronospora complex on Compositae. Sydowia 41: 79-107.
