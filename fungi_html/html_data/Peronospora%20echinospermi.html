Nomenclature data for Peronospora echinospermi

Peronospora echinospermi (Swingle) Swingle 1892 (Oomycetes, Peronosporales)

≡ Peronospora cynoglossi var. echinospermi Swingle 1889

Distribution: Widespread.

Disease Note: Downy mildew.

Host: Boraginaceae.
