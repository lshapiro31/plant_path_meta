Nomenclature data for Peronospora effusa

Peronospora effusa (Grev.) Rabenh. 1854 (Oomycetes, Peronosporales)

≡ Botrytis effusa Grev. 1824

≡Peronospora effusa var. effusa

= Peronospora farinosa f. sp. spinaciae Byford 1967

Notes: Per Choi et. al., P. farinosa from spinach is distinct and should be included in P. effusa (Grev.) Rabenh..

Distribution: Worldwide.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Downy mildew of spinach.

Host: Restricted to Spinacia (Amaranthaceae - including Chenopodiaceae).
