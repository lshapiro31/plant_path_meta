Nomenclature data for Peronospora euphorbiae

Peronospora euphorbiae Fuckel 1863 (Oomycetes, Peronosporales)

Distribution: Widespread in northern hemisphere.

Disease Note: Downy mildew.

Host: Typically Euphorbia (Euphorbiaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Constantinescu, O. 1991. An annotated list of Peronospora names. Thunbergia 15: 1-110.
