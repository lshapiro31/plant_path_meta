Nomenclature data for Peronospora ganglioniformis

Bremia lactucae Regel 1843 (Oomycetes, Peronosporales)

≡Bremia lactucae var. lactucae

= Botrytis gangliformis Berk. 1846

    ≡ Peronospora gangliformis (Berk.) Tul. 1854  Note: Isonym of (Berk.) de Bary 1863.

    Variant spelling Peronospora ganglioniformis (Berk.) Tul. 1854  Note: Isonym of (Berk.) de Bary 1863.

  Variant spelling Botrytis ganglioniformis Berk. 1846

    ≡ Bremia gangliformis (Berk.) C.G. Shaw 1949

  Variant spelling Bremia ganglioniformis (Berk.) C.G. Shaw 1949

Notes: Host range may be more limited than previously thought (Choi et al. 2011).

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Downy mildew.

Host: Asteraceae. Especially Lactuca.
