Nomenclature data for Peronospora isatidis

Hyaloperonospora isatidis (Gäum.) Göker, Riethm., Voglmayr, M. Weiss & Oberw. 2004 (Oomycetes, Peronosporales)

≡ Peronospora isatidis Gäum. 1918

Distribution: Eurasia.

Disease Note: Downy mildew.

Host: Isatis spp. (Brassicaceae).
