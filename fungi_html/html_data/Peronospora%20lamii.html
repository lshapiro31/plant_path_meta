Nomenclature data for Peronospora lamii

Peronospora lamii A. Braun 1857 (Oomycetes, Peronosporales)

≡Peronospora lamii var. lamii

= Peronospora calaminthae Fuckel 1866

= Peronospora thymi Syd. 1887  Note: Nom. inval. (Constantinescu 1991).

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Downy mildew.

Host: Lamiaceae.
