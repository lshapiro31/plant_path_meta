Nomenclature data for Peronospora lepidii-virginici

Perofascia lepidii (McAlpine) Constant. 2002 (Oomycetes, Peronosporales)

≡ Peronospora parasitica var. lepidii McAlpine 1895

≡Peronospora lepidii (McAlpine) G.W. Wilson 1914

= Peronospora coronopi Gäum. 1918

= Peronospora lepidii-virginici Gäum. 1918

  Variant spelling Peronospora lepidii virginici Gäum.

= Peronospora maublancii Savul. & Rayss 1934

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Leaves, stems, fruit.

Disease Note: Downy mildew.

Host: Brassicaceae.

Supporting Literature:
