Nomenclature data for Peronospora lunariae

Hyaloperonospora lunariae (Gäum.) Constant. 2002 (Oomycetes, Peronosporales)

≡ Peronospora lunariae Gäum. 1918

= Peronospora senecionis Fuckel 1870

Distribution: Europe, South America (Brazil).

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Downy mildew.

Host: Brassicaceae.
