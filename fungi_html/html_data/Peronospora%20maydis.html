Nomenclature data for Peronospora maydis

Peronosclerospora maydis (Racib.) C.G. Shaw 1978 (Oomycetes, Sclerosporales)

≡ Peronospora maydis Racib. 1897

≡Sclerospora maydis (Racib.) E.J. Butler 1913  Note: Not Reinking 1918.

= Sclerospora javanica Palm 1918

Distribution: Australia, Asia, South America (Venezuela, Argentina). Reports in Africa are unreliable (EPPO notes, CPC website).

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Downy mildew.

Host: Primary host: corn, Zea mays; Secondary hosts other Poaceae.

Supporting Literature:

Shaw, C.G. 1978. Peronosclerospora species and other downy mildews of the Gramineae. Mycologia 70: 594-604.
