Nomenclature data for Peronospora polygoni

Peronospora polygoni (Thüm.) A. Fisch. 1892 (Oomycetes, Peronosporales)

≡ Peronospora effusa var. polygoni Thüm. 1876

      Peronospora effusa var. polygoni-convolvuli Thüm.

Notes: Halst. is an invalid slip of the pen (Constantinescu 1991).

Distribution: Widespread.

Disease Note: Downy mildew.

Host: Polygonum (Polygonaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Constantinescu, O. 1991. An annotated list of Peronospora names. Thunbergia 15: 1-110.
