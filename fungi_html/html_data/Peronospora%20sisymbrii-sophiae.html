Nomenclature data for Peronospora sisymbrii-sophiae

Hyaloperonospora sisymbrii-sophiae (Gäum.) Göker, Voglmayr & Oberw. 2009 (Oomycetes, Peronosporales)

≡ Peronospora sisymbrii-sophiae Gäum. 1918

Distribution: Eurasia.

Substrate: Leaves, pedicels, flowers, stems, pods.

Disease Note: Downy mildew.

Host: Descurainia sophia (Brassicaceae).

Supporting Literature:
