Nomenclature data for Peronospora symphyti

Peronospora symphyti Gäum. 1923 (Oomycetes, Peronosporales)

Distribution: Europe.

Disease Note: Downy mildew.

Host: Symphytum (Boraginaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Constantinescu, O. 1991. An annotated list of Peronospora names. Thunbergia 15: 1-110.
