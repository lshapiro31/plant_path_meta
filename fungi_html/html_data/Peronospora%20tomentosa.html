Nomenclature data for Peronospora tomentosa

Peronospora alsinearum Casp. 1855 (Oomycetes, Peronosporales)

= Peronospora tomentosa Fuckel 1863

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Downy mildew.

Host: Caryophyllaceae.
