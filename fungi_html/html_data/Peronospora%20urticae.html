Nomenclature data for Peronospora urticae

Pseudoperonospora urticae (Lib. ex Berk.) E.S. Salmon & W.N. Ware 1925 (Oomycetes, Peronosporales)

≡ Botrytis urticae Lib. ex Berk.

≡Peronoplasmopara urticae (Lib. ex Berk.) Savul. & O. Savul. 1951

≡Peronospora urticae (Lib. ex Berk.) Casp. 1855

Distribution: Widespread.

Disease Note: Downy mildew.

Host: Urtica spp. especially U. dioica.
