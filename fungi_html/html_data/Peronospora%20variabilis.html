Nomenclature data for Peronospora variabilis

Peronospora variabilis Gäum. 1919 (Oomycetes, Peronosporales)

Notes: Choi et al. (2010) provided evidene that this represents a species distinct from P. farinosa.

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Downy mildew.

Host: Chenopodium spp. (Amaranthaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Baiswar, P., Chandra, S., Kumar, R., and Ngachan, S.V. 2010. Peronospora variabilis on Chenopodium murale in India. Australasian Plant Disease Notes 5: 45-47.

Choi, Y.-J. , Danielsen, S., Lubeck, M., Hong, S.-B., Delhey, R., and Shin, H.-D. 2010. Morphological and molecular characterization of the causal agent of downy mildew on quinoa (Chenopodium quinoa). Mycopathologia 169: 403-412.
