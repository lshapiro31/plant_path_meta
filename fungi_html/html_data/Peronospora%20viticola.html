Nomenclature data for Peronospora viticola

Plasmopara viticola (Berk. & M.A. Curtis) Berl. & De Toni 1888 (Oomycetes, Peronosporales)

≡ Botrytis viticola Berk. & M.A. Curtis 1848

≡Peronospora viticola (Berk. & M.A. Curtis) Casp. 1855

≡Phytophthora viticola (Berk. & M.A. Curtis) W. Reichelt 1887  Note: See Waterhouse 1963.

≡Rhysotheca viticola (Berk. & M.A. Curtis) G.W. Wilson 1907

= Plasmopara amurensis Prots. 1946

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Living leaves, branches.

Disease Note: Downy mildew.

Host: Vitis spp. (grapevine) and other Vitaceae.
