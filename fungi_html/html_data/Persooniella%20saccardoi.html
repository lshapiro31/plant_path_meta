Nomenclature data for Persooniella saccardoi

Puccinia saccardoi F. Ludw. 1889 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

≡Dicaeoma saccardoi (F. Ludw.) Kuntze 1898

≡Persooniella saccardoi (F. Ludw.) Syd. 1922

Distribution: Australia.

Substrate: Fruit, stems, upper and lower leaf surfaces.

Disease Note: Rust.

Host: Goodenia spp. (Goodeniaceae).

Supporting Literature:
