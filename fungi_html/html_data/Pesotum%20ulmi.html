Nomenclature data for Pesotum ulmi

Ophiostoma ulmi (Buisman) Nannf. 1934 (Ascomycetes, Ophiostomatales)

≡ Ceratostomella ulmi Buisman 1932

≡Ceratocystis ulmi (Buisman) C. Moreau 1952

= Graphium ulmi M.B. Schwarz 1922

  ≡Pesotum ulmi (M.B. Schwartz) J.L. Crane & Schokn. 1973

Notes: Synanamorphs pesotum and sporothrix-like.

Distribution: Europe, North America primarily. Also, Africa (South Africa) and Oceania (New Zealand).

Disease Note: Dutch elm diseae.

Host: Primarily Ulmus spp. (Ulmaceae).
