Nomenclature data for Pestalotia adusta

Pestalotiopsis adusta (Ellis & Everh.) Steyaert 1953 (Ascomycetes, Xylariales)

≡ Pestalotia adusta Ellis & Everh. 1888

      Variant spelling Pestalozzia adusta Ellis & Everh.

Notes: On leaves and stems of a variety of woody dicotyledons. Synonymy follows Guba (Monograph of Monochaetia and Pestalotia. p. 117. 1961 as Pestalotia adusta.

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Multiple families.
