Nomenclature data for Pestalotia disseminata

Pestalotiopsis disseminata (Thüm.) Steyaert 1949 (Ascomycetes, Xylariales)

≡ Pestalotia disseminata Thüm. 1880

      Variant spelling Pestalozzia disseminata Thüm. 1880

Distribution: North America (USA: FL, VA), Caribbean, South America (Venezuela), Europe (Portugal), Asia, Africa.

Substrate: Living and dead leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot. Listed by APHIS as a Regulated Plant Pest.

Host: Multiple plant families.

Supporting Literature:

Das, R., Chutia, M., Das, K., and Jha, D.K. 2010. Factors affecting sporulation of Pestalotiopsis disseminata causing grey blight disease of Persea bombycina Kost., the primary food plant of muga silkworm. Crop Protect. 29: 963-968.
