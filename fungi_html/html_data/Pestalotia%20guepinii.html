Nomenclature data for Pestalotia guepinii

Pestalotiopsis guepinii (Desm.) Steyaert 1949 (Ascomycetes, Xylariales)

≡ Pestalotia guepinii Desm. 1840

      Variant spelling Pestalotia guepini Desm. 1840

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Twigs, leaves, flowers.

Disease Note: Grey leaf spot, petal rot, wilt, stem canker, twig dieback.

Host: Multiple genera in multiple families.
