Nomenclature data for Pestalotia lupini

Pleiochaeta setosa (Kirchn.) S. Hughes 1951 (Ascomycetes, Incertae sedis)

≡ Ceratophorum setosum Kirchn. 1892

= Pestalotia lupini Sorauer 1898

  Variant spelling Pestalozzia lupini Sorauer

    ≡ Mastigosporium lupini (Sorauer) Cavara 1924

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Leaves, stems, pods.

Disease Note: Lesions.

Host: Crotalaria spp., Cystius spp., Lupinus spp., Ornithopus spp. (Fabaceae).
