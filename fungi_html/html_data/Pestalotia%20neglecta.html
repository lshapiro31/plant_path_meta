Nomenclature data for Pestalotia neglecta

Pestalotiopsis neglecta (Thüm.) Steyaert 1953 (Ascomycetes, Xylariales)

≡ Pestalotia neglecta Thüm. 1879

      Variant spelling Pestalozzia neglecta Thüm. 1879

Notes: This synonymy follows Steyaert (Darwiniana 12:162. 1961) except for Pestalotiopsis funerea (Mordue. C.M.I. Descr. 514. 1976).

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Wide host range.

Supporting Literature:

Tagne, A., and Mathur, S.B. 2001. First report of chlorotic spot of maize caused by Pestalotiopsis neglecta. New Disease Reports 3: 1.
