Nomenclature data for Pestalotia vaccinii

Pestalotia vaccinii (Shear) Guba 1929 (Ascomycetes, Xylariales)

≡ Pestalotia guepinii var. vaccinii Shear 1902

      Variant spelling Pestalozzia guepini var. vaccinii Shear 1902

Distribution: Europe, North America (USA), New Zealand.

Substrate: Leaves, twigs, fruit.

Disease Note: Leaf and fruit rot.

Host: Ericaceae.

Supporting Literature:

Ceponis, M.J., Kaufman, J., and Tietjen, W.H. 1973. Decays of New Jersey blueberries in marketing channels. Pl. Dis. Reporter 57: 487-488.

Jeewon, R., Liew, E.C.Y., and Kyde, K.D. 2003. Molecular systematics of the Amphisphaeriaceae based on cladistic analyses of partial LSU rDNA gene sequences. Mycol. Res. 107: 1392-1402.
