Nomenclature data for Pestalotia versicolor

Pestalotiopsis versicolor (Speg.) Steyaert 1949 (Ascomycetes, Xylariales)

≡ Pestalotia versicolor Speg. 1879

      Variant spelling Pestalozzia versicolor Speg.

Notes: Guba (1961) lists several synonyms.

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Fruits, leaves, stems.

Disease Note: Canker, dieback, leaf spot, etc.

Host: Multiple genera in multiple families.
