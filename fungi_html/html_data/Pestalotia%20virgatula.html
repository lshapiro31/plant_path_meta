Nomenclature data for Pestalotia virgatula

Pestalotiopsis virgatula (Kleb.) Steyaert 1949 (Ascomycetes, Xylariales)

≡ Pestalotia virgatula Kleb. 1914

      Variant spelling Pestalozzia virgatula Kleb.

= Pestalotia funerea var. mangiferae Sacc. 1884

= Pestalotia microspora var. philippinensis Sacc. & P. Syd. 1913

  Variant spelling Pestalozzia microspora var. philippinensis Sacc. & P. Syd. 1913

    ≡ Pestalotiopsis microspora var. philippinensis (Sacc. & P. Syd.) Bat. & Peres 1966  Note: Nom. inval.

Notes: Nag Raj (1993) included this taxon with excluded and unobserved taxa.

Distribution: Africa, Asia, North America (Mexico, USA: HI).

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Assorted genera in several families.

Supporting Literature:

Guba, E.F. 1961. Monograph of Monochaetia and Pestalotia. Harvard Univ. Press, Cambridge, MA, 342 pages.

Nag Raj, T.R. 1993. Coelomycetous anamorphs with appendage-bearing conidia. Mycologue Publications, Waterloo, Ontario, 1-1101 pages.
