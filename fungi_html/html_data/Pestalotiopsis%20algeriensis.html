Nomenclature data for Pestalotiopsis algeriensis

Pestalotiopsis algeriensis (Sacc. & Berl.) W.P. Wu 1992 (Ascomycetes, Xylariales)

≡ Pestalotia funerea var. algeriensis Sacc. & Berl. 1886

≡Pestalotia algeriensis (Sacc. & Berl.) Guba 1961

Distribution: Widespread in subtropical to tropical regions.

Substrate: Fruits, leaves, stems.

Disease Note: Leaf spot. Also on fruits and canes.

Host: Multiple genera in multiple families.

Supporting Literature:

Wu, W.-p. 1992. [Note on some coelomycetes on Euonymus japonicas I. Melanconiales]. J. Hebei Acad. Sci. 1992: 65-74.
