Nomenclature data for Pestalotiopsis foedans

Neopestalotiopsis foedans (Sacc. & Ellis) Maharachch., K.D. Hyde & Crous 2014 (, )

≡ Pestalozzia foedans Sacc. & Ellis 1882

≡Pestalotiopsis foedans (Sacc. & Ellis) Steyaert 1949

= Pestalotiopsis chamaecyparidis Sawada 1950

= Pestalosphaeria gubae Tak. Kobay., Ishihara & Yas. Ono 2001

= Pestalosphaeria hansenii Shoemaker & J. Simpson 1981

= Pestalotiopsis shiraiana Henn. 1905

Notes: Differs from P. austroamericana, P. concentrica, and P. elaeidis in shape and size of the ascospores and conidia of their respective anamorphs (Shoemaker and Simpson. Canad. J. Bot. 59:986. 1981).

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: On needles, leaves, twigs, or bark.

Disease Note: Saprobic and leaf spots.

Host: Hosts on several families.
