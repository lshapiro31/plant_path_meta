Nomenclature data for Pestalotiopsis funerea

Pestalotiopsis funerea (Desm.) Steyaert 1949 (Ascomycetes, Xylariales)

≡ Pestalotia funerea Desm. 1843

      Variant spelling Pestalozzia funerea Desm. 1843

Notes: Many literature reports are based on broad taxonomic concepts not accepted by all authors. Many infraspecific taxa have been named.

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf and stem blights, cankers, dieback, root rot.

Host: Multiple genera in multiple families especially conifers.
