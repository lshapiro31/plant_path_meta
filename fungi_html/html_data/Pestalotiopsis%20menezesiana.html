Nomenclature data for Pestalotiopsis menezesiana

Pestalotiopsis menezesiana (Bres. & Torrend) Bissett 1982 (Ascomycetes, Xylariales)

≡ Pestalotia menezesiana Bres. & Torr. 1909

      Variant spelling Pestalozzia menezesiana Bres. & Torr.

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot/blight, postharvest fruit rot.

Host: Vitis vinifera (Vitaceae) and numerous other hosts.
