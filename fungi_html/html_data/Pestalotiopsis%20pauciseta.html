Nomenclature data for Pestalotiopsis pauciseta

Pestalotiopsis pauciseta (Sacc.) Y.X. Chen 1993 (Ascomycetes, Xylariales)

≡ Pestalotia pauciseta Sacc. 1914

      Variant spelling Pestalozzia pauciseta Sacc.

Distribution: Asia.

Substrate: Leaves, stems.

Disease Note: Leaf blight.

Host: Multiple genera in multiple families.

Supporting Literature:

Liu, A.-R., Wu, X.-P., Xu, T., Guo, L.D., and Wei, J.-G. 2006. Endophytic Pestalotiopsis species from Hainan, China. Mycosystema 25: 389-397.
