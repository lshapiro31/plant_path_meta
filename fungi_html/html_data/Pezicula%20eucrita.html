Nomenclature data for Pezicula eucrita

Pezicula eucrita (P. Karst.) P. Karst. 1871 (Ascomycetes, Helotiales)

≡ Peziza eucrita P. Karst. 1869

≡Allophylaria eucrita (P. Karst.) P. Karst. 1870

≡Dermatea eucrita (P. Karst.) Rehm 1889

≡Dermatella eucrita (P. Karst.) Sacc. 1889

= Cryptosporiopsis abietina Petr. 1925

Distribution: Europe; one report from NY.

Disease Note: Canker.

Host: Primarily on Pinaceae.

Supporting Literature:

Verkley, G.J.M. 1999. A monograph of the genus Pezicula and its anamorphs. Stud. Mycol. 44: 1-180.
