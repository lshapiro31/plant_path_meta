Nomenclature data for Peziza earliana

Diplocarpon fragariae (Sacc.) Rossman 2014 (Ascomycetes, Helotiales)

≡ Leptothyrium fragariae Lib. 1832

= Peziza earliana Ellis & Everh. 1884  Note: From Diplocarpon earlianum - As 'earliana'. Strawberry leaf scorch (Sivanesan & Gibson. C.M.I. Descr. 486. 1976).

    ≡ Diplocarpon earlianum (Ellis & Everh.) F.A. Wolf 1924

    ≡ Fabraea earliana (Ellis & Everh.) Seaver 1951

= Phyllosticta potentillae Desm. 1847

    ≡ Marssonina potentillae (Desm.) Magnus 1906

= Marssonina potentillae var. fragariae Sacc. 1937

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf scorch.

Host: Rosaceae.
