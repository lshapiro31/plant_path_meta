Nomenclature data for Peziza strigosa

Dinemasporium strigosum (Pers. : Fr.) Sacc. 1881 (Ascomycetes, Incertae sedis)

≡ Peziza strigosa Pers. : Fr. 1801

≡Polynema strigosa (Pers. : Fr.) Fuckel 1870

= Excipula betulae Fuckel 1860

    ≡ Polynema betulae (Fuckel) Fuckel 1870

    ≡ Dinemasporium betulae (Fuckel) Sacc. 1884

= Ellisiellina biciliata Sousa da Câmara 1949

= Pycnidiochaeta biciliata Sousa da Câmara 1950

= Vermicularia graminum Lib. 1837

    ≡ Dinemasporium graminum (Lib.) Lév. 1846

    ≡ Excipula graminum (Lib.) Corda 1839

= Dinemasporium longisetum Syd. 1936

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: On leaves, culms and stems as well as decorticated wood. Common on many graminicolous and other hosts, recorded on at least 46 different host genera (Sutton. The Coelomycetes. p. 458. 1980). Nag Raj (Coelomycetous anamorphs. p. 270. 1993).

Disease Note: Leaf spots but primarily saprobic.

Host: Multiple genera on various families.

Supporting Literature:
