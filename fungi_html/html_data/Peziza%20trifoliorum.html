Nomenclature data for Peziza trifoliorum

Pseudopeziza trifolii (Biv. : Fr.) Fuckel 1870[1869 & 1870] (Ascomycetes, Helotiales)

≡ Ascobolus trifolii Biv. : Fr. 1816

= Peziza trifoliorum Lib. 1837

Notes: Regarded by some as a synonym of Pseudopeziza medicaginis (Fr.) Fuckel; Booth & Waller 1979, Schmiedeknecht 1964, regard P. medicaginis as a distinct species. No recent studies or molecular work exist to support or weaken this separation that has been based on host and ascospore shape.

Distribution: North America, South America (Brazil, Chile), Australia, Europe, Asia (China, Pakistan), Africa.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Clover, Trifolium spp., Medicago spp., and other Fabaceae.
