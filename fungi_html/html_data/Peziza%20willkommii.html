Nomenclature data for Peziza willkommii

Lachnellula willkommii (R. Hartig) Dennis 1962 (Ascomycetes, Helotiales)

≡ Peziza willkommii Hartig 1874

      Variant spelling Peziza willkomii Hartig 1874

≡Dasyscyphus willkommii (R. Hartig) Rehm 1881  Note: as -Dasyscypha-

≡Helotium willkommii (Hartig) Wettst. 1887

≡Lachnella willkommii (Hartig) Seaver 1951

≡Trichoscypha willkommii (Hartig) Boud. 1907

≡Trichoscyphella willkommii (R. Hartig) Nannf. 1932

Distribution: Native to Asia, established in Europe. Eradication efforts in North America (Canada, USA: MA, ME) have been mostly successful but occasional local outbreaks occur.

Substrate: Bark, branches and stems.

Disease Note: Canker, girdling dieback. Not epidemic.

Host: Larix spp., Pseudolarix spp. (Pinaceae). According to Buczacki (1975) reports on other conifers are almost certainly erroneous.

Supporting Literature:

Buczacki, S.T. 1975. Trichoscyphella willkommii. C.M.I. Descr. Pathog. Fungi Bact. 450: 1-2.
