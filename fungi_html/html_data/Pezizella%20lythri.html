Notes: Pilidium proposed for conservation over Discohainesia, Hainesia and Sclerotiopsis by Johnston et al. (2014).------ Not Gloeosporium tremellinum Gaum. 1921, a different fungus from Indonesia.

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Fruit, leaves, stems.

Disease Note: Fruit rot, leaf spot, anthracnose.

Host: Numerous hosts in diverse families.
