Nomenclature data for Phacellium alborosellum

Phacellium alborosellum (Desm.) U. Braun 1990 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Stysanus alborosellus Desm. 1853

≡Isariopsis alborosella (Desm.) Sacc. 1881

  Variant spelling Isariopsis albo-rosella (Desm.) Sacc. 1881

≡Ramularia alborosella (Desm.) Gjaerum 1968

= Didymobotryopsis cerastii Solheim 1960

= Ramularia cerastii I.E. Brezhnev 1939

= Phacellium inhonestum Bonord. 1860

= Ramularia malachii Ying X. Wang & Xue Y. Wang 1997

= Isariopsis pusilla Fresen. 1863

Distribution: Asia, Europe, North America, South America.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Cerastium spp. (Caryophyllaceae).
