Nomenclature data for Phacellium irregulare

Phacellium irregulare (Peck) U. Braun 1990 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Oidium irregulare Peck 1874

≡Ovularia irregularis (Peck) Trel. 1884

= Ovularia isarioides Sacc. 1882

    ≡ Ramularia isarioides (Sacc.) Ellis & Everh. 1885

Distribution: Central and eastern North America.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Staphylea trifolia (Staphyleaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Braun, U. 1990. Studies on Ramularia and allied genera (III). Nova Hedwigia 50: 499-521.

Braun, U. 1998. A Monograph of Cercosporella, Ramularia and Allied Genera (Phytopathogenic Hyphomycetes). IHW-Verlag 2: 337.
