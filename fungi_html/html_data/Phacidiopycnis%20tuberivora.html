Nomenclature data for Phacidiopycnis tuberivora

Phacidiopycnis tuberivora (H.T. Güssow & W.R. Foster) B. Sutton 1980 (Ascomycetes, Rhytismatales)

≡ Phomopsis tuberivora H.T. Güssow & W.R. Foster 1932

Distribution: Temperate regions.

Substrate: Stems, tubers.

Disease Note: Stem-end rot, progressive dry rot, corky rot.

Host: Diverse hosts, notably Solanum tuberosum (Solanaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Gent, D.H., Mueller-Warrant, G., Woods, J.L., Putnam, M.L., and Twomey, M.C. 2013. Red crown rot of hop in Oregon caused by Phomopsis tuberivora. Pl. Health Progr. Online: doi:10.1094/PHP-2013-0624-01-BR.
