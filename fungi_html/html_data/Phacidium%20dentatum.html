Nomenclature data for Phacidium dentatum

Coccomyces dentatus (J.C. Schmidt & Kunze : Fr.) Sacc. 1877 (Ascomycetes, Rhytismatales)

≡ Phacidium dentatum J.C. Schmidt ex Kuntze 1817

= Coccomyces dentatus var. lauri Rehm 1910

= Coccomyces pentagonus Kirschst. 1936

= Leptostroma quercinum Lasch 1845

    ≡ Leptothyrium quercinum (Lasch) Sacc. 1880

Notes: On dead leaves of angiosperms, especially Fagaceae and Ericaceae. May be conspecific with Coccomyces bromeliacearum (Johnston. Sydowia 45:32. 1993).

Distribution: Warm-temperate regions.

Substrate: On fallen leaves.

Disease Note: Possibly endophytic, saprobic.

Host: On various leaves of hardwoods.
