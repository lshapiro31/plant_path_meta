Gremmenia infestans (P. Karst.) Crous 2014 (Ascomycetes, Helotiales)

≡ Phacidium infestans P. Karst. 1888

Distribution: Eurasia.

Substrate: Needles, buds, bark.

Disease Note: Snow/needle blight.

Host: Abies spp., Picea spp. (Pinaceae).
