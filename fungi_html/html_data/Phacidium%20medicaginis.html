Nomenclature data for Phacidium medicaginis

Pseudopeziza medicaginis (Lib.) Sacc. 1887 (Ascomycetes, Helotiales)

≡ Phacidium medicaginis Lib. 1832  Note: Not Lasch, year unknown (valid?).

Notes: Regarded by some as a synonym of Pseudopeziza trifolii (Fr.) Fuckel; Booth & Waller 1979, Schmiedeknecht 1964, regard P. medicaginis as a distinct species. No recent studies or molecular work exist to support or weaken this separation that has been based on host and ascospore shape.

Distribution: Worldwide in Medicago-growing regions.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Medicago spp. (Fabaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Booth, C., and Waller, J.M. 1979. Pseudopeziza medicaginis. C.M.I. Descr. Pathog. Fungi Bact. 637: 1-2.
