Nomenclature data for Phacidium piceae

Lophodermium piceae (Fuckel) Höhn. 1917 (Ascomycetes, Rhytismatales)

≡ Phacidium piceae Fuckel 1874

≡Coccomyces piceae (Fuckel) Sacc. 1889

Alternate State (Anamorph):

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Needles.

Disease Note: Endophyte; apparently not pathogenic.

Host: Abies spp. (Pinaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Barklund, P. 1987. Occurrence and pathogenicity of Lophodermium piceae appearing as an endophyte in needles of Picea abies. Trans. Brit. Mycol. Soc. 89: 307-313.
