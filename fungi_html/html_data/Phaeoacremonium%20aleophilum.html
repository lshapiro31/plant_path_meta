Nomenclature data for Phaeoacremonium aleophilum

Phaeoacremonium minimum (Tul. & C. Tul.) D. Gramaje, L. Mostert & Crous 2015 (Ascomycetes, Incertae sedis)

≡ Calosphaeria minima Tul. & C. Tul. 1863

≡Erostella minima (Tul. & C. Tul.) Traverso 1906

≡Togninia minima (Tul. & C. Tul.) Berl. 1900

≡Pleurostoma minimum (Tul. & C. Tul.) M.E. Barr, J.D. Rogers & Y.M. Ju 1993

= Phaeoacremonium aleophilum W. Gams, Crous, M.J. Wingf. & Mugnai 1996

= Calosphaeria alnicola Ellis & Everh. 1891 [1890]

    ≡ Togninia alnicola (Ellis & Everh.) Berl. 1900

= Longoa paniculata Curzi 1927

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Roots, stem, leaf sheath, peduncles.

Disease Note: Yellowing, dieback, tissue discoloration.

Host: Diverse hosts, although primarily on Vitis (Vitaceae).
