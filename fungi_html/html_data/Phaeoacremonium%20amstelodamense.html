Nomenclature data for Phaeoacremonium amstelodamense

Phaeoacremonium amstelodamense L. Mostert, Summerb. & Crous 2005 (Ascomycetes, Incertae sedis)

Distribution: Europe (Netherlands).

Substrate: Wood, people.

Disease Note: Wood streaking.

Host: Vitis vinifera (Vitaceae), Homo sapiens (Hominidae).

Supporting Literature:

Mostert, L., Groenewald, J.Z., Summerbell, R.C., Gams, W., and Crous, P.W. 2006. Taxonomy and pathology of Togninia (Diaporthales) and its Phaeoacremonium anamorphs. Stud. Mycol. 54: 1-115.
