Nomenclature data for Phaeoacremonium chlamydosporum

Phaeomoniella chlamydospora (W. Gams, Crous, M.J. Wingf. & Mugnai) Crous & W. Gams 2000 (Ascomycetes, Incertae sedis)

≡ Phaeoacremonium chlamydosporum W. Gams, Crous, M.J. Wingf. & Mugnai 1996

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Roots, trunks, cordons.

Disease Note: Cause of black measles, also called esca disease of grape.

Host: Vitis spp. (Vitaceae).
