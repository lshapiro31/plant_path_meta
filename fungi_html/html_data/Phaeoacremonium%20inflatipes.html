Nomenclature data for Phaeoacremonium inflatipes

Phaeoacremonium inflatipes W. Gams, Crous & M.J. Wingf. 1996 (Ascomycetes, Incertae sedis)

Distribution: Europe (Finland, Germany), North America (Cosat Rica, USA; CA, TX), South America (Venezuela).

Substrate: Stems, roots; human skin and nails.

Disease Note: Wilt, decline.

Host: Vitis vinifera (Vitaceae), Quecus virginiana (Fagaceae), Sorbus intermedia (Rosaceae), Nectandra sp. (Lauraceae); Homo sapiens (Hominidae).
