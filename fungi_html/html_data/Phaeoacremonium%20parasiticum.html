Nomenclature data for Phaeoacremonium parasiticum

Phaeoacremonium parasiticum (Ajello, Georg & C.J.K. Wang) W. Gams, Crous & M.J. Wingf. 1996 (Ascomycetes, Incertae sedis)

≡ Phialophora parasitica Ajello, Georg & C.J.K. Wang 1974

= Togninia parasitica L. Mostert, W. Gams & Crous 2006

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Roots, leaves, rachis, trunk.

Disease Note: Dieback, brown stripe, tissue discoloration.

Host: Humans and diverse plant hosts.
