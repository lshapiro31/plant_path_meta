Nomenclature data for Phaeoacremonium viticola

Phaeoacremonium viticola J. Dupont 2000 (Ascomycetes, Incertae sedis)

= Togninia viticola L. Mostert, W. Gams & Crous 2006

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Stems, roots.

Disease Note: Esca disease of grape.

Host: Primarily known from Vitis (Vitaceae) but also on other plant hosts.
