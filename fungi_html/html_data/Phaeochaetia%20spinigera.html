Nomenclature data for Phaeochaetia spinigera

Triposporiopsis spinigera (Höhn.) W. Yamam. 1955 (Ascomycetes, Capnodiales)

≡ Limacinia spinigera Höhn. 1907

≡Phaeochaetia spinigera (Höhn.) Bat. & Cif. 1962

≡Aithaloderma spinigerum (Höhn.) Höhn. 1918

≡Chaetothyrium spinigerum (Höhn.) W. Yamam. 1941

≡Microxyphium spinigerum (Höhn.) Speg. 1918

Notes: Reynolds (1982) treated this as a synonym of Trichomerium grandisporum.

Distribution: Asia.

Disease Note: Sooty mold.

Host: Multiple genera in multiple families.

Supporting Literature:

Reynolds, D.R. 1982. Foliocolous Ascomycetes. 4. The capnodiaceous genus Trichomerium Spegazzini Emend. Mycotaxon 14: 189-220.
