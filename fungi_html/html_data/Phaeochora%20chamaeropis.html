Nomenclature data for Phaeochora chamaeropis

Phaeochora steinheilii (Mont.) E. Müll. 1965 [1964] (Ascomycetes, Phyllachorales)

≡ Sphaeria steinheilii Mont. 1834

≡Anthostomella steinheilii (Mont.) Sacc. 1882

≡Sphaerodothis steinheilii (Mont.) P. Joly 1961

= Dothidea chamaeropis Cooke 1879

    ≡ Sphaerodothis chamaeropis (Cooke) Shear 1909

    ≡ Auerswaldia chamaeropis (Cooke) Cooke 1885

  ≡Phaeochora chamaeropis (Cooke) Höhn. 1909

= Sphaeropsis dothideoides Sacc. & Roum. 1881

    ≡ Haplosporella dothideoides (Sacc. & Roum.) Sacc. 1884

= Sordaria palmicola Auersw. 1867

Distribution: Europe.

Substrate: Leaves, petioles.

Disease Note: Lesions/tar spot.

Host: Chamaerops humilis (Arecaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Hyde, K.D., and Cannon, P.F. 1999. Fungi causing tar spots on palms. Mycol. Pap. 175: 1-114.
