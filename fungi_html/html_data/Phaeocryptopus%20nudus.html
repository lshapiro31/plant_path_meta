Nomenclature data for Phaeocryptopus nudus

Phaeocryptopus nudus (Peck) Petr. 1938 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

≡ Asterina nuda Peck 1885

≡Asterella nuda (Peck) Sacc. 1891

≡Adelopus nudus (Peck) Theiss. 1914

≡Cryptopus nudus (Peck) Theiss. 1914

Notes: Dimerosporium balsamicola (Peck) Ellis & Everh. is not synonomous with Phaeocryptopus nudus; as pointed out by Hahn, Peck intended to erect the distinctly different species in different genera (Winton et al 2007).

Distribution: Northern hemisphere.

Substrate: Needles.

Disease Note: Needle blight.

Host: Abies spp., Tsuga spp. (Pinaceae).
