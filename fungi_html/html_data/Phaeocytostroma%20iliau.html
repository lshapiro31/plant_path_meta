Nomenclature data for Phaeocytostroma iliau

Clypeoporthe iliau (Lyon) M.E. Barr 1978 (Ascomycetes, Diaporthales)

≡ Gnomonia iliau Lyon 1912

= Melanconium iliau Lyon 1912

  ≡Phaeocytostroma iliau (Lyon) Sivan. 1981

Notes: Iliau disease of sugarcane (Sivanesan & Holliday. C.M.I. Descr. 705. 1981).

Distribution: Southeastern United States; West Indies; South America; Australia; Mauritius; The Philippines; South Pacific Islands.

Substrate: On living culms and leaf sheath.

Disease Note: Leaf sheath spot.

Host: Primarily on Saccharum (Poaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Sivanesan, A., and Holliday, P. 1981. Clypeoporthe iliau. C.M.I. Descr. Pathog. Fungi Bact. 705: 1-2.
