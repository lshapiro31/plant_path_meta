Nomenclature data for Phaeocytostroma sacchari

Phaeocytostroma sacchari (Ellis & Everh.) B. Sutton 1964 (Ascomycetes, Incertae sedis)

≡ Trullula sacchari Ellis & Everh. 1892

= Melanconium sacchari Massee 1896

    ≡ Pleocyta sacchari (Massee) Petr. & Syd. 1927 [1926]

Distribution: Subtropical and tropical regions.

Substrate: Stallks.

Disease Note: Stalk rot, rind disease; causes severe losses in sugarcane.

Host: Saccharum officinarum and reports from other Poaceae.
