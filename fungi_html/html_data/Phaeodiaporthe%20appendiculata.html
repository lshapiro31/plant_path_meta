Nomenclature data for Phaeodiaporthe appendiculata

Phaeodiaporthe appendiculata (G.H. Otth) Lar. N. Vassiljeva 1994 (, )

≡ Diaporthe appendiculata G.H. Otth 1871 [1870]

≡Melanconiella appendiculata (G.H. Otth) Sacc. 1895

≡Melanconis appendiculata (G.H. Otth) Wehm. 1941

≡Prosthecium appendiculatum (G.H. Otth) M.E. Barr 1978

= Phaeodiaporthe keissleri Petr. 1920

Distribution: Central and eastern North America; Europe.

Substrate: Dead, corticated branches.

Disease Note: Saprobic.

Host: Acer (Sapindaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Voglmayr, H., and Jaklitsch, W.M. 2014. Stilbosporaceae resurrected: generic reclassification and speciation. Persoonia 33: 61-82.
