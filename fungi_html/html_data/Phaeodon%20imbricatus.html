≡ Hydnum imbricatum L. 1753

≡Phaeodon imbricatus (L. : Fr.) Schroet. 1888

= Hydnum subsquamosum Batsch 1783

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Terrestrial; around/above roots of host tree.

Disease Note: Ectomycorrhizal; not pathogenic.

Host: Conifers.
