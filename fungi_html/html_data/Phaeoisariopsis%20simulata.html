Nomenclature data for Phaeoisariopsis simulata

Pseudocercospora simulata (Ellis & Everh.) U. Braun & R.F. Castañeda 1989 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Cercospora simulata Ellis & Everh. 1885

≡Passalora simulata (Ellis & Everh.) U. Braun 1992

≡Phaeoisariopsis simulata (Ellis & Everh.) L.G. Br. & Morgan-Jones 1977

Distribution: Nearly cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Cassia spp., Chamaecrista nictitans, Senna spp. (Fabaceae).
