Nomenclature data for Phaeoramularia psidii-guajavae

Pseudocercospora psidii (Rangel) R.F. Castañeda & U. Braun 1989 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Cercospora psidii Rangel 1915

≡Cercosporidium psidii (Rangel) Sacc. 1931

= Phaeoramularia psidii-guajavae S.K. Singh, R.K. Chaudhary & Meenu 1997

Notes: Superfluous isonyms by (Rangel) Sarbajna & Chattopadh. 1991 and (Rangel) Y.L. Guo & X.J. Liu 1992.

Distribution: Asia, North America, South America (Brazil).

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Psidium spp. (Myrtaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Chupp, C. 1954. Monograph of the fungus genus Cercospora. Published by the Author, Ithaca, New York, 667 pages.

Crous, P.W., and Braun, U. 2003. Mycosphaerella and its anamorphs: 1. Names published in Cercospora and Passalora. Centraalbureau voor Schimmelcultures, Utrecht, 571 pages.
