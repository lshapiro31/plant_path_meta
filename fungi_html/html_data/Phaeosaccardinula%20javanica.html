Nomenclature data for Phaeosaccardinula javanica

Limacinula javanica (Zimm.) Höhn. 1909 (Ascomycetes, Capnodiales)

≡ Capnodium javanicum Zimm. 1902

≡Limacinia javanica (Zimm.) Sacc. & D. Sacc. 1905

≡Phaeosaccardinula javanica (Zimm.) W. Yamam. 1940

≡Chaetothyrium javanicum (Zimm.) Boedijn 1931

= Naetrocymbe diospyri Bat., Cif. & Maia 1963

Notes: The complicated nomenclatural history of this fungus is discussed in Reynolds (1971).

Distribution: Asia, North America (Caribbean).

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Sooty mold.

Host: Multiple genera in multiple families.

Supporting Literature:

Reynolds, D.R. 1971. The sooty mold ascomycete genus Limacinula. Mycologia 63: 1173-1209.
