Nomenclature data for Phakopsora zeae

Phakopsora zeae (Mains) Buriticá 1994 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

≡ Angiopsora zeae Mains 1938

≡Physopella zeae (Mains) Cummins & Ramachar 1958

Notes: Aecia unknown. Seymour & Miller (Florida State Hort. Soc. 84:124. 1974) reported that this fungus was eradicated from Florida. Laundon & Waterston (C.M.I. Descr. 5. 1964).

Distribution: FL; West Indies; Central and South America.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Rust; premature leaf death.

Host: Zea mays (Poaceae).

Supporting Literature:
