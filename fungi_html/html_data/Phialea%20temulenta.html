Nomenclature data for Phialea temulenta

Gloeotinia temulenta (Prill. & Delacr.) M. Wilson, Noble & E. Gray 1954 (Ascomycetes, Helotiales)

≡ Phialea temulenta Prill. & Delacr. 1892

≡Ciboria temulenta (Prill. & Delacr.) Prill. & Delacr. 1893

≡Sclerotinia temulenta (Prill. & Delacr.) Rehm 1903

≡Stromatinia temulenta (Prill. & Delacr.) Prill. & Delacr. 1897

≡Hymenoscyphus temulentus (Prill. & Delacr.) Kuntze 1898

= Phialia mucosa E.G. Gray 1942

= Sclerotinia secalincola Rehm 1900

= Endoconidium temulentum Prill. & Delacr. 1892

Distribution: Europe, North America.

Substrate: Seeds.

Disease Note: Blind seed disease.

Host: Festuca, Lolium, Poa, Secale and other Poaceae.

Supporting Literature:

Alderman, S.C. 1998. Gloeotinia temulenta and G. granigena, two distinct species. Mycologia 90: 422-426.
