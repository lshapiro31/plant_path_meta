Nomenclature data for Phialophora cinerescens

Phialophora cinerescens (Wollenw.) J.F.H. Beyma 1940 (Ascomycetes, Incertae sedis)

≡ Verticillium cinerescens Wollenw. 1930  Note: as 'cinerascens'

Distribution: Europe, Asia (China, Russia), North America (Canada, USA: OR, CO), South America (Colombia), New Zealand.

Substrate: Stem vascular tissue.

Disease Note: Wilt, chlorosis.

Host: Primary host: Carnation, Dianthus caryophyllus; secondary hosts, other Caryophyllaceae.
