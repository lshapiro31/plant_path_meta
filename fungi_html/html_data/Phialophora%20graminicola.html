Nomenclature data for Phialophora graminicola

Harpophora graminicola (Deacon) W. Gams 2000 (Ascomycetes, Incertae sedis)

≡ Phialophora radicicola var. graminicola Deacon 1974

≡Phialophora graminicola (Deacon) J. Walker 1980

= Gaeumannomyces cylindrosporus D. Hornby, D. Slope, R. Gutteridge & Sivan. 1977

Notes: Previously considered the causal agent of summer patch disease of turf (Smiley, 1987), this fungus appears to be a weak saprophyte with some biocontrol potential (Jackson & Landschoot, 1986).

Distribution: North America; Australia; Europe.

Substrate: Roots, leaves, and endophyte.

Disease Note: Summer patch disease of turf.

Host: Poaceae.
