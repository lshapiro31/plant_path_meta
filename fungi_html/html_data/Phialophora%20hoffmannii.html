Nomenclature data for Phialophora hoffmannii

Coniochaeta hoffmannii (J.F.H. Beyma) Z.U. Khan, Gené & Guarro 2013 (Ascomycetes, Sordariales)

≡ Margarinomyces hoffmannii J.F.H. Beyma 1938

≡Phialophora hoffmannii (J.F.H. Beyma) Schol-Schwarz 1970

Distribution: Europe, North America.

Substrate: Wood, leaves.

Disease Note: Associated with pruning wounds.

Host: Diverse hosts.
