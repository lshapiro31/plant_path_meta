Nomenclature data for Phialophora luteo-olivacea

Cadophora luteo-olivacea (J.F.H. Beyma) T.C. Harr. & McNew 2003 (Ascomycetes, Incertae sedis)

≡ Phialophora luteo-olivacea J.F.H. Beyma 1940

= Phialophora goidanichii Delitala 1952

Distribution: Europe (Germany, Italy, Switzerland), South America (Uruguay), Asia (Japan).

Substrate: Woody plant parts.

Disease Note: Wood streaking, dieback.

Host: Actinidia chinensis, A. deliciosa (Actinidiaceae), Malus sylvestris (Rosaceae), Vitis vinifera (Vitaceae).
