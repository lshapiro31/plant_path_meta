Nomenclature data for Phloeospora asiminae

Pseudocercospora asiminae (Ellis & Morgan) U. Braun & Crous 2008 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Phloeospora asiminae Ellis & Morgan 1887

      Variant spelling Phleospora asiminae Ellis & Morgan 1887

≡Rhopaloconidium asiminae (Ellis & Morgan) Petr. 1952

= Cercospora asiminae Ellis & Kellerm. 1887

    ≡ Mycocentrospora asiminae (Ellis & Kellerm.) Deighton 1972

    ≡ Centrospora asiminae (Ellis & Kellerm.) Deighton 1971

Distribution: North America (USA), Oceania(?) (Niue).

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Asimina triloba (Annonaceae); doubtful record from Annona squamosa in Niue.

Supporting Literature:
