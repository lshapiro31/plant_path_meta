Nomenclature data for Phloeospora crataegi

Pseudocercosporella crataegi (Ellis & Everh.) U. Braun 1994 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Cylindrosporium crataegi Ellis & Everh. 1894

≡Cercoseptoria crataegi (Ellis & Everh.) Davis 1929

≡Phloeospora crataegi (Ellis & Everh.) Höhn. 1924

= Cercosporella mirabilis Peck 1912 [1911]

Distribution: North America.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Crataegus spp. (Rosaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Braun, U. 1995. A Monograph of Cercosporella, Ramularia and Allied Genera (Phytopathogenic Hyphomycetes), Vol. 1. IHW-Verlag, 333 pages.
