Nomenclature data for Phloeospora idahoensis

Didymella festucae (H. Wegelin) L. Holm 1953 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

≡Didymosphaeria festucae H. Wegelin 1896

= Phloeospora idahoensis R. Sprague 1948

Notes: Stem eyespot disease (Corlett. Canad. J. Bot. 59:2028. 1981; Smith & Shoemaker. Canad. J. Bot. 52:2061. 1974).

Distribution: Temperate regions. From Phloeospora idahoensis - ID.

Substrate: On living culms.

Disease Note: Stem eye spot, leaf blight.

Host: Primarily on Festuca (Poaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Sivanesan, A. 1990. Didymella festucae. C.M.I. Descr. Pathog. Fungi Bact. 981: 1-2.
