Nomenclature data for Phloeosporella padi

Phloeosporella padi (Lib.) Arx 1961 (Ascomycetes, Helotiales)

Distribution: Widespread.

Disease Note: Shot hole, leaf spot.

Host: Especially Prunus spp. (Rosaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Nauta, M.M., and Spooner, B. 2000. British Dermateaceae: 4B. Dermateoideae genera B-E. Mycologist 14: 21-28.
