Nomenclature data for Phloeosporella ribis

Pseudocercosporella ribis (Davis) U. Braun 1998 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Cylindrosporium ribis Davis 1910

≡Cercoseptoria ribis (Davis) Dearn. & House 1940

≡Phloeosporella ribis (Davis) B. Sutton, Crous & Shamoun 1996

≡Cercospora septoriopsis Chupp 1954

Distribution: Africa (South Africa), Asia (Japan), North America.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Ribes spp. (Grossulariaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Braun, U. 1998. A Monograph of Cercosporella, Ramularia and Allied Genera (Phytopathogenic Hyphomycetes). IHW-Verlag 2: 337.
