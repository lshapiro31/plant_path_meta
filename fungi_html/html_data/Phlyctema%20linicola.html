Nomenclature data for Phlyctema linicola

Septoria linicola (Speg.) Garassini 1938 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Phlyctema linicola Speg. 1910

      Variant spelling Phlyctaena linicola Speg.

= Mycosphaerella linicola Naumov 1926

= Sphaerella linorum Wollenw. 1938

    ≡ Mycosphaerella linorum (Wollenw.) Garcia-Rada 1942

Distribution: Worldwide in flax-growing regions.

Substrate: On living leaves and seeds.

Disease Note: Pasmo disease.

Host: Linum (Linaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Priest, M.J. 2006. Fungi of Australia. Septoria. ABRS, Canberra; CSIRO Publishing, Melbourne, 259 pages.

Sivanesan, A., and Holliday, P. 1981. Mycosphaerella linicola. C.M.I. Descr. Pathog. Fungi Bact. 709: 1-2.
