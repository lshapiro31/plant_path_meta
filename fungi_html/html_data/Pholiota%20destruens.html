Nomenclature data for Pholiota destruens

Pholiota populnea (Pers. : Fr.) Kuyper & Tjall.-Beukers 1986 (Basidiomycetes, Agaricales)

≡ Agaricus populneus Pers. 1828

≡Hemipholiota populnea (Pers.) Bon 1986

= Agaricus destruens Brond. 1829

  ≡Pholiota destruens (Brond.) Gill. 1876

Notes: For additional synonyms, see Index Fungorum.

Distribution: Temperate northern hemisphere.

Substrate: Wood.

Disease Note: Butt/heart rot; also saprobic on recently dead trees and logs.

Host: Populus spp. (Salicaceae), Betula spp. (Betulaceae); questionable report from Pseudotsuga menziesii (Pinaceae).
