Nomenclature data for Pholiota squarrosa

Pholiota squarrosa (Vahl) P. Kumm 1871 (Basidiomycetes, Agaricales)

≡ Agaricus squarrosus Vahl 1770

Notes: For additional synonyms, see Index Fungorum. ------ O. Schmidt, O. Gaiser, D. Dujesiefken" Molecular identification of decay fungi in the wood of urban trees" European Journal of Forest Research May 2012, Volume 131, Issue 3, pp 885-891. ------ http://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/10.1002/ejoc.200700090/full

Distribution: Temperate North America; Europe.

Substrate: On wood.

Disease Note: Root rot; also saprobic.

Host: A wide range of hard and soft woods.

Supporting Literature:

Hinds, T.E., and Laurent, T.H. 1978. Common aspen diseases found in Alaska. Pl. Dis. Reporter 62: 972-975.
