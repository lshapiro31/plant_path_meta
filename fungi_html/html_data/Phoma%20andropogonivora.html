Nomenclature data for Phoma andropogonivora

Phoma andropogonivora (R. Sprague & Rogerson) Gruyter 2002 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

≡ Phyllosticta andropogonivora R. Sprague & Rogerson 1958

≡Ascochyta andropogonivora (R. Sprague & Rogerson) Morgan-Jones 1991

Distribution: North America (USA).

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Andropogon gerardii and Schizachyrium scoparium (Poaceae).
