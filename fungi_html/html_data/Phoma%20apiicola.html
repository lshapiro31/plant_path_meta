Nomenclature data for Phoma apiicola

Subplenodomus apiicola (Kleb.) Gruyter, Aveskamp & Verkley 2012 (, )

≡ Phoma apiicola Kleb. 1910

Notes: From Phoma apiicola - Phoma apiicola 'macroforma' and 'microforma' Goossens 1928 nom. inval.

Distribution: Temperate North America; Europe, Australia, Asia

Substrate: Roots, petioles, soil-borne, seed-borne.

Disease Note: Root rot, scab, Phoma rot, crown rot (black neck), seedling canker.

Host: On celeriac and celery, Apium graveolens, and other Apiaceae.
