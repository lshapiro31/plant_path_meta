Nomenclature data for Phoma astragali

Stagonosporopsis astragali (Cooke & Harkn.) Aveskamp, Gruyter & Verkley 2010 (Ascomycetes, Incertae sedis)

≡ Phoma astragali Cooke & Harkn. 1885  Note: Not Ellis & Kellerm., invalid herbarium name.

Distribution: Central Asia, Europe (Hungary, Portugal), North America.

Substrate: Stems.

Disease Note: Probably an opportunistic parasite.

Host: Astragalus (Fabaceae).
