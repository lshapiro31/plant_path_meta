Nomenclature data for Phoma australis

Neofusicoccum eucalyptorum (Crous, H. Sm. ter & M.J. Wingf.) Crous, Slippers & A.J.L. Phillips 2006 (Incertae sedis, Incertae sedis)

≡ Fusicoccum eucalyptorum Crous, H. Sm. ter & M.J. Wingf. 2001

= Phoma australis Cooke 1886

    ≡ Idiocercus australis (Cooke) H.J. Swart 1988

= Botryosphaeria eucalyptorum Crous, H. Sm. ter & M.J. Wingf. 2001

Distribution: Africa (South Africa, Zimbabwe), Australia, South America (Uruguay).

Substrate: Leaves, stems, branches.

Disease Note: Canker.

Host: Eucalyptus spp., Myrceugenia glaucsens, Myrrhinium atropurpureum var. octandrum, Blepharcalyx salicifolius (Myrtaceae), Fraxinus excelsior (Oleaceae).
