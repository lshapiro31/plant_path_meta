Nomenclature data for Phoma belgica

Boeremia exigua var. linicola (Naumov & Vassiljevsky) Aveskamp, Gruyter & Verkley 2010 (, )

≡ Ascochyta linicola Naumov & Vassiljevsky 1926

≡Phoma exigua f. sp. linicola (Naumov & Vassiljevsky) Malc. & E.G. Gray 1968

≡Phoma exigua var. linicola (Naumov & Vassiljevsky) P.W.T. Maas 1965

= Phoma belgica Cash 1972

  [=  Phoma linicola É.J. Marchal & Verpl. 1926 - illegitimate later homonym, not included in search]  Note: Not Bubák 1914 nor Naumov 1926.

= Diplodina lini Moesz & Smarods 1930

Distribution: Europe, New Zealand, probably throughout host range.

Substrate: Base of stems, dead stalks.

Disease Note: Seed-borne, damping-off, foot rot.

Host: Primary host flax, Linum usitatissimum (Linaceae); sometimes other plants.
