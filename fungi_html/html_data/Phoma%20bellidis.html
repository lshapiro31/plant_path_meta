Nomenclature data for Phoma bellidis

Didymella bellidis (Neerg.) Qian Chen & L. Cai 2015 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

≡ Phoma bellidis Neerg. 1950

= Phyllosticta bellidicola Nelen 1966

[= Phyllosticta bellidis Sawada 1958 - illegitimate later homonym, not included in search]

= Phyllosticta bellidis Bond.-Mont. 1923

Notes: From Phyllosticta bellidis - Not Bond.-Mont. 1923, nor Hollós 1926. From Phyllosticta bellidis - Not Hollós 1926 nor Sawada 1958. From Phoma bellidis - Phoma bellidis Neerg. 1941 as 'Phoma (Phyllosticta)'. nom. inval.. (Danish diagnosis). Oldest name not in correct genus.

Distribution: Europe, Asia (China).

Substrate: Seeds, seedlings, leaves.

Disease Note: Seed death, damping off, found in association with leaf necroses.

Host: Bellis perennis (Asteraceae), Angelica dahurica (Apiaceae), Eleocharis dulcis (Cyperaceae).
