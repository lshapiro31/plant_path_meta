Nomenclature data for Phoma carteri

Querciphoma minuta (J.C. Carter) Crous & P.M. Kirk 2017 (, )

≡ Pyrenochaeta minuta J.C. Carter 1941

≡Coniothyrium carteri (Gruyter & Boerema) Verkley & Gruyter 2012

≡Phoma carteri Gruyter & Boerema 2002

≡Querciphoma carteri (Gruyter & Boerema) Crous 2017

Notes: Querciphoma carteri (Gruyter & Boerema) Crous is a superfluous combination (Art. 52.1) based on the nom. nov. Phoma carteri Gruyter & Boerema (2002). Unlike in Phoma and Coniothyrium, use of the oldest epithet is not blocked in Querciphoma and it ought to have been adopted.

Distribution: North America and Europe (Germany, Netherlands).

Substrate: Bark, wood.

Disease Note: Probably not responsible for dieback, but only an opportunistic pathogen.

Host: Quercus spp. (Fagaceae).
