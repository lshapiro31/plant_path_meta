Nomenclature data for Phoma chrysanthemicola

Paraphoma chrysanthemicola (Hollós) Gruyter, Aveskamp & Verkley 2010 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

≡ Phoma chrysanthemicola Hollós 1907  Note: Sensu lato. Synonymy with Phyllosticta leucanthemi Speg. suggested by van der Aa & Vanev 2002, but Boerema disagrees because P. chrysanthemicola has never been isolated from leaves.

= Phoma radicis-oxycocci Ternetz 1907

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Soil, roots, stems.

Disease Note: Leaf spot, yellowing, defoliation; also saprobic.

Host: Various herbaceous plants especially Asteraceae.
