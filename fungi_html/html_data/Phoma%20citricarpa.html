Nomenclature data for Phoma citricarpa

Phyllosticta citricarpa (McAlpine) Aa 1973 (Ascomycetes, Dothideales)

≡ Phoma citricarpa McAlpine 1899

≡Phyllostictina citricarpa (McAlpine) Petr. 1953

= Phyllosticta circumsepta Sacc. 1914

= Guignardia citricarpa Kiely 1948

[= Phyllosticta citricola Hori ex Hara 1917 - illegitimate later homonym, not included in search]  Note: Not Sacc. 1915. Not Hori 1913, invalid name, without description.

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Fruits, leaves.

Disease Note: Citrus black spot.

Host: Apparently restricted to Citrus spp. (Rutaceae) (Baayen et al. 2002). Records on other hosts are doubtful, and presumably refer to Phyllosticta capitalensis (=Guignardia mangiferae).
