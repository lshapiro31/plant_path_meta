Nomenclature data for Phoma clematidina

Calophoma clematidina (Thüm.) Qian Chen & L. Cai 2015 (, )

= Diplodina clematidina Fautrey & Roum. 1892

= Phyllosticta clematidis Brunaud 1889

    ≡ Phyllosticta clematidicola Brunaud 1898  Note: Superfluous name.

[= Phyllosticta clematidis Ellis & Dearn. 1893 - illegitimate later homonym, not included in search]

= Ascochyta davidiana Kabát & Bubák 1904

= Ascochyta indusiata Bres. 1896

= Ascochyta vitalbae Briard & Har. 1891

    ≡ Diplodina vitalbae (Briard & Har.) Allesch. 1900[1901]

= Didymella vitalbina Petr. 1940

Notes: From Phyllosticta clematidis - Not Brunaud 1889. From Phyllosticta clematidis - Not Ellis & Dearn. 1893.

Distribution: North America, Australia, Asia, Europe.

Substrate: Naturally wilting leaves, stems.

Disease Note: Leaf spots, stem lesions. Opportunistic parasite.

Host: Clematis spp. (Ranunculaceae).
