Nomenclature data for Phoma coonsii

Chaetosphaeronema coonsii (Boerema & Loer.) Gruyter, Aveskamp & Verkley 2010 (Ascomycetes, Incertae sedis)

≡ Phoma coonsii Boerema & Loer. 1985

Distribution: Report from USA appears to be incorrect (de Gruyter et al. 2010), Asia (Japan).

Substrate: Bark and wood.

Disease Note: Reported as bark canker but probably only an opportunistic parasite (Boerema et al. 2004).

Host: Apple, Malus pumila (Rosaceae).

Supporting Literature:
