Nomenclature data for Phoma destructiva

Remotididymella destructiva (Plowr.) Valenzuela-Lopez, Cano, Crous, Guarro & Stchigel 2017 (, )

≡ Phoma destructiva Plowr. 1881

≡Diplodina destructiva (Plowr.) Petr. 1921

≡Phoma destructiva var. destructiva Plowr. 1881

= Phyllosticta lycopersici Peck 1887

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Fruits, leaves, and stems. Seed-borne for paprika.

Disease Note: Fruit rot, leaf and stem blight.

Host: Tomato Solanum lycopersicum and paprika, Capsicum annuum (Solanaceae), Ziziphus jujuba (Rhamnaceae).
