Nomenclature data for Phoma dictamnicola

Heterophoma nobilis (Kabát & Bubák) Qian Chen & L. Cai 2015 (, )

≡ Ascochyta nobilis Kabát & Bubák 1904

≡Phoma dictamnicola Boerema, Gruyter & Noordel. 1992

= Diplodina dictamni Kabát & Bubák 1912

= Phyllosticta dictamni Fairm. 1910

Notes: Phoma dictamnicola is a nom. nov. - non Phoma nobilis Sacc. 1882.

Distribution: Europe, Asia, North America (USA: NY).

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Dictamnus spp. (Rutaceae).
