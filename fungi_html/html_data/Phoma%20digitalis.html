Nomenclature data for Phoma digitalis

Didymella molleriana (G. Winter) Qian Chen & L. Cai 2015 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

≡ Ascochyta molleriana G. Winter 1883[1884]

      Variant spelling Ascochyta moelleriana G. Winter

= Phoma digitalis Boerema 1979

Distribution: Europe, New Zealand, probably throughout host range.

Substrate: Leaves, seed-borne.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Digitalis spp. (Plantaginaceae).

Supporting Literature:
