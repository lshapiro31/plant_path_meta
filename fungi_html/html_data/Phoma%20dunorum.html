Nomenclature data for Phoma dunorum

Paraboeremia putaminum (Speg.) Qian Chen & L. Cai 2015 (, )

≡ Phoma putaminum Speg. 1881

≡Aposphaeria putaminum (Speg.) Sacc. 1884

≡Coniothyrium putaminum (Speg.) Kuntze 1898

= Phoma dunorum Houten 1939

= Phoma radicicola McAlpine 1902

Notes: From Phoma radicicola - Not Maubl. 1905. From Phoma putaminum - Not Hollós 1907.

Distribution: North America, South America (Venezuela), Europe, Australia, Africa (Nigeria), SE Asia.

Substrate: Soil, roots.

Disease Note: Saprophyte, may act as opportunistic parasite on roots.

Host: Various herbaceous and woody plants.
