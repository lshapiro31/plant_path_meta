= Leptophoma doliolum Höhn. 1915

    ≡ Plenodomus doliolum (Höhn.) Höhn. 1918

    ≡ Phoma hoehnelii Kesteren 1972

  [≡ Plenodomus doliolum (Höhn.) Petr. 1923 - illegitimate later homonym, not included in search]

= Leptosphaeria doliolum subsp. errabunda Boerema, Gruyter & Kesteren 1994

Notes: From Phoma errabunda - Year changed from 1839.

Distribution: From Phoma acuta subsp. errabunda - North America; Europe.

Substrate: Stems.

Disease Note: From Phoma acuta subsp. errabunda - Plurivorous, necrophyte.

Host: Asteraceae and Apiaceae, especially Solidago in North America and Angelica in Europe.
