Nomenclature data for Phoma eucalyptica

Didymella eucalyptica (Sacc.) Qian Chen & L. Cai 2015 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

≡ Phoma eucalyptica Sacc. 1884

≡Coniothyrium eucalypti Thüm. 1880

≡Peyronellaea eucalyptica (Sacc.) Aveskamp, Gruyter & Verkley 2010

Notes: Phoma eucalyptica Sacc. (1884), based on Coniothyrium eucalypti Thüm (1879) is a nom. nov. created to avoid conflict with Phoma eucalypti Cooke & J. Kickx f. (1881). However, the epithet 'eucalypti' should have been adopted by Aveskamp et al. (2010) upon transfer to Peyronellaea, an error perpetuated by Chen et al. (2015) upon transfer to Didymella, rendering both names nomenclaturally superfluous and therefore illegimate (Art. 52.1).

Distribution: Asia, Australia.

Substrate: Seeds, shoots, and leaves.

Disease Note: Opportunistic parasite. Associated with shoot wilt, leaf necroses, blisters.

Host: Myrtaceae.
