Nomenclature data for Phoma foveata

Boeremia foveata (Foister) Aveskamp, Gruyter & Verkley 2010 (, )

≡ Phoma foveata Foister 1940 [1943]

≡Phoma exigua f. sp. foveata (Foister) Malc. & E.G. Gray 1968

≡Phoma exigua var. foveata (Foister) Boerema 1967

≡Phoma solanicola f. foveata (Foister) Malc. 1958

Distribution: Europe, indigenous to South America.

Substrate: Tubers, stalks.

Disease Note: Tuber lesions (gangrene), brown stalk rot.

Host: Solanum tuberosum (Solanaceae), Chenopodium quinoa (Amarathaceae).
