Notes: From Peyronellaea veronensis - No latin diagnosis. From Phyllosticta berberidis - Not Rabenh. 1854. From Phoma hominis - A. Agostini & Tredici 1935 invalid (no Latin description). From Phoma polymorpha - As 'n. nom.' for Alternaria polymorpha L. Planchon. From Peyronellaea stipae - Lacoste 1955 invalid, no Latin description. From Peyronellaea zhdanovii - As 'zhdanovi'.

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Soil, dead seed coats, animal tissues, and inorganic material (e.g. paint, chemical products) (plurivorous).

Disease Note: Opportunistic pathogen. Found in association with blights, leaf spots, fruit rots.

Host: Various plant genera.
