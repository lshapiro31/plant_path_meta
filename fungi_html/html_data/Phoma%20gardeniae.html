Nomenclature data for Phoma gardeniae

Didymella gardeniae (S. Chandra & Tandon) Qian Chen & L. Cai 2015 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

≡ Pyrenochaeta gardeniae S. Chandra & Tandon 1966

≡Peyronellaea gardeniae (S. Chandra & Tandon) Aveskamp, Gruyter & Verkley 2010

≡Phoma gardeniae (S. Chandra & Tandon) Boerema 1980

Distribution: Asia (India), Europe (Netherlands).

Substrate: Soil, airborne.

Disease Note: Opportunistic pathogen.

Host: Woody and herbaceous plants.
