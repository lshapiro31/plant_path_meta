Notes: Oldest epithet not in correct genus. From Phoma glaucii - In herb., ined. From Diplodina chelidonii - Not Naumov 1915. From Diplodina chelidonii - Not Ade 1924.. From Ascochyta dicentrae - Boerema 2004 has 'dicentra'. From Diplodina glaucii - Boerema 2004 has 'glauci'.

Distribution: Europe.

Substrate: Living and dead leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Chelidonium, Corydalis, Dicentra, Glaucium spp. (Papaveraceae).
