Nomenclature data for Phoma gossypiicola

Nothophoma gossypiicola (Gruyter) Qian Chen & L. Cai 2015 (, )

≡ Phoma gossypiicola Gruyter 2002

≡Ascochyta gossypii Woron. 1914 [1914]

[= Ascochyta gossypii Syd. & P. Syd. 1916 - illegitimate later homonym, not included in search]

Notes: Phoma gossypiicola Gruyter is a replacement name for the illegitimate later homonym Ascochyta gossypii Woron. (non Sacc. 1880).

Distribution: Cosmopolitan, throughout host range.

Substrate: Living leaves, stems, seed-borne; soil-borne.

Disease Note: Leaf spot, stem canker, wet weather blight.

Host: Cotton, Gossypium spp. (Malvaceae). May occur on other crop hosts, but records may also be Phoma pomorum Th√ºm or Phoma exigua Desm.
