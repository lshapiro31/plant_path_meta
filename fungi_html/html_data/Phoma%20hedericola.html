Nomenclature data for Phoma hedericola

Boeremia hedericola (Durieu & Mont.) Aveskamp, Gruyter & Verkley 2010 (, )

≡ Phyllosticta hedericola Durieu & Mont. 1849

≡Phyllosticta destructiva f. hederae Oudem. ex Sacc. 1884

≡Phoma hedericola (Durieu & Mont.) Boerema 1976

= Phyllosticta destructiva var. hederae Desm. 1859  Note: Not (Durieu & Mont.) Oudem. 1873. Synonymy suggested by Boerema 2004 (not seen); could also be a synonym of Phyllosticta destructiva = Phoma exigua var. exigua.

[≡Phyllosticta destructiva var. hederae (Durieu & Mont.) Oudem. 1873 - illegitimate later homonym, not included in search]  Note: Not Desm. 1859.

Distribution: Europe; possibly throughout host range.

Substrate: Leaves and stems.

Disease Note: Associated with necroses on leaves and stems; leaf spot.

Host: Hedera spp. (Araliaceae).
