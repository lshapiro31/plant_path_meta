Nomenclature data for Phoma heteromorphospora

Heterospora chenopodii (Westend.) Gruyter, Aveskamp & Verkley 2012 (, )

≡ Phyllosticta chenopodii Westend. 1857

≡Phoma heteromorphospora Aa & Kesteren 1980

≡Septoria westendorpii G. Winter 1887

[≡Phoma variospora Aa & Kesteren 1979 [1978] - illegitimate later homonym, not included in search]

Notes: From Phoma variospora - Nov. 1979 not Shreem. July 1979 From Phyllosticta chenopodii - not Sacc. 1884

Distribution: Temperate North America; Europe.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Chenopodium spp. (Amaranthaceae).
