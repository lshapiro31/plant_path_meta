Nomenclature data for Phoma heveae

Phomopsis heveae (Petch) Boedijn 1929 (Ascomycetes, Diaporthales)

≡ Phoma heveae Petch 1906

Notes: New combination needed in Diaporthe.

Distribution: Asia, South America (Brazil); rubber growing areas.

Substrate: Seedlings.

Disease Note: Dieback.

Host: Hevea brasiliensis (Euphorbiaceae).

Supporting Literature:
