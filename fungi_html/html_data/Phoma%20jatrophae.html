Nomenclature data for Phoma jatrophae

Ascochyta medicaginicola var. medicaginicola Qian Chen & L. Cai 2015 (Ascomycetes, Incertae sedis)

≡ Phoma medicaginis Malbr. & Roum. 1886

≡Phoma medicaginis var. medicaginis Malbr. & Roum. 1886

= Phoma cuscutae Negru & Verona 1966

= Phoma jatropae Shreem. 1978

    Variant spelling Phoma jatrophae Shreem. 1978

Notes: Ascochyta medicaginicola var. medicaginicola is a nom. nov., as the epithet "medicaginis" is already occupied in Ascochyta. From Phoma medicaginis var. medicaginis - Not Fuckel 1870, a name wrongly attributed to Fuckel; see Morgan-Jones 1987.

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Seed-borne, stems.

Disease Note: Black stem; also saprobic.

Host: Medicago spp., Melilotus spp. (Fabaceae) and other plants.
