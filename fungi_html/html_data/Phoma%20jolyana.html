Nomenclature data for Phoma jolyana

Didymella musae (P. Joly) Qian Chen & L. Cai 2015 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

≡ Peyronellaea musae P. Joly 1961

≡Phoma jolyana Piroz. & Morgan-Jones 1968

≡Phoma jolyana var. jolyana Piroz. & Morgan-Jones 1968

≡Phoma jolyi M. Morelet 1968

[≡Phoma musae (P. Joly) Boerema, Dorenb. & Kesteren 1965 - illegitimate later homonym, not included in search]

= Peyronellaea nainiensis Tandon & Bilgrami 1961

Notes: From Phoma musae - Not (Cooke) Sacc. 1884 nor C.W. Carp. 1919[1918] From Peyronellaea nainiensis - Boerema has 'nainensis'. From Phoma jolyana - Nom. nov. From Phoma jolyi - Nom. nov.

Distribution: Widespread based on scattered reports.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot. Probably a secondary invader of diseased plant tissue.

Host: Diverse hosts.
