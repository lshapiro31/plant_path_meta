Nomenclature data for Phoma juglandina

Diaporthe juglandina (Fuckel) Nitschke 1870 (Ascomycetes, Diaporthales)

= Phoma juglandina Sacc. 1879

    ≡ Phomopsis juglandina (Sacc.) Höhn. 1906

Distribution: Asia (China, Pakistan), Europe, North America (USA: CA).

Substrate: Limbs, twigs.

Disease Note: Twig blight.

Host: Juglans spp. (Juglandaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Gomes, R.R., Glienke, C., Videira, S.I.R., Lombard, L., Groenewald, J.Z., and Crous, P.W. 2013. Diaporthe: a genus of endophytic, saprobic and plant pathogenic fungi. Persoonia 31: 1-41.

Sun, J. 2013. Identification of walnut twig blight in Liaoning province. J. Fruit Sci. 30: 669-671.
