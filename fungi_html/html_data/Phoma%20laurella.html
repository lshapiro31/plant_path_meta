Nomenclature data for Phoma laurella

Diaporthe nobilis Sacc. & Speg. 1878 (Ascomycetes, Diaporthales)

= Phoma laurella Sacc. 1882

    ≡ Phomopsis laurella (Sacc.) Traverso 1906

Distribution: Asia, Europe, North America.

Substrate: Leaves, branches, fruit.

Disease Note: Leaf spot, fruit rot.

Host: Diverse dicot hosts.

Supporting Literature:
