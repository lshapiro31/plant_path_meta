= Phoma polyanthis Died. 1916

[= Phoma terrestris R.K. Saksena, Nand & A.K. Sarbhoy 1966 - illegitimate later homonym, not included in search]  Note: Not H.N. Hansen 1929

Distribution: Worldwide in warm temperate and subtropical regions.

Substrate: Leaves, soil-borne.

Disease Note: Leaf spot, opportunistic pathogen, may cause blight and dieback. Often in association with cysts of phytonematodes.

Host: Various plants (plurivorous).

Supporting Literature:
