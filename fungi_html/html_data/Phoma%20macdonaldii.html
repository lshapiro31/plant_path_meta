Nomenclature data for Phoma macdonaldii

Plenodomus lindquistii (Frezzi) Gruyter, Aveskamp & Verkley 2012 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

≡ Leptosphaeria lindquistii Frezzi 1968

= Phoma macdonaldii Boerema 1970

Distribution: Europe (France, Romania), North America (Canada, USA), South America (Argentina). Probably wherever host is cultivated.

Substrate: Stems, petioles, leaves, inflorescences.

Disease Note: Lesions, black stem, black spot.

Host: Helianthus annuus (Asteraceae).
