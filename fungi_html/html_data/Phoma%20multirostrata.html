Nomenclature data for Phoma multirostrata

Ectophoma multirostrata (P.N. Mathur, S.K. Menon & Thirum.) Valenzuela-Lopez, Cano, Crous, Guarro & Stchigel 2017 (, )

≡ Sphaeronaema multirostratum P.N. Mathur, S.K. Menon & Thirum. 1959

≡Phoma multirostrata var. multirostrata (P.N. Mathur, S.K. Menon & Thirum.) Dorenb. & Boerema 1973

= Phoma ushtrina T.R.N. Rai & J.K. Misra 1981

Notes: Ectophoma multirostrata published online 2017. Synonomy of Phoma ushtrina follows Boerema et al. (2004).

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Leaves, stems, soil.

Disease Note: Lesions.

Host: Cucumis sativus (Cucurbitaceae).
