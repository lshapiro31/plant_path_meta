Plenodomus lingam (Tode) Höhn. 1911 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

≡ Sphaeria lingam Tode : Fr. 1791

≡Phoma lingam (Tode : Fr.) Desm. 1849

= Aposphaeria brassicae Thüm. 1880

    ≡ Phoma brassicae Thüm. 1884

= Depazea brassicicola Desm. 1826

    ≡ Depazea vagans var. brassicae J.J. Kickx 1835

    ≡ Septoria brassicae Westend. & Wallays 1847

    ≡ Phyllosticta brassicae Westend. 1851  Note: Erroneously listed as a transfer from Sphaeria brassicae Curr. 1859

= Phoma densiuscula Sacc. & Roum. 1884

= Sphaeria maculans Desm. 1846

    ≡ Leptosphaeria maculans (Desm.) Ces. & De Not. 1863

  [=  Sphaeria maculans Sowerby ex Berk. & Broome 1852 - illegitimate later homonym, not included in search]  Note: Not Desm. Not a taxonomic synonym of L. maculans.

= Phoma napobrassicae Rostr. 1892

    ≡ Phoma lingam var. napobrassicae (Rostr.) Grove 1935

= Plenodomus rabenhorstii Preuss 1851

= Sclerotium sphaeriaeforme Lib. 1834

= Phyllosticta turritis Lobik 1928

Notes: From Plenodomus rabenhorstii - Type of Phoma sect. Plenodomus (Preuss) Boerema et al. 1981. From Sphaeria lingam - Sanctioned by Fries 1823. From Phoma lingam - Type of Phoma sect. Plenodomus. From Sphaeria maculans - Not Sowerby ex Berk. & Broome 1852.

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Living leaves, stems, plant residues, seed-borne.

Disease Note: Black leg, canker, dry rot, leaf spot.

Host: Brassica spp. (Brassicaceae) and numerous other herbaceous and woody plants.
