Nomenclature data for Phoma negriana

Didymella negriana (Thüm.) Qian Chen & L. Cai 2015 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

≡ Phoma negriana Thüm. 1878

≡Phyllosticta negriana (Thüm.) Allesch. 1898(1901)

= Phyllosticta vitis Sacc. 1878

Notes: From Phoma negriana - As 'negrianum'.

Distribution: Southern Europe.

Substrate: Leaves, fruits, stems.

Disease Note: Associated with necroses of leaves, fruits or stems.

Host: Vitis vinifera (Vitaceae).
