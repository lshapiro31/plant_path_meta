Nomenclature data for Phoma nigricans

Didymella nigricans (P.R. Johnst. & Boerema) Qian Chen & L. Cai 2015 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

≡ Phoma nigricans P.R. Johnst. & Boerema 1982 [1981]

≡Peyronellaea australis Aveskamp, Gruyter & Verkley 2010

Notes: From Peyronellaea australis - Nom. nov. since the epithet 'nigricans' is occupied in Peyronellaea.

Distribution: New Zealand, probably also common in Australia.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Saprophyte.

Host: Various woody and herbaceous plants.

Supporting Literature:

Aveskamp, M. , de Gruyter, H., Woudenberg, J. , Verkley, G., and Crous, P.W. 2010. Highlights of the Didymellaceae: A polyphasic approach to characterise Phoma and related pleosporalean genera. Stud. Mycol. 65: 1-64.
