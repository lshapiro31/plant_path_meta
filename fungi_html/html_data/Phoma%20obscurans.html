Nomenclature data for Phoma obscurans

Phomopsis obscurans (Ellis & Everh.) B. Sutton 1965 (Ascomycetes, Diaporthales)

≡ Phoma obscurans Ellis & Everh. 1894

≡Dendrophoma obscurans (Ellis & Everh.) H.W. Anderson 1920

≡Phyllosticta obscurans (Ellis & Everh.) Tassi 1902

≡Sphaeropsis obscurans (Ellis & Everh.) Kuntze 1898

= Sphaeronaemella fragariae F. Stevens & Peterson 1916

Notes: New combination needed in Diaporthe.

Distribution: Widespread.

Disease Note: Leaf blight, fruit rot.

Host: Fragaria spp. (Rosaceae). One report from Photinia.
