Nomenclature data for Phoma pereupyrena

Ectophoma pomi (A.S. Horne) Valenzuela-Lopez, Cano, Crous, Guarro & Stchigel 2017 (, )

≡ Polyopeus pomi A.S. Horne 1920

= Phoma pereupyrena Gruyter, Noordel. & Boerema 1993

Notes: Published online 2017.

Distribution: Eurasia.

Substrate: Fruits and leaves.

Disease Note: Opportunistic parasite.

Host: Woody and herbaceous plants.
