Nomenclature data for Phoma protuberans

Didymella protuberans (Lév.) Qian Chen & L. Cai 2015 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

≡ Phoma protuberans Lév. 1846

≡Peyronellaea protuberans (Lév.) Aveskamp, Gruyter & Verkley 2010

= Didymella alectorolophi Rehm 1923  Note: From Peyronellaea alectorolophi - Peyronellaea has an asexual state type.

    ≡ Peyronellaea alectorolophi (Rehm) Aveskamp, Gruyter & Verkley 2010

= Phoma alectorolophi Boerema, Gruyter & Noordel. 1997

= Phoma carotae Died. 1912

= Phyllosticta lycii Ellis & Kellerm. 1883

= Phoma obtusa Fuckel 1870 [1869 & 1870]

    ≡ Peyronellaea obtusa (Fuckel) Aveskamp, Gruyter & Verkley 2010

Distribution: Europe, North America, probably throughout host range.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Lycium spp. (Solanaceae), Daucus carota (Apiaceae), Spinacia oleracea (Amaranthaceae), Rhinanthus spp. (Orobanchaceae).
