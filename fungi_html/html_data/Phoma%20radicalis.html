  [≡ Phoma radicicola Maubl. 1905 - illegitimate later homonym, not included in search]  Note: Not McAlpine 1902.

= Phoma radicis-callunae R.W. Rayner 1922

= Phoma suecica J.F.H. Beyma 1942

Notes: From Phoma bulbicola - Not Tassi 1900. From Phoma bulbicola - Not Hollós 1907.

Distribution: Temperate North America; northern Africa; Europe, Asia.

Substrate: Ubiquitous in soil, on roots, or other organic substrates (meat, gelatin).

Disease Note: Opportunistic root pathogen.

Host: Diverse hosts.
