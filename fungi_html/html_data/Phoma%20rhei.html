Nomenclature data for Phoma rhei

Didymella rhei (Ellis & Everh.) Qian Chen & L. Cai 2015 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

≡ Ascochyta rhei Ellis & Everh. 1893

≡Phoma rhei (Ellis & Everh.) Aa & Boerema 2002

= Phyllosticta halstediana Allesch. 1898 [1901]

[≡Phyllosticta rhei Ellis & Everh. 1889 - illegitimate later homonym, not included in search]

Notes: From Phyllosticta rhei - Not Roum. 1887. Complementary description listed as separate entry, see Boerema 2004. From Ascochyta rhei - Authors corrected from (Ellis & Everh.) Ellis & Everh.; based on an illegitimate name, Art. 58.1.

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Rheum spp. (Polygonaceae).
