Nomenclature data for Phoma tracheiphila

Plenodomus tracheiphilus (Petri) Gruyter, Aveskamp & Verkley 2012 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

≡ Deuterophoma tracheiphila Petri 1929

≡Bakerophoma tracheiphila (Petri) Cif. 1946

≡Phoma tracheiphila (Petri) L.A. Kantsch. & Gikaschvili 1948

Distribution: Europe (Greece), Asia (Russia).

Substrate: Twigs, branches, around leaf scars.

Disease Note: Mal secco disease, vascular pathogen. Red strands in stem xylem, veinal chlorosis, wilt and shedding of leaves, dieback of twigs and branches. Infection through stomata and wounds.

Host: Lemons and other Citrus spp. (Rutaceae).
