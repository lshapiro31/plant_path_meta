= Phyllosticta typhina Sacc. & Malbr. 1880

    ≡ Phoma typhina (Sacc. & Malbr.) Aa 2002

Notes: From Phoma typhae - Not Oudem. 1902.

Distribution: Europe, and probably throughout host range.

Substrate: Living and dead leaves, stems.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Typha latifolia, Typha angustifolia (Typhaceae).
