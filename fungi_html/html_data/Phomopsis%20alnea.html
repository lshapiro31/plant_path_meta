Nomenclature data for Phomopsis alnea

Diaporthe alnea Fuckel 1870 (Ascomycetes, Diaporthales)

= Phoma alnea Nitschke ex Sacc. 1880

  ≡Phomopsis alnea (Nitschke ex Sacc.) Höhn. 1906

Notes: Authorship of synonyms given on Index Fungorum is correct; verified and listed here. See von Höhnel (1906).

Distribution: Europe, Asia (Russia), North America (USA).

Substrate: Woody tissue.

Disease Note: Basal stem canker and dieback.

Host: Alnus glutinosa and other Alnus spp. (Betulaceae).
