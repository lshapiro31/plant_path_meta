Nomenclature data for Phomopsis ampelopsidis

Diaporthe ampelina (Berk & M.A. Curtis) R.R. Gomes, C. Glienke & Crous 2013 (Ascomycetes, Diaporthales)

    ≡ Phomopsis viticola (Reddick) Goid. 1937

= Phoma viticola Sacc. 1880  Note: From Phomopsis viticola - Not Phomopsis viticola (Sacc.) Grove 1917 or Phomopsis viticola (Reddick) Goid. 1937.

    ≡ Phomopsis viticola (Sacc.) Sacc. 1915

    ≡ Phomopsis viticola var. viticola (Sacc.) Sacc. 1919

    ≡ Diaporthe neoviticola Udayanga, Crous & K.D. Hyde 2012

= Sphaeropsis viticola Cooke 1883

    ≡ Macrophoma viticola (Cooke) Berl. & Voglino 1886

= Phomopsis viticola var. ampelopsidis Grove 1919

Notes: Kaliterna, J. et al. "Grapevine trunk diseases associated with fungi from the Diaporthaceae family in Croatian vineyards" Arh Hig Toksikol (2012). Diaporthe neoviticola a superfluous nom. nov. created to prevent conflict with Diaporthe viticola Nitschke (1870); Phoma ampelina Berk. & M.A. Curtis (1873) provides an older epithet.

Distribution: Temperate northern hemisphere.

Substrate: Leaves, stems, rachis.

Disease Note: Leaf spot, infection of pruning wounds, cankers, dieback

Host: Vitis spp., Ampelopsidis spp. (Vitaceae).
