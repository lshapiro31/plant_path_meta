Nomenclature data for Phomopsis asparagi

Phoma asparagi Sacc. 1878 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

≡ Phomopsis asparagi (Sacc.) Bubák 1935

Distribution: Asia, Europe, North America.

Substrate: Stems.

Disease Note: Stem blight.

Host: Asparagus officinalis (Asparagaceae).

Supporting Literature:
