Nomenclature data for Phomopsis batatas

Diaporthe batatas Harter & E.C. Field 1912 (Ascomycetes, Diaporthales)

    Variant spelling Diaporthe batatatis Harter & E.C. Field

≡Diaporthe phaseolorum var. batatae (Harter & E.C. Field) Wehm. 1933

  Variant spelling Diaporthe phaseolorum var. batatatis (Harter & E.C. Field) Wehm. 1933

= Phoma batatas Ellis & Halst. 1890

  Variant spelling Phoma batatae Ellis & Halst. 1890

  ≡Phomopsis batatas (Ellis & Halst.) Trotter 1928

Notes: Phomopsis batatas formerly and incorrectly attributed to (Ellis & Halst.) Harter & E.C. Field.

Distribution: North America (USA); widespread reports from other areas.

Substrate: Tubers.

Disease Note: Dry rot.

Host: Ipomoea batatas (Convolvulaceae).
