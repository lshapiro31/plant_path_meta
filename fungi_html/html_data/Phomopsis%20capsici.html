Nomenclature data for Phomopsis capsici

Diaporthe capsici Punith. 1981 (Ascomycetes, Diaporthales)

= Phoma capsici Magnaghi 1902  Note: From Phomopsis capsici - Dieback and leathery fruit rot of Capsicum; Punithalingam. Mycol. Pap. 149:2. 1981).

  ≡Phomopsis capsici (Magnaghi) Sacc. 1916

Distribution: Widespread (no USA records).

Substrate: Fruit.

Disease Note: Black rot, pod blight.

Host: Capsicum spp. (Solanaceae), Phaseolus lunatus (Fabaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Punithalingam, E. 1981. Studies on Sphaeropsidales in culture. III. Mycol. Pap. 149: 1-42.

Stevenson, J.A. 1975. Fungi of Puerto Rico and the American Virgin Islands. Contr. Reed Herb. 23: 743.
