Nomenclature data for Phomopsis diachenii

Diaporthe angelicae (Berk.) D.F. Farr & Castl. 2003 (Ascomycetes, Diaporthales)

≡ Sphaeria angelicae Berk. 1837

≡Diaporthopsis angelicae (Berk.) Wehm. 1933

≡Mazzantia angelicae (Berk.) Lar.N. Vassiljeva 1993

= Phoma asteriscus Berk. & Broome 1850

    ≡ Phomopsis asteriscus (Berk. & Broome) Grove 1917

= Phomopsis diachenii Sacc. 1915

= Leptosphaeria nigrella Auersw. 1869

    ≡ Diaporthe nigrella (Auersw.) Niessl 1872

    ≡ Diaporthopsis nigrella (Auersw.) Fabre 1883

Distribution: Europe, North America.

Substrate: Stems, living leaves, fruit, seed.

Disease Note: Stem decay, leaf spot.

Host: Heracleum sphondylium, Foeniculum vulgare, Eryngium maritimum and other members of the Apiaceae.
