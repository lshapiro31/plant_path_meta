Nomenclature data for Phomopsis oncostoma

Diaporthe oncostoma Fuckel 1870 [1869] (Ascomycetes, Diaporthales)

≡ Sphaeria oncostoma Duby 1854

= Phoma oncostoma Thuem. 1877

  ≡Phomopsis oncostoma (Thüm.) Traverso 1906

     Phoma oncostoma Thüm. 1877

Distribution: Temperate northern hemisphere.

Substrate: On limbs of hardwoods.

Disease Note: Canker, dieback and bark necrosis.

Host: Primarily known on Robinia but also on other temperate hardwood trees.

Supporting Literature:

Gomes, R.R., Glienke, C., Videira, S.I.R., Lombard, L., Groenewald, J.Z., and Crous, P.W. 2013. Diaporthe: a genus of endophytic, saprobic and plant pathogenic fungi. Persoonia 31: 1-41.
