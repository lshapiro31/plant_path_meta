Nomenclature data for Phomopsis padina

Diaporthe padi var. padi G.H. Otth 1870 (Ascomycetes, Diaporthales)

≡Diaporthe padi G.H. Otth 1870  Note: See type variety.

= Diaporthe decorticans Sacc. & Roum. 1881  Note: Author changed from (Lib.) Sacc. & Roum because Sphaeria decorticans Lib. is an illegitimate homonym.

  [≡ Sphaeria decorticans Lib. 1837 - illegitimate later homonym, not included in search]  Note: Illegitimate homonym of Sphaeria decorticans Fr.:Fr. 1817 = Valsa ceratosperma.

= Phoma padina Sacc. & Roum. 1884

  ≡Phomopsis padina (Sacc. & Roum.) Died. 1912

= Diaporthe patria Speg. 1881

Distribution: Europe, North America (USA), Asia (Japan).

Substrate: Bark of limbs.

Disease Note: Canker.

Host: Prunus spp. and other Rosaceae. Also reported on Rhamnus (Rhamnaceae).
