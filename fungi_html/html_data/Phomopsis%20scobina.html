Nomenclature data for Phomopsis scobina

Diaporthe scobina Nitschke 1870 (Ascomycetes, Diaporthales)

≡ Phomopsis scobina Höhn. 1906

Distribution: Europe.

Substrate: Living and dead twigs.

Disease Note: Canker.

Host: Fraxinis excelsior (Oleaceae).

Supporting Literature:
