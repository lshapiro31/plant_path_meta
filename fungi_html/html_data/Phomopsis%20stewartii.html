Nomenclature data for Phomopsis stewartii

Diaporthe stewartii A.L. Harrison 1935 (Ascomycetes, Diaporthales)

= Phomopsis stewartii Peck 1910

Distribution: North America (USA).

Substrate: Stems.

Disease Note: Stem canker.

Host: Cosmos bipinnatus (Asteraceae).
