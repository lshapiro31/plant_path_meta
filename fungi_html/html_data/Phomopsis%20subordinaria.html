Nomenclature data for Phomopsis subordinaria

Diaporthe subordinaria (Desm.) R.R. Gomes, C. Glienke & Crous 2013 (Ascomycetes, Diaporthales)

≡ Phoma subordinaria Desm. 1849

≡Phomopsis subordinaria (Desm.) Traverso 1906

= Sphaeria adunca Roberge ex Desm. 1851

    ≡ Diaporthe adunca (Roberge ex Desm.) Niessl 1872

Distribution: United States; Europe.

Substrate: Stems, seeds.

Disease Note: Causes a stalk disease.

Host: Plantago spp. (Plantaginaceae).
