Nomenclature data for Phomopsis theicola

Diaporthe foeniculina (Sacc.) Udayanga & Castl. 2014 (Ascomycetes, Diaporthales)

≡ Phoma foeniculina Sacc. 1884

≡Phomopsis foeniculina (Sacc.) Camara 1947

= Phomopsis californica H.S. Fawc. 1922

= Diaporthe neotheicola A.J.L. Phillips & J.M. Santos 2009

= Diaporthe rhusicola Crous 2011

= Diaporthe theicola Curzi 1927

= Phomopsis theicola Curzi 1927

Distribution: Africa (South Africa), Australia, Europe, New Zealand North America (USA; CA), South America (Argentina).

Substrate: Stems, fruit, leaves.

Disease Note: Shoot blight, leaf spot; potential for infection of a wide range of fruits as an opportunistic pathogen.

Host: Wide host range of dicots.
