Nomenclature data for Phomopsis vaccinii

Diaporthe vaccinii Shear 1931 (Ascomycetes, Diaporthales)

= Phomopsis vaccinii Shear 1931

Distribution: Temperate North America, Europe.

Substrate: On stems, leaves, twigs and fruit.

Disease Note: Stem and twig blight, canker, leaf spot, fruit rot.

Host: Vaccinium spp. (Ericaceae).
