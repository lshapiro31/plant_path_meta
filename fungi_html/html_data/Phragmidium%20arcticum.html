Nomenclature data for Phragmidium arcticum

Phragmidium arcticum Lagerh. ex Liro 1908 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

Distribution: Asia, Europe, North America (Canada).

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Rust.

Host: Rubus spp. (Rosaceae).

Supporting Literature:
