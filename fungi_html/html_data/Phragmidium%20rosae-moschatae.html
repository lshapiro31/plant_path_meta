Nomenclature data for Phragmidium rosae-moschatae

Phragmidium rosae-moschatae Dietel 1905 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

Distribution: Asia (India, Pakistan).

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Rust.

Host: Rosa spp. (Rosaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Ali, B., Sohail, Y., and Mumtaz, A.S. 2016. New host records of rust fungi (Pucciniales) from Pakistan. Mycotaxon 131(3): 503-509.
