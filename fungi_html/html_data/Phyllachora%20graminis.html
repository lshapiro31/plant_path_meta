Nomenclature data for Phyllachora graminis

Phyllachora graminis (Pers. : Fr.) Fuckel 1870 (Ascomycetes, Phyllachorales)

≡ Sphaeria graminis Pers. 1796

≡Dothidea graminis (Pers.) Chevall. 1826

≡Hypopteris graminis (Pers.) Fr. ex P. Karst. 1866

≡Nemania graminis (Pers.) Gray 1821

≡Polystigma graminis (Pers.) Link 1833

= Leptostromella graminis Grove 1937

    ≡ Linochora graminis (Grove) Parbery 1967

Distribution: Temperate regions.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Black leaf spot or tar spot of grasses.

Host: Poaceae.
