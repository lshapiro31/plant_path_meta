Nomenclature data for Phyllachora maculata

Plectosphaera eucalypti (Cooke & Massee) H.J. Swart 1981 (Ascomycetes, Phyllachorales)

≡ Trabutia eucalypti Cooke & Massee 1888

≡Phyllachora eucalypti (Cooke & Massee) Theiss. & Syd. 1915

≡Placostroma eucalypti (Cooke & Massee) Hansf. 1956

= Phyllachora maculata Cooke 1891  Note: Listed by Summerell et al. (2006) as a questionable synonym.

Distribution: Widespread based on scattered reports.

Disease Note: Tar spot.

Host: Eucalyptus spp. (Myrtaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Summerell, B.A., Groenewald, J.Z., Carnegie, A.J., Summerbell, R.C., and Crous, P.W. 2006. Eucalyptus microfungi known from culture. 2. Alysicdiella, Fusculina and Phlogicylindrium genera nova, with notes on some other poorly known taxa. Fung. Diversity 23: 323-350.
