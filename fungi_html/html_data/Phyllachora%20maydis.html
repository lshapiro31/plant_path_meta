Nomenclature data for Phyllachora maydis

Phyllachora maydis Maubl. 1904 (Ascomycetes, Phyllachorales)

Distribution: North America (Mexico, USA: IA, IL), South America.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Tar spot.

Host: Zea mays (Poaceae).
