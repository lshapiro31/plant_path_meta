Nomenclature data for Phyllachora nervisequia

Phyllachora americana Parbery 1967 (Ascomycetes, Phyllachorales)

≡ Sphaeria nervisequia Schwein. 1832

≡Phyllachora nervisequia (Schwein.) Orton 1885

= Sphaeria andropogonis Schwein. 1832

    ≡ Phyllachora andropogonis (Schwein.) Ellis & Everh. 1893

  Variant spelling Phyllachora andropogi (Schwein.) Ellis & Everh.

Notes: Phyllachora americana is a nom. nov. created to prevent conflict with Phyllachora nervisequia G. Winter 1885.

Distribution: Temperate North America; West Indies; possibly Asia, South America, Australia.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf/tar spot.

Host: Poaceae, especially Andropogon spp..

Supporting Literature:

Parbery, D.G. 1967. Studies on graminicolous species of Phyllachora Nke. in Fckl. V. A taxonomic monograph. Austral. J. Bot. 15: 271-375.
