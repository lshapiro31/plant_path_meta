Nomenclature data for Phyllachora palmicola

Phyllachora palmicola Speg. 1885 (Ascomycetes, Phyllachorales)

= Catacauma sabal Chardón 1946

    ≡ Phyllachora sabal (Chardón) Cif. 1961

Distribution: Caribbean (Dominican Republic), North America (USA: FL, GA, TX), South America (Argentina, Brazil).

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Lesions/tar spot, browning.

Host: Numerous Arecaceae genera.

Supporting Literature:

Hyde, K.D., and Cannon, P.F. 1999. Fungi causing tar spots on palms. Mycol. Pap. 175: 1-114.

Updated on Mar 11, 2014

Phaeochoropsis palmicola (F. Stevens) K.D. Hyde & P.F. Cannon 1999 (Ascomycetes, Phyllachorales)

≡ Catacauma palmicola F. Stevens 1920

Distribution: Caribbean (Puerto Rico, US Virgin Islands).

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Lesions/tar spot.

Host: Coccothrinax argentea, Thrinax morrisii (Arecaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Hyde, K.D., and Cannon, P.F. 1999. Fungi causing tar spots on palms. Mycol. Pap. 175: 1-114.

Updated on Mar 11, 2014
