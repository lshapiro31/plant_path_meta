Nomenclature data for Phyllachora penniseti

Phyllachora minutissima (Welw. & Curr.) A.L. Sm. 1898 (Ascomycetes, Phyllachorales)

≡ Isothea minutissima Welw. & Curr. 1868

= Phyllachora apiculata Speg. 1898

= Phyllachora bokensis Henn. 1908

= Phyllachora bonariensis Speg. 1880

= Phyllachora chaetochloae F. Stevens 1924

= Phyllachora cornispora G.F. Atk. 1897

= Phyllachora penniseti Syd. & P. Syd. 1915

= Phyllachora transvaalensis Doidge 1942

Distribution: Subtropical and tropical regions.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Tar spot.

Host: Primarily on Poaceae.

Supporting Literature:
