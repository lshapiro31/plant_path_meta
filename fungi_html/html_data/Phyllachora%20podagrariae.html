Nomenclature data for Phyllachora podagrariae

Septoria aegopodii Desm. ex J. Kickx 1867 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

= Sphaeria podagrariae Roth : Fr. 1797

    ≡ Mycosphaerella podagrariae (Roth : Fr.) Petr. 1921

    ≡ Dothidea podagrariae (Roth : Fr.) Fr. 1823

  ≡Phyllachora podagrariae (Roth : Fr.) P. Karst. 1873

    ≡ Oligostroma podagrariae (Roth : Fr.) Höhn. ex Petr. 1921

    ≡ Carlia podagrariae (Roth : Fr.) Höhn. 1923

    ≡ Sphaeria aegopodii var. podagrariae (Roth : Fr.) Pers. 1801

Notes: Correct publication date for S. aegopodii is 1867. Oldest epithet not in correct genus. Septoria podagrariae Lasch is a nomen nudum.

Distribution: Europe, North America (Canada, USA).

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Aegopodium podagraria, Pimpenella spp. (Apiaceae).
