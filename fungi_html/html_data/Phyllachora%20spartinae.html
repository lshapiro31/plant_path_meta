Nomenclature data for Phyllachora spartinae

Phyllachora spartinae Orton 1944 (Ascomycetes, Phyllachorales)

Distribution: Eastern United States, South America (Argentina).

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Tar spot/lesions.

Host: Spartina alterniflora, S. densiflora (Poaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Gabel, A.C., Gabel, M.L., and Cabral, D. 1999. New records of Phyllachora on Poaceae from Argentina. Mycotaxon 70: 477-488.

Orton, C.R. 1944. Graminicolous species of Phyllachora in North America. Mycologia 36: 18-53.

Parbery, D.G. 1967. Studies on graminicolous species of Phyllachora Nke. in Fckl. V. A taxonomic monograph. Austral. J. Bot. 15: 271-375.
