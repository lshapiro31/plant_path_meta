Nomenclature data for Phyllachora tetraspora

Phyllachora travancorica T.S. Ramakr. 1956 (Ascomycetes, Phyllachorales)

[≡Phyllachora tetraspora K. Ramakr. 1953 - illegitimate later homonym, not included in search]

Notes: Phyllachora travancorica is a nom. nov. created to replace the illegitimate homonym P. terataspora K. Ramakr. (1953), non P. terataspora Chardón (1932).

Distribution: Asia (India).

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Polyalthia longifolia (Annonaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Hosagoudar, V.B., Archana, G.R., and Sabeena, A. 2009. Two rare foliicolous fungi from the Western Ghats Region of Kerala State, India. Indian J. Sci. Tech. 2(6): 28.
