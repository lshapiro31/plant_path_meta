Nomenclature data for Phyllachora torrendiella

Camarotella torrendiella (Bat.) J.L. Bezerra & Vitoria 2008 (Ascomycetes, Phyllachorales)

≡ Catacauma torrendiella Bat. 1948

≡Phyllachora torrendiella (Bat.) Subileau, Renard & Dennet. 1993

≡Sphaerodothis torrendiella (Bat.) J.L. Bezerra 1991  Note: Nom. inval.

Distribution: South America (Brazil).

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Small verrucosis disease.

Host: Cocos nucifera (Arecaceae).
