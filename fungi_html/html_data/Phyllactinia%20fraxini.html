Nomenclature data for Phyllactinia fraxini

Phyllactinia fraxini (DC.) Fuss 1878 (Ascomycetes, Erysiphales)

≡ Erysiphe fraxini DC. 1805

[≡Phyllactinia fraxini (DC.) Homma 1878 - illegitimate later homonym, not included in search]

Distribution: Asia, Europe.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Powdery mildew.

Host: Reported on multiple hosts in multiple families, particularly Oleaceae.
