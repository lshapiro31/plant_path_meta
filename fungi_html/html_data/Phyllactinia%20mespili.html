Nomenclature data for Phyllactinia mespili

Phyllactinia mali (Duby) U. Braun 1978 (Ascomycetes, Erysiphales)

≡ Phyllactinia mali Duby 1830

= Erysiphe guttata var. mespili Castagne 1851

  ≡Phyllactinia mespili (Castagne) S. Blumer 1933

= Phyllactinia pyri Homma 1937

Distribution: Africa (Algeria, Morocco), Asia, Europe, North America Canada, USA).

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Powdery mildew.

Host: Rosaceae.

Supporting Literature:

Braun, U., and Cook, R.T.A. 2012. Taxonomy Manual of the Erysiphales (Powdery Mildews). CBS Biodivers. Ser. 11: 703.
