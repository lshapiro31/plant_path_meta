Nomenclature data for Phyllactinia moricola

Phyllactinia moricola (Henn.) Homma 1930 (Ascomycetes, Erysiphales)

≡ Phyllactinia suffulta var. moricola Henn. 1901

= Ovulariopsis moricola Delacr. 1903

Distribution: Temperate regions.

Substrate: On living leaves, stems.

Disease Note: Powdery mildew.

Host: On Moraceae.

Supporting Literature:

Braun, U., and Cook, R.T.A. 2012. Taxonomy Manual of the Erysiphales (Powdery Mildews). CBS Biodivers. Ser. 11: 703.
