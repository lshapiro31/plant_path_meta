Nomenclature data for Phylloporia ribis

Phylloporia ribis (Schumach. : Fr.) Ryvarden 1978 (Basidiomycetes, Hymenochaetales)

≡ Boletus ribis Schumach. : Fr. 1803

≡Fomes ribis (Schumach. : Fr.) Gill. 1878

≡Phellinus ribis (Schumach. : Fr.) Quél. 1886

≡Polyporus ribis (Schumach. : Fr.) Fr. 1821

Notes: At bases of stems of living shrubs.

Distribution: Temperate northern hemisphere.

Substrate: Collar of living plants; rarely on dead material.

Disease Note: Collar rot, root rot.

Host: Commonly Ribes spp. (Grossulariaceae); numerous other woody hosts.

Supporting Literature:

Wagner, T., and Ryvarden, L. 2002. Phylogeny and taxonomy of the genus Phylloporia (Hymenochaetales). Mycol. Progr. 1(1): 105-116.
