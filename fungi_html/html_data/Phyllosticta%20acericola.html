Nomenclature data for Phyllosticta acericola

Phyllosticta minima (Berk. & M.A. Curtis) Underw. & Earle 1897 (Ascomycetes, Dothideales)

≡ Sphaeropsis minima Berk. & M.A. Curtis 1874

= Phyllosticta acericola Cooke & Ellis 1879  Note: Not Migula after 1933.

= Phyllosticta arida Earle 1898

[= Phyllosticta minima Gucevic 1897 - illegitimate later homonym, not included in search]

Notes: Not (Berk. & Curt.) Ellis & Everh. 1900 nor Gutsevitch 1962.

Distribution: North America, Asia (China).

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Acer spp. (Sapindaceae).
