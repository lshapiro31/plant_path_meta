Nomenclature data for Phyllosticta ambrosioidis

Phoma multirostrata var. microspora (Allesch.) Boerema 1986 [1985] (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

≡ Phoma decorticans var. microspora Allesch. 1898 [1901]

= Phoma ambrosioides Kamal & P. Kumar 1981

  Variant spelling Phoma ambrosioidis Kamal & P. Kumar 1981 [1980]

= Phyllosticta ambrosioides Thüm. 1881 [1880-1881]

    Variant spelling Phyllosticta ambrosioidis Thüm.

= Phyllosticta geranii Ellis & Everh. 1887

= Phoma jaunpurensis R.C. Srivast. 1981

= Phoma liliana S. Chandra & Tandon 1965

[= Phoma lucknowensis S.C. Agarwal & J.K. Misra 1981 - illegitimate later homonym, not included in search]  Note: Misapplied. Not R.K. Saksena, Nand & A.K. Sarbhoy 1968.

[= Phoma microspora P. Balas. & Narayanas. 1980 - illegitimate later homonym, not included in search]  Note: Not Sacc. 1885 nor Pat. 1889.

= Phyllosticta plumierae Tassi 1901

Distribution: Worldwide in subtropical regions, also in glasshouses.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Plurivorous soil-borne opportunistic pathogen, leaf spot, also blight and dieback.
