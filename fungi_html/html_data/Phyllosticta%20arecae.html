Nomenclature data for Phyllosticta arecae

Phyllosticta arecae Höhn. 1912 (Ascomycetes, Dothideales)

= Phyllostictina arecae Petr. & Syd. 1934

  ≡Phyllosticta arecae Died. 1916  Note: Not Höhn. 1912.

= Phyllostictina banosensis Petr. 1934

Notes: Not Died. 1916.

Distribution: Asia, Philippines, Papua New Guinea.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Areca spp., Calamus spp., Caryota spp. (Arecaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Van Der AA, H.A., and Vanev, S. 2002. A revision of the species described in Phyllosticta. Centraalbureau voor Schimmelcultures, Utrecht, 510 pages.
