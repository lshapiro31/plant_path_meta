Nomenclature data for Phyllosticta batatas

Phyllosticta batatas (Thüm.) Cooke 1884 (Ascomycetes, Dothideales)

≡ Depazea batatas Thüm. 1876

      Variant spelling Depazea batatae Thüm.

= Phomopsis ipomoeae-batatas Punith. 1982

Distribution: HI, southcentral and southeastern United States; worldwide in warm sweet potato-growing regions.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Ipomoea batatas (Convolvulaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Punithalingam, E. 1982. Phomopsis ipomoeae-batatas. C.M.I. Descr. Pathog. Fungi Bact. 739: 1-2.
