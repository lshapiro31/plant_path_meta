Nomenclature data for Phyllosticta borszczowii

Septoria caraganae Henn. 1902 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

= Ascochyta bondarzewii Henn. 1903

= Phyllosticta borszczowii Thüm. 1880  Note: From Asteromella borszczowii - This is the spermatial state.

    ≡ Asteromella borszczowii (Thüm.) Aa 2002

= Phloeospora caraganae Jacz. 1900

  Variant spelling Phleospora caraganae Jacz. 1900

  [≡ Septoria caraganae (Jacz.) Died. 1914 - illegitimate later homonym, not included in search]  Note: Not Henn. 1902. Nom. illeg.

= Mycosphaerella jaczewskii Potebnia 1910

    ≡ Sphaerella jaczewskii (Potebnia) Sacc. & Traverso 1911

Distribution: Widespread in northern hemisphere.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Fabaceae, especially Caragana spp.

Supporting Literature:

Andrianova, T.V., and Minter, D.W. 2005. Mycosphaerella jaczewskii. I.M.I. Descr. Fungi Bact. 1625: 1-5.
