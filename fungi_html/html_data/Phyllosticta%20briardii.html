Nomenclature data for Phyllosticta briardii

Asteromella mali (Briard) Boerema 1965 [1964] (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

≡ Phyllosticta mali Briard 1888  Note: Not Prill. & Delacr. 1890.

      Phyllosticta mali var. mali Briard 1888  Note: Autonym unnecessary.

≡Phyllosticta briardii var. briardii Sacc. 1892  Note: Superfluous name, see Boerema 1965.

  Variant spelling Phyllosticta briardi Sacc. 1892  Note: Superfluous name, see Boerema 1965.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot, commonly in association with nutrient deficiency.

Host: Apple, Malus spp. (Rosaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Boerema, G.H., and Dorenbosch, M.M.J. 1965. [Phoma-like fungi associated with apple leaf spots]. Versl. PlZiekt. Dienst 142: 138-151.
