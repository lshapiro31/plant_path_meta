Nomenclature data for Phyllosticta caricaecola

Phyllosticta caricaecola Sawada 1943 (Ascomycetes, Dothideales)

    Variant spelling Phyllosticta caricicola Sawada 1943

Notes: This name is invalid, no Latin diagnosis. Van der Aa and Vanev (2002) state that the identity of this fungus can not be known due to a vague description. Reports in the literature should be viewed with caution.

Distribution: Asia (China, Japan), South America (Venezuela).

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf blight.

Host: Carica papaya (Caricaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Sawada, K. 1943. Descriptive catalogue of the Formosan fungi. Part VIII. Rep. Dept. Agric. Gov. Res. Inst. Formosa 85: 1-130.

Van Der AA, H.A., and Vanev, S. 2002. A revision of the species described in Phyllosticta. Centraalbureau voor Schimmelcultures, Utrecht, 510 pages.
