Notes: From Phoma enteroleuca - See type variety. From Plenodomus glechomae - Invalid herbarium name, not published. From Phyllosticta catalpicola - Not (Schwein.) Ellis & Everh. 1892 From Phoma cornicola - not Oudem. 1900 From Phomopsis enteroleuca - misapplied (Boerema 2004)

Distribution: From Phoma enteroleuca var. enteroleuca - United States; Europe.

Substrate: From Phoma enteroleuca var. enteroleuca - Bark, wood, fruits. Occasionally herbaceous plant residues.

Disease Note: From Phoma enteroleuca var. enteroleuca - Opportunistic parasite.

Host: From Phoma enteroleuca var. enteroleuca - Various trees, shrubs.
