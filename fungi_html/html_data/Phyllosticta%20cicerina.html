Nomenclature data for Phyllosticta cicerina

Ascochyta rabiei (Pass.) Labr. 1931 (Ascomycetes, Incertae sedis)

≡ Zythia rabiei Pass. 1867

≡Phoma rabiei (Pass.) Khune ex Gruyter 2002

≡Phyllosticta rabiei (Pass.) Trotter 1918

= Phyllosticta cicerina Prill. & Delacr. 1893

= Mycosphaerella rabiei Kovatsch. ex Gruyter 2002

     Didymella rabiei (Kovatsch.) Arx 1962

Notes: From Zythia rabiei - From Phoma rabiei - Invalid name, no reference to basionym. Not (Pass.) Khune ex Gruyter 2002. From Phoma rabiei - Invalid name, no page # reference to basionym. Not (Pass.) Khune ex Gruyter 2002. From Phoma rabiei - Not (Pass.) Khune 1979, invalid, nor (Pass.) Khune & J.N. Kapoor 1980, invalid. From Mycosphaerella rabiei - Gruyter (2002) validated this name. From Mycosphaerella rabiei - Nom. inval. via no Latin. From Didymella rabiei - Nom. inval. since based on an invalid name.

Distribution: Worldwide in chick-pea growing regions.

Substrate: Living leaves, stems; seed-borne.

Disease Note: Anthracnose, chickpea blight, or 'Ascochyta' blight.

Host: Chickpea, Cicer arietinum (Fabaceae).
