Nomenclature data for Phyllosticta cocos

Diaporthe cocoina (Cooke) Rossman & Udayanga 2015 (Ascomycetes, Diaporthales)

≡ Phoma cocoina Cooke 1877

≡Phomopsis cocoina (Cooke) Punith. 1975

= Phyllosticta cocos Cooke 1880

Notes: From Phomopsis cocoina - Leaf spot of coconut. Punithalingam (C.M.I. Descr. 828. 1985).

Distribution: Africa, Asia, Australia, Oceania, West Indies.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Cocos nucifera, Rhapis excelsa, Caryota sp. (Arecaceae).

Supporting Literature:
