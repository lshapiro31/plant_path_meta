Nomenclature data for Phyllosticta codiaei

    ≡ Phyllostictina codiaei (Died.) Petr. 1929  Note: Not N.D. Sharma 1975.

[= Phyllostictina codiaei N.D. Sharma 1975 - illegitimate later homonym, not included in search]  Note: Not (Died.) Petr. 1929. Not listed by van der Aa as a tax. syn.

  Variant spelling Phyllostictina codiae N.D. Sharma 1975  Note: Original spelling, corrected to genus Codiae-um (root) plus genitive ending -i-.

[= Phyllosticta codiaeicola Gonz. Frag. & Cif. 1925 - illegitimate later homonym, not included in search]  Note: Not Died. 1916.

= Phyllosticta domingensis Cif. & Gonz. Frag. 1927

= Phyllostictina euphorbiae Petr. 1953

= Phyllosticta reyesii H.S. Yates 1922

  ≡Phyllosticta codiaei Sacc. 1917  Note: Not Died. 1916 nor Stevens & Young 1925 nor Frag. & Cif. 1926.

    ≡ Phyllosticta codiaeana Sacc. 1931  Note: Superfluous name.

Distribution: Asia, Hawaii.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Various genera of Euphorbiaceae.

Supporting Literature:
