Nomenclature data for Phyllosticta colocasiae

Passalora alocasiae (Syd. & P. Syd.) Rossman & W.C. Allen 2016 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Mycosphaerella alocasiae Syd. & P. Syd. 1913

≡Sphaerella alocasiae (Syd. & P. Syd.) Trotter 1928

= Cercospora caladii var. colocasiae Höhn. 1907

    ≡ Cercospora colocasiae (Höhn.) Chupp 1954

    ≡ Passalora colocasiae (Höhn.) U. Braun 1999

= Phyllosticta colocasiae Höhn. 1907

Notes: Although the oldest specific name at the rank of species is provided by Phyllosticta colocasiae Höhn. (1907), that name is already occupied in Passalora. Therefore, a new combination with Mycosphaerella alocasiae Syd. & P. Syd. (1913) was made.

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: On living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Alocasia, Colocasia (Araceae).
