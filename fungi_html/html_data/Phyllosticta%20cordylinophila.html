Nomenclature data for Phyllosticta cordylinophila

Phyllosticta cordylinophila P.A. Young 1925 (Ascomycetes, Dothideales)

= Sphaeropsis cordylines Thüm. 1881

    ≡ Phyllostictina cordylines (Thüm.) Petr. & Syd. 1927

    ≡ Phoma cordylines (Thüm.) Sacc. 1884

    ≡ Macrophoma cordylines (Thüm.) Berl. & Voglino 1886  Note: Not N.D. Sharma 1975.

Distribution: North America (USA: HI, Puerto Rico), New Zealand, Europe (Portugal).

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Cordyline spp. (Liliaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Van Der AA, H.A. 1973. Studies in Phyllosticta I. Stud. Mycol. 5: 1-110.

Van Der AA, H.A., and Vanev, S. 2002. A revision of the species described in Phyllosticta. Centraalbureau voor Schimmelcultures, Utrecht, 510 pages.
