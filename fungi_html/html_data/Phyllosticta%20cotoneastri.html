Didymella aliena (Fr. : Fr.) Qian Chen & L. Cai 2015 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

≡ Sphaeria aliena Fr. : Fr. 1823

≡Phoma aliena (Fr. : Fr.) Aa & Boerema 1998

≡Phyllosticta aliena (Fr. : Fr.) Sacc. 1881

≡Perisporium alienum (Fr. : Fr.) Fr. 1829

= Phyllosticta asiatica Cooke 1885

    ≡ Phoma asiatica (Cooke) Aa 2002

= Phoma berberidicola Vestergr. 1897

= Phyllosticta cotoneastri Allesch. 1897

Notes: From Phoma berberidicola - not Brunaud 1898

Distribution: Asia, Europe, North America.

Substrate: Leaves and woody stems; seed-borne.

Disease Note: Opportunistic parasite.

Host: Woody plants, especially shrubs, including both evergreens and conifers.
