Nomenclature data for Phyllosticta glaucispora

Foliophoma fallens (Sacc.) Crous 2017 (, )

≡ Phoma fallens Sacc. 1892

≡Pleospora fallens (Sacc.) Gruyter & Verkley 2012

= Phyllosticta glaucispora Delacr. 1893

    ≡ Phoma glaucispora (Delacr.) Noordel. & Boerema 1988 [1987]

= Phyllosticta oleandri Gutner 1933

Distribution: Europe, New Zealand.

Substrate: Fruits and leaves.

Disease Note: Associated with leaf and fruit spots, but pathogenicity untested.

Host: Olive, Olea europaea (Oleaceae), Nerium oleander (Apocynaceae).
