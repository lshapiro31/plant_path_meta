Nomenclature data for Phyllosticta hamamelidis

Phyllosticta hamamelidis Peck 1886 (Ascomycetes, Dothideales)

= Gloeosporium hamamelidis Cooke 1883

  Phyllosticta hamamelidis Cooke 1881  Note: Invalid herbarium name (checked exsiccatus; listed as type specimen!). Not Peck 1887.

    ≡ Phyllostictina hamamelidis (Cooke) Petr. & Syd. 1927 [1926]

Notes: Not Cooke 1881, invalid herbarium name.

Distribution: North America.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Hamamelis virginiana (Hamamelidaceae).
