Notes: From Phoma platensis - No indication of type. Not Speg. 1880. From Depazea prunicola - Without description. From Phyllosticta indica - As 'indicii'. Not Roum. & P. Karst. 1890. From Phoma herbarum var. tulostomatis - 'tylostomatis' in Saccardo XIV:888 but 'tulostomatis' in Pat. 1897 From Phyllosticta cydoniicola - Not Henn. 1902. From Phyllosticta perforans - Not Sacc. & Matt. 1899. From Phyllosticta pruni-spinosae - In Saccardo XI:472 as 'spinosa' but corrected in XII:561 From Phoma pomorum var. pomorum - Commonly treated as Phoma prunicola in older literature.

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Leaves, soil; seed-borne (plurivorous).

Disease Note: Opportunistic parasite. Black root rot complex.

Host: Rosaceae and Ericaceae (Boerema 2004). Also reported on other plant families.

Supporting Literature:

Aveskamp, M. , de Gruyter, H., Woudenberg, J. , Verkley, G., and Crous, P.W. 2010. Highlights of the Didymellaceae: A polyphasic approach to characterise Phoma and related pleosporalean genera. Stud. Mycol. 65: 1-64.
