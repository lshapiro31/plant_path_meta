Nomenclature data for Phyllosticta paviae

Phyllosticta sphaeropsoidea Ellis & Everh. 1883 (Ascomycetes, Dothideales)

    Variant spelling Phyllosticta sphaeropsidea Ellis & Everh.

≡Phyllostictina sphaeropsoidea (Ellis & Everh.) Petr. 1957

= Laestadia aesculi Peck 1885

    ≡ Guignardia aesculi (Peck) V.B. Stewart 1916

    ≡ Botryosphaeria aesculi (Peck) M.E. Barr 1972

Distribution: North America, Europe.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Buckeye blotch, leaf blotch, or black rot.

Host: Aesculus spp. (Hippocastanaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Sivanesan, A. 1984. The bitunicate Ascomycetes and their anamorphs. J. Cramer, Vaduz, 701 pages.
