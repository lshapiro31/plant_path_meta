Notes: From Phyllosticta glumarum - Not (Ellis & Tracy) I. Miyake 1910. Padwick From Phyllosticta hawaiiensis - Not Petr. 1953.

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Plurivorous. Soil and seed-borne.

Disease Note: Canker, leaf spot. Ring spot of leaves and leaf-sheaths. Of little economic importance (Boerema 2004).

Host: Wide host range; often on grasses (Poaceae), especially sugarcane, Saccharum officinarum.
