     Phoma tabifica (Prill.) Sacc. 1892  Note: Unintentional error of citation by Saccardo, see Boerema 1973 but also Shoemaker 1999.

Notes: From Pleospora betae - Not (Berl.) Nevod. 1915. From Pleospora betae - Not Björl. 1944. From Phoma sphaerosperma - Not Fuckel 1867 nor P. Karst 1885. From Phyllosticta spinaciae - Author changed from A. Zimmerm. From Phyllosticta tabifica - Erroneously cited as 'Phoma tabifica Prill.'. From Phoma betae - Type of Phoma sect. Pilosa Boerema, Gruyter & Noordel. 1997. Nom. cons. prop. (Shoemaker & Redhead, 1999).

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Living roots, seedlings, leaves. Seed-borne.

Disease Note: Black leg, damping-off of seedlings, root rot, storage rot, leaf spot.

Host: Beta spp. especially beet, Beta vulgaris; spinach, Spinacia oleracea; and other Amaranthaceae.
