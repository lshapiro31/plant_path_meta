Nomenclature data for Phyllosticta vincae-majoris

Boeremia exigua var. heteromorpha (Schulzer & Sacc.) Aveskamp, Gruyter & Verkley 2010 (, )

≡ Phoma heteromorpha Schulzer & Sacc. 1884

≡Phoma exigua var. heteromorpha (Schulzer & Sacc.) Noordel. & Boerema 1989 [1988]  Note: Phoma exigua var. inoxydabilis Boerema & Vegh 1974 tax. syn. pro parte.

≡Ascochyta heteromorpha (Schulzer & Sacc.) Curzi 1932

= Phoma herbarum f. vincae Brunaud 1886  Note: Synonymy uncertain.

= Phoma oleandrina Delacr. 1905

= Phyllosticta vincae-majoris Allesch. 1898 [1901]  Note: Synonymy uncertain.

= Phyllosticta vincae-minoris Bres. & K. Krieg. 1901  Note: Synonymy uncertain.

Distribution: Europe, North America, possibly throughout host range.

Substrate: Stems, leaves.

Disease Note: Stem blight and leaf spot.

Host: Nerium oldeander and Vinca spp. (Apocynaceae).
