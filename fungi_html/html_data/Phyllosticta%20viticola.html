  ≡Phyllosticta viticola Thüm. 1878 Jan.  Note: Published Oct. 1878, this is a later homonym of Phyllosticta viticola Sacc. & Speg. published Jan. 1878.

    ≡ Phyllostictina viticola Petr. 1940  Note: Published as (Thüm.) Petr. but see Art. 58.1.

    ≡ Phyllosticta vulpinae Allesch. 1898  Note: A superfluous name introduced to replace the homonym P. viticola Thüm. 1878.

Distribution: Cosmopolitan, except not in Australasia, Scandinavia, UK, Western USA.

Substrate: Leaves, fruits, stems.

Disease Note: Black rot of grapevine.

Host: Vitis spp., also Ampelopsis, Cissus, Parthenocissus spp. (Vitaceae).
