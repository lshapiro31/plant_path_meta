Nomenclature data for Phyllostictina concentrica

= Asteromella saccardoi (Thüm.) Petr. 1934  Note: According to Pirozynski (1974) this is a microconidial synanamorph of Phyllosticta concentrica, teleomorph Discochora philoprina (as Botryosphaeria rhodorae).

    ≡ Phyllosticta saccardoi Thüm.

= Sphaeria taxi Sowerby : Fr. 1803  Note: Sanctioned by Fr. 1823 (describing what he termed perithecia, but accepted by later authors as an anamorph species). Not Duby (erroneous citation) nor Schw. (invalid herbarium name). Phoma ilicis Desm. is a synonym pro parte.

     Sphaeria taxi Duby

    ≡ Phyllostictina taxi (Sowerby : Fr.) Wollenw. & Hochapfel 1942

    ≡ Gloeosporium taxi (Sowerby : Fr.) P. Karst. & Har. 1890

Distribution: North America, South America (Brazil), Asia, Europe, Australia.

Substrate: Living and dead leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Various plant families, including Taxus (Taxaceae), Hedera (Araliaceae), Rhododendron (Ericaceae).
