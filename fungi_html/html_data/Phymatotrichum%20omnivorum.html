Nomenclature data for Phymatotrichum omnivorum

Phymatotrichopsis omnivora (Shear) Hennebert 1973 (Pezizomycetes, Pezizales)

≡ Ozonium omnivorum Shear 1907

≡Phymatotrichum omnivorum (Shear) Duggar 1916  Note: Sometimes listed with the author citation Duggar as a new species based on a different morph (Hennebert 1973).

Notes: As '(Duggar) Hennebert. Considerable taxonomic confusion exists. This fungus has been linked to various basidiomycete taxa (Hennebert 1973). However, Marek (2005) showed that this fungus was an ascomycete. Marek et al. (2009) accept Ozonium omnivorum Shear as the correct basionym for this fungus. Accepted practice in the past, however, listed the author citation as (Duggar) Hennebert with the liberal interpretation that a new anamorphic species (morph) was introduced by Duggar.

Distribution: North America (Southwestern USA, Mexico).

Disease Note: Root rot.

Host: Multiple genera in multiple families.

Supporting Literature:

Hennebert, G.L. 1973. Botrytis and Botrytis-like genera. Persoonia 7: 183-204.

Marek, S. 2005. Molecular phylogeny of Phymatotrichopsis omnivora. Phytopathology 95: s65.

Marek, S.M., Hansen, K., Romanish, M., and Thorn, R.G. 2009. Molecular systematics of the cotton root rot pathogen, Phymatotrichopsis omnivora. Persoonia 22: 63-74.
