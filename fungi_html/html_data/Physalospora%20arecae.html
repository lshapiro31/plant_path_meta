Nomenclature data for Physalospora arecae

Guignardia calami (Syd.) Arx & E. Müll. 1954 (Ascomycetes, Dothideales)

≡ Physalospora calami Syd. 1911

≡Melanops calami (Syd.) Petr. 1934

= Guignardia arecae Sacc. 1917

= Physalospora arecae Höhn. 1912

= Melanops banosensis Petr. 1934

    ≡ Guignardia banosensis (Petr.) Arx & E. Müll. 1954

Distribution: Asia, Philippines, Papua New Guinea.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Areca spp., Calamus spp., Caryota spp. (Arecaceae).
