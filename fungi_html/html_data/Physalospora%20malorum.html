Notes: From Physalospora corni - Not (Sacc.) Sacc. 1882.

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Plurivorous.

Disease Note: Cankers, dieback, fruit rot, leaf spot.

Host: Multiple genera in multiple families.
