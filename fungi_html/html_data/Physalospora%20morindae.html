Nomenclature data for Physalospora morindae

Phyllosticta morindae (Petr. & Syd.) Aa 1973 (Ascomycetes, Dothideales)

≡ Phyllostictina morindae Petr. & Syd. 1927

= Physalospora morindae Koord. 1907

    ≡ Guignardia morindae (Koord.) Aa 1973

    ≡ Puiggarina morindae (Koord.) Speg. 1919

Distribution: Asia (Indonesia), Australia, Hawaii.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Morinda citrifolia (Rubiaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Van Der AA, H.A. 1973. Studies in Phyllosticta I. Stud. Mycol. 5: 1-110.

Van Der AA, H.A., and Vanev, S. 2002. A revision of the species described in Phyllosticta. Centraalbureau voor Schimmelcultures, Utrecht, 510 pages.

Wulandari, N.F., Bhat, D.J., and To-anun, C. 2013. A modern account of the genus Phyllosticta. Pl. Pathol & Quarantine 3(2): 145-159.
