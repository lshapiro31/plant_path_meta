Nomenclature data for Physalospora vaccinii

Physalospora vaccinii (Shear) Arx & E. Müll. 1954 (Ascomycetes, Xylariales)

≡ Acanthorhynchus vaccinii Shear 1907

Distribution: Eastern North America; Europe.

Substrate: Leaves, fruit.

Disease Note: Fruit rot; blotch rot.

Host: Vaccinium spp. (Ericaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Barr, M.E. 1970. Some amerosporous ascomycetes on Ericaceae and Empetraceae. Mycologia 62: 377-394.

Olatinwo, R.O., Hanson, E.J., and Schilder, A.M. 2003. A first assessment of the cranberry fruit rot complex in Michigan. Pl. Dis. 87: 550-556.
