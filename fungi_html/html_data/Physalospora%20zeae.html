Nomenclature data for Physalospora zeae

Botryosphaeria zeae (G.L. Stout) Arx & E. Müll. 1954 (Ascomycetes, Dothideales)

≡ Physalospora zeae G.L. Stout 1930

= Macrophoma zeae Tehon & E.Y. Daniels 1927

Notes: Gray ear rot of maize. Sivanesan & Holliday (C.M.I. Descr. 774. 1983) suggested that the anamorph belongs in Dothiorella.

Distribution: United States; eastern and southern Africa; France.

Substrate: On living inflorescences.

Disease Note: Gray ear rot.

Host: Zea mays (Poaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Sivanesan, A., and Holliday, P. 1983. Botryosphaeria zeae. C.M.I. Descr. Pathog. Fungi Bact. 774: 1-2.
