Nomenclature data for Piptoporus portentosus

Laetiporus portentosus (Berk.) Rajchenb. 1995 (Basidiomycetes, Polyporales)

≡ Polyporus portentosus Berk. 1844

≡Piptoporus portentosus (Berk.) G. Cunn. 1965

= Polyporus leucocreas Cooke 1879

Notes: For additional synonyms, see Index Fungorum.

Distribution: Southern hemisphere.

Substrate: Heartwood.

Disease Note: Cubical heart-rot; a severe pathogen.

Host: Nothofagus spp. (Nothofagaceae), Eucalyptus spp. (Myrtaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Rajchenberg, M., and Robledo, G. 2013. Pathogenic polypores in Argentina. Forest Pathol. 43: 171-184.
