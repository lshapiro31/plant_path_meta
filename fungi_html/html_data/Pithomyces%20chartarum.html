Nomenclature data for Pithomyces chartarum

Pseudopithomyces chartarum (Berk. & M.A. Curtis) J.F. Li, Ariyawansa & K.D. Hyde 2015 (, )

≡ Sporidesmium chartarum Berk. & M.A. Curtis 1874

≡Piricauda chartarum (Berk. & M.A. Curtis) R.T. Moore 1959

≡Pithomyces chartarum (Berk. & M.A. Curtis) M.B. Ellis 1960

= Sporidesmium bakeri Syd. & P. Syd. 1914

= Leptosphaerulina chartarum Cec. Roux 1986

Distribution: From Pithomyces chartarum - Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: From Pithomyces chartarum - Leaves.

Disease Note: From Pithomyces chartarum - Usually saprobic: may cause leaf spot and discoloration.

Host: From Pithomyces chartarum - Diverse plant families, but especially Poaceae.
