Nomenclature data for Plasmodiophora brassicae

Plasmodiophora brassicae Woronin 1877 (Plasmodiophoromycetes, Plasmodiophorales)

Distribution: Widespread, especially in tropical regions but probably occurring wherever crucifers are cultivated.

Substrate: Roots.

Disease Note: Clubroot.

Host: Typically Brassicaceae.
