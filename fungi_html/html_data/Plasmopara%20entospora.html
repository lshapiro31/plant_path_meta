Nomenclature data for Plasmopara entospora

Basidiophora entospora Roze & Cornu 1869 (Oomycetes, Peronosporales)

≡Peronospora entospora (Roze & Cornu) Berk. & Broome 1871

≡Plasmopara entospora (Roze & Cornu) J. Schröt. 1886

= Basidiophora montana R.W. Barreto 1991

= Gilletia spinuligera Sacc. & Therry 1882

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Downy mildew.

Host: Asteraceae.
