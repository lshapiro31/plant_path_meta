Plasmopara geranii (Peck) Berl. & De Toni 1888 (Oomycetes, Peronosporales)

≡ Peronospora geranii Peck 1876

≡Rhysotheca geranii (Peck) G.W. Wilson 1907

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Downy mildew.

Host: Geranium spp. (Geraniaceae).
