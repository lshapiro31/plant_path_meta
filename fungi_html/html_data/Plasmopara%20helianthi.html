Nomenclature data for Plasmopara helianthi

Plasmopara halstedii (Farl.) Berl. & De Toni 1888 (Oomycetes, Peronosporales)

≡ Peronospora halstedii Farl. 1882

= Plasmopara helianthi Novot. 1962

    ≡ Plasmopara helianthi f. helianthi .

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Leaves, soil, stems, roots.

Disease Note: Downy mildew.

Host: Multiple genera of Asteraceae.
