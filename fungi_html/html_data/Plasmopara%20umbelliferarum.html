Nomenclature data for Plasmopara umbelliferarum

Plasmopara umbelliferarum (Casp.) J. Schröt. ex Wartenw. 1919 (Oomycetes, Peronosporales)

≡Peronospora umbelliferarum Casp. 1855

≡Rhysotheca umbelliferarum (Casp.) G.W. Wilson 1907

≡Peronospora umbelliferarum var. aegopodii Casp. 1855

    ≡ Plasmopara aegopodii (Casp.) Trotter 1926  Note: Nom. illeg.

≡Peronospora umbelliferarum var. umbelliferarum

= Peronospora umbelliferarum var. berkeleyi Ces. 1857

Notes: Constantinescu (1992) discusses the difficult nomenclature associated with this species.

Distribution: Eurasia.

Disease Note: Downy mildew.

Host: Apiaceae.

Supporting Literature:
