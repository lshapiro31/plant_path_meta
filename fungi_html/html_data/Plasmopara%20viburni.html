Nomenclature data for Plasmopara viburni

Plasmopara viburni Peck 1890 (Oomycetes, Peronosporales)

Distribution: Central and eastern United States.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Downy mildew.

Host: Viburnum (Adoxaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Dick, M.W. 2001. Straminipilous fungi: systematics of the Peronosporomycetes including accounts of the marine straminipilous protists, the plasmodiophorids and similar organisms. Kluwer Academic Publishers, 670 pages.
