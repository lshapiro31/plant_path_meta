Nomenclature data for Plenodomus meliloti

Leptosphaeria sclerotioides (Sacc.) Gruyter, Aveskamp & Verkley 2012 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

≡ Phoma sclerotioides Preuss ex Sacc. 1892

= Plenodomus meliloti Mark.-Let. 1928

[= Plenodomus meliloti Dearn. & Sanford 1930 - illegitimate later homonym, not included in search]

= Plenodomus sorghi Morochk. 1933

Notes: From Plenodomus meliloti - Not Dearn. & Sanford 1930. From Plenodomus meliloti - Not Mark.-Let. 1928. From Plenodomus sclerotioides - Invalid name, without description.

Distribution: North America, Europe, Asia.

Substrate: Roots, base of stems.

Disease Note: Brown root rot, low temperature parasite.

Host: Herbaceous plants, especially Melilotus spp., Medicago spp., (Fabaceae), grasses and cereals at low temperatures.
