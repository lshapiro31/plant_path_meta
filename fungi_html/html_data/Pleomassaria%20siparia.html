Nomenclature data for Pleomassaria siparia

Prosthemium betulinum Kunze 1817 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

= Sphaeria siparia Berk. & Broome 1852  Note: From Pleomassaria siparia - The oldest epithet is not being used. This name may be conserved.

  ≡Pleomassaria siparia (Berk. & Broome) Sacc. 1883

    ≡ Massaria siparia (Berk. & Broome) C. Tul. & Tul. 1863

    ≡ Splanchnonema siparium (Berk. & Broome) M.E. Barr 1993

Notes: The genus Prosthemium 1817 based on P. betulinum competes for use with Pleomassaria 1880 based on P. siparia. The names are synonymous. Given the recent work by Tanaka et al. (2010), the widespread use of Prosthemium and its priority, it seems best to use Prosthemium.

Distribution: North temperate regions.

Substrate: Woody plant parts.

Disease Note: Saprobic.

Host: Betula (Betulaceae).
