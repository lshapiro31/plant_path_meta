Nomenclature data for Pleomeris pygmaea

Puccinia pygmaea Erikss. 1895 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

≡Pleomeris pygmaea (Erikss.) Syd. 1921

≡Dicaeoma pygmaeum (Erikss.) Arthur & Fromme 1920

= Puccinia milii Erikss. 1895

Distribution: Temperate regions.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Heterecious rust.

Host: Berberis spp. (Berberidaceae), Poaceae from tribes Agrostideae and Festuceae.

Supporting Literature:

Arthur, J.C. 1934. Manual of the Rusts in the United States and Canada. Purdue Res. Found., Lafayette, Ind., 438 pages.
