Nomenclature data for Pleomeris triticina

Puccinia triticina Erikss. 1899 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

≡Puccinia perplexans var. triticina (Erikss.) Z. Urb. 1967

≡Puccinia persistens subsp. triticina (Erikss.) Z. Urb. & J. Marková 1998

≡Puccinia persistens var. triticina (Erikss.) Z. Urb. & J. Marková 1977

≡Puccinia recondita f. sp. triticina (Erikss.) D.M. Hend. 1963  Note: As comb. nov., but f. sp. have no standing in the ICBN.

≡Pleomeris triticina (Erikss.) Syd. 1921

= Puccinia dispersa f. sp. tritici Erikss. 1894  Note: No standing in the ICBN.

    ≡ Puccinia recondita f. sp. tritici (Erikss.) C. O. Johnston 1963  Note: No standing in the ICBN.

Notes: Although Cummins (1971) listed this as a member of the Puccinia recondita species complex, most recent authors have been treating this as a distinct species.

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Wheat leaf rust.

Host: Poaceae, especially Triticum spp.
