Nomenclature data for Pleosphaerulina oryzae

Leptosphaerulina australis McAlpine 1902 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

= Pleospora gaeumannii E. Müll. 1951  Note: As 'gaeumanni'

    ≡ Pseudoplea gaeumannii (E. Müll.) Wehm. 1955

    ≡ Leptosphaerulina gaeumannii (E. Müll.) Wehm. 1961

= Pleosphaerulina oryzae I. Miyake 1910

    ≡ Pringsheimia oryzae (T. Miyake) Hara 1959

Distribution: Widely distributed.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Numerous hosts.
