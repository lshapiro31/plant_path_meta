= Leptosphaerulina vignae Tehon & G.L. Stout 1928

= Pleosphaerulina zeicola G.L. Stout 1930

Notes: Booth (1967) and Irwin (1985) diagree on the synonymy of this species.

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Multiple genera in multiple families.
