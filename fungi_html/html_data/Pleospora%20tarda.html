Nomenclature data for Pleospora tarda

Stemphylium botryosum Wallr. 1886 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

= Macrosporium commune Rabenh. 1870

= Pleospora tarda E.G. Simmons 1986 [1985]

Notes: Use of oldest epithet in correct genus blocked by Stemphylium commune (Rabenh.) N.F. Buchw. (1939).

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Stems, leaves.

Disease Note: Black mold rot, leaf blight, purple spot disease of asparagus.

Host: Numerous hosts.
