Nomenclature data for Pleurostomophora richardsiae

Pleurostoma richardsiae (Nannf.) Réblová & Jaklitsch 2015 (Ascomycetes, Calosphaeriales)

≡ Cadophora richardsiae Nannf. 1934

≡Phialophora richardsiae (Nannf.) Conant 1937

≡Pleurostomophora richardsiae (Nannf.) L. Mostert, W. Gams & Crous 2004

= Cadophora brunnescens R.W. Davidson 1935

    ≡ Phialophora brunnescens (R.W. Davidson) Conant 1937

= Phialophora calyciformis G. Sm. 1962

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Wood.

Disease Note: Wood streaking, canker; also a human pathogen.

Host: Olea europaea (Oleaceae), Vitis vinifera (Vitaceae), Prunus dulcis (Rosaceae) and reports from diverse hosts.
