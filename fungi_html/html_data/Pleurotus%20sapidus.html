Nomenclature data for Pleurotus sapidus

Pleurotus ostreatus (Jacq. : Fr.) P. Kumm. 1871 (Basidiomycetes, Agaricales)

≡ Agaricus ostreatus Jacq. : Fr. 1774  Note: Sanctioned by Fries, Syst. Mycol. I:182, 1821.

= Pleurotus limpidus (Fr.) Gill. 1887

= Pleurotus salignus (Pers. : Fr.) P. Kumm. 1871

= Pleurotus sapidus Kalchbr. 1887

Notes: Type of the genus Pleurotus, nom. cons. Peterson & Krisai-Greilhuber (1996) have selected an epitype and designated an engraving by Jacquin as lectotype.

Distribution: Europe, North America, Asia, Africa.

Substrate: Living and dead wood.

Disease Note: White rot; of uncertain pathogenicity and primarily saprobic.

Host: Various woody plant families.

Supporting Literature:

Petersen, R.H., and Krisai-greilhuber, I. 1996. An epitype specimen for Pleurotus ostreatus. Mycol. Res. 100: 229-235.

Vail, M.E., Gubler, W.D., and Adaskaveg, J.E. 1995. First report of Pleurotus ostreatus causing a wood rot of grapevines in California vineyards. Pl. Dis. 79: 1187.
