Notes: Per Wijayawardene et al (2014), the link between Sydowia polyspora and Sphaeronema pythiophilum has not been proven by molecular analysis and must be considered tenative. From Hormonema dematioides - Wood-bluing fungus (Hermanides-Nijhof. Aureobasidium and allied genera. Stud. Mycol. 15:168. 1977). Hormonema dematioides is a synanamorph of Sclerophoma pithyophila.

Distribution: Worldwide.

Substrate: Branches, needles.

Disease Note: Wound pathogen associated with pine needle midge scausing leaf blight and dieback. Commonly an endophyte or saprobe.

Host: Cupressaceae, Pinaceae.
