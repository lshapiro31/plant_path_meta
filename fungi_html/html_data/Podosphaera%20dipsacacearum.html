Nomenclature data for Podosphaera dipsacacearum

Podosphaera dipsacacearum (Tul. & C. Tul.) U. Braun & S. Takam. 2000 (Ascomycetes, Erysiphales)

≡ Erysiphe dipsacacearum Tul. & C. Tul. 1861

      Variant spelling Erysiphe dipsacearum Tul. & C. Tul. 1861

≡Sphaerotheca dipsacacearum (Tul. & C. Tul.) L. Junell 1967

Notes: The epithet dipsacearum is an orthographical variant.

Distribution: Asia, Europe, North America.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Powdery mildew.

Host: Cephalaria spp., Dipsacus spp., Knautia spp., Morina persica, Pterocephalus spp., Scabiosa spp., Tremastelma palaestinum (Dipsacaceae).
