Nomenclature data for Podosphaera drabae

Podosphaera drabae (Juel) U. Braun & S. Takam. 2000 (Ascomycetes, Erysiphales)

≡ Sphaerotheca drabae Juel 1890

≡Desetangsia drabae (Juel) Nieuwl. 1916

≡Sphaerotheca fuliginea f. drabae (Juel) Jacz. 1926

Distribution: Asia, Europe.

Substrate: Living leaves, stems, pods.

Disease Note: Powdery mildew.

Host: Multiple genera and multiple species of Brassicaceae.

Supporting Literature:

Braun, U., and Takamatsu, S. 2000. Phylogeny of Erysiphe, Microsphaera, Uncinula (Erysipheae) and Cystotheca, Podosphaera, Sphaerotheca (Cystotheceae) inferred from rDNA ITS sequences - some taxonomic consequences. Schlechtendalia 4: 1-33.

Braun, U., and Cook, R.T.A. 2012. Taxonomy Manual of the Erysiphales (Powdery Mildews). CBS Biodivers. Ser. 11: 703.
