Nomenclature data for Podosphaera erodii

Podosphaera erodii (Durieu & Mont.) U. Braun & S. Takam. 2000 (Ascomycetes, Erysiphales)

≡ Erysiphe erodii Durieu & Mont. 1849

≡Sphaerotheca erodii (Durieu & Mont.) Rayss 1940

≡Sphaerotheca macularis f. erodii (Durieu & Mont.) Jacz. 1926

Distribution: Africa (Northern), Asia, Europe, Oceania (Australia).

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Powdery mildew.

Host: Biebersteinia, Erodium (Geraniaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Braun, U., and Takamatsu, S. 2000. Phylogeny of Erysiphe, Microsphaera, Uncinula (Erysipheae) and Cystotheca, Podosphaera, Sphaerotheca (Cystotheceae) inferred from rDNA ITS sequences - some taxonomic consequences. Schlechtendalia 4: 1-33.
