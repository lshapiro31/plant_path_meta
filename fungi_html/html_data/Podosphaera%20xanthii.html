Nomenclature data for Podosphaera xanthii

Podosphaera xanthii (Castagne) U. Braun & Shishkoff 2000 (Ascomycetes, Erysiphales)

≡ Erysiphe xanthii Castagne 1845

≡Sphaerotheca xanthii (Castagne) L. Junell 1966

= Oidium balsaminae Rajd. 1966

    ≡ Fibroidium balsaminae (Rajd.) U. Braun & R.T.A. Cook 2012

= Meliola calendulae Malbr. & Roum. 1886

    ≡ Sphaerotheca calendulae (Malbr. & Roum.) Malbr. 1888

    ≡ Sphaerotheca fuliginea f. calendulae (Malbr. & Roum.) Jacz. 1926

    ≡ Albigo calendulae (Malbr. & Roum.) Kuntze 1898

= Sphaerotheca caricae-papayae Tanda & U. Braun 1985  Note: From Podosphaera caricae-papayae - The anamorph is not Oidium caricae as previously supposed by Braun (1987).

    ≡ Podosphaera caricae-papayae (Tanda & U. Braun) U. Braun & S. Takam. 2000

= Oidium caricicola J.M. Yen & Chin C. Wang 1973

= Oidium citrulli J.M. Yen & Chin C. Wang 1973

= Sphaerotheca fuliginea f. cucurbitae Jacz. 1926

     Sphaerotheca cucurbitae Z.Y. Zhao 1979

  Variant spelling Sphaerotheca fusca var. cucumae Y.S. Paul & V. Thakur 2006  Note: Nom. inval. via ICBN Art. 37.6.

= Erysiphe fuscata Berk. & M.A. Curtis 1876

    ≡ Sphaerotheca fuscata (Berk. & M.A. Curtis) Serbinov 1891

  Variant spelling Sphaerotheca heteropogoni Y.S. Paul & V. Thakur 2006  Note: Nom. inval. via ICBN Art. 37.6.

= Sphaerotheca indica Patw. 1964

= Euoidium leguminosarum Y.S. Paul & J.N. Kapoor 1987

= Sphaerotheca microcarpa Haszl. 1878

= Oidium saintpauliae Gorlenko ex U. Braun 1995

= Sphaerotheca verbenae Savul. & Negru 1955

Notes: Acording to Braun and Takamatsu (2000), the name Sphaerotheca cucurbitae is not valid. P. xanthii is a species complex awaiting revision, per Braun & Cook (2012).

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Living flowers, stems, leaves.

Disease Note: Powdery mildew.

Host: Multiple genera in multiple famililes.
