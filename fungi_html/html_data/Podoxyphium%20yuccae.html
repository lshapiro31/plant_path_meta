Nomenclature data for Podoxyphium yuccae

Podoxyphium yuccae Bat. & Nascim. 1963 (Ascomycetes, Incertae sedis)

Distribution: Asia (Brunei), North America (Caribbean, Panama), South America (Brazil).

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Saprobic.

Host: Multiple genera in multiple families.

Supporting Literature:

Minter, D.W., Rodríguez Hernández, M., and Mena Portales, J. 2001. Fungi of the Caribbean: an annotated checklist. PDMS Publishing, 946 pages.
