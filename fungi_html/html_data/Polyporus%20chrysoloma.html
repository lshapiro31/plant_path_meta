Nomenclature data for Polyporus chrysoloma

Porodaedalea chrysoloma (Fr.) Fiasson & Niemelä 1984 (Basidiomycetes, Hymenochaetales)

≡ Polyporus chrysoloma Fr. 1861

≡Phellinus chrysoloma (Fr.) Donk 1971

Notes: For additional synonyms, see Index Fungorum.

Distribution: Europe; per Brazee & Lindner (2013), P. chrysoloma s.s. does not occur in North America.

Disease Note: White pocket rot.

Host: Picea spp. (Pinaceae); host records from other genera and families represent different fungi.

Supporting Literature:

Brazee, N.J., Marra, R.E., and Wick, R.L. 2012. Genotypic diversity of Armillaria gallica from mixed oak forests in Massachusetts. Mycologia 104: 53-61.

Tomsovsky, M., Sedlak, P., and Jankovsky, L. 2010. Species recognition and phylogenetic relationships of European Porodaedalea (Basidiomycota, Hymenochaetales). Mycol. Progr. 9: 225-233.
