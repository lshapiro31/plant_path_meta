Nomenclature data for Polyporus cincinnatus

Laetiporus cincinnatus (Morgan) Burds., Banik & T.J. Volk 1998 (Basidiomycetes, Polyporales)

≡ Polyporus cincinnatus Morgan 1885

Distribution: North America (USA; eastern).

Substrate: Roots, butt.

Disease Note: Root rot, butt rot; also saprobic.

Host: Quercus spp. (Fagaceae), Acer saccharum (Sapindaceae), Celtis occidentalis (Cannabaceae).
