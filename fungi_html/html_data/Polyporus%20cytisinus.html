Nomenclature data for Polyporus cytisinus

Rigidoporus ulmarius (Sowerby : Fr.) Imazeki 1952 (Basidiomycetes, Polyporales)

≡ Boletus ulmarius Sowerby 1797

≡Fomitopsis ulmaria (Sowerby : Fr.) Bondartsev & Singer 1941

≡Fomes ulmarius (Sowerby : Fr.) Gill. 1874

= Polyporus cytisinus Berk. 1836

    ≡ Fomes cytisinus (Berk.) Gillet 1874

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: On dead wood or wounds of living trees.

Disease Note: Heart rot.

Host: Numerous hardwood and to a lesser extent, softwood hosts.
