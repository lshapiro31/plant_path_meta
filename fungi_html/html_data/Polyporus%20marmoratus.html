Nomenclature data for Polyporus marmoratus

= Polyporus sclerodermeus Lév. 1846

    ≡ Fomes sclerodermeus (Lév.) Cooke 1888

Distribution: AZ, southeastern United States; tropical western hemisphere.

Substrate: On living or dead hardwoods.

Disease Note: Butt rot, white heart rot.

Host: Various woody plants.
