Nomenclature data for Polyporus microporus

Rigidoporus microporus (Sw. : Fr.) Overeem 1924 (Basidiomycetes, Polyporales)

≡ Boletus microporus Sw. 1806

≡Rigidoporus lignosus (Klotzsch) Imazeki 1952

≡Polyporus microporus (Sw. : Fr.) Fr. 1821

= Polyporus lignosus Klotzsch 1833

Notes: According to Oghenekaro et al., Rigidoporus microporus may consist of three species, separated by continent. For additional synonyms, see Index Fungorum.

Distribution: Tropical to subtrpical America, Africa and Asia; see notes.

Substrate: Roots, wood.

Disease Note: White root rot.

Host: Numerous hosts; a major pathogen of Hevea brasiliensis (Euphorbiaceae).

Supporting Literature:
