Nomenclature data for Polyporus munzii

Inonotus munzii (Lloyd) Gilb. 1969 (Basidiomycetes, Hymenochaetales)

≡ Polyporus munzii Lloyd 1922

Distribution: North America (USA; AZ).

Substrate: Heartwood.

Disease Note: Heartrot.

Host: Platanus wrightii (Plantanaceae), Populus spp., Salix spp. (Salicaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Gilbertson, R.L. 1969. A new species of Inonotus on southwestern sycamores. Southw. Naturalist 14: 123-125.
