Nomenclature data for Polyporus pini

Porodaedalea pini (Brot.) Murrill 1905 (Basidiomycetes, Hymenochaetales)

≡ Boletus pini Brot. 1804

≡Inonotus pini (Brot.) Teixeira 1992

≡Phellinus pini (Brot.) Bondartsev & Singer 1941

≡Polyporus pini (Brot.) Pers. 1825

≡Trametes pini (Brot.) Fr. 1838

= Boletus pini Thore 1803

    ≡ Fomes pini (Thore) P. Karst. 1882

Notes: Boletus pini Brot. sanctioned by Fries. ------ The combination Phellinus pini is not, as reported by some sources, attibutable to A. Ames. ------ For additional synonyms, see Index Fungorum.

Distribution: Europe; per Brazee & Lindner (2012), North American records represent species yet to be described.

Substrate: Wood of living trees.

Disease Note: White heart rot; a cause of great losses in forestry.

Host: Conifers.
