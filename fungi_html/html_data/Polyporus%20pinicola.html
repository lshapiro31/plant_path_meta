Nomenclature data for Polyporus pinicola

Fomitopsis pinicola (Sw. : Fr.) P. Karst. 1881 (Basidiomycetes, Polyporales)

≡ Boletus pinicola Sw. : Fr. 1810

≡Fomes pinicola (Sw. : Fr.) Fr. 1849

≡Placodes pinicola (Sw. : Fr.) Pat. 1887

≡Polyporus pinicola (Sw. : Fr.) Fr. 1821  Note: Sanctioned by Fries Syst. Mycol. I:372.

≡Pseudofomes pinicola (Sw. : Fr.) Lázaro Ibiza 1916

≡Trametes pinicola (Sw. : Fr.) P. Karst. 1882

= Favolus pinihalepensis Pat. 1897

= Fomes subungulatus Murrill 1908

Distribution: Temperate northern hemisphere.

Substrate: On wood, usually conifers. Heart rot of living conifers and black cherry; important in decay of lumber and wood in service.

Disease Note: Heart rot of living conifers and black cherry; important in decay of lumber and wood in service.

Host: Primarily on Pinaceae; also on other woody plants.

Supporting Literature:

Gilbertson, R.L., and Ryvarden, L. 1986. North American Polypores Vol. 1. Abortiporus - Lindtneria. Fungiflora, Oslo, 433 pages.
