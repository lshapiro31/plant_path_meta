Nomenclature data for Polyporus schweinitzii

Phaeolus schweinitzii (Fr. : Fr.) Pat. 1900 (Basidiomycetes, Polyporales)

≡ Polyporus schweinitzii Fr. : Fr. 1821

Notes: For numerous additional synonyms, see Index Fungorum.

Distribution: Temperate northern hemisphere.

Substrate: On stumps or buried roots.

Disease Note: Brown root and butt rot.

Host: Gymnosperms.
