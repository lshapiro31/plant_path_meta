Nomenclature data for Polyporus serialis

Antrodia serialis (Fr. : Fr.) Donk 1966 (Basidiomycetes, Polyporales)

≡ Polyporus serialis Fr. : Fr. 1821

≡Trametes serialis (Fr. : Fr.) Fr. 1874

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: On wood, usually of conifers.

Disease Note: Brown cubical heart-rot.

Host: Numerous hosts.

Supporting Literature:

Rajchenberg, M., and Robledo, G. 2013. Pathogenic polypores in Argentina. Forest Pathol. 43: 171-184.
