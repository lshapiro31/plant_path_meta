Nomenclature data for Polyporus squamosus

Polyporus squamosus (Huds. : Fr.) Fr. 1821 (Basidiomycetes, Polyporales)

≡ Boletus squamosus Huds. 1778

= Polyporus westii Murrill 1938

Notes: For numerous additional synonyms, see Index Fungorum.

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Wood.

Disease Note: White heartrot of wounded trees; also saprobic.

Host: Hardwoods.

Supporting Literature:
