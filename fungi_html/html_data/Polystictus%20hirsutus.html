Nomenclature data for Polystictus hirsutus

Trametes hirsuta (Wulfen : Fr.) Quél. 1939 (Basidiomycetes, Polyporales)

≡ Boletus hirsutus Wulfen 1791

≡Coriolus hirsutus (Wulfen : Fr.) Quél. 1897

≡Polyporus hirsutus (Wulfen : Fr.) Fr. 1821

≡Polystictus hirsutus (Wulfen : Fr.) Fr. 1821

Notes: For numerous aditional synonyms, see Index Fungorum.

Distribution: Temperate regions.

Substrate: Sapwood, heartwood.

Disease Note: An opportunistic pathogen entering through wounds and causing a white rot of living trees, persisting as a saprobe after tree death.

Host: Numerous hard and softwood hosts.
