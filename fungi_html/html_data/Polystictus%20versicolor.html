Nomenclature data for Polystictus versicolor

Trametes versicolor (L. : Fr.) Pilát 1920 [1921] (Basidiomycetes, Polyporales)

≡ Boletus versicolor L. 1753

≡Coriolus versicolor (L. : Fr.) Quél. 1886

≡Polyporus versicolor (L. : Fr.) Fr. 1821

≡Polystictus versicolor (L. : Fr.) Fr. 1851

Notes: Several varieties based on variation in gross morphology have been described; for numerous additional synonyms (many varieties and forma speciales), see Index Fungorum.

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Wood.

Disease Note: Commonly saprobic; also opportunistically pathogenic, causing papery bark, weak bud unions, dieback and white rot.

Host: Numerous hosts.
