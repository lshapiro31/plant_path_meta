Nomenclature data for Polystigma fulvum

Polystigma fulvum DC. : Fr. 1815 (Ascomycetes, Phyllachorales)

≡Dothidea fulva (DC. : Fr.) Fr. 1823

≡Hypocrea fulva (DC. : Fr.) de Not. 1865

= Xyloma aurantiacum Schleich. 1807

= Sphaeria ochracea Wahlenb. 1812

    ≡ Polystigma ochraceum (Wahlenb.) Sacc. 1876

= Polystigmina pallescens Petr. 1954

Notes: Polystigma fulvum adopted by Fries and sanctioned over the earlier name Sphaeria ochracea Wahlenb. (an illegitimate name per article 53.1; non Sphaeria ochracea Pers. 1801); by extension, Polystigma ochraceum (Wahlenb.) Sacc. (erroneously (Fr.) Sacc.) is also invalid. Xyloma aurantiacum Schleich. nom. inval. (no description). For additional synonyms, see Cannon (1996).

Distribution: Europe, Asia.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Red leaf blotch; per Cannon (1996), while infections can be extensive, they are not known to cause serious damage to host trees.

Host: Prunus spp. (Rosaceae); per Cannon (1996) records on almonds may be a separate species.
