Nomenclature data for Pseudocercospora butleri

Pseudocercospora butleri (Syd.) U. Braun 2000 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Fusicladium butleri Syd. 1916

= Pseudocercospora jasminicola Deighton 1976

     Cercospora jasminicola A.S. Mull. & Chupp 1936  Note: Invalid name.

Distribution: Widespread, especially subtropical and tropical regions.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Jasmium spp. (Oleaceae).
