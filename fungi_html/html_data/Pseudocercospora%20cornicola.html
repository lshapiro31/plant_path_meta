Nomenclature data for Pseudocercospora cornicola

Pseudocercospora cornicola (Tracy & Earle) Y.L. Guo & X.J. Liu 1989 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Cercospora cornicola Tracy & Earle 1896

Distribution: Widespread in northern hemisphere based on scattered reports.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Cornus spp. (Cornaceae).

Supporting Literature:
