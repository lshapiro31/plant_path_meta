Nomenclature data for Pseudocercospora cotizensis

Pseudocercospora cotizensis (A.S. Mull. & Chupp) Deighton 1976 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Cercospora cotizensis A.S. Mull. & Chupp 1950

[= Cercospora crotalariae Syd. 1930 - illegitimate later homonym, not included in search]  Note: Not Sacc. 1913. Nomen illeg.

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Crotalaria spp. (Fabaceae).
