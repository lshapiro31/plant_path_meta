Nomenclature data for Pseudocercospora cypripedii

Pseudocercospora cypripedii (Ellis & Dearn.) U. Braun & Crous 2003 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Cercospora cypripedii Ellis & Dearn. 1899

Distribution: North America.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Cypripedium spp. (Orchidaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Braun, U., Crous, P.W., and Nakashima, C. 2014. Cercosporoid fungi (Mycosphaerellaceae) 2. Species on monocots (Acoraceae to Xyridaceae, excluding Poaceae). IMA Fungus 5: 203-390.
