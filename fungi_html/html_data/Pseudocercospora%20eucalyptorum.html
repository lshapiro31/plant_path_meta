Nomenclature data for Pseudocercospora eucalyptorum

Pseudocercospora eucalyptorum Crous, M.J. Wingf., Marasas & B. Sutton 1989 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

= Pseudocercospora pseudoeucalyptorum Crous 2004

Distribution: Africa (South Africa), Asia, Europe (Spain), New Zealand.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Eucalyptus spp. (Myrtaceae).
