Nomenclature data for Pseudocercospora hurae

Pseudocercospora hurae (F. Stevens) Deighton 1976 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Cercospora hurae F. Stevens 1917

Distribution: North America, South America.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Hura crepitans (Euphorbiaceae).
