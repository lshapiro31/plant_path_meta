Nomenclature data for Pseudocercospora irregulariramosa

Pallidocercospora irregulariramosa (Crous & M.J. Wingf.) Crous & M.J. Wingf. 2012 (, )

≡ Pseudocercospora irregulariramosa Crous & M.J. Wingf. 1997

= Mycosphaerella irregulariramosa Crous & M.J. Wingf. 1997

Distribution: Africa (South Africa).

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Eucalyptus saligna, E. grandis (Myrtaceae).
