Nomenclature data for Pseudocercospora juniperi

Pseudocercospora juniperi (Ellis & Everh.) B. Sutton & Hodges 1990 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Cercospora sequoiae var. juniperi Ellis & Everh. 1887

      Variant spelling Cercospora sequojae var. juniperi Ellis & Everh. 1887

Distribution: Asia, North America (USA).

Substrate: Needles.

Disease Note: Needle disease.

Host: Cupressaceae.

Supporting Literature:

Braun, U., Nakashima, C., and Crous, P.W. 2013. Cercosporoid fungi (Mycosphaerellaceae) 1. Species on other fungi, Pteridophyta and Gymnospermae. IMA Fungus 4: 265-345.
