Nomenclature data for Pseudocercospora kurimensis

Pseudocercospora kurimensis (Fukui) U. Braun 1996 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Cercospora kurimensis Fukui 1933

      Variant spelling Cercospora kurimaensis Fukui 1933

= Cercospora nerii-indici W. Yamam. 1934

= Mycosphaerella nerii-odori A.K. Pande 1975

Notes: From Mycosphaerella nerii-odori - As 'nerium-odorum'.

Distribution: Widespread, especially Asia and Oceania.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Nerium spp. (Apocynaceae).
