Nomenclature data for Pseudocercospora mombin

Pseudocercospora mombin (Petr. & Cif.) Deighton 1976 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Cercospora mombin Petr. & Cif. 1932

Notes: Includes the type variety.

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Spondias spp. (Anacardiaceae). And a report from Viburnum cylindricum (Caprifoliaceae)..
