Nomenclature data for Pseudocercospora mori

Pseudocercospora mori (Hara) Deighton 1976 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Cercospora mori Hara 1918

[= Cercospora mori É.J. Marchal & Steyaert 1929 - illegitimate later homonym, not included in search]  Note: Not Hara 1918. Nomen illeg.

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Morus spp. (Moraceae).
