Nomenclature data for Pseudocercospora neoliquidambaris

Pseudocercospora liquidambaricola (J.M. Yen) U. Braun 2000 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Cercospora liquidambaricola J.M. Yen 1978

≡Cercoseptoria liquidambaricola (J.M. Yen) J.M. Yen 1981

≡Pseudocercospora neoliquidambaris C. Nakash. & Tak. Kobay. 2002

= Pseudocercospora liquidambaris Goh & W.H. Hsieh 1990

Notes: From Pseudocercospora neoliquidambaris - Nomen illeg. Superfluous. The epithet 'liquidambaricola' should have been adopted. From Cercospora liquidambaris - Invalid name. No Latin (Japanese description only). Would be a nomen illeg. (homonym) as not Cooke & Ellis ex G.F. Atk. 1892.

Distribution: Asia, North America.

Substrate: On living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Liquidambar spp.(Altingiaceae).
