Nomenclature data for Pseudocercospora neriella

Pseudocercospora neriella (Sacc.) Deighton 1976 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Cercospora neriella Sacc. 1881

≡Cercoseptoria neriella (Sacc.) Dingley 1970

Notes: Records by Hsieh & Goh and Guo in 1990 and 1998, respectively, refer to Pseudocercospora kurimensis (Crous and Braun 2003).

Distribution: Widespread, especially warmer regions.

Disease Note: Leaf spot. Fruit spot.

Host: Himatanthus obovatus, Nerium spp. (Apocynaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Chupp, C. 1954. Monograph of the fungus genus Cercospora. Published by the Author, Ithaca, New York, 667 pages.

Crous, P.W., and Braun, U. 2003. Mycosphaerella and its anamorphs: 1. Names published in Cercospora and Passalora. Centraalbureau voor Schimmelcultures, Utrecht, 571 pages.
