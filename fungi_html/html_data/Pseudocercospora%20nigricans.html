Nomenclature data for Pseudocercospora nigricans

Pseudocercospora nigricans (Cooke) Deighton 1976 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Cercospora nigricans Cooke 1883

≡Phaeoisariopsis nigricans (Cooke) L.G. Brown & Morgan-Jones 1977

= Cercospora atromaculans Ellis & Everh. 1887

  Variant spelling Cercospora atro-maculans Ellis & Everh. 1887

= Cercospora torae Tharp 1917

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Several taxa of Fabaceae especially Cassia spp. and Senna spp. Reports from Aralia were based on host misidentifications.
