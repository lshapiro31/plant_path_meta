Nomenclature data for Pseudocercospora nymphaeacea

Pseudocercospora nymphaeacea (Cooke & Ellis) Deighton 1987 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Cercospora nymphaeacea Cooke & Ellis 1878

≡Cercoseptoria nymphaeacea (Cooke & Ellis) Deighton 1976

= Cercospora exotica Ellis & Everh. 1893

= Cercospora nelumbonis Tharp 1917

     Cercospora nelumbii Tharp  Note: Citation error by Saccardo. Invalid name.

  Variant spelling Cercospora nelumbinis Tharp 1917  Note: Spelled this way by Crous and Braun (2003). Correct variant uncertain.

    ≡ Cercosporina nelumbonis (Tharp) Sacc. 1931

     Cercosporina nelumbii (Tharp) Sacc.  Note: Citation error by Saccardo. Invalid name.

Notes: The nomenclature of this fungus is riddled with mistakes that have been perpetuated in the literature. Indian records on Nymphoides indicum are doubtful (Crous and Braun 2003).

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: On living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Nelumbo spp. (Nelumbonaceae), Nymphaeaceae, especially Nymphaea spp.
