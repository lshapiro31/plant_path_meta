Nomenclature data for Pseudocercospora oenotherae

Pseudocercospora oenotherae (Ellis & Everh.) Y.L. Guo & X.J. Liu 1992 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Cercospora oenotherae Ellis & Everh. 1894

Distribution: Asia, North America (USA).

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Oenothera spp. (Onagraceae).
