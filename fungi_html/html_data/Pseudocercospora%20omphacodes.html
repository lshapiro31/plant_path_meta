Nomenclature data for Pseudocercospora omphacodes

Passalora omphacodes (Ellis & Holw.) Crous & U. Braun 1996 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Cercospora omphacodes Ellis & Holw. 1885  Note: As 'omphakodes'.

≡Pseudocercospora omphacodes (Ellis & Holw.) U. Braun & Rogerson 1993  Note: Misapplied (Crous and Braun 2003).

= Cercospora phlogina Peck 1911 [1910]

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Phlox spp., Polemonium reptans (Polemoniaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Chupp, C. 1954. Monograph of the fungus genus Cercospora. Published by the Author, Ithaca, New York, 667 pages.

Crous, P.W., and Braun, U. 2003. Mycosphaerella and its anamorphs: 1. Names published in Cercospora and Passalora. Centraalbureau voor Schimmelcultures, Utrecht, 571 pages.
