Nomenclature data for Pseudocercospora phyllanthi

Pseudocercospora phyllanthi (Chupp) Deighton 1976 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Cercospora phyllanthi Chupp 1931  Note: As 'phyllanthae'.

= Cercospora pakistanica S.A. Khan & Kamal 1963

= Cercospora phyllanthi-niruri J.M. Yen 1967  Note: New name due to occupation of previous epithet in Cercospora.

    ≡ Pseudocercospora phyllanthi-niruri (J.M. Yen) J.M. Yen 1980

  [≡ Cercospora phyllanthicola J.M. Yen 1965 - illegitimate later homonym, not included in search]  Note: Not S.A. Khan & Kamal 1963. Nomen illeg.

= Cercospora phyllanthi-pentandri J.M. Yen & Gilles 1975 [1974]

    ≡ Pseudocercospora phyllanthi-pentandri (J.M. Yen & Gilles) J.M. Yen 1979 [1978]

= Cercospora phyllanthina Petr. & Cif. 1932

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Phyllanthus spp. (Phyllanthaceae).

Supporting Literature:
