Nomenclature data for Pseudocercospora polysciatis-pinnatae

Pseudocercospora panacis (Thirum. & Chupp) Guo & X. Liu 1992 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Cercospora panacis Thirum. & Chupp 1948

≡Passalora panacis (Thirum. & Chupp) Crous & U. Braun 2001

= Pseudocercospora polysciatis-pinnatae U. Braun & Mouch. 1999

Distribution: Africa, Asia, Oceania, South America (Venezuela).

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spots.

Host: Multiple genera of Araliaceae.

Supporting Literature:
