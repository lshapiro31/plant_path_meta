Nomenclature data for Pseudocercospora protearum

Pseudocercospora protearum var. protearum (Cooke) U. Braun & Crous 2002 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Cercospora protearum Cooke 1883

≡Cercostigmina protearum (Cooke) U. Braun & Crous 1994

≡Pseudocercospora protearum (Cooke) U. Braun & Crous 2002

≡Stigmina protearum (Cooke) M.B. Ellis 1972

≡Cercospora protearum var. leucospermi Cooke 1883

≡Cercospora protearum var. protearum Cooke 1883

≡Cercostigmina protearum var. protearum (Cooke) U. Braun & Crous 1994

≡Stigmina protearum var. protearum (Cooke) M.B. Ellis 1972

Notes: Cooke validly published two varieties, but there was no type variety or typification of the type species. Ellis, confusingly, lectotypified Cercospora protearum with the type of Cercospora protearum var. leucospermi at the same time that he made a new combination in Stigmina. The correct name of the original type variety should be Cercospora protearum var. protearum even though Ellis (1972) does not mention this taxon when he makes the new combination, Stigmina protearum var. protearum.

Distribution: Africa.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Proteaceae. More restricted in the sense of the type variety.
