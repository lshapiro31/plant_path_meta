Nomenclature data for Pseudocercospora sordida

Pseudocercospora sordida (Sacc.) Deighton 1976 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Cercospora sordida Sacc. 1880

= Mycosphaerella tecomae F.A. Wolf 1943

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Living leaves and wood.

Disease Note: Leaf blight and spot.

Host: Campsis spp., Tecoma spp. (Bignoniaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Chupp, C. 1954. Monograph of the fungus genus Cercospora. Published by the Author, Ithaca, New York, 667 pages.
