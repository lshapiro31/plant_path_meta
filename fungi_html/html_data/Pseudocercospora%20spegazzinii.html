Nomenclature data for Pseudocercospora spegazzinii

Passalora spegazzinii U. Braun 1997 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡Cercospora dubia Speg. 1880  Note: Not Cercospora dubia (Riess) G. Winter 1883.

≡Pseudocercospora spegazzinii (Sacc.) Y.L. Guo & X.J. Liu 1993  Note: Misapplied, redescribed as Pseudocercospora pteroceltidis. Also, superfluous since the epithet 'dubia' was available in Pseudocercospora.

Notes: Cercospora dubia Speg. should have been the epithet used in Passalora, but Passalora dubia (Riess) U. Braun already exists in the genus. Cercospora spegazzinii is superfluous because C. dubia Speg. was cited as a synonym, thus P. spegazzinii is a new name.

Distribution: Europe (Armenia), North America (USA: FL, IA), South America (Argentina).

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Celtis spp. (Cannabaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Braun, U., and Melnik, V.A. 1997. Cercosporoid fungi from Russia and adjacent countries. Russian Academy of Sciences Komarov Botanical Institute 20: 1-112.
