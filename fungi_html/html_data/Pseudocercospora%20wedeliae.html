Nomenclature data for Pseudocercospora wedeliae

Pseudocercospora wedeliae (Kar & Mandal) Deighton 1976 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Cercospora wedeliae Kar & Mandal 1970

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Wedelia spp., Wulffia spp. (Asteraceae).
