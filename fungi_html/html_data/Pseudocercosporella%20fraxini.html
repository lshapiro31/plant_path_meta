Nomenclature data for Pseudocercosporella fraxini

Pseudocercosporella fraxini (Ellis & Kellerm.) U. Braun 1994 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Cercospora fraxini Ellis & Kellerm. 1885

≡Cylindrosporium fraxini (Ellis & Kellerm.) Ellis & Everh. 1885

≡Phloeospora fraxini (Ellis & Kellerm.) Höhn. 1924

= Ramularia fraxinea Davis 1915

Distribution: Northern hemisphere, mostly Europe, North America.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Fraxinus spp. (Oleaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Braun, U. 1995. A Monograph of Cercosporella, Ramularia and Allied Genera (Phytopathogenic Hyphomycetes), Vol. 1. IHW-Verlag, 333 pages.
