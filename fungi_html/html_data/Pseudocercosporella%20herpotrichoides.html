Oculimacula yallundae (Wallwork & Spooner) Crous & W. Gams 2003 (Ascomycetes, Helotiales)

≡ Tapesia yallundae Wallwork & Spooner 1988

≡Tapesia yallundae var. yallundae

  ≡Pseudocercosporella herpotrichoides (Fron) Deighton 1973

    ≡ Helgardia herpotrichoides (Fron) Crous & W. Gams 2003

    ≡ Ramulispora herpotrichoides var. herpotrichoides

    ≡ Ramulispora herpotrichoides (Fron) Arx 1983

    ≡ Ramulispora herpotrichoides var. herpotrichoides

Notes: This epithet will be proposed for conservation.

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Stems.

Disease Note: Eye spot.

Host: Poaceae.
