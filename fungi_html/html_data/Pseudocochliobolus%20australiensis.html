Nomenclature data for Pseudocochliobolus australiensis

Curvularia tsudae (Tsuda & Ueyama) H. Deng, Y.P. Tan & R.G. Shivas 2014 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

≡ Pseudocochliobolus australiensis Tsuda & Ueyama 1981

≡Cochliobolus australiensis (Tsuda & Ueyama) Alcorn 1983

Notes: Curvularia tsudae is a nom. nov created to prevent conflict with Curvularia australiensis (M.B. Ellis) Manamgoda (2012).

Distribution: Africa, Asia, Australia.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Poaceae.

Supporting Literature:
