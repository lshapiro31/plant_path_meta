Nomenclature data for Pseudocochliobolus pallescens

Curvularia pallescens Boedijn 1933 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

= Curvularia leonensis M.B. Ellis 1966

= Pseudocochliobolus pallescens Tsuda & Ueyama 1983

    ≡ Cochliobolus pallescens (Tsuda & Ueyama) Sivan. 1987

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Living flowers, leaves, seeds.

Disease Note: Leaf diseases and other rots including withertip.

Host: Multiple genera in multiple families.
