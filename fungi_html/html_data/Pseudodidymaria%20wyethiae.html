Nomenclature data for Pseudodidymaria wyethiae

Pseudodidymaria wyethiae (Ellis & Everh.) U. Braun 1993 (Ascomycetes, Incertae sedis)

≡ Marssonia wyethiae Ellis & Everh. 1894

≡Didymaria wyethiae (Ellis & Everh.) Solheim 1970

≡Marssonina wyethiae (Ellis & Everh.) Magnus 1906

= Didymaria conferta Syd. & P. Syd. 1905

    ≡ Ramularia conferta (Syd. & P. Syd.) U. Braun 1988

Distribution: North America (western USA).

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Helianthella uniflora, Wyethia spp. (Asteraceae).

Supporting Literature:

Braun, U. 1998. A Monograph of Cercosporella, Ramularia and Allied Genera (Phytopathogenic Hyphomycetes). IHW-Verlag 2: 337.
