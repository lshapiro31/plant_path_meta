Nomenclature data for Pseudodiscosia avenae

Spermospora avenae (R. Sprague & Aar.G. Johnson) R. Sprague 1950 (Ascomycetes, Incertae sedis)

≡ Pseudodiscosia avenae R. Sprague & Aar.G. Johnson 1936

Distribution: Northwestern North America; Turkey.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Red leather leaf; stunting, leaf deformation and discoloration.

Host: Avena spp., Arrhenatherum elatius (Poaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Braun, U. 1995. A Monograph of Cercosporella, Ramularia and Allied Genera (Phytopathogenic Hyphomycetes), Vol. 1. IHW-Verlag, 333 pages.
