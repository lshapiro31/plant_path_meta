Nomenclature data for Pseudoperonospora celtidis

Pseudoperonospora celtidis (Waite) G.W. Wilson 1907 (Oomycetes, Peronosporales)

≡ Peronospora celtidis Waite 1892

≡Peronoplasmopara celtidis (Waite) G.P. Clinton 1905

≡Plasmopara celtidis (Waite) Berl. 1898

≡Pseudoperonospora celtidis var. celtidis

Distribution: Asia, North America (USA: Eastern states).

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Downy mildew.

Host: Celtis spp. (Cannabaceae).
