Nomenclature data for Pseudoperonospora sparsa

Peronospora sparsa Berk. 1862 (Oomycetes, Peronosporales)

≡Peronoplasmopara sparsa (Berk.) Uljan. 1967

≡Pseudoperonospora sparsa (Berk.) Jacz. 1928

= Peronospora rosae-gallicae Savul. & Rayss 1934

= Peronospora rubi Rabenh. ex J. Schröt. 1886

= Peronospora sanguisorbae Gäum. 1923

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Leaves, stems, flowers.

Disease Note: Downy mildew.

Host: Rosaceae, especially Rosa.
