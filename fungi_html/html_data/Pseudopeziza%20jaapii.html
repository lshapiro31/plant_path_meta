Notes: Blumeriella jaapii has been for conservation.

Distribution: Widespread. From Microgloeum pruni - North America; temperate Europe.

Substrate: On living leaves.

Disease Note: Shot hole, leaf spot.

Host: Especially Prunus spp. (Rosaceae).
