Nomenclature data for Pseudovalsa modonia

Pseudovalsa modonia (Tul.) Höhn. 1918 (Ascomycetes, Diaporthales)

≡ Melanconis modonia Tul. 1863

= Coryneum kunzei var. castaneae Sacc. 1884

    ≡ Coryneum castaneae (Sacc. & Roum.) Petr. 1970

= Stilbospora modonia Sacc. 1884

    ≡ Coryneum modonium (Sacc.) Griffon & Maubl. 1910

Distribution: Temperate northern hemisphere.

Substrate: On limbs.

Disease Note: Bark canker, twig blight.

Host: Primarily on Castanea.
