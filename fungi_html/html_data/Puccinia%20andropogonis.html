Nomenclature data for Puccinia andropogonis

Puccinia cesatii J. Schröt. 1879 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

[= Puccinia andropogonis G.H. Otth 1863 - illegitimate later homonym, not included in search]  Note: Publication year also cited by Sacc. as 1873 and 1896. Not Schwein. 1832.

Notes: Autoecious; on Poaceae.

Distribution: Southwestern United States; Mexico; China; Egypt; southern Europe; India; Iran.

Updated on Jul 07, 2005

Puccinia andropogonis Schwein. 1832 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

    Variant spelling Puccinia andropogi Schwein. 1832

≡Puccinia andropogonis var. andropogonis Schwein. 1832

= Aecidium aesculi Ellis & Kellerm. 1884

Notes: See type variety. From Puccinia andropogonis var. andropogonis - Anamorph connection established by reciprocal inoculation.

Distribution: North America.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Heteroecious rust.

Host: Aecial host, several families of angiosperms; telial host, Andropogon spp.
