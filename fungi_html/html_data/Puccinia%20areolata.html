Nomenclature data for Puccinia areolata

Puccinia areolata Dietel & Holw. 1894 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

≡Dicaeoma areolatum (Dietel & Holw.) Kuntze 1898

Distribution: Western North America.

Substrate: Living leaves, petioles, flower stalks.

Disease Note: Autoecious rust.

Host: Caltha spp. (Ranunculaceae).

Supporting Literature:
