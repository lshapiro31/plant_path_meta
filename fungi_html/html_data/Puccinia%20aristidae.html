Nomenclature data for Puccinia aristidae

Puccinia aristidae Tracy 1893 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

= Aecidium caspicum Jacz. 1900

Notes: The name P. aristidae has been misapplied to collections of P. subnitens Dietel in North America.

Distribution: Northern Africa; Middle East.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Rust.

Host: Heteroecious; aecial host - Heliotropium europaeum (Boraginaceae); telial host - Aristida spp. (Poaceae).
