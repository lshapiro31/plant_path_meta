Nomenclature data for Puccinia asarina

Puccinia asarina Kunze 1817 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

≡Micropuccinia asarina (Kunze) Arthur & H.S. Jacks. 1921

= Puccinia asari Link 1825

Notes: Puccinia asarina sensu Cooke (1871) is now regarded as P. fergusonii Berk. & Br. (1875).

Distribution: Western North America; Japan; Soviet Union.

Substrate: On living leaves.

Disease Note: Rust fungus.

Host: Asarum (Aristolochiaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Legon, N. 2013. Puccinia asarina new to Britain. Field Mycol. 14: 136-138.
