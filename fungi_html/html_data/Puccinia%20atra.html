Nomenclature data for Puccinia atra

Puccinia atra Dietel & Holw. 1897 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

Notes: Illegitimate later homonym of Puccinia atra (Link.) Spreng. (1827).

Distribution: North America (Mexico).

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Rust.

Host: Setaria grisebachii (Poaceae), Mirabilis longiflora (Nyctaginaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Demers, J.E., Liu, M., Hambleton, S., and Castlebury, L.A. 2017. Rust fungi on Panicum. Mycologia 109(1): 1-17.
