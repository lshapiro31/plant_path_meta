Nomenclature data for Puccinia barbeyi

Puccinia barbeyi (Roum.) Magnus 1883 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

≡ Oomyces barbeyi Roum. 1880

≡Aecidium barbeyi (Roum.) Roum. 1881

= Aecidium asphodeli Castagne 1842

= Puccinia heldreichiana Dietel 1889

Distribution: Africa, Asia, Europe.

Substrate: Living leaves, scapes.

Disease Note: Rust; galling, growth deformation.

Host: Asphodelus spp. (Xanthorrhoeaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Cannon, P.F. 2011. Puccinia barbeyi. I.M.I. Descr. Fungi Bact. 1872: 1-3.
