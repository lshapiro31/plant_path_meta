Nomenclature data for Puccinia cenchri

Puccinia cenchri Dietel & Holw. 1897 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

≡Puccinia cenchri var. cenchri Dietel & Holw. 1952

= Uredo cenchrophila Speg. 1909

Notes: From Uredo cenchrophila - Includes the type variety.

Distribution: Widespread.

Disease Note: Rust.

Host: Poaceae, especially Cenchrus spp.
