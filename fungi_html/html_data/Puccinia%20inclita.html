Nomenclature data for Puccinia inclita

Puccinia inclita Arthur 1919 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

≡Dicaeoma inclitum (Arthur) Arthur & Fromme 1920

Distribution: Widespread, especially Caribbean, South America.

Disease Note: Rust.

Host: Poaceae, especially Ichnanthus spp., Oplismenus spp.

Supporting Literature:

Cummins, G.B. 1971. The Rust Fungi of Cereals, Grasses and Bamboos. Springer-Verlag, New York, 570 pages.

Ramachar, P., and Cummins, G.B. 1965. The species of Puccinia on the Paniceae. Mycopathol. Mycol. Appl. 25: 7-60.
