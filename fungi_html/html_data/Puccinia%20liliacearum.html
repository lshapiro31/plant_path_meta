Nomenclature data for Puccinia liliacearum

Puccinia liliacearum Duby 1830 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

≡Dicaeoma liliacearum (Duby) Kuntze 1898

Distribution: Northeastern North America (rare); South Africa; Eurasia.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Autoecious rust: leaf damage, inhibited fowering.

Host: Ornithogalum spp. (Asparagaceae).
