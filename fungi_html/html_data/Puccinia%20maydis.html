Nomenclature data for Puccinia maydis

Puccinia sorghi Schwein. 1832 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

≡ Dicaeoma sorghi (Schwein.) Kuntze 1898

= Tilletia epiphylla Berk. & Broome 1882

= Puccinia maydis Berenger 1844

= Aecidium oxalidis Thüm. 1876

= Puccinia zeae Berenger 1851

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Rust. Maize rust.

Host: Euchlaena spp., Holcus sorghum, Sorghum vulgare, Tripsacum lanceolatum, Triticum aestivum, Zea mays (Poaceae), Oxalis spp. (Oxalidaceae).
