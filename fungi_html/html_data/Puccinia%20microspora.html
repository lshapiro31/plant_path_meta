Nomenclature data for Puccinia microspora

Puccinia microspora Dietel 1905 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

Distribution: Asia, North America, South America.

Substrate: Abaxial leaf surface.

Disease Note: Rust.

Host: Poaceae.
