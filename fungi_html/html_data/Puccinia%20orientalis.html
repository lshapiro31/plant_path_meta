Nomenclature data for Puccinia orientalis

Puccinia orientalis (Syd., P. Syd. & E.J. Butler) Arthur & Cummins 1936 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

≡ Diorchidium orientale Syd., P. Syd. & E.J. Butler 1907

= Puccinia ottochloae T.S. Ramakr. 1956

Distribution: Africa, Asia, Oceania.

Disease Note: Rust.

Host: Several taxa of Poaceae.

Supporting Literature:

Cummins, G.B. 1971. The Rust Fungi of Cereals, Grasses and Bamboos. Springer-Verlag, New York, 570 pages.

Ramachar, P., and Cummins, G.B. 1965. The species of Puccinia on the Paniceae. Mycopathol. Mycol. Appl. 25: 7-60.
