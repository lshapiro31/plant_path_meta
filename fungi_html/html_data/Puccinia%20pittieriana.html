Nomenclature data for Puccinia pittieriana

Puccinia pittieriana Henn. 1904 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

≡Micropuccinia pittieriana (Henn.) Arthur & H.S. Jacks. 1922

Notes: This taxon has nothing to do with Gerwasia pittieriana and its synonyms.

Distribution: North America (Mexico), Central and South America.

Substrate: Leaves predominantly.

Disease Note: Rust. Potato rust.

Host: Lycopersicon esculentum, Solanum spp. including Solanum tuberosum (Solanaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Arthur, J.C. 1922. Aecidiaceae in North America. Flora 7: 481-604.

Laundon, G.F., and Rainbow, A.F. 1971. Puccinia pitteriana. C.M.I. Descr. Pathog. Fungi Bact. 286: 1-2.
