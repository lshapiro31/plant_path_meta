Nomenclature data for Puccinia prunicolor

Puccinia purpurea Cooke 1876 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

≡Dicaeoma purpureum (Cooke) Kuntze 1898

= Puccinia penniseti Barclay 1891

= Puccinia prunicolor Syd., P. Syd. & E.J. Butler 1906

    ≡ Dicaeoma prunicolor (Syd., P. Syd. & E.J. Butler) Syd. 1922

= Puccinia sanguinea Dietel 1897

[= Uredo sorghi Fuckel 1871 - illegitimate later homonym, not included in search]

= Uredo sorghi Pass. 1867

= Uredo sorghi-halepensis Pat. 1903

Notes: Oldest epithet not in correct genus.

Distribution: Circumglobal in warmer regions.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Rust.

Host: Uredinial and telial stages on Poaceae, especially Sorghum spp.
