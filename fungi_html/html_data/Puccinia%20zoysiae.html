Nomenclature data for Puccinia zoysiae

Puccinia zoysiae Dietel 1902 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

≡Dicaeoma zoysiae (Dietel) Syd. 1922

Distribution: HI, central and southern United States; eastern Asia.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Heterecious rust.

Host: Aecial host - Paederia spp. (Rubiaceae); telial host - Zoysia spp. (Poaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Harada, Y. 1980. Inoculation experiments and some morphological observations on Puccinia festucae and P. zoysiae. Trans. Mycol. Soc. Japan 21: 449-455.
