Nomenclature data for Pulcherricium caeruleum

Terana caerulea (Schrad. Lam.) Kuntze 1891 (Basidiomycetes, Polyporales)

≡ Byssus caerulea Schrad. ex Lam. 1779

≡Corticium caeruleum (Schrad. ex Lam.) Fr. 1838

≡Pulcherricium caeruleum (Schrad. ex Lam.) Parmasto 1968

Notes: From Pulcherricium caeruleum - as 'coeruleum'

Distribution: Wide distribution in warm and temperate areas.

Substrate: Dead wood.

Disease Note: Saprobic; not a pathogen.

Host: Hardwoods.

Updated prior to 1989
