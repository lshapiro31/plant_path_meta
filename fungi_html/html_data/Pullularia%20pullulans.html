Nomenclature data for Pullularia pullulans

Aureobasidium pullulans (de Bary) G. Arnaud 1918 (Ascomycetes, Dothideales)

≡ Dematium pullulans de Bary 1866

≡Anthostomella pullulans (de Bary) F.T. Benn. 1928

≡Pullularia pullulans (de Bary) Berkhout 1923

= Aureobasidium vitis Viala & Boyer 1891

    ≡ Exobasidium vitis (Viala & Boyer) Prill. & Delacr. 1894

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: On leaf surfaces and other plant parts; a common contaminant.

Disease Note: Saprobic; also causing post harvest fruit rot and coalescing red rotting spots on stems.

Host: Diverse hosts.
