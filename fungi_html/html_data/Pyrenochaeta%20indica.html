Nomenclature data for Pyrenochaeta indica

Pyrenochaetopsis indica (T.S. Viswan.) Gruyter, Aveskamp & Verkley 2010 (, )

≡ Pyrenochaeta indica T.S. Viswan. 1957

≡Phoma indica (T.S. Viswan.) Gruyter & Boerema 2002

Distribution: Asia (India).

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot, associated with Melanospora sp. No data on pathogenicity.

Host: Sugarcane, Saccharum officinarum (Poaceae).
