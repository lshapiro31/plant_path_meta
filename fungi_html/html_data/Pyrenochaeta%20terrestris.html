Nomenclature data for Pyrenochaeta terrestris

Setophoma terrestris (H.N. Hansen) Gruyter, Aveskamp & Verkley 2010 (, )

≡ Phoma terrestris H.N. Hansen 1929  Note: Not R.K. Saksena, Nand & A.K. Sarbhoy 1966.

≡Pyrenochaeta terrestris (H.N. Hansen) Gorenz, J.C. Walker & Larson 1948  Note: Author changed from Gorentz, J.C. Walker & R. H. Larson

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Soil, roots.

Disease Note: Pink root on Allium spp., also on Zea mays and Oryza sativa. Asymptomatic on other hosts.

Host: Various Poaceae, Amaryllidaceae, and other plants.
