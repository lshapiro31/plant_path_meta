Nomenclature data for Pyrenophora dactylidis

Pyrenophora dactylidis Ammon 1963 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

= Drechslera dactylidis Shoemaker 1962

Distribution: Temperate regions including New Zealand.

Substrate: On living leaves and seeds.

Disease Note: Leaf spot, leaf blotch.

Host: On Dactylis (Poaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Sanchez Marquez, S., Bills, G.F., and Zabalgogeazcoa, I. 2007. The endophytic mycobiota of the grass Dactylis glomerata. Fung. Diversity 27: 171-195.

Sivanesan, A. 1987. Graminicolous species of Bipolaris, Curvularia, Drechslera, Exserohilum and their teleomorphs. Mycol. Pap. 158: 1-261.

Zeiders, K.E. 1980. A variable-spored isolate of Drechslera dactylidis pathogenic on orchardgrass and corn. Pl. Dis. 64: 211-213.
