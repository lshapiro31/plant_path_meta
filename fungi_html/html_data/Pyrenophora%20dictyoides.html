Nomenclature data for Pyrenophora dictyoides

Pyrenophora dictyoides A.R. Paul & Parbery 1968 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

= Helminthosporium dictyoides Drechsler 1923  Note: From Drechslera dictyoides - Net blotch of fescues and other grasses.

    ≡ Drechslera dictyoides (Drechsler) Shoemaker 1978

Distribution: North America; Australia; temperate Europe.

Substrate: On living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf and net blotch, leaf spot.

Host: Festuca spp., Lolium spp., Triticum spp. and other Poaceae.

Supporting Literature:

Ellis, M.B., and Waller, J.M. 1976. Pyrenophora dictyoides. C.M.I. Descr. Pathog. Fungi Bact. 493: 1-2.

Sivanesan, A. 1987. Graminicolous species of Bipolaris, Curvularia, Drechslera, Exserohilum and their teleomorphs. Mycol. Pap. 158: 1-261.
