Nomenclature data for Pyrenophora graminea

Pyrenophora graminea Ito & Kurib. 1931 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

= Helminthosporium gramineum Rabenh. 1888  Note: From Drechslera graminea - Leaf stripe of barley and other grasses (Ellis & Waller. C.M.I. Descr. 388. 1973).

    ≡ Drechslera graminea (Rabenh.) Shoemaker 1930

    ≡ Brachysporium gracile var. gramineum (Rabenh.) Sacc. 1886

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: On living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot, leaf stripe.

Host: On various Poaceae.
