Nomenclature data for Pyricularia angulata

Pyricularia angulata Hashioka 1971 (Ascomycetes, Incertae sedis)

Distribution: Asia, Australia.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf lesions; banana blast.

Host: Musa (Musaceae).

Supporting Literature:
