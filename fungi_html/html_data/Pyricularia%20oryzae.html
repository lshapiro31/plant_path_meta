Nomenclature data for Pyricularia oryzae

Pyricularia oryzae Cavara 1891 (Ascomycetes, Incertae sedis)

    Variant spelling Piricularia oryzae Cavara 1891

≡Dactylaria oryzae (Cavara) Sawada 1916

= Magnaporthe oryzae B.C. Couch 2002

Notes: This is the correct scientific name for the cause of wheat blast as well as rice blast. From Magnaporthe oryzae - This name was given to a distinct part of the Magnaporthe grisea species complex by Couch and Kohn (2002).

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Living leaves, inflorescences.

Disease Note: Rice blast, wheat blast.

Host: Poaceae, especially important on Oryzae. but increasing also on Triticum.
