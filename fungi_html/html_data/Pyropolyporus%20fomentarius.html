Nomenclature data for Pyropolyporus fomentarius

Fomes fomentarius L. : Fr. 1849 (Basidiomycetes, Polyporales)

≡ Boletus fomentarius L. 1753

≡Ungulina fomentaria (L.) Pat. 1900

≡Polyporus fomentarius L. : Fr. 1821

≡Pyropolyporus fomentarius (L. : Fr.) Teng 1963

= Fomes griseus Lázaro Ibiza 1916

Distribution: Temperate northern hemisphere.

Substrate: On dead or living hardwoods. Duenas and Telleria (Ruizia 5:49. 1988).

Disease Note: White, mottled rot, trunk rot. Known as "hoof fungus" or "tinder fungus".

Host: On various woody trees.

Supporting Literature:

Hashemi, H., and Mohammadi, H. 2016. Identification and characterization of fungi associated with internal wood lesions and decline disease of willow and poplar trees in Iran. Forest Pathol. 46(4): 341-352.
