Nomenclature data for Pythium acanthicum

Pythium acanthicum Drechsler 1930 (Oomycetes, Pythiales)

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: On roots, fruits, and seedlings of a wide variety of plants.

Disease Note: Downy mildew. Blight, damping off, rots, etc.

Host: Multiple genera in multiple families.

Supporting Literature:

van der Plaats-Niterink, A.J. 1981. Monograph of the genus Pythium. Stud. Mycol. 21: 1-242.

Yang, X., and Hong, C.X. 2016. Diversity and populations of Phytophthora, Phytopythium and Pythium species recovered from sediments in an agricultural run-off sediment reservoir. Pl. Pathol. 65: 1118-1125.
