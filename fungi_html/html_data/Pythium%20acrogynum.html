Nomenclature data for Pythium acrogynum

Globisporangium acrogynum (Y.N. Yu) Uzuhashi, Tojo & Kakish. 2010 (, )

≡ Pythium acrogynum Y.N. Yu 1973

Distribution: North America (USA: MN).

Substrate: Seedlings, soil.

Disease Note: Seed rot, damping off.

Host: Glycine max, Medicago sativa (Fabaceae).

Supporting Literature:
