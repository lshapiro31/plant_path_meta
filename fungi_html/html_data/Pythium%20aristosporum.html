Nomenclature data for Pythium aristosporum

Pythium aristosporum Vanterp. 1938 (Oomycetes, Pythiales)

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Roots, soil.

Disease Note: Root rot.

Host: Several hosts, usually Poaceae.

Supporting Literature:

Alcala, A.V.C., Paulitz, T.C., Schroeder, K.L., Porter, L.D., Derie, M.L., and du Toit, L.J. 2016. Pythium species associated with damping-off of pea in certified organic fields in the Columbia basin of Central Washington. Pl. Dis. 100(5): 916-925.
