Nomenclature data for Pythium artotrogus

Pythium hydnosporum (Mont.) J. Schröt. 1879 (Oomycetes, Pythiales)

≡ Artotrogus hydnosporus Mont. 1845

≡Pythium artotrogus de Bary 1881  Note: Nom. illeg. via superfluous.

= Pythium artotrogus var. macracanthum Sideris 1932

= Pythium micracanthum de Bary 1881

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Disease Note: Associated with damping-off, root and stem rot.

Host: Various hosts.
