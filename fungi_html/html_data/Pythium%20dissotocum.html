Nomenclature data for Pythium dissotocum

Pythium dissotocum Drechsler 1930 (Oomycetes, Pythiales)

= Pythium araiosporon Sideris 1932

= Pythium oryzae Ito & Tokun. 1933

= Pythium perigynosum Sparrow 1936

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Seedlings, roots.

Disease Note: Damping off, rots especially root, etc.

Host: Multiple genera in multiple families; also a fish and fungi parasite.
