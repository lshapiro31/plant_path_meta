Nomenclature data for Pythium graminicola

Pythium graminicola Subram. 1928 (Oomycetes, Pythiales)

    Variant spelling Pythium graminicolum Subram. 1928

= Pythium graminicola var. stagni Höhnk 1953

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Roots, collar, seedlings.

Disease Note: Blight, root rot.

Host: Various hosts especially Poaceae.

Supporting Literature:

van der Plaats-Niterink, A.J. 1981. Monograph of the genus Pythium. Stud. Mycol. 21: 1-242.

Waterhouse, G.M., and Waterston, J.M. 1964. Pythium graminicola. C.M.I. Descr. Pathog. Fungi Bact. 38: 1-2.
