Nomenclature data for Pythium hypogynum

Globisporangium hypogynum (Middleton) Uzuhashi, Tojo & Kakish. 2010 (, )

≡ Pythium hypogynum Middleton 1943  Note: Invalid in 1941.

Distribution: Widespread.

Disease Note: Root rots.

Host: Various hosts, especially Poaceae.
