Nomenclature data for Pythium indicum

Pythium deliense Meurs 1934 (Oomycetes, Pythiales)

= Pythium indicum M.S. Balakr. 1948

Distribution: Widespread, especially warmer regions.

Substrate: Numerous plant parts.

Disease Note: Downy mildew. Damping-off, rots, stem burn, wilt, etc.

Host: Multiple genera in multiple families.
