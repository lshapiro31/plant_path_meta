Nomenclature data for Pythium spinosum

Globisporangium spinosum (Sawada) Uzuhashi, Tojo & Kakish. 2010 (, )

≡ Pythium spinosum Sawada 1926

Notes: Van der Plaats-Niterkink (1981) listed some other potential synonyms.

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Roots, crown, seedlings.

Disease Note: Blight, damping off, root and crown rot.

Host: Multiple genera in multiple families.
