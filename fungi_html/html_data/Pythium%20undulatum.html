Nomenclature data for Pythium undulatum

Elongisporangium undulatum (H.E. Petersen) Uzuhasi, Tojo & Kakish. 2010 (, )

≡ Pythium undulatum H.E. Petersen 1909

≡Phytophthora undulata (H.E. Petersen) M.W. Dick 1989

≡Pythiomorpha undulata (H.E. Petersen) Apinis 1930

≡Pythium undulatum var. undulatum H.E. Petersen 1953  Note: Type variety not needed.

= Pythium undulatum var. litorale Höhnk 1953

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Soil, decaying leaves, stems, fruits.

Disease Note: Pathogenic, also isolated from soil and water.

Host: Multiple hosts in multiple families.
