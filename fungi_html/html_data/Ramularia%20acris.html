Nomenclature data for Ramularia acris

Ramularia acris Lindr. 1902 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Septocylindrium ranunculi Peck 1881

= Ramularia aequivoca var. andrei M. Morelet 1967

Notes: Ramularia acris is a nom. nov. created to prevent conflict with R. ranunculi Peck (1883).

Distribution: Asia, Europe, North America.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Ranunculus spp. (Ranunculaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Braun, U. 1998. A Monograph of Cercosporella, Ramularia and Allied Genera (Phytopathogenic Hyphomycetes). IHW-Verlag 2: 337.

Videira, S.I.R., Groenewald, J.Z., Braun, U., Shin, H.D., and Crous, P.W. 2016. All that glitters is not Ramularia. Stud. Mycol. 83: 49-163.
