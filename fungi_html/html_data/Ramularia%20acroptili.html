Nomenclature data for Ramularia acroptili

Ramularia acroptili Bremer 1948 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡Cercosporella acroptili (Bremer) U. Braun 1993

= Cercosporella centaureicola Berner, U. Braun & Eskand. 2006

Distribution: Europe (Turkey), Central Asia.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Rhaponticum repens, Centaurea solstitales, Cynara cardunculus (Asteraceae).

Supporting Literature:

Braun, U. 1995. A Monograph of Cercosporella, Ramularia and Allied Genera (Phytopathogenic Hyphomycetes), Vol. 1. IHW-Verlag, 333 pages.

Videira, S.I.R., Groenewald, J.Z., Braun, U., Shin, H.D., and Crous, P.W. 2016. All that glitters is not Ramularia. Stud. Mycol. 83: 49-163.
