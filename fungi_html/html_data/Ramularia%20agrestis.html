Nomenclature data for Ramularia agrestis

Ramularia agrestis Sacc. 1882 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡Cylindrosporium agrestis (Sacc.) J. Schröt. 1897

= Ramularia lactea var. violae-tricoloris Thüm. 1886

= Ramularia violae var. violae-tricoloris Thüm. 1874

Distribution: Europe, North America.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Viola spp. (Violaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Braun, U. 1998. A Monograph of Cercosporella, Ramularia and Allied Genera (Phytopathogenic Hyphomycetes). IHW-Verlag 2: 337.
