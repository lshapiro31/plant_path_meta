Nomenclature data for Ramularia anchusae-officinalis

Ramularia anchusae C. Massal. 1894 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

= Ramularia alcannae Osipjan 1975

= Ramularia anchusae-officinalis A.G. Eliasson 1897

Distribution: Africa (Morocco), Asia, Europe.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Anchusa spp. (Boraginaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Braun, U. 1998. A Monograph of Cercosporella, Ramularia and Allied Genera (Phytopathogenic Hyphomycetes). IHW-Verlag 2: 337.
