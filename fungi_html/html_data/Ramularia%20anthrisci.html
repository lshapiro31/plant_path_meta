Nomenclature data for Ramularia anthrisci

Ramularia chaerophylli Ferraris 1902 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

= Ramularia anthrisci Höhn. 1903

Distribution: Asia, Europe.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Anthriscus, Chaerophyllum, Myrrhis (Apiaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Braun, U. 1998. A Monograph of Cercosporella, Ramularia and Allied Genera (Phytopathogenic Hyphomycetes). IHW-Verlag 2: 337.
