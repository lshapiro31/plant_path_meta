Nomenclature data for Ramularia areola

Ramulariopsis gossypii (Speg.) U. Braun 1993 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Cercosporella gossypii Speg. 1886

≡Ramularia gossypii (Speg.) Cif. 1962

≡Septocylindrium gossypii (Speg.) Subram. 1971

= Mycosphaerella areola J. Ehrlich & F.A. Wolf 1932

= Ramularia areola G.F. Atk. 1890

Distribution: Worldwide in cotton-growing regions.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Areolate mildew.

Host: Gossypium spp. (Malvaceae).
