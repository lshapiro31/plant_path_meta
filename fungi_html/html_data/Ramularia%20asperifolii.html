Nomenclature data for Ramularia asperifolii

Ramularia calcea Ces. 1852 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

= Ramularia asperifolii Sacc. 1876

    ≡ Ovularia asperifolii (Sacc.) Sacc. 1882

= Ovularia asperifolii var. symphyti-tuberosi Allesch. 1894

    ≡ Ramularia symphyti-tuberosi (Allesch.) Jaap 1916

= Hormodendrum farinosum Bonord. 1860

    ≡ Ovularia farinosa (Bonord.) Sacc. 1886

= Ramularia nonneae Lobik 1928

= Ovularia symphyti-cordati Savul. & Sandu 1933

= Ramularia trachystemonis Siemaszko 1915

Distribution: Europe.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Symphytum spp., Trachystemon orientalis (Boraginaceae).
