Nomenclature data for Ramularia carthami

Ramularia cynarae Sacc. 1879 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

= Ramularia balcanica Bubák & Ranoj. 1910

= Ramularia cardui P. Karst. 1887  Note: Published as ad. int.

    ≡ Ramularia cardui var. cardui

= Ramularia cardui var. personatae Allesch. 1895

  Variant spelling Ramularia cardui var. parsonata Allesch. 1895

= Ramularia carthami Zaprom. 1926

= Ramularia cirsii Allesch. 1892

    ≡ Ramularia cirsii var. cirsii

= Ramularia cirsii var. cirsii-arvensis C. Massal. 1908

= Ramularia cousiniae Vasyag. 1973

= Ramularia jurineae Hollós 1907

= Ramularia onopordi C. Massal. 1899

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Asteraceae, tribe Cynareae.
