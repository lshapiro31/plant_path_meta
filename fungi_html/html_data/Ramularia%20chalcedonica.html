Nomenclature data for Ramularia chalcedonica

Ramularia didymarioides Briosi & Sacc. 1892 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

= Ramularia chalcedonica Allesch. 1894

= Didymaria kriegeriana Bres. 1893

    ≡ Cylindrosporium kriegerianum (Bres.) J. Schröt. 1897

= Ramularia lychnicola var. chalcedonica W. Voss 1892

= Ramularia viscariae Kabát & Bubák 1909  Note: Cylindrosporium kriegerianum often published as "Cylindrosporium kriegeriana", the specific epithet not agreeing in gender with the genus name; "Cylindrosporia" is an orthographic variant of Cylindrosporium later introduced by Unger and the gender of the specific epithet had been changed to match .

Distribution: Asia, Europe.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Necrotic lesions.

Host: Silene spp., Lychnis spp. (Caryophyllaceae).

Supporting Literature:
