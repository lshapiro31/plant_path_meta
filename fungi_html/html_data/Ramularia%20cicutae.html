Nomenclature data for Ramularia cicutae

Ramularia heraclei (Oudem.) Sacc. 1886 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Cylindrosporium heraclei Oudem. 1873

= Ramularia cicutae P. Karst. 1884

= Ramularia coriandri Moesz & Smarods 1930

= Ramularia heraclei var. apii-graveolentis Sacc. & Berl. 1888

= Cylindrospora levistici J. Schröt. 1897

= Ramularia levistici Oudem. 1886

    ≡ Ovularia levistici (Oudem.) Berl. & Voglino 1886

= Ramularia nevodovskii Vasjagina 1973

= Ramularia vestergreniana Allesch. 1902

Distribution: Africa, Asia, Europe, New Zealand, North America, West Indies.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Apium, Cicuta, Conium, Coriandrum, Hansenia, Heracleum, Levisticum, Malabaila, Pastinaca (Apiaceae, subfamily Apoideae).
