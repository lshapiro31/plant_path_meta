Nomenclature data for Ramularia cyclaminicola

Ramularia cyclaminicola Trel. 1916 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

= Cladosporium cyclaminis Massey & Tilford 1932

Distribution: North America.

Substrate: Living leaves, crown, petiole, peduncle, corms, roots.

Disease Note: Cyclamen stunt disease; discoloration, stunting, indistinct leaf spotting.

Host: Cyclamen spp. (Myrsinaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Braun, U. 1998. A Monograph of Cercosporella, Ramularia and Allied Genera (Phytopathogenic Hyphomycetes). IHW-Verlag 2: 337.

Videira, S.I.R., Groenewald, J.Z., Braun, U., Shin, H.D., and Crous, P.W. 2016. All that glitters is not Ramularia. Stud. Mycol. 83: 49-163.
