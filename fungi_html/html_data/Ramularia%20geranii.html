Nomenclature data for Ramularia geranii

Ramularia geranii (Westend.) Fuckel 1870 [1869] (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Fusidium geranii Westend. 1851

Distribution: Southern and western United States; Europe.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Geranium spp. (Geraniaceae).
