Nomenclature data for Ramularia glauca

Ramularia sambucina Sacc. 1882 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡Cylindrospora sambucina (Sacc.) J. Schröt. 1897

= Ramularia glauca Ellis & Everh. 1903

Notes: Associated with the teleomorphic name Mycosphaerella ebulina Petr. (1915) though a connection has not been proven.

Distribution: Temperate Asia, North America, Europe.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Sambucus spp. (Caprifoliaceae).
