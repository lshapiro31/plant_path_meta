Nomenclature data for Ramularia hordeicola

Ramularia collo-cygni B. Sutton & Waller 1988 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Ophiocladium hordei Cavara 1893

≡Ovularia hordei (Cavara) R. Sprague 1946

≡Ramularia hordeicola U. Braun 1988

Notes: Ramularia collo-cygni is a nom nov. created to prevent conflict with Ramularia hordei McAlpine, as is Ramularia hordeicola U. Braun.

Distribution: Temperate North America; Mexico; Europe.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Poaceae spp.; especially barley. Cannabis sativa (Cannabaceae).
