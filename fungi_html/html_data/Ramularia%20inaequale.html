Nomenclature data for Ramularia inaequale

Ramularia inaequalis (Preuss) U. Braun 1998 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

    Variant spelling Ramularia inaequale (Preuss) U. Braun 1998

≡ Fusoma inaequale Preuss 1855

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Taraxacum spp. (Asteraceae).
