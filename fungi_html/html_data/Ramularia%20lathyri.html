Nomenclature data for Ramularia lathyri

Ramularia deusta (Fuckel) Karak. 1950 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Scolicotrichum deustum Fuckel 1870

≡Ovularia deusta (Fuckel) Sacc. 1886

= Ramularia deusta f. latifolii K. Baker, W.C. Snyder & W.H. Davis, nom. inval. 1950

= Ramularia lathyri W.B. Cooke & C.G. Shaw 1952

Notes: From Ramularia deusta f. latifolii - Published without a latin diagnosis.

Distribution: Asia, Europe, North America, South America.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Lathyrus spp. (Fabaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Baker, K.F., Snyder, W.C., and Davis, L.H. 1950. Ramularia leaf spots of Lathyrus odoratus and L. latifolius. Mycologia 42: 403-422.
