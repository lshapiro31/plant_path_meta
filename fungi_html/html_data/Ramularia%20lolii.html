Notes: Sutton & Waller (Trans. Brit. Mycol. Soc. 90:55. 1988); Savile (Canad. J. Bot. 35:203. 1957). Type species of Ramularia.

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.
