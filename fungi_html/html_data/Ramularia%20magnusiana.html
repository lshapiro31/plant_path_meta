Nomenclature data for Ramularia magnusiana

Spermosporina magnusiana (Sacc.) U. Braun 1993 (Ascomycetes, Incertae sedis)

≡ Septocylindrium magnusianum Sacc. 1878

≡Ramularia magnusiana (Sacc.) Lindau 1906 [1907]

≡Cylindrocarpon magnusianum (Sacc.) Wollenw. 1926  Note: Misapplied. Not Cylindrocarpon magnusianum Wollenw. 1928, described as a new species, but which is an illegitimate later homonym and a superfluous name.

Distribution: Europe, North America (Canada, USA).

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Trientalis spp. (Myrsinaceae). Reports on other hosts are probably Cylindrocarpon magnusianum Wollenw. 1928
