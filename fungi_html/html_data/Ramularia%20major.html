Nomenclature data for Ramularia major

Ramularia major (Unger) U. Braun 1988 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Cylindrospora major Unger 1833

= Ramularia cervina Speg. 1879

    ≡ Cylindrospora cervina (Speg.) J. Schröt. 1897

= Ramularia cervina var. petasitis Bäumler 1888

= Ramularia variegata Ellis & Holw. 1886

= Ramularia variegata var. petasitidis-tomentosae Karak. 1915

= Ramularia variegata var. petasitis-officinalis Allesch. 1895

Notes: Additional synonym: Ramularia petasite-tomentosae Savul & Sandu (1933).

Distribution: Asia, Europe, North America.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Adenostyles spp., Homogyne alpina, Petasites spp. (Asteraceae, tribe Senecioneae).

Supporting Literature:

Videira, S.I.R., Groenewald, J.Z., Braun, U., Shin, H.D., and Crous, P.W. 2016. All that glitters is not Ramularia. Stud. Mycol. 83: 49-163.
