Nomenclature data for Ramularia nivosa

Ramularia nivosa (Ellis & Everh.) W.B. Cooke & C.G. Shaw 1952 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Cercosporella nivosa Ellis & Everh. 1895

= Ramularia penstemonis W.B. Cooke & C.G. Shaw 1949

Distribution: Pacific Northwest.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Penstemon spp. (Plantaginaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Braun, U. 1998. A Monograph of Cercosporella, Ramularia and Allied Genera (Phytopathogenic Hyphomycetes). IHW-Verlag 2: 337.

Savile, D.B.O. 1968. Some fungal parasites of Scrophulariaceae. Canad. J. Bot. 46: 461-471.
