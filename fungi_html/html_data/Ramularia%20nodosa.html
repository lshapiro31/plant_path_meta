Nomenclature data for Ramularia nodosa

Ramularia carneola (Sacc.) Nannf. 1950 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Ovularia carneola Sacc. 1881

= Ramularia borghettiana C. Massal. 1912

= Ovularia duplex Sacc. 1881

= Ramularia nicolai Bubák 1903

= Ramularia nodosa Tho 1972

= Ramularia scrophulariae Fautrey & Roum. 1891

Distribution: Asia, Europe, North America.

Substrate: Living, fading and dying leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Scrophularia spp. (Scrophulariaceae).
