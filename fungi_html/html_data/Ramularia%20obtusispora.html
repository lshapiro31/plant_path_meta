Nomenclature data for Ramularia obtusispora

Neonectria obtusispora (Cooke & Harkn.) Rossman, L. Lombard & Crous 2014 (Ascomycetes, Hypocreales)

≡ Fusarium obtusisporum Cooke & Harkn. 1884

≡Ramularia obtusispora (Cooke & Harkn.) Wolenw. 2014

≡Cylindrocarpon obtusisporum (Cooke & Harkn.) Wollenw. 1926

= Nectria tawa Dingley 1951

Distribution: Africa (Tanzania), Asia (India, Iran), Australia, New Zealand, Europe, North America (Canada, USA: CA, UT).

Substrate: Roots, soil. Water and soil-borne.

Disease Note: Root rot.

Host: Multiple plant families.
