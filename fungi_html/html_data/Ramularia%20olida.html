≡Cylindrocarpon olidum var. olidum (Wollenw.) Wollenw. 1916

= Cylindrocarpon curvatum Hochapfel ex Wollenw. 1931

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: On rotting roots of various plants.

Disease Note: Black foot disease.

Host: Diverse hosts.
