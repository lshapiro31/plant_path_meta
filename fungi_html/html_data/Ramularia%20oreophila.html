Nomenclature data for Ramularia oreophila

Ramularia oreophila Sacc. 1881 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡Cylindrosporium oreophilum (Sacc.) J. Schröt. 1897

= Ramularia saniculae Linh. 1882

Distribution: Europe.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Astrantia (Apiaceae).

Supporting Literature:
