Nomenclature data for Ramularia oxyriae-digynae

Ramularia pratensis Sacc. 1882 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

= Ramularia oxyriae-digynae Gjaerum 1971

= Ramularia rhei Allesch. 1896

= Ovularia rumicis A.G. Eliasson 1897

    ≡ Ramularia rumicis-crispi Sawada 1943

Notes: R. rumicis-crispi is a nom. nov. created to prevent conflict with R. rumicis Kalchbr. & Cooke (1880).

Distribution: Asia, Europe, North America, South America.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Oxyria digyna, Rheum spp., Rumex spp. (Polygonaceae).
