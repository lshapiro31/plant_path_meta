Nomenclature data for Ramularia peckii

Ramularia rhabdospora (Berk. & Broome) Nannf. 1950 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Cylindrosporium rhabdosporum Berk. & Broome 1875

= Ramularia lanceolata Dearn. & House 1918

= Cylindrosporium plantaginis J. Schröt. 1897

  Variant spelling Ramularia plantaginea Sacc. & Berl. 1885

    ≡ Ramularia plantaginis Sacc. & Berl. 1885

= Ramularia plantaginis Peck 1880

  ≡Ramularia peckii Sacc. 1899

Notes: Ramularia peckii is an apparently superfluous nom. nov. for the legitimate Peck 1880. Cylindrosporium plantaginis is a nom. nov. replacing R. plantaginis (as "plantaginea") Sacc. & Berl. (1885)(also, non Peck 1880).

Distribution: Asia, Europe, North America, New Zealand, South America.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Plantago spp. (Plantaginaceae).
