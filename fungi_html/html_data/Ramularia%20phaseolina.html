Nomenclature data for Ramularia phaseolina

Ramularia phaseoli (O.A. Drumm.) Deighton 1967 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Ovularia phaseoli O.A. Drumm. 1945

≡Mycovellosiella phaseoli (O.A. Drumm.) Deighton 1974

= Ramularia phaseolina Petr. 1950

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Floury leaf spot.

Host: Reported from Fabaceae bean crops, especially Glycine max, Phaseolus vulgaris.

Supporting Literature:

Braun, U. 1998. A Monograph of Cercosporella, Ramularia and Allied Genera (Phytopathogenic Hyphomycetes). IHW-Verlag 2: 337.
