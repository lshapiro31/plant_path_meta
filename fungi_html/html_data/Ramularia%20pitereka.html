Nomenclature data for Ramularia pitereka

Quambalaria pitereka (J. Walker & Bertus) J.A. Simpson 2000 (Incertae sedis, Incertae sedis)

≡ Ramularia pitereka J. Walker & Bertus 1971

≡Sporothrix pitereka (J. Walker & Berterus) U. Braun 1998

Distribution: Asia (China - introduced), Australia.

Substrate: Shoots, leaves, petioles.

Disease Note: Shoot blight, leaf blight.

Host: Corymbia spp. (Myrtaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Braun, U. 1998. A Monograph of Cercosporella, Ramularia and Allied Genera (Phytopathogenic Hyphomycetes). IHW-Verlag 2: 337.
