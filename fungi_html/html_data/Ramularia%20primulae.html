Nomenclature data for Ramularia primulae

Ramularia primulae Thüm. 1878 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡Cylindrosporium primulae (Thüm.) J. Schröt. 1897

Distribution: Asia, Australia, Europe, New Zealand, North America, South America.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Primula spp. (Primulaceae).
