Nomenclature data for Ramularia ranunculi

Ramularia simplex Pass. 1882 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡Ovularia simplex (Pass.) Sacc. 1892

= Ovularia decipiens Sacc. 1881

= Ramularia didyma f. ranunculi-repentis Thüm. 1881

= Ramularia ranunculi Peck 1884

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Anemone spp., Pulsatilla patens, Ranunculus spp., Trollius altaicus (Ranunculaceae).
