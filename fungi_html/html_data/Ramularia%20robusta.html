Nomenclature data for Ramularia robusta

Ilyonectria robusta (A.A. Hildenbr.) A. Cabral & Crous 2012 (, )

≡ Ramularia robusta A.A. Hildebr. 1935

Distribution: Africa (Tunisia), Canada, China, Europe (Austria, Netherlands, Portugal, Spain).

Substrate: Roots and rootstock.

Disease Note: Rusty root; lesions.

Host: Plants in various families.
