Nomenclature data for Ramularia rosea

Ramularia rosea Sacc. 1882 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡Ovularia rosea (Sacc.) Massee 1893

≡Cylindrosporium roseum (Sacc.) J. Schröt. 1908

= Ramularia lucidae Davis 1919

Notes: Ramularia rosea is a replacement name based on the replaced illegitimate synonym Fusidium roseum Fuckel (non Fusidium roseum Link 1809); specific epithet reused.

Distribution: Asia, Europe, North America.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Populus tremula, Salix spp. (Salicaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Braun, U. 1998. A Monograph of Cercosporella, Ramularia and Allied Genera (Phytopathogenic Hyphomycetes). IHW-Verlag 2: 337.
