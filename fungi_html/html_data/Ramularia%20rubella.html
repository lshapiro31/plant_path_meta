Nomenclature data for Ramularia rubella

Ramularia rubella (Bonord.) Nannf. 1950 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Crocysporium rubellum Bonord. 1861

≡Ovularia rubella (Bonord.) Sacc. 1886

= Ramularia circumfusa Ellis & Everh. 1895

= Oidium monosporium Westend. 1863

    ≡ Torula monosporia (Westend.) J.J. Kickx 1867

= Peronospora obliqua Cooke 1865

    ≡ Ovularia obliqua (Cooke) Oudem. 1883

    ≡ Ramularia obliqua (Cooke) Oudem. 1872

= Ovularia obliqua var. canaegricola Henn. 1897

    ≡ Ovularia canaegricola (Henn.) Henn. 1899

= Ramularia obovata Fuckel 1866

    ≡ Ovularia obovata (Fuckel) Sacc. 1881

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Primarily Rumex and other members of the Polygonaceae.
