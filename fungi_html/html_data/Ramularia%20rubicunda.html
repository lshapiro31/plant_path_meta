Nomenclature data for Ramularia rubicunda

Zasmidium subsanguineum (Ellis & Everh.) U. Braun 2010 (Ascomycetes, Incertae sedis)

≡ Cercospora subsanguinea Ellis & Everh. 1888

≡Ramularia subsanguinea (Ellis & Everh.) Karak. 1937

≡Stenella subsanguinea (Ellis & Everh.) U. Braun 1993

= Ramularia rubicunda Bres. 1896

Notes: From Ramularia subsanguinea - Isonym.

Distribution: Asia, Europe, North America.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Maianthemum spp. (Asparagaceae).
