Nomenclature data for Ramularia rumicis

Ramularia rumicis Kalchbr. & Cooke 1880 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

= Ramularia decipiens Ellis & Everh. 1885

Distribution: Africa, Asia, Europe, North America.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Rumex spp. (Polygonaceae).
