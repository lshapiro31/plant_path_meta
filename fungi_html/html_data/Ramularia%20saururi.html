Nomenclature data for Ramularia saururi

Passalora saururi (Ellis & Everh.) Crous & U. Braun 2003 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Cercospora saururi Ellis & Everh. 1887

≡Phaeoramularia saururi (Ellis & Everh.) Crous & El-Gholl 1999

≡Ramularia saururi (Ellis & Everh.) Tharp 1917

Distribution: Asia, North America.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Anemopsis californica, Saururus spp. (Saururaceae).
