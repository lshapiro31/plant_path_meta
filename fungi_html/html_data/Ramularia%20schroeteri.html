Nomenclature data for Ramularia schroeteri

Ramularia aplospora Speg. 1879 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡Ovularia aplospora (Speg.) Magnus 1904

= Mycosphaerella alchemillae Vasil'evskij 1937

= Ramularia schroeteri J.G. Kühn 1881

    ≡ Ovularia schroeteri (J.G. Kühn) Sacc. 1886

Distribution: Asia, Europe.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Alchemilla spp., Aphanes arvensis (Rosaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Braun, U. 1998. A Monograph of Cercosporella, Ramularia and Allied Genera (Phytopathogenic Hyphomycetes). IHW-Verlag 2: 337.

Videira, S.I.R., Groenewald, J.Z., Braun, U., Shin, H.D., and Crous, P.W. 2016. All that glitters is not Ramularia. Stud. Mycol. 83: 49-163.
