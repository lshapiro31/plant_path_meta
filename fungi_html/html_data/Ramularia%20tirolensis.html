Nomenclature data for Ramularia tirolensis

Cercosporella primulae Allesch. 1891 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

= Cercospora primulae Fautrey 1891  Note: As 'drimulae'.

= Ramularia tirolensis Maire 1907

Distribution: Northern hemisphere, predominantly Europe.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Primula spp. (Primulaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Braun, U. 1995. A Monograph of Cercosporella, Ramularia and Allied Genera (Phytopathogenic Hyphomycetes), Vol. 1. IHW-Verlag, 333 pages.
