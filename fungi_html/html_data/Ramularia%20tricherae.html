Nomenclature data for Ramularia tricherae

Ramularia tricherae Lindr. 1902 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

= Ramularia succisae var. knautiae C. Massal. 1889

    ≡ Ramularia knautiae (C. Massal.) Bubák 1903

= Ovularia tricherae Vestergr. 1899

Distribution: Europe.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Knautia spp. (Dipsacaceae).
