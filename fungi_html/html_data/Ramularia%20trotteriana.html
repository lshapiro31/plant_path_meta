Nomenclature data for Ramularia trotteriana

Ramularia gei (A.G. Eliasson) Lindr. 1902 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Ovularia gei A.G. Eliasson 1897

= Acrotheca gei Fuckel 1860

  [≡ Cercospora gei (Fuckel) Jaap 1908 - illegitimate later homonym, not included in search]  Note: Not Bubák 1903. Nomen illeg.

    ≡ Pseudocercospora gei (Fuckel) Y.L. Guo & X.J. Liu 1986  Note: Basionym somewhat unclear. Treated as an invalid name by Crous and Braun (2003). It is misapplied (Crous and Braun 2003).

= Cercospora gei Bubák 1903

= Ramularia gei-aleppici Savul. & Sandu 1933

  Variant spelling Ramularia gei-allepici Savul. & Sandu 1933

= Ramularia submodesta Höhn. 1902

= Ramularia trotteriana Sacc. 1902

= Ramularia trotteriana var. gei-urbani C. Massal. 1906

= Ramularia vaccarii Ferraris 1902

Notes: Isonym (A.G. Eliasson) Höhn. 1904.

Distribution: Widespread in northern hemisphere.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Geum spp. (Rosaceae).
