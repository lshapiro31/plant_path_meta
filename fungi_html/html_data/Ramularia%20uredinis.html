Nomenclature data for Ramularia uredinis

Ramularia uredinis (W. Voss) Sacc. 1886 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Cylindrosporium uredinis W. Voss 1879

= Ramularia nambuana Henn. 1904

Distribution: Asia, Europe.

Substrate: Uredinia of rust fungi.

Disease Note: Hyperparasitic.

Host: Cronartium (Cronartiaceae), Melampsora (Melampsoraceae), Melampsoridium, Pucciniastrum (Pucciniastraceae).

Supporting Literature:

Braun, U. 1998. A Monograph of Cercosporella, Ramularia and Allied Genera (Phytopathogenic Hyphomycetes). IHW-Verlag 2: 337.
