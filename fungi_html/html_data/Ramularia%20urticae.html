Nomenclature data for Ramularia urticae

Ramularia urticae Ces. 1863 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡Cylindrosporium urticae (Ces.) J. Schröt. 1897

≡Septocylindrium urticae (Ces.) Subram. 1971

= Sphaerella superflua Fuckel 1870

    ≡ Mycosphaerella superflua (Fuckel) Petr. 1940

    ≡ Didymella superflua (Fuckel) Sacc. 1882

    ≡ Didymosphaeria superflua (Fuckel) Niessl 1879

Notes: The synonomy of Sphaerella superflua and its synonyms is not proven per Andrianova (2011) and several authors.

Distribution: Asia, Europe, North America, South America.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Urtica spp. (Urticaceae); older reports from numerous other plant families are questionable.
