Nomenclature data for Ramularia vallisumbrosae

Ramularia vallisumbrosae Cavara 1899 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

= Ramularia narcissi Chittenden 1906

= Ramularia ucrainica Petr. 1921

Distribution: Europe, North America.

Substrate: Leaves and stems.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Amaryllidaceae.

Supporting Literature:
