Nomenclature data for Ramularia variabilis

Ramularia variabilis Fuckel 1870 [1869] (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡Cylindrosporium variabilis (Fuckel) J. Schröt. 1897

≡Entylomella variabilis (Fuckel) Cif. 1928

≡Ovularia variabilis (Fuckel) Roum. & M. Rousseau 1884

= Sphaerella mariae Sacc. & E. Bommer 1886

    ≡ Mycosphaerella mariae (Sacc. & E. Bommer) Lindau 1903

= Mycosphaerella variabilis Killian 1926

Notes: Hosts given by Braun (1998) as Verbascum spp. (Scrophulariaceae) and Digitalis spp., the latter now classified in the family Plantaginaceae. It was therefore unclear if records on plants from what are now considered distinct families (APG III) were indeed separate entities; per Videira et al. (2016), that appears to be the case. Lectotype on Verbascum thapsus.

Distribution: Asia, Europe, North America.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Verbascum spp. (Scrophulariaceae); see notes for more information.
