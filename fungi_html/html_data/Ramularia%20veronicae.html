Nomenclature data for Ramularia veronicae

Protoventuria veronicae (Bat.) Rossman & W.C. Allen 2016 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

≡ Ramularia veronicae Fuckel 1870 [1869]

≡Ovularia veronicae (Fuckel) Sacc. 1881

= Protoventuria parahebicola Pascoe & B. Sutton 1990

= Ramalia veronicae Bat. 1957

    ≡ Fusicladium veronicae (Bat.) B. Sutton & Pascoe 1988

= Ovularia veronicae var. microsticta Sacc. 1886

= Ramularia veronicae-cymbalariae Killian 1928

= Ramularia veronicae-peduncularis Lobik 1928

Notes: Savile (Canad. J. Bot. 46:467. 1968) provided a complete synonymy and comments on North American specimens.

Distribution: Temperate Asia, Europe, North America, Australia.

Substrate: Leaves, petioles, stems.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Veronica spp. (Plantaginaceae).
