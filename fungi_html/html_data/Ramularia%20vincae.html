Nomenclature data for Ramularia vincae

Ramularia vincae Sacc. 1881 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

= Ramularia vincae var. vincae-mediae Maire 1905

Distribution: Asia, Europe, West Indies.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Vinca spp. (Apocynaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Braun, U. 1998. A Monograph of Cercosporella, Ramularia and Allied Genera (Phytopathogenic Hyphomycetes). IHW-Verlag 2: 337.
