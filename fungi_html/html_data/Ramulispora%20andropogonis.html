Nomenclature data for Ramulispora andropogonis

Ramulispora sorghi (Ellis & Everh.) L.S. Olive & Lefebvre 1946 (Ascomycetes, Helotiales)

≡ Septorella sorghi Ellis & Everh. 1903

= Ramulispora andropogonis Miura 1920

    ≡ Titaeospora andropogonis (Miura) F.L. Tai 1932

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Sooty blotch or leaf stripe.

Host: Poaceae, especially Sorghum spp.
