Nomenclature data for Ramulispora sorghicola

Ramulispora sorghicola E. Harris 1960 (Ascomycetes, Helotiales)

Distribution: Africa, Asia.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Sorghum spp. (Poaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Braun, U. 1995. A Monograph of Cercosporella, Ramularia and Allied Genera (Phytopathogenic Hyphomycetes), Vol. 1. IHW-Verlag, 333 pages.
