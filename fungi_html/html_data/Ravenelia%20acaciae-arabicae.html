Nomenclature data for Ravenelia acaciae-arabicae

Ravenelia evansii Syd. & P. Syd. 2008 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

= Ravenelia acaciae-arabicae Mundkur & Thirumalachar 1946

Distribution: Africa (Ethiopia, South Africa, Zambia), Asia (India, Pakistan).

Substrate: Living leaves, stems. fruit.

Disease Note: Autoecious rust.

Host: Acacia spp. (Fabaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Cannon, P.F. 2008. Ravenelia evansii. I.M.I. Descr. Fungi Bact. 1754: 1-3.
