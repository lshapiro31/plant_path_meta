Nomenclature data for Ravenelia acaciae-pennatulae

Ravenelia acaciae-pennatulae Dietel 1906 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

≡Haploravenelia acaciae-pennatulae (Dietel) Syd. 1921

= Uredo quichensis Cummins 1943

Notes: From Uredo quichensis - Association established by Joe Hennen (unpublished). Uredinial anamorph.

Distribution: Central America (Mexico, Guatemala).

Substrate: Leaflets.

Disease Note: Rust.

Host: Acacia spp., perhaps primarily A. pennatula. Erroneously listed as Calliandra conzattiana.

Supporting Literature:

Cummins, G.B. 1978. Rust Fungi on legumes and composites in North America. University of Arizona Press, 424 pages.
