Nomenclature data for Ravenelia cassiicola

Ravenelia cassiicola var. cassiicola G.F. Atk. 1891 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

≡Haploravenelia cassiicola (G.F. Atk.) Syd. 1921  Note: as 'cassiaecola'

Distribution: North America (Eastern United States), Puerto Rico, Barbados.

Substrate: Leaves, stems, pods.

Disease Note: Autoecious rust.

Host: Chamaecrista spp. (and Cassia spp.?) (Fabaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Cummins, G.B. 1978. Rust Fungi on legumes and composites in North America. University of Arizona Press, 424 pages.
