Nomenclature data for Ravenelia evernia

Ravenelia evernia Syd. 1933 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

≡Ravenelia fragrans var. evernia (Syd.) J.W. Baxter 1965

= Ravenelia dysocarpae Long & Goodd. 1939

= Ravenelia mimosae-himalayanae S. Ahmad 1956

Notes: Synonymy of Ravenelia dysocarpae is uncertain.

Distribution: Asia (India, Pakistan).

Substrate: Leaves, stems, fruit.

Disease Note: Autoecious rust.

Host: Mimosa spp. (Fabaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Cannon, P.F. 2008. Ravenelia evernia. I.M.I. Descr. Fungi Bact. 1755: 1-3.
