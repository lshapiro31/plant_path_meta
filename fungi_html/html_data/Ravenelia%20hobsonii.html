Nomenclature data for Ravenelia hobsonii

Ravenelia hobsonii Cooke 1880 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

    Variant spelling Ravenelia hobsoni Cooke 1880

≡Haploravenelia hobsonii (Cooke) S. Ito 1943

Distribution: Asia.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Autoecious rust.

Host: Pongamia spp. (Fabaceae).
