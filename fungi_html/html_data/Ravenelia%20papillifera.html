Nomenclature data for Ravenelia papillifera

Ravenelia spinulosa var. papillifera (Syd. & P. Syd.) Cummins & J.W. Baxter 1976 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

≡ Ravenelia papillifera Syd. & P. Syd. 1903

Distribution: North America (Southwestern USA, Mexico), Bahamas.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Autoecious rust.

Host: Cassia spp. = Senna spp. (Fabaceae), possibly only herbaceous spp.

Supporting Literature:

Cummins, G.B. 1978. Rust Fungi on legumes and composites in North America. University of Arizona Press, 424 pages.
