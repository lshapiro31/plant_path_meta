Nomenclature data for Rhabdocline pseudotsugae

Rhabdocline pseudotsugae Syd. 1922 (Ascomycetes, Helotiales)

Notes: Rhabdogloeum pseudotsugae, described with Rhabdocline pseudotsugae by Sydow as an anamorph, is instead connected with Rhabdocline weirii. ------ Rhabdocline protected over Rhabogloeum, Meria and Hartigiella per Johnston et al. (2014).

Distribution: Northeastern and northwestern United States; Europe.

Substrate: Needles.

Disease Note: Needle cast.

Host: Pseudotsuga menziesii (Pinaceae).
