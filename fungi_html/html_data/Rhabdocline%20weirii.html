Nomenclature data for Rhabdocline weirii

Rhabdocline weirii A.K. Parker & J. Reid 1969 (Ascomycetes, Helotiales)

= Rhabdogloeum pseudotsugae Syd. 1922

Notes: Rhabdocline weirii is the only species in its genus with an asexual state assigned to Rhabdogloeum; as first proposed but not confirmed by Parker & Reid, Rhabdogloeum pseudotsugae belongs to it but not Rhabdocline pseudotsugae Syd. Given that the association of Rhabdocline weirii and Rhabdogloeum pseudotsugae is accepted and the older specific epithet pseudotsugae is already used in Rhabdocline, Rhabdocline weirii is the correct name for this species.

Distribution: Northwestern North America.

Substrate: Needles.

Disease Note: Needle cast.

Host: Pseudotsuga menziesii (Pinaceae).
