Nomenclature data for Rhabdospora cannabina

Septoria cannabis (Lasch) Sacc. 1884 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Ascochyta cannabis Lasch 1846

= Rhabdospora cannabina Fautrey 1899

= Septoria cannabina Westend. 1857

= Spilosphaeria cannabis Rabenh. 1864

Notes: Septoria cannabina Peck (1882) is an illegitimate later homonym (Art. 53.1) belonging to a different taxon; Ascochyta cannabis - non Ascochyta cannabis (L.A. Kirchn.) Voglino 1913, also an illegitimate later homonym, but of uncertain placement.

Distribution: Asia, Europe, North America (Canada, USA).

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Cannabis sativa (Cannabaceae).
