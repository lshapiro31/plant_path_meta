Nomenclature data for Rhabdospora geranii

Septoria geranii Roberge ex Desm. 1853 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡Rhabdospora geranii (Roberge ex Desm.) Petr. 1958 [1957]

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Geranium spp. (Geraniaceae).
