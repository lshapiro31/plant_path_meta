Nomenclature data for Rhizoctonia monteithiana

Sclerotinia homoeocarpa F.T. Benn. 1937 (Ascomycetes, Helotiales)

    Variant spelling Sclerotinia homeocarpa F.T. Benn.

Notes: Although this species does not belong to Sclerotinia, it has not been placed in an appropriate genus (Kohn. Mycotaxon 9:413. 1979 and Walsh, 1999).

Distribution: Temperate regions.

Substrate: Leaves, stems, basal plant parts.

Disease Note: Turf rot, dollar spot.

Host: Turf grasses and other Poaceae; by innoculation on other hosts.
