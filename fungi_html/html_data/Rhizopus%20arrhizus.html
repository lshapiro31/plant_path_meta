Nomenclature data for Rhizopus arrhizus

Rhizopus arrhizus A. Fisch. 1892 (Zygomycetes, Mucorales)

= Rhizopus oryzae Went & Prins. Geerl. 1895

= Rhizopus tritici Saito 1904

Notes: Zheng et al. (2007) provide the extensive synonymy for this species and its three included varieties.

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Roots, fruit.

Disease Note: Root rot, fruit rot, chlorosis, wilting.

Host: Multiple genera in multiple families.
