Nomenclature data for Rhizosphaera abietis

Rhizosphaera pini (Corda) Maubl. 1907 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

≡ Coniothyrium pini Corda 1840

≡Leptothyrium pini (Corda) Sacc. 1884

≡Sacidium pini (Corda) Fr. 1849

= Rhizosphaera abietis L. Mangin & Har. 1907

Distribution: Circumboreal.

Substrate: Needles.

Disease Note: Needle blight.

Host: Abies spp., Pinus sp., Tsuga diversifolia, Picea spp. (Pinaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Diamandis, S., and Minter, D.W. 1980. Rhizosphaera pini. C.M.I. Descr. Pathog. Fungi Bact. 657: 1-2.
