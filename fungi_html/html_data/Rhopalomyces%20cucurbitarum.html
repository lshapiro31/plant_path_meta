Nomenclature data for Rhopalomyces cucurbitarum

Choanephora cucurbitarum (Berk. & Ravenel) Thaxt. 1903 (Zygomycetes, Mucorales)

≡ Rhopalomyces cucurbitarum Berk. & Ravenel 1875

= Choanephora americana A. Möller 1901

Notes: See Kirk (1984) for additional synonyms and a key to the species of Choanephora.

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Flowers, leaves, fruit, etc.

Disease Note: Blossom blight and rot, leaf blight, leaf and fruit rots, etc.

Host: Multiple genera in multiple families.
