Nomenclature data for Rhytisma punctatum

Rhytisma punctatum (Pers. : Fr.) Fr. 1823 (Ascomycetes, Rhytismatales)

≡ Xyloma punctatum Pers. 1800

= Melasmia punctata Sacc. & Roum. 1882

Distribution: Asia, Europe, North America.

Substrate: On living leaves.

Disease Note: Punctate maple tar spot.

Host: Acer spp. (Sapindaceae).

Supporting Literature:
