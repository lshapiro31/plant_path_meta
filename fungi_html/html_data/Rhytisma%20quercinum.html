Nomenclature data for Rhytisma quercinum

Trabutia quercina (Rudolphi ex Fr.) Sacc. & Roum. 1881 (Ascomycetes, Phyllachorales)

≡ Rhytisma quercinum F. Rudophi ex Fr. 1830

= Didymotrichella quercina G. Arnaud 1954

    ≡ Baeumleria quercina (G. Arnaud) Petr. 1929

Distribution: Southeastern United States; northern Africa; southern Europe.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Tar spot.

Host: Quercus spp. (Fagaceae).

Supporting Literature:
