Nomenclature data for Rhytisma rhododendri

Rhytisma rhododendri Fr. : Fr. 1931 (Ascomycetes, Rhytismatales)

= Melasmia rhododendri Henn. & Shirai 1900

= Rhytisma shiraiana Hemmi & Kurata 1931

Notes: From Rhytisma shiraiana - as 'shiraianum'.

Distribution: Northern hemisphere including Asia.

Substrate: On leaves.

Disease Note: Tar spot.

Host: Rhododendron (Ericaceae).

Updated on Mar 09, 2015
