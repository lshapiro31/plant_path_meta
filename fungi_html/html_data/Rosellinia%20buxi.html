Nomenclature data for Rosellinia buxi

Rosellinia buxi Fabre 1878 (Ascomycetes, Xylariales)

≡Byssosphaeria buxi (Fabre) Cooke 1887

≡Hypoxylon buxi (Fabre) P.M.D. Martin 1976

Distribution: Europe (England, France).

Substrate: Stems just above the ground.

Disease Note: Leaf loss, slow rotting.

Host: Buxus sempervirens (Buxaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Petrini 2013. Rosellinia - a world monograph. Biblioth. Mycol. 205: 410.
