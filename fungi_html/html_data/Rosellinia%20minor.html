Nomenclature data for Rosellinia minor

Rosellinia mycophila (Fr.: Fr.) Sacc. 1882 (Ascomycetes, Xylariales)

≡ Sphaeria mycophila Fr.: Fr. 1823

≡Amphisphaerella mycophila (Fr.: Fr.) Kirschst. 1933

= Rosellinia andurnensis var. minor Ces. & De Not. 1919

= Rosellinia minor (Höhn. ex Strasser) S.M. Francis 1986

Notes: Anamorph: nodulisporium-like.

Distribution: Europe, North America.

Substrate: Basidiocarps, seedlings or twigs and needles.

Disease Note: Needle blight.

Host: Polyporus sp. (Polyporaceae), Picea abies, P. excelsa, P. sitchensis, Pseudotsuga menziesii (Pinaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Francis, S.M. 1986. Needle blights of conifers. Trans. Brit. Mycol. Soc. 87: 397-400.

Petrini 2013. Rosellinia - a world monograph. Biblioth. Mycol. 205: 410.
