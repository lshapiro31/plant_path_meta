Nomenclature data for Rostrupia elymi

Puccinia elymi Westend. 1851 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

≡Dicaeoma elymi (Westend.) Kuntze 1898

≡Rostrupia elymi (Westend.) Lagerh. 1889

≡Puccinia graminis var. elymi (Westend.) J. Kickx f. 1867

Distribution: Asia, Europe; reports from New Zealand.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Hetercious rust.

Host: Poaceae, aecical host Thalictrum spp. (Ranunculaceae).

Supporting Literature:
