Nomenclature data for Schiffnerula camelliae

Schiffnerula camelliae (Syd., P. Syd. & E.J. Butler) S. Hughes 1987 (Ascomycetes, Incertae sedis)

≡ Asterina camelliae Syd., P. Syd. & E.J. Butler 1911

Distribution: Asia.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Black mildew.

Host: Camellia sinensis, C. japonica (Theaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Hosagoudar, V.B. 2011. The genus Schiffnerula in India. Pl. Pathol & Quarantine 1(2): 131-204.
