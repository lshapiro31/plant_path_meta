Nomenclature data for Schiffnerula pulchra

Sarcinella pulchra (Sacc.) Seifert 2016 (Ascomycetes, Incertae sedis)

≡ Apiosporium pulchrum Sacc. 1875

≡Schiffnerula pulchra (Sacc.) Petr. 1928

≡Dimerosporium pulchrum (Sacc.) Sacc. 1882

= Sarcinella heterospora Sacc. 1877

= Questieriella pulchra S. Hughes 1983

Distribution: Central and eastern North America; Europe.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Sooty mold.

Host: Diverse dicot hosts.
