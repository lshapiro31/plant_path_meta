Nomenclature data for Schizonella cocconii

Schizonella cocconii (Morini) Liro 1938 (Ustilaginomycetes, Ustilaginales)

    Variant spelling Schizonella cocconi (Morini) Liro 1938

≡ Tolyposporium cocconii Morini 1884

Distribution: Asia, Europe.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Smut.

Host: Carex (Cyperaceae).

Supporting Literature:
