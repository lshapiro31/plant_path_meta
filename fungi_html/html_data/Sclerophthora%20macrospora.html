Nomenclature data for Sclerophthora macrospora

Sclerophthora macrospora (Sacc.) Thirum., C.G. Shaw & Naras. 1953 (Oomycetes, Sclerosporales)

≡ Sclerospora macrospora Sacc. 1890

≡Phytophthora macrospora (Sacc.) Ito & I. Tanaka 1940

= Sclerospora kriegeriana Magnus 1896

= Sclerospora oryzae Brizi 1919

    ≡ Phytophthora oryzae (Brizi) Hara 1930  Note: Not the illegitimate later homonym Phytophthora oryzae (S.M. Ito & Nagai) G.M. Waterh. 1958 = Pythiomorpha oryzae.

Notes: Thirumalachar (1953) made this species the type of the new genus Sclerophthora, but Waterhouse (1963) argued that it should be retained in Phytophthora, followed by Ho & Chang (1992). Erwin & Ribeiro (1996) followed Stamps (1990) in listing it as a Sclerophthora, but stated that they were not convinced that this species belonged in Sclerophthora, even though the name was then widely used. The generic affinity of this species requires further study.

Distribution: Cosmopolitan (C.M.I. 1986).

Substrate: Leaves, sheaths, spikelets, seeds. Systemic.

Disease Note: Downy mildew, yellow dwarf, yellow wilt of rice; crazy top disease.

Host: Cyperaceae, Poaceae, including Oryza sativa (rice).
