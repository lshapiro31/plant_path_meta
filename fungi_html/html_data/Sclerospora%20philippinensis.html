Nomenclature data for Sclerospora philippinensis

Peronosclerospora philippinensis (W. Weston) C.G. Shaw 1978 (Oomycetes, Sclerosporales)

≡ Sclerospora philippinensis W. Weston 1920  Note: Published as a new species, but citing S. maydis Reinking, therefore this should be considered a replacement name for the illegitimate homonym Sclerospora maydis Reinking 1918 non (Racib.) Butler 1913.

= Sclerospora indica E.J. Butler 1931

[≡Sclerospora maydis Reinking 1918 - illegitimate later homonym, not included in search]  Note: Not (Racib.) Butler 1913.

Notes: According to Bonde et al. (1984) and Yao et al. (1991) Peronosclerospora philippinensis may be a synonym of Peronosclerospora sacchari.

Distribution: Africa (Congo, South Africa), Asia. Not reported in the USA.

Substrate: Leaves. Possibly seed borne.

Disease Note: The APS common name is Philippine downy mildew. This is an APHIS Select Agent and has recently been added to the APHIS Regulated Plant Pest List.

Host: Poaceae including Zea mays (maize).

Supporting Literature:
