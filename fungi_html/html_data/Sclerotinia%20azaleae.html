Nomenclature data for Sclerotinia azaleae

Ovulinia azaleae Weiss 1940 (Ascomycetes, Helotiales)

≡Sclerotinia azaleae (Weiss) Dennis 1956

= Ovulitis azaleae N.F. Buchw. 1970

Notes: Petal blight. Dennis (Mycol. Pap. 62:159. 1956 as Sclerotinia azaleae).

Distribution: Temperate regions.

Substrate: On flowers and branches.

Disease Note: Flower and petal blight.

Host: Primarily on Rhododendron and other Ericaceae.

Supporting Literature:

Holst-Jensen, A, Kohn, L.M., and Schumacher, T. 1997. Nuclear rDNA phylogeny of the Sclerotiniaceae. Mycologia 89: 885-899.
