    ≡ Oospora laxa (Ehrenb.) Wallr. 1833

= Monilia oregonensis Barss 1923

Notes: From Oidium laxum - Not Duby 1830. Previously considered to be invalid due to later starting point in earlier codes. (checked protologue in rare books). From Monilia cinerea - Not Sacc. & Vogl. 1886 nor Woronin 1900

Distribution: North America (Pacific Northwest), South America, Europe, Asia, Australia, Africa (South Africa).

Substrate: Blossoms, shoots, twigs, fruits.

Disease Note: Wilt, blight, canker, brown fruit rot. Overwinters in mummified fruits.

Host: Rosaceae, primarily Prunus spp., also apples (Malus spp.), pears (Pyrus spp.) and other Pomoideae.
