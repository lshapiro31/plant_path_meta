Nomenclature data for Sclerotinia draytonii

Botrytis gladiolorum Timmerm. 1941 (Ascomycetes, Helotiales)

= Sclerotinia draytonii Buddin & Wakef. 1946  Note: From Botryotinia draytonii - as 'draytoni'

    ≡ Botryotinia draytonii (Buddin & Wakef.) Seaver 1951

Distribution: North America; Europe.

Substrate: On corms, living leaves.

Disease Note: Grey mould, botrytis blight.

Host: Various genera of Iridaceae.

Supporting Literature:

Mirzaei, S., Goltapeh, E.M., Shams-Bakhsh, M., and Safaie, N. 2008. Identification of Botrytis spp. on plants grown in Iran. J. Phytopathol. 156(1): 21-28.
