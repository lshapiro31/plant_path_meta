Nomenclature data for Sclerotinia seaveri

Monilinia seaveri (Rehm) Honey 1936 (Ascomycetes, Helotiales)

≡ Sclerotinia seaveri Rehm 1905

= Monilia seaveri J.M. Reade 1908

Distribution: Central and eastern North America.

Substrate: On leaves, twigs, and fruits.

Disease Note: Fruit rot. Twig and leaf blight.

Host: Prunus (Rosaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Batra, L.R. 1991. World species of Monilinia (Fungi): Their ecology, biosystematics and control. Mycol. Mem. 16: 1-246.
