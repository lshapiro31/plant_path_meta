Nomenclature data for Sclerotium cepivorum

Stromatinia cepivora (Berk.) Whetzel 1945 (Ascomycetes, Helotiales)

≡ Sclerotium cepivorum Berk. 1841

Notes: Although Xu et al. (2010) determined that it belongs in the family Sclerotiniaceae, the generic placement of this species is still uncertain.

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Bulbs.

Disease Note: White rot.

Host: Allium spp. (Amaryllidaceae).
