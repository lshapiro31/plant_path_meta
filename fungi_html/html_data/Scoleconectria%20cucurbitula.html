Nomenclature data for Scoleconectria cucurbitula

Thyronectria cucurbitula (Tode : Fr.) Jaklitsch & Voglmayr 2014 (Ascomycetes, Incertae sedis)

≡ Sphaeria cucurbitula Tode : Fr. 1791

≡Chilonectria cucurbitula (Tode : Fr.) Sacc. 1878

≡Nectria cucurbitula (Tode : Fr.) Fr. 1849

≡Pleonectria cucurbitula (Tode: Fr.) Hirooka, Rossman & P. Chaverri 2012

≡Scoleconectria cucurbitula (Tode : Fr.) C. Booth 1959

= Nectria cylindrospora Sollm. 1864

    ≡ Ophionectria cylindrospora (Sollm.) Berl. & Voglino 1891

= Zythia pinastri P. Karst. 1885

    ≡ Zythiostroma pinastri (P. Karst.) Höhn. 1931

= Ophionectria scolecospora Bref. & Tavel 1876

    ≡ Scoleconectria scolecospora (Bref.) Seaver 1909

Distribution: Europe, North America (Canada, USA) (Rossman 1983). Also reported from Asia (China, Pakistan, Hong Kong).

Substrate: Bark, needles of recently killed trees.

Disease Note: Conifer dieback.

Host: Pinaceae.
