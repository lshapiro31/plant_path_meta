Nomenclature data for Seimatosporium kriegerianum

Seimatosporium kriegerianum (Bres.) Morgan-Jones & B. Sutton 1964 (Ascomycetes, Xylariales)

≡ Pestalotia kriegeriana Bres. 1894

      Variant spelling Pestalozzia kriegeriana Bres.

≡Monochaetia kriegeriana (Bres.) Sacc. 1902

≡Diploceras kriegerianum (Bres.) Nag Raj 1993

≡Monoceras kriegerianum (Bres.) Guba 1961

= Discosia bubakii Kabát 1912

= Discostromopsis callistemonis H.J. Swart 1979

    ≡ Discostroma callistemonis (H.J. Swart) Sivan. 2002

= Pseudodiscosia chamaenerii Negru 1967

Distribution: Northern hemisphere.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Chamaenerion angustifolium, Epilobium angustifolium (Onagraceae).
