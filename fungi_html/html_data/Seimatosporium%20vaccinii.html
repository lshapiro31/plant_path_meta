Nomenclature data for Seimatosporium vaccinii

Seimatosporium vaccinii (Fuckel) B. Erikss. 1974 (Ascomycetes, Xylariales)

≡ Coryneum vaccinii Fuckel 1873

≡Coryneum microstictum var. staphyleae W.H. Davis 1931

≡Sporocadus vaccinii (Fuckel) M. Morelet 1985

Distribution: Temperate regions.

Substrate: Stems and twigs.

Disease Note: Twig blight.

Host: Primarily on Ericaceae.

Supporting Literature:

Norphanphoun, C., Maharachchikumbura, S.S.N., Daranagama, D.A., Bulgakov, T.S., Bhat, D.J., Bahkali, A.H., and Hyde, K.D. 2015. Towards a backbone tree for Seimatosporium, with S. physocarpi sp. nov. Mycosphere 6(3): 385-400.
