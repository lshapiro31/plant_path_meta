Nomenclature data for Septocylindrium rufomaculans

Ramularia rufomaculans Peck 1881 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

    Variant spelling Ramularia rufo-maculans Peck 1881

≡Septocylindrium rufomaculans (Peck) Pound & Clem. 1896

= Ramularia albomaculans Sawada 1958

= Ramularia polygoni Pandotra & Ganguly 1964

= Ramularia rufomaculans var. gallica Sacc. 1916

  Variant spelling Ramularia rufo-maculans var. gallica Sacc. 1916

Distribution: Asia, Europe, North America.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Bistorta spp., Persicaria spp. (Polygonaceae).
