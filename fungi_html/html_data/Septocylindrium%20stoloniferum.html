Nomenclature data for Septocylindrium stoloniferum

Ramularia stolonifera Ellis & Everh. 1891 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡Septocylindrium stoloniferum (Ellis & Everh.) Pound & Clem. 1896

= Mycovellosiella corni Y.L. Guo 1993

Distribution: Scattered reports from northern hemisphere.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Cornus spp. (Cornaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Braun, U. 1998. A Monograph of Cercosporella, Ramularia and Allied Genera (Phytopathogenic Hyphomycetes). IHW-Verlag 2: 337.

Crous, P.W., and Braun, U. 2003. Mycosphaerella and its anamorphs: 1. Names published in Cercospora and Passalora. Centraalbureau voor Schimmelcultures, Utrecht, 571 pages.
