Septoria ruborum (Lib.) Westend.

Notes: No publication date for Septoria ruborum could be found, assuming it was ever actually published.

Distribution: Temperate North America; Europe.

Substrate: Stems.

Disease Note: Purple blotch, stem spot disease; resultant fading and shriveling of plants.

Host: Rubus spp. (Rosaceae).
