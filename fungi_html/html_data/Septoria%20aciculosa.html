Nomenclature data for Septoria aciculosa

Septoria aciculosa Ellis & Everh. 1884 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

Distribution: North America, Asia, Australia, New Zealand.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Fragaria x ananassa (Rosaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Priest, M.J. 2006. Fungi of Australia. Septoria. ABRS, Canberra; CSIRO Publishing, Melbourne, 259 pages.
