Nomenclature data for Septoria anemones

Septoria anemones Desm. 1838 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

Distribution: North America, Europe, Australia.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Anemone spp. (Ranunculaceae).

Supporting Literature:
