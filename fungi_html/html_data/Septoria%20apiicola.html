Nomenclature data for Septoria apiicola

Septoria apiicola Speg. 1887 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡Rhabdospora apiicola (Speg.) Kuntze 1898

= Septoria apii Chester 1891

= Septoria apii-graveolentis Dorogin 1915

= Septoria petroselini var. apii Briosi & Cavara 1891

Distribution: Temperate regions.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Apium australe, A. graveolens var. graveolens (celery), A. graveolens var. rapaceum (celeriac), A. prostratum (Apiaceae).
