Nomenclature data for Septoria artemisiae

Septoria artemisiae Pass. 1879 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

Notes: Non S. artemisiae Unamuno (1923), an illegitimate later homonym.

Distribution: Asia, Europe, North America.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Artemisia spp. (Asteraceae).

Supporting Literature:

Shin, H.D., and Sameva, E.F. 2004. Septoria in Korea. National Institute of Agricultural Science and Technology, Suwon, Korea, 183 pages.
