Nomenclature data for Septoria astragali

Septoria astragali Roberge ex Desm. 1843 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

Distribution: Temperate regions.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Astragalus glycphyllus; reported from other Astragalus spp. and Lathyrus spp. (Fabaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Verkley, G.J.M., Quaedvlieg, W., Shin, H.-D., and Crous, P.W. 2013. A new approach to species delimitation in Septoria. Stud. Mycol. 75: 213-305.
