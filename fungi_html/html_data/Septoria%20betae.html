Nomenclature data for Septoria betae

Septoria betae Westend. 1851 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

Notes: See also: Arden F. Sherf: Vegetable Diseases and Their Control (1986).

Distribution: Temperate regions.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Beta vulgaris (Amaranthaceae).
