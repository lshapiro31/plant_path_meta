Nomenclature data for Septoria cerastii

Septoria cerastii Roberge ex Desm. 1849 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

Distribution: North America (USA), Europe, Asia, Australia.

Substrate: Living leaves; withering leaves, stems and bracts.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Cerastium spp., Stellaria holostea (Caryophyllaceae).
