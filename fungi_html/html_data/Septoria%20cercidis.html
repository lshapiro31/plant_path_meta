Nomenclature data for Septoria cercidis

Sphaerulina cercidis (Fr.) Quaedvlieg, Verkley & Crous 2013 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Septoria cercidis Fr. 1848

= Septoria provencialis Crous 2006

Distribution: Temperate regions.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Eucalyptus sp. (Myrtaceae), Cercis spp. (Fabaceae).

Supporting Literature:
