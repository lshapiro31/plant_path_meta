Nomenclature data for Septoria cirsii

Septoria cirsii Niessl 1864 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

Distribution: Temperate regions.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Cirsium spp. (Asteraceae).

Supporting Literature:

Shin, H.D., and Sameva, E.F. 2004. Septoria in Korea. National Institute of Agricultural Science and Technology, Suwon, Korea, 183 pages.
