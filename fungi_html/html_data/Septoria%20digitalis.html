Nomenclature data for Septoria digitalis

Septoria digitalis Pass. (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

Distribution: WI; Europe; Asia.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Digitalis spp. (Plantaginaceae).
