Nomenclature data for Septoria donacis

Pseudoseptoria donacis (Pass.) B. Sutton 1977 (Ascomycetes, Incertae sedis)

≡ Septoria donacis Pass. 1879

≡Selenophoma donacis (Pass.) R. Sprague & Aar.G. Johnson 1940

= Septoria oxyspora Penz. & Sacc. 1884

Notes: Halo spot of cereals and grasses (Punithalingam & Waller. C.M.I. Descr. 400. 1973).

Distribution: Temperate regions.

Substrate: Living leaves and stems.

Disease Note: Eye spot, halo spot, stem speckle.

Host: On Poaceae.

Supporting Literature:

Punithalingam, E., and Waller, J. 1973. Selenophoma donacis. C.M.I. Descr. Pathog. Fungi Bact. 400: 1-2.
