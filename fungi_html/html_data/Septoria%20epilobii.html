Nomenclature data for Septoria epilobii

Septoria epilobii Westend. 1852 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

Notes: Host genus Chamaenerion now included within Epilobium (Onagraceae).

Distribution: Temperate regions.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf lesions.

Host: Epilobium spp. (Onagraceae).

Supporting Literature:

Verkley, G.J.M., Quaedvlieg, W., Shin, H.-D., and Crous, P.W. 2013. A new approach to species delimitation in Septoria. Stud. Mycol. 75: 213-305.
