Nomenclature data for Septoria erigerontis

Septoria erigerontis Peck 1872 [1871] (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

    Variant spelling Septoria erigeronis Peck 1872 [1871]

    Variant spelling Septoria erigerontea Peck 1872 [1871]  Note: Spelling given by Saccardo.

= Septoria chanousii Ferraris 1903 [1902]

= Septoria erigeronata Thüm. 1881  Note: Possibly validly published earlier in 1881.

[= Septoria erigerontis Hollós 1926 - illegitimate later homonym, not included in search]  Note: Not Peck 1872 nec Berk. & M.A. Curtis 1876. Nom. illeg.

[= Septoria erigerontis Berk. & M.A. Curtis 1874 - illegitimate later homonym, not included in search]  Note: Not Peck 1872. Nom. illeg.

= Rhabdospora schnabliana Allesch. 1895

    ≡ Septoria schnabliana (Allesch.) Died. 1914

= Septoria stenactidis Vill. 1910  Note: As 'stenactis'. Published by Sydow who credited Villars with the name and probably the validating description.

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Erigeron spp. (Asteraceae).
