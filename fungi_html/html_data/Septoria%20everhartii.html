Nomenclature data for Septoria everhartii

Pseudoseptoria everhartii (Sacc. & Syd.) B. Sutton 1980 (Ascomycetes, Incertae sedis)

≡ Septoria everhartii Sacc. & Syd. 1902  Note: as 'everhardtii'

≡Selenophoma everhartii (Sacc. & Syd.) R. Sprague & Aar.G. Johnson 1945

Notes: Eye spot of grasses.

Distribution: Temperate North America; northern Africa; Soviet Union.

Substrate: Living leaves and stems.

Disease Note: Eye spot, stem speckle.

Host: On Poaceae.

Supporting Literature:

Sutton, B.C. 1980. The Coelomycetes. Fungi Imperfecti with Pycnidia, Acervuli and Stromata. Commonwealth Mycological Institute, Kew, Surrey, England, 696 pages.
