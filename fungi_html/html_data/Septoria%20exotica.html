Nomenclature data for Septoria exotica

Septoria exotica Speg. 1880 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

Distribution: CA, HI, WI; Argentina; England; Germany, Australia.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Derwentia derwentiana, Hebe spp. (Plantaginaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Priest, M.J. 2006. Fungi of Australia. Septoria. ABRS, Canberra; CSIRO Publishing, Melbourne, 259 pages.
