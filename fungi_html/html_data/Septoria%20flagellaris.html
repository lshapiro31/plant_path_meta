Nomenclature data for Septoria flagellaris

Septoria convolvuli Desm. 1842 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

= Septoria convolvuli var. convolvuli-persici O. Savul. & Eliade 1957

= Septoria convolvuli var. soldanellae Brunaud 1886

= Septoria flagellaris Desm. 1883

    ≡ Rhabdospora flagellaris (Ellis & Everh.) Kuntze 1898

= Septoria fuscella Berk. 1859

= Sphaeria gentianaecola var. convolvulicola DC. 1823

    ≡ Depazea convolvulicola (DC) Berk 1841

= Sphaeria lichenoides var. convolvulicola DC. 1815

= Septoria septulata Beach 1919

Distribution: Africa, (Libya), North America (USA; WA, WY), Europe (Germany, Netherlands, Spain), Asia (China, South Korea, Pakistan), New Zealand.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf lesions.

Host: Calystegia spp. Convolvulus (Convolvulaceae).
