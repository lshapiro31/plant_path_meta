Nomenclature data for Septoria gaurina

Septoria gaurina Ellis & Kellerm. 1883 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

Distribution: Central and western United States, Australia.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Gaura spp. (Onagraceae).
