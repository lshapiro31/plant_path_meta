Nomenclature data for Septoria halophila

Zymoseptoria halophila (Speg.) M. Razavi, Quaedvlieg & Crous 2011 (, )

≡ Septoria halophila Speg. 1910

Distribution: Widespread based on scattered reports.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Hordeum (Poaceae).

Supporting Literature:
