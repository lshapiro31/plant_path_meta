Nomenclature data for Septoria helianthi

Septoria helianthi Ellis & Kellerm. 1883 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

Distribution: Temperate regions.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot, defoliation.

Host: Helianthus spp. (Asteraceae).
