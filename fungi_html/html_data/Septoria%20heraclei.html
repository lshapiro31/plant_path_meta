Nomenclature data for Septoria heraclei

Septoria heraclei (Lib.) Desm. 1831 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Ascochyta heraclei Lib. 1830

≡Phloeospora heraclei (Lib.) Petr. 1919

≡Cylindrosporium umbelliferarum Wehm. 1947  Note: Cylindrosporium umbelliferarum is a nom. nov.

= Septoria heraclei-palmati Maire 1905

Notes: Cylindrosporium heraclei (Lib.) Höhn. (1906) illegitimate (Art. 53.1); see C. heraclei Oudem. (1873).

Distribution: Asia, Europe, North America.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Heracleum spp. (Apiaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Verkley, G.J.M., Quaedvlieg, W., Shin, H.-D., and Crous, P.W. 2013. A new approach to species delimitation in Septoria. Stud. Mycol. 75: 213-305.
