Nomenclature data for Septoria lactucicola

Septoria lactucae Pass. 1879 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

= Septoria consimilis Ellis & G. Martin 1885

= Ascochyta lactucae Rostr. 1882

[= Septoria lactucae Peck 1879 - illegitimate later homonym, not included in search]  Note: A homonym exists for this name.

= Septoria lactucicola Ellis & G. Martin 1882

Notes:

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Leaves, seeds.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Lactuca sativa, L. serriola (Asteraceae).
