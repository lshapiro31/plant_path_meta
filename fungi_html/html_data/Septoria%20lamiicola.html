Nomenclature data for Septoria lamiicola

Septoria lamiicola Sacc. 1884 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Septoria heterochroa f. lamii Desm. 1847

= Septoria lamii Westend. 1852

[= Septoria lamii Pass. 1879 - illegitimate later homonym, not included in search]

Notes: Septoria lamiicola is a nom. nov. created to prevent conflict with Septoria lamii Pass., which is a later homonym of S. lamii Westend.; S. lamiicola is valid regardless of which homonym is earlier.

Distribution: Europe, Australia.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Lamium spp. (Lamiaceae).
