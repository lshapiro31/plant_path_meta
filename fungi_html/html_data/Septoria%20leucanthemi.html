Nomenclature data for Septoria leucanthemi

Septoria leucanthemi Sacc. & Speg. 1878 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡Rhabdospora leucanthemi (Sacc. & Speg.) Petr. 1958

Distribution: North and South America; Africa; Asia; Europe.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot, leaf blotch.

Host: Chrysanthemum spp., Tagetes spp., Centaurea spp., Helianthus spp. (Asteraceae).

Supporting Literature:

Verkley, G.J.M., Quaedvlieg, W., Shin, H.-D., and Crous, P.W. 2013. A new approach to species delimitation in Septoria. Stud. Mycol. 75: 213-305.
