Nomenclature data for Septoria lycopersici

Septoria lycopersici var. lycopersici Speg. 1881 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡Septoria lycopersici Speg. 1881

Distribution: Cosmopolitan; throughout host range.

Substrate: Leaves, seed-borne.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Primarily tomato, Solanum lycopersicum; also other Solanaceae.
