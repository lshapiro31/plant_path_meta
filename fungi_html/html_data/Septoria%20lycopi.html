Nomenclature data for Septoria lycopi

Septoria lycopi Pass. 1878 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

Distribution: Asia (Japan, Korea), Europe (Greece, Romania, Scotland), North America (USA).

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Lycopus spp. (Lamiaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Shin, H.D., and Sameva, E.F. 2004. Septoria in Korea. National Institute of Agricultural Science and Technology, Suwon, Korea, 183 pages.
