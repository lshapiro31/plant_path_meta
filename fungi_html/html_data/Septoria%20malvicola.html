Nomenclature data for Septoria malvicola

Septoria malvicola Ellis & G. Martin 1887 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

Distribution: North America (USA), Australia.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Althaea rosea, Malva spp. (Malvaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Priest, M.J. 2006. Fungi of Australia. Septoria. ABRS, Canberra; CSIRO Publishing, Melbourne, 259 pages.
