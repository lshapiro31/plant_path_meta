Nomenclature data for Septoria matricariae

Septoria matricariae Hollós 1910 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

= Septoria chamomillae Andrian. 1996

Distribution: North America (USA; WI) Europe (Netherlands, Romania) Asia.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf lesions.

Host: Matricaria spp. (Asteraceae).

Supporting Literature:

Verkley, G.J.M., Quaedvlieg, W., Shin, H.-D., and Crous, P.W. 2013. A new approach to species delimitation in Septoria. Stud. Mycol. 75: 213-305.

Updated prior to 1989
