Nomenclature data for Septoria melandrii

Caryophylloseptoria lychnidis (Desm.) Verkley, Quaedvlieg & Crous. 2013 (, )

= Septoria melandrii Pass. 1879

= Septoria melandryi-albi Bäumler 1903

= Septoria silenicola Ellis & G. Martin 1882

Distribution: Temperate regions in the Northern Hemisphere, Australia.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Lychnis spp., Silene spp. (Caryophyllaceae).
