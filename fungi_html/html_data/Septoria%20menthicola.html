Nomenclature data for Septoria menthicola

Septoria menthae (Thüm.) Oudem. 1875 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Depazea menthae Thüm. 1874

[≡Septoria menthae Sacc. & Let. 1881 - illegitimate later homonym, not included in search]

= Septoria menthicola Sacc. & Letendre 1884

Distribution: Temperate regions.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Mentha spp. (Lamiaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Shin, H.D., and Sameva, E.F. 2004. Septoria in Korea. National Institute of Agricultural Science and Technology, Suwon, Korea, 183 pages.
