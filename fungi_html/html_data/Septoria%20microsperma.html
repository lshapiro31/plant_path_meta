Nomenclature data for Septoria microsperma

Cylindrosporella microsperma (Peck) Petr. 1924 (Ascomycetes, Diaporthales)

≡ Septoria microsperma Peck 1884

≡Asteroma microspermum (Peck) B. Sutton 1980

≡Sacidium microspermum (Peck) Davis 1915

= Gloeosporium betulae-luteae Sacc. & Dearn. 1915

Distribution: Scattered reports in northern hemisphere.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Betula spp. (Betulaceae).
