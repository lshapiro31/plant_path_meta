Nomenclature data for Septoria miuraei

Septoria taraxaci Hollós 1907 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

= Septoria miuraei Trotter 1913

= Septoria taraxacicola Miura 1913

Distribution: Asia, Europe; possibly worldwide.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Taraxacum spp. (Asteraceae).

Supporting Literature:

Shin, H.D., and Sameva, E.F. 2004. Septoria in Korea. National Institute of Agricultural Science and Technology, Suwon, Korea, 183 pages.
