Nomenclature data for Septoria musiva

Sphaerulina musiva (Peck) Quaedvlieg, Verkley & Crous 2013 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Septoria musiva Peck 1883

= Cylindrosporium oculatum Ellis & Everh. 1889

= Mycosphaerella populorum G.E. Thomps. 1941

    ≡ Davidiella populorum (G.E. Thomps.) Aptroot 2006

Distribution: North America (eastern Canada and US), report from South America (Argentina).

Substrate: Leaves, twigs, branches.

Disease Note: Leaf lesions, canker.

Host: Populus spp., Salix lucida (Salicaceae), report from Sorbus aucuparia (Rosaceae).
