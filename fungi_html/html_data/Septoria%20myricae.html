Nomenclature data for Septoria myricae

Septoria myricae Ellis & Everh. 1897 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

Notes: Illegitimate name; the reports from the U.S. are all for the Ellis & Everhart name, which is a later homonym of Septoria myricae Trail (1888). Grove (British stem and leaf fungi. Vol. I. p. 393. 1935) suggested that these are probably the same species.

Distribution: Eastern United States.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Concentric leaf spot.

Host: Myrica cerifera (Myricaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Gardner, D.E. 1999. Septoria hodgesii sp. nov.: a potential biocontrol agent for Myrica faya in Hawaii. Mycotaxon 70: 247-253.
