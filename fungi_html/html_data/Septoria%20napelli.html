Nomenclature data for Septoria napelli

Septoria napelli Speg. 1879 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

Distribution: Asia (Korea), Europe (Austraia, Poland, Romania).

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Aconitum spp. (Ranunculaceae).

Supporting Literature:
