Nomenclature data for Septoria obesa

Septoria obesa Syd. 1914 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

Distribution: North America (USA; FL), Australia, Asia (China, India, South Korea), Europe (Germany).

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Artemisia spp., Chrysanthemum spp., Leucanthemum maximum (Asteraceae).
