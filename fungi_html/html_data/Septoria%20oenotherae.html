Nomenclature data for Septoria oenotherae

Septoria oenotherae Westend. 1857 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

Distribution: Temperate North America; Europe.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Oenothera spp. (Onagraceae).

Supporting Literature:
