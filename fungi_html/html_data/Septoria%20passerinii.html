Nomenclature data for Septoria passerinii

Zymoseptoria passerinii (Sacc.) Quaedvlieg & Crous 2011 (, )

≡ Septoria passerinii Sacc. 1884  Note: Nom. nov.

[≡Septoria murina Pass. 1879 - illegitimate later homonym, not included in search]  Note: Not Septoria murina Thüm. 1879. Nom. illeg.

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Speckled leaf blotch of barley.

Host: Hordeum and possibly other grasses.
