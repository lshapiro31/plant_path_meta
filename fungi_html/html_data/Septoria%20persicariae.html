Nomenclature data for Septoria persicariae

Septoria polygonorum Desm. 1842 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡Spilosphaeria polygonorum (Desm.) Rabenh. 1856

= Septoria persicariae O'Gara 1917

= Septoria polygonicola Lasch ex Sacc. 1890

= Septoria polygonina Thüm. 1880

Distribution: Temperate northern hemisphere, Australia.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Fallopia convolvulus, Persicaria spp., Polygonum spp., Rheum officinale (Polygonaceae).
