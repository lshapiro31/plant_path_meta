Nomenclature data for Septoria petroselini

Septoria petroselini (Lib.) Desm. 1843 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Ascochyta petroselini Lib. 1834

≡Phloeospora petroselini (Lib.) Westend. 1845

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Petroselinum crispum, other Petroselinum spp., Coriandrum sativum (Apiaceae).
