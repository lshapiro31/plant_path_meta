Nomenclature data for Septoria phlogis

Septoria phlogis Sacc. & Speg. 1878 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

    Variant spelling Septoria phlocis Sacc. & Speg. 1878

= Septoria drummondii Ellis & Everh. 1892

= Septoria phlogina Bondartsev 1921

Notes: Though Septoria phlogis and S. divaricata are accepted as separate species, the taxonomy of both species has yet to be resolved, and under which S. drummondii is a synonym has yet to be determined.

Distribution: Asia (China, India, South Korea), Europe, New Zealand, North America (USA: CA, FL, MI, PA).

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf lesions.

Host: Phlox spp. (Polemoniaceae).
