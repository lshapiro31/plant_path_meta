Nomenclature data for Septoria poae-trivialis

Septoria macropoda Pass. 1879 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

= Septoria annua Ellis & Everh. 1900

= Septoria poae-trivialis Cocc. 1896

Notes: Leaf blotch.

Distribution: Temperate regions.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf blotch and leaf spot.

Host: On grasses especially turfgrasses in Poa (Poaceae).
