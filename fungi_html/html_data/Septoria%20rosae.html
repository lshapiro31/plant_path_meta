Nomenclature data for Septoria rosae

Septoria rosae Desm. 1831 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

= Sphaerulina rehmiana Jaap 1910

Notes: Jørstad (Skr. Norske Videnski.-Akad. Oslo, Mat.-Naturvidensk. Kl. 22: 57. 1965); Boerema (Neth. J. Pl. Pathol. 69:76. 1963)

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf scorch and spot.

Host: Primarily on Rosa (Rosaceae).
