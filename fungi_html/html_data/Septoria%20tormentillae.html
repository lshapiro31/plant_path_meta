Nomenclature data for Septoria tormentillae

Septoria tormentillae Roberge ex Desm. 1847 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

= Sphaerella ariadna subsp. tormentillae Sacc. 1878

    ≡ Mycosphaerella tormentillae (Sacc.) Tomilin 1979

= Septoria corcontica Kabát & Bubák 1907

= Septoria potentillae Hollós 1926

= Septoria sparsa Fuckel 1870

Distribution: Asia, Europe.

Substrate: Living and fading leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Potentilla spp. (Rosaceae).

Supporting Literature:
