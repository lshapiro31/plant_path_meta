Nomenclature data for Septoria urens

Septoria cruciatae Roberge ex Desm. 1847 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

= Septoria aparines Ellis & Kellerm. 1889

    ≡ Rhabdospora aparines (Ellis & Kellerm.) Kuntze 1898

= Septoria asperulae Bäumler 1890

[= Septoria galli-borealis Bubák & Kabát 1912 - illegitimate later homonym, not included in search]

= Septoria galli-borealis Henn. 1905

= Septoria urens Pass. 1879

Distribution: United States; Europe; Asia.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Galium spp. (Rubiaceae).
