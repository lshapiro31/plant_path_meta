Nomenclature data for Septotinia podophyllina

Septotinia podophyllina Whetzel ex J.W. Groves & M.E. Elliott 1937 (Ascomycetes, Helotiales)

= Gloeosporium podophyllinum Ellis & Everh. 1888  Note: From Septotis podophyllina - As 'podophyllinum'. Leaf blight of mayapple. Recently reported to cause a dieback of Populus, Prunus and Salix in Europe (Gremmen. Mycotaxon 28:255. 1987).

    ≡ Septotis podophyllina (Ellis & Everh.) Arx 1970

    ≡ Septogloeum podophyllinum (Ellis & Everh.) Sacc. 1892

Notes: Leaf blight of mayapple. Recently this fungus is reported to cause a dieback of Populus, Prunus and Salix in Europe (Gremmen. Mycotaxon 28:255. 1987).

Distribution: Central and eastern United States; Europe (introduced).

Substrate: On living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf blight.

Host: Primarily on Podophyllum (Berberidaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Gremmen, J. 1987. Notes on the mayapple and the life history of the fungus Septotinia podophyllina. Mycotaxon 28: 255-256.
