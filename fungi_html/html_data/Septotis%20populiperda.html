Nomenclature data for Septotis populiperda

Septotinia populiperda Waterman & Cash ex B. Sutton 1980 (Ascomycetes, Helotiales)

= Septogloeum populiperdum Moesz & Smarods 1932

  ≡Septotis populiperda (Moesz & Smarods) B. Sutton 1980

Notes: Leaf blotch of poplar (Waterman & Cash. Mycologia 42:374. 1950).

Distribution: Eastern North America; Europe.

Disease Note: Poplar leaf blotch.

Host: On Populus (Salicaceae).
