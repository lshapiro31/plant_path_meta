Nomenclature data for Serpula lacrymans

Serpula lacrymans (Wulfen : Fr.) J. Schröt. 1885 (Basidiomycetes, Boletales)

≡ Boletus lacrimans Wulfen 1781

      Variant spelling Boletus lacrymans Wulfen

≡Merulius lacrymans (Wulfen) Schumach. 1801

  Variant spelling Merulius lacrimans (Wulfen) Schumach.

≡Merulius vastator Tode : Fr. 1821

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Wood; usually in artificial environments.

Disease Note: Dry rot; a major destroyer of construction materials.

Host: Numerous hosts.
