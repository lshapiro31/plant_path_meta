Nomenclature data for Setomelanomma holmii

Setomelanomma holmii M. Morelet 1980 (Ascomycetes, Dothideales)

Distribution: Asia (China), Europe (France), North America.

Substrate: Branches.

Disease Note: Spotting.

Host: Picea spp. (Pinaceae).
