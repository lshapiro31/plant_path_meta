Nomenclature data for Seuratia millardetii

Atichia glomerulosa (Ach. ex H. Mann) Stein 1850 (Ascomycetes, Incertae sedis)

≡ Collema glomerulosum Ach. 1810

= Atichia millardetii Racib. 1909  Note: From Seuratia millardetii - Oldest epithet not in correct genus, however, Atichia based on A. mosigii, has priority over Seuratia.

  ≡Seuratia millardetii (Racib.) Meeker 1975

  Variant spelling Atichia millardeti Racib.

= Atichia mosigii Flot. 1850

Notes: Atichia based on A. mosigii has priority over Seuratia.

Distribution: OR; warm temperate to tropical regions.

Substrate: On evergreen leaves and coniferous barks. Dennis (British Ascomycetes. p. 398. 1978).

Disease Note: Superificial on leaves.

Supporting Literature:

Gillis, I.M., and Glawe, D.A. 2008. Characterization of Seuratia millardetii on Camellia species and in artificial culture. N. Amer. Fungi 3: 215-229.
