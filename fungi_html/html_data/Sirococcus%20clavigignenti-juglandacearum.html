Nomenclature data for Sirococcus clavigignenti-juglandacearum

Ophiognomonia clavigignenti-juglandacearum (N.B. Nair, Kostichka & J.E. Kuntze) Broders & Boland 2010 (Ascomycetes, Diaporthales)

≡ Sirococcus clavigignenti-juglandacearum N.B. Nair, Kostichka & J.E. Kuntze 1979

Distribution: North America.

Substrate: Stems, roots; isolated from leaves, flowers and other plant parts.

Disease Note: Canker; butternut canker.

Host: Juglans cinerea (primary host) and other Juglans spp., Carya spp. (Juglandaceae).
