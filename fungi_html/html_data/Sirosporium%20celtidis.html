Nomenclature data for Sirosporium celtidis

Sirosporium celtidis (Biv.) M.B. Ellis 1963 (Ascomycetes, Incertae sedis)

≡ Monilia celtidis Biv. 1815

≡Gyrocerus celtidis (Biv.) Mont. & Ces. 1856  Note: Spelling corrected from celtis.

  Variant spelling Gyroceras celtidis (Biv.) Mont. & Ces. 1856  Note: Spelling corrected from Gyrocerus celtis.

≡Helicoceras celtidis (Biv.) Linder 1931

Distribution: Africa, Asia, Europe (Italy, Portugal), North America (USA: AR, KS, MO).

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Dark mildew.

Host: Celtis spp. (Cannabaceae).
