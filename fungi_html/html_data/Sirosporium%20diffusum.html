Nomenclature data for Sirosporium diffusum

Ragnhildiana diffusa (Heald & F.A. Wolf) Videira & Crous 2017 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Clasterosporium diffusum Heald & F.A. Wolf 1911

≡Sirosporium diffusum (Heald & F.A. Wolf) Deighton 1976

≡Cercospora fusca F.V. Rand 1914

Notes: Cercospora fusca - nom. nov. - non C. diffusa Ellis & Everh. (1888).

Distribution: Africa, North America, South America.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Brown leaf spot.

Host: Carya spp., Juglans regia (Juglandaceae).
