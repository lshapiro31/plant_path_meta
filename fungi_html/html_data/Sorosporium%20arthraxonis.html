Nomenclature data for Sorosporium arthraxonis

Sporisorium tranzschelianum (Lavrov) Karatygin 1989 (Ustilaginomycetes, Ustilaginales)

≡ Sorosporium tranzschelianum Lavrov 1936

= Sorosporium arthraxonis S. Ahmad 1956

[= Sphacelotheca arthraxonis Mishra 1957 - illegitimate later homonym, not included in search]  Note: Not (Pat.) Zundel 1953. Nom. illeg.

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Inflorescences.

Disease Note: Smut.

Host: Arthraxon spp. (Poaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Vanky, K. 2012. Smut Fungi of the World. APS Press, St. Paul, Minnesota, 1458 pages.

Vanky, K., Vanky, C., and Denchev, C.M. 2011. Smut fungi in Africa- a checklist. Mycol. Balcan. 8: 1-77.
