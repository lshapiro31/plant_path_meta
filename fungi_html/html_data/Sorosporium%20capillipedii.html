Nomenclature data for Sorosporium capillipedii

Macalpinomyces chrysopogonicola (Mundk. & Thirum.) Vánky 2001 (Ustilaginomycetes, Ustilaginales)

≡ Sphacelotheca chrysopogonicola Mundk. & Thirum. 1951

= Sorosporium capillipedii Mishra 1957

    ≡ Endosporisorium capillipedii (Mishra) Vánky 1995

= Ustilago chrysopogonis S. Ahmad 1956

    ≡ Macalpinomyces chrysopogonis (S. Ahmad) Vánky 1997

Distribution: Asia, North America (Cuba).

Disease Note: Smut.

Host: Several taxa of Poaceae.

Supporting Literature:
