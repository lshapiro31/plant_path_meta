= Ustilago holci-sorghi Rivolta 1873

= Ustilago reiliana f. zeae Pass. 1876

Notes: Vanky (2011) includes additional synonyms.

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Inflorescences.

Disease Note: Head smut.

Host: Several taxa of Poaceae.
