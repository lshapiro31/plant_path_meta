Nomenclature data for Sorosporium reverdattoanum

Tranzscheliella minima (Arthur) Vánky 2003 (Ustilaginomycetes, Ustilaginales)

≡ Ustilago minima Arthur 1884

= Sorosporium reverdattoanum Lavrov 1934

= Ustilago stipae Cif. 1931

= Sphacelotheca valesiaca Arthur 1911

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Disease Note: Smut.

Host: Several taxa of Poaceae.
