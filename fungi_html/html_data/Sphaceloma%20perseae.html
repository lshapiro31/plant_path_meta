Nomenclature data for Sphaceloma perseae

Elsinoe perseae (Jenkins) Rossman & W.C. Allen 2016 (Ascomycetes, Myriangiales)

≡ Sphaceloma perseae Jenkins 1934

Notes: http://www.growables.org/information/documents/DiseasesOfAvocadoInFlorida.pdf ---- Elsinoë protected over Sphaceloma by Wijayawardene et al. (2014).

Distribution: Widespread especially in subtropical to tropical regions.

Substrate: Fruits, leaves, stems.

Disease Note: Scab on avocado., leaf spot/shot hole.

Host: Persea spp. (Lauraceae). Also reported on Ananas comosus (Bromeliaceae).
