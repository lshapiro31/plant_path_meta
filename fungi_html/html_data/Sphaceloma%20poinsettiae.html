Nomenclature data for Sphaceloma poinsettiae

Elsinoe poinsettiae (Jenkins & Ruehle) Rossman & W.C. Allen 2016 (Ascomycetes, Myriangiales)

≡ Sphaceloma poinsettiae Jenkins & Ruehle 1942

Notes: Doidge (1950) mentioned a connection to the teleomorphic name Elsinoe poinsettiae Jenkins, but no record exists of valid publication of this name. Elsinoë protected over Sphaceloma by Wijayawardene et al. (2014).

Distribution: Africa (South Africa, Zimbabwe), Caribbean and Central America, North America (Mexico, USA), South America (Brazil), Pacific Islands (Hawaii, Fiji).

Substrate: Leaves, stems.

Disease Note: Poinsettia scab, spot anthracnose.

Host: Euphorbia pulcherrima (=Poinsettia pulcherrima), rarely on other Euphorbia spp. (Euphorbiaceae).
