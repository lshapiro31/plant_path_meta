Elsinoe psidii (Bitanc. & Jenkins) Romberg & W.C. Allen 2016 (Ascomycetes, Myriangiales)

≡ Sphaceloma psidii Bitanc. & Jenkins 1950

Distribution: South America (Brazil), New Zealand.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Psidium guajava, Feijoa sellowiana (Myrtaceae).
