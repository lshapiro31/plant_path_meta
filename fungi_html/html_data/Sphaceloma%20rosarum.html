Nomenclature data for Sphaceloma rosarum

Elsinoe rosarum Jenkins & Bitanc. 1957 (Ascomycetes, Myriangiales)

= Phyllosticta rosarum Pass. 1881  Note: From Sphaceloma rosarum - Rose anthracnose.

  ≡Sphaceloma rosarum (Pass.) Jenkins 1932

    ≡ Gloeosporium rosarum (Pass.) Grove 1937

Distribution: Temperate regions.

Substrate: Living leaves and stems.

Disease Note: Rose anthracnose.

Host: Rosa (Rosaceae).
