Nomenclature data for Sphacelotheca austroamericana

Melanopsichium austroamericanum (Speg.) Beck 1894 (Ustilaginomycetes, Ustilaginales)

    Variant spelling Melanopsichium austro-americanum (Speg.) Beck 1894

≡ Ustilago austroamericana Speg. 1881

      Variant spelling Ustilago austro-americana Speg. 1881

≡Sphacelotheca austroamericana (Speg.) Liro 1924

  Variant spelling Sphacelotheca austro-americana (Speg.) Liro 1924

Distribution: Widespread, but uncertain due to taxonomic confusion.

Substrate: Inflorescences, stems, leaves.

Disease Note: Smut; galls.

Host: Polygonum, Persicaria (Polygonaceae).
