Nomenclature data for Sphacelotheca paspali-notati

Sporisorium paspali (Speg.) Vánky 2007 (Ustilaginomycetes, Ustilaginales)

≡ Ustilago paspali Speg. 1899

= Ustilago aristidicola Henn. 1908

    ≡ Sporisorium paspalicola M. Piepenbr. 2003  Note: Nom. nov. since the epithet 'aristidicola' was occupied in the genus.

= Sphacelotheca paspali-notati G.P. Clinton 1902  Note: As (Henn.) G.P. Clinton.

     Ustilago paspali-notati Henn. 1902  Note: In herb. Nomen inval.

     Sporisorium paspali-notati Sporisorium paspali-notati 2002  Note: Nomen inval. since based on an invalid name.

Distribution: Scattered reports, especially the Americas.

Disease Note: Smut.

Host: Paspalum spp. (Poaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Vanky, K. 2007. Taxonomic studies on Ustilaginomycetes - 27. Mycotaxon 99: 1-70.

Vanky, K. 2012. Smut Fungi of the World. APS Press, St. Paul, Minnesota, 1458 pages.
