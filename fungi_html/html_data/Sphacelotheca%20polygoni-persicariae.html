Nomenclature data for Sphacelotheca polygoni-persicariae

Sphacelotheca polygoni-serrulati Maire 1917 (Urediniomycetes, Microbotryales)

≡Ustilago polygoni-serrulati (Maire) Cif. 1959

= Sphacelotheca doliaris Liro 1924

= Sphacelotheca polygoni-persicariae G. Deml & Oberw. 1985

= Sphacelotheca tropicoafricana Zundel 1944

  Variant spelling Sphacelotheca tropico-africana Zundel 1944

Notes: Vanky (2011) mentions some other synonyms with confused nomenclatural histories.

Distribution: Africa, Australasia.

Substrate: Inflorescences.

Disease Note: Smut.

Host: Persicaria (Polygonaceae).
