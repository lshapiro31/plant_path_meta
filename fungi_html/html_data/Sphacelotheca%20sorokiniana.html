Nomenclature data for Sphacelotheca sorokiniana

Sporisorium cruentum (Kühn) Vánky 1985 (Ustilaginomycetes, Ustilaginales)

≡ Ustilago cruenta J.G. Kühn 1872

≡Sphacelotheca cruenta (J.G. Kühn) A.A. Potter 1912

= Sphacelotheca holci H.S. Jacks. 1934

= Endothlaspis sorghi Sorokin 1890

    ≡ Cintractia sorghi (Sorokin) De Toni 1888

  ≡Sphacelotheca sorokiniana Cif. 1928  Note: Nom. nov. due to S. sorghi (Ehrenb. ex Link) G.P. Clinton 1902.

= Ustilago sorghi Pass. 1873

Notes: Sphacelotheca sorokiniana is a nom. nov. created to prevent conflict with Sporisorium sorghi Ehrenb. ex Link (1825).

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Inflorescences.

Disease Note: Smut.

Host: Sorghum spp. and other Poaceae.
