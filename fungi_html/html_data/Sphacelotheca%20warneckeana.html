Nomenclature data for Sphacelotheca warneckeana

Sporisorium moniliferum (Ellis & Everh.) L. Guo 1990 (Ustilaginomycetes, Ustilaginales)

≡ Ustilago monilifera Ellis & Everh. 1895

≡Sphacelotheca monilifera (Ellis & Everh.) G.P. Clinton 1902

= Sphacelotheca ischaemi f. heteropogonis Bacc. 1917

= Ustilago warneckeana Henn. 1905

  ≡Sphacelotheca warneckeana (Henn.) Zundel 1930

Notes: Ustilago andropogonis-contorti Henn. never formally published.

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Ovaries.

Disease Note: Smut.

Host: Heteropogon (Poaceae).
