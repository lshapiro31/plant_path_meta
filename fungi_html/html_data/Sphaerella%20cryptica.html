Nomenclature data for Sphaerella cryptica

Teratosphaeria cryptica (Cooke) Crous & U. Braun 2007 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

≡ Sphaerella cryptica Cooke 1891

≡Mycosphaerella cryptica (Cooke) Hansf. 1956

= Colletogloeum nubilosum Ganap. & Corbin 1979

    ≡ Readeriella nubilosa (Ganap. & Corbin) Crous & U. Braun 2007

    ≡ Colletogloeopsis nubilosum (Ganap. & Corbin) Crous & M.J. Wingf. 1997

    =  Kirramyces nubilosum (Ganap. & Corbin) Andjic 2007

Distribution: Widespread, especially southern hemisphere.

Substrate: On living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Eucalyptus spp. (Myrtaceae).
