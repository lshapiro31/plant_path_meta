Notes: Chen (2002) offers more synonyms. From Sphaeria acuta - Sanctioned by Fries 1823.

Distribution: Cosmopolitan in northern hemisphere.

Substrate: Leaves, stems.

Disease Note: Not known; at least saprobic in some instances.

Host: Multiple genera in multiple families.
