Nomenclature data for Sphaeria agnita

Plenodomus agnitus (Desm.) Gruyter, Aveskamp & Verkley 2012 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

≡ Sphaeria agnita Desm. 1851

≡Leptosphaeria agnita (Desm.) Ces. & De Not. 1863

= Phoma agnita Gonz. Frag. 1920

= Plenodomus chondrillae Died. 1912

Notes: From Phoma acuta f. petasites - Without description, nom. nud.

Distribution: Europe. Reports of this fungus in North America are doubtful.

Substrate: Dead stems.

Disease Note: Saprobic.

Host: Asteraceae, especially Eupatorium, and other herbaceous plants.
