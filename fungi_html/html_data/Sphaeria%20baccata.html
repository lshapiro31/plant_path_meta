Nomenclature data for Sphaeria baccata

Fusarium lateritium Nees : Fr. 1817 [1816-17] (Ascomycetes, Hypocreales)

= Sphaeria baccata Wallr. 1833  Note: From Gibberella baccata - According to Leslie and Summerell (2006), the taxonomy of this species is in flux as it is recognized as a species complex. Since the nomenclature is unsettled, we have taken a conservative approach in the listing of synonyms.

    ≡ Gibberella baccata (Wallr.) Sacc. 1878

    ≡ Gibbera baccata (Wallr.) Fuckel 1870

    ≡ Gibberella pulicaris subsp. baccata (Wallr.) Sacc. 1878

Notes: According to Leslie and Summerell (2006), the taxonomy of this species is in flux as it is recognized as a species complex. Since the nomenclature is unsettled, we have taken a conservative approach in the listing of synonyms.

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Frequently on twigs and wood, also leaves.

Disease Note: Blight, canker, dieback, stem rot.

Host: Multiple genera in multiple families.
