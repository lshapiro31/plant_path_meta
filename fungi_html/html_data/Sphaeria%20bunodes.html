Nomenclature data for Sphaeria bunodes

Rosellinia bunodes (Berk. & Broome) Sacc. 1882 (Ascomycetes, Xylariales)

≡ Sphaeria bunodes Berk. & Broome 1875

≡Hypoxylon bunodes (Berk. & Broome) P.M.D. Martin 1976

= Rosellinia echinata Massee 1901

= Hypoxylon goliath Speg. 1889

    ≡ Rosellinia goliath (Speg.) Höhn. 1907

= Rosellinia zingiberis F. Stevens & Atienza 1931

Distribution: Widespread in subtropical to tropical regions.

Substrate: Stems, roots.

Disease Note: Root rot.

Host: Multiple genera in multiple families.
