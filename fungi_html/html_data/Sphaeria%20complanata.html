Nomenclature data for Sphaeria complanata

Calophoma complanata (Tode : Fr.) Qian Chen & L. Cai 2015 (, )

≡ Sphaeria complanata Tode : Fr. 1791

≡Phoma complanata (Tode : Fr.) Desm. 1881

≡Sclerophomella complanata (Tode : Fr.) Höhn 1918

= Phoma anethicola Allesch. 1898

     Phoma herbarum var. anethi Westend. 1874

= Phoma punctoidea P. Karst. 1905

= Pyrenochaeta rivini Allesch. 1897

    ≡ Sclerochaetella rivini (Allesch.) Höhn. 1918

    ≡ Diploplenodomus rivini (Allesch.) Petr. 1944

Notes: From Plenodomus complanatus - Manuscript name in herbarium, invalid name. From Sclerophomella complanata - Type of Phoma sect. Sclerophomella (Höhn) Boerema et al. 1997

Distribution: North America, Europe, Asia.

Substrate: Dead stems, roots, leaves, seed-borne.

Disease Note: Leaf and root canker, leaf spot.

Host: Apiaceae. Reports on other host plants are misidentifications.
