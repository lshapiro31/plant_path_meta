Nomenclature data for Sphaeria cyanogena

Fusarium sulphureum Schltdl. 1824 (Ascomycetes, Hypocreales)

≡Fusarium discolor var. sulphureum (Schltdl.) Appel & Wollenw.

= Sphaeria cyanogena Desm. 1848  Note: From Gibberella cyanogena - The name Gibberella saubinetii (= G. cyanogena) has frequently been misapplied to the teleomorph of Fusarium graminearum, which is instead Gibberella zeae (Booth 1971).

    ≡ Gibberella cyanogena (Desm.) Sacc. 1883

= Gibbera saubinetii Mont. 1856

    ≡ Gibberella saubinetii (Mont.) Sacc. 1879

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Stems, seeds, roots.

Disease Note: Stem rot, potato tuber dry rot.

Host: Numerous hosts; commonly Poaceae.

Supporting Literature:
