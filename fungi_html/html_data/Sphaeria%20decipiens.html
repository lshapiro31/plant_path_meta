= Cytospora decipiens Sacc. 1884

Notes: Lopadostoma decipiens originally published illegitimately by the same author without reference to the basionym in 1969 (Art. 41.3).

Distribution: Europe, North America (Canada, USA: MN).

Substrate: Bark.

Disease Note: Canker; hornbeam blight. Also saprobic.

Host: Carpinus betulus (Betulaceae); found on other hardwoods as a saprobe.
