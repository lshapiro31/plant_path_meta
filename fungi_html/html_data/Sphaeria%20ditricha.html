Nomenclature data for Sphaeria ditricha

Venturia ditricha (Fr. : Fr.) P. Karst. 1873 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

≡ Sphaeria ditricha Fr. : Fr. 1826

= Asteroma betulae Roberge ex Desm. 1843

= Fusicladium betulae Aderh. 1897

= Venturia maculans Peck 1876

Notes: From Fusicladium betulae - Type lost; Schubert 2003 has proposed a neotype.

Distribution: Temperate North America; Europe.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Betula spp. (Betulaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Schubert, K., Ritschel, A., and Braun, U. 2003. A monograph of Fusicladium s. lat. (Hyphomycetes). Schlechtendalia 9: 1-132.
