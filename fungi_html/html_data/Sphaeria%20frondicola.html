Nomenclature data for Sphaeria frondicola

Sphaerulina frondicola (Fr.) Verkley, Quaedvlieg & Crous 2013 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Septoria populi Desm. 1843

≡Sphaeria frondicola (Fr.) Fr. 1822

= Sphaeria populi Auersw. 1869

    ≡ Mycosphaerella populi (Auersw.) J. Schröt. 1894

Notes: Septoria populi Desm. is a nom nov. replacing Depazia frondicola Fr..

Distribution: From Septoria populi - Temperate North America; Europe.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Populus spp. (Salicaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Verkley, G.J.M., Quaedvlieg, W., Shin, H.-D., and Crous, P.W. 2013. A new approach to species delimitation in Septoria. Stud. Mycol. 75: 213-305.
