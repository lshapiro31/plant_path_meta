Notes: Tanaka et al. (Persoonia 26: 85, 2012) resolves the phylogeny of this species placing the type of Sporocadus in Seimatosporium.

Distribution: Europe, North America (USA), Asia (China, India), Africa (Ethiopia, South Africa), Australia, New Zealand.

Substrate: Twigs, branches, leaves, fruit.

Disease Note: Leaf spot, post-harvest fruit rot.

Host: Woody trees and shrubs of multiple plant families.
