Notes: From Physalospora ilicella - Author erroneously cited by van der Aa as Tengwall.

Distribution: North America, South America (Brazil), Asia, Europe, Australia.

Substrate: Living, withering, fallen, or dead leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Various plant families, including Taxus (Taxaceae), Hedera (Araliaceae), Rhododendron (Ericaceae).
