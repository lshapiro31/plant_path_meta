Nomenclature data for Sphaeria laburni

Camarosporidiella laburni (Pers. : Fr.) Wanas., Bulgakov, Camporesi & K.D. Hyde 2017 (, )

≡ Sphaeria laburni Pers. : Fr. 1796

≡Cucurbitaria laburni (Pers. : Fr.) De Not. 1862

≡Gibberidea laburni (Pers. : Fr.) Kuntze 1898

= Camarosporium laburni Sacc. & Roum. 1892

Distribution: Eastern United States; Europe.

Substrate: On dead limbs and twigs.

Disease Note: Causes dieback and canker; also saprobic.

Host: On woody Fabaceae.

Supporting Literature:
