Nomenclature data for Sphaeria marginata

Biscogniauxia marginata (Fr. : Fr.) Pouzar 1979 (Ascomycetes, Xylariales)

≡ Sphaeria marginata Fr. : Fr. 1828

≡Nummulariella marginata (Fr. : Fr.) Eckblad & Granmo 1978

= Sphaeria discreta Schwein. 1832

    ≡ Nummularia discreta (Schwein.) Tul. & C. Tul. 1863

    ≡ Diatrype discreta (Schwein.) M.A. Curtis 1867

    ≡ Hypoxylon discretum (Schwein.) Berk. 1876

    ≡ Biscogniauxia discreta (Schwein.) Kuntze 1891

    ≡ Numulariola discreta (Schwein.) House 1925

= Sphaeria excavata Schwein. 1832

    ≡ Nummularia excavata (Schwein.) Ellis & Everh. 1889

= Nummularia peziziformis C.G. Lloyd 1924

Notes: Sphaeria marginata Sowerby 1803 unpriorable because S. marginata Fr. is sanctioned. Non Sphaeria marginata Schwein. 1832 and Sphaeria marginata Wallr. 1833, both illegitimate per ICN Article 53.1.

Distribution: Eastern North America.

Substrate: Branches, limbs; wood.

Disease Note: Nail-head or blister disease; canker.

Host: Mostly Rosaceae; reports from several other families.
