Nomenclature data for Sphaeria maydis

Stenocarpella maydis (Berk.) B. Sutton 1980 (Ascomycetes, Incertae sedis)

≡ Sphaeria maydis Berk. 1847

≡Diplodia maydis (Berk.) Sacc. 1884

= Diplodia maydicola Speg. 1910

= Diplodia zeae Lév. 1848

    ≡ Macrodiplodia zeae (Lév.) Petr. & Syd. 1923

    ≡ Sphaeria zeae Schwein. 1832  Note: Léveillé appears to have renamed Sphaeria zeae sensu Schwein 1832, which was misapplied. Subsequent authors treated the Schweinitz name as the basionym, but may have been unaware of certain nomenclatural problems.

    ≡ Hendersonia zeae (Lév.) Hazsl. 1872

    ≡ Phaeostagonosporopsis zeae (Lév.) Woron. 1925

= Diplodia zeae-maydis Mekht. 1962

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Stalks, ears, seedlings, roots.

Disease Note: Stalk rot, white ear rot, and seedling blight of maize.

Host: Zea mays (Poaceae) and a few reports from other grasses.
