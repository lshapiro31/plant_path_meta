= Sphaeria melantera Peck 1878 [1875]

Notes: ITS and morphology similar to Coniothyrium sporulosum (Muthumeenakshi 2001, see also Verkley et al. 2004). The oldest epithet not in correct genus. Cannot determine correct genus. Kalmusia coniothyrium and Paraconiothyrium fuckelii are both based on Sphaeria coniothyrium.

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Canes (stems).

Disease Note: Cane and/or stem blight and canker. Wound parasite. Air, soil, and water-borne.

Host: Rubus spp. Rosa spp., and other Rosaceae. Wide range of hosts as a wound parasite.
