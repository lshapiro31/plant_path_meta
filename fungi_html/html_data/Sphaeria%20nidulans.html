Nomenclature data for Sphaeria nidulans

Meliola nidulans (Schwein.) Cooke 1882 (Ascomycetes, Meliolales)

≡ Sphaeria nidulans Schwein. 1822

≡Chaetosphaeria nidulans (Schwein.) Rehm 1875

Notes: Reports of this species on Vaccinium may be M. niessleana Winter (Hansford. Beih. Sydowia 2:478. 1961).

Distribution: Southeastern United States.

Substrate: Epiphytic on leaves, stems, branches.

Disease Note: Black mildew.

Host: Cornus spp. (Cornaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Hongsanan, S., Tian, Q., Persoh, D., Zeng, X.-Y., Hyde, K.D., Chomnunti, P., Boonmee, S., Bahkali, A.H., and Wen, T.-C. 2015. Meliolales. Fung. Diversity 74: 91-141.
