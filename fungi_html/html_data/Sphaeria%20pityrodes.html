Nomenclature data for Sphaeria pityrodes

Clonostachys pityrodes Schroers 2001 (Ascomycetes, Hypocreales)

= Nectria gallifera Pat. 1913

= Nectria juruensis Henn. 1904

= Nectria macrospora Starbäck 1899

= Creonectria macrosporicola Chardón 1939

= Sphaeria pityrodes Mont. 1842

    ≡ Nectria pityrodes (Mont.) Mont. 1856

    ≡ Bionectria pityrodes (Mont.) Schroers 2001

    ≡ Cucurbitaria pityrodes (Mont.) Kuntze 1898

= Nectria pityrodes var. saccharina Berk. & Broome 1873

Distribution: Widespread especiallyin tropical regions.

Substrate: On newly fallen woody plants.

Disease Note: Endophytic.

Host: On diverse hardwoods.

Supporting Literature:

Samuels, G.J., and et al. 1990. Contributions toward a mycobiota of Indonesia: Hypocreales, synnematous Hyphomycetes, Aphyllophorales, Phragmobasidiomycetes, and Myxomycetes. Mem. New York Bot. Gard. 59: 1-180.
