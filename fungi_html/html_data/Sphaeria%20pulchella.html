Nomenclature data for Sphaeria pulchella

Calosphaeria pulchella (Pers. : Fr.) J. Schröt. 1897 (Ascomycetes, Calosphaeriales)

≡ Sphaeria pulchella Pers. : Fr. 1794

≡Valsa pulchella (Pers. : Fr.) Fr. 1849

≡Circinostoma pulchellum (Pers. : Fr.) Gray 1821

≡Cryptosphaeria pulchellum (Pers. : Fr.) Grev. 1824

= Calosphaeria princeps Tul. & C. Tul. 1863

= Calosphaeriophora pulchella Réblová, L. Mostert, W. Gams & Crous 2004

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: On limbs.

Disease Note: Dieback.

Host: Reports from Rosaceae, especially Prunus spp.

Supporting Literature:

Berbegal, M., Garcia-Jimenez, J., and Armengol, J. 2014. First report of Calosphaeria pulchella causing canker and branch dieback of sweet cherry trees in Spain. Pl. Dis. 98(7): 1008.
