Nomenclature data for Sphaeria pulicaris

Fusarium sambucinum Fuckel 1869 (Ascomycetes, Hypocreales)

= Sphaeria pulicaris Fr. : Fr. 1823

    ≡ Gibberella pulicaris (Fr. : Fr.) Sacc. 1877

Notes: Wollenweber & Reinking (1935) list additional synonyms.

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Bark, dead wood, roots, potatoes in storage.

Disease Note: Canker, dieback, root rot, storage rot of potatoes.

Host: Various plant families.
