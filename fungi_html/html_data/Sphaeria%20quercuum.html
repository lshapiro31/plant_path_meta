Nomenclature data for Sphaeria quercuum

Botryosphaeria quercuum (Schwein. : Fr.) Sacc. 1882 (Ascomycetes, Dothideales)

≡ Sphaeria quercuum Schwein. : Fr. 1822

≡Melanops quercuum (Schwein. : Fr.) Rehm 1919

≡Melogramma quercuum (Schwein. : Fr.) Berk. 1876

Notes: The taxonomic history of this organism has taken many turns. Von Arx and Mueller (1954) provided an extensive list of synonyms that Barr (1972) added to. This list was based on similarity of the teleomorphs and these similarities are now recognized to not follow the diversity of species in this group (Sivanesan 1984; Alves et al. 2004). Currently, a modern account of the synonymy is lacking and historical records, host ranges, and distributional records should be viewed with appropriate caution.

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: On woody plant parts and leaves.

Disease Note: Canker, dieback, leaf spot.

Host: Multiple genera in multiple families, especially Quercus (Fagaceae).
