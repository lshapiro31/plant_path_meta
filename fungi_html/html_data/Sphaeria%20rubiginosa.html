Nomenclature data for Sphaeria rubiginosa

Hypoxylon rubiginosum (Pers.) Fr. 1849 (Ascomycetes, Xylariales)

≡ Sphaeria rubiginosa Pers. 1801

= Hypoxylon botrys Nitschke 1867

Notes: See Index Fungorum for additional synonyms.

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Trunks and branches, decayed wood.

Disease Note: A mild pathogen on most hosts, occasionally causing cankers; more pathogenic to Hevea, causing a "traumatic disease".

Host: Numerous hosts.

Supporting Literature:

Hawksworth, D.L. 1972. Hypoxylon rubiginosum. C.M.I. Descr. Pathog. Fungi Bact. 357: 1-2.

Hsieh, H.-M, Yu, Y.-M., and Rogers, J.D. 2005. Molecular phylogeny of Hypoxylon and closely related genera. Mycologia 97: 844-865.
