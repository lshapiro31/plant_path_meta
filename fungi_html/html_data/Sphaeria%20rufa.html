Nomenclature data for Sphaeria rufa

Trichoderma viride Pers. : Fr. 1794 (Ascomycetes, Hypocreales)

= Trichoderma glaucum E.V. Abbott 1927

= Trichoderma lignorum (Tode) Harz 1871

= Sphaeria rufa Pers. : Fr. 1796  Note: From Hypocrea rufa - Most reports of T. viride refer to T. asperellum or Eidamia viridescens (Samuels et al. 2006).

    ≡ Hypocrea rufa (Pers. : Fr.) Fr. 1849

Notes: Most reports of T. viride refer to T. asperellum or Eidamia viridescens (Samuels et al. 2006).

Distribution: North America, Europe, uncommon. Many reports refer to other spp. of Trichoderma (Samuels et al. 2006).

Substrate: Wood, roots, fruit, seeds.

Disease Note: Dieback, root rot, post harvest fruit rot.

Host: Well known as causing post harvest rot of lemons; Pinus nigra (Pinaceae); records on other hosts unclear.
