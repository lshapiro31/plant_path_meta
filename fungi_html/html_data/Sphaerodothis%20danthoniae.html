Nomenclature data for Sphaerodothis danthoniae

Sphaerodothella danthoniae (McAlpine) C.A. Pearce & K.D. Hyde 2001 (Ascomycetes, Phyllachorales)

≡ Anthostomella danthoniae McAlpine 1902

≡Sphaerodothis danthoniae (McAlpine) J. Walker & S.M. Francis 1977

= Sphaerodothis calospora Syd. 1924

= Sphaerodothis magnifica Petr. 1947

Distribution: Oceania.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Danthonia spp. (Poaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Pearce, C.A., and Hyde, K.D. 2001. Two new genera in the Phyllachoraceae: Sphaerodothella to accommodate Sphaerodothis danthoniae, and Parbeya gen. nov. Fung. Diversity 6: 83-97.
