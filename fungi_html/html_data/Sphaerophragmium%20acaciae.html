Nomenclature data for Sphaerophragmium acaciae

Sphaerophragmium acaciae (Cooke) Magnus 1891 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

≡ Triphragmium acaciae Cooke 1880

= Sphaerophragmium luzonicum H.S. Yates 1918

Distribution: North America (USA (FL), Mexico), South America (Brazil), Africa, Australia, Asia.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Rust.

Host: Albizia lebbek (=Acacia lebbek) (Fabaceae).
