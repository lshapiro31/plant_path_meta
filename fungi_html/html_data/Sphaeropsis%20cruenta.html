Notes: Type of Phyllosticta.

Distribution: Europe, Asia. Reports in North America could not be confirmed; probably based on misidentifications (Bissett 1979).

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Polygonatum spp., Convallaria (Convallariaceae), Maianthemum (Asparagaceae).
