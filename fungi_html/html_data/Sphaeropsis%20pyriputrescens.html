Nomenclature data for Sphaeropsis pyriputrescens

Sphaeropsis pyriputrescens C.L. Xiao & J.D. Rogers 2004 (Ascomycetes, Dothideales)

Distribution: North America (USA: WA).

Substrate: Fruit.

Disease Note: Stem-end rot, calyx-end rot, wound-associated rot.

Host: Malus domesticus, M. sylvestris, Pyrus communis (Rosaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Sikdar, P., Okubara, P., Mazzola, M., and Xiao, C.L. 2014. Development of PCR Assays for Diagnosis and Detectin of the Pathogens Phacidiopycnis washingtonensis and Sphaeropsis pyriputrescens in Apple Fruit. Pl. Dis. 98: 241-246.

Xiao, C.L., Kim, Y.K., and Boal, R.J. 2014. Sources and Availability of Inoculum and Seasonal Survival of Sphaeropsis pyriputrescens in Apple Orchards. Pl. Dis. 98: 1043-1049.
