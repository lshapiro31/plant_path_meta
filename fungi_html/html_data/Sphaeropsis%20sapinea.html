Nomenclature data for Sphaeropsis sapinea

Diplodia sapinea (Fr.) Fuckel 1870 (Ascomycetes, Dothideales)

≡ Sphaeria sapinea Fr. : Fr. 1823

≡Macrophoma sapinea (Fr. : Fr.) Petr. 1962 [1961]

≡Sphaeropsis sapinea (Fr. : Fr.) Dyko & B. Sutton 1980

= Sphaeria pinea Desm. 1842

    ≡ Diplodia pinea (Desm.) J. Kickx fil. 1867

    ≡ Macrophoma pinea (Desm.) Syd. & Petr. 1926

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Stems, twigs, and needles.

Disease Note: Twig blight, canker, collar rot, root rot.

Host: Cupressaceae, Pinaceae, Corylus (Betulaceae).
