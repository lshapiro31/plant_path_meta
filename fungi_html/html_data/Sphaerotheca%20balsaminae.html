Nomenclature data for Sphaerotheca balsaminae

Podosphaera balsaminae (Kari ex U. Braun) U. Braun & S. Takam. 2000 (Ascomycetes, Erysiphales)

≡ Sphaerotheca balsaminae Kari ex U. Braun 1995

Notes: Braun provided the first valid publication of the name Sphaerotheca balsaminae.

Distribution: Asia, Europe.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Powdery mildew.

Host: Impatiens noli-tangere (Balsaminaceae); records from other Impatiens are referred to other species in the P. xanthii complex.

Supporting Literature:

Braun, U., and Takamatsu, S. 2000. Phylogeny of Erysiphe, Microsphaera, Uncinula (Erysipheae) and Cystotheca, Podosphaera, Sphaerotheca (Cystotheceae) inferred from rDNA ITS sequences - some taxonomic consequences. Schlechtendalia 4: 1-33.

Braun, U., and Cook, R.T.A. 2012. Taxonomy Manual of the Erysiphales (Powdery Mildews). CBS Biodivers. Ser. 11: 703.
