Nomenclature data for Sphaerotheca erigerontis-canadensis

Podosphaera erigerontis-canadensis (Lev.) U. Braun & T.Z. Liu 2010 (Ascomycetes, Erysiphales)

≡ Erysiphe erigerontis-canadensis Lév. 1843

      Sphaerotheca erigerontis-canadensis (Lév.) L. Junell 1966

= Sphaerotheca detonsa J. Kickx. f. 1867

= Sphaerotheca erigerontis Oudem. 1897

Distribution: Europe, also known from Asia and the Middle East.

Substrate: Living leaves, stems.

Disease Note: Powdery mildew.

Host: Asteraceae.
