Nomenclature data for Sphaerotheca euphorbiae

Podosphaera euphorbiae (Castagne) U. Braun & S. Takam. 2000 (Ascomycetes, Erysiphales)

≡ Botrytis euphorbiae Castagne 1851

≡Ramularia euphorbiae (Castagne) Sacc. 1892

≡Sphaerotheca euphorbiae (Castagne) E.S. Salmon 1902

= Oidium cyparissiae var. euphorbiae-preslii Massal. 1902

= Erysiphe tomentosa G.H. Otth 1866

    ≡ Sphaerotheca tomentosa (G.H. Otth) Sacc. 1896

Distribution: Cosmopolitan (mostly Asia and Europe). Not reported from USA.

Substrate: Living leaves, stems.

Disease Note: Powdery mildew.

Host: Euphorbia, Mallotus, Pedilanthus and various other genera in the Euphorbiaceae.

Supporting Literature:

Braun, U., and Takamatsu, S. 2000. Phylogeny of Erysiphe, Microsphaera, Uncinula (Erysipheae) and Cystotheca, Podosphaera, Sphaerotheca (Cystotheceae) inferred from rDNA ITS sequences - some taxonomic consequences. Schlechtendalia 4: 1-33.

Braun, U., and Cook, R.T.A. 2012. Taxonomy Manual of the Erysiphales (Powdery Mildews). CBS Biodivers. Ser. 11: 703.
