Nomenclature data for Sphaerotheca fugax

Podosphaera fugax (Penz. & Sacc.) U. Braun & S. Takam. 2000 (Ascomycetes, Erysiphales)

≡ Sphaerotheca fugax Penz. & Sacc. 1884

≡Albigo fugax (Penz. & Sacc.) Kuntze 1898

Distribution: Cosmopolitan, widespread in Asian and Europe. Not reported from USA.

Substrate: Living leaves, stems.

Disease Note: Powdery mildew.

Host: Erodium moschatum, Geranium spp., Pelargonium spp. (Geraniaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Braun, U., and Takamatsu, S. 2000. Phylogeny of Erysiphe, Microsphaera, Uncinula (Erysipheae) and Cystotheca, Podosphaera, Sphaerotheca (Cystotheceae) inferred from rDNA ITS sequences - some taxonomic consequences. Schlechtendalia 4: 1-33.
