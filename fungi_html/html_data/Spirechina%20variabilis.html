Nomenclature data for Spirechina variabilis

Gerwasia variabilis (Mayor) Buriticá 1994 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

≡ Uromyces variabilis Mayor 1913

≡Mainsia variabilis (Mayor) H. Jacks. & Holw. 1931

≡Morispora variabilis (Mayor) Salazar-Yepes, Pardo-Card. & Buriticá 2007  Note: Introduced as new anamorph name. Valid only as new combination based on holomorph description. See ICBN Art. 59.

≡Spirechina variabilis (Mayor) Dietel 1928

Distribution: South America.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Rust.

Host: Rubus spp. (Rosaceae).

Supporting Literature:
