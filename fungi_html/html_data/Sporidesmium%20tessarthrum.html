Nomenclature data for Sporidesmium tessarthrum

Spegazzinia tessarthra (Berk. & M.A. Curtis) Sacc. 1886 (Ascomycetes, Incertae sedis)

≡ Sporidesmium tessarthrum Berk. & M.A. Curtis 1868

= Spegazzinia ornata Sacc. 1880

Notes: Saprobic on plant debris and in soil. Cole (Canad. J. Bot. 52:1259. 1974) studied the conidiophore and conidium ontogeny of this species. Non R. desmazieri Sacc. 1878.

Distribution: Warm-temperate to tropical regions.

Substrate: Living and rotting leaves and stalks.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Primarily on monocotyledonous plants.

Supporting Literature:

Petrini 2013. Rosellinia - a world monograph. Biblioth. Mycol. 205: 410.
