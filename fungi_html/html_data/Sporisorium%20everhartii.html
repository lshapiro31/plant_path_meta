Nomenclature data for Sporisorium everhartii

Anthracocystis everhartii (Ellis & Galloway) McTaggart & R.G. Shivas 2012 (, )

≡ Sorosporium everhartii Ellis & Galloway 1890

≡Sporisorium everhartii (Ellis & Galloway) M. Piepenbr. 1999

≡Tolyposporium everhartii (Ellis & Galloway) Dietel 1897

Distribution: North America, Africa (Guinea), Caribbean (Cuba).

Substrate: Spikelets.

Disease Note: Smut.

Host: Andropogon spp., Hyparrhenia spp. and other related Poaceae.
