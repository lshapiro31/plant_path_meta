Nomenclature data for Sporisorium linderi

Sporisorium linderi (Zundel) Vánky 1999 (Ustilaginomycetes, Ustilaginales)

≡ Sphacelotheca linderi Zundel 1944

    ≡ Sorosporium inconspicuum (Pole-Evans) Zundel 1938  Note: No Latin. Nom. inval.

= Sporisorium pole-evansii Vánky 1999

Notes: Ustilago inconspicua Pole-Evans was never formally published. Its recombination in Sporisorium by Zundel is invalid.

Distribution: Africa.

Substrate: Living inflorescences.

Disease Note: Smut.

Host: Digitaria (Poaceae).

Supporting Literature:
