Nomenclature data for Sporotrichum poae

Fusarium poae (Peck) Wollenw. 1913 (Ascomycetes, Hypocreales)

≡ Sporotrichum poae Peck 1902

≡Fusarium tricinctum f. sp. poae (Peck) W.C. Snyder & H.N. Hansen 1945

= Sporotrichum anthophilum Peck 1906

Distribution: Temperate regions.

Substrate: Roots, seeds.

Disease Note: Seed rot, root rot.

Host: Poaceae spp., Glycine max (Fabaceae) and many grass hosts (Poaceae); report from Solanum lycopersicum (tomato: Solanaceae).
