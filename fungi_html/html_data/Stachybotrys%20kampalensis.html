Nomenclature data for Stachybotrys kampalensis

Stachybotrys kampalensis Hansf. 1943 (Ascomycetes, Incertae sedis)

Distribution: Widespread, especially subtropical to tropical regions.

Substrate: Dead stems.

Disease Note: Saprobic.

Host: Multiple genera in multiple families.

Supporting Literature:

Ellis, M.B. 1971. Dematiaceous Hyphomycetes. Commonwealth Mycological Institute, Kew, Surrey, England, 608 pages.
