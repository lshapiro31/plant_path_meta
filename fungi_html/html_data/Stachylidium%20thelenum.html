Nomenclature data for Stachylidium thelenum

Rosellinia thelena (Fr.) Rabenh. 1865 (Ascomycetes, Xylariales)

≡ Sphaeria thelena Fr. 1823

≡Byssosphaeria thelena (Fr. : Fr.) Cooke 1887

≡Hypoxylon thelenum (Fr.: Fr.) P.M.D. Martin 1968

= Rosellinia pulcherrima Ellis & Everh. 1893

= Stachylidium thelenum Sacc. 1877

    ≡ Nodulisporium thelenum (Sacc.) G. Sm. 1951

Notes: Reported to sporulate on roots, this organism has been isolated as an endophyte by Petrini & Petrini (Sydowia 38:230. 1985). Pathogenic on oak seedlings, present but not pathogenic on Pinus.

Distribution: Europe, North America, New Zealand.

Substrate: Twigs and needles of coniferous wood; root collar of hardwood.

Disease Note: Death.

Host: Quercus (Fagaceae), Pinus

Supporting Literature:

Petrini 2013. Rosellinia - a world monograph. Biblioth. Mycol. 205: 410.
