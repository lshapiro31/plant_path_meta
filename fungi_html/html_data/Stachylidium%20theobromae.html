Nomenclature data for Stachylidium theobromae

Musicillium theobromae (Turconi) Zare & W. Gams 2007 (, )

≡ Stachylidium theobromae Turconi 1920

≡Verticillium theobromae (Turconi) E.W. Mason & S. Hughes 1951

Distribution: Widespread in tropical regions.

Substrate: Fruit.

Disease Note: Cigar-end rot of banana.

Host: Primarily Musa spp. (Musaceae). Also reports from a few other genera in few families.
