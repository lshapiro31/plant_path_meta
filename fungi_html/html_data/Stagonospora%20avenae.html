Nomenclature data for Stagonospora avenae

Parastagonospora avenae (A.B. Frank) Quaedvlieg, Verkley & Crous 2013 (, )

≡ Septoria avenae A.B. Frank 1895

≡Stagonospora avenae (A.B. Frank) Bissett 1982

= Leptosphaeria avenaria G.F. Weber 1922  Note: Two different pathogenic forms have been distinguished (Boerema et al. Neth. J. Pl. Pathol. 98(1):16. 1992).

    ≡ Phaeosphaeria avenaria (G.F. Weber) O.E. Erikss. 1967

= Phaeosphaeria avenaria f. sp. avenaria O.E. Erikss. 1967

= Pleospora tritici Garov. 1874  Note: From Phaeosphaeria tritici - Leuchtmann (Sydowia 37:105. 1984). Hendersonia anamorph.

    ≡ Phaeosphaeria tritici (Garov.) Hedjaroude 1968

    ≡ Leptosphaeria tritici (Garov.) Pass. 1878

Notes: Oldest epithet for this species is Pleospora tritici Garov. (1874); "avenae" well established in literature and proposed for conservation by Quaedvlieg et al. (2013).

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Avena spp., Echinochloa spp., Glyceria spp., Triticum aestivum, T. vulgare and other Poaceae.
