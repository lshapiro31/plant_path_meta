Nomenclature data for Stagonospora chenopodii

Heterospora dimorphospora (Speg.) Gruyter, Aveskamp & Verkley 2012 (, )

≡ Phyllosticta dimorphospora Speg. 1910

≡Phoma dimorphospora (Speg.) Aa & Kesteren 1979

= Stagonospora chenopodii Peck 1887

Notes: From Stagonospora chenopodii - Year changed from 1920.

Distribution: From Phoma dimorphospora - North America, South America. From Stagonospora chenopodii - ND.

Substrate: From Phoma dimorphospora - Leaves and stems.

Disease Note: From Phoma dimorphospora - Leaf and stem spot.

Host: From Phoma dimorphospora - Chenopodium spp. (Chenopodiaceae).
