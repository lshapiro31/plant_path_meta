    ≡ Phyllosticta oudemansii Sacc. & P. Syd. 1902

= Phyllosticta narcissi Aderh. 1900

    ≡ Phoma narcissi (Aderh.) Boerema, Gruyter & Noordel. 1993

= Stagonospora narcissi Hollós 1906

Notes: From Stagonospora crini - From host genus Crinus; genitive crini is correct (Art. 60.12). From Phyllosticta narcissi - May 1900 not Oudem. Aug. 1900. From Phyllosticta narcissi - Aug. 1900 not Aderh. May 1900. Phyllosticta oudemansii is a nom. nov. (non Phyllosticta narcissi Oudem. 1901.

Distribution: From Peyronellaea curtisii - Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: From Peyronellaea curtisii - Leaves.

Disease Note: From Peyronellaea curtisii - Leaf scorch, neck rot, red spot disease, red leaf spot.

Host: From Peyronellaea curtisii - Narcissus spp., Hippeastrum spp., and other Amaryllidaceae.
