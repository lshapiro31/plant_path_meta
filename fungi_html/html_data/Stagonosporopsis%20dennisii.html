Nomenclature data for Stagonosporopsis dennisii

Stagonosporopsis dennisii Boerema, Gruyter & Noordel. 1997 (Ascomycetes, Incertae sedis)

= Phoma dennisii Boerema 1976

    ≡ Phoma dennisii var. dennisii Boerema 1997  Note: Stagonosporopsis dennisii is a synanamorph of Phoma dennisii var. dennisii.

    ≡ Phoma oleracea var. solidaginis Sacc. 1884

Notes: Stagonosporopsis dennisii is a synanamorph of Phoma dennisii var. dennisii.

Distribution: Europe, North America.

Substrate: Dead stems.

Disease Note: No data on pathogenicity.

Host: Solidago spp. (Asteraceae).
