Nomenclature data for Stegocintractia hyperborea

Stegocintractia hyperborea (A. Blytt) M. Piepenbr. 2000 (Ustilaginomycetes, Ustilaginales)

≡ Ustilago hyperborea A. Blytt 1896

≡Cintractia dovrensis Jørst. 1953  Note: Nom. nov.

[≡Cintractia hyperborea (A. Blytt) Liro 1935 - illegitimate later homonym, not included in search]  Note: Not Cif. 1931. Nom. illeg.

Distribution: Widespread in northern hemisphere.

Disease Note: Smut.

Host: Luzula (Juncaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Vanky, K. 2012. Smut Fungi of the World. APS Press, St. Paul, Minnesota, 1458 pages.
