Nomenclature data for Stegocintractia luzulae

Stegocintractia luzulae (Sacc.) M. Piepenbr., Begerow & Oberw. 1999 (Ustilaginomycetes, Ustilaginales)

≡ Ustilago luzulae Sacc. 1873

≡Cintractia luzulae (Sacc.) G.P. Clinton 1902

Distribution: Northern hemisphere.

Substrate: Spikelets.

Disease Note: Smut.

Host: Luzula (Poaceae).
