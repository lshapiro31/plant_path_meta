Nomenclature data for Stegonsporium pyriforme

Stegonsporium pyriforme (Hoffm. : Fr.) Corda 1839 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

    Variant spelling Stegonsporium piriforme (Hoffm. : Fr.) Corda

≡ Stilbospora pyriformis Hoffm. : Fr. 1795

      Stilbospora piriformis Hoffm. : Fr. 1795

= Stilbospora ovata Pers. 1796

    ≡ Stegonsporium ovatum (Pers.) S. Hughes 1958

= Prosthecium pyriforme Jaklitsch & Voglmayr 2008

Notes: Authorship of Stagonosporium pyriforme mistakenly given as (Hoffm.) ex Corda in Shoemaker (1977).

Distribution: Eastern North America, Europe.

Substrate: Dead, corticated branches.

Disease Note: Dieback.

Host: Acer pseudoplatanus (Sapindaceae).
