Nomenclature data for Stegophora ulmea

Stegophora ulmea (Schwein. : Fr.) Syd. & P. Syd. 1916 (Ascomycetes, Diaporthales)

≡ Sphaeria ulmea Schwein. : Fr. 1823

≡Dothidella ulmea (Schwein. : Fr.) Ellis & Everh. 1892

≡Gnomonia ulmea (Schwein. : Fr.) Thüm. 1878

= Gloeosporium ulmeum Miles 1921

    ≡ Asteroma ulmeum (Miles) B. Sutton 1980

    ≡ Cylindrosporella ulmea (Miles) Arx 1957

Distribution: North America; report from Asia (India).

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Anthracnose, black leaf spot.

Host: Ulmus (Ulmaceae); the report on Ostrya is erroneous.
