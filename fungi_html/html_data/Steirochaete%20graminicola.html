Nomenclature data for Steirochaete graminicola

Colletotrichum graminicola (Ces.) G.W. Wilson 1914 (Ascomycetes, Phyllachorales)

≡ Dicladium graminicola Ces. 1852

≡Colletotrichopsis graminicola (Ces.) Munt.-Cvetk. 1952

≡Steirochaete graminicola (Ces.) Sacc. 1886

= Glomerella graminicola D.J. Politis 1975

= Vermicularia graminicola Westend. 1861  Note: Publication not confirmed.

Notes: Von Arx (1957) listed numerous synonyms. Subsequent workers such as Sutton (1980) have limited this species to those isolates found on Zea.

Distribution: Probably cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Leaves, stems.

Disease Note: Leaf spot, stalk rot, etc.

Host: Zea mays (Poaceae).
