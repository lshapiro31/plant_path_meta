Nomenclature data for Stemphylium solani

Stemphylium solani G.F. Weber 1930 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

≡Thyrospora solani (Weber) Sawada 1931

Notes: Thyrospora solani incorrectly given as a taxonomic synonym of Stemphylium lycopersici and with incorrect authorship by M.B. Ellis (1975).

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot, yellowing, defoliation, reduced fruit yield.

Host: Primarily Solanaceae; reports from Allium (Amaryllidaceae), Lactuca (Asteraceae), Gossypium hirsutum (Malvaceae) and numerous other plant families.
