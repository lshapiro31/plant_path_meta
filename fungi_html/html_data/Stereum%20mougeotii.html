Nomenclature data for Stereum mougeotii

Hymenochaete mougeotii (Fr.) Cooke 1890 (Basidiomycetes, Hymenochaetales)

≡ Thelephora mougeotii Fr. 1828

≡Corticium mougeotii (Fr.) Fr. 1838

≡Merulius mougeotii (Fr.) Pat. 1890

≡Stereum mougeotii (Fr.) Quél. 1854

Distribution: Temperate regions.

Substrate: Wood.

Disease Note: Saprobic and not conclusively pathogenic; related species reported to cause occasional heart rot of living trees.

Host: Quercus (Fagaceae), Rhododendron (Ericaceae) and numerous other hard and soft woods.
