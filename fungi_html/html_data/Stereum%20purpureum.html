Nomenclature data for Stereum purpureum

Chondrostereum purpureum (Pers. : Fr.) Pouzar 1959 (Basidiomycetes, Polyporales)

≡ Stereum purpureum Pers. : Fr. 1794

= Stereum rugosiusculum Berk. & M.A. Curtis 1873

Notes: Many more synonyms exist.

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Wood.

Disease Note: Silver-leaf disease, wood rot.

Host: Multiple genera in multiple families.
