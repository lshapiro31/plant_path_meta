Stereum complicatum (Fr. : Fr.) Fr. 1838 (Basidiomycetes, Russulales)

≡ Thelephora complicata Fr. : Fr. 1828

= Thelephora ramealis Schwein. 1822

  ≡Stereum rameale (Schwein.) Burt 1890

Distribution: Asia, Europe, North America.

Substrate: Dead angiosperm wood.

Disease Note: Saprobic; also a wound pathogen causing white heart rot.

Host: Numerous hosts.
