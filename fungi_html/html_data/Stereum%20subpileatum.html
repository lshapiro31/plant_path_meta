Nomenclature data for Stereum subpileatum

Xylobolus subpileatus (Berk. & M.A. Curtis) Boidin 1958 (Basidiomycetes, Russulales)

≡ Stereum subpileatum Berk. & M.A. Curtis 1849

≡Stereum frustulatum var. subpileatum (Berk. & M.A. Curtis) A.L. Welden 1975

= Stereum insigne Bres. 1891

Distribution: Warmer parts of eastern North America; Central and South America; Europe; Asia.

Substrate: Living and dead wood.

Disease Note: Heart rot.

Host: Quercus spp. (Fagaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Lentz, P.L. 1955. Stereum and allied genera of fungi in the Upper Mississippi Valley. Agric. Monogr. U.S.D.A. 24: 1-74.
