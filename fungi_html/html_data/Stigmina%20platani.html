Nomenclature data for Stigmina platani

Stigmina platani (Fuckel) Sacc. 1880 (Ascomycetes, Dothideales)

≡ Stigmella platani Fuckel 1871

= Mycosphaerella polymorpha D.J. Sm. & C.O. Sm. 1941

= Mycosphaerella stigmina-platani F.A. Wolf 1938

Distribution: Central and eastern North America; Europe; India.

Substrate: On leaves of Platanus species; leaf blight. Ellis (Mycol. Pap. 72:64. 1959).

Disease Note: Leat blight, leaf spot.

Host: Platanus (Platanaceae).

Updated prior to 1989
