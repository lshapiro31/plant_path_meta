Nomenclature data for Stigmina proteae

Batcheloromyces proteae Marasas, P.S. van Wyk & Knox-Dav. 1975 (Ascomycetes, Dothideales)

≡Spilocaea proteae (Marasas, Van der Westh., Van Warmelo & Papendorf) Arx 1981

≡Stigmina proteae (Marasas, P.S. van Wyk & Knox-Dav.) B. Sutton & Pascoe 1989

Distribution: Africa (South Africa).

Substrate: Living leaves, petioles (but not stems).

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Protea spp. (Proteaceae).
