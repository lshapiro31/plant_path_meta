Nomenclature data for Stilbum javanicum

Tubercularia lateritia (Berk.) Seifert 1985 (Ascomycetes, Hypocreales)

= Stilbum aurantiocinnabarinum var. fuscipes (Speg.) Sacc. 1886

    ≡ Stilbum aurantio-cinnabarinum var. fuscipes (Speg.) Sacc. 1886

= Sphaerostilbe incerta Ces. 1879

= Stilbum javanicum Henn. 1893

= Sphaerostilbe rosea Kalchbr. 1880

Notes: This is wrong. Nectria is conserved over Tubercularia!

Distribution: Pantropical. The asexual state extends into temperate regions and is more common in North America (USA: FL, LA, NC).

Substrate: Branches, trunk.

Disease Note: Canker, dieback; also saprobic.

Host: Woody plants of various families.

Supporting Literature:
