Notes: As '(Berk. & Broome) Seifert'.

Distribution: Cosmopolitan, more frequent in tropics and subtropics.

Substrate: Plant and fungus substrates.

Disease Note: Saprotrophic.

Host: Multiple genera in multiple families.
