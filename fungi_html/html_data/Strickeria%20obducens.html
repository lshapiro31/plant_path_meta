≡Teichospora obducens (Schumach.) Fuckel 1870

= Sphaeria plateata Curr. 1859

Distribution: Temperate regions.

Substrate: Wood.

Disease Note: Saprobic; possibly parasitic.

Host: Numerous dicot hosts.
