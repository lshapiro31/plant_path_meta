Nomenclature data for Suillus luteus

Suillus luteus (L.) Roussel 1796 (Basidiomycetes, Boletales)

≡ Boletus luteus L. 1753

Notes: For additional synonyms, see Index Fungorum.

Distribution: North America, New Zealand.

Substrate: On the ground under Pinus species.

Disease Note: Ectomycorrhizal; not a pathogen.

Host: Associated with Pinus spp. (Pinaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Fries, N., and Neumann, W. 1990. Sexual incompatibility in Suillus luteus and S. granulatus. Mycol. Res. 94: 64-70.
