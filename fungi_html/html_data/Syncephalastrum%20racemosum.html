Nomenclature data for Syncephalastrum racemosum

Syncephalastrum racemosum Cohn ex J. Schröt. 1886 (Zygomycetes, Mucorales)

Notes: Isolated from soil, dung, and various organic substrates (Benjamin. Mycologia 58:5. 1966).

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Fruit.

Disease Note: Rot.

Host: Numerous hosts.

Supporting Literature:

Misra, A.K., Garg, N., and Yadav, K.K. 2016. First report of shell soft rot of Bael (Aegle marmelos) caused by Syncephalastrum racemosum in North India. Pl. Dis. 100(8): 1779.
