Nomenclature data for Taphrina carpini

Taphrina carpini (Rostr.) Johanson 1886 (Taphrinomycetes, Taphrinales)

≡ Exoascus carpini Rostr. 1881

= Lalaria carpini Á. Fonseca, J. Inácio & M.G. Rodrigues 2004

Distribution: Asia, Europe.

Substrate: Branches.

Disease Note: Witch's brooms, leaf deformation.

Host: Carpinus spp. (Betulaceae).

Supporting Literature:
