Nomenclature data for Taphrina communis

Taphrina communis (Sadeb.) Giesenh. 1895 (Taphrinomycetes, Taphrinales)

≡ Exoascus communis Sadeb. 1893

= Lalaria communis R.T. Moore 1990

= Exoascus decipiens G.F. Atk. 1894

    ≡ Taphrina decipiens (G.F. Atk.) Giesenh. 1895

= Exoascus decipiens var. superficialis G.F. Atk. 1894

= Exoascus longipes G.F. Atk. 1894

    ≡ Taphrina longipes (G.F. Atk.) Giesenh. 1895

= Exoascus rhizipes G.F. Atk. 1894

    ≡ Taphrina rhizipes (G.F. Atk.) Giesenh. 1895

Distribution: Central and eastern North America.

Substrate: Twigs, fruit.

Disease Note: Twig deformation, fruit pocket (swollen and hollow fruit), leaf curl.

Host: Prunus spp. (Rosaceae).
