Nomenclature data for Taphrina johansonii

Taphrina johansonii Sadeb. 1890 (Taphrinomycetes, Taphrinales)

= Exoascus johansonii Sadeb. 1893

= Lalaria johansonii R.T. Moore 1990

Distribution: Asia, Europe, North America.

Substrate: Catkins.

Disease Note: Catkin deformity.

Host: Populus spp. (Salicaceae).

Supporting Literature:
