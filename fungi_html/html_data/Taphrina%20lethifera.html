Nomenclature data for Taphrina lethifera

Taphrina letifera (Peck) Sacc 1892 (Taphrinomycetes, Taphrinales)

    Variant spelling Taphrina lethifera (Peck) Sacc. 1892

≡ Ascomyces letifer Peck 1887

      Variant spelling Ascomyces lethifera Peck 1887

= Lalaria letifera R.T. Moore 1990

Distribution: Eastern North America.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot/blight.

Host: Acer spicatum (Sapindaceae).
