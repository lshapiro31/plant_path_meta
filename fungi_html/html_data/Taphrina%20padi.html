Nomenclature data for Taphrina padi

Taphrina padi (Jacz.) Mix 1947 (Taphrinomycetes, Taphrinales)

≡ Taphrina pruni var. padi Jacz. 1926

Distribution: Europe.

Substrate: Fruit.

Disease Note: Fruit deformation.

Host: Prunus padus (Rosaceae).
