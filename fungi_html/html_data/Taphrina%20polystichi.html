Nomenclature data for Taphrina polystichi

Taphrina polystichi Mix 1938 (Taphrinomycetes, Taphrinales)

Distribution: Eastern North America.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Polystichum acrostichoides (Dryopteridaceae).
