Nomenclature data for Taphrina robinsoniana

Taphrina robinsoniana Giesenh. 1892 (Taphrinomycetes, Taphrinales)

≡Exoascus robinsonianus (Giesenh.) Sacc. & Trotter 1913

= Lalaria robinsoniana R.T. Moore 1990

= Taphrina rugosa W.W. Ray 1939

Distribution: Eastern North America.

Substrate: On catkins, leaves.

Disease Note: Alder tongues, leaf curl.

Host: Alnus spp. (Betulaceae).

Supporting Literature:
