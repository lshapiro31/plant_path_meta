Nomenclature data for Taphrina rostrupiana

Taphrina pruni (Fuckel) Tul. 1866 (Taphrinomycetes, Taphrinales)

≡ Exoascus pruni Fuckel 1860

= Taphrina pruni var. divaricata Jacz. 1926

= Exoascus rostrupianus Sadeb. 1893

  ≡Taphrina rostrupiana (Sadeb.) Giesenh. 1895

Distribution: Temperate northern hemisphere.

Substrate: Shoots, fruit.

Disease Note: Fruit pocket (swollen, hollow fruit), twig deformation, leaf curl.

Host: Prunus spp. (Rosaceae).
