Nomenclature data for Taphrina sacchari

Taphrina sacchari Jenk. 1938 (Taphrinomycetes, Taphrinales)

Notes: Jenkins (J. Wash. Acad. Sci. 28:350. 1938) accepted this taxon as distinct from T. acericola because of differences in host relationships and geographic distribution.

Distribution: Central and eastern North America.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot/blister.

Host: Acer saccharum (Sapindaceae).

Supporting Literature:
