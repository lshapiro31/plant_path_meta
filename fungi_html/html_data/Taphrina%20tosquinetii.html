Nomenclature data for Taphrina tosquinetii

Taphrina tosquinetii (Westend.) Magnus 1890 (Taphrinomycetes, Taphrinales)

≡ Ascomyces tosquinetii Westend. 1861

= Taphrina media Palm 1917

= Lalaria tosquinetii R.T. Moore 1990

Distribution: Northeastern North America; Europe.

Substrate: Leaves, shoots.

Disease Note: Leaf curl; does not cause true witch's brooms per Mix (1949).

Host: Alnus glutinosa, A. crispa var. mollis (Betulaceae).
