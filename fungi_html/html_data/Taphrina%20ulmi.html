Nomenclature data for Taphrina ulmi

Taphrina ulmi (Fuckel) Johans. 1886 [1885] (Taphrinomycetes, Taphrinales)

≡ Exoascus ulmi Fuckel 1874

= Exoascus campester Sacc. 1880

  Variant spelling Exoascus campestris Sacc. 1880

= Lalaria ulmi R.T. Moore 1990

Distribution: Eastern North America; Europe.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Ulmus spp. (Ulmaceae).
