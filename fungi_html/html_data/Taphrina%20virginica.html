Nomenclature data for Taphrina virginica

Taphrina virginica Sadeb. 1895 (Taphrinomycetes, Taphrinales)

= Lalaria virginica R.T. Moore 1990

Distribution: Eastern North America.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Ostrya virginiana (Betulaceae).
