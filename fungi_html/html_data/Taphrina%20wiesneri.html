Nomenclature data for Taphrina wiesneri

Taphrina wiesneri (Rathay) Mix 1954 (Taphrinomycetes, Taphrinales)

≡ Exoascus wiesneri Ráthay 1880

= Lalaria cerasi R.T. Moore 1990

= Exoascus deformans f. cerasi Fuckel 1870

    ≡ Exoascus cerasi (Fuckel) Sadeb. 1893

    ≡ Taphrina cerasi (Fuckel) Sadeb. 1890

= Taphrina minor Sadeb. 1890

    ≡ Exoascus minor (Sadeb.) Sacc. 1892

= Taphrina pseudocerasi Shirai 1895

  Variant spelling Taphrina pseudo-cerasi Shirai 1895

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Living leaves; overwintering on branches.

Disease Note: Leaf curl, witches brooms.

Host: Prunus spp. (Rosaceae).
