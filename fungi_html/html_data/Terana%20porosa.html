Nomenclature data for Terana porosa

Gloeocystidiellum porosum (Berk. & M.A. Curtis) Donk 1931 (Basidiomycetes, Russulales)

≡ Corticium porosum Berk. & M.A. Curtis 1879

≡Terana porosa (Berk. & M.A. Curtis) Kuntze 1891

≡Gloeocystidium porosum (Berk. & M.A. Curtis) Wakef. 1928

= Corticium stramineum Bres. 1900

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Wood.

Disease Note: White rot; pathogenicity toward live trees uncertain.

Host: Numerous hosts.
