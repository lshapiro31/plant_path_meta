Nomenclature data for Teratosphaeria maculiformis

Teratosphaeria maculiformis (G. Winter) Joanne E. Taylor & Crous 1999 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

≡ Didymella maculiformis G. Winter 1884

≡Oligostroma maculiformis (G. Winter) Doidge 1921

= Euryachora maculiformis Nel 1942

= Oligostroma proteae Syd. & P. Syd. 1914

    ≡ Mycosphaerella proteae (Syd. & P. Syd.) Arx 1962

Distribution: Africa (South Africa, Zimbabwe).

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Protea spp. (Proteaceae).
