Nomenclature data for Thelephora galactina

Scytinostroma galactinum (Fr.) Donk 1956 (Basidiomycetes, Russulales)

≡ Thelephora galactina Fr. 1851

≡Corticium galactinum (Fr.) Burt 1909

Notes: Nakasone and Micales (Mycologia 80:546. 1988) have separated this species complex into two species (the other being S. protrusum) distinguished by substrate and isozyme profiles.

Distribution: Temperate northern hemisphere.

Substrate: Roots, butt, collar.

Disease Note: Root, butt, and collar rots.

Host: Conifers.

Supporting Literature:

Nakasone, K.K., and Micales, J.A. 1988. Scytinostroma galactinum species complex in the United States. Mycologia 80: 546-559.
