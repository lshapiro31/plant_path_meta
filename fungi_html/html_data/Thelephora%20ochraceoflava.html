Nomenclature data for Thelephora ochraceoflava

Stereum ochraceoflavum (Schwein.) Ellis (Basidiomycetes, Russulales)

    Variant spelling Stereum ochraceo-flavum (Schwein.) Ellis

≡ Thelephora ochraceoflava Schwein. 1832

      Variant spelling Thelephora ochraceo-flava Schwein. 1832

≡Stereum striatum var. ochraceoflavum (Schwein.) A.L. Welden 1971

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Dead limbs, pruning wounds.

Disease Note: White rot.

Host: Hardwoods.
