Nomenclature data for Therrya fuckelii

Therrya fuckelii (Rehm) Kujala 1950 (Ascomycetes, Rhytismatales)

≡ Coccophacidium pini var. fuckelii Rehm 1888

Distribution: North America; temperate Europe.

Substrate: On dying and dead limbs and branches.

Disease Note: Canker.

Host: Pinus (Pinaceae).
