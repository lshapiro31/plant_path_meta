Nomenclature data for Thyrinula eucalypti

Aulographina eucalypti (Cooke & Massee) Arx & E. Müll. 1960 (Ascomycetes, Incertae sedis)

≡ Aulographum eucalypti Cooke & Massee 1889

= Thyrinula eucalypti Petr. & Syd. 1988

= Lembosiopsis eucalyptina Petr. & Syd. 1924

Distribution: HI, southern Africa, Australia, Europe (Great Britain).

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Eucalyptus (Myrtaceae).

Supporting Literature:
