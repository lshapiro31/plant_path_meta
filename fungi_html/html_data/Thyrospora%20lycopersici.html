Nomenclature data for Thyrospora lycopersici

Stemphylium lycopersici (Enjoji) W. Yamam. 1960 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

≡ Thyrospora lycopersici Enjoji 1931

= Stemphylium floridanum Hannon & G.F. Weber 1955

= Stemphylium oblongum Yong Wang bis & X.G. Zhang 2009

= Stemphylium plantaginis Yong Wang bis & X.G. Zhang 2006

= Stemphylium platycodontis J.X. Deng & S.H. Yu 2013

= Stemphylium pruni Yong Wang bis & X.G. Zhang 2006

= Stemphylium pyrinum Yong Wang bis & X.G. Zhang 2009

= Stemphylium sophorae Yong Wang bis & X.G. Zhang 2009

= Stemphylium xanthosomatis B. Huguenin 1966

  Variant spelling Stemphylium xanthosomae B. Huguenin 1966

Notes: Stemphylium platycodontis published online 2013.

Distribution: Widespread, especially in tropical regions.

Substrate: Flowers, fruits, leaves.

Disease Note: Flower and leaf blight, fruit rots.

Host: Multiple genera in multiple families.
