Nomenclature data for Tilletia aculeata

Ustilago serpens (P. Karst.) B. Lindeb. 1959 (Ustilaginomycetes, Ustilaginales)

≡ Tilletia serpens P. Karst. 1866

= Tilletia aculeata Ule 1884

    ≡ Ustilago aculeata (Ule) Liro 1915

= Ustilago elymicola Syd. 1934

= Ustilago michnoana Lavrov 1936

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Leaves, leaf sheaths.

Disease Note: Smut.

Host: Agropyron spp., Elymus spp. and other Poaceae.

Supporting Literature:

Kruse, J., Dietrich, W., Zimmermann, H., Klenke, F., Richter, U., Richter, H., and Thines, M. 2018. Ustilago species causing leaf-stripe smut revisited . IMA Fungus 9(1): 49-73.

Vanky, K. 2012. Smut Fungi of the World. APS Press, St. Paul, Minnesota, 1458 pages.
