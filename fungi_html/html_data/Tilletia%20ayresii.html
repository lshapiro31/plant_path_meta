Nomenclature data for Tilletia ayresii

Conidiosporomyces ayresii (Berk.) Vánky 1992 (Ustilaginomycetes, Tilletiales)

≡ Tilletia ayresii Berk. 1899

= Ustilago evansii Henn. 1908

[≡Ustilago heterospora Henn. 1895 - illegitimate later homonym, not included in search]

    ≡ Tilletia heterospora (Henn.) Zundel 1938

Notes: From Ustilago heterospora - Not Niessl 1872. Nom. illeg. From Tilletia heterospora - Is a nom. nov.

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Ovaries of inflorescence.

Disease Note: Smut.

Host: Poaceae.
