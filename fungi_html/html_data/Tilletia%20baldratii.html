Nomenclature data for Tilletia baldratii

Tilletia baldratii Montemart. 1934 (Ustilaginomycetes, Tilletiales)

= Tilletia durangensis Durán 1971 [1970]

Distribution: Africa, North America.

Substrate: Ovaries.

Disease Note: Smut.

Host: Eragrostis (Poaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Vanky, K. 2012. Smut Fungi of the World. APS Press, St. Paul, Minnesota, 1458 pages.

Vanky, K., Vanky, C., and Denchev, C.M. 2011. Smut fungi in Africa- a checklist. Mycol. Balcan. 8: 1-77.
