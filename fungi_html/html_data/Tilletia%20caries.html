Nomenclature data for Tilletia caries

Tilletia caries (DC.) Tul. & C. Tul. 1847 (Ustilaginomycetes, Tilletiales)

≡ Uredo caries DC. 1815

= Uredo sitophila Ditmar 1816

    ≡ Caeoma sitophilum (Ditmar) Link 1825

    ≡ Tilletia sitophila (Ditmar) J. Schröt. 1877

= Lycoperdon tritici Bjerk. 1775  Note: From Tilletia tritici - As comb. nov., but according to Vanky (2011) it is based on a nom. nud. A validating description is presented by Winter, but the listing of numerous additional synonyms renders the name superfluous.

    ≡ Tilletia tritici (Bjerk.) G. Winter 1884

Notes: From Lycoperdon tritici - Nom. nud.

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Ovaries of inflorescence.

Disease Note: Smut.

Host: Poaceae.
