Nomenclature data for Tilletia foetens

Tilletia laevis J.G. Kühn 1873 (Ustilaginomycetes, Tilletiales)

= Ustilago foetens Berk. & M.A. Curtis 1874

  ≡Tilletia foetens (Berk. & M.A. Curtis) J. Schröt. 1877

= Erysibe foetida Wallr. 1833

    ≡ Tilletia foetida (Wallr.) Liro 1920

Notes: From Erysibe foetida - Vanky (2011) lists these names as synonyms of Tilletia laevis, but states that they are both illeg. since they are superfluous nom. nov. for Caeoma sitophilum and Uredo caries (both listed as synonyms of Tilletia caries (Vanky 2011)). These 'foetida' names should be homotypic synonyms of one of these names that are accepted by Vanky (2011) as synonyms of T. caries.

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Ovaries of inflorescence.

Disease Note: Smut.

Host: Poaceae.
