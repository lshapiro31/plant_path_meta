Nomenclature data for Tilletia hordei

Tilletia hordei Körn. 1877 (Ustilaginomycetes, Tilletiales)

≡Tilletia hordei var. hordei .

= Tilletia hordeina Ranoj. 1914

= Tilletia trabutii Jacz. 1893

  Variant spelling Tilletia trabuti Jacz. 1893

Distribution: Widespread.

Disease Note: Smut.

Host: Critesion, Psathyrostachys (Poaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Vanky, K. 2012. Smut Fungi of the World. APS Press, St. Paul, Minnesota, 1458 pages.

Vanky, K., Vanky, C., and Denchev, C.M. 2011. Smut fungi in Africa- a checklist. Mycol. Balcan. 8: 1-77.
