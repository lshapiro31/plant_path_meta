Nomenclature data for Tilletia olida

Tilletia olida (Riess) J. Schröt. 1877 (Ustilaginomycetes, Tilletiales)

≡ Uredo olida Riess 1852

≡Ustilago olida (Riess) Cif. 1938

= Tilletia endophylla de Bary

= Ustilago macrospora Desm. 1851  Note: A confused name. Also listed by Vanky (2011) under Urocystis ulei and Ustilago serpens in the sense of other authors.

    ≡ Tuburcinia macrospora (Desm.) Liro 1922

Notes: Isonym of (Riess) G. Winter 1881.

Distribution: Asia, Europe.

Disease Note: Smut.

Host: Brachypodium (Poaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Vanky, K. 2012. Smut Fungi of the World. APS Press, St. Paul, Minnesota, 1458 pages.
