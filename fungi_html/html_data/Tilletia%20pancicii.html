Tilletia tritici subsp. nanifica F. Wagner 1950  Note: Nom. inval. via no Latin. Indication of rank not clear but subspecies suggested.

  Variant spelling Tilletia tritici-repentis F. Wagner 1950  Note: Unclear trinomial sometimes given in this form in later literature.

     Tilletia nanifica (F. Wagner) Savul. 1956  Note: Nom. inval.

Tilletia tritici-repentis (DC.) Liro 1938  Note: Combination based on an invalid name fide Vanky (2011). The name would be superfluous at the species rank as a new species name.

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Ovaries of inflorescence.

Disease Note: Smut.

Host: Poaceae.
