Nomenclature data for Tilletia rugispora

Tilletia rugispora Ellis 1893 (Ustilaginomycetes, Tilletiales)

= Tilletia ulei J. Schröt. & Henn. 1896

Distribution: North and South America. Eradicated in New Zealand.

Disease Note: Smut.

Host: Paspalum (Poaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Vanky, K. 2012. Smut Fungi of the World. APS Press, St. Paul, Minnesota, 1458 pages.
