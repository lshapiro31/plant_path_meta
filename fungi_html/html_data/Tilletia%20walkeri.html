Nomenclature data for Tilletia walkeri

Tilletia walkeri Castl. & Carris 1999 (Ustilaginomycetes, Tilletiales)

Distribution: North America (USA), Australia, New Zealand.

Substrate: Seed heads, florets.

Disease Note: Seed bunt.

Host: Ryegrass, Lolium spp. (Poaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Castlebury, L.A., and Carris, L.M. 1999. Tilletia walkeri, a new species on Lolium multiflorum and L. perenne. Mycologia 91: 121-131.

Vanky, K. 2012. Smut Fungi of the World. APS Press, St. Paul, Minnesota, 1458 pages.
