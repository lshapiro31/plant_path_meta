Nomenclature data for Tilletia wilcoxiana

Tilletia hyalospora Massee 1899 (Ustilaginomycetes, Tilletiales)

≡Ingoldiomyces hyalosporus (Massee) Vánky 1996

= Tilletia boliviensis Liro 1939

= Tilletia hypsophila Speg. 1902

= Tilletia wilcoxiana Griffiths 1904

Notes: The genus Ingoldiomyces typified by I. hyalosporus was shown to fall inside the genus Tilletia by Castlebury et al. (2005).

Distribution: From Ingoldiomyces hyalosporus - North America (USA: CA), South America.

Substrate: From Ingoldiomyces hyalosporus - Ovaries.

Disease Note: From Ingoldiomyces hyalosporus - Smut.

Host: From Ingoldiomyces hyalosporus - Poaceae.
