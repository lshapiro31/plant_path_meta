Nomenclature data for Tolyposporella sporoboli

Jamesdicksonia sporoboli (H.S. Jacks.) M. Piepenbr. 2003 (Ustilaginomycetes, Georgefischeriales)

≡ Tolyposporella sporoboli H.S. Jacks. 1926

[≡Melanotaenium sporoboli (H.S. Jacks.) Thirum., M.D. Whitehead & M.J. O'Brien 1967 - illegitimate later homonym, not included in search]  Note: Not Thirum. & M.C. Sriniv. 1964. Nomen illeg.

Distribution: Widespread based on scattered reports.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Smut.

Host: Sporobolus spp. (Poaceae).
