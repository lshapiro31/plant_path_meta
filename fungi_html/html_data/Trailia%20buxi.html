Nomenclature data for Trailia buxi

Puccinia buxi Sowerby 1809 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

≡Dicaeoma buxi (Sowerby) Gray 1821  Note: Isonym of (Sowerby) Kuntze 1891. Kuntze lists both Puccinia buxi Sowerby and Puccinia buxi DC. in making this combination. Both names are presumed to be synonyms.

= Dasyspora buxi Arthur 1906  Note: As '(DC.) Arthur'. Treated as a nom. nov., ICBN Art. 58.

  ≡Trailia buxi (Arthur) Syd. 1922  Note: As '(DC.) Syd.'.

    ≡ Micropuccinia buxi (Arthur) Arthur & H.S. Jacks. 1921  Note: As '(DC.) Arthur & H.S. Jacks.'.

[≡Puccinia buxi DC. 1815 - illegitimate later homonym, not included in search]

Notes: From Puccinia buxi - Not Sowerby 1809. Nomen illeg.

Distribution: Asia, Europe.

Disease Note: Rust.

Host: Buxus spp. (Buxaceae).
