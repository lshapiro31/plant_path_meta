Nomenclature data for Trematosphaeria circinans

Byssothecium circinans Fuckel 1861 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

≡Heptameria circinans (Fuckel) Cooke 1889

≡Leptosphaeria circinans (Fuckel) Sacc. 1883

≡Passeriniella circinans (Fuckel) Sacc. 1895

≡Trematosphaeria circinans (Fuckel) G. Winter 1885

= Melanomma vindelicorum (Rehm) Sacc. 1881

    ≡ Trematosphaeria vindelicorum (Rehm) Sacc. 1883

Notes: A saprobe or weak parasite on woody substrates (Boise. Mycologia 75:666. 1983).

Distribution: Europe, North America.

Substrate: Crowns, rhizomes, roots.

Disease Note: Crown and root rot.

Host: Medicago sativa (Fabaceae).

Supporting Literature:

De Gruyter, J., Woudenberg, J.H.C., Aveskamp, M.M., Verkley, G.J.M., Groenewald, J.Z., and Crous, P.W. 2013. Redisposition of phoma-like anamorphs in Pleosporales. Stud. Mycol. 75: 1-36.
