Nomenclature data for Trichoconiella padwickii

Trichoconiella padwickii (Ganguly) B.L. Jain 1976 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

≡ Trichoconis padwickii Ganguly 1948

≡Alternaria padwickii (Ganguly) M.B. Ellis 1971

Notes: This fungus is distinct from Alternaria and Trichoconiella appears to be the oldest and correct genus name (Emory Simmons, personal communication). DNA data also supports a distinction from Alternaria.

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Coleoptiles, fruit, leaves, roots, seeds.

Disease Note: Stackburn, seedling blight, leaf spot.

Host: Oryza sativa (Poaceae). Few reports from other hosts.
