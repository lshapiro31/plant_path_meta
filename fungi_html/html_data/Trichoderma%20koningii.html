Nomenclature data for Trichoderma koningii

Trichoderma koningii Oudem. 1902 (Ascomycetes, Hypocreales)

= Hypocrea koningii Lieckf., Samuels & W. Gams 1999

Notes: Often confused with T. hamatum

Distribution: The anamorph was until recently regarded as cosmopolitan, but its reports are confirmed only in Europe (UK, Hungary) and North America (USA: WI). The teleomorph has been reported from Europe (Austria, the Netherlands) and North America (USA: MD, PA).

Substrate: Teleomorph: decorticated wood. Anamorph: soil.

Disease Note: Saprobic.
