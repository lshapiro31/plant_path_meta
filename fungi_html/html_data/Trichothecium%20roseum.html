Nomenclature data for Trichothecium roseum

Trichothecium roseum (Pers. : Fr.) Link 1809 (Ascomycetes, Incertae sedis)

≡ Trichoderma roseum Pers. : Fr. 1794

≡Hyphelia rosea (Pers. : Fr.) Fr. 1825

≡Puccinia rosea (Pers. : Fr.) Corda 1905

= Cephalothecium roseum Corda 1838

Notes: A few more obscure synonyms may exist.

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: On a wide variety of organic substrates.

Disease Note: Fruit rots especially apple, stipe rot (fungus).

Host: Multiple plant genera in multiple families, Coprinus comatus (Agaricaceae).
