Nomenclature data for Trichothyrium reptans

Trichothyrium reptans (Berk. & M.A. Curtis) S. Hughes 1953 (Ascomycetes, Microthyriales)

≡ Asterina reptans Berk. & M.A. Curtis 1868

≡Trichopeltis reptans (Berk. & M.A. Curtis) Speg. 1889

= Isthmospora glabra F. Stevens 1918

Notes: Müller & Arx (Beitr. Kryptogamenfl. Schweiz 11(2):55. 1962); Farr (Acta Amaz., Suppl. 15:33. 1985);Sivanesan (The bitunicate Ascomycetes. p. 124. 1984).

Distribution: Tropical regions.

Substrate: On living leavevs.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: On diverse tropical plants.

Supporting Literature:

Sivanesan, A. 1984. The bitunicate Ascomycetes and their anamorphs. J. Cramer, Vaduz, 701 pages.
