Nomenclature data for Triphragmium koelreuteriae

Nyssopsora koelreuteriae (Syd. & P. Syd.) Tranzschel 1925 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

≡ Triphragmium koelreuteriae Syd. & P. Syd. 1913

Distribution: Asia (China, Japan).

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Rust.

Host: Koelreuteria bipinnata, K. paniculata (Sapindaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Yang, Y., Yu, Y., and Bi, C. 2016. First report of rust disease on Koelreuteria bipinnata caused by Nyssopsora koelreuteriae in China. Pl. Dis. 100(5): 1014.
