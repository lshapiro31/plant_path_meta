Nomenclature data for Tripospermum myrti

Tripospermum myrti (Lind) S. Hughes 1951 (Ascomycetes, Capnodiales)

≡ Triposporium myrti Lind 1913

Distribution: Europe, Asia (Japan).

Substrate: Leaves, twigs, fruit.

Disease Note: Lesions, rot.

Host: Diverse woody hosts.

Supporting Literature:

Grabowski, M. 2007. The study of new fungus species causing apple sooty blotch. Folia Hort. 19(2): 89-97.

Wrona, B., and Grabowski, M. 2004. Etiology of apple sooty blotch in Poland. J. Pl. Protect. Res. 44(4): 293-297.
