Nomenclature data for Trochila salicis

Drepanopeziza salicis (Tul. & C. Tul.) Höhn. 1920 (Ascomycetes, Helotiales)

≡ Trochila salicis Tul. & C. Tul. 1865

≡Pseudopeziza salicis (Tul. & C. Tul.) Potebnia 1910

= Gloeosporium salicis Westend. 1859

    ≡ Monostichella salicis (Westend.) Arx 1957

    ≡ Gloeosporidiella salicis (Westend.) Nannf. 1931

Distribution: Temperate North America; Europe.

Substrate: On living leaves.

Disease Note: Willow anthracnose; leaf spot.

Host: On Salix (Salicaceae).
