Nomenclature data for Trolliomyces rosae

Phragmidium kamtschatkae (H.W. Anderson) Arthur & Cummins 1933 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

≡ Puccinia kamtschatkae H.W. Anderson 1891

≡Dicaeoma kamtschatkae (H.W. Anderson) Kuntze 1898

≡Teloconia kamtschatkae (H.W. Anderson) Hirats. f. 1943

= Dicaeoma rosae Kuntze 1898

  [≡ Puccinia rosae Barclay 1889 - illegitimate later homonym, not included in search]  Note: Not Schumach. 1803.

    ≡ Teloconia rosae (Kuntze) Syd. 1921

  Trolliomyces rosae (Kuntze) Ulbr. 1938  Note: Nom. illeg., superfluous Art. 52.

    ≡ Phragmidium rosae (Kuntze) Tranzschel 1914  Note: Not (Pers.) Rostr. 1902.

     Gymnoconia rosae (Kuntze) Liro 1908  Note: Nom. illeg., superfluous Art. 52.

Notes: The first new combination based on Puccinia rosae Barclay was treated as a nom. nov. since P. rosae is an illegitimate later homonym.

Distribution: Asia, Europe.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Rust.

Host: Rosa spp. (Rosaceae).
