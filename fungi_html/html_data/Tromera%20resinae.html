Nomenclature data for Tromera resinae

Sarea resinae (Fr. : Fr.) Kuntze 1898 (Ascomycetes, Agyriales)

≡ Sphaeria resinae Fr. 1815

≡Biatora resinae (Fr.) Tuck. 1872

≡Biatorella resinae (Fr. : Fr.) Th. Fr. 1861 [1860]

≡Biatoridium resinae (Fr.) Uloth 1865

≡Tromera resinae (Fr.) Körb. 1865

= Cytospora resinae Ehrenb. 1818

= Pycnidiella resinae (Fr. : Fr.) Höhn. 1915

Distribution: Northern hemisphere.

Substrate: Bark.

Disease Note: Associated with cankers; pathogenicity uncertain, as it may be a secondary invader.

Host: Conifers.

Supporting Literature:

Hawksworth, D.L. 1980. Sarea resinae. C.M.I. Descr. Pathog. Fungi Bact. 677: 1-2.
