Nomenclature data for Typhula itoana

Typhula incarnata Lasch ex Fr. 1838 (Basidiomycetes, Agaricales)

= Clavaria elegantula P. Karst. 1882  Note: New combination?

= Typhula elegantula P. Karst. 1871

    ≡ Pistillaria elegantula (P. Karst.) Corner 1950

= Typhula itoana S. Imai 1930 [1929]

Distribution: Northern hemisphere, cooler parts.

Disease Note: Snow mold.

Host: Poaceae.
