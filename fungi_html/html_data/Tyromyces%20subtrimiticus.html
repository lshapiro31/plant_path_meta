Nomenclature data for Tyromyces subtrimiticus

Fomitopsis spraguei (Berk. & M.A. Curtis) Gilb. & Ryvarden 1985 (Basidiomycetes, Polyporales)

≡ Polyporus spraguei Berk. & M.A. Curtis 1872

≡Trametes spraguei (Berk. & M.A. Curtis) Ryvarden 1980

≡Tyromyces spraguei (Berk. & M.A. Curtis) Murrill 1907

= Polyporus sordidus Cooke 1886

= Tyromyces subtrimiticus Corner 1989

Notes: Heart rot, also on dead wood.

Distribution: Central and eastern North America, rare in the northwest; Europe.

Disease Note: Brown, cubical root rot.

Host: On various woody plants.

Supporting Literature:

Gilbertson, R.L., and Ryvarden, L. 1986. North American Polypores Vol. 1. Abortiporus - Lindtneria. Fungiflora, Oslo, 433 pages.
