Nomenclature data for Uncinula clandestina

Erysiphe ulmi Castagne 1845 (Ascomycetes, Erysiphales)

= Erysiphe bivonae Tul. & C. Tul. 1861

= Erysiphe clandestina Biv. 1815

  ≡Uncinula clandestina (Biv.) J. Schröt. 1893

Notes: From Erysiphe clandestina - Eysiphe clandestina Biv. is not available for use since Erysiphe clandestina (Wallr. : Fr.) Link (syn. Alphitomorpha clandestina Wallr.) was sanctioned by Fries. From Uncinula bivonae - Nom. illeg. Superfluous. From Erysiphe bivonae - As (Léveillé), but it is a nom. nov. Includes the type variety. U. Braun & Minnis 2008 is a superfluous isonym.

Distribution: Africa (Algeria), Asia, Europe; records from USA doubtful per Braun (2012).

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Powdery mildew.

Host: Ulmus spp.(Ulmaceae).
