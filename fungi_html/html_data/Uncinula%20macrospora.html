Nomenclature data for Uncinula macrospora

Erysiphe macrospora (Peck) U. Braun & S. Takam. 2000 (Ascomycetes, Erysiphales)

≡ Uncinula macrospora Peck 1872

= Uncinula intermedia Berk. & M.A. Curtis 1876

Distribution: North America.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Powdery mildew.

Host: Ulmus spp. (Ulmaceae).
