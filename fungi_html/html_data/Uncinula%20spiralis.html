Nomenclature data for Uncinula spiralis

Erysiphe necator var. necator Schwein. 1834 (Ascomycetes, Erysiphales)

≡Erysiphe necator Schwein. 1834  Note: The type variety is the accepted name.

≡Uncinula necator (Schwein.) Burrill 1892

= Uncinula americana Howe 1872

= Sphaerotheca castagnei var. vitis Fuckel 1869

= Uncinula spiralis Berk. & M.A. Curtis 1876

= Uncinula spiralis var. racemosum Thüm. 1878

= Uncinula subfusca Berk. & M.A. Curtis 1876

= Erysiphe tuckeri Berk. 1855

= Oidium tuckeri Berk. 1847

    ≡ Acrosporium tuckeri (Berk.) Sumst. 1913

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: On living fruits and leaves.

Disease Note: Powdery mildew.

Host: Vitis spp. (Vitaceae). Reports on Parthenocissus spp. may refer to Erysiphe necator var. ampelopsidis (Braun 1995).
