Nomenclature data for Uncinula tulasnei

Sawadaea tulasnei (Fuckel) Homma 1937 (Ascomycetes, Erysiphales)

    Variant spelling Sawadaia tulasnei (Fuckel) Homma 1937

≡ Uncinula tulasnei Fuckel 1866

≡Uncinula aceris var. tulasnei (Fuckel) E.S. Salmon 1900

= Erysiphe varium Fr. 1815

  Variant spelling Erysiphe varia Fr.

Notes: Uncinula bicornis=Erysiphe bicornis=Sawadaea bicornis are listed by Homma (1937) as synonyms pro parte.

Distribution: Europe, Asia, North America (USA: ID, OH, WI).

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Powdery mildew.

Host: Acer spp. (Sapindaceae).
