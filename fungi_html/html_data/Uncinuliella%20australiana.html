Nomenclature data for Uncinuliella australiana

Erysiphe australiana (McAlpine) U. Braun & S. Takam. 2000 (Ascomycetes, Erysiphales)

≡ Uncinula australiana McAlpine 1899

≡Uncinuliella australiana (McAlpine) Zheng & Chen 1982

Distribution: Temperate regions.

Substrate: Leaves, stems, inflorescences.

Disease Note: Powdery mildew, necrosis.

Host: Lagerstroemia spp. (Lythraceae).
