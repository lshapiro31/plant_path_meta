Nomenclature data for Uncinuliella flexuosa

Erysiphe flexuosa (Peck) U. Braun & S. Takam. 2000 (Ascomycetes, Erysiphales)

≡ Uncinula flexuosa Peck 1872

≡Uncinuliella flexuosa (Peck) U. Braun 1981

Distribution: Europe, North America.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Powdery mildew.

Host: Aesculus spp., Sapindus spp. (Sapindaceae).
