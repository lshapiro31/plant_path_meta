Nomenclature data for Uredo acaciae

Uromycladium acaciae (Cooke) P. Syd. & Syd. 1915 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

≡ Uredo acaciae Cooke 1890

= Uromycladium bisporum McAlpine 1905

Distribution: Africa (South Africa, Swaziland), Australia; unconfirmed report from South America (Brazil).

Substrate: Leaves, petiole, rachis, seedlings.

Disease Note: Rust; defoliation, gummosis, seedling death, stunting, dieback.

Host: Acacia spp. (Fabaceae).

Supporting Literature:

McTaggart, A.R., Doungsa-ard, C., Wingfield, M.J., and Roux, J. 2015. Uromycladium acaciae, the cause of a sudden, severe disease epidemic on Acacia mearnsii in South Africa. Australas. Pl. Pathol. 44: 637-645.
