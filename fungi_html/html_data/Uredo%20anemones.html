≡ Uredo anemones Pers. 1797

≡Tuburcinia anemones (Pers.) Liro 1922

≡Urocystis anemones var. anemones .

Notes: Vanky (2011) lists additional unranked synonyms.

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Leaves, petioles, stems.

Disease Note: Smut.

Host: Anemone, Anemonella (Ranunculaceae).
