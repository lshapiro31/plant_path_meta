Nomenclature data for Uredo bixae

Crossopsora bixae Buriticá 1978 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

= Uredo bixae Arthur 1915

Notes: Autoecious.

Distribution: FL, Caribbean; South America.

Disease Note: Rust.

Host: Bixa (Bixaceae).
