Nomenclature data for Uredo bullata

Puccinia angelicae Fuckel 1870 [1869] (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

= Uredo angelicae Schumach. 1803

= Uredo bullata Pers. : Pers. 1796  Note: Persoon described teliospores therefore this is a teleomorphic name in an anamorphic genus.

  [≡ Puccinia bullata (Pers. : Pers.) J. Schröt. 1879 - illegitimate later homonym, not included in search]  Note: Sometimes erroneously cited as (Pers.) G. Winter 1884 or (Pers.) J. Schrot. 1889. Illegitimate later homonym of Puccinia bullata Link 1816 = Puccinia ribis.

Notes: Reflecting the confused history of this name, Hiratsuka (1992) lists Puccinia bullata T. Miyake 1906 as a synonym of Puccinia jogashimensis; Puccinia bullata Dietel 1901 as a synonym of Puccinia nanbuana; and Puccinia bullata Winter 1884 as a synonym of Puccinia angelicae, which we follow here.

Distribution: Asia, Europe, North America.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Autoecious rust.

Host: Apiaceae.
