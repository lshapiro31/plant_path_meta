Nomenclature data for Uredo ciceris-arietini

Uromyces ciceris-arietini Jacz. 1894 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

≡Uromyces ciceris-arietini var. ciceris-arietini Jacz. 1894

= Uredo ciceris-arietini Grognot 1863

= Uromyces ciceris-arietini var. aetnensis Scalia 1899

Notes: As Uromyces ciceris-arientini (Grognot) Jacz.. Author reference corrected to Jacz. (Art. 59.6). Listed as a synonym of Uromyces anthyllidis by some authors, but treated by other authors as distinct because it lacks pycnia and aecia and there is no evidence it can infect Anthyllis spp.. Oldest epithet not in correct genus.

Distribution: Africa (Kenya, Morocco, Libya), Asia (India, Pakistan), Europe, North America (Mexico, SD, TX), South America (Brazil).

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Small, round or oval, cinnamon-brown pustules; may cause premature defoliation, reduced yield.

Host: Cicer arietinum, Trigonella polycerata (Fabaceae).
