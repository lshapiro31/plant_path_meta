Nomenclature data for Uredo cissi

Mycosyrinx cissi (DC.) Beck 1894 (Ustilaginomycetes, Ustilaginales)

≡ Uredo cissi DC. 1808

≡Schroeteria cissi (DC.) De Toni 1888

≡Ustilago cissi (DC.) Tul. & C. Tul. 1847

= Geminella exotica J. Schröt. 1876

= Puccinia incarcerata Lév. 1845

Distribution: Widespread in subtropical and tropical regions.

Substrate: Branches.

Disease Note: Smut.

Host: Cissus spp. (Vitaceae).
