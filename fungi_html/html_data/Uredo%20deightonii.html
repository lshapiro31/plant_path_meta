Nomenclature data for Uredo deightonii

Hemileia deightonii Syd. 1937 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

≡Uredo deightonii (Syd.) Cummins 1960

= Hemileia rauvolfiae J.M. Yen & Gilles 1976

Distribution: Africa (Ghana, Ivory Coast, Sierra Leone).

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Host affected little or not at all.

Host: Rauvolfia vomitoria (Apocynaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Judith, C., and Rossman, A. 2014. Revised scientific names of the genus Hemileia (Pucciniales) based on the new ICN. MycoKeys 8: 1-10.

Ritschel, A. 2005. Monograph of the genus Hemileia (Uredinales). Biblioth. Mycol. 200: 132.
