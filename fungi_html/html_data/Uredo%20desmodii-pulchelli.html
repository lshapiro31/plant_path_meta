Nomenclature data for Uredo desmodii-pulchelli

Phakopsora mangalorica T.S. Ramakr. & Sundaram 1952 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

= Uredo desmodii-heterocarpi Petch 1917

= Uredo desmodii-pulchelli Syd. & P. Syd. 1902

    ≡ Physopella desmodii-pulchelli (Syd. & P. Syd.) Y. Ono, Buriticá & J.F. Hennen 1992

= Uredo desmodii-triquetri Petch 1917

Notes: Commonly reported as Phakopsora meibomiae. Oldest epithet not in correct genus.

Distribution: Asia, Phillipines, Papua New Guinea.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Rust.

Host: Desmodium spp., Uraria spp., Phyllodium spp., Pteroloma spp. (Fabaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Ono, Y., Buritica, P., and Hennen, J.F. 1992. Delimitation of Phakopsora, Physopella and Cerotelium and their species on Leguminosae. Mycol. Res. 96: 825-850.

Ramakrishnan, T.S. 1952. Additions to fungi of Madras - XII. Proc. Indian Acad. Sci. 35: 111-121.
