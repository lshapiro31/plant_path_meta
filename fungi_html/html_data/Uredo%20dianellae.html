Nomenclature data for Uredo dianellae

Puccinia dianellae (Dietel) McTaggart & R.G. Shivas 2017 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

≡ Uredo dianellae Dietel 1898

Distribution: Asia (China, Japan, Taiwan).

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Rust.

Host: Dianella spp. (Asphodelaceae).
