Nomenclature data for Uredo dioscoreae-sativae

Uredo dioscoreae-sativae Syd. & P. Syd. 1912 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

= Uredo dioscoreae-pentaphyllae Petch 1912

Distribution: Asia (Sri Lanka, Indonesia, Philippines), Papua New Guinea. Also reported from Africa (Uganda).

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Autoecious rust.

Host: Dioscorea spp. (Dioscoreaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Ono, Y. 1982. Rusts of yams in Southeast Asia and South Pacific. Trans. Brit. Mycol. Soc. 79: 423-429.
