Nomenclature data for Uredo moricola

Cerotelium fici (Castagne) Arthur 1917 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

≡ Uredo fici Castagne 1848

≡Kuehneola fici (Castagne) E.J. Butler 1914

≡Physopella fici (Castagne) Arthur 1906

= Uredo moricola Henn. 1902

Notes: Laundon & Rainbow (1971) give incorrect authorship of Cerotelium fici and Kuehneola fici, the latter an error originated by Arthur.

Distribution: North America (USA, Mexico), Central America (Guatemala), South America, Caribbean, Africa, Europe, Asia, Australia.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Rust.

Host: Ficus spp. and other Moraceae. The rust on Morus spp. may be a distinct species. Some records on Ficus spp. other than Ficus carica may refer to Uredo ficina (Laundon & Rainbow 1971).
