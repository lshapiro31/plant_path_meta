Nomenclature data for Uredo musae

Uredo musae Cummins 1941 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

Distribution: Widespread in Asia, Oceania.

Disease Note: Banana leaf rust.

Host: Musa spp. (Musaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Cummins, G.B. 1941. Uredinales of New Guinea -- III. Mycologia 33: 143-154.

Shivas, R.G. 1987. Citrus canker (Xanthomonas campestris pv. citri) and banana leaf rust (Uredo musae) at Christmas Island, Indian Ocean. Australas. Pl. Pathol. 16: 38-39.
