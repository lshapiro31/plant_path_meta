Nomenclature data for Uredo pallida

Phakopsora pallescens (Arthur) Buriticá & J.F. Hennen 1996 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

≡ Puccinia pallescens Arthur 1919

≡Angiopsora pallescens (Arthur) Mains 1934

≡Dicaeoma pallescens (Arthur) Arthur & Fromme 1920

≡Physopella pallescens (Arthur) Cummins & Ramachar 1958

= Uredo pallida Dietel & Holw. 1897

Notes: Oldest epithet not in correct genus.

Distribution: North America (USA: FL - eradicated), new world tropical and subtropical areas.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Rust.

Host: Tripsacum and other Poaceae.

Supporting Literature:

Buritica, P., and Pardo Cardona, V.M. 1996. Flora Uredineana Colombiana. Rev. Acad. Colomb. Cienc. 20: 183-236.

Cummins, G.B., and Ramachar, P. 1958. The genus Physopella (Uredinales) replaces Angiopsora. Mycologia 50: 741-744.

Seymour, C.P., and Miller, J.W. 1974. The eradication of two potentially dangerous rusts, Physopella pallescens and P. zeae, from Florida. Proc. Florida State Hort. Soc. 84: 124-126.
