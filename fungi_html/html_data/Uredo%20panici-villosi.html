Notes: From Uredo henningsii - Nom. nov. From Aecidium brasiliense - This is the aecial stage. From Puccinia panicicola - Based on uredinia (Cummins 1971). From Uredo eriochloana - Nom. nov.

Distribution: Circumglobal in warmer regions.

Disease Note: Rust.

Host: Aecial stage on Cordia spp. (Boraginaceae), uredinial and telial stages on numerous taxa of Poaceae.
