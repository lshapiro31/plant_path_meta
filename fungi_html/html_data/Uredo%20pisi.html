Nomenclature data for Uredo pisi

Uromyces pisi (DC.) G.H. Otth 1863 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

≡ Puccinia pisi DC. 1805

= Uredo appendiculata var. pisi Pers. 1795

  [≡ Uromyces pisi (Pers.) G. Winter 1880 - illegitimate later homonym, not included in search]  Note: Not (DC.) G.H. Otth 1863.

  Variant spelling Uredo appendiculata (b.) pisi Pers.

  Variant spelling Uredo appendiculata ß pisi Pers.

= Uredo appendiculata var. pisi-sativi Pers. : Pers. 1801

    ≡ Uromyces pisi-sativi (Pers.) Liro 1908

  Variant spelling Uredo appendiculata ß pisi-sativi Pers.

= Uredo pisi F. Strauss 1811

  [≡ Uromyces pisi (F. Strauss) J. Schröt. 1875 - illegitimate later homonym, not included in search]  Note: Not (DC.) G.H. Otth 1863.

Notes: Not (Pers.) de Bary 1863 (nom. nud.?) nor (F. Strauss) J. Schröt. 1875, nor (Pers.) G. Winter 1880.

Distribution: Africa, Asia, Europe, South America (Argentina, Chile).

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Rust.

Host: Pycnia and aecia on Euphorbia (Euphorbeaceae), uredia and telia on Lathyrus (=Orobus), Pisum, and Vicia spp. (Fabaceae).
