Nomenclature data for Uredo rhododendri

Chrysomyxa rhododendri de Bary 1879 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

≡Chrysomyxa ledi var. rhododendri (De Bary) Savile 1955

= Melampsora rhododendri Thüm. 1880  Note: Possibly an invalid herbarium name.

= Uredo rhododendri DC. 1815

     Chrysomyxa ledi var. rhododendri (DC.) Savile 1950

    ≡ Caeoma rhododendri (DC.) Link 1825

    ≡ Melampsoropsis rhododendri (DC.) Arthur 1906

    ≡ Erysibe rhododendri (DC.) Wallr. 1833

    ≡ Coleosporium rhododendri (DC.) J. Schröt. 1879

Notes: Historical records from the USA typically represent interceptions and due to taxonomic confusion, it is thought that this species does not occur in the USA as there are no recent confirmed reports of its presence and persistence.

Distribution: Europe, North America (Canada, Greenland), Asia. Introduced to New Zealand and Australia. Aecial state does not occur in North America. Not confirmed in USA.

Substrate: Leaves, petioles, fruit pedicels, twigs.

Disease Note: Heteroecious rust. Listed by APHIS as a Regulated Plant Pest.

Host: Spermogonia and aecia on Picea (Pinaceae). Not in North America. Uredinia and telia on Rhododendron (Ericaceae).
