Nomenclature data for Uredo sojae

Phakopsora pachyrhizi Syd. & P. Syd. 1914 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

    Variant spelling Phakopsora pachyrrhizi Syd. & P. Syd.

≡Physopella pachyrhizi (Syd. & P. Syd.) Azbukina 1970  Note: Teleomorph in an anamorph genus.

= Phakopsora calothea Syd. 1931

= Phakopsora erythrinae Gäum. 1922

= Uredo erythrinae Henn. 1908

= Uredo sojae Henn. 1903

    ≡ Malupa sojae (Henn.) Y. Ono, Buriticá & J.F. Hennen 1992

= Uromyces sojae Syd. 1906  Note: Treated as new teleomorph species.

Notes: See Ono 1992 for detailed discussion of nomenclature.------ Oldest epithet cannot be placed in correct genus.

Distribution: Cosmopolitan except Europe. North America (USA: AL, AR, FL, GA, HI, IA, IL, IN, KS, KY, LA, MN, MO, MS, NC, NE, OK, SC, SD, TN, TX, VA).

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Rust.

Host: Several genera of Fabaceae; notably on Glycine max.
