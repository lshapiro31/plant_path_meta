Nomenclature data for Uredo sphaerococca

Tilletia sphaerococca (Wallr.) A.A. Fisch. Waldh. 1867 (Ustilaginomycetes, Tilletiales)

≡ Erysibe sphaerococca Wallr. 1833

≡Tilletia decipiens (Pers.) Körn. 1877

≡Uredo decipiens (Pers.) F. Strauss 1810  Note: Nom. illeg. via superfluous.

≡Uredo segetum e decipiens Pers. 1801  Note: As 'Uredo segetum unranked Uredo decipiens'.

≡Uredo sphaerococca (Wallr.) Rabenh. 1844

≡Ustilago sphaerococca (Wallr.) J.G. Kühn 1859

= Tilletia schenckiana Henn. 1906

Notes: The nomenclature for this species is confusing, complicated, and needs some reexamination. T. decipiens may have priority.

Distribution: Widespread.

Disease Note: Smut.

Host: Agrostis, Deschampsia (Poaceae).
