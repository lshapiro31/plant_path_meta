Nomenclature data for Uredo transversalis

Uromyces transversalis G. Winter 1884 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

= Uredo transversalis Thüm. 1876

= Uromyces watsoniae Syd. & P. Syd. 1910

Distribution: Africa, South America (Argentina, Brazil, Venezuela), Europe, Australia, New Zealand. Recently intercepted from Mexico.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Rust. Listed by USDA APHIS as a Regulated Plant Pest.

Host: Multiple genera of Iridaceae.
