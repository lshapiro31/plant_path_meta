Nomenclature data for Uredo vitis

Phakopsora euvitis Y. Ono 2000 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

= Uredo vialae Lagerh. 1890

    ≡ Physopella viala (Lagerh.) Buriticá & J.F. Hennen 1994  Note: Should be vialae.

= Uredo vitis Thüm. 1878  Note: From Physopella vitis - This is the uredinial state. Aecidium meliosmae-myrianthi is the aecial state.

    ≡ Physopella vitis (Thüm.) Arthur 1906

    =  Aecidium meliosmae-myrianthi Henn. & Shirai 1900  Note: Aecial state.

Notes: Oldest epithet not in correct genus.

Distribution: Africa, Asia, North America (USA), Caribbean, Australia, South America.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Rust.

Host: Meliosma spp. (Sabiaceae), Vitis spp. (Vitaceae).
