  Variant spelling Ustilago zeae-mays G. Winter 1881

Notes: The very widely and long used specific epithet provided by Uredo maydis (1815) to be proposed for conservation by McTaggart et. al. over the older Lycoperdon zeae (1768).

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Stems, leaves, inflorescences.

Disease Note: Smut. Corn smut.

Host: Euchlaena, Zea spp. (Poaceae).
