Nomenclature data for Urocystis clintoniae

Urocystis clintoniae Kom. ex Govorova 1990 (Ustilaginomycetes, Urocystales)

≡ Tuburcinia clintoniae Kom. 1899

≡Ginanniella clintoniae (Kom.) Ulbr. 1940

Notes: Urocystis clintoniae (Kom.) Vánky 1990 is invalid via ICBN Art. 33. Urocystis clintoniae (Kom.) Uljan. 1968 is also invalid.

Distribution: Asia, North America.

Substrate: Leaves, petioles.

Disease Note: Smut.

Host: Smilacina, Streptopus (Liliaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Denchev, C.M., and Minter, D.W. 2008. Urocystis clintoniae. I.M.I. Descr. Fungi Bact. 1766: 1-3.

Vanky, K. 2012. Smut Fungi of the World. APS Press, St. Paul, Minnesota, 1458 pages.
