Nomenclature data for Urocystis dioscoreae

Urocystis dioscoreae Syd. & P. Syd. 1909 (Ustilaginomycetes, Urocystales)

≡Tuburcinia dioscoreae (Syd. & P. Syd.) Liro 1922

Distribution: Asia.

Substrate: Leaves, stems.

Disease Note: Smut.

Host: Dioscorea (Dioscoreaceae).
