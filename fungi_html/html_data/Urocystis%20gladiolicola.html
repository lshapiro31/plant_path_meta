Nomenclature data for Urocystis gladiolicola

Urocystis gladiolicola Ainsw. 1950 (Ustilaginomycetes, Urocystales)

≡Tuburcinia gladiolicola (Ainsw.) Cif. 1963

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Leaves, scape, bulbs.

Disease Note: Smut.

Host: Gladiolus spp., Crocus spp. (Iridaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Vanky, K. 2012. Smut Fungi of the World. APS Press, St. Paul, Minnesota, 1458 pages.

Vanky, K., Vanky, C., and Denchev, C.M. 2011. Smut fungi in Africa- a checklist. Mycol. Balcan. 8: 1-77.
