Nomenclature data for Urocystis poae

Urocystis poae (Liro) Padwick & A. Khan 1944 (Ustilaginomycetes, Urocystales)

≡ Tuburcinia poae Liro 1922

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Leaves, leaf sheaths.

Disease Note: Smut.

Host: Poa spp. (Poaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Vanky, K. 2012. Smut Fungi of the World. APS Press, St. Paul, Minnesota, 1458 pages.

Vanky, K., Vanky, C., and Denchev, C.M. 2011. Smut fungi in Africa- a checklist. Mycol. Balcan. 8: 1-77.
