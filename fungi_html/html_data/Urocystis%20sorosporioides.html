Nomenclature data for Urocystis sorosporioides

≡Tuburcinia sorosporioides (Körn. ex A.A. Fisch. Waldh.) Liro 1922

Distribution: Northern hemisphere.

Disease Note: Smut.

Host: Thalictrum (Ranunculaceae) and possibly other genera in the family (Vanky 2011).
