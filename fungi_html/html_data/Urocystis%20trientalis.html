Nomenclature data for Urocystis trientalis

Urocystis trientalis (Berk. & Broome) B. Lindeb. 1959 (Ustilaginomycetes, Urocystales)

≡ Tuburcinia trientalis Berk. & Broome 1850

≡Ginanniella trientalis (Berk. & Broome) Cif. 1938

≡Sorosporium trientalis (Berk. & Broome) Cooke 1877

Notes: Vanky (2011) lists Polycystis opaca and its obligate synonyms as synonyms pro parte.

Distribution: Northern hemisphere.

Disease Note: Smut.

Host: Trientalis (Primulaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Vanky, K. 2012. Smut Fungi of the World. APS Press, St. Paul, Minnesota, 1458 pages.
