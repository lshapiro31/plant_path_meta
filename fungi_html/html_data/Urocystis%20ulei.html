Nomenclature data for Urocystis ulei

Urocystis ulei Magnus 1878 (Ustilaginomycetes, Urocystales)

= Urocystis festucae Ule 1884

= Tuburcinia festucae-elatioris Hintikka 1924

     Tuburcinia occulta var. festucae-elatioris (Hintikka) Cif. 1963  Note: Nom. inval.

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Leaves, leaf sheaths.

Disease Note: Smut.

Host: Festuca spp. (Poaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Denchev, T.T., and Denchev, C.M. 2016. Contribution to the smut fungi Greece. Willdenowia 46: 233-244.

Vanky, K. 2012. Smut Fungi of the World. APS Press, St. Paul, Minnesota, 1458 pages.
