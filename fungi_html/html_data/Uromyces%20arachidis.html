Nomenclature data for Uromyces arachidis

Puccinia arachidis Speg. 1884 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

≡Dicaeoma arachadis (Speg.) Kuntze 1898

≡Bullaria arachidis (Speg.) Arthur & Mains 1922

= Uromyces arachidis Henn. 1896

Distribution: Tropical and subtropical regions.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Rust; leaf scorch.

Host: Arachis spp. (Fabaceae).
