Nomenclature data for Uromyces commelinae

Uromyces commelinae Cooke 1888 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

≡Caeomurus commelinae (Cooke) Kuntze 1898

Notes: Hiratsuka et al. (1992) list some additional synonyms.

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Rust.

Host: Reports from several hosts, especially Commelinaceae.
