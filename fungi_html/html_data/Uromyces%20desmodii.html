Nomenclature data for Uromyces desmodii

Uromyces hedysari-paniculati (Schwein.) Farl. 1879 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

≡ Puccinia hedysari-paniculati Schwein. 1822

= Uromyces desmodii Cooke 1878

= Uredo desmodii-leiocarpi Henn. 1902

= Uromyces desmodii-leiocarpi Henn. 1909

= Phragmidium hedysari Schwein. 1834

Notes: Uromyces solidus B. & C. 1873 listed as a taxonomic synonym by Sydow 1910, but differs according to Saccardo (VII 555).

Distribution: North America, Central America, South America.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Autoecious rust.

Host: Desmodium spp. (Fabaceae).
