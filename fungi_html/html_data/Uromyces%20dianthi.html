Nomenclature data for Uromyces dianthi

Uromyces dianthi Niessl 1872 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

= Nigredo caryophyllina (G. Wint.) Arthur 1921

= Lycoperdon caryophyllinum Schrank 1789

= Uromyces caryophyllinus G. Wint. 1884

= Uredo dianthi Pers. : Pers. 1801

= Uredo dianthicola Har. 1900

= Aecidium euphorbiae-gerardianae E. Fisch. 1904

= Uromyces sinensis Speg. 1879  Note: Year not known.

Notes: There may be a few more obscure synonyms. From Uredo dianthi - These are all anamorph names. Oldest epithet not in correct genus.

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Leaves, stems.

Disease Note: Carnation rust.

Host: Euphorbia spp. (Euphorbiaceae) aecial state, multiple genera of Caryophyllaceae especially Dianthus for uredinial and telial states. Rare reports on Vicia spp. (Fabaceae).?
