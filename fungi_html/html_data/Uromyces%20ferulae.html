Nomenclature data for Uromyces ferulae

Uromyces ferulae Juel 1901 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

Distribution: Asia (Iran, Israel, Pakistan), Europe, Africa (Morocco), Australia.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Rust.

Host: Ferula spp., Heracleum cachemiricum (Apiaceae).

Supporting Literature:
