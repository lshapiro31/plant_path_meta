Nomenclature data for Uromyces graminicola

Puccinia graminicola (Burrill) Demers & Castl. 2017 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

≡ Uromyces graminicola Burrill 1884

≡Caeomurus graminicola (Burrill) Kuntze 1898

≡Nigredo graminicola (Burrill) Arthur 1920

≡Puccinella graminicola (Burrill) Syd. 1922

= Uredo panici Arthur 1902

Distribution: Widespread, especially North America.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Rust.

Host: Aecial stage on Euphorbiaceae, uredinial and telial stages on Panicum spp. (Poaceae).

Supporting Literature:
