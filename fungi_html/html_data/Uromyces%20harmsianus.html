Nomenclature data for Uromyces harmsianus

Uromyces crotalariae (Arthur) J.W. Baxter 1963 [1962] (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

≡ Uropyxis crotalariae Arthur 1918

≡Haplopyxis crotalariae (Arthur) Syd. & P. Syd. 1920 [1919]

= Uredo harmsiana Henn. 1900

= Uromyces harmsianus Doidge 1927

Notes: Oldest epithet not in correct genus.

Distribution: Central America, South America (Brazil), North America (Mexico), Africa.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf rust.

Host: Crotalaria spp. (Fabaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Cummins, G.B. 1978. Rust Fungi on legumes and composites in North America. University of Arizona Press, 424 pages.

Hennen, J.F., Hennen, M.M., and Figueiredo, M.B. 1982. [Index of the rust fungi (Uredinales) of Brazil]. Arch. Inst. Biol. (Sao Paulo), Suppl. 1 49: 1-201.
