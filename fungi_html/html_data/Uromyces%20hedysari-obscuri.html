Nomenclature data for Uromyces hedysari-obscuri

Uromyces hedysari-obscuri (DC.) Carestia & Picc. 1871 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

≡ Puccinia hedysari-obscuri DC. 1806

= Uromyces astragalicola Henn. 1898

= Pucciniola hedysari-obscuri Arthur 1921

Distribution: Asia (China, Japan), Western North America.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Rust.

Host: Hedysarum spp. (Fabaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Arthur, J.C. 1934. Manual of the Rusts in the United States and Canada. Purdue Res. Found., Lafayette, Ind., 438 pages.
