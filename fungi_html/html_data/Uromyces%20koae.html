Nomenclature data for Uromyces koae

Endoraecium koae (Arthur) M. Scholler & Aime 2006 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

≡ Uromyces koae Arthur 1925

≡Atelocauda koae (Arthur) Cummins & Y. Hirats. 1983

≡Racospermyces koae (Arthur) J. Walker 2001

Distribution: Pacific Islands (USA: HI).

Substrate: Juvenile leaves, phyllodes, shoots (Walker 2001).

Disease Note: Autoecious rust.

Host: Acacia koa var. koa and var. latifolia (Fabaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Scholler, M., and Aime, M.C. 2006. On some rust fungi (Uredinales) collected in an Acacia koa-Metrosideros polymorpha woodland, Mauna Loa Road, Big Island, Hawaii. Mycoscience 47: 159-165.

Walker, J. 2001. A revision of the genus Atelocauda (Uredinales) and description of Racospermyces gen. nov. for some rusts of Acacia. Australas. Mycol. 20: 3-29.
