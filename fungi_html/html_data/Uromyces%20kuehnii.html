Nomenclature data for Uromyces kuehnii

Puccinia kuehnii (W. Krüger) E.J. Butler 1914 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

≡ Uromyces kuehnii W. Krüger 1890

≡Caeomurus kuehnii (W. Krüger) Kuntze 1898

= Uredo kuehnii Wakker & Went 1898

Distribution: Asia, North America (USA: FL), Central and South America.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Sugarcane rust.

Host: Saccharum spp., Sclerostachya fusca (Poaceae).
