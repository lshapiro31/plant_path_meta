Nomenclature data for Uromyces laburni

Uromyces laburni (DC.) G.H. Otth 1863 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

≡ Puccinia laburni DC. 1805

= Uredo appendiculata var. genistae-tinctoriae Pers. : Pers. 1801  Note: As U. appendiculata gamma genistae-tinctoriae Pers. 1801. Description of teliospores; teleomorph in anamorph genus (Art. 51.1).

  Variant spelling Uredo appendiculata ? genistae-tinctoriae Pers. 1801  Note: As U. appendiculata gamma genistae-tinctoriae Pers. 1801.

= Uromyces caraganicola Henn. 1901

= Uromyces coluteae Arthur 1910  Note: American name given for introduced Uromyces laburni (DC.) Otth. (Cummins 1978).

= Uromyces genistae Fuckel 1869  Note: Not Schroeter 1889.

= Uromyces genistae-tinctoriae (Pers.) G. Winter 1880  Note: Basionym Uredo appendiculata var. genistae-tinctoriae Pers. 1801; no priority at this rank (Art. 11.2).

= Uredo laburni DC. 1815

[= Uromyces laburni Fuckel 1869 - illegitimate later homonym, not included in search]  Note: Not (DC) Otth 1863.

= Uromyces laburni f. sp. genistae-tinctoriae (Pers.) Kobel ex Gäum.  Note: Boerema & Verhoeven (Neth. J. Pl. Pathol. 78(1):52. 1972) provided nomenclatural notes.

Notes: Not Fuckel 1869. Synonymy follows Hiratsuka (1992) and Boerema (1979). Wilson & Henderson (1966) and Hylander et al. (1953) place U. laburni under Uromyces pisi.

Distribution: Europe, Asia, Africa (reported by Hiratsuka 1992). Reports of Uromyces coluteae in USA (KS) are based on an introduction; fungus was not found subsequently.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Rust.

Host: Aecial host: Euphorbia spp. Telial host: Various genera of Fabaceae.
