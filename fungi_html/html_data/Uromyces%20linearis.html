Nomenclature data for Uromyces linearis

Puccinia peradeniyae Demers & Castl. 2017 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

≡ Uromyces linearis Berk. & Broome 1875

≡Caeomurus linearis (Berk. & Broome) Kuntze 1898

≡Puccinella linearis (Berk. & Broome) Syd. 1922

Notes: Nom. nov. - non Puccinia linearis Röhl. (1813).

Distribution: Widespread, especially Africa, Asia.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Rust.

Host: Panicum spp. (Poaceae).
