Nomenclature data for Uromyces lupinicola

Uromyces lupinicola Bubák 1902 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

    Variant spelling Uromyces lupinicolus Bubák 1902

[= Uromyces lupini Sacc. 1873 - illegitimate later homonym, not included in search]  Note: Synonym pro parte. Not Berk. & Curtis 1858.

Distribution: Europe, Africa (Morocco, unconfirmed).

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Rust.

Host: Lupinus spp. (Fabaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Guyot, A.L. 1957. Les Rouilles des Legumineuses Fourrageres et Spontanees. Editions Paul Lechevalier, Paris, 647 pages.
