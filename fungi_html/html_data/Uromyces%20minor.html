Nomenclature data for Uromyces minor

Uromyces minor J. Schröt. 1887 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

≡Caeomurus minor Kuntze 1898  Note: Genus rejected in favor of Uromyces.

Notes: Pucciniola oblonga Arthur 1921 and Caeomurus oblongus O. Kuntze 1898 listed by Hiratsuka as synonyms, but probably due to confusion surrounding Uromyces oblongus Vize 1877, see Guyot 1957, p. 348.

Distribution: North America, Europe, Asia, New Zealand. Report from South America (Chile) could be Uromyces oblongus Vize based on host plant.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Autoecious rust.

Host: Trifolium spp. (Fabaceae).
