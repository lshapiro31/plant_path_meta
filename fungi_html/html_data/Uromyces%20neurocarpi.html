Nomenclature data for Uromyces neurocarpi

Uromyces neurocarpi Dietel 1895 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

= Uromyces erythrinae Lagerh. 1910

= Uromyces insularis Arthur 1906

= Uromyces rostratus Henn. 1896

Distribution: Central America (Mexico to Panama), Caribbean, South America.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Rust.

Host: Clitoria cajanifolia (= Neurocarpum cajanifolia) and other Clitoria spp. (Fabaceae).

Supporting Literature:
