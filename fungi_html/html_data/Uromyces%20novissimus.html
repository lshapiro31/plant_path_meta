Nomenclature data for Uromyces novissimus

Uromyces novissimus Speg. 1880 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

≡Caeomurus novissimus (Speg.) Kuntze 1898

= Uromyces appelianus Gassner 1922

= Uromyces cajaponiae Henn. 1896

= Uredo novissima Speg. 1898

Notes: Autoecious.

Distribution: Asia (India), North and South America.

Disease Note: Rust.

Host: Cucurbitaceae.
