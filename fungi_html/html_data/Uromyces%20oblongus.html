Nomenclature data for Uromyces oblongus

Uromyces oblongus Vize 1877 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

≡Pucciniola oblonga (Vize) Arthur 1921  Note: Listed by Arthur (1934) as a synonym of Uromyces minor not Uromyces oblongus Vize.

≡Caeomurus oblongus Kuntze 1898  Note: Genus rejected in favor of Uromyces.

Notes: Confusion over host identification resulted in listing of Uromyces minor J. Schröt. as a taxonomic synonym; see Guyot 1957, p. 348.

Distribution: North America (USA: CA, type).

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Autoecious rust.

Host: Medicago hispida (misidentified as clover, Trifolium sp. and as burr cloves, Lappa sp.).

Supporting Literature:

Guyot, A.L. 1957. Les Rouilles des Legumineuses Fourrageres et Spontanees. Editions Paul Lechevalier, Paris, 647 pages.
