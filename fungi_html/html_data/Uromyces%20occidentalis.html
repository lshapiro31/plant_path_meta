Nomenclature data for Uromyces occidentalis

Uromyces occidentalis Dietel 1903 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

≡Nigredo occidentalis Arthur 1912  Note: Genus rejected in favor of Uromyces.

= Uromyces rugosus Arthur 1905

= Nigredo substriata Arthur 1912  Note: Genus rejected in favor of Uromyces.

= Uromyces substriatus Syd. & P. Syd. 1906

Distribution: North America (Western USA, Mexico). Also reported from Africa (Nigeria) with different host genus.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Heteroecious rust.

Host: Aecial host: Euphorbia spp. (Euphorbiaceae); telial host: Lupinus spp. (Fabaceae). Reported from Nigeria on Crotalaria retusa.

Supporting Literature:

Arthur, J.C. 1934. Manual of the Rusts in the United States and Canada. Purdue Res. Found., Lafayette, Ind., 438 pages.

Cummins, G.B. 1978. Rust Fungi on legumes and composites in North America. University of Arizona Press, 424 pages.

Guyot, A.L. 1957. Les Rouilles des Legumineuses Fourrageres et Spontanees. Editions Paul Lechevalier, Paris, 647 pages.
