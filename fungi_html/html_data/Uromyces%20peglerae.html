Nomenclature data for Uromyces peglerae

Uromyces peglerae Pole-Evans ex Syd. & P. Syd. 1914 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

    Variant spelling Uromyces pegleriae Pole-Evans ex Syd. & P. Syd. 1914  Note: Incorrect spelling found in some lit.

≡Uromyces peglerae var. peglerae Pole-Evans ex Syd. & P. Syd. 1963

= Uromyces digitariae-adscendentis Qing-zhi Wang 1965

= Uredo paspali-longiflori Petch 1917

= Uredo tacita Arthur 1933

Notes: Includes the type variety.

Distribution: Widespread.

Disease Note: Rust.

Host: Poaceae, especially Digitaria spp.
