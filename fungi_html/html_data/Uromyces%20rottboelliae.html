Nomenclature data for Uromyces rottboelliae

Uromyces vossiae Barclay 1890 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

≡Caeomurus vossiae (Barclay) Kuntze 1898

= Uromyces rottboelliae Arthur 1902

    ≡ Puccinella rottboelliae (Arthur) Syd. 1922

= Uromyces superfluus P. Syd. & Syd. 1910

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Rust.

Host: Several taxa of Poaceae.

Supporting Literature:
