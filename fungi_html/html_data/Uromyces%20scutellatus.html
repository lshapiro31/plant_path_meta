Nomenclature data for Uromyces scutellatus

Uromyces scutellatus (Schrank) Lév. 1847 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

≡ Lycoperdon scutellatum Schrank 1789

≡Uredo scutellata (Schrank) Pers. 1801

≡Aecidium scutellatum (Schrank) J.F. Gmel. 1792

≡Caeoma scutellatum (Schrank) Schltdl. 1824

≡Coeomurus scutellatus (Schrank) Kuntze 1898

Notes: Uromyces scutellatus listed by Index Fungorum as a nom. inval. (Art. 35.2), but examination of Léveillé's work indicates that the combination appears to be validly made.

Distribution: Europe.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Autoecious rust.

Host: Euphorbiaceae spp. (Euphorbiaceae).
