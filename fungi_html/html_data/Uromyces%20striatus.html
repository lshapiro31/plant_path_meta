Nomenclature data for Uromyces striatus

Uromyces striatus var. striatus J. Schröt. 1870 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

≡Uromyces striatus J. Schröt. 1870 [1869]

= Uredo fabae var. medicaginis-falcatae DC. 1815

= Uredo lupulinae Speg. 1912

= Uredo medicaginicola Speg. 1899

= Uredo medicaginis Speg. 1902

= Uromyces medicaginis Pass. 1872

    ≡ Uromyces striatus var. medicaginis (Pass.) Arthur 1934

    ≡ Nigredo medicaginis (Pass.) Arthur 1912

= Uromyces medicaginis-falcatae G. Winter 1884

Notes: From Uredo fabae var. medicaginis-falcatae - Uredo fabae = Uromyces viciae-fabae. Teleomorph? From Uredo lupulinae - Listed as synonym by Guyot 1957.

Distribution: Cosmopolitan. From Uredo lupulinae - South America. From Uredo medicaginicola - Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Living leaves. From Uredo medicaginicola - Living leaves.

Disease Note: Rust. From Uredo medicaginicola - Rust.

Host: Heteroecious; aecial host - Euphorbia spp.; telial host - Medicago spp., possibly also Trifolium spp., Hosakia spp., depending on species concept (some controversy exists). From Uredo medicaginicola - Heteroecious; aecial host - Euphorbia spp.; telial host - Medicago spp., possibly also Trifolium spp., Hosakia spp., depending on species concept (some controversy exists).
