Nomenclature data for Uromyces triquetrus

Uromyces hyperici-frondosi (Schwein.) Arthur 1883 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

≡ Aecidium hyperici-frondosi Schwein. 1822

≡Caeomurus hyperici-frondosi (Schwein.) Arthur 1899

≡Nigredo hyperici-frondosi (Schwein.) Arthur 1906

= Trichobasis hyperici W.R. Gerard 1873

= Uredo hyperici Spreng. 1827

= Uromyces hyperici M.A. Curtis 1867

= Uromyces triquetrus Cooke 1862

  Variant spelling Uromyces triquetra Cooke 1862

  Variant spelling Uromyces triqueter Cooke 1862  Note: Saccardo's Syll. fung. VII: 539

Notes: Synonomy follows Arthur 1934 and Hiratsuka 1973. The correct author citation for Uromyces hyperici-frondosi is (Schwein.) Arthur 1883, and as this is based on Aecidium hyperici-frondosi Schwein. 1822, it must be used over the newer Uromyces triquetrus Cooke 1862. Sources attributing Uredo hyperici to Schwein. 1854 are apparently in error, as no reference to that name can be found anywhere in the cited publication; if validly published, it would be an illegitimate later homonym of U. hyperici Spreng. 1827.

Distribution: Asia (China, Japan, Taiwan), North America, South America.

Substrate: Leaves, stem.

Disease Note: Rust.

Host: Hypericum spp. (Hypericaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Arthur, J.C. 1934. Manual of the Rusts in the United States and Canada. Purdue Res. Found., Lafayette, Ind., 438 pages.
