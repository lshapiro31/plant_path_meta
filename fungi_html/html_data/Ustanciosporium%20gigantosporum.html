Nomenclature data for Ustanciosporium gigantosporum

Ustanciosporium gigantosporum (Liro) M. Piepenbr. 2000 (Ustilaginomycetes, Ustilaginales)

≡ Cintractia gigantospora Liro 1934

≡Ustilago gigantospora (Liro) Lehtola 1942

= Ustilago rhynchosporae Saut. 1854

Distribution: Widespread, especially northern hemisphere.

Disease Note: Smut.

Host: Rhynchospora (Cyperaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Vanky, K. 2010. The smut fungi (Ustilaginomycotina) of Rhynchospora (Cyperaceae). Mycol. Balcan. 7: 93-104.

Vanky, K. 2012. Smut Fungi of the World. APS Press, St. Paul, Minnesota, 1458 pages.
