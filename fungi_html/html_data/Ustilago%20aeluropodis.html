Nomenclature data for Ustilago aeluropodis

Crozalsiella aeluropodis (Trotter) Maire 1917 (Ustilaginomycetes, Ustilaginales)

    Variant spelling Crozalsiella aeluropi (Trotter) Maire 1917

≡ Sphacelotheca aeluropodis Trotter 1913

      Variant spelling Sphacelotheca aeluropi Trotter 1913

≡Ustilago aeluropodis (Trotter) Vánky 1985

  Variant spelling Ustilago aeluropi (Trotter) Vánky 1985

= Sorosporium aeluropodis S. Ahmad 1956

Notes: From Sorosporium aeluropodis - As 'aeluropidis'. From Ustilago aeluropodis - There has been some confusion about the correct form of the genitive for Aeluropus.

Distribution: Africa, Eurasia.

Substrate: Culms.

Disease Note: Smut.

Host: Aeluropus spp. (Poaceae).
