Nomenclature data for Ustilago affinis

Ustilago affinis Ellis & Everh. 1893 (Ustilaginomycetes, Ustilaginales)

= Ustilago americana Speg. 1899

= Ustilago microspora J. Schröt. & Henn. 1896

    ≡ Sphacelotheca microspora (J. Schröt. & Henn.) Cif. 1932

    ≡ Sporisorium microsporum (J. Schröt. & Henn.) M. Piepenbr. 1999

[= Ustilago stenotaphri Massee 1899 - illegitimate later homonym, not included in search]  Note: Not McAlpine 1895 nec Henn. 1898. Nom. illeg.

Notes: From Ustilago microspora - Not the illegitimate later homonym Ustilago microspora (Speg.) Liro 1924.

Distribution: Cosmopolitan in warmer regions.

Substrate: Spikelets.

Disease Note: Smut.

Host: Stenotaphrum (Poaceae).
