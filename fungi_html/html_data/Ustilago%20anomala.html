Nomenclature data for Ustilago anomala

Bauhinus anomalus (J. Kunze ex G. Winter) R.T. Moore 1992 (Urediniomycetes, Microbotryales)

≡ Ustilago anomala J. Kunze ex G. Winter 1881

≡Microbotryum anomalum (J. Kunze ex G. Winter) Vánky 1998

= Ustilago carnea Liro 1921

    ≡ Ustilago anomala var. carnea (Liro) Hirschh. 1947

[= Ustilago pallida J. Schröt. 1877 - illegitimate later homonym, not included in search]  Note: Not Körn. 1877. Nom. illeg.

= Ustilago raciborskiana Siemaszko & Wróbl. 1933

Notes: (J. Kunze ex G. Winter) Denchev & R.T. Moore 2006 was given as a new combination and (J. Kunze ex G. Winter) R.T. Moore 1992 was listed as invalid. (Denchev et al. 2006).

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Disease Note: Smut.

Host: Especially Polygonaceae.
