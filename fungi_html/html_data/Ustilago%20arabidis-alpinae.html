Nomenclature data for Ustilago arabidis-alpinae

Thecaphora thlaspeos (Beck) Vánky 2004 (Ustilaginomycetes, Ustilaginales)

≡ Tilletia thlaspeos Beck 1886

≡Bauhinus thlaspeos (Beck) Denchev 1997

≡Tothiella thlaspeos (Beck) Vánky 1999

≡Ustilago thlaspeos (Beck) Lagerh. 1897

= Ustilago arabidis-alpinae Liro 1939

= Ustilago cardamines Liro 1939

= Ustilago seminum Juel 1894

Distribution: Eurasia.

Substrate: Systemic: attacking seeds.

Disease Note: Smut.

Host: Brassicaceae.
