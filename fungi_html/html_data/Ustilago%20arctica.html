Nomenclature data for Ustilago arctica

Orphanomyces arcticus (Rostr.) Savile 1974 (Ustilaginomycetes, Ustilaginales)

≡ Tilletia arctica Rostr. 1886

≡Cintractia arctica (Rostr.) Lagerh. 1895

≡Ustilago arctica (Rostr.) B. Lindeb. 1959

Distribution: Northern hemisphere.

Substrate: Leaves.

Disease Note: Smut.

Host: Carex (Cyperaceae).
