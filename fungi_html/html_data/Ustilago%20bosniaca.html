Nomenclature data for Ustilago bosniaca

Bauhinus bosniacus (Beck) Denchev 1997 (Urediniomycetes, Microbotryales)

≡ Ustilago bosniaca Beck 1894

≡Sphacelotheca bosniaca (Beck) Maire 1909 [1907]

≡Microbotryum bosniacum (G. Beck) Vánky 1998

= Sphacelotheca alpina Schellenb. 1907

= Ustilago punctata G.P. Clinton 1906

Distribution: Northern hemisphere.

Disease Note: Smut.

Host: Polygonum spp. (Polygonaceae).
