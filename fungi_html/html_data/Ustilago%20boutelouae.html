Nomenclature data for Ustilago boutelouae

Macalpinomyces spermophorus (Berk. & M.A. Curtis ex de Toni) Vánky 2003 (Ustilaginomycetes, Ustilaginales)

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Ovaries.

Disease Note: Smut.

Host: Several taxa of Poaceae.
