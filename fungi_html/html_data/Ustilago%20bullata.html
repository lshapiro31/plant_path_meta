Nomenclature data for Ustilago bullata

Ustilago bullata Berk. 1855 (Ustilaginomycetes, Ustilaginales)

= Ustilago asprellae G. Cunn. 1930

Notes: In the strict sense, the host range and distribution of this species is more limited (Vanky and McKenzie 2002, Vanky and Shivas 2008, Vanky 2011). See also Ustilago bromivora.

Distribution: New Zealand.

Substrate: Spikelets of inflorescence.

Disease Note: Smut.

Host: Elymus spp. (Poaceae).
