Nomenclature data for Ustilago commelinae

Bauerago commelinae (Kom.) Denchev 2003 (Urediniomycetes, Microbotryales)

≡ Tilletia commelinae Kom. 1899

≡Ustilago commelinae (Kom.) Zundel 1953

Distribution: Northern hemisphere.

Disease Note: Smut.

Host: Commelina spp. (Commelinaceae).
