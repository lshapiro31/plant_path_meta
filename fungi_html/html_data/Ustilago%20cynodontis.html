Nomenclature data for Ustilago cynodontis

Ustilago cynodontis (Henn.) Henn. 1893 (Ustilaginomycetes, Ustilaginales)

≡ Ustilago segetum var. cynodontis Henn. 1892

= Sporisorium agropyri Bag & D.K. Agarwal 2001

= Ustilago carbo var. cynodontis Pass. 1871  Note: Vanky (2011) lists the rank in parentheses.

  ≡Ustilago cynodontis (Pass.) Curzi 1927  Note: Not (Henn.) Henn. 1893. Nomen illeg.

= Ustilago nebrodensis Gonz. Frag. 1919

= Ustilago pallida Körn. 1877  Note: Nom. nov. Later homonyms exist.

  [≡ Uredo digitariae Rabenh. 1847 - illegitimate later homonym, not included in search]  Note: Not Kunze 1830. Nom. illeg. fide Vanky (2011).

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Inflorescences.

Disease Note: Smut.

Host: Cynodon spp. (Poaceae).
