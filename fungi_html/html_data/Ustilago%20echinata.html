Nomenclature data for Ustilago echinata

Ustilago echinata J. Schröt. 1869 (Ustilaginomycetes, Ustilaginales)

= Ustilago arctagrostis Roiv. 1953  Note: Nom. inval. via no Latin fide Vanky (2011).

= Ustilago arthurii H.H. Hume 1902

  Variant spelling Ustilago arthuri H.H. Hume 1902

= Ustilago baldingerae Vestergr. 1899  Note: Nom. nov. in Jan.

    ≡ Ustilago vestergreni Sacc. & P. Syd. 1899  Note: Nom. nov. in Aug. Nom. illeg. via superfluous.

  [≡ Ustilago verrucosa Vestergr. 1897 - illegitimate later homonym, not included in search]  Note: Not J. Schröt. 1896. Nom. illeg.

= Ustilago scolochloae Griffiths 1904

Distribution: Northern hemisphere.

Disease Note: Smut.

Host: Poaceae.

Supporting Literature:

Vanky, K. 2012. Smut Fungi of the World. APS Press, St. Paul, Minnesota, 1458 pages.
