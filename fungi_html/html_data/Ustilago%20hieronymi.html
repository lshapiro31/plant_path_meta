Ustilago buchloes Ellis & Tracy 1890 (Ustilaginomycetes, Ustilaginales)

= Ustilago coloradensis Zundel 1933

= Ustilago filifera Norton 1896

= Ustilago hieronymi J. Schröt. 1896

= Ustilago hieronymi var. insularis Cif. 1933

= Ustilago pseudohieronymi Zundel 1933

Notes: Sensu lato.

Distribution: Widespread, especially the Americas.

Disease Note: Smut.

Host: Several taxa of Poaceae.
