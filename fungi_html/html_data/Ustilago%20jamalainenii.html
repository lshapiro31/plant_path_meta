Notes: Sensu lato. The name bringing epithet comes from the unranked taxon Ustilago carbo alpha vulgaris d. bromivora Tul. & C. Tul. 1847. Excluded from Ustilago bullata sensu lato, but more work may reveal sevaral taxa in a species complex (Vanky 2011).

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Spikelets.

Disease Note: Smut.

Host: Poaceae.
