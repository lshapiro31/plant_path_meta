≡Yenia kusanoi (Syd. & P. Syd.) T.N. Liou 1949

= Ustilago arundinellae-hirtae S. Ito 1935

= Ustilago consimilis Syd. 1924

= Ustilago morobiana Zundel 1944

= Sphacelotheca nankinensis Zundel 1939

Disease Note: Smut.

Host: Poaceae especially Miscanthus spp, Saccharum spp.
