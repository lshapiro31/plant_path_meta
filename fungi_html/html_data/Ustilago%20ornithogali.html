Nomenclature data for Ustilago ornithogali

Vankya ornithogali (J.C. Schmidt & Kunze) Ershad 2000 (Ustilaginomycetes, Urocystales)

≡ Uredo ornithogali J.C. Schmidt & Kunze 1819

≡Ustilago ornithogali (J.C. Schmidt & Kunze) Magnus 1875  Note: It is unclear as to which name has priority and which is an isonym.

  ≡Ustilago ornithogali (J.C. Schmidt & Kunze) Kühn 1875

= Ustilago heterospora Niessl 1872

= Caeoma ornithogali Schltdl. 1824

= Ustilago umbrina J. Schröt. 1869

Distribution: Africa, Asia, Europe.

Substrate: Leaves, bracts.

Disease Note: Smut.

Host: Gagea (Liliaceae).
