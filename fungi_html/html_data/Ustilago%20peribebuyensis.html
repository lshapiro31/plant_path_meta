Nomenclature data for Ustilago peribebuyensis

Cintractia axicola (Berk.) Cornu 1883 (Ustilaginomycetes, Ustilaginales)

≡ Ustilago axicola Berk. 1852

= Cintractia fimbristylidis-kagiensis Sawada ex S. Ito 1935  Note: This name may have been validly published in 1922.

= Cintractia fimbristylidis-kagiensis var. fukiensis L. Ling & T.L. Chen 1945

= Ustilago fimbristylis Thüm. 1876  Note: Some advocate the use of the orthographic variant 'fimbristylidis'.

= Cintractia mundkurii S. Chowdhury 1944  Note: As 'mundkuri'.

= Ustilago peribebuyensis Speg. 1884

    ≡ Cintractia peribebuyensis (Speg.) Speg. 1888  Note: (Speg.) Sawada 1922 is an isonym.

    ≡ Cintractia axicola f. peribebuyensis (Sawada) Zambett. 1980 [1979]

= Cintractia peribebuyensis var. major Pavgi & Mundk. 1949 [1948]

= Cintractia pilulifera L. Ling & T.L. Chen 1945

= Cintractia suedae Sawada ex S. Ito 1935  Note: This name may have been validly published earlier, perhaps 1922.

    ≡ Anthracoidea suedae (Sawada ex S. Ito) Vánky 1987 [1986]

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Floral axis.

Disease Note: Smut.

Host: Cyperaceae.
