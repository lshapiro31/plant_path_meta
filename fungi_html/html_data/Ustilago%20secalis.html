Nomenclature data for Ustilago secalis

Tilletia secalis (Corda) Körn. 1872 (Ustilaginomycetes, Tilletiales)

≡ Uredo secalis Corda 1848

[= Tilletia secalis J.G. Kühn 1876 - illegitimate later homonym, not included in search]  Note: Not (Corda) Körn. 1872. Nom. illeg.

Uredo secalis Rabenh. 1849

  ≡Ustilago secalis (Rabenh.) J.G. Kühn 1876

Notes: As 'secales'. Isonym of (Corda) J.Schröt. 1877. Nomenclatural treatment follows Vanky (2011) without verification.

Distribution: Widespread.

Disease Note: Smut.

Host: Secale (Poaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Vanky, K. 2012. Smut Fungi of the World. APS Press, St. Paul, Minnesota, 1458 pages.
