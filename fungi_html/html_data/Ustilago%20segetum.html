  ≡Ustilago segetum (Bull.) Roussel 1806  Note: Nom. rej.

    ≡ Uredo segetum (Bull.) Pers. 1797  Note: Nom. rej.

Notes: Nom. cons. over Ustilago hordei Bref. 1888. See Vanky and McKenzie (2002), Vanky and Shivas (2008), and Vanky (2011) for some additional synonyms.

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Spikelets of inflorescence.

Disease Note: Smut.

Host: Arrhenatherum, Avena, Hordeum, Triticum and other grasses (Poaceae).
