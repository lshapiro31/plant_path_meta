Nomenclature data for Ustilago subnitens

Aurantiosporium subnitens (J. Schröt. & Henn.) M. Piepenbr., Vánky & Oberw. 1996 (Urediniomycetes, Microbotryales)

≡ Ustilago subnitens J. Schröt. & Henn. 1896

≡Cintractia subnitens (J. Schröt. & Henn.) Castell. & Cif. 1938

= Aurantiosporium colombianum M. Piepenbr. 2002

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Spikelets.

Disease Note: Smut.

Host: Scleria (Cyperaceae).
