Nomenclature data for Ustilago verruculosa

Conidiosporomyces verruculosus (Wakef.) Vánky 1993 (Ustilaginomycetes, Tilletiales)

≡ Ustilago verruculosa Wakef. 1922

≡Tilletia verruculosa (Wakef.) M. Piepenbr. 2004

Distribution: Africa.

Substrate: Ovaries of inflorescence.

Disease Note: Smut.

Host: Setaria spp. (Poaceae).
