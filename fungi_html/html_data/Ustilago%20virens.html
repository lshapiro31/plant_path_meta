Nomenclature data for Ustilago virens

Ustilaginoidea virens (Cooke) Takah. 1896 (Ascomycetes, Incertae sedis)

≡ Ustilago virens Cooke 1878

≡Sphacelotheca virens (Cooke) Omori 1896

= Ustilaginoidea albicans Shu Wang & J.K. Bai 1997

= Tilletia oryzae Pat. 1887

    ≡ Ustilaginoidea oryzae (Pat.) Bref. 1895

= Claviceps virens M. Sakurai ex Nakata 1934

    ≡ Villosiclava virens (M. Sakurai ex Nakata) E. Tanaka & C. Tanaka 2008

Distribution: Worldwide in rice-growing regions.

Substrate: Inflorescence/infructescense.

Disease Note: False smut.

Host: Oryza sativa, Zea mays, Brachiaria brizantha (Poaceae).
