Nomenclature data for Ustilago williamsii

Tranzscheliella williamsii (Griffiths) Dingley & Versluys 1977 (Ustilaginomycetes, Ustilaginales)

≡ Sorosporium williamsii Griffiths 1902

≡Ustilago williamsii (Griffiths) Lavrov 1936  Note: Isonym of (Griffiths) G.W. Fisch. & Hirschh 1945.

= Ustilago appendiculata Speg. 1909

    ≡ Tranzscheliella appendiculata (Speg.) Lavrov 1936

= Tranzscheliella otophora Lavrov 1936

    ≡ Ustilago otophora (Lavrov) Gutner 1941

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Upper internodes, aborted inflorescence branches.

Disease Note: Smut.

Host: Poaceae, especially Oryzopsis.

Supporting Literature:
