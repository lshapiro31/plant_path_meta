Nomenclature data for Uwebraunia lateralis

Uwebraunia dekkeri (de Hoog & Hijwegen) Crous 2012 (Ascomycetes, Mycosphaerellales)

≡ Dissoconium dekkeri de Hoog & Hijwegen 1991

= Mycosphaerella lateralis Crous & M.J. Wingf. 1996

= Uwebraunia lateralis Crous & M.J. Wingf. 1996

= Mycosphaerella shimabarensis H.C. Evans & P.F. Cannon 2009

Distribution: Widespread in temperate and tropical regions.

Substrate: Leaves, needles and fruits.

Disease Note: Leaf and fruit spot; occurs in association with related fungi but also a primary pathogen.

Host: Multiple species in various families.
