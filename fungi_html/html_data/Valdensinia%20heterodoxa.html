Nomenclature data for Valdensinia heterodoxa

Valdensia heterodoxa Peyronel 1923 (Ascomycetes, Helotiales)

= Asterobolus gaultheriae Redhead & P.W. Perrin 1972

= Valdensinia heterodoxa Peyronel 1953

Distribution: Widespread in north temperate regions.

Substrate: Living leaves.

Disease Note: Leaf spot.

Host: Members of the Ericaceae and other families.

Supporting Literature:

Dzieciol, R., Mirzwa-Mroz, E., Zielinska, E., Winska-Krysiak, M., and Wakulinski, W. 2014. Valdensinia heterodoxa peyronel as a new pathogen of blueberry in Poland. Pl. Dis. 98(5): 688.
