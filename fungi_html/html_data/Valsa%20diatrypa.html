Nomenclature data for Valsa diatrypa

Valsella diatrypa (Fr.) Z. Urb. 1957 (Ascomycetes, Diaporthales)

≡ Sphaeria diatrypa Fr. 1823

≡Engizostoma diatrypa (Fr.) Kuntze 1898

≡Valsa diatrypa (Fr.) Fr. 1849

Distribution: Asia (Japan), Europe.

Substrate: Shoots, twigs, branches.

Disease Note: Probably saprobic.

Host: Alnus glutinosa, A. incana, Betula sp., Corylus avellana (Betulaceae), Crataegus oxyacantha, Prunus domestica, P. spinosa (Rosaceae), Eleagnus angustifolia (Eleagnaceae), Populus tremula (Salicaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Hayova, V.P., and Minter, D.W. 2012. Valsella diatrypa. I.M.I. Descr. Fungi Bact. 1929: 1-4.
