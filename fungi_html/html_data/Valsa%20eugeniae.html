Nomenclature data for Valsa eugeniae

Cytospora eugeniae (Nutman & F.M. Roberts) G.C. Adams & Rossman 2015 (Ascomycetes, Diaporthales)

≡ Valsa eugeniae Nutman & F.M. Roberts 1953

Distribution: Australia, Asia (Indonesia, West Malaysia), Africa (Tanzania).

Substrate: Bark.

Disease Note: Associated with sudden death disease of clove trees.

Host: Eucalyptus grandis, Eugenia caryophyllus = Syzygium aromaticum (Myrtaceae, cloves), Tibouchina spp. (Melastomataceae).

Supporting Literature:

Adams, G.C., Wingfield, M.J., Common, R., and Roux, J. 2005. Phylogenetic relationships and morphology of Cytospora species and related teleomorphs (Ascomycota, Diaporthales, Valsaceae) from Eucalyptus. Stud. Mycol. 52: 1-144.
