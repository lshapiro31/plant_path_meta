Nomenclature data for Valsa friesii

Valsa friesii (Duby) Fuckel 1870 [1869] (Ascomycetes, Diaporthales)

≡ Sphaeria friesii Duby 1830

≡Engizostoma friesii (Duby) Kuntze 1898

= Cytospora dubyi Sacc. 1884

= Valsa juniperina Cooke 1878

= Cytospora pinastri Fr. : Fr. 1823

Notes: From Cytospora pinastri - Some consider this a synonym of Cytospora abietis.

Distribution: From Cytospora pinastri - Circumboreal on conifers.

Substrate: From Cytospora pinastri - Branches, needles and twigs.

Disease Note: From Cytospora pinastri - Blight, canker.

Host: From Cytospora pinastri - Primarily on Pinaceae.

Supporting Literature:

Hayova, V.P., and Minter, D.W. 2012. Valsa friesii. I.M.I. Descr. Fungi Bact. 1924: 1-8.
