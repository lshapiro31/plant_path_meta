Nomenclature data for Valsa germanica

Cytospora germanica Sacc. 1884 (Ascomycetes, Diaporthales)

= Valsa germanica Nitschke 1870

Distribution: Asia (China, Japan, Europe.

Substrate: Wood.

Disease Note: Canker.

Host: Salicaceae.
