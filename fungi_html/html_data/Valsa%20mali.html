Nomenclature data for Valsa mali

Cytospora mali Grove 1935 (Ascomycetes, Diaporthales)

= Valsa mali Miyabe & G. Yamada 1915

Notes: On Pyrus malus causing a canker. Grove (British stem and leaf fungi. Vol. I. pp. 279-280. 1935) enumerates on the specimen identified in the United States by Stevens.

Distribution: England; United States.

Disease Note: From Valsa mali - Canker.

Supporting Literature:

Adams Jr., G.C., Surve-Iyer, R.S., and Iezzoni, A.F. 2002. Ribosomal DNA sequence divergence and group I introns within the Leucostoma species L. cinctum, L. persoonii, and L parapersoonii sp. nov., Ascomycetes that cause Cytospora canker of fruit trees. Mycologia 94: 947-967.
