Nomenclature data for Valsa pini

Cytospora pini Desm. 1843 (Ascomycetes, Diaporthales)

= Sphaeria pini Alb. & Schwein. 1805

  ≡Valsa pini (Alb. & Schwein.) Fr. 1849

Distribution: North temperate regions.

Substrate: Branches, twigs.

Host: Cupressaceae and Pinaceae.
