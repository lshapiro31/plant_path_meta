Nomenclature data for Valsa pruinosa

Cytospora pruinosa (Fr. : Fr.) Sacc. 1879 (Ascomycetes, Diaporthales)

≡ Sphaeria pruinosa Fr. 1817

= Cytospora annularis Ellis & Everh. 1897

= Cytospora chionanthi Ellis & Everh. 1894

= Valsa chionanthi Ellis & Everh. 1894

    ≡ Engizostoma chionanthi (Ellis & Everh.) Kuntze 1898

= Sphaeria cypri Tul. 1856

    ≡ Valsa cypri (Tul.) Tul. & C. Tul. 1863

= Valsa fraxinina Peck 1884

= Sphaeria ligustri Schwein. 1832

    ≡ Valsa ligustri (Schwein.) J. Schröt. 1897

Distribution: North America, Europe.

Substrate: Wood, branches and twigs.

Disease Note: Canker, dieback.

Host: Fraxinus spp. and other Oleaceae.
