Nomenclature data for Valsa punica

Cytospora punicae Sacc. 1878 (Ascomycetes, Diaporthales)

    Variant spelling Cytospora punica Sacc. 1878

= Valsa punica Sacc. & F. Sacc. 1878

Distribution: Central Asia, Africa (Libya, South Africa), Europe, North America (USA: CA).

Substrate: Branches, trunk.

Disease Note: Canker, dieback, yellowing.

Host: Punica granatum (Lythraceae).

Supporting Literature:
