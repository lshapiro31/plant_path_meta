Nomenclature data for Venturia nashicola

Venturia nashicola S. Tanaka & S. Yamam. 1964 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

= Fusicladium nashicola K. Schub. & U. Braun 2003

Distribution: Asia.

Substrate: Living leaves, fruit.

Disease Note: Scab, leaf spot/lesions.

Host: Pyrus spp. (Rosaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Ishii, H., and Yanase, H. 2000. Venturia nashicola, the scab fungus of Japanese and Chinese pears: a species distinct from V. pirina. Mycol. Res. 104: 755-759.

Schubert, K., Ritschel, A., and Braun, U. 2003. A monograph of Fusicladium s. lat. (Hyphomycetes). Schlechtendalia 9: 1-132.
