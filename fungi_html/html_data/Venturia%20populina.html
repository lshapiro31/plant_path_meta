Nomenclature data for Venturia populina

Venturia populina (Vuill.) L. Fabricius 1902 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

≡ Didymosphaeria populina Vuill. 1889

= Pollaccia elegans Servazzi 1939  Note: From Fusicladium elegans - Taxonomic synonyms p.p.: Fusicladium tremulae A.B. Frank 1883 p.p.; Napicladium tremulae auct. p.p.; Stigmina radiosa auct. p.p.; Fusicladium radiosum auct. p.p.

= Fusicladium radiosum var. balsamiferae Davis 1922

  Variant spelling Fusicladium radiosa var. balsamiferae Davis 1922

    ≡ Pollaccia balsamiferae (Davis) M. Morelet 1972

Distribution: Temperate North America; Europe.

Substrate: Leaves, shoots.

Disease Note: Leaf and shoot blight.
