Nomenclature data for Vermicularia acicola

Neopyrenochaeta acicola (Moug. & Lév.) Valenzuela-Lopez, Crous, Stchigel, Guarro & Cano 2017 (, )

≡ Vermicularia acicola Moug. & Lév. 1848

≡Pyrenochaeta acicola (Moug. & Lév.) Sacc. 1884

≡Phoma leveillei var. leveillei Boerema & G.J. Bollen 1975

= Pyrenochaeta anthyllidis Manoliu & Mítítíuc 1976

= Pyrenochaeta calligoni Kravtzev 1961

= Pyrenochaeta lupini Sibilia 1930

= Pyrenochaeta oryzae Shirai ex I. Miyake 1910

= Pyrenochaeta phlogis Massee 1907

= Pyrenochaeta spinaciae Verona & Negru 1966

Notes: From Pyrenochaeta lupini - Year changed from 1929. From Pyrenochaeta oryzae - Author changed from I. Miyake From Vermicularia acicola - Author changed from Lev. From Pyrenochaeta acicola - Author changed from (Lev.) Sacc.

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Soil, necrotic plant tissue, underground plant parts.

Disease Note: Saprophyte, in necrotic plant tissue. May affect basal and underground parts of plants.

Host: Poaceae, Amaryllidaceae.
