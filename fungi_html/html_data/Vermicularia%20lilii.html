Nomenclature data for Vermicularia lilii

Colletotrichum lilii Plakidas ex Boerema & Hamers 1988 (Ascomycetes, Phyllachorales)

Distribution: Scattered reports in northern hemisphere.

Substrate: Bulbs.

Disease Note: Black scale rot.

Host: Liliaceae, especially Lilium spp.

Supporting Literature:

Boerema, G.H., and Hamers, M.E.C. 1988. Check-list for scientific names of common parasitic fungi. Series 3a: fungi on bulbs: Liliaceae. Netherlands J. Pl. Pathol. 94: 1-32.

Damm, U., Woudenberg, J.H.C., Cannon, P.F., and Crous, P.W. 2009. Colletotrichum species with curved conidia from herbaceous hosts. Fung. Diversity 39: 45-87.
