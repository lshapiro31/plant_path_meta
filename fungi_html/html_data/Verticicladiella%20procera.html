Leptographium procerum (W.B. Kendr.) M.J. Wingf. 1985 (Ascomycetes, Ophiostomatales)

≡ Verticicladiella procera W.B. Kendr. 1962

Notes: Teleomorph unknown.

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Roots, butt.

Disease Note: Root rot. canker, decline.

Host: Pinaceae.
