Grosmannia serpens Goid. 1936 (Ascomycetes, Ophiostomatales)

≡Ceratocystis serpens (Goid.) Moreau 1952

≡Ophiostoma serpens (Goid.) Arx 1952

    =  Leptographium galleciae Fern. Magán 1983

= Scopularia serpens Goid. 1936

    ≡ Leptographium serpens (Goid.) Siemaszko 1939

     Leptographium serpens (Goid.) M.J. Wingf. 1985  Note: Isonym, superfluous.

  ≡Verticicladiella serpens (Goid.) W.B. Kendr. 1962

     Leptographium serpens (Goid.) M.J. Wingf. 1985  Note: Isonym, superfluous.

Notes: Leptographium galleciae - As 'gallaeciae'. Nom. inval. in 1981, valid in 1983. ----- Anamorph leptographium-like.

Distribution: Widespread.

Disease Note: Canker.

Host: Predominantly Pinus spp. (Pinaceae).
