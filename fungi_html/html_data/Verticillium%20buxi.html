Nomenclature data for Verticillium buxi

Clonostachys buxi (J.C. Schmidt ex Link) Schroers 2001 (Ascomycetes, Hypocreales)

≡ Fusidium buxi J.C. Schmidt ex Link 1817

≡Fusisporium buxi (J.C. Schmidt ex Link) Fr. 1832

≡Paecilomyces buxi (J.C. Schmidt ex Link) J.L. Bezerra 1963

≡Sesquicillium buxi (J.C. Schmidt ex Link) W. Gams 1968

≡Verticillium buxi (J.C. Schmidt ex Link) Sacc. 1883

= Nectriella coronata Juel 1925

    ≡ Pseudonectria coronata (Juel) Lowen 1993

    ≡ Bionectria coronata (Juel) Schroers 2001

Distribution: Temperate regions.

Substrate: Twigs, roots, and leaves.

Disease Note: Canker, dieback, root rot, basal stem rot and moribund leaves.

Host: Buxus (Buxaceae).

Supporting Literature:

Schroers, H.-j. 2001. A monograph of Bionectria (Ascomycota, Hypocreales, Bionectriaceae) and its Clonostachys anamorphs. Stud. Mycol. 46: 1-214.
