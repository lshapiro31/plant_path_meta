Notes: For additional synonyms, see Schroers et al. (2001). Tubercularia carpogena Corda 1837 (as carpigena), Clonostachys araucaria Corda, and Clonostachys araucaria var. rosea Preuss 1852 are possible synonyms (Schroers et al. 2001). From Nectria tjibodensis - Not Penz. & Sacc. 1897.

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Various plant parts both living and newly killed.

Disease Note: Dieback, pod rot, crown and root rot, saprobic and endophytic.

Host: Diverse plant hosts.
