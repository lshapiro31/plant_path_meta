Nomenclature data for Volutella colletotrichoides

Lectera colletotrichoides (J.E. Chilton) P.F. Cannon 2012 (, )

≡ Volutella colletotrichoides J.E. Chilton 1954

= Volutella colletotrichoides var. setosa J.E. Chilton 1954

Distribution: Widespread including in USA (IA).

Substrate: Stem and leaves.

Disease Note: Stem and leaf blight of alfalfa and other fabaceous crops.

Host: Fabaceae and other herbaceous plants.

Supporting Literature:

Cannon, P.F., Buddie, A.G., Bridge, P.D., de Neergaard, E., Lubeck, M., and Askar, M.M. 2012. Lectera, a new genus of the Plectosphaerellaceae for the legume pathogen Volutella colletotrichoides. MycoKeys 3: 23-36.

Eken, C., Demirci, E., and Verkley, G.J.M. 2002. First report of Volutella colletotrichoides in Turkey. New Disease Reports 5: 19.
