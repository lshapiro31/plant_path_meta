Nomenclature data for Waitea circinata

Waitea circinata Warcup & Talbot 1962 (Basidiomycetes, Ceratobasidiales)

≡Waitea circinata var. circinata Warcup & Talbot 1962  Note: This name appears in some literature. No other validly published varieties exist in the literature.

= Waitea nuda Clémençon 1990

     Moniliopsis oryzae (Ryker & Gooch) R.T. Moore 1987  Note: Nom. inval. No Latin.

= Rhizoctonia zeae Voorhees 1934

    ≡ Moniliopsis zeae (Voorhees) R.T. Moore 1987

     Chrysorhiza zeae (Voorhees) T.F. Andersen & Stalpers 1996  Note: Nom. inval.

Notes: From Rhizoctonia oryzae - Nom. inval. No Latin.----Type specimen severely damaged and not referable by morphological characters to Rhizoctonia (Ceratobasidiaceae); referable to Waitea circinata (Corticiaceae) by DNA analysis per Stalpers (1994). Oldest epithet not in correct genus; new combination needed or W. circinata should be conserved.

Distribution: Widespread.

Substrate: Roots, soil.

Disease Note: Brown ring patch, sclerotial rot of corn, heath spot of rice, damping off, root rot.

Host: Isolated from various plants.
