Nomenclature data for Xenosporella rosea

Xenosporium thaxteri (Linder) Piroz. 1966 (Ascomycetes, Pleosporales)

≡ Xenosporella thaxteri Linder 1929

= Xenosporella rosea P.H.B. Talbot 1956

Distribution: Widespread based on scattered reports.

Disease Note: Sabrobic.

Host: Numerous hosts in several families.

Supporting Literature:
