Nomenclature data for Xyloma lini

Melampsora lini var. lini (Ehrenb.) Lév. 1870 (Urediniomycetes, Uredinales)

≡ Xyloma lini Ehrenb. 1818

≡Melampsora lini (Ehrenb.) Lév. 1847  Note: Autoecious. Laundon & Waterston (C.M.I. Descr. 51. 1965).

= Uredo lini DC. 1805

= Melampsora lini var. liniperda Körn. 1865

    ≡ Melampsora liniperda (Körn.) Palm 1910

= Melampsora lini var. major Fuckel 1870

= Macrosporium lini-cathartici Unamuno 1933

= Melampsora lini-usitatissimi Kuprev. 1957

Distribution: Cosmopolitan.

Substrate: Leaves, stems. Also seed-borne.

Disease Note: Flax rust.

Host: Linum spp. (Linaceae).
